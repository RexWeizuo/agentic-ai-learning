## ruthless

- **音标**: 'ru:θlis
- **词性**:
- **释义**: a. 无情的, 残忍的, 毫不留情的, 彻底的\n[法] 无情的, 残忍的
- **英文释义**: having no pity or compassion; cruel and determined to achieve one's goals at any cost
- **例句**:

  - The company launched a ruthless cost-cutting campaign, laying off hundreds of employees without warning.
  - Her ruthless ambition drove her to undermine colleagues and seize every opportunity for promotion.
- **词根词缀**: ruth(怜悯)+less(无)→无情的
- **考频**: 1
