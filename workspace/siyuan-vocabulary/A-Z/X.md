## x-ray

- **音标**: 'eks'rei
- **词性**:
- **释义**: a. X射线的\nvt. 用X光检查, 照X光\nvi. 使用X光
- **英文释义**: a type of high-energy electromagnetic radiation used to view internal structures of the body; to examine or image something using X-rays
- **例句**:

  - The radiologist ordered an X-ray to check for a fractured rib.
  - The surgeon decided to X-ray the patient's knee before scheduling the arthroscopy.
- **词根词缀**: x-ray
