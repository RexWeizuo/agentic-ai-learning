## luxury

- **音标**: 'lʌkʃәri
- **词性**:
- **释义**: n. 奢侈, 豪华\na. 奢侈的, 豪华的
- **英文释义**: something expensive and enjoyable but not necessary for basic living
- **例句**:

  - Owning a private jet is a luxury few can afford.
  - The hotel offers luxury accommodation with panoramic sea views.
- **词根词缀**: luxur(奢侈)+y(名词)→奢侈
- **考频**: 59

## lyric

- **音标**: 'lirik
- **词性**:
- **释义**: n. 抒情诗, 歌词\na. 抒情的
- **英文释义**: A short poem expressing personal emotions, or the words of a song.
- **例句**:

  - Her early lyric poems reveal a deep sensitivity to nature and loss.
  - The album's success was partly due to its poetic lyric and melodic simplicity.
- **词根词缀**: lyr(竖琴)+ic(形容词)→抒情诗的
- **考频**: 1
