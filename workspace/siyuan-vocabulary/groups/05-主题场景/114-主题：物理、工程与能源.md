# 主题：物理、工程与能源

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 理工科背景文章核心词汇，turbine (涡轮机) 和 valve (阀门) 在机械与能源类阅读中常见。

**7 词**

---

## volt

- **音标**: vәult
- **释义**: n. 伏特\n[化] 伏特
- **英文释义**: The SI unit of electric potential, electromotive force, and voltage.
- **词根词缀**: volt(伏特)→伏特
- **例句**:
- A standard AA battery provides about 1.5 volts.
- The circuit malfunctioned when the input voltage exceeded 24 volts.
- **考频**: 1
## voltage

- **音标**: 'vәultidʒ
- **释义**: n. 电压, 伏特数\n[化] 电压
- **英文释义**: The electrical potential difference between two points in a circuit, measured in volts.
- **词根词缀**: volt(伏特)+age(名词)→伏特数→电压
- **例句**:
- High voltage is required to transmit electricity efficiently over long distances.
- A sudden surge in voltage damaged several components of the circuit.
- **考频**: 2
## watt

- **音标**: wɒt
- **释义**: n. 瓦(特)\n[化] 瓦; 瓦特
- **英文释义**: The SI unit of power, equal to one joule per second.
- **词根词缀**: watt(瓦特)→瓦特
- **例句**:
- A typical LED light bulb consumes about 10 watts of electrical power.
- The new laptop's processor has a thermal design power (TDP) of only 15 watts, making it highly energy-efficient.
- **考频**: 1
## x-ray

- **音标**: 'eks'rei
- **释义**: a. X射线的\nvt. 用X光检查, 照X光\nvi. 使用X光
- **英文释义**: a type of high-energy electromagnetic radiation used to view internal structures of the body; to examine or image something using X-rays
- **词根词缀**: x-ray
- **例句**:
- The radiologist ordered an X-ray to check for a fractured rib.
- The surgeon decided to X-ray the patient's knee before scheduling the arthroscopy.
## ultraviolet

- **音标**: .ʌltrә'vaiәlit
- **释义**: a. 紫外线的\n[化] 紫外线的; 紫外线
- **英文释义**: relating to or employing electromagnetic radiation with wavelengths shorter than visible light but longer than X-rays
- **词根词缀**: ultra(超出)+violet(紫色)→超出紫色→紫外线
- **例句**:
- Prolonged exposure to ultraviolet radiation from the sun can damage skin DNA and increase the risk of melanoma.
- The laboratory used an ultraviolet spectrophotometer to analyze the absorption characteristics of the organic compound.
- **考频**: 2
## turbine

- **音标**: 'tә:bain
- **释义**: n. 涡轮机\n[化] 涡轮机
- **英文释义**: A rotary mechanical device that extracts energy from a fluid flow and converts it into useful work, typically used in power generation and propulsion.
- **词根词缀**: turb(旋转)+ine(名词)→旋转的机器→涡轮
- **例句**:
- The power plant uses gas turbines to generate electricity efficiently.
- Modern jet engines rely on high-speed turbine blades to compress air and produce thrust.
- **考频**: 3
## valve

- **音标**: vælv
- **释义**: n. 活瓣, 阀, 活门\nv. 装阀, 用阀调节
- **英文释义**: A device that controls the flow of a fluid or gas by opening, closing, or partially obstructing a passage.
- **词根词缀**: valv(门)+e→阀门
- **例句**:
- The heart's mitral valve ensures blood flows in one direction.
- Engineers installed a pressure-relief valve to prevent system failure during overload.
- **考频**: 1
