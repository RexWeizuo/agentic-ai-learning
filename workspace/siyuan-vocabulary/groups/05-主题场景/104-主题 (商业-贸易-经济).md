# 主题 (商业/贸易/经济)

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 商业与经济场景。shipment(装船/出货), slump(暴跌/衰退), sponsor(赞助商), smuggle(走私)。

**6 词**

---

## salesman

- **音标**: 'seilzmәn
- **释义**: n. 售货员, 推销员\n[经] 售货员, 店员, 推销员
- **英文释义**: A person whose job is to sell goods or services, often directly to customers or businesses.
- **词根词缀**: sale(销售)+s+man(人)→销售员
- **例句**:
- The seasoned salesman closed the deal after a persuasive presentation to the client's procurement team.
- She challenged the traditional role of the salesman by using data analytics to tailor her pitch to each prospect's needs.
- **考频**: 29
## shipment

- **音标**: 'ʃipmәnt
- **释义**: n. 装船, 出货\n[经] 运送, 运送(寄出)货物, 装载
- **英文释义**: The act of sending goods by ship, plane, or other transport; a consignment of goods sent from one place to another.
- **词根词缀**: ship(船)+ment(名词)→装船→运输
- **例句**:
- The shipment of electronic components was delayed due to customs inspection.
- We expect the next shipment to arrive at the warehouse by Friday, as stipulated in the contract.
- **考频**: 5
## shortage

- **音标**: 'ʃɒ:tidʒ
- **释义**: n. 不足, 缺乏\n[化] 缺额
- **英文释义**: a situation in which there is not enough of something that is needed
- **词根词缀**: short(短)+age(名词)→短缺
- **例句**:
- The global semiconductor shortage has severely disrupted automobile production.
- Many hospitals are facing a critical shortage of nursing staff, affecting patient care quality.
- **考频**: 134
## slump

- **音标**: slʌmp
- **释义**: n. 暴跌, 垂头弯腰的姿态\nvi. 猛然掉落, 陷入, 衰落(经济等)
- **英文释义**: a sudden and sharp decline in value, activity, or condition; or to fall or sink suddenly and heavily
- **词根词缀**: slump(词根)→暴跌
- **例句**:
- The stock market experienced a severe slump following the announcement of new trade tariffs.
- After losing the election, the party's popularity began to slump rapidly.
- **考频**: 12
## sponsor

- **音标**: 'spɒnsә
- **释义**: n. 保证人, 赞助者, 发起者, 倡议者, 教父\nvt. 发起, 赞助, 倡议
- **英文释义**: A person or organization that provides financial or other support for an event, activity, or person; or one who initiates or advocates for a proposal or action.
- **词根词缀**: spons(承诺)+or(人)→承诺的人→赞助者
- **例句**:
- The multinational corporation agreed to sponsor the international conference on sustainable development.
- As the primary sponsor of the bill, she led the parliamentary debate and secured bipartisan support.
- **考频**: 54
## smuggle

- **音标**: 'smʌgl
- **释义**: vt. 偷运, 走私, 私运\nvi. 走私
- **英文释义**: to move goods or people illegally across a border, especially to avoid paying taxes or to bypass immigration controls
- **词根词缀**: smuggle(词根)→走私
- **例句**:
- Armed guards were deployed to prevent criminals from smuggling drugs across the border.
- The court sentenced him to five years in prison for attempting to smuggle endangered wildlife into the country.
- **考频**: 4
