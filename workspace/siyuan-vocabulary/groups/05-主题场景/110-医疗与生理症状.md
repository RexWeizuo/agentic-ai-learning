# 医疗与生理症状

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 医学场景核心词汇。注意 symptom (单一症状) 与 syndrome (综合征/并发症状) 的区别。

**6 词**

---

## surgeon

- **音标**: 'sә:dʒәn
- **释义**: n. 外科医生, 军医, 船医\n[医] 外科医师
- **英文释义**: A medical doctor who performs surgical operations.
- **词根词缀**: sur(手)+geon(工作)→用手工作的人→外科医生
- **例句**:
- The surgeon successfully removed the tumor during a six-hour operation.
- She trained for over ten years before becoming a board-certified neurosurgeon.
- **考频**: 28
## surgery

- **音标**: 'sә:dʒәri
- **释义**: n. 外科, 手术, 手术室, 换球术
- **英文释义**: A medical procedure involving incision, removal, or repair of tissues or organs; also refers to the branch of medicine concerned with such procedures or the room where they are performed.
- **词根词缀**: sur(手)+gery(工作)→用手工作→外科手术
- **例句**:
- The patient underwent emergency surgery to remove a ruptured appendix.
- After completing her residency in general surgery, she specialized in pediatric cardiac surgery.
- **考频**: 42
## symptom

- **音标**: 'simptәm
- **释义**: n. 症状, 征候, 征兆\n[医] 症状
- **英文释义**: A physical or mental feature indicating the presence of a disease or condition.
- **词根词缀**: sym(一起)+ptom(落下)→一起落下的东西→症状
- **例句**:
- Fever and cough are common symptoms of influenza.
- The sudden loss of memory can be an early symptom of Alzheimer's disease.
- **考频**: 83
## syndrome

- **音标**: 'sindrәum
- **释义**: n. 并发症状, 综合征, 同时存在的事物\n[计] 校验子; 并发位
- **英文释义**: A group of symptoms that consistently occur together or a condition characterized by a set of associated symptoms.
- **词根词缀**: syn(一起)+drome(跑)→一起跑→综合征
- **例句**:
- Down syndrome is a genetic disorder caused by the presence of an extra chromosome 21.
- The patient exhibited a classic syndrome of fever, rash, and joint pain, suggesting systemic lupus erythematosus.
- **考频**: 25
## therapy

- **音标**: 'θerәpi
- **释义**: n. 治疗\n[医] 疗法, 治疗
- **英文释义**: medical or psychological treatment intended to relieve or cure a disorder or illness
- **词根词缀**: therap(治疗)+y(名词)→治疗
- **例句**:
- Cognitive-behavioral therapy has proven highly effective for treating anxiety disorders.
- Many patients with chronic pain benefit from a combination of physical therapy and medication.
- **考频**: 38
## tissue

- **音标**: 'tiʃu:
- **释义**: n. 薄的织物, 薄纱, 棉纸, 组织, 一套\n[化] 组织
- **英文释义**: A thin, soft paper product used for wiping or cleaning; or a group of similar cells that perform a specific function in an organism.
- **词根词缀**: tissue→组织
- **例句**:
- The surgeon carefully examined the damaged tissue under the microscope.
- She dabbed her eyes with a tissue after hearing the emotional speech.
- **考频**: 20
