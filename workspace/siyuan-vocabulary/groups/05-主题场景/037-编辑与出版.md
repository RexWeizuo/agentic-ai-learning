## edit

- **音标**: 'edit
- **释义**: vt. 编辑, 编校, 修订, 剪辑\n[计] 编辑; DOS外部命令:该命令是一个用于编辑文本文件的全屏幕编辑程序
- **英文释义**: to revise, correct, or prepare written, visual, or audio material for publication or presentation
- **词根词缀**: e(出)+dit(给)→给出→编辑
- **例句**:
- The editor spent hours editing the manuscript to improve its clarity and coherence.
- Before uploading the video, she used professional software to edit out unnecessary footage and add subtitles.
- **考频**: 36
