## electrical

- **音标**: i'lektrikәl
- **释义**: a. 电的, 有关电的\n[医] 电的
- **英文释义**: relating to electricity or its uses
- **词根词缀**: electr(电)+ical(形容词)→电的
- **例句**:
- The device requires proper electrical insulation to prevent short circuits.
- Electrical engineering is a core discipline in modern technological development.
- **考频**: 34
## electrician

- **音标**: .ilek'triʃәn
- **释义**: n. 电学家, 电气技师, 电工\n[化] 电工; 电气工人
- **英文释义**: A person who installs, maintains, and repairs electrical systems and equipment.
- **词根词缀**: electr(电)+ician(人)→电工
- **例句**:
- The electrician diagnosed the short circuit in the basement wiring within minutes.
- Before renovating the old building, we hired a licensed electrician to ensure all new outlets met safety codes.
- **考频**: 1
## electron

- **音标**: i'lektrɒn
- **释义**: n. 电子\n[化] 电子
- **英文释义**: A stable subatomic particle with a negative electric charge, found in all atoms and involved in chemical bonding and electricity.
- **词根词缀**: electr(电)+on(粒子)→电子
- **例句**:
- The behavior of electrons in semiconductors is fundamental to modern electronics.
- In redox reactions, electrons are transferred from the reducing agent to the oxidizing agent.
- **考频**: 1
