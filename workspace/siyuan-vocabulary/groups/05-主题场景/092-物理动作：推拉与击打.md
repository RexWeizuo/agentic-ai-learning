# 物理动作：推拉与击打

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 基础物理动作，propel 强调推进/驱使，puff 强调喷气/喘息。

**5 词**

---

## pull

- **音标**: pul
- **释义**: vt. 拉, 拖, 拔, 牵, 撕开, 吸引\nvi. 拉, 拖, 拔, 有吸引力\nn. 拉, 拖, 拔, 拉力, 牵引力, 划船, 吸引
- **英文释义**: to use force to move something or someone toward oneself or in a particular direction; also used figuratively to mean attracting attention or influence
- **词根词缀**: pull(拉)→拉
- **例句**:
- The government is pulling out of the international climate agreement, raising concerns among environmentalists.
- Her compelling research and articulate presentation pulled the committee's attention away from more established candidates.
- **考频**: 104
## push

- **音标**: puʃ
- **释义**: n. 推, 推动, 奋斗, 攻击, 进取心\nvt. 推, 推动, 使伸出, 推行, 逼迫, 增加\nvi. 推, 推进, 增加, 努力争取
- **英文释义**: to exert force on something to move it away from oneself or in a particular direction; also used figuratively to mean promoting, urging, or striving for something
- **词根词缀**: push(推)→推
- **例句**:
- The government is pushing for stricter environmental regulations to combat climate change.
- Despite fierce competition, she maintained her push toward a leadership role in the multinational corporation.
- **考频**: 167
## punch

- **音标**: pʌntʃ
- **释义**: n. 打洞器, 钻孔机, 冲压机, 冲床, 潘趣酒\nvt. 以拳重击, 开洞, 冲压\nvi. 用拳猛击
- **英文释义**: A tool or machine for making holes or shaping metal by pressure; also, a strong blow with the fist.
- **词根词缀**: punch(冲压)→打孔
- **例句**:
- The engineer used a hydraulic punch to create precise holes in the steel plate.
- She punched a hole in the metal sheet before assembling the frame.
- **考频**: 9
## puff

- **音标**: pʌf
- **释义**: n. 一阵喷烟, 肿块, 喘息\nv. 喷出, 张开, 吹捧, (使)充气, 喘气, (使)骄傲, (使)膨胀
- **英文释义**: A short burst of smoke, air, or sound; or to exhale forcefully, inflate, or boast.
- **词根词缀**: puff(拟声)→吹气
- **例句**:
- He took a puff on his cigarette and exhaled slowly.
- The politician's latest policy announcement was met with more puff than substance, drawing criticism from economists.
- **考频**: 3
## propel

- **音标**: prәu'pel
- **释义**: vt. 推进, 驱使\n[机] 推进
- **英文释义**: to drive or push something forward, often with force or energy
- **词根词缀**: pro(向前)+pel(推)→向前推→推进
- **例句**:
- The new policy is expected to propel the economy toward faster growth.
- Her determination and intellect propelled her to the top of her field despite numerous obstacles.
- **考频**: 15
