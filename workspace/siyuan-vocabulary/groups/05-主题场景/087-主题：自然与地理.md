# 主题：自然与地理

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 自然物质与地理词汇。oxygen(氧), ozone(臭氧), peninsula(半岛), polar(极地的), petroleum(石油)。

**8 词**

---

## oxygen

- **音标**: 'ɒksәdʒәn
- **释义**: n. 氧\n[化] 氧O-2
- **英文释义**: A colorless, odorless, gaseous chemical element essential for respiration and combustion, with atomic number 8.
- **词根词缀**: oxy(尖锐)+gen(产生)→产生酸的物质→氧气
- **例句**:
- The human body requires a continuous supply of oxygen to sustain cellular metabolism.
- In aerobic respiration, glucose reacts with oxygen to produce carbon dioxide, water, and ATP.
- **考频**: 31
## ozone

- **音标**: 'әuzәun
- **释义**: n. 新鲜的空气, 臭氧, 使人愉快的影响\n[化] 臭氧
- **英文释义**: A pale blue gas with a sharp, chlorine-like odor, formed by electrical discharges or ultraviolet radiation; it is an allotrope of oxygen (O₃) and plays a critical role in absorbing harmful UV radiation in the stratosphere.
- **词根词缀**: oz(气味)+one(名词)→有气味的气体→臭氧
- **例句**:
- The depletion of the ozone layer has raised global concerns about increased skin cancer rates.
- Ground-level ozone, a major component of smog, is harmful to human respiratory health.
- **考频**: 7
## peninsula

- **音标**: pi'ninsjulә
- **释义**: n. 半岛, 突出的地方, 伊比利亚半岛
- **英文释义**: a piece of land that is surrounded by water on three sides and connected to a larger landmass on the fourth
- **词根词缀**: pen(几乎)+insula(岛)→几乎成为岛→半岛
- **例句**:
- The Iberian Peninsula is home to Spain, Portugal, and Andorra.
- Geographers often study how peninsulas influence climate patterns and maritime trade routes.
- **考频**: 3
## polar

- **音标**: 'pәulә
- **释义**: a. 两极的, 极地的, 正好相反的, 极性的\nn. 极线, 极面
- **英文释义**: Relating to the North or South Pole; having opposite or contrasting properties; exhibiting polarity.
- **词根词缀**: polar(词根:极)→极地的
- **例句**:
- The polar regions are experiencing accelerated ice melt due to global warming.
- Her views on climate policy are polar to those of her predecessor, reflecting a fundamental ideological divide.
- **考频**: 11
## pebble

- **音标**: 'pebl
- **释义**: n. 小圆石, 小鹅卵石, 水晶透镜, 碎石花纹\n[医] 小石
- **英文释义**: A small, smooth, rounded stone, typically worn by water erosion.
- **词根词缀**: pebble(词根：卵石)→卵石
- **例句**:
- The children collected colorful pebbles along the riverbank.
- Geologists examined the pebble-rich sediment layer to infer past fluvial activity.
- **考频**: 2
## pearl

- **音标**: pә:l
- **释义**: n. 珍珠, 珠灰色, 杰出者, 珍贵之物\nvt. 用珍珠镶, 使成珠状\nvi. 成珠子状, 采珍珠, 用珍珠做
- **英文释义**: A hard, lustrous, rounded object produced within the soft tissue of a living bivalve mollusk; also used metaphorically to denote something rare, valuable, or outstanding.
- **词根词缀**: pearl(词根：珍珠)→珍珠
- **例句**:
- Her voice was a pearl of clarity and warmth in an otherwise monotonous lecture.
- The ancient manuscript, long thought lost, proved to be a pearl of medieval scholarship.
- **考频**: 15
## peanut

- **音标**: 'pi:nʌt
- **释义**: n. 花生, 小人物, 极小的数额\na. 渺小的, 微不足道的
- **英文释义**: A small edible nut grown underground; also used figuratively to refer to a person or thing of little importance or influence.
- **词根词缀**: pea(豌豆)+nut(坚果)→豌豆状的坚果→花生
- **例句**:
- He dismissed the critic's opinion as a peanut compared to the expert consensus.
- The budget allocated for the pilot project was just a peanut—barely enough to cover basic materials.
- **考频**: 1
## petroleum

- **音标**: pә'trәuliәm
- **释义**: n. 石油\n[化] 石油
- **英文释义**: A naturally occurring, flammable liquid mixture of hydrocarbons found beneath the Earth's surface, refined to produce fuels and chemicals.
- **词根词缀**: petr(石头)+oleum(油)→石油
- **例句**:
- The global economy remains heavily dependent on petroleum for energy and transportation.
- Due to environmental concerns, many countries are investing in renewable alternatives to reduce their reliance on petroleum.
- **考频**: 11
