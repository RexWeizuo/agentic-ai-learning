# 主题：学术与文学

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 学术研究与文学创作词汇。philosophy(哲学), phenomenon(现象), phrase(短语), poetry(诗歌)。

**5 词**

---

## philosophy

- **音标**: fi'lɒsәfi
- **释义**: n. 哲学, 人生观, 哲学思想, 哲理, 基本原理, 见解, 达观, 沉着
- **英文释义**: The study of fundamental questions about existence, knowledge, values, reason, and reality; also refers to a particular system of beliefs or principles guiding thought or conduct.
- **词根词缀**: philo(爱)+soph(智慧)+y(名词)→爱智慧→哲学
- **例句**:
- Her teaching philosophy emphasizes critical thinking and student autonomy.
- The company's management philosophy is rooted in transparency, accountability, and long-term sustainability.
- **考频**: 61
## philosopher

- **音标**: fi'lɒsәfә
- **释义**: n. 哲学家, 哲人, 思想开创人, 达观的人\n[法] 哲学家, 哲学研究者
- **英文释义**: A person who studies or writes about philosophy, especially one who seeks wisdom or fundamental truths about existence, knowledge, and ethics.
- **词根词缀**: philo(爱)+soph(智慧)+er(人)→爱智慧的人→哲学家
- **例句**:
- Plato, one of the most influential philosophers in Western thought, laid the foundations for metaphysics and epistemology.
- A true philosopher does not merely accumulate knowledge but questions assumptions and cultivates critical reflection.
- **考频**: 44
## phenomenon

- **音标**: fi'nɒminәn
- **释义**: n. 现象, 迹象, 表现, 奇迹, 奇才\n[化] 现象
- **英文释义**: an observable fact or event, especially one that is unusual or difficult to explain
- **词根词缀**: phen(显现)+omen(预兆)+on(名词)→显现出来的现象→现象
- **例句**:
- Climate change is a complex global phenomenon that requires interdisciplinary research.
- Her ability to master six languages by age twelve made her a linguistic phenomenon.
- **考频**: 88
## phrase

- **音标**: freiz
- **释义**: n. 惯用语, 词组, 成语, 措词, 乐句\nvt. 用短语表达, 把(乐曲)分成短句\n[计] 短语
- **英文释义**: A small group of words that function as a single unit in meaning but do not form a complete sentence.
- **词根词缀**: phrase(短语)→短语
- **例句**:
- The phrase 'by and large' is an idiomatic expression meaning 'in general'.
- She carefully phrased her response to avoid sounding confrontational.
- **考频**: 259
## poetry

- **音标**: 'pәuitri
- **释义**: n. 诗, 韵文, 诗歌艺术
- **英文释义**: Literary work in verse, characterized by aesthetic language, rhythm, and often metaphorical expression.
- **词根词缀**: poet(词根:诗人)+ry(名词后缀)→诗歌
- **例句**:
- Her debut collection of poetry won critical acclaim for its lyrical intensity and thematic depth.
- The professor argued that modernist poetry challenges conventional syntax to evoke fragmented consciousness.
- **考频**: 76
