# 数据、信息、网络

> **类型**: 主题

## data

- **音标**: 'deitә
- **释义**: pl. 资料, 数据\n[计] 数据; DOS内部命令:用于显示或设定系统的日期
- **英文释义**: facts and statistics collected together for reference or analysis
- **词根词缀**: dat(give)+a
- **例句**:
- The research team analyzed a large volume of data to identify trends in consumer behavior.
- Recent data from the World Health Organization indicate a significant decline in global malaria cases.
## database

- **音标**: 'deitәbeis
- **释义**: n. 数据库\n[计] 数据库
- **英文释义**: An organized collection of data stored and accessed electronically.
- **词根词缀**: data(数据)+base(基础)→数据库
- **例句**:
- The research team built a comprehensive database of climate change indicators to support longitudinal analysis.
- Access to the university's academic database is restricted to enrolled students and faculty members.
- **考频**: 49
## copyright

- **音标**: 'kɒpirait
- **释义**: n. 版权, 著作权\n[法] 著作权
- **英文释义**: The legal right granted to an author, composer, or creator to control the use and distribution of their original work.
- **词根词缀**: copy(复制)+right(权利)→复制权→版权
- **例句**:
- The publisher must obtain the author's permission before reprinting the book, as the copyright remains with the author.
- Under international copyright law, digital works are protected automatically upon creation, without requiring formal registration.
- **考频**: 37
## copy

- **音标**: 'kɒpi
- **释义**: n. 副本, 摹仿, 一册\nv. 复印, 抄袭, 复制\n[计] 副本; 复制; DOS内部命令:复制文件\n将几个文件合并成一个文件, 以及将文件传至外设或在设备之间传送
- **英文释义**: A duplicate or reproduction of an original document, file, or object; to make such a duplicate.
- **词根词缀**: copy(抄写)→复制
- **例句**:
- The journalist submitted a polished copy of her article before the deadline.
- Copying software without permission violates copyright law and is strictly prohibited in academic and professional settings.
- **考频**: 98
