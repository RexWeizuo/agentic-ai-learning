# 主题：空间、位置与地理

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 表示地理位置、空间方位、距离及都市环境的词汇。

**8 词**

## midst

- **音标**: midst
- **释义**: n. 中间\nprep. 在...中间
- **英文释义**: the middle part or center of something; among or surrounded by
- **词根词缀**: mid(中间)+st→中间
- **例句**:
- She remained calm even in the midst of chaos.
- The research was conducted in the midst of a global pandemic, posing significant logistical challenges.
- **考频**: 12
## mile

- **音标**: mail
- **释义**: n. 英里, 很大距离\n[机] 英里, 哩
- **英文释义**: A unit of length equal to 1,760 yards or approximately 1.609 kilometers.
- **词根词缀**: mile(千步)→英里
- **例句**:
- The marathon is just over 26 miles long, demanding exceptional endurance from runners.
- Although the two cities are only 50 miles apart, traffic congestion can make the journey feel like a hundred miles.
- **考频**: 147
## nearby

- **音标**: 'niәbai
- **释义**: a. 附近的, 近旁的\nadv. 在附近, 近旁地\nprep. 在...附近
- **英文释义**: not far away in distance; close at hand
- **词根词缀**: near(近)+by(在旁)→附近
- **例句**:
- There are several high-quality research institutes nearby, making collaboration convenient for graduate students.
- The conference venue is located nearby the university campus, so attendees can walk there in under ten minutes.
- **考频**: 51
## neighborhood

- **释义**: n. 附近, 邻近

## orbit

- **音标**: 'ɒ:bit
- **释义**: n. 轨道, 常轨, 眼眶\nvt. 绕...轨道而行\nvi. 进入轨道, 盘旋
- **英文释义**: the curved path of an object around a star, planet, or moon; or the area of influence or activity
- **词根词缀**: orb(圆)+it(名词后缀)→圆形轨道→轨道
- **例句**:
- The International Space Station orbits Earth every 90 minutes.
- Her political career has long orbited the central issue of economic inequality.
- **考频**: 28
## mount

- **音标**: maunt
- **释义**: n. 乘骑用马, 框, 衬纸, 山\nvi. 乘马, 爬上, 增长\nvt. 爬上, 使上马, 装上, 装裱, 安放, 制作...的标本, 设置, 上演\n[计] 安装
- **英文释义**: to fix or attach something securely; to get on a horse or vehicle; to increase in amount or intensity; (computing) to make a storage device accessible to the operating system
- **词根词缀**: mount(山)→登上
- **例句**:
- The researchers mounted the specimen on a glass slide for microscopic examination.
- Before running the software, you must mount the external drive in the system directory.
- **考频**: 34
## metropolitan

- **音标**: .metrә'pɒlitn
- **释义**: n. 大都市居民, 都主教, 宗主国的公民\na. 大都市的, 都主教区的, 宗主国的
- **英文释义**: relating to a large city or its surrounding area; or denoting the chief bishop of an ecclesiastical province
- **词根词缀**: metro(母亲)+polit(城市)+an(形容词)→大都市的
- **例句**:
- The metropolitan area of Tokyo encompasses over 37 million people, making it the world's largest urban agglomeration.
- As the metropolitan of the province, she presided over the synod of bishops and exercised oversight over several dioceses.
- **考频**: 16
