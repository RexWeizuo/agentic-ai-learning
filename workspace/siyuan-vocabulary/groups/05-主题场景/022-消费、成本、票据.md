# 消费、成本、票据

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 经济与消费场景词汇，coupon 指优惠券或息票。

**4 词**

---

## consume

- **音标**: kәn'sju:m
- **释义**: vt. 消耗, 消费, 消灭\nvi. 耗尽, 毁灭
- **英文释义**: to use up resources, energy, or time; to eat or drink something
- **词根词缀**: con(完全)+sum(拿取)+e→完全拿取→消费
- **例句**:
- The manufacturing process consumes vast amounts of electricity.
- Her obsession with social media consumed all her spare time, leaving little room for study.
- **考频**: 141
## consumption

- **音标**: kәn'sʌmpʃәn
- **释义**: n. 消费, 消费量, 痨病\n[化] 消耗量; 耗量
- **英文释义**: the act of using up resources, goods, or services; the amount of something used, especially energy or fuel
- **词根词缀**: con(完全)+sumpt(拿取)+ion(名词)→完全拿取→消费
- **例句**:
- Rising energy consumption in developing countries is putting pressure on global carbon emissions.
- The government introduced new policies to curb excessive consumption of single-use plastics.
- **考频**: 187
## costly

- **音标**: 'kɒstli
- **释义**: a. 昂贵的, 奢华的, 费用大的
- **英文释义**: expensive; involving a high cost or great sacrifice
- **词根词缀**: cost(成本)+ly(形容词)→昂贵的
- **例句**:
- The company abandoned the project because it proved too costly to implement.
- Losing the key witness was a costly mistake that jeopardized the entire case.
- **考频**: 55
## coupon

- **音标**: 'ku:pɒn
- **释义**: n. 息票, 赠券\n[经] 息票, 利息单, 联票
- **英文释义**: A detachable part of a bond that entitles the holder to receive interest payments; also, a voucher offering a discount or promotional benefit.
- **词根词缀**: coupon(票券)→优惠券
- **例句**:
- Investors collect annual interest by submitting the physical coupon attached to the bond certificate.
- She saved over $50 on her online purchase by applying a digital coupon at checkout.
- **考频**: 15
