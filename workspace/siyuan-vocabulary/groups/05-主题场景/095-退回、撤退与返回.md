# 退回、撤退与返回

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: recede 指后退/减弱，recession 常指经济衰退，retreat 指军队撤退或逃避。

**4 词**

---

## recede

- **音标**: ri'si:d
- **释义**: vi. 向后退, 退却, 收回, 降低, 减弱\n[医] 退缩
- **英文释义**: to move back or become less noticeable, intense, or severe
- **词根词缀**: re(回)+cede(走)→走回→后退
- **例句**:
- As the floodwaters began to recede, residents returned to assess the damage.
- Her fever receded after two days of treatment, signaling improvement in her condition.
- **考频**: 3
## recession

- **音标**: ri'seʃәn
- **释义**: n. 后退, 凹处, 衰退, 归还\n[医] 退缩
- **英文释义**: A period of temporary economic decline during which trade and industrial activity are reduced, generally identified by a fall in GDP for two consecutive quarters.
- **词根词缀**: re(回)+cess(走)+ion(名词)→走回→衰退
- **例句**:
- The country entered a severe recession in 2008, leading to widespread job losses and business closures.
- Policymakers implemented stimulus measures to mitigate the impact of the global recession on small enterprises.
- **考频**: 119
## retreat

- **音标**: ri'tri:t
- **释义**: n. 休息寓所, 撤退, 隐居, 退避\nvi. 撤退, 隐退, 向后倾\nvt. 退(棋)
- **英文释义**: to withdraw from a position, situation, or engagement; or a place for quiet seclusion or rest
- **词根词缀**: re(回)+treat(拉)→拉回→撤退
- **例句**:
- The army was forced to retreat after suffering heavy casualties.
- She decided to retreat from public life and spend more time at her countryside cottage.
- **考频**: 29
## return

- **音标**: ri'tә:n
- **释义**: n. 回来, 返回, 来回票, 归还, 报答, 利润率, 报告书\na. 返回的, 回程的, 报答的, 反向的, 重现的, 复原的\nvi. 返回, 归还, 回来\nvt. 归还, 还, 回报, 产生, 反射, 报告, 申报, 退回\n[计] 返回
- **英文释义**: to go or come back to a place, person, or condition; to give something back; to yield profit or results
- **词根词缀**: re(回)+turn(转)→转回→返回
- **例句**:
- After a three-year absence, she made a triumphant return to the academic world.
- The company's latest investment is expected to return a 12% annual profit.
- **考频**: 328
