# 思想/认知/直觉

> **类型**: 主题场景

> **学习提示**: 认知与哲学场景。hypothesis (假设)；ideology (意识形态/思想体系)。

**5 词**

---

## hypothesis

- **音标**: hai'pɒθәsis
- **释义**: n. 假设\n[化] 假设
- **英文释义**: A proposed explanation for a phenomenon, based on limited evidence, to be tested through further investigation.
- **词根词缀**: hypo(在...下)+thes(放置)+is→放在下面的想法→假说
- **例句**:
- The researchers formulated a hypothesis that increased screen time correlates with reduced attention span in adolescents.
- Before conducting the experiment, the team registered their hypothesis with an independent review board to ensure scientific rigor.
- **考频**: 26
## ideology

- **音标**: .aidi'ɒlәdʒi
- **释义**: n. 思想体系, 意识形态, 观念学, 空论\n[医] 观念学, 观念形态
- **英文释义**: A system of ideas and ideals, especially one that forms the basis of economic or political theory and policy.
- **词根词缀**: ideo(思想)+logy(学科)→思想体系→意识形态
- **例句**:
- Her political decisions are deeply rooted in a liberal ideology.
- The novel critiques the dominant ideology of consumerism in contemporary society.
- **考频**: 13
## ideal

- **音标**: ai'diәl
- **释义**: n. 理想, 典范, 观念, 思想, 最后目标\na. 理想的, 完美的, 空想的, 观念的, 唯心论的
- **英文释义**: a standard of perfection or excellence; a person or thing regarded as perfect or exemplary
- **词根词缀**: idea(想法)+al→想法的→理想的
- **例句**:
- She has always pursued an ideal of social justice that transcends political expediency.
- While the free-market model is often presented as the ideal economic system, its real-world implementation faces numerous structural challenges.
- **考频**: 130
## intuition

- **音标**: .intju:'iʃәn
- **释义**: n. 直觉, 直觉的知识
- **英文释义**: the ability to understand something immediately without conscious reasoning
- **词根词缀**: in(进入)+tuit(看)+ion(名词)→向内看→直觉
- **例句**:
- Her intuition told her that the deal was too good to be true.
- In complex ethical dilemmas, moral intuition often complements rational analysis.
- **考频**: 14
## instinct

- **音标**: 'instiŋkt
- **释义**: n. 本能, 直觉\na. 充满着的
- **英文释义**: a natural or innate impulse or behavior that is not learned
- **词根词缀**: in(在)+stinct(刺)→内在的刺激→本能
- **例句**:
- Her decision to flee the burning building was driven by pure instinct, not rational thought.
- Some scientists argue that altruism in humans has an evolutionary basis rooted in social instinct.
- **考频**: 34
