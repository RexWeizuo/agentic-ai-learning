# 动物与生物

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 包含现实动物、昆虫及神话生物，drone 特指雄蜂或无人机。

**6 词**

## dinosaur

- **音标**: 'dainәsɒ:
- **释义**: n. 恐龙
- **英文释义**: a large extinct reptile that lived millions of years ago, especially one of the group that walked on two or four legs and included the largest animals ever to live on land
- **词根词缀**: de(加强)+ino(可怕的)+saur(蜥蜴)→可怕的蜥蜴→恐龙
- **例句**:
- The discovery of well-preserved dinosaur fossils in Mongolia has provided crucial evidence for understanding their behavior and evolution.
- Modern birds are now widely regarded as the only surviving lineage of theropod dinosaurs, challenging traditional classifications.
- **考频**: 7
## donkey

- **音标**: 'dɒŋki
- **释义**: n. 驴子, 笨蛋, 顽固者, 辅助发动机, 辅助泵
- **英文释义**: A domesticated mammal (Equus africanus asinus) used for carrying loads or riding; also used informally to refer to a stubborn or foolish person.
- **词根词缀**: don(驴)+key→驴子
- **例句**:
- The farmer relied on his donkey to transport hay across the rocky terrain.
- Calling someone a donkey in a formal debate is inappropriate and undermines constructive discourse.
- **考频**: 8
## dove

- **音标**: dʌv
- **释义**: n. 鸽子\ndive的过去式
- **英文释义**: a gentle bird symbolizing peace; or the past tense and past participle of 'dive'
- **词根词缀**: dove
- **例句**:
- The white dove released at the ceremony became a powerful symbol of reconciliation.
- After the stock market dove 15% in a single day, investors panicked.
## dragon

- **音标**: 'drægәn
- **释义**: n. 龙, 凶暴的人\n[法] 凶恶的人, 凶恶严厉的监护人
- **英文释义**: A legendary fire-breathing reptile with wings, often symbolizing power or danger; also used metaphorically to describe a fierce, intimidating, or authoritarian person.
- **词根词缀**: drag(拉)+on(名词)→拉长的东西→龙
- **例句**:
- The ancient manuscript depicted a dragon guarding the treasure deep within the mountain.
- Her history teacher, though brilliant, was known as a dragon in the classroom—no one dared submit an incomplete assignment.
- **考频**: 2
## drone

- **音标**: drәun
- **释义**: n. 雄蜂, 懒惰者, 嗡嗡的声音, 无人驾驶飞机(或船)\nvi. 嗡嗡作声, 混日子\nvt. 低沉地说
- **英文释义**: a male bee that does not work and exists only to mate with the queen; also: an unmanned aircraft, a monotonous buzzing sound, or a lazy or unproductive person
- **词根词缀**: drone→无人机
- **例句**:
- The military deployed drones for surveillance over the conflict zone.
- His monotonous lecture droned on for over an hour, causing many students to lose focus.
- **考频**: 8
## eagle

- **音标**: 'i:gl
- **释义**: n. 鹰, 鹰状标饰
- **英文释义**: A large, powerful bird of prey with a heavy beak, keen eyesight, and broad wings; often symbolizing strength, freedom, or authority.
- **词根词缀**: eagle→鹰
- **例句**:
- The bald eagle is the national emblem of the United States.
- Her sharp analytical skills earned her the nickname 'eagle' among colleagues—nothing escaped her notice.
- **考频**: 13
