# 会议、集会、大会

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 政治、商业或社会集会相关词汇，convene 为动词“召集”。

**5 词**

---

## conference

- **音标**: 'kɒnfәrәns
- **释义**: n. 会议\n[经] 会议, 讨论会, 协商会
- **英文释义**: A formal meeting where people gather to discuss a particular topic, often involving presentations, discussions, and networking.
- **词根词缀**: con(一起)+fer(带来)+ence(名词)→带来一起→会议
- **例句**:
- The international climate conference brought together scientists, policymakers, and activists from over 190 countries.
- She presented her latest research findings at the annual linguistics conference held in Cambridge.
- **考频**: 120
## congress

- **音标**: 'kɒŋgres
- **释义**: n. 国会, 会议, 讨论会\n[医] 会议, 会合
- **英文释义**: a formal meeting, especially of representatives or delegates; in the US, the national legislative body consisting of the Senate and the House of Representatives
- **词根词缀**: con(共同)+gress(走)→走到一起→会议
- **例句**:
- The bill was passed by Congress after months of intense debate.
- Scientists from over 30 countries attended the international medical congress to present their latest research findings.
- **考频**: 67
## convention

- **音标**: kәn'venʃәn
- **释义**: n. 大会, 协定, 惯例, 约定\n[计] 约定
- **英文释义**: A formal meeting of people with a shared interest; an agreement or established practice; a widely accepted custom or rule.
- **词根词缀**: con(一起)+vent(来)+ion(名词)→来到一起→会议
- **例句**:
- The International Climate Convention brought together delegates from over 190 countries to negotiate binding emission targets.
- In programming, it is a common convention to use camelCase for variable names in Java.
- **考频**: 49
## council

- **音标**: 'kaunsәl
- **释义**: n. 会议, 委员会\n[经] 委员会
- **英文释义**: a group of people elected or appointed to make decisions, give advice, or manage affairs
- **词根词缀**: council(会议)→委员会
- **例句**:
- The city council approved a new plan to reduce traffic congestion.
- She was appointed to the national education council to help revise the curriculum standards.
- **考频**: 75
## convene

- **音标**: kәn'vi:n
- **释义**: vt. 集合, 召集, 召唤\nvi. 聚集, 集合
- **英文释义**: to formally gather people for a meeting or official purpose
- **词根词缀**: con(一起)+ven(来)+e→来到一起→召集
- **例句**:
- The United Nations Security Council will convene an emergency session to address the crisis.
- The faculty committee is scheduled to convene next Monday to review the new curriculum proposal.
- **考频**: 6
