# 主题：文学/艺术/戏剧/娱乐

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 艺术娱乐词汇：cast(演员阵容), chorus(合唱队), comedy(喜剧), comic(漫画/滑稽的)。

**5 词**

---

## cartoon

- **音标**: kɑ:'tu:n
- **释义**: n. 卡通画, 漫画\nv. 画漫画
- **英文释义**: a humorous or satirical drawing, often published in newspapers or magazines; or a moving picture with animated characters
- **词根词缀**: cart(纸板)+oon(小)→小纸板画→卡通
- **例句**:
- The editorial cartoon sharply criticized the government's new policy on climate change.
- She was commissioned to cartoon the presidential candidates for the magazine's election special issue.
- **考频**: 17
## cast

- **音标**: kɑ:st. kæst
- **释义**: n. 演员阵容, 投掷, 铸件, 预测, 特性\nvt. 投, 掷, 抛, 脱落, 铸, 使弯曲, 计算\nvi. 投, 计算, 浇铸成型
- **英文释义**: to throw or propel something, especially with force; also refers to a group of actors in a production
- **词根词缀**: cast(投掷)→投掷
- **例句**:
- The director carefully selected the cast for the award-winning film.
- She cast a quick glance over her shoulder before sprinting down the alley.
- **考频**: 32
## chorus

- **音标**: 'kɒ:rәs
- **释义**: n. 合唱队, 齐声\nv. 齐声说道, 合唱
- **英文释义**: A group of singers performing together, or a repeated section in a song; to sing or speak in unison.
- **词根词缀**: chor(合唱)+us(名词后缀)→合唱团
- **例句**:
- The university chorus performed Beethoven's 'Ode to Joy' at the graduation ceremony.
- When the professor announced the extension of the deadline, the entire class chorused 'Thank you!'
- **考频**: 4
## comedy

- **音标**: 'kɒmidi
- **释义**: n. 喜剧, 有趣的事情
- **英文释义**: A dramatic work intended to be humorous and typically ending happily.
- **词根词缀**: com(宴会)+edy(诗歌)→宴会的诗歌→喜剧
- **例句**:
- Shakespeare's 'A Midsummer Night's Dream' is a classic example of Elizabethan comedy.
- The film blends satire and romance, challenging the conventional boundaries of comedy.
- **考频**: 13
## comic

- **音标**: 'kɒmik
- **释义**: n. 连环漫画, 喜剧演员, 滑稽的人\na. 滑稽的, 有趣的, 喜剧的
- **英文释义**: n. a professional entertainer who tells jokes and performs humorous routines; also, a magazine or book containing comic strips. adj. causing laughter; humorous or absurd.
- **词根词缀**: com(一起)+ic(形容词后缀)→与喜剧相关的→喜剧的
- **例句**:
- The stand-up comic delivered a sharp, politically astute routine that left the audience both laughing and reflecting.
- Her dry wit and impeccable timing made her one of the most acclaimed comics in contemporary British theatre.
- **考频**: 10
