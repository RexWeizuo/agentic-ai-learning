# 争论、竞争

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 涉及辩论、争议或竞争的场景，contend 可指“主张”或“竞争”。

**4 词**

---

## controversial

- **音标**: .kɒntrә'vә:ʃәl
- **释义**: a. 争论的, 论争的, 被议论的
- **英文释义**: Causing disagreement or debate among people
- **词根词缀**: contro(相反)+vers(转)+ial(形容词)→转向相反的→有争议的
- **例句**:
- The government's controversial decision to cut education funding sparked nationwide protests.
- Her latest theory on climate change remains highly controversial within the scientific community.
- **考频**: 38
## controversy

- **音标**: 'kɒntrәvә:si
- **释义**: n. 论争, 辩论, 论战, 争论\n[法] 论战, 争论, 争吵
- **英文释义**: a public disagreement or debate, often involving strong opposing opinions
- **词根词缀**: contro(相反)+vers(转)+y(名词)→转向相反→争论
- **例句**:
- The new education policy sparked widespread controversy among parents and educators.
- Her controversial remarks about climate change ignited a heated controversy in the scientific community.
- **考频**: 51
## contend

- **音标**: kәn'tend
- **释义**: vi. 奋斗, 斗争, 竞争\nvt. 为...斗争
- **英文释义**: to struggle or compete for something; to assert or maintain a position, especially in argument or debate
- **词根词缀**: con(一起)+tend(伸展)→一起伸展→竞争
- **例句**:
- The two candidates contended fiercely for the presidential nomination.
- Scholars have long contended that economic inequality is a root cause of political instability.
- **考频**: 25
## contest

- **音标**: 'kɒntest
- **释义**: n. 竞赛, 争论\nvt. 竞争, 争取, 争辩\nvi. 竞争
- **英文释义**: A competition or formal dispute; to challenge or compete for something.
- **词根词缀**: con(一起)+test(证明)→一起证明→竞赛
- **例句**:
- The national debate contest attracted over two hundred participants from top universities.
- She decided to contest the election results in court, citing irregularities in vote counting.
- **考频**: 61
