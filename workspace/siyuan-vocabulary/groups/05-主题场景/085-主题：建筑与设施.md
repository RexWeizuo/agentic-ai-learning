# 主题：建筑与设施

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 建筑、地理与设施词汇。pavement(人行道), pillar(柱子), plateau(高原), panel(嵌板/仪表板)。

**6 词**

---

## pavement

- **音标**: 'peivmәnt
- **释义**: n. 路面, 人行道, 铺面路, 铺路材料
- **英文释义**: a hard surface covering a road or path, especially the part for pedestrians alongside a street
- **词根词缀**: pave(铺)+ment(名词)→铺好的地面→人行道
- **例句**:
- She tripped on a loose slab of pavement while rushing to her graduate seminar.
- Urban planners are advocating for permeable pavement to reduce stormwater runoff in campus infrastructure projects.
- **考频**: 8
## pillar

- **音标**: 'pilә
- **释义**: n. 柱子, 栋梁, 台柱, 重要的支持者\n[医] 柱, 脚
- **英文释义**: A vertical support structure, often cylindrical; a person or thing that provides essential support or stability.
- **词根词缀**: pillar(柱子)→柱子
- **例句**:
- The ancient temple was supported by massive marble pillars.
- She is a pillar of the research team, consistently delivering groundbreaking insights.
- **考频**: 4
## playground

- **音标**: 'pleigraund
- **释义**: n. 运动场, 操场, 度假胜地, 活动场所
- **英文释义**: An outdoor area designed for children's recreation and physical activity, typically equipped with play structures; also used metaphorically for any domain where ideas, strategies, or influence are tested and exercised.
- **词根词缀**: play(词根:玩)+ground(词根:地面)→操场
- **例句**:
- The city council approved funding to renovate the aging playground with inclusive, accessible equipment.
- Silicon Valley has long served as a global playground for technological innovation and venture capital.
- **考频**: 28
## plateau

- **音标**: plæ'tәu
- **释义**: n. 高地, 高原, (上升后的)稳定水平\n[医] 高原, 平顶, 坪
- **英文释义**: a flat, elevated area of land; or a period of little or no change in a process or measurement after a period of activity or growth
- **词根词缀**: plat(词根:平坦)+eau(名词后缀)→高原
- **例句**:
- The Tibetan Plateau is the highest and largest plateau in the world.
- After months of rapid improvement, her language proficiency reached a plateau, requiring new strategies to progress further.
- **考频**: 3
## plantation

- **音标**: plæn'teiʃәn
- **释义**: n. 种植园, 大农场, 植树造林, 新开垦地\n[医] 栽植(如牙或其他组织的植入、再植和移植)
- **英文释义**: A large agricultural estate, typically in a tropical or subtropical region, devoted to the cultivation of a single crop such as cotton, sugar, or coffee; also used for reforestation or medical tissue transplantation.
- **词根词缀**: plant(词根:种植)+ation(名词后缀)→种植园
- **例句**:
- The novel vividly depicts life on a 19th-century Southern cotton plantation.
- Recent advances in regenerative medicine have improved the success rate of dental pulp plantation in patients with severe tooth trauma.
- **考频**: 3
## panel

- **音标**: 'pænl
- **释义**: n. 嵌板, 仪表板, 专题讨论小组, 全体陪审员\nvt. 嵌镶板
- **英文释义**: A flat, rectangular section of material used as part of a surface (e.g., wall, door, or dashboard), or a group of people assembled for discussion, judgment, or evaluation.
- **词根词缀**: pan(面板)+el(小)→小面板→面板；讨论小组
- **例句**:
- The control panel in the cockpit displays critical flight data for the pilot.
- The conference featured a panel of leading economists who debated monetary policy implications.
- **考频**: 55
