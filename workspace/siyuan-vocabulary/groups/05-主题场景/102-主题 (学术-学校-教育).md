# 主题 (学术/学校/教育)

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 校园与学术场景。scholarship(奖学金/学问), semester(学期), sophomore(大二学生)。

**5 词**

---

## scholar

- **音标**: 'skɒlә
- **释义**: n. 学者, 奖学金获得者, 有文化者, 学习者
- **英文释义**: a person who has extensive knowledge in a particular academic field or who studies seriously
- **词根词缀**: schol(学习)+ar(人)→学者
- **例句**:
- As a renowned scholar in medieval history, she has published numerous peer-reviewed articles.
- The university offers full scholarships to outstanding scholars who demonstrate exceptional academic promise.
- **考频**: 84
## scholarship

- **音标**: 'skɒlәʃip
- **释义**: n. 学问, 学术成就, 奖学金
- **英文释义**: 1. Academic knowledge or achievement; 2. Financial aid awarded to a student based on academic merit or other criteria.
- **词根词缀**: scholar(学者)+ship(状态)→奖学金
- **例句**:
- Her scholarship in medieval literature earned her a prestigious research fellowship.
- He was awarded a full scholarship to Harvard University based on his outstanding academic record and leadership experience.
- **考频**: 48
## science

- **音标**: 'saiәns
- **释义**: n. 科学, 学科, 学问, 自然科学\n[医] 科学
- **英文释义**: the systematic study of the structure and behaviour of the physical and natural world through observation and experiment
- **词根词缀**: sci(知道)+ence(名词)→科学
- **例句**:
- Advances in computer science have revolutionized data analysis in biomedical research.
- While philosophy explores fundamental questions, science relies on empirical evidence and testable hypotheses.
- **考频**: 773
## semester

- **音标**: si'mestә
- **释义**: n. 学期
- **英文释义**: One of the two main divisions of an academic year, typically lasting about 15–18 weeks.
- **词根词缀**: se(六)+mester(月)→六个月→学期
- **例句**:
- She plans to study abroad during the spring semester.
- The final exam accounts for 40% of the grade for this semester.
- **考频**: 39
## sophomore

- **音标**: 'sɒfәmɒ:
- **释义**: n. (大学)二年级生\na. 二年级学生的, 二年级的
- **英文释义**: a second-year student at a college or university
- **词根词缀**: soph(智慧)+omore(愚蠢)→大二学生
- **例句**:
- As a sophomore, she declared her major in computer science and began taking advanced programming courses.
- The university offers specialized research opportunities for sophomores who demonstrate strong academic performance.
