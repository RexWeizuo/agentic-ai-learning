> **类型**: 主题场景

> **学习提示**: 描述心理状态与情绪。indignant (愤慨的) 强调因不公而愤怒；hysterical (歇斯底里的)。

## hesitate

- **音标**: 'heziteit
- **释义**: vi. 犹豫, 迟疑, 踌躇, 支吾, 停顿
- **英文释义**: to pause before acting or speaking, often due to doubt, fear, or uncertainty
- **词根词缀**: hesit(犹豫)+ate(动词)→犹豫
- **例句**:
- She didn't hesitate for a moment before volunteering for the dangerous mission.
- He hesitated when asked about his qualifications, suggesting he lacked confidence in his experience.
- **考频**: 31
