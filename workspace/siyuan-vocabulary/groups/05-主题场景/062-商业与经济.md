## investment

- **音标**: in'vestmәnt
- **释义**: n. 投资\n[医] 包埋料, 围模料, 包埋法, 围模法
- **英文释义**: The act of putting money, time, or effort into something to gain a future benefit or profit
- **词根词缀**: in(进入)+vest(衣服)+ment(名词)→投资
- **例句**:
- Long-term investment in education yields significant returns in career advancement.
- The company announced a $500 million investment in renewable energy infrastructure to meet its sustainability goals.
- **考频**: 189
## management

- **音标**: 'mænidʒmәnt
- **释义**: n. 经营, 支配, 管理\n[化] 管理处
- **英文释义**: the process of dealing with or controlling things or people
- **词根词缀**: man(手)+age(管理)+ment(名词)→管理
- **例句**:
- Effective management of resources is critical to the success of any large-scale project.
- The company's poor financial performance was attributed to weak strategic management and lack of innovation.
- **考频**: 259
## manager

- **音标**: 'mænidʒә
- **释义**: n. 经理, 管理员, 管理器\n[计] 管理器
- **英文释义**: A person responsible for controlling and organizing the work of others, especially in a business or organization.
- **词根词缀**: man(手)+age(管理)+er(人)→经理
- **例句**:
- The project manager coordinated cross-departmental efforts to meet the tight deadline.
- As the IT infrastructure manager, she oversees system security, upgrades, and technical support.
- **考频**: 261
