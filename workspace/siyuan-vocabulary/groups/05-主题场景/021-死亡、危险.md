# 死亡、危险

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: deadly 指“致命的”，dangerous 指“危险的”，deadline 原指“死线”。

**4 词**

---

## dead

- **音标**: ded
- **释义**: a. 死的, 不活泼的, 麻木的, 熄灭的\nn. 死者\nadv. 完全地, 直接地
- **英文释义**: no longer alive; lacking activity, sensation, or energy; completely or exactly
- **词根词缀**: dead(死)→死的
- **例句**:
- The patient was declared dead on arrival at the hospital.
- After hours of intense negotiation, the talks reached a dead end.
- **考频**: 73
## deadline

- **音标**: 'dedlain
- **释义**: n. 最后期限, 截止期限\n[经] 截止日期
- **英文释义**: The latest time or date by which something must be completed or submitted.
- **词根词缀**: dead(死)+line(线)→死线→截止日期
- **例句**:
- The research paper must be submitted by the deadline, or it will not be considered for publication.
- Missing the project deadline could result in financial penalties and damage to the company's reputation.
- **考频**: 39
## deadly

- **音标**: 'dedli
- **释义**: a. 致命的, 致死的\nadv. 非常, 如死一般地
- **英文释义**: causing or able to cause death; extremely
- **词根词缀**: dead(死)+ly(形容词)→致命的
- **例句**:
- The virus proved to be deadly to immunocompromised patients.
- Her gaze was deadly serious, leaving no room for misunderstanding.
- **考频**: 24
## dangerous

- **音标**: 'deindʒәrәs
- **释义**: a. 危险的\n[法] 危险的, 危害的
- **英文释义**: likely to cause harm, injury, or death
- **词根词缀**: danger(危险)+ous(形容词)→危险的
- **例句**:
- Driving at high speed in heavy rain is extremely dangerous.
- The chemical is highly dangerous if inhaled or ingested.
- **考频**: 133
