# 质量与数量评估

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: qual- 关乎质量与资格，quant- 关乎数量与定量，考研阅读中常对比出现。

**5 词**

---

## qualification

- **音标**: .kwɒlifi'keiʃәn
- **释义**: n. 资格, 条件, 限制\n[计] 限定
- **英文释义**: a skill, experience, or official document that shows a person is suitable to do a particular job or activity
- **词根词缀**: qual(性质)+ific(使)+ation(名词)→使具有性质→资格
- **例句**:
··
- Her teaching qualification enabled her to apply for positions at several prestigious universities.
- The job posting listed advanced programming skills as a mandatory qualification, not just a preference.
- **考频**: 48
## qualify

- **音标**: 'kwɒlifai
- **释义**: vi. 取得资格, 有资格\nvt. 使有资格, 使合格, 限定, 限制, 准予
- **英文释义**: to meet the necessary conditions or standards for a particular role, activity, or status; to limit or modify the meaning of a statement
- **词根词缀**: qual(性质)+ify(使)→使具有性质→合格
- **例句**:
- All candidates must pass the written exam to qualify for the interview.
- Her statement was carefully worded to qualify any potential legal liability.
- **考频**: 20
## qualitative

- **音标**: 'kwɒlitәtiv
- **释义**: a. 性质的, 质的, 定性的
- **英文释义**: Relating to the quality or nature of something rather than its quantity or measurement.
- **词根词缀**: qualitat+-ive(有…倾向的/…的/…人)
- **例句**:
- The study adopted a qualitative approach, using in-depth interviews to explore participants' experiences.
- Unlike quantitative data, qualitative findings are often rich in contextual detail but not statistically generalizable.
## quantify

- **音标**: 'kwɒntifai
- **释义**: vt. 定量, 用数量表示, 测量
- **英文释义**: to express or measure something as a number or quantity
- **词根词缀**: quant(数量)+ify(使)→使有数量→量化
- **例句**:
- It is difficult to quantify the long-term environmental impact of industrial pollution.
- Researchers attempted to quantify students' critical thinking skills through standardized assessments.
- **考频**: 13
## quantitative

- **音标**: 'kwɒntitәtiv
- **释义**: a. 数量的, 定量的\n[医] 定量的, 数量的
- **英文释义**: relating to or measured by the quantity rather than the quality of something
- **词根词缀**: quant(数量)+it(连接)+ative(形容词)→数量的→定量的
- **例句**:
- The study employed both qualitative and quantitative methods to analyze patient outcomes.
- A quantitative analysis of the data revealed a statistically significant correlation between sleep duration and cognitive performance.
- **考频**: 10
