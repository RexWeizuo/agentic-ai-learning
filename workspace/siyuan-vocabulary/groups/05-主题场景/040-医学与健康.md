## diabetes

- **音标**: daiә'bi:ti:z
- **释义**: n. 糖尿病, 多尿症\n[医] 糖尿病; 多尿症
- **英文释义**: A chronic metabolic disorder characterized by high blood glucose levels due to insulin deficiency or resistance.
- **词根词缀**: dia(通过)+bet(走)+es(名词)→通过身体流失→糖尿病
- **例句**:
- Type 2 diabetes is strongly associated with obesity and sedentary lifestyle.
- Early diagnosis and management of diabetes can significantly reduce the risk of complications such as neuropathy and retinopathy.
- **考频**: 19
## diagnose

- **音标**: 'daiәgnәuz
- **释义**: v. 诊断
- **英文释义**: to identify a disease or problem by examining symptoms and test results
- **词根词缀**: dia(通过)+gnos(知道)+e→通过知道→诊断
- **例句**:
- The doctor diagnosed her with type 2 diabetes after reviewing the blood glucose tests.
- Early diagnosis is crucial, as many neurological disorders are difficult to diagnose accurately in their initial stages.
- **考频**: 24

## dose

- **音标**: dәus
- **释义**: n. 剂量, 服用量\nvt. 给药, (用药)医治\nvi. 服药
- **英文释义**: a measured amount of a drug or other substance taken at one time
- **词根词缀**: dose(给予)→给予的量→剂量
- **例句**:
- The doctor prescribed a low dose of the antidepressant to minimize side effects.
- She accidentally took a double dose of her medication and had to seek medical advice.
- **考频**: 28

## dizzy

- **音标**: 'dizi
- **释义**: a. 晕眩的, 眼花缭乱的\nvt. 使晕眩
- **英文释义**: feeling unsteady, confused, or as if everything is spinning; causing such a sensation
- **词根词缀**: diz(旋转)+zy(形容词)→旋转的→头晕的
