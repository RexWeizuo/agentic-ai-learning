# 释放、减轻与休闲

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: release 为释放/发行，relief/relieve 为减轻(痛苦/负担)，recreation 为娱乐/消遣。

**4 词**

---

## release

- **音标**: ri'li:s
- **释义**: n. 释放, 发泄, 豁免, 发行, 释放证书\nvt. 释放, 解除, 放松, 豁免, 免除, 发布, 放弃, 让与\nn. 发布\n[计] 版本, 发布
- **英文释义**: to set free from confinement, obligation, or restraint; to make available to the public (e.g., a product, document, or information); a version of software or a published work
- **词根词缀**: re(再次)+lease(放松)→再次放松→释放
- **例句**:
- The government announced the release of the hostages after three days of negotiations.
- The company plans to release its latest software update next month, marking the official launch of version 3.0.
- **考频**: 136
## recreation

- **音标**: .rekri'eiʃәn
- **释义**: n. 娱乐, 消遣, 休息, 再创造\n[医] 娱乐, 休养
- **英文释义**: activity done for enjoyment, relaxation, or refreshment, especially during leisure time
- **词根词缀**: re(再)+creat(创造)+ion(名词)→再创造→娱乐
- **例句**:
- Regular physical recreation is essential for maintaining both mental and physical health.
- The government has invested in public parks to promote accessible outdoor recreation for all citizens.
- **考频**: 29
## relief

- **音标**: ri'li:f
- **释义**: n. 减轻, 解除, 救济, 安慰, 调剂, 浮雕, 换班, (地势的)起伏\n[医] 缓减, 减轻, 浮雕(绘画中)
- **英文释义**: the feeling of reassurance and relaxation following release from anxiety, distress, or pain; also refers to assistance provided to those in need or a method of alleviating a problem
- **词根词缀**: re(加强)+lief(轻)→减轻→救济
- **例句**:
- The relief was palpable when the exam results were announced and she had passed with distinction.
- Humanitarian aid organizations delivered food and medical relief to the earthquake-stricken region.
- **考频**: 55
## relieve

- **音标**: ri'li:v
- **释义**: vt. 减轻, 救济, 解除, 使免除, 换...的班, 使得到调剂, 使不单调, 衬托, 使显著\nvi. 救济, 当替补投手, 呈鲜明突出
- **英文释义**: to reduce or remove something unpleasant, such as pain, anxiety, or a duty; to take over someone's responsibilities temporarily
- **词根词缀**: re(加强)+lieve(轻)→减轻→缓解
- **例句**:
- The new policy aims to relieve the financial burden on low-income families.
- She was relieved of her duties after the audit revealed serious compliance issues.
- **考频**: 54
