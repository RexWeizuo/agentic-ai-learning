# 主题：出版与文具

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 出版物与文具词汇。pamphlet(小册子), paperback(平装本), periodical(期刊)。

**5 词**

---

## pamphlet

- **音标**: 'pæmflit
- **释义**: n. 小册子, 活页文选
- **英文释义**: A short, unbound booklet containing information or arguments on a particular subject.
- **词根词缀**: pam(爱)+phlet(小册子)→小册子
- **例句**:
- The environmental NGO distributed a pamphlet outlining the impacts of plastic waste on marine ecosystems.
- Before the seminar, attendees received a pamphlet summarizing key research findings and policy recommendations.
- **考频**: 9
## paperback

- **音标**: 'peipәbæk
- **释义**: n. 平装本, 纸面本
- **英文释义**: A book with a flexible paper cover, typically less expensive and less durable than a hardcover.
- **词根词缀**: paper(纸)+back(背)→纸背装订的书→平装书
- **例句**:
- She prefers reading novels in paperback for their portability and affordability.
- The publisher released a revised edition of the textbook in both hardcover and paperback formats.
- **考频**: 8
## periodical

- **音标**: .piәri'ɒdikl
- **释义**: n. 期刊\na. 定期出版的, (有关)期刊的, 间歇(性的), 周期的, 定期的
- **英文释义**: A publication issued at regular intervals, such as weekly, monthly, or quarterly.
- **词根词缀**: peri(周围)+od(路)+ical(形容词)→围绕路→周期性的
- **例句**:
- Scholars often publish their latest research findings in academic periodicals.
- The library subscribes to over two hundred scientific periodicals to support graduate-level research.
- **考频**: 9
## page

- **音标**: peidʒ
- **释义**: n. 页, 记录, 事件, 专栏, 男侍\nvt. 标明...的页数, 翻...的书页, 分页排版, 呼叫, 侍候\nvi. 翻书页, 侍侯\n[计] 页; 页面
- **英文释义**: A single sheet of paper in a book, magazine, or document; also used to refer to a webpage or a unit of information display.
- **词根词缀**: page(词根：固定)→一页纸→页
- **例句**:
- The conclusion is discussed on page 47 of the research paper.
- She paused to page through the technical manual before attempting the repair.
- **考频**: 129
## pencil

- **音标**: 'pensl
- **释义**: n. 铅笔, 色笔, 眉笔, 画笔, 光线束\nvt. 用铅笔写或涂, 草拟
- **英文释义**: A thin cylindrical writing or drawing instrument with a graphite or colored core enclosed in wood or another material.
- **词根词缀**: penc(尾巴)+il(小)→小尾巴→铅笔
- **例句**:
- She sketched the landscape quickly with a pencil, capturing the light and shadow before the scene changed.
- The committee asked him to pencil in a tentative date for the policy review meeting.
- **考频**: 24
