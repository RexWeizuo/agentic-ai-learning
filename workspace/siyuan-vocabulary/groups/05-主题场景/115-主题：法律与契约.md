# 主题：法律与契约

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: treaty (条约)，verdict (裁决)，veto (否决权)，warrant (授权/搜查令)，warranty (担保/保修)。

**5 词**

---

## treaty

- **音标**: 'tri:ti
- **释义**: n. 条约, 谈判\n[经] 协议, 协定, 协商
- **英文释义**: A formal written agreement between two or more states or parties, especially one signed by governments.
- **词根词缀**: treat(处理)+y(名词)→处理事务的文件→条约
- **例句**:
- The Paris Agreement is a landmark international treaty aimed at combating climate change.
- Negotiations for a new trade treaty between the two countries are expected to conclude next month.
- **考频**: 15
## verdict

- **音标**: 'vә:dikt
- **释义**: n. 裁决, 判决, 判断性意见, 定论, 结论\n[法] 定论, 判断, 意见
- **英文释义**: the formal decision made by a jury or judge in a court case; also used more broadly to mean a conclusive judgment or opinion
- **词根词缀**: ver(真实)+dict(说)→说出真相→裁决
- **例句**:
- The jury returned a unanimous verdict of guilty after three days of deliberation.
- After reviewing all the evidence, the committee issued its verdict that the policy was both ineffective and unjust.
- **考频**: 9
## veto

- **音标**: 'vi:tәu
- **释义**: n. 否决权\nvt. 否决, 禁止
- **英文释义**: the power to reject a proposal or decision, especially by a person or body with official authority
- **词根词缀**: veto(禁止)→否决
- **例句**:
- The president used his constitutional veto to block the controversial bill.
- The Security Council permanent members hold an absolute veto over substantive resolutions.
- **考频**: 6
## warrant

- **音标**: 'wɒ:rәnt
- **释义**: n. 授权, 正当理由, 根据, 证明, 批准, 凭证, 令状, 委任状\nvt. 授权给, 保证, 担保, 批准, 使有正当理由
- **英文释义**: A formal authorization or justification; a guarantee or assurance that something is true or will happen.
- **词根词缀**: warr(保证)+ant(名词)→保证→授权
- **例句**:
- The court issued a search warrant based on probable cause.
- His extensive research warrants serious consideration of the new theory.
- **考频**: 11
## warranty

- **音标**: 'wɒ:rәnti
- **释义**: n. 正当理由, 授权, 担保, 保证, 根据\n[经] 保单, 担保书, 保证书
- **英文释义**: A written guarantee promising to repair or replace a product if it fails to meet specified standards within a certain period.
- **词根词缀**: warr(保证)+ant(名词)+y→保证→保修
- **例句**:
- The manufacturer offers a two-year warranty on all electronic devices.
- Before signing the contract, she carefully reviewed the warranty clause to ensure coverage for structural defects.
- **考频**: 3
