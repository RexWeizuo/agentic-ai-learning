# 新闻与出版

## journal

- **音标**: 'dʒә:nәl
- **释义**: n. 日记, 杂志, 日报\n[化] 轴颈
- **英文释义**: A daily record of personal experiences and thoughts, or a periodical publication containing scholarly articles or news.
- **词根词缀**: journ(日)+al→日报→日记，期刊
- **例句**:
- She has kept a journal for over ten years to reflect on her academic growth.
- The research findings were published in a peer-reviewed scientific journal.
- **考频**: 155
## journalist

- **音标**: 'dʒә:nәlist
- **释义**: n. 新闻记者, 从事新闻杂志业的人
- **英文释义**: A person who collects, writes, and distributes news or other current information, typically for newspapers, magazines, television, or online media.
- **词根词缀**: journal(期刊)+ist(人)→记者
- **例句**:
- The investigative journalist uncovered corruption within the local government.
- As a seasoned journalist, she has reported from over a dozen conflict zones around the world.
- **考频**: 61
## magazine

- **音标**: .mægә'zi:n
- **释义**: n. 杂志, 仓库, 弹盒, 胶卷盒\n[计] 卡片箱, 介质装卸程序
- **英文释义**: A periodical publication containing articles, stories, and illustrations, typically issued weekly or monthly.
- **词根词缀**: magaz(仓库)+ine→杂志
- **例句**:
- She regularly contributes essays to a prestigious literary magazine.
- The engineering journal is more technical than general-interest magazines like National Geographic.
- **考频**: 160
## media

- **音标**: 'mi:diә
- **释义**: n. 媒体\n[计] 媒质
- **英文释义**: plural of medium; means channels of communication such as television, radio, newspapers, and the internet, or materials used for storing or transmitting data
- **词根词缀**: medi(middle)+a
- **例句**:
- The media played a crucial role in shaping public opinion during the election.
- Different storage media, such as SSDs and optical discs, have distinct advantages in data access speed and durability.
## manuscript

- **音标**: 'mænjuskript
- **释义**: n. 手稿, 原稿, 底稿\na. 手写的
- **英文释义**: A written or typed document, especially the original version of a book, article, or other work before it is published
- **词根词缀**: manu(手)+script(写)→手稿
- **例句**:
- The author submitted her manuscript to three major publishers for consideration.
- Peer reviewers carefully evaluated the scientific rigor and clarity of the manuscript before recommending acceptance.
- **考频**: 6
## leaflet

- **音标**: 'li:flit
- **释义**: n. 小叶, 传单\n[医] 小叶
- **英文释义**: A small leaf or a single page of printed material, often distributed for advertising or informational purposes.
- **词根词缀**: leaf(词根:叶子)+let(小)→小叶子→传单
- **例句**:
- The botanist examined the leaflet structure of the compound leaf under the microscope.
- She handed out a colorful leaflet describing the university's graduate programs to prospective students.
- **考频**: 4
## letter

- **音标**: 'letә
- **释义**: n. 信, 字母, 证书, 字面意义, 铅字, 学问, 出租人\nvt. 写字母于, 在...上刻字母, 用字母标明\nvi. 写印刷体字\n[计] 字母
- **英文释义**: A written or printed communication sent to a person; a symbol representing a speech sound in an alphabet.
- **词根词缀**: letter(字母)→字母
- **例句**:
- She received a heartfelt letter from her former professor urging her to pursue graduate studies.
- The word 'psychology' begins with the silent letter 'p', a common point of confusion for English learners.
- **考频**: 1422
## issue

- **音标**: 'isju
- **释义**: n. 发行, 问题, 后果, 流出, 出口, 争端\nvi. 发行, 流出, 造成...结果, 传下\nvt. 使流出, 放出, 发行, 发布, 发给
- **英文释义**: A matter or problem that is open to discussion or debate; also, the act of officially releasing or distributing something (e.g., a publication, statement, or product).
- **词根词缀**: ex(出)+it(走)→走出去→发出，问题
- **例句**:
- The government has pledged to address the key social issues raised in the report.
- The company will issue its quarterly financial statements next week.
- **考频**: 489
