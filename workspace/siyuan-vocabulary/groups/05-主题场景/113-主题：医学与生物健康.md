## tumor

- **音标**: 'tju:mә
- **释义**: n. 肿大, 肿块, 瘤, 赘生物, 肿瘤\n[医] 瘤; 肿胀, 肿块
- **英文释义**: An abnormal mass of tissue that results from excessive cell division, which may be benign or malignant.
- **词根词缀**: tum(肿胀)+or(名词)→肿胀→肿瘤
- **例句**:
- The MRI revealed a small benign tumor in the patient's frontal lobe.
- Early detection of malignant tumors significantly improves the prognosis in oncology patients.
- **考频**: 5
## vaccine

- **音标**: 'væksi:n
- **释义**: n. 牛痘苗, 疫苗\na. 疫苗的, 牛痘的
- **英文释义**: A substance used to stimulate the production of antibodies and provide immunity against one or several diseases, typically administered by injection.
- **词根词缀**: vacc(牛)+ine(名词)→牛痘→疫苗
- **例句**:
- The development of an effective vaccine against the novel coronavirus was a major scientific breakthrough.
- Public health officials emphasized that widespread vaccination is essential to achieving herd immunity.
- **考频**: 18
## virus

- **音标**: 'vaiәrәs
- **释义**: n. 病毒, 滤过性病毒, 毒害\n[化] 病毒
- **英文释义**: A microscopic infectious agent that replicates only inside the living cells of an organism.
- **词根词缀**: vir(毒)+us(名词)→毒物→病毒
- **例句**:
- The novel coronavirus is a highly contagious virus that caused a global pandemic.
- Computer scientists developed antivirus software to detect and remove malicious virus programs.
- **考频**: 36
## vitamin

- **音标**: 'vaitәmin
- **释义**: n. 维生素\n[化] 维生素
- **英文释义**: An organic compound essential in small amounts for normal physiological function and health, typically obtained from food.
- **词根词缀**: vit(生命)+amin(胺)→生命胺→维生素
- **例句**:
- A balanced diet provides all the necessary vitamins to support immune function and energy metabolism.
- Deficiency in vitamin D, often due to insufficient sunlight exposure, can lead to weakened bones and increased risk of osteoporosis.
- **考频**: 55
## vein

- **音标**: vein
- **释义**: n. 血管, 静脉, 纹理, 气质, 情绪\nvt. 使有脉络, 像脉络般分布于

- **词根词缀**: vein

- **释义**: n. 素食者, 食草动物\na. 素食的
- **英文释义**: a person who does not eat meat, and sometimes other animal products
- **词根词缀**: veget(植物)+arian(人)→吃植物的人→素食者
- **例句**:

## vegetation

- **音标**: .vedʒi'teiʃәn
- **释义**: n. 植物\n[医] 贽生物, 贽疣, 增殖体; 生长, 增殖
- **英文释义**: plants collectively, especially the plants native to a particular region or habitat
- **词根词缀**: veget(植物)+ation(名词后缀)→植物→植被
