# f- 填充/过滤

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 喂养、填充与过滤相关词汇

**5 词**

---

## feed

- **音标**: fi:d
- **释义**: n. 饲料, 一餐, 饲养\nvt. 喂, 饲养, 放牧, 靠...为生\nvi. 吃东西, 用餐, 流入\n[计] 送纸
- **英文释义**: to give food to a person or animal; food provided for animals or livestock; a single meal; (computing) the process of guiding paper into a printer or machine
- **词根词缀**: feed(食物)→喂养
- **例句**:
- The farmer feeds the cattle twice a day to ensure optimal growth.
- This high-protein feed significantly improves dairy cows' milk yield.
- **考频**: 103
## feedback

- **音标**: 'fi:dbæk
- **释义**: n. 反馈, 反应\n[计] 反馈
- **英文释义**: Information about how good or useful something is, especially given to help improve it
- **词根词缀**: feed(喂养)+back(回)→反馈
- **例句**:
- The professor provided detailed feedback on my thesis proposal, which helped me refine my research questions.
- User feedback collected during the beta testing phase led to significant improvements in the software's interface.
- **考频**: 50
## fill

- **音标**: fil
- **释义**: vt. 装满, 填充, 弥漫, 供给, 满足, 供应\nvi. 充满, 变得沉重\nn. 满足, 装满, 充分, 填方\nvt. 填充\nvi. 填充\n[计] 填充
- **英文释义**: to make or become full; to occupy space or time completely; to satisfy a need or requirement
- **词根词缀**: fill(充满)→充满
- **例句**:
- The auditorium was filled with students eager to hear the keynote speech.
- The sudden silence filled the room, making everyone uneasy.
- **考频**: 400
## film

- **音标**: film
- **释义**: n. 软片, 薄膜, 胶卷, 电影\nvt. 覆以薄膜, 拍摄\nvi. 生薄膜, 拍电影
- **英文释义**: a thin layer of material or a flexible strip of plastic coated with light-sensitive emulsion used for recording images; also refers to a motion picture
- **词根词缀**: film(薄膜)→薄膜,电影
- **例句**:
- The director decided to shoot the entire film on 35-mm celluloid to achieve a classic cinematic texture.
- A greasy film had formed on the surface of the water, indicating chemical contamination.
- **考频**: 167
## filter

- **音标**: 'filtә
- **释义**: n. 滤波器, 过滤器, 滤光器, 过滤嘴, 去尘器\nvt. 过滤, 渗透, 走漏\nvi. 滤过, 渗入\n[计] 过滤器, 筛选
- **英文释义**: A device or process that removes unwanted elements from a substance, signal, or data set; or the act of passing something through such a device or process.
- **词根词缀**: filt(毡)+er(名词)→过滤材料→过滤器
- **例句**:
- The audio engineer used a low-pass filter to remove high-frequency noise from the recording.
- Sensitive information must be filtered out before the report is published to prevent data leaks.
- **考频**: 13
