# 主题：宗教/神职/信仰

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 宗教场景词汇：cathedral(大教堂), clergy(神职人员), choir(唱诗班), catholic(天主教的/天主教徒)。

**6 词**

---

## cathedral

- **音标**: kә'θi:drәl
- **释义**: n. 大教堂
- **英文释义**: A large and important Christian church, typically the principal church of a diocese and the seat of a bishop.
- **词根词缀**: cat(向下)+hedr(座位)+al(形容词)→主教的座位→大教堂
- **例句**:
- The Gothic cathedral in Chartres is renowned for its stained-glass windows and architectural grandeur.
- Her research focused on the role of medieval cathedrals as centers of both religious worship and civic life.
- **考频**: 31
## catholic

- **音标**: 'kæθәlik
- **释义**: n. 天主教徒\na. 天主教的, 普遍的, 广泛的, 宽宏大量的
- **英文释义**: n. a member of the Roman Catholic Church; a. relating to the Roman Catholic Church; universal, inclusive, or broad in scope or interest
- **词根词缀**: cathol+-ic(…的/…学)
- **例句**:
- As a Catholic, she attends Mass every Sunday and observes Lenten traditions.
- His catholic taste in literature spans ancient Greek epics, Victorian novels, and contemporary African poetry.
## Christ

- **音标**: kraist
- **释义**: n. 基督, 救世主
- **英文释义**: The title given to Jesus of Nazareth by Christians, meaning 'the Anointed One' and signifying his role as the Messiah and savior.
- **词根词缀**: chr+-ist(…主义者/…家/从事…者)
- **例句**:
- Christ is central to Christian theology as the divine Son of God who died and was resurrected for humanity's salvation.
- Scholars debate whether the historical Jesus explicitly claimed to be Christ or whether this title was conferred by his early followers.
## Christian

- **音标**: 'kristʃәn
- **释义**: n. 基督徒, 正派人\na. 基督的, 基督教的
- **英文释义**: a person who believes in Jesus Christ and follows Christianity; also used to describe something related to Christ or Christianity
- **词根词缀**: christ+-ian(…的/…人/…学家)
- **例句**:
- As a devout Christian, she volunteers regularly at the local church.
- The painting reflects medieval Christian iconography and theological themes.
## clergy

- **音标**: 'klә:dʒi
- **释义**: n. 牧师, 僧侣, 神职人员
- **英文释义**: the group of people who are ordained to perform religious duties and lead worship, especially in Christianity
- **词根词缀**: clerg(牧师)+y(集体)→牧师团体→神职人员
- **例句**:
- The clergy played a central role in medieval European education and record-keeping.
- In many countries, members of the clergy are exempt from certain civil obligations, such as military service.
- **考频**: 2
## choir

- **音标**: 'kwaiә
- **释义**: n. 唱诗班, 唱诗班的席位\nv. 合唱
- **英文释义**: A group of people who sing together, especially in a church or as part of a musical performance.
- **词根词缀**: 来自拉丁语chorus，意为合唱团
- **例句**:
- The cathedral choir performed a stunning rendition of Handel's 'Messiah' during the Easter service.
- Students from the university choir were selected to represent the country at the International Choral Competition.
- **考频**: 2
