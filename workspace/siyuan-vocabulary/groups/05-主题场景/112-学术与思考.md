# 学术与思考

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 学术研究与思考场景。thesis (论文/论点), theme (主题), theoretical (理论的)。

**4 词**

---

## theoretical

- **音标**: θiә'retikәl
- **释义**: a. 理论的, 理论上的, 假设的, 推理的\n[计] 理论的
- **英文释义**: Based on theory rather than experience or practice; not practical or applied.
- **词根词缀**: theor(看)+etic(形容词)+al(形容词)→理论的
- **例句**:
- The proposal is still at a theoretical stage and requires empirical validation.
- While the algorithm shows promising theoretical efficiency, its real-world performance remains untested.
- **考频**: 27
## thesis

- **音标**: 'θi:sis
- **释义**: n. 论题, 论文
- **英文释义**: A statement or theory that is put forward as a premise to be maintained or proved, especially in an academic paper or dissertation.
- **词根词缀**: thes(放置)+is(名词)→放置的论点→论文
- **例句**:
- Her master's thesis examined the impact of social media on adolescent mental health.
- The central thesis of the article is that economic inequality undermines democratic institutions.
- **考频**: 31
## theme

- **音标**: θi:m
- **释义**: n. 主题, 话题, 题目
- **英文释义**: the main subject or idea that is explored in a piece of writing, art, music, or discussion
- **词根词缀**: them(放置)+e→放置的主题→主题
- **例句**:
- The novel's central theme is the conflict between individual freedom and societal expectation.
- In her thesis, she analyzes how colonialism functions as a recurring theme in postwar British literature.
- **考频**: 50
## thoughtful

- **音标**: 'θɒ:tful
- **释义**: a. 深思的, 体贴的
- **英文释义**: showing careful consideration or empathy toward others
- **词根词缀**: thought(思考)+ful(充满)→充满思考的→深思的
- **例句**:
- Her thoughtful comments during the seminar demonstrated both intellectual depth and genuine engagement with the topic.
- It was a thoughtful gesture to bring notes and tea for the exhausted professor after the all-day conference.
- **考频**: 16
