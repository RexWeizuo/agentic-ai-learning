# 主题：发声与低语

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 表示低声说话、抱怨或静音的词汇。

**4 词**

---

## moan

- **音标**: mәun
- **释义**: n. 呻吟, 悲叹\nvi. 呻吟, 抱怨, 悲叹\nvt. 呻吟着说
- **英文释义**: a low, mournful sound expressing pain, sorrow, or pleasure; to make such a sound or complain in a dreary way
- **词根词缀**: moan(呻吟)→呻吟
- **例句**:
- She let out a soft moan as the dentist began the procedure.
- The audience couldn't help but moan at the film's predictable plot and wooden dialogue.
- **考频**: 7
## murmur

- **音标**: 'mә:mә
- **释义**: n. 低语, 低声的怨言\nvi. 低语, 低声而言\nvt. 低声说
- **英文释义**: a low, indistinct sound made by speaking quietly or continuously; to speak or say something in a low, quiet voice
- **词根词缀**: murmur(拟声词根)→低语声→咕哝
- **例句**:
- The audience began to murmur disapprovingly as the speaker repeated the same point for the third time.
- She murmured an apology without meeting his eyes, her voice barely audible over the café's background noise.
- **考频**: 5
## mutter

- **音标**: 'mʌtә
- **释义**: n. 喃喃低语\nvi. 喃喃自语, 作低沉声\nvt. 出怨言, 抱怨地说
- **英文释义**: to speak quietly and indistinctly, often in complaint or dissatisfaction
- **词根词缀**: mutt(拟声)+er(反复)→咕哝
- **例句**:
- He muttered something under his breath when the professor criticized his thesis proposal.
- As the meeting dragged on, several attendees began to mutter about the lack of clear agenda and wasted time.
- **考频**: 1
## mute

- **音标**: mju:t
- **释义**: n. 哑子, 哑音字母, 弱音器\na. 哑的, 无声的, 沉默的\nvt. 减弱...的声音\nvi. 排泄
- **英文释义**: adj. unable to speak or produce sound; silent; n. a device that reduces the volume or alters the tone of a musical instrument; vt. to reduce the volume or intensity of sound
- **词根词缀**: mut(改变)+e→改变声音→沉默的
- **例句**:
- The violinist used a mute to soften the tone during the delicate passage.
- She remained mute during the interrogation, refusing to utter a single word.
- **考频**: 6
