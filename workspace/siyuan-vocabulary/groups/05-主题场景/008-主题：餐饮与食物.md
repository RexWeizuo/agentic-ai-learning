# 主题：餐饮与食物

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 餐饮与食物场景。bacon(熏肉), bake(烘焙), banquet(宴会), barbecue(烤肉), beverage(饮料), buffet(自助餐)等。

**10 词**

---

## bacon

- **音标**: 'beikәn
- **释义**: n. 熏猪肉\n[机] 腌肉
- **英文释义**: Cured and smoked pork, typically from the back or side of a pig, often sliced thin and fried.
- **词根词缀**: bac(背部)+on(小)→猪背肉→培根
- **例句**:
- Bacon adds a rich, savory flavor to salads and breakfast dishes.
- The study found that high consumption of processed meats like bacon is associated with an increased risk of colorectal cancer.
- **考频**: 10
## bake

- **音标**: beik
- **释义**: vt. 烘焙, 烤\nvi. 烤面包\nn. 烘焙, 烤
- **英文释义**: to cook food using dry heat in an oven
- **词根词缀**: bak(烤)+e→烤
- **例句**:
- She bakes sourdough bread every Sunday to perfect her technique.
- The recipe instructs you to bake the casserole at 375°F for 45 minutes until golden and bubbling.
- **考频**: 8
## banquet

- **音标**: 'bæŋkwit
- **释义**: n. 宴会
- **英文释义**: A formal large meal, often held to celebrate a special occasion or honor someone.
- **词根词缀**: ban(宴会)+quet(小)→宴会
- **例句**:
- The university hosted a banquet to honor its distinguished alumni.
- She delivered a speech at the annual charity banquet attended by over two hundred guests.
- **考频**: 9
## barbecue

- **音标**: 'bɑ:bikju:
- **释义**: n. 烤肉\nvt. 烤肉, 烧烤
- **英文释义**: a social event where food, especially meat, is cooked and eaten outdoors over an open fire or on a grill; to cook (meat or other food) in this way
- **词根词缀**: barb(胡须)+ecue(烤架)→烤肉架→烧烤
- **例句**:
- The community organized a barbecue in the park to celebrate the summer solstice.
- She decided to barbecue salmon with a soy-ginger marinade for the dinner party.
- **考频**: 3
## beef

- **音标**: bi:f
- **释义**: n. 牛肉, 肌肉\nvt. 养(牛), 宰(牛)\nvi. 抱怨, 告发
- **英文释义**: 1. The flesh of cattle used as food; 2. Informal: a complaint or grievance.
- **词根词缀**: beef(牛)→牛肉
- **例句**:
- The recipe calls for lean beef to reduce saturated fat intake.
- She raised a beef about the unfair grading policy during the department meeting.
- **考频**: 23
## beer

- **音标**: biә
- **释义**: n. 啤酒\n[化] 啤酒
- **英文释义**: an alcoholic drink made from fermented barley, hops, and water
- **词根词缀**: beer(啤酒)→啤酒
- **例句**:
- He ordered a pint of beer after finishing the grueling exam.
- The study found a correlation between excessive beer consumption and impaired cognitive performance in graduate students.
- **考频**: 36
## beverage

- **音标**: 'bevәridʒ
- **释义**: n. 饮料\n[化] 饮料(如牛奶、茶、咖啡、啤酒等)
- **英文释义**: A drink, especially one other than water.
- **词根词缀**: bever(喝)+age(名词)→喝的东西→饮料
- **例句**:
- The restaurant offers a wide selection of non-alcoholic beverages, including herbal tea and fresh fruit juice.
- In many cultures, coffee is not merely a beverage but a central part of social ritual and daily routine.
- **考频**: 13
## buffet

- **音标**: 'bʌfit
- **释义**: n. 自助餐, 小卖部, 碗橱, 殴打, 冲击\nvt. 连续打, 冲击, 搏斗\nvi. 奋斗
- **英文释义**: n. a meal where guests serve themselves from a variety of dishes; also, a piece of furniture for storing tableware or a small shop selling snacks; v. to strike repeatedly or be subjected to repeated adverse forces
- **词根词缀**: buff(打击)+et(小)→小打击→自助餐
- **例句**:
- The conference provided a lavish buffet with international cuisines, accommodating diverse dietary preferences.
- Despite being buffeted by economic uncertainty and political instability, the reform agenda continued to gain momentum.
- **考频**: 1
## cafeteria

- **音标**: .kæfi'tiәriә
- **释义**: n. 自助餐厅\n[经] 自助餐厅
- **英文释义**: A dining area where customers serve themselves from a variety of food options displayed on counters.
- **词根词缀**: cafe(咖啡)+teria(场所)→咖啡厅→自助餐厅
- **例句**:
- Students often grab lunch quickly at the university cafeteria before afternoon lectures.
- The company introduced a new healthy-menu initiative in its on-site cafeteria to promote employee wellness.
- **考频**: 10
## cake

- **音标**: keik
- **释义**: n. 蛋糕, 块, 饼\nvt. 使结块, 加块状物于\nvi. 结块
- **英文释义**: a sweet baked food made from flour, sugar, eggs, and other ingredients; or a solid mass formed by drying or hardening
- **词根词缀**: cake→蛋糕
- **例句**:
- The birthday cake was decorated with intricate icing and fresh berries.
- The wet clay began to cake on the surface as it dried in the sun.
- **考频**: 22
