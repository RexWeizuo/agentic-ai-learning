# 主题：国家、政治与军事

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 涉及国家行政、市政管理与军事武装的术语。

**6 词**

---

## nation

- **音标**: 'neiʃәn
- **释义**: n. 国家, 民族\n[法] 民族, 国家
- **英文释义**: a large group of people who share the same language, culture, and history, and usually live in a particular territory governed by one political authority
- **词根词缀**: nat(出生)+ion(名词)→出生地→国家
- **例句**:
- The United Nations was founded to promote peace and cooperation among nations.
- Economic globalization has blurred the boundaries between nations while reinforcing national identity in some cases.
- **考频**: 428
## national

- **音标**: 'næʃәnәl
- **释义**: a. 国家的, 国立的, 全国性的, 民族的\n[经] 全国性的, 国家的, 国民的
- **英文释义**: relating to a nation as a whole, especially its government, people, or culture
- **词根词缀**: nation(国家)+al(形容词)→国家的
- **例句**:
- The national anthem was played before the opening ceremony of the Olympic Games.
- The government has launched a national campaign to improve literacy rates among rural populations.
- **考频**: 481
## municipal

- **音标**: mju:'nisipl
- **释义**: a. 市政的, 自治区的, 内政的\n[经] 市政的, 市的
- **英文释义**: relating to a city or town, especially its local government
- **词根词缀**: muni(公共)+cip(拿)+al(形容词)→公共事务的→市政的
- **例句**:
- The municipal government announced new regulations to improve public transportation.
- Municipal bonds are often considered low-risk investments for conservative portfolios.
- **考频**: 18
## ministry

- **音标**: 'ministri
- **释义**: n. 部, 内阁, 服务\n[经] 部
- **英文释义**: A government department headed by a minister; the collective body of ministers responsible for governing a country.
- **词根词缀**: ministr(服务)+y(名词)→部
- **例句**:
- The Ministry of Education announced new policies to reform university admissions.
- She served as Deputy Minister in the Ministry of Health for five years, overseeing public health initiatives.
- **考频**: 14
## militant

- **音标**: 'militәnt
- **释义**: a. 好战的
- **英文释义**: aggressively active in support of a cause, often using confrontational or violent methods
- **词根词缀**: milit(战斗)+ant(形容词)→好战的
- **例句**:
- The group adopted a militant stance against government policies on climate change.
- Her militant advocacy for workers' rights earned her both admiration and criticism in the labor movement.
- **考频**: 1
## military

- **音标**: 'militәri
- **释义**: n. 军队\na. 军事的, 军人的, 适于战争的
- **英文释义**: relating to soldiers, armed forces, or war; or the armed forces collectively
- **词根词缀**: milit(战斗)+ary(形容词)→军事的
- **例句**:
- The government announced new policies to strengthen its military capabilities.
- She pursued a military career after graduating from the academy.
- **考频**: 104
