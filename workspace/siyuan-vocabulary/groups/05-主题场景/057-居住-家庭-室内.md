# 居住/家庭/室内

> **类型**: 主题场景

> **学习提示**: household 指一家人或家庭的；inhabit 是及物动词，直接加地点 (inhabit the island)。

**4 词**

---

## household

- **音标**: 'haushәuld
- **释义**: n. 一家人, 家庭, 家族, 王室\na. 家庭的, 家常的, 王室的
- **英文释义**: a group of people who live together in one house, especially a family
- **词根词缀**: house(房子)+hold(持有)→持有房子的人→家庭
- **例句**:
- The average household income in the region has increased by 5% over the past year.
- Energy-efficient appliances are now common in most urban households.
- **考频**: 112
## inhabit

- **音标**: in'hæbit
- **释义**: vt. 居住于, 占据, 栖息
- **英文释义**: to live in or occupy a place, especially as a permanent resident
- **词根词缀**: in(在…内)+habit(居住)→居住在内→栖息
- **例句**:
- The island is inhabited by fewer than 500 people.
- Ancient cave paintings suggest that early humans once inhabited this region.
- **考频**: 15
## inhabitant

- **音标**: in'hæbitәnt
- **释义**: n. 居民, 居住者\n[医] 居民
- **英文释义**: a person who lives in a particular place
- **词根词缀**: in(在…内)+habit(居住)+ant(人)→居住在内的人→居民
- **例句**:
- The inhabitants of the coastal town were evacuated before the hurricane hit.
- Urban planners must consider the needs of all inhabitants, including elderly and disabled residents.
- **考频**: 28
## indoor

- **音标**: 'indɒ:
- **释义**: a. 户内的, 室内的
- **英文释义**: taking place, located, or designed for use inside a building
- **词根词缀**: in(在…内)+door(门)→在门内→室内的
- **例句**:
- The university canceled all indoor sports activities due to poor air quality.
- Compared with outdoor exercise, indoor workouts are less affected by seasonal weather changes.
- **考频**: 25
