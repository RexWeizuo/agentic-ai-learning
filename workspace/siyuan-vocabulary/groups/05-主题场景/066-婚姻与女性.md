# 婚姻与女性

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 婚姻状态及未婚女性相关词汇

**4 词**

## marry

- **音标**: 'mæri
- **释义**: vt. 与...结婚, 娶, 嫁\nvi. 结婚
- **英文释义**: to enter into a legal or formal union with someone as husband or wife
- **词根词缀**: marry(结婚)→结婚
- **例句**:
- She decided to marry her longtime partner in a small ceremony abroad.
- He refused to marry until he had established a stable career and financial security.
- **考频**: 116
## marital

- **音标**: 'mæritәl
- **释义**: a. 丈夫的, 婚姻的, 夫妇间的\n[医] 婚姻的
- **英文释义**: relating to marriage or the relationship between husband and wife
- **词根词缀**: marit(婚姻)+al(形容词)→婚姻的
- **例句**:
- Marital status is a key demographic variable collected in national censuses.
- Couples undergoing marital counseling often report improved communication and conflict resolution skills.
- **考频**: 13
## maid

- **音标**: meid
- **释义**: n. 少女, 未婚女子, 女仆
- **英文释义**: A young unmarried woman; formerly, a female servant.
- **词根词缀**: maid→少女
- **例句**:
- The novel portrays the maid as both intelligent and resilient, challenging stereotypes of domestic workers.
- In 19th-century literature, the virtuous maid often serves as a moral compass for the aristocratic protagonists.
- **考频**: 5
## maiden

- **音标**: 'meidn
- **释义**: n. 年轻未婚女子, 少女, 处女\na. 未婚的, 处女的, 初次的, 无经验的
- **英文释义**: a young unmarried woman, especially one who is virgin; also used as an adjective meaning 'first' or 'initial'
- **词根词缀**: maid(少女)+en(名词)→少女
- **例句**:
- The maiden speech of the new MP was widely praised for its clarity and conviction.
- She remained a maiden until her marriage at the age of thirty-two.
- **考频**: 2
