# 主题：餐饮、食物与营养

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 与饮食、餐具、食材及营养摄取相关的词汇。

**6 词**

---

## mess

- **音标**: mes
- **释义**: n. 食堂, 伙食, 用膳, 一份食品, 混乱, 乱七八糟, 困境\nvt. 将...弄糟, 妨碍, 使紊乱, 使就餐\nvi. 陷入困境, 搞乱, 用膳
- **英文释义**: A state of disorder or confusion; a dining area or meal, especially in institutions; to make something dirty, untidy, or dysfunctional.
- **词根词缀**: mess(混乱)→混乱
- **例句**:
- The project deadline was missed because the team's workflow was in a complete mess.
- Cadets are required to eat in the mess hall at designated times.
- **考频**: 20
## napkin

- **音标**: 'næpkin
- **释义**: n. 餐巾, 餐巾纸, 尿布
- **英文释义**: A small square or rectangular piece of cloth or paper used at the table to protect clothing and wipe the mouth and hands.
- **词根词缀**: nap(桌布)+kin(小)→小桌布→餐巾
- **例句**:
- She folded the linen napkin neatly on her lap before the formal dinner began.
- During the presentation, he nervously crumpled the paper napkin in his hand.
- **考频**: 1
## onion

- **音标**: 'ʌnjәn
- **释义**: n. 洋葱\nvt. 因洋葱使掉泪
- **英文释义**: A round, edible bulb with a strong smell and taste, commonly used in cooking; to cause someone to cry (often humorously or figuratively, referencing the tear-inducing effect of cutting onions).
- **词根词缀**: on(一)+ion→一个球→洋葱
- **例句**:
- The recipe calls for one finely chopped onion to add depth and aroma to the stew.
- She was onioned within seconds of slicing the pungent bulb—tears streamed down her cheeks despite her best efforts.
- **考频**: 3
## oven

- **音标**: 'ʌvәn
- **释义**: n. 烤箱, 灶, 子宫\n[化] 烘箱
- **英文释义**: A heated enclosed compartment used for cooking, baking, or drying substances.
- **词根词缀**: oven(炉)→烤箱
- **例句**:
- She preheated the oven to 180°C before putting in the cake batter.
- In materials science, the sample was annealed in a high-temperature furnace oven for two hours.
- **考频**: 16
## nourish

- **音标**: 'nә:riʃ
- **释义**: vt. 滋养, 使健壮, 怀有
- **英文释义**: to provide the food or other substances necessary for health and growth; to support the development of (an idea, feeling, or belief)
- **词根词缀**: nour(滋养)+ish(动词)→滋养
- **例句**:
- A balanced diet helps nourish both body and mind.
- She has long nourished a deep commitment to environmental justice.
- **考频**: 7
## nutrition

- **音标**: nju:'triʃәn
- **释义**: n. 营养, 营养学\n[医] 营养, 营养品
- **英文释义**: The process of taking food into the body to provide energy and support growth and health.
- **词根词缀**: nutri(营养)+tion(名词)→营养
- **例句**:
- A balanced diet is essential for good nutrition and long-term disease prevention.
- Recent research highlights the critical role of early-life nutrition in cognitive development and academic performance.
- **考频**: 41
