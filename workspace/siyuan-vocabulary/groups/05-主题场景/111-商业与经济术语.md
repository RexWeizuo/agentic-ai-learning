# 商业与经济术语

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 经济场景词汇。tariff (关税), stock (股票/库存), stake (股份/赌注), tenant (承租人), token (代币/象征)。

**5 词**

---

## tariff

- **音标**: 'tærif
- **释义**: n. 关税, 关税表, 价格表, 收费表\nvt. 课以关税\n[计] 价目表
- **英文释义**: A tax imposed by a government on imported or exported goods; also refers to an official list of prices or charges.
- **词根词缀**: tariff(阿拉伯语)→关税
- **例句**:
- The U.S. government announced new tariffs on steel imports to protect domestic manufacturers.
- Consumers were frustrated by the sudden tariff hike on solar panels, which significantly increased installation costs.
- **考频**: 3
## stock

- **音标**: stɒk
- **释义**: n. 树干, 祖先, 血统, 原料, 备料, 库存, 牲畜, 股票, 股份, 保留剧目\na. 存货的, 常备的, 平凡的, 普通的, 股票的, 保留剧目的, 繁殖用的\nvt. 装把手于, 进货, 备有, 放牧\nvi. 出新芽, 备货, 囤积
- **英文释义**: 1. n. goods kept for sale or use; 2. n. shares representing ownership in a company; 3. n. the main stem of a plant or tree; 4. n. a person's ancestry or lineage
- **词根词缀**: stock→库存
- **例句**:
- The company reported a sharp decline in inventory stock after the supply chain disruption.
- Investors are increasingly shifting funds from bonds to blue-chip stocks amid rising inflation concerns.
- **考频**: 121
## stake

- **音标**: steik
- **释义**: n. 桩, 炮烙刑, 木柱, 赌注, 奖金\nvt. 打桩, 用桩撑, 下赌注, 资助\nvi. 打赌
- **英文释义**: A post driven into the ground; a sum of money risked in a bet; or a personal or financial interest in an outcome.
- **词根词缀**: stake→桩,赌注
- **例句**:
- The company has a significant stake in the renewable energy sector.
- Before building the fence, workers drove a stake into the ground to mark the boundary.
- **考频**: 15
## tenant

- **音标**: 'tenәnt
- **释义**: n. 承租人, 房客, 居住者\nvt. 租借\n[计] 占据者
- **英文释义**: a person or organization that rents and occupies land, a building, or other property from a landlord
- **词根词缀**: ten(持有)+ant(人)→持有者→租户
- **例句**:
- The tenant must pay rent by the fifth of each month, as stipulated in the lease agreement.
- Under the new data protection regulations, each cloud service tenant is responsible for securing its own data.
- **考频**: 28
## token

- **音标**: 'tәukәn
- **释义**: n. 表征, 记号, 代币\na. 象征的, 表意的\n[计] 记号
- **英文释义**: a symbol or sign that represents something else, often used to indicate presence, intent, or authorization; in computing, a unit of data recognized as a single entity
- **词根词缀**: token→象征
- **例句**:
- She made a token effort to apologize, but her insincerity was obvious.
- In Java, whitespace characters are often discarded during lexical analysis, while keywords and identifiers are treated as distinct tokens.
- **考频**: 11
