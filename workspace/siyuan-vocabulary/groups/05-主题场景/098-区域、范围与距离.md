# 区域、范围与距离

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: region 为地区，realm 为领域/王国，reach 为范围/触及，remote 为遥远的。

**4 词**

---

## region

- **音标**: 'ri:dʒәn
- **释义**: n. 区域, 地带, 地区, 领域, 范围, 区\n[计] 区, 区域
- **英文释义**: a particular area or part of the world, often with distinct geographical, political, cultural, or functional characteristics
- **词根词缀**: reg(统治)+ion(名词)→统治的区域→地区
- **例句**:
- The government has launched a new economic policy targeting underdeveloped regions.
- In computer science, memory is divided into different regions, such as the stack and the heap.
- **考频**: 173
## realm

- **音标**: relm
- **释义**: n. 王国, 领土, 领域\n[计] 领域
- **英文释义**: a field of activity, interest, or knowledge; a kingdom or domain ruled by a monarch
- **词根词缀**: realm(王国)→领域
- **例句**:
- The study of artificial intelligence has rapidly expanded into new scientific and ethical realms.
- Historians often debate the boundaries between the religious and political realms in medieval Europe.
- **考频**: 14
## reach

- **音标**: ri:tʃ
- **释义**: n. 伸出, 延伸, 区域, 范围, 流域, 岬\nvt. 到达, 达到, 伸出, 延伸, 影响\nvi. 达到, 延伸, 伸出手, 传到
- **英文释义**: to arrive at a place, achieve a goal, or extend one's hand or influence; the extent or scope of something
- **词根词缀**: reach(到达)→到达
- **例句**:
- The research findings have reached a wide academic audience, demonstrating their significant impact.
- She stretched out her arm to reach the book on the top shelf, but it was just beyond her grasp.
- **考频**: 340
## remote

- **音标**: ri'mәut
- **释义**: a. 遥远的, 偏僻的, 疏远的, 微少的\n[计] 远程, 远程访问实用程序
- **英文释义**: distant in space, time, or relationship; unlikely to happen or be true
- **词根词缀**: re(远离)+mot(移动)+e→移动远离→遥远的
- **例句**:
- The research team conducted fieldwork in a remote mountain village with no internet access.
- There is only a remote possibility that the proposed policy will be approved by the committee.
- **考频**: 79
