# 主题：医学与健康

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 医学、生理与疾病词汇。pneumonia(肺炎), pharmacy(药房), physiology(生理学), paralyse(麻痹), parasite(寄生虫)。

**8 词**

## pneumonia

- **音标**: nju:'mәunjә
- **释义**: n. 肺炎\n[医] 肺炎
- **英文释义**: A serious lung infection that inflames the air sacs in one or both lungs, often causing cough, fever, chills, and difficulty breathing.
- **词根词缀**: pneumon(词根:肺)+ia(名词后缀)→肺炎
- **例句**:
- The patient was hospitalized with severe pneumonia requiring intravenous antibiotics.
- Elderly individuals are at higher risk of developing complications from pneumonia, such as respiratory failure.
- **考频**: 2
## pharmacy

- **音标**: 'fɑ:mәsi
- **释义**: n. 药房, 配药学, 制药业\n[医] 药学, 药剂学, 调剂学, 药房
- **英文释义**: A place where medicines are prepared and sold, or the science of preparing and dispensing drugs.
- **词根词缀**: pharmac(药)+y(名词)→药学
- **例句**:
- She works as a pharmacist in a community pharmacy.
- The university offers a four-year degree program in pharmacy, covering pharmacology, medicinal chemistry, and clinical practice.
- **考频**: 6
## physiology

- **音标**: .fizi'ɒlәdʒi
- **释义**: n. 生理学, 生理机能\n[医] 生理学
- **英文释义**: The scientific study of the normal functions and mechanisms of living organisms and their parts.
- **词根词缀**: physi(自然)+ology(学科)→自然学→生理学
- **例句**:
- Understanding human physiology is essential for medical students to diagnose diseases accurately.
- Recent advances in cellular physiology have revolutionized our approach to treating metabolic disorders.
- **考频**: 1
## paralyse

- **音标**: 'pærәlais
- **释义**: vt. 使麻痹, 使瘫痪, 使无力, 使气馁, 终止\n[医] 使麻痹, 使瘫痪
- **英文释义**: to make someone unable to move, act, or think normally, often due to shock, fear, or physical illness
- **词根词缀**: para(旁边)+lyse(松开)→旁边松开→瘫痪
- **例句**:
- The sudden news of the merger paralysed the board's decision-making process.
- A stroke can paralyse one side of the body, severely impairing mobility and speech.
- **考频**: 1
## parasite

- **音标**: 'pærәsait
- **释义**: n. 寄生生物, 寄生虫, 食客\n[医] 寄生物; 寄生胎
- **英文释义**: An organism that lives on or in a host organism and benefits at the host's expense.
- **词根词缀**: para(旁边/超越)+it(go)+e
- **例句**:
- Malaria is caused by a protozoan parasite transmitted through mosquito bites.
- Critics accused the unproductive executive of being a corporate parasite, contributing nothing while drawing a high salary.
