# 拒绝、反驳与抗议

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: refuse/refusal 为一般拒绝，refute 为驳斥/反驳(论点)，protest 为抗议。

**4 词**

---

## refusal

- **音标**: ri'fju:zl
- **释义**: n. 拒绝, 推却, 优先决定权\n[法] 拒绝, 谢绝, 取舍权
- **英文释义**: the act of refusing something, especially formally or firmly
- **词根词缀**: re(回)+fus(倒)+al(名词)→倒回→拒绝
- **例句**:
- Her refusal to sign the contract undermined the entire negotiation.
- The court upheld the company's refusal to disclose confidential information on grounds of commercial privilege.
- **考频**: 16
## refuse

- **音标**: ri'fju:z
- **释义**: vt. 拒绝, 谢绝\nvi. 拒绝\nn. 废物\na. 扔掉的, 无用的
- **英文释义**: to decline to accept, do, or agree to something; also, waste material
- **词根词缀**: re(回)+fuse(倒)→倒回→拒绝
- **例句**:
- The committee refused to approve the controversial proposal, citing ethical concerns.
- Environmental scientists warn that improper disposal of industrial refuse can contaminate groundwater.
- **考频**: 118
## refute

- **音标**: ri'fju:t. ri:-
- **释义**: vt. 反驳, 驳斥；驳倒
- **英文释义**: to prove a statement or theory to be false or incorrect
- **词根词缀**: re(回)+fute(打)→打回→驳斥
- **例句**:
- The defense attorney refuted the prosecution's key witness with newly uncovered evidence.
- Recent empirical studies refute the long-held assumption that bilingualism delays language development in children.
- **考频**: 12
## protest

- **音标**: prә'test
- **释义**: n. 抗议, 反对, 抗议书, 断言\nv. 反对, 抗议, 断言
- **英文释义**: a statement or action expressing strong objection to something, often publicly and formally
- **词根词缀**: pro(向前)+test(证明)→向前证明→抗议
- **例句**:
- The students organized a peaceful protest against the proposed tuition hike.
- She protested vigorously when her proposal was rejected without explanation.
- **考频**: 62
