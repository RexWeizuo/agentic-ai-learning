# 主题 (法律与犯罪)

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 法律场景核心词。fraud 是欺诈，gaol 是监狱（英式拼写，同 jail），guilt/guilty 是罪行/有罪的。

**4 词**

---

## fraud

- **音标**: frɒ:d
- **释义**: n. 欺骗, 欺诈, 诡计, 骗子\n[经] 欺诈, 舞弊, 骗子
- **英文释义**: the act of deceiving or misrepresenting to gain an unfair advantage, especially for financial gain
- **词根词缀**: fraud(欺骗)→欺诈
- **例句**:
- The company was accused of securities fraud after falsifying its quarterly earnings reports.
- Detecting insurance fraud requires careful analysis of claim patterns and inconsistencies.
- **考频**: 21
## gaol

- **音标**: dʒeil
- **释义**: n. 监禁, 监狱\nvt. 监禁
- **英文释义**: A prison, especially in British English; to imprison.
- **词根词缀**: gaol
- **例句**:
- The corrupt official was sentenced to three years' gaol for bribery.
- She was gaoled for contempt of court after refusing to testify.
## guilt

- **音标**: gilt
- **释义**: n. 罪行, 内疚\n[法] 罪, 犯罪, 罪行
- **英文释义**: the feeling of having done something wrong or committed a crime; moral responsibility for wrongdoing
- **词根词缀**: guilt(罪)→罪
- **例句**:
- Her confession was motivated by overwhelming guilt over the harm she had caused.
- The prosecution failed to prove the defendant's guilt beyond a reasonable doubt.
- **考频**: 34
## guilty

- **音标**: 'gilti
- **释义**: a. 犯罪的, 有过失的, 自觉有错的, 心虚的\n[法] 有罪的, 犯罪的, 自觉有罪的
- **英文释义**: responsible for a crime or wrongdoing; feeling regret or shame for having done something wrong
- **词根词缀**: guilt(罪)+y(形容词后缀)→有罪的
- **例句**:
- The jury found the defendant guilty of fraud after reviewing the evidence.
- She avoided eye contact during the meeting, looking distinctly guilty after being caught exaggerating her accomplishments.
- **考频**: 39
