# 乡村、地理、区域

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 表示不同的空间或区域概念，cyberspace 为网络虚拟空间。

**4 词**

---

## countryside

- **音标**: 'kʌntrisaid
- **释义**: n. 乡下地方, 乡下居民
- **英文释义**: rural areas outside towns and cities, especially those with farms, fields, and natural landscapes
- **词根词缀**: country(乡村)+side(边)→乡村地区
- **例句**:
- The countryside around Oxford is renowned for its picturesque villages and rolling hills.
- Many urban dwellers seek weekend retreats in the countryside to escape noise and pollution.
- **考频**: 34
## county

- **音标**: 'kaunti
- **释义**: n. 县, 郡
- **英文释义**: An administrative division of a state or country, typically larger than a city and smaller than a state.
- **词根词缀**: count(伯爵)+y(名词)→伯爵领地→郡
- **例句**:
- The county government announced new regulations to improve rural infrastructure.
- She was elected as the sheriff of Montgomery County, the most populous county in the state.
- **考频**: 19
## corridor

- **音标**: 'kɒridɒ:
- **释义**: n. 走廊, 回廊, 人口密集地带
- **英文释义**: a long narrow passage in a building, often with doors on both sides
- **词根词缀**: corridor(走廊)→走廊
- **例句**:
- The students hurried down the corridor to their next class.
- The Balkan Peninsula has historically been regarded as a geopolitical corridor between Europe and Asia.
- **考频**: 14
## cyberspace

- **释义**: [计] 空间, 网控空间
- **英文释义**: The virtual environment of computer networks, especially the internet, where communication, data exchange, and digital interaction occur.
- **词根词缀**: cyber(网络)+space(空间)→网络空间
- **例句**:
- As cybercrime proliferates, governments are strengthening regulations to ensure security in cyberspace.
- Scholars debate the extent to which national sovereignty can be exercised in cyberspace.
- **考频**: 20
