# 军事与冲突

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 与军队、武器、冲突相关的词汇，array 可指“阵列/大批”，agitate 指“煽动/使不安”。

**5 词**

---

## army

- **音标**: 'ɑ:mi
- **释义**: ==n. 军队, 陆军==
- **英文释义**: a large organized group of soldiers trained for war
- **词根词缀**: arm(weapon)+y→armed force→军队
- **例句**:
- The government deployed a contingent of the army to maintain order during the crisis.
- Historians argue that the decline of the Roman Empire was accelerated by the increasing reliance on mercenary troops rather than a loyal, professional army.
- **考频**: 53
## arrow

- **音标**: 'ærәu
- **释义**: ==n. 箭, 箭状物, 箭头记号==
- **英文释义**: A pointed projectile shot from a bow, or any symbol or shape resembling it, especially an indicator showing direction.
- **词根词缀**: arrow(箭)→箭
- **例句**:
- The archer released the arrow with perfect aim, hitting the bullseye on the target.
- In the flowchart, each arrow denotes a logical transition from one process step to the next.
- **考频**: 4
## array

- **音标**: ә'rei
- **释义**: ==n. 排列, 衣服, 大批, 军队\nvt. 布署, 打扮, 排列\n[计] 数组; 阵列==
- **英文释义**: An ordered arrangement of elements, especially in rows and columns; also, a large and impressive group or collection.
- **词根词缀**: ar(朝向)+ray(排列)→排列
- **例句**:
- The data was stored in a two-dimensional array for efficient matrix operations.
- She entered the ballroom in a dazzling array of jewels and silk, commanding immediate attention.
- **考频**: 24
## aggressive

- **音标**: ә'gresiv
- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: ==a. 侵略的, 挑畔的, 进取的\n[法] 侵略的, 爱挑衅的, 行为过火的==
- **英文释义**: a. having or showing determination and energetic pursuit of your ends; positive in his convictions"\ns. tending to spread quickly\ns. characteristic of an enemy or one eager to fight
- **同义词**: hostile, assertive
- **反义词**: peaceful
- **词根词缀**: aggress(攻击)+ive→侵略的
- **搭配**: aggressive behavior, aggressive driving, aggressive growth
- **例句**:
- The company adopted an aggressive marketing strategy to capture market share.
- He became increasingly aggressive during the debate, interrupting others and raising his voice.
- **考频**: 56
## agitate

- **音标**: 'ædʒiteit
- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: ==vt. 使摇动, 搅动, 使激动, 使不安\nvi. 鼓动, 煽动==
- **英文释义**: v. try to stir up public opinion\nv. cause to be agitated, excited, or roused\nv. change the arrangement or position of
- **同义词**: stir, disturb, provoke
- **反义词**: calm, soothe
- **词根词缀**: ag(做)+itat→一再行动→鼓动
- **搭配**: agitate for change, agitate against something, agitate public opinion
- **例句**:
- The speaker tried to agitate the crowd with passionate rhetoric.
- Don't agitate the mixture too vigorously, or it will foam over.
- **考频**: 1
