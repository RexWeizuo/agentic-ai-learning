# 服装、外观、装饰

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 与物品外观、覆盖物及装饰布置相关。

**4 词**

## costume

- **音标**: 'kɒstju:m
- **释义**: n. 装束, 服装
- **英文释义**: a set of clothes worn to represent a particular character, role, historical period, or culture, especially for a performance or special occasion
- **词根词缀**: costume(服装)→服装
- **例句**:
- The actors spent hours perfecting their historical costumes for the Shakespearean production.
- During the cultural festival, students wore traditional costumes from over twenty countries.
- **考频**: 10
## cover

- **音标**: 'kʌvә
- **释义**: n. 盖子, 封面, 藉口\nvt. 覆盖, 掩饰, 保护, 掩护, 包括\nvi. 覆盖
- **英文释义**: to place something over or upon to protect, conceal, or include; a protective or decorative layer or surface
- **词根词缀**: cover(覆盖)→覆盖
- **例句**:
- The report's cover featured a minimalist design that reflected its academic rigor.
- The journalist's undercover assignment required her to cover her true identity while investigating corporate corruption.
- **考频**: 218
## couch

- **释义**: n. 长沙发, 睡椅, 卧榻\nvt. 横躺, 表达\nvi. 躺下, 蹲伏
- **英文释义**: A piece of furniture for sitting or lying on, typically with a back and arms; to express something in a particular way.
- **词根词缀**: couch(沙发)→沙发
- **例句**:
- She reclined on the couch while reviewing her GRE vocabulary list.

- **考频**: 8

- **例句**:
