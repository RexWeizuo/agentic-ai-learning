## administer

- **音标**: әd'ministә
- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: ==vt. 管理, 料理, 执行\nvi. 执行遗产管理人的职责, 给予帮助==
- **英文释义**: v. work in an administrative capacity; supervise or be in charge of\nv. perform (a church sacrament) ritually\nv. give or apply (medications)\nv. direct the taking of
- **同义词**: manage, run
- **反义词**: neglect, abandon
- **词根词缀**: ad(加强)+minister(仆人)→管理
- **搭配**: administer justice, administer medication, administer an oath
- **例句**:
- The nurse will administer the vaccine to each patient before noon.
- The court appointed a trustee to administer the estate after the testator's death.
- **考频**: 11
## administration

- **音标**: әd.mini'streiʃәn
- **词性**: n.
- **释义**: ==n. 行政, 管理, 政府机关\n[化] 给药==
- **英文释义**: n. a method of tending to or managing the affairs of a some group of people (especially the group's business affairs)\nn. the persons (or committees or departments etc.) who make up a body for the purpose of administering something\nn. the act of administering medication\nn. the act of meting out justice according to the law
- **同义词**: management, governance, administration (as in 'the current administration')
- **反义词**: anarchy, chaos
- **词根词缀**: ad-(to, toward)+minister(serve, attend)+-ation(noun-forming suffix)→literally 'serving/tending toward [a task or authority]'→organized service to manage affairs→'administrative function or body'
- **搭配**: public administration, school administration, drug administration
- **例句**:
- The hospital's administration approved the new policy on patient care.
- She works in university administration, handling faculty appointments and budget planning.
- **考频**: 86
