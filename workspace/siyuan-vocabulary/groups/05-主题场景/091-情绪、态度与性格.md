# 情绪、态度与性格

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 描述人物心理与行为倾向，注意 rash(轻率的) 与 reckless(鲁莽的) 的近义辨析。

**7 词**

---

## rage

- **音标**: reidʒ
- **释义**: n. 愤怒, 情绪激动, 狂暴\nvi. 大怒, 狂吹, 流行
- **英文释义**: intense anger or violent emotion; also, to be extremely popular or widespread
- **词根词缀**: rage(狂怒)→狂怒
- **例句**:
- Her face flushed with rage as she read the unfair verdict.
- The new fitness trend raged across campuses last semester.
- **考频**: 21
## rash

- **音标**: ræʃ
- **释义**: a. 轻率的, 匆忙的, 鲁莽的\nn. 皮疹
- **英文释义**: adj. acting or done without careful consideration; n. a skin condition with many small red spots or bumps
- **词根词缀**: rash(皮疹)→皮疹
- **例句**:
- His rash decision to quit the job without another offer led to serious financial difficulties.
- She developed a widespread rash after taking the new medication, prompting an immediate visit to the dermatologist.
- **考频**: 4
## reckless

- **音标**: 'reklis
- **释义**: a. 不介意的, 大意的, 鲁莽的, 不顾后果的\n[法] 不注意的, 粗心大意的, 鲁莽的
- **英文释义**: acting without thinking about the consequences; dangerously careless
- **词根词缀**: reck(注意)+less(无)→无注意→鲁莽的
- **例句**:
- His reckless decision to invest all his savings in cryptocurrency led to significant financial loss.
- The driver was fined for reckless driving after speeding through a red light in heavy traffic.
- **考频**: 13
## reluctant

- **音标**: ri'lʌktәnt
- **释义**: a. 不情愿的, 勉强的\n[法] 不愿的, 勉强的, 难以处理的
- **英文释义**: unwilling and hesitant to do something
- **词根词缀**: re(反对)+luct(挣扎)+ant(形容词)→挣扎反对→不情愿的
- **例句**:
- She was reluctant to accept the promotion, fearing the increased responsibility.
- The government remained reluctant to implement sweeping reforms without broad public support.
- **考频**: 61
## resent

- **音标**: ri'zent
- **释义**: vt. 愤恨, 憎恶, 怨恨
- **英文释义**: to feel bitter or indignant about something, especially because it seems unfair
- **词根词缀**: re(加强)+sent(感觉)→强烈感觉→怨恨
- **例句**:
- She deeply resented being passed over for promotion despite her seniority.
- Many students resent the excessive workload imposed by the new curriculum.
- **考频**: 14
## reproach

- **音标**: ri'prәutʃ
- **释义**: n. 责备, 耻辱\nvt. 责备, 申斥, 使丢脸
- **英文释义**: to express disapproval or disappointment, especially in a quiet or severe way; a feeling of shame or disgrace resulting from failure or wrongdoing
- **词根词缀**: re(回)+proach(接近)→接近回来→责备
- **例句**:
- Her silence was a silent reproach to his reckless decision.
- He felt deep reproach for having broken his promise to his mentor.
- **考频**: 4
## rejoice

- **音标**: ri'dʒɒis
- **释义**: vt. 使欢喜\nvi. 欣喜, 高兴, 庆祝
- **英文释义**: to feel or show great joy or delight
- **词根词缀**: re(加强)+joice(快乐)→使快乐→欣喜
- **例句**:
- The entire nation rejoiced at the historic peace agreement.
- She rejoiced in the success of her students, seeing their hard work finally bear fruit.
- **考频**: 3
