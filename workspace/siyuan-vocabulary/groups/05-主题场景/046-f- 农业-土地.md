# f- 农业/土地

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 农业、土地、饥荒相关词汇

**5 词**

---

## farm

- **音标**: fɑ:m
- **释义**: n. 农场, 农田\nvt. 耕种\nvi. 种田
- **英文释义**: a piece of land used for growing crops or raising animals
- **词根词缀**: farm(固定)→固定的土地→农场
- **例句**:
- The family has owned and operated the farm for over three generations.
- Many young people are now choosing to farm organically to meet the rising demand for sustainable food.
- **考频**: 274
## farmer

- **音标**: 'fɑ:mә
- **释义**: n. 农夫, 农场主\n[法] 农民, 农场主, 承包者
- **英文释义**: a person who owns or manages a farm and grows crops or raises animals
- **词根词缀**: farm(农场)+er(人)→农民
- **例句**:
- The farmer diversified his operations by adding organic vegetable production to his livestock business.
- Climate change poses unprecedented challenges to the modern farmer, especially in drought-prone regions.
- **考频**: 218
## fertile

- **音标**: 'fә:tail
- **释义**: a. 可繁殖的, 肥沃的, 生产多的\n[医] 能生育的
- **英文释义**: capable of producing abundant growth, offspring, or ideas; rich in nutrients or reproductive ability
- **词根词缀**: fertil(生产)+e→能生产的→肥沃的
- **例句**:
- The Nile River valley has long been celebrated for its fertile soil, supporting intensive agriculture.
- After undergoing fertility treatment, she received confirmation that her ovaries were once again fertile.
- **考频**: 24
## fertiliser

- **音标**: 'fә:tilaizә
- **释义**: [化] 肥料
- **英文释义**: A substance added to soil to improve its fertility and promote plant growth.
- **词根词缀**: fertil(生产)+is(使)+er(物)→肥料
- **例句**:
- Excessive use of chemical fertiliser can lead to soil degradation and water pollution.
- Organic fertilisers, such as compost and manure, are increasingly favoured in sustainable agriculture.
- **考频**: 5
## famine

- **音标**: 'fæmin
- **释义**: n. 饥荒, 缺乏, 饥饿
- **英文释义**: A severe and widespread shortage of food, often leading to starvation and death.
- **词根词缀**: fam(饥饿)+ine(名词)→饥荒
- **例句**:
- The country was devastated by a prolonged famine that left millions malnourished.
- Economic sanctions exacerbated the existing famine, triggering a humanitarian crisis.
- **考频**: 7
