# 主题：纺织与衣物

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: weave (编织)，wardrobe (衣橱/全部衣服)，velvet (天鹅绒)，vest (背心)。

**4 词**

---

## weave

- **音标**: wi:v
- **释义**: n. 编法, 织法, 编织式样\nvt. 编织, 组合, 编排, 使迂回前进\nvi. 纺织, 迂回行进
- **英文释义**: to make fabric by interlacing threads, or to combine elements skillfully into a coherent whole
- **词根词缀**: weave(编织)→编织
- **例句**:
- The author masterfully weaves historical facts with fictional narratives in her latest novel.
- Drivers must weave through heavy traffic during rush hour in downtown Beijing.
- **考频**: 13
## wardrobe

- **音标**: 'wɒ:drәub
- **释义**: n. 衣橱, 衣室, 全部衣服
- **英文释义**: A large cupboard or closet for storing clothes, or the entire collection of clothes owned by a person.
- **词根词缀**: ward(守卫)+robe(长袍)→守卫长袍的地方→衣柜
- **例句**:
- She redesigned her wardrobe to include more versatile, professional pieces for the new job.
- The film's costume department spent months curating a historically accurate wardrobe for the 19th-century drama.
- **考频**: 10
## velvet

- **音标**: 'velvit
- **释义**: n. 天鹅绒\na. 天鹅绒的
- **英文释义**: A soft, dense fabric with a smooth, luxurious pile, typically made from silk, cotton, or synthetic fibers.
- **词根词缀**: velv+-et(小)
- **例句**:
- The antique armchair was upholstered in deep burgundy velvet, lending an air of old-world elegance.
- Her voice was as smooth and rich as velvet, effortlessly commanding attention during the debate.
## vest

- **音标**: vest
- **释义**: n. 背心, 汗背心\nvt. 使穿衣服, 授予\nvi. 穿衣服, 归属
- **英文释义**: A sleeveless garment worn on the upper body; or to confer authority, rights, or legal title upon someone.
- **词根词缀**: vest(衣服)→背心
- **例句**:
- He wore a tailored wool vest under his suit jacket for a polished, professional look.
- The power to appoint judges vests solely in the president, as stipulated by the Constitution.
- **考频**: 1
