# 主题：运输/车辆/载具

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 运输场景词汇：cargo(货物), carriage(马车/客车), carrier(运送者/带菌者), coach(教练/四轮大马车)。

**4 词**

---

## cargo

- **音标**: 'kɑ:gәu
- **释义**: n. 货物\n[经] 货柜
- **英文释义**: goods transported by ship, aircraft, or other vehicle
- **词根词缀**: car(车)+go(走)→车载货物→货物
- **例句**:
- The cargo ship was loaded with containers carrying electronics and textiles.
- Customs officials inspected the cargo for prohibited items before clearing it for import.
- **考频**: 10
## carriage

- **音标**: 'kæridʒ
- **释义**: n. 马车, 客车, 举止, 运输\n[经] 搬运费, 运费
- **英文释义**: A wheeled vehicle drawn by horses; a railway passenger coach; the act of carrying or transporting goods; or a person's posture and bearing.
- **词根词缀**: car(车)+riage(名词)→车辆→马车
- **例句**:
- The antique carriage was displayed in the museum as a relic of 19th-century transportation.
- Her graceful carriage and confident demeanor impressed the interview panel.
- **考频**: 11
## carrier

- **音标**: 'kæriә
- **释义**: n. 运送者, 邮递员, 带菌者\n[计] 载波
- **英文释义**: a person or thing that carries or transports something, such as goods, messages, disease-causing organisms, or a signal (e.g., in telecommunications)
- **词根词缀**: car(车)+ier(人)→搬运的人→运输工具
- **例句**:
- The postal service employs thousands of carriers to deliver mail across the country.
- In epidemiology, an asymptomatic carrier can spread infectious disease without showing symptoms.
- **考频**: 20
## coach

- **音标**: kәutʃ
- **释义**: n. 四轮大马车, 教练\nvt. 训练, 指导\nvi. 坐马车旅行, 作指导
- **英文释义**: a person who trains and directs athletes or teams; also, to train or instruct someone in a skill or subject
- **词根词缀**: coach(马车)→教练(原指马车载人)
- **例句**:
- The basketball coach devised a rigorous training regimen to prepare the team for the national championship.
- She hired a writing coach to help her refine her academic essays for graduate school applications.
- **考频**: 73
