## calorie

- **音标**: 'kælәri
- **释义**: n. 卡路里（热量单位）
- **英文释义**: A unit of energy equal to the amount of heat needed to raise the temperature of one gram of water by one degree Celsius, commonly used to measure the energy content of food.
- **词根词缀**: calor(热)+ie(小)→小热量→卡路里
- **例句**:
- Eating a balanced diet means being mindful of the number of calories consumed each day.
- This granola bar contains 200 calories, making it a moderate-energy snack for sustained focus during exams.
- **考频**: 113

- **释义**: n. 癌, 恶性肿瘤\n[医] 癌

## carbohydrate

- **音标**: .kɑ:bәu'haidreit
- **释义**: n. 碳水化合物, 糖类\n[化] 糖类; 碳水化合物
- **英文释义**: An organic compound composed of carbon, hydrogen, and oxygen, typically with a hydrogen:oxygen atom ratio of 2:1, serving as a major source of energy in living organisms.
- **词根词缀**: carbo(碳)+hydr(水)+ate(名词)→碳和水→碳水化合物
- **例句**:
- Complex carbohydrates, such as whole grains and legumes, provide sustained energy and are recommended over simple sugars in a balanced diet.
- The biochemistry exam required students to distinguish between monosaccharides, disaccharides, and polysaccharides—all classes of carbohydrates.
- **考频**: 8
## chronic

- **音标**: /ˈkrɒnɪk/
- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 慢性的，长期的；习惯性的
- **英文释义**: (of an illness) persisting for a long time; long-established
- **同义词**: persistent, long-term, persistent, habitual
- **反义词**: acute, temporary, short-term
- **词根词缀**: chron(时间)+ic→长时间的→慢性的
- **搭配**: chronic disease, chronic pain, chronic shortage
- **例句**:
- He suffers from chronic back pain.
- The city has a chronic housing shortage.
- **考频**: 46
## cocaine

- **音标**: kә'kein
- **释义**: n. 可卡因, 古柯碱\n[化] 可卡因; 古柯碱
- **英文释义**: A powerful and highly addictive stimulant drug derived from the leaves of the coca plant, often abused for its euphoric effects.
- **词根词缀**: coca(古柯)+ine(化学物质)→古柯碱→可卡因
- **例句**:
- The use of cocaine is strictly prohibited under international drug control treaties.
- Neuroimaging studies have revealed significant structural changes in the brains of chronic cocaine users.
- **考频**: 12
