# 时间、日期、周期

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 表示时间跨度、周期或时代背景。

**4 词**

---

## date

- **音标**: deit
- **释义**: n. 日期, 约会, 枣椰树\nvt. 约会, 定日期\nvi. 注明日期, 过时
- **英文释义**: A specific day marked on the calendar; a social meeting between two people, especially one romantically intended; the fruit of the date palm; to assign or record a date; to become outdated.
- **词根词缀**: date(日期)→日期
- **例句**:
- The contract must be signed no later than the date specified in clause 4.
- She declined his invitation to date, citing academic commitments ahead of the graduate entrance exam.
- **考频**: 145
## decade

- **音标**: 'dekeid
- **释义**: n. 十年, 十
- **英文释义**: A period of ten years
- **词根词缀**: deca(十)+de(年)→十年
- **例句**:
- The country's economy has grown steadily over the past decade.
- She has devoted the better part of a decade to researching climate change in polar regions.
- **考频**: 345
## cycle

- **音标**: 'saikl
- **释义**: n. 周期, 循环, 自行车, 一段时间, 整套\nvi. 循环, 轮转, 骑自行车\nvt. 使循环, 使轮转\n[计] 环路; 周期; 循环
- **英文释义**: A repeated series of events or processes; a single complete occurrence of such a series; or a bicycle.
- **词根词缀**: cycle(圆)→循环
- **例句**:
- The economic cycle typically includes phases of expansion, peak, contraction, and trough.
- Modern operating systems use scheduling algorithms to cycle through multiple processes efficiently.
- **考频**: 94
## contemporary

- **音标**: kәn'tempәrәri
- **释义**: n. 同时代的人\na. 同时代的, 属于同一时期的
- **英文释义**: existing or happening at the same time; a person living or occurring in the same period
- **词根词缀**: con(共同)+tempor(时间)+ary(形容词)→共同时间的→当代的
- **例句**:
- Many of Shakespeare's contemporaries, such as Christopher Marlowe, also made significant contributions to English drama.
- Contemporary economic theories often challenge classical assumptions about market efficiency.
- **考频**: 49
