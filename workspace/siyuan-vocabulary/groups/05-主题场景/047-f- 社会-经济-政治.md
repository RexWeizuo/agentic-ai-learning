## federal

- **音标**: 'fedәrәl
- **释义**: a. 联邦的, 联合的, 同盟的\n[法] 联邦的, 联邦制的, 联盟的
- **英文释义**: relating to a federal system of government, where power is divided between a central authority and constituent political units (e.g., states or provinces)
- **词根词缀**: feder(联盟)+al(形容词)→联盟的→联邦的
- **例句**:
- The U.S. Constitution establishes a federal system in which powers are shared between the national government and individual states.
- Federal regulations often override conflicting state laws in areas such as environmental protection and civil rights.
- **考频**: 200
## federation

- **词根词缀**: feder(联盟)+ation(名词)→联盟
- **例句**:

- The International Olympic Committee recognizes national sports federations as the sole governing bodies for their respective disciplines.
- **考频**: 19
