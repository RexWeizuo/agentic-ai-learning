# 居住与建筑空间

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 与人类居住环境、建筑附属结构及水利沟渠相关的名词和动词。

**5 词**

---

## dwell

- **音标**: dwel
- **释义**: vi. 居住, 居住(于), 存在(于)
- **英文释义**: to live in a particular place
- **词根词缀**: dwell→居住
- **例句**:
- She has dwelt in rural Scotland for over twenty years.
- Philosophers often dwell on the nature of consciousness and self-awareness.
- **考频**: 21
## dwelling

- **音标**: 'dweliŋ
- **释义**: n. 住处\n[医] 住房
- **英文释义**: a place where someone lives; a house or apartment
- **词根词缀**: dwell(居住)+ing(名词)→住所
- **例句**:
- The old stone cottage is a charming rural dwelling.
- Urban planners are addressing the shortage of affordable dwellings in major cities.
- **考频**: 2
## doorway

- **音标**: 'dɒ:wei
- **释义**: n. 门口, 途径
- **英文释义**: an entrance or opening in a wall where a door is or can be placed; a means of access to an opportunity or experience
- **词根词缀**: door(门)+way(路)→门路→门口
- **例句**:
- She stood hesitantly in the doorway, unsure whether to enter the crowded conference room.
- Education serves as a vital doorway to social mobility and professional advancement.
- **考频**: 11
## dock

- **音标**: dɒk
- **释义**: n. 码头, 船坞, 被告席, 尾巴的骨肉部分\nvt. 使靠码头, 使(船)进港, 剪短\nvi. 进港
- **英文释义**: A structure built on the shore for loading/unloading ships; a place where ships are repaired; the area in a courtroom where the defendant stands; to cut short (e.g., a tail or pay); to bring a ship to a dock.
- **词根词缀**: dock(船坞)→船坞
- **例句**:
- The cargo ship docked at the main commercial dock before dawn.
- The defense attorney challenged the evidence presented while her client stood nervously at the dock.
- **考频**: 4
## ditch

- **音标**: ditʃ
- **释义**: n. 沟渠\nvt. 在...开沟\nvi. 开沟
- **英文释义**: A long narrow trench dug in the ground, typically for drainage, defense, or as a boundary.
- **词根词缀**: dig(挖)+tch→挖出的沟→沟渠
- **例句**:
- The farmers dug a ditch to divert rainwater away from the crops.
- The army fortified its position by digging a deep ditch around the camp.
- **考频**: 5
