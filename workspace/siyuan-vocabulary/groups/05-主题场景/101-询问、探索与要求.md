# 询问、探索与要求

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: query 为疑问/查询，quest 为探索/追求，questionnaire 为问卷。

**4 词**

---

## query

- **音标**: 'kwiәri
- **释义**: n. 疑问, 疑问号, 质问, 查询\nv. 询问, 质问\n[计] 查询
- **英文释义**: a question or request for information, especially one submitted to a database or search system; to ask a question or express doubt
- **词根词缀**: quer(问)+y(名词)→询问
- **例句**:
- The researcher submitted a complex SQL query to retrieve patient records from the hospital database.
- She queried the lecturer about the ambiguity in the exam instructions, seeking clarification before submitting her answers.
- **考频**: 6
## quest

- **音标**: kwest
- **释义**: n. 探索, 寻求, 调查\nv. 寻找, 找, 追寻猎物
- **英文释义**: a long or difficult search for something, especially something important or abstract
- **词根词缀**: quest(问)→寻求
- **例句**:
- Her academic quest for the origins of language led her to study ancient manuscripts.
- The government launched a quest to identify the root causes of the economic downturn.
- **考频**: 28
## questionnaire

- **音标**: kwestʃә'nєә
- **释义**: n. 调查表, 问卷\n[经] 调查表
- **英文释义**: A set of printed or written questions with a choice of answers, devised for gathering information from a number of people.
- **词根词缀**: quest(问)+ion(名词)+naire(名词后缀)→问卷
- **例句**:
- Participants were asked to complete a detailed questionnaire about their dietary habits and physical activity levels.
- The researcher distributed the questionnaire to 200 undergraduate students to collect empirical data for the thesis.
- **考频**: 15
## requirement

- **音标**: ri'kwaiәmәnt
- **释义**: n. 需求, 必要条件, 要求\n[化] 要求; 合同要求
- **英文释义**: Something that is needed or demanded, especially as a condition for success, participation, or compliance.
- **词根词缀**: re(加强)+quire(寻求)+ment(名词)→要求
- **例句**:
- All applicants must meet the language proficiency requirement to be considered for the scholarship.
- The new safety regulation imposes stricter technical requirements on manufacturing equipment.
- **考频**: 106
