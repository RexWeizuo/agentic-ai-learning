# 规则、违背、缺陷

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 涉及规则遵守、系统缺陷或防御/违抗行为。

**4 词**

---

## default

- **音标**: di'fɒ:lt
- **释义**: n. 违约, 不履行责任, 缺席, 默认值\nv. 疏怠职责, 缺席, 拖欠, 默认\n[计] 默认; 默认值; 缺省值
- **英文释义**: Failure to fulfill an obligation, especially to repay a loan or appear in court; also, a preset value or setting automatically used when no alternative is specified.
- **词根词缀**: de(加强)+fault(错误)→错误→违约
- **例句**:
- If the borrower fails to make payments for three consecutive months, the loan will go into default.
- The software uses 'UTF-8' as the default encoding unless the user specifies otherwise.
- **考频**: 14
## defect

- **音标**: di'fekt
- **释义**: n. 缺点\n[医] 缺损, 缺陷
- **英文释义**: A shortcoming, imperfection, or flaw in something or someone
- **词根词缀**: de(离开)+fect(做)→做离开→缺陷
- **例句**:
- The software was withdrawn from the market after a critical security defect was discovered.
- Congenital heart defect is one of the most common types of birth defects encountered in pediatric cardiology.
- **考频**: 15
## defense

- **音标**: di'fens
- **释义**: n. 防卫, 防卫物\n[医] 防御
- **英文释义**: the action of protecting someone or something from attack, harm, or danger
- **词根词缀**: de(加强)+fens(打击)+e→打击→防御
- **例句**:
- The government has increased its defense budget to strengthen national security.
- The body's immune system provides a natural defense against infections.
- **考频**: 50
## defy

- **音标**: di'fai
- **释义**: vt. 藐视, 挑衅, 使落空\nn. 挑战
- **英文释义**: to openly resist or refuse to obey an authority, rule, or expectation
- **词根词缀**: de(离开)+fy(信任)→不信任→违抗
- **例句**:
- Her decision to pursue a career in science defied traditional gender roles.
- The new policy has defied expectations by significantly reducing carbon emissions within its first year.
- **考频**: 18
