# 协议、让步、一致

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 商业或谈判中的达成一致与让步，concession 指特许或让步。

**4 词**

---

## contract

- **音标**: 'kɒntrækt
- **释义**: n. 合约, 婚约, 契约\nvt. 使皱缩, 使缩短, 感染, 订约, 缔结\nvi. 皱缩, 订约, 收缩
- **英文释义**: A legally binding agreement between two or more parties; to become smaller, shorter, or tighter; to acquire (a disease or obligation) unintentionally or formally.
- **词根词缀**: con(一起)+tract(拉)→拉在一起→合同
- **例句**:
- The company signed a five-year contract with the supplier to ensure stable raw material supply.
- When exposed to heat, the fabric tends to contract, so it should be washed in cold water.
- **考频**: 97
## consent

- **音标**: kәn'sent
- **释义**: n. 同意, 许可\nvi. 同意, 赞同
- **英文释义**: Permission for something to happen or agreement to do something
- **词根词缀**: con(共同)+sent(感觉)→共同感觉→同意
- **例句**:
- Medical treatment cannot be administered without the patient's informed consent.
- The proposal was adopted unanimously, as all members consented to the revised terms.
- **考频**: 19
## consensus

- **音标**: kәn'sensәs
- **释义**: n. 合意, 一致, 同感\n[计] 合意
- **英文释义**: General agreement among a group of people, especially after discussion
- **词根词缀**: con(共同)+sens(感觉)+us(名词)→共同感觉→一致
- **例句**:
- After hours of debate, the committee reached a consensus on the new policy.
- There is broad scientific consensus that climate change is driven by human activity.
- **考频**: 32
## concession

- **音标**: kәn'seʃәn
- **释义**: n. 特许, 让步, 认可\n[经] 核准, 许可, 特殊(权)
- **英文释义**: Something granted, often reluctantly, in response to demands or to reach an agreement; a privilege or right officially allowed.
- **词根词缀**: con(完全)+cess(走)+ion(名词)→完全走开→让步
- **例句**:
- The government made a major concession to the protesters by agreeing to repeal the controversial tax law.
- In international negotiations, neither side was willing to make significant concessions without reciprocal guarantees.
- **考频**: 10
