# 主题：商业/经济

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 商业场景词汇：commerce(商业), commercial(商业的/广告), commodity(商品/农产品)。

**4 词**

---

## commerce

- **音标**: 'kɒmә:s
- **释义**: n. 商业, 商务, 贸易\n[经] 商业, 贸易, 商务
- **英文释义**: The activity of buying and selling goods and services, especially on a large scale.
- **词根词缀**: com(一起)+merce(贸易)→贸易
- **例句**:
- The rapid development of e-commerce has transformed traditional retail models.
- International commerce requires careful negotiation of tariffs, regulations, and cultural differences.
- **考频**: 56
## commercial

- **音标**: kә'mә:ʃәl
- **释义**: a. 商业的, 商用的, 商品化的\nn. 商业广告节目
- **英文释义**: relating to commerce, trade, or business activities; or a short advertisement broadcast on radio or television
- **词根词缀**: com(一起)+merc(贸易)+ial(形容词)→贸易的→商业的
- **例句**:
- The company launched a new commercial product after extensive market research.
- During the Super Bowl, viewers expect high-budget and creative commercials.
- **考频**: 168
## company

- **音标**: 'kʌmpәni
- **释义**: n. 公司, 友伴, 交往, 连队, 朋友, 一群\nvt. 陪伴\nvi. 交往
- **英文释义**: a business organization; a group of people assembled together; the action or state of being with someone
- **词根词缀**: com(一起)+pany(面包)→一起吃面包→公司
- **例句**:
- Apple Inc. is a multinational technology company headquartered in Cupertino, California.
- She declined the invitation to dinner, saying she preferred not to keep company with people who held such extreme views.
- **考频**: 1267
## commodity

- **音标**: kә'mɒditi
- **释义**: n. 农产品, 商品, 有用的物品\n[经] 商品, 货物, 日用品
- **英文释义**: A raw material or primary agricultural product that can be bought and sold, especially in bulk on markets.
- **词根词缀**: com(一起)+mod(模式)+ity(名词)→共同模式→商品
- **例句**:
- Crude oil and wheat are among the most traded global commodities.
- The sharp decline in commodity prices triggered concerns about deflation in emerging economies.
- **考频**: 45
