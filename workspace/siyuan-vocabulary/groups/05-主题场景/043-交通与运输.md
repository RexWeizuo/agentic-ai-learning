## deliver

- **音标**: di'livә
- **释义**: vt. 递送, 陈述, 释放, 发表, 引渡, 投递, 交付\n[经] 交运
- **英文释义**: to carry and hand over something to a person or place; to give a formal speech or presentation
- **词根词缀**: de(离开)+liver(自由)→使自由→交付
- **例句**:
- The courier will deliver the package to your office by noon.
- She delivered a compelling keynote address on climate policy at the international summit.
- **考频**: 114
