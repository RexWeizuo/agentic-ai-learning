## recollect

- **音标**: .rekә'lekt
- **释义**: vt. 回忆, 回想, 忆起\nvi. 回忆
- **英文释义**: to remember something from the past, often with some effort
- **词根词缀**: re(再)+collect(收集)→再收集→回忆
- **例句**:
- She struggled to recollect the exact date of their first meeting.
- The witness was asked to recollect everything he had seen at the scene of the crime.

## remember

- **释义**: vt. 记得, 回忆起, 记住, 铭记, 纪念\nvi. 记得

- **词根词缀**: re(再次)+member(记忆)→再次记忆→记住
- **例句**:
