# 主题：视觉、观察与视角

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 与视觉感知、观察行为及光学特性相关的词汇。

**4 词**

## observation

- **音标**: .ɒbzә:'veiʃәn
- **释义**: n. 观察, 注意, 观测, 观察力\n[化] 观察
- **英文释义**: the process of watching and recording something carefully, especially to learn more about it
- **词根词缀**: ob(加强)+serv(服务)+ation(名词)→观察行为→观察
- **例句**:
- Her keen observation of patient behavior led to an early diagnosis.
- The experiment requires precise observation and accurate data recording.
- **考频**: 73
## observe

- **音标**: әb'zә:v
- **释义**: vt. 觉察到, 遵守, 注意到, 庆祝\nvi. 注意, 评论
- **英文释义**: to notice or perceive something; to comply with a rule, law, or custom; to celebrate a formal occasion
- **词根词缀**: ob(加强)+serv(服务)+e→观察
- **例句**:
- Scientists observed a significant decrease in carbon emissions after the policy was implemented.
- All employees are required to observe the company's code of conduct strictly.
- **考频**: 153
## opaque

- **音标**: әu'peik
- **释义**: n. 不透明物\na. 不透明的, 不传热的, 不传导的, 阴暗的\n[计] 白底
- **英文释义**: not allowing light to pass through; not transparent or translucent
- **词根词缀**: op(不)+aque(水)→不透水→不透明的
- **例句**:
- The stained-glass window was made of opaque panels to ensure privacy.
- In computer graphics, an opaque object blocks the rendering of objects behind it.
- **考频**: 2
## optical

- **音标**: 'ɒptikl
- **释义**: a. 眼睛的, 视觉的, 光学的\n[机] 光学上的, 光学的
- **英文释义**: Relating to light, vision, or the optics branch of physics
- **词根词缀**: opt(视力)+ical(形容词)→视觉的
- **例句**:
- The optical fibers used in high-speed internet transmission rely on total internal reflection.
- Her optical illusion artwork deceived viewers by manipulating visual perception.
- **考频**: 6
