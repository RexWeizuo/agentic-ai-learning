# 液体与流动

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 液体、汁液及泄漏相关词汇

**4 词**

---

## liquid

- **音标**: 'likwid
- **释义**: n. 液体, 流体, 流音\na. 液体的, 透明的, 明亮的, 流动的, 易变的
- **英文释义**: A substance that flows freely and takes the shape of its container, such as water or oil.
- **词根词缀**: liqu(液体)+id(形容词)→液体的
- **例句**:
- The experiment requires precise measurement of the liquid volume before heating.
- Unlike solids, liquids have no fixed shape but maintain a relatively constant volume.
- **考频**: 40
## liquor

- **音标**: 'likә
- **释义**: n. 酒, 液体, 汁, 溶液\nvt. 浸水\nvi. 喝酒
- **英文释义**: an alcoholic drink, especially distilled spirits such as whiskey or rum
- **词根词缀**: liqu(液体)+or(名词)→液体→酒
- **例句**:
- The regulation strictly limits the sale of liquor to adults over 21.
- After the accident, the wound was cleaned with antiseptic liquor to prevent infection.
- **考频**: 6
## juice

- **音标**: dʒu:s
- **释义**: n. 汁, 活力, 体液\nvt. 挤出汁来, 加汁
- **英文释义**: the liquid extracted from fruits, vegetables, or other substances; also used figuratively to mean energy, vitality, or influence
- **词根词缀**: juice→果汁
- **例句**:
- She started her day with a glass of freshly squeezed orange juice.
- The young entrepreneur brought fresh ideas and juice to the stagnant team.
- **考频**: 18
## leak

- **音标**: li:k
- **释义**: n. 漏洞, 漏处, 漏出, 泄漏\nvi. 漏, 泄漏\nvt. 使渗漏
- **英文释义**: An unintended escape of liquid, gas, or information; or the act of such escape or unauthorized disclosure.
- **词根词缀**: leak(词根:泄漏)→泄漏
- **例句**:
- The government launched an investigation after a classified document leak compromised national security.
- A small leak in the pipe caused water to drip steadily onto the basement floor.
- **考频**: 23
