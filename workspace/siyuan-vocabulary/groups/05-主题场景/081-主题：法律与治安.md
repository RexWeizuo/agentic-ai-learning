# 主题：法律与治安

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 法律与治安场景词汇。pact(契约), penalty(处罚), patent(专利), plagiarism(剽窃), patrol(巡逻)。

**8 词**

---

## pact

- **音标**: pækt
- **释义**: n. 契约, 协定, 条约\n[化] 合同
- **英文释义**: A formal agreement between two or more parties, especially governments or organizations.
- **词根词缀**: pact(词根：约定)→协定→条约
- **例句**:
- The two countries signed a non-aggression pact to ensure regional stability.
- Critics argue that the environmental pact lacks enforceable mechanisms and binding targets.
- **考频**: 6
## penalty

- **音标**: 'penәlti
- **释义**: n. 处罚, 刑罚, 罚款, 罚球, 报应, 不利结果, 妨碍\n[经] 罚金(款), 违约金
- **英文释义**: A punishment or disadvantage imposed for breaking a rule, law, or agreement
- **词根词缀**: pen(惩罚)+alty(名词)→惩罚→处罚
- **例句**:
- The company faced a heavy financial penalty for violating environmental regulations.
- In the final minutes of the match, the referee awarded a penalty kick after a foul in the penalty area.
- **考频**: 44
## patent

- **音标**: 'pætnt. 'peitnt
- **释义**: n. 专利权, 许可证, 执照, 专利品, 素质\na. 专利的, 特许的, 显著的, 新奇的\nvt. 取得...的专利权, 请准专利
- **英文释义**: A legal right granted by a government to an inventor, giving them exclusive rights to make, use, or sell an invention for a limited period; also, something obvious or clearly visible.
- **词根词缀**: pat(公开)+ent(形容词)→公开的→专利
- **例句**:
- The company filed a patent for its new battery technology to prevent competitors from copying the design.
- Her exceptional problem-solving ability was a patent strength that immediately impressed the interview panel.
- **考频**: 154
## pirate

- **音标**: 'paiәrәt
- **释义**: n. 海盗, 盗印者, 侵犯专利权者\nvt. 盗印, 掠夺, 翻印\nvi. 做海盗, 从事劫掠
- **英文释义**: A person who attacks and robs ships at sea; also, a person or company that illegally copies and distributes copyrighted material or patents.
- **词根词缀**: pirate(海盗)→海盗
- **例句**:
- The film studio sued the website for pirating its latest blockbuster.
- Historically, pirates operated off the coast of Somalia, hijacking commercial vessels for ransom.
- **考频**: 3
## plagiarism

- **音标**: 'pleidʒiәrizm
- **释义**: n. 剽窃, 抄袭, 剽窃物\n[法] 抄袭, 剽窃, 剽窃作品
- **英文释义**: the act of using someone else's words, ideas, or work without giving proper credit
- **词根词缀**: plagi(词根:斜)+arism(名词后缀)→抄袭
- **例句**:
- Submitting a paper copied from an online source without citation constitutes academic plagiarism.
- The professor accused the student of plagiarism after detecting identical phrasing in two unrelated assignments.
- **考频**: 5
## police

- **音标**: pә'li:s
- **释义**: n. 警察, 警察当局, 治安\nvt. 维持治安, 管辖
- **英文释义**: a civil force responsible for maintaining public order, preventing and detecting crime, and enforcing the law
- **词根词缀**: police(词根:城市)→警察
- **例句**:
- The police launched a nationwide operation to crack down on cybercrime.
- Local authorities decided to police the new regulations more strictly to ensure public safety.
- **考频**: 167

- **释义**: n. 警察\n[化] 淀帚

- **词根词缀**: police(词根:城市)+man(人)→警察

- **考频**: 42
## patrol

- **音标**: pә'trәul
- **释义**: n. 巡逻, 巡逻员, 侦察队\nv. 巡逻, 巡视
- **英文释义**: to move around an area regularly to check for problems or danger, or a group of people doing this
- **词根词缀**: patr(父亲)+ol(名词)→父亲般的守护→巡逻
- **例句**:
- The border patrol conducts routine inspections to prevent illegal crossings.
- Soldiers were ordered to patrol the perimeter of the base every two hours.
- **考频**: 13
