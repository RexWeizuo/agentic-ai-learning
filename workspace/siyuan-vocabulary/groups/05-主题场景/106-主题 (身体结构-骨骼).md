# 主题 (身体结构/骨骼)

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 身体与结构。shape(形状), skeleton(骨骼/梗概), skull(头盖骨), spine(脊柱)。

**4 词**

---

## shape

- **音标**: ʃeip
- **释义**: n. 形状, 形态, 外形, 形式, 身材\nvt. 定形, 使成形, 塑造, 计划, 使符合\nvi. 成形, 形成, 成长\n[计] 形状
- **英文释义**: the external form, outline, or configuration of something; to give form or structure to something
- **词根词缀**: shape(形状)→形状
- **例句**:
- The future of the industry is being shaped by rapid technological advances.
- Her academic performance improved dramatically after she adopted a more disciplined study shape.
- **考频**: 177
## skeleton

- **音标**: 'skelәtn
- **释义**: n. 骨骼, 骨瘦如柴的人, 梗概, 骨干, 残骸\na. 骨骼的, 概略的, 基层的, 骨干的\n[计] 骨架
- **英文释义**: the internal framework of bones supporting the body; a minimal outline or basic structure of something
- **词根词缀**: skelet(干枯)+on→干枯的骨架→骨架
- **例句**:
- The human skeleton provides support, protection, and mobility for the body.
- Before writing the full report, she drafted a skeleton outline to organize her main arguments.
- **考频**: 8
## skull

- **音标**: skʌl
- **释义**: n. 头盖骨, 头脑, 好学生\n[医] 头颅
- **英文释义**: The bony structure that forms the head and encloses the brain
- **词根词缀**: skull(颅骨)→头骨
- **例句**:
- The forensic anthropologist examined the skull to determine the victim's age and sex.
- In anatomy class, students studied the sutures of the skull and their developmental significance.
- **考频**: 2
## spine

- **音标**: spain
- **释义**: n. 背骨, 脊柱, 尖刺\n[医] 脊柱; 棘, 刺; 马蹄嵴
- **英文释义**: The series of vertebrae extending from the skull to the pelvis; also refers to a stiff, sharp projection on a plant or animal.
- **词根词缀**: sp+-ine(…的/似…的/女性)
- **例句**:
- Damage to the spine can result in permanent paralysis.
- The cactus has numerous spines that deter herbivores.
