# 情感、性格、态度

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 描述人的性格特征、心理状态或情感倾向。

**5 词**

---

## coward

- **音标**: 'kauәd
- **释义**: n. 懦弱的人, 胆小的人
- **英文释义**: A person who lacks the courage to face danger, difficulty, or pain.
- **词根词缀**: coward(胆小)→懦夫
- **例句**:
- He was branded a coward for refusing to testify against the corrupt official.
- True leadership demands integrity—not the silence of a coward.
- **考频**: 6
## crazy

- **音标**: 'kreizi
- **释义**: a. 发狂的, 狂热的
- **英文释义**: extremely enthusiastic or obsessed about something; mentally ill or behaving irrationally
- **词根词缀**: craz(碎裂)+y(形容词)→精神碎裂→疯狂的
- **例句**:
- She's absolutely crazy about classical music and attends every major concert in the city.
- The proposal was so impractical that many experts dismissed it as completely crazy.
- **考频**: 21
## cruel

- **音标**: 'kru:әl
- **释义**: a. 残酷的, 令人极痛苦的\n[法] 残忍的, 残酷的
- **英文释义**: causing intense pain or suffering, either physically or emotionally; deliberately harsh or inhumane
- **词根词缀**: cruel(残酷)→残酷的
- **例句**:
- The dictator's cruel policies led to widespread famine and human rights abuses.
- It was cruel of him to mock her public speaking failure in front of the entire class.
- **考频**: 17
## cunning

- **音标**: 'kʌniŋ
- **释义**: a. 狡猾的, 巧妙的, 可爱的\n[法] 狡猾的的, 狡诈的, 熟练的
- **英文释义**: clever in a deceitful or secretive way; skillfully inventive
- **词根词缀**: cunn+-ing(正在…/…的动作/…的)
- **例句**:
- The spy used cunning tactics to evade detection without raising suspicion.
- Her cunning plan to restructure the department was both ingenious and ethically ambiguous.
## curiosity

- **音标**: .kjuәri'ɒsiti
- **释义**: n. 好奇心, 新奇的事物, 珍品
- **英文释义**: a strong desire to know or learn something
- **词根词缀**: cur(关心)+ios(形容词后缀)+ity(名词)→关心的事物→好奇心
- **例句**:
- Her insatiable curiosity about quantum physics led her to pursue a PhD in theoretical physics.
- The museum's collection includes rare artifacts that are both historical treasures and objects of scientific curiosity.
- **考频**: 92
