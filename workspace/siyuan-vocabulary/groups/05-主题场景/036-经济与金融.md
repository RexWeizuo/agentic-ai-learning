# 经济与金融

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 涵盖宏观经济、微观金融及商业交易常用词汇。

**8 词**

---

## economy

- **音标**: i'kɒnәmi
- **释义**: n. 经济, 理财, 节约\n[医] 经济, 整体
- **英文释义**: the system of production, distribution, and consumption of goods and services in a country or region
- **词根词缀**: eco(家)+nom(管理)+y(名词)→家庭管理→经济
- **例句**:
- The government introduced new policies to stimulate the national economy.
- Her careful budgeting reflects a strong personal economy.
- **考频**: 454
## economic

- **音标**: .i:kә'nɒmik
- **释义**: a. 经济上的, 实用的, 节省的\n[经] 经济的
- **英文释义**: relating to the production, distribution, and consumption of goods and services; financially efficient or cost-effective
- **词根词缀**: eco(经济)+nom(管理)+ic(形容词)→经济的
- **例句**:
- The government introduced new economic policies to stimulate growth and reduce unemployment.
- Choosing public transportation is not only environmentally friendly but also an economic choice for budget-conscious commuters.
- **考频**: 715
## economical

- **音标**: .i:kә'nɒmikәl
- **释义**: a. 节俭的, 经济的, 合算的\n[经] 经济的, 节约的
- **英文释义**: Using resources, especially money or fuel, carefully and without waste
- **词根词缀**: eco(家)+nom(管理)+ical(形容词)→家庭管理→经济的
- **例句**:
- Choosing public transportation is more economical than driving a car in congested cities.
- The new engine design is highly economical, reducing fuel consumption by 15% without sacrificing performance.
- **考频**: 12
## economics

- **音标**: .i:kә'nɒmiks
- **释义**: n. 经济学\n[经] 经济学
- **英文释义**: The social science that studies the production, distribution, and consumption of goods and services.
- **词根词缀**: econom+-ics(…学/…学科)
- **例句**:
- Economics helps us understand how governments formulate fiscal policy to control inflation.
- A solid foundation in economics is essential for analyzing global trade patterns and market behavior.
## dividend

- **音标**: 'dividend
- **释义**: n. 被除数, 股利\n[计] 被除数
- **英文释义**: A number that is divided by another number in a division operation; also, a share of a company's profits paid to shareholders.
- **词根词缀**: di(分开)+vid(分)+end(名词)→分出的东西→红利
- **例句**:
- In the division problem 15 ÷ 3 = 5, 15 is the dividend.
- Investors closely monitor quarterly earnings reports to assess whether the company will maintain or increase its dividend.
- **考频**: 9
## discount

- **音标**: 'diskaunt
- **释义**: n. 折扣, 贴现率\nvt. 打折扣\nvi. 贴现
- **英文释义**: a reduction in the usual price of something; or the interest rate used to calculate the present value of a future payment
- **词根词缀**: dis(去掉)+count(计算)→去掉计算→折扣
- **例句**:
- Students are eligible for a 15% discount on all textbooks.
- The central bank lowered the discount rate to stimulate economic growth.
- **考频**: 58
## deposit

- **音标**: di'pɒzit
- **释义**: n. 存款, 定金, 堆积物\nvt. 存放, 堆积\nvi. 沉淀
- **英文释义**: A sum of money placed in a bank account or paid as a guarantee; a natural accumulation of material; to place something for safekeeping or storage; to accumulate or settle gradually.
- **词根词缀**: de(向下)+posit(放置)→放下→存放
- **例句**:
- The buyer must pay a 10% deposit before the contract is signed.
- Sediment deposits on the riverbed are critical indicators of long-term environmental change.
- **考频**: 34
## demand

- **音标**: di'mɑ:nd
- **释义**: n. 要求, 需求, 需要\nv. 要求, 查询
- **英文释义**: n. a strong request or need for something; v. to ask for something firmly or insistently
- **词根词缀**: de(加强)+mand(命令)→命令→要求
- **例句**:
- The rapid growth of e-commerce has increased the demand for skilled logistics professionals.
- The union demanded that management improve workplace safety conditions immediately.
- **考频**: 391
