# 医学、疾病、健康

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 疾病、症状及健康损害相关词汇。

**4 词**

## coronavirus

- **音标**: ,kɔrәnә'vairәs
- **释义**: n. [微]日冕形病毒,冠状病毒
- **英文释义**: A type of virus characterized by crown-like spikes on its surface, some strains of which cause respiratory illnesses in humans, including COVID-19.
- **词根词缀**: corona(冠)+virus(病毒)→冠状病毒
- **例句**:
- The SARS-CoV-2 virus is a novel coronavirus responsible for the global pandemic.
- Researchers are investigating how mutations in the coronavirus genome affect its transmissibility and immune evasion.
- **考频**: 5
## contagious

- **音标**: kәn'teidʒәs
- **释义**: a. 触染性的, 会蔓延的\n[医] (接)触(传)染的
- **英文释义**: capable of being spread by direct or indirect contact, especially referring to diseases; also used figuratively to describe emotions or behaviors that spread easily among people
- **词根词缀**: con(一起)+tag(接触)+ious(形容词)→接触传染的→传染性的
- **例句**:
- The virus is highly contagious and can spread through airborne droplets.
- Her enthusiasm was contagious, quickly energizing the entire team during the brainstorming session.
- **考频**: 2
## cough

- **音标**: kɒf
- **释义**: n. 咳嗽\nvi. 咳嗽\nvt. 咳出
- **英文释义**: a sudden, forceful expulsion of air from the lungs, often to clear the throat or airways; or the act of doing so
- **词根词缀**: cough(咳嗽)→咳嗽
- **例句**:
- She stifled a cough during the silent reading section of the exam.
- The patient began to cough up blood, prompting immediate medical intervention.
- **考频**: 14
## cripple

- **音标**: 'kripl
- **释义**: n. 跛者, 残废\nvt. 使跛, 使成残废, 削弱\na. 跛的, 残废的
- **英文释义**: to severely impair or disable something, especially a person's physical ability or a system's function
- **词根词缀**: cripp(爬行)+le(动词)→使跛行→跛子
- **例句**:
- The economic sanctions crippled the country's import-dependent manufacturing sector.
- A spinal injury in his twenties crippled him, ending his athletic career.
- **考频**: 9
