# 数量增减与强化

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: reduce/reduction 为减少，redundant 为多余/冗余(常考贬义)，reinforce 为加强/增援。

**4 词**

---

## reduce

- **音标**: ri'dju:s
- **释义**: vt. 减少, 分解, 降低, 使衰退, 把...分解, 把...归纳\nvi. 减少, 减肥, 缩小\n[计] 缩小
- **英文释义**: to make something smaller in size, amount, degree, or intensity
- **词根词缀**: re(回)+duce(引导)→引回→减少
- **例句**:
- The government has introduced new policies to reduce carbon emissions.
- Regular exercise and a balanced diet can help reduce the risk of heart disease.
- **考频**: 469
## reduction

- **音标**: ri'dʌkʃәn
- **释义**: n. 减少, 缩影, 变化\n[化] 还原
- **英文释义**: the act or process of making something smaller in size, amount, degree, or number; in chemistry, a reaction involving the gain of electrons or loss of oxygen
- **词根词缀**: re(回)+duct(引导)+ion(名词)→引回→减少
- **例句**:
- The government announced a 15% reduction in corporate tax to stimulate economic growth.
- In redox reactions, reduction always occurs simultaneously with oxidation.
- **考频**: 78
## redundant

- **音标**: ri'dʌndәnt
- **释义**: a. 多余的, 过多的, 冗长的\n[医] 过多的, 多余的
- **英文释义**: Unnecessary, especially through being more than enough or no longer needed
- **词根词缀**: re(再)+dund(波浪)+ant(形容词)→再波浪→多余的
- **例句**:
- The new software update made several legacy features redundant.
- His position was declared redundant after the company restructured its management hierarchy.
- **考频**: 5
## reinforce

- **音标**: .ri:in'fɒ:s
- **释义**: vt. 加强, 增援, 补充\nvi. 求援, 得到增援\nn. 加固材料
- **英文释义**: to strengthen or support something, especially an idea, belief, or behavior, often by adding evidence, encouragement, or material
- **词根词缀**: re(再次)+in(进入)+force(力量)→再次注入力量→加强
- **例句**:
- The study's findings reinforce the importance of early childhood education.
- Teachers should reinforce positive behavior with consistent praise and feedback.
- **考频**: 47
