# ex- 探索/远征

## expedition

- **音标**: .ekspi'diʃәn
- **释义**: n. 远征, 探险队, 迅速
- **英文释义**: A journey undertaken for a specific purpose, especially one involving exploration, research, or military action.
- **词根词缀**: ex(出)+ped(脚)+ition(名词)→出脚→远征
- **例句**:
- The scientific expedition to Antarctica aimed to study climate change effects on glacial retreat.
- Her swift expedition to finalize the contract impressed the board with her efficiency and decisiveness.
- **考频**: 26
## explore

- **音标**: ik'splɒ:
- **释义**: v. 探险, 探测, 探究
- **英文释义**: to travel through or examine an unfamiliar area to learn about it
- **词根词缀**: ex(出)+plor(哭泣)+e→哭出来→探索
- **例句**:
- Scientists will explore the deep ocean trenches using advanced submersibles.
- The essay invites readers to explore the philosophical implications of artificial intelligence.
- **考频**: 126
## excursion

- **音标**: ik'skә:ʒәn
- **释义**: n. 远足, 游览圆, 短程旅行\n[医] 移动, 离轨, 逸出
- **英文释义**: A short journey or trip, typically for pleasure or education.
- **词根词缀**: ex(出)+curs(跑)+ion(名词)→跑出去→远足
- **例句**:
- The class organized an excursion to the ancient temple to study Tang Dynasty architecture.
- Her excursion into experimental surgery was groundbreaking but highly controversial in the medical community.
- **考频**: 10
## export

- **音标**: ik'spɒ:t
- **释义**: n. 输出品, 输出\nvt. 输出, 出口\nvi. 输出物资\n[计] 导出
- **英文释义**: to send goods or services from one country to another for sale or use; also, the goods or services themselves
- **词根词缀**: ex(向外)+port(运送)→向外运送→出口
- **例句**:
- China's rapid economic growth has been fueled by its massive export of manufactured goods.
- The software allows users to export data to CSV format for further analysis in Excel.
- **考频**: 71
