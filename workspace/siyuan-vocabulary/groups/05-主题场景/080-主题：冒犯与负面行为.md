# 主题：冒犯与负面行为

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 表示冒犯、令人不快、淘气或可怕事物的负面词汇。

**5 词**

---

## offend

- **音标**: ә'fend
- **释义**: v. 犯罪, 冒犯, 违反, 进攻
- **英文释义**: to cause someone to feel upset, annoyed, or insulted; to violate a rule, law, or principle
- **词根词缀**: of(加强)+fend(打击)→打击→冒犯
- **例句**:
- His blunt remarks offended several members of the committee during the debate.
- Advertising that exploits children's vulnerabilities offends public morality and may breach regulatory standards.
- **考频**: 25
## offensive

- **音标**: ә'fensiv
- **释义**: a. 令人不快的, 侮辱的, 攻击性的\n[法] 攻击的, 进攻的, 冒犯的
- **英文释义**: causing anger, discomfort, or resentment; insulting or disrespectful; intended to attack or advance aggressively
- **词根词缀**: of(加强)+fend(打击)+ive(形容词)→冒犯的
- **例句**:
- His remarks about gender roles were widely condemned as offensive and outdated.
- The military launched a large-scale offensive to regain control of the border region.
- **考频**: 11
## naughty

- **音标**: 'nɒ:ti
- **释义**: a. 顽皮的, 没规矩的, 不适当的, 下流的
- **英文释义**: disobedient or badly behaved, especially in a playful or mildly offensive way
- **词根词缀**: aug(increase)+h+-ty(性质/状态/…十)
- **例句**:
- The naughty child hid his sister's shoes as a prank.
- Her 'naughty' comment about the CEO's tie raised eyebrows at the formal meeting.
## nasty

- **音标**: 'nɑ:sti
- **释义**: a. 污秽的, 下流的, 险恶的
- **英文释义**: unpleasant, offensive, or morally repugnant; often implying dirtiness, cruelty, or vulgarity
- **词根词缀**: nast(脏)+y(形容词)→肮脏的
- **例句**:
- The committee condemned the politician's nasty remarks about minorities as both racist and unprofessional.
- After days of heavy rain, the flooded basement had a nasty smell and was covered in mold.
- **考频**: 13
## nightmare

- **音标**: 'naitmєә
- **释义**: n. 梦魇, 恶梦, 可怕的事物(或情景、人物)\n[医] 梦魇, 恶梦
- **英文释义**: a frightening or disturbing dream; also used figuratively to describe an extremely unpleasant, distressing, or overwhelming experience
- **词根词缀**: night(夜)+mare(梦魇)→夜间的梦魇→噩梦
- **例句**:
- The soldier still suffers from nightmares about the battlefield long after returning home.
- Negotiating the contract turned into a complete nightmare due to conflicting legal requirements and tight deadlines.
- **考频**: 11
