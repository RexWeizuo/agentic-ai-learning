# 步伐与行走动作

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 描述不同行走状态的动词：stride (大步走), stroll (闲逛), stumble (绊倒), stagger (蹒跚), stalk (潜行/高视阔步)。

**6 词**

---

## step

- **音标**: step
- **释义**: n. 步骤, 步, 步幅, 脚步声, 踏级, 步伐, 短距离, 步态, 手段, 等级\nvt. 踏, 以步测量, 跨步, 使成阶梯状\nvi. 跨步, 轻快地走, 跳舞, 踩, 踏上, 行走\n[计] 步骤
- **英文释义**: A single movement of one foot forward, upward, or sideways; a stage in a process; a measured unit of distance or action.
- **词根词缀**: step→步
- **例句**:
- Taking the first step toward climate action requires political courage and public support.
- The software executes each instruction in a precise sequence of steps.
- **考频**: 268
## stride

- **音标**: straid
- **释义**: n. 大步, 步幅, 步态, 进步\nv. 迈大步走, 跨过, 跨
- **英文释义**: n. a long, decisive step; v. to walk with long, purposeful steps
- **词根词缀**: stride→大步
- **例句**:
- She strode confidently across the stage to accept her award.
- The company has made significant strides in artificial intelligence research over the past five years.
- **考频**: 6
## stroll

- **音标**: strәul
- **释义**: n. 闲逛, 漫步\nv. 闲逛, 漫步
- **英文释义**: to walk in a slow, relaxed, and leisurely way
- **词根词缀**: stroll→漫步
- **例句**:
- After dinner, we took a quiet stroll along the riverbank.
- She strolled through the art gallery, pausing occasionally to admire the paintings.
- **考频**: 10
## stumble

- **音标**: 'stʌmbl
- **释义**: n. 绊倒, 蹒跚而行, 失足\nvt. 使绊倒, 使困惑\nvi. 绊倒, 失足, 失策, 犯错, 蹒跚, 踌躇
- **英文释义**: to trip and nearly fall; to speak or act hesitantly or clumsily; to make a mistake or error
- **词根词缀**: stumb(绊倒)+le(反复)→反复绊倒→绊倒
- **例句**:
- She stumbled over a loose cobblestone while rushing to her graduate seminar.
- The candidate stumbled during the debate when asked about fiscal policy, revealing a lack of preparation.
- **考频**: 10
## stagger

- **音标**: 'stægә
- **释义**: n. 蹒跚, 踌躇\nvi. 蹒跚, 犹豫\nvt. 使摇摆, 使踌躇, 交错, 错开\na. 交错的, 错开的
- **英文释义**: to walk or move unsteadily, as if about to fall; to hesitate or delay in acting; to arrange items alternately or at irregular intervals
- **词根词缀**: stagger→蹒跚,摇晃
- **例句**:
- The exhausted runner staggered across the finish line.
- To avoid overwhelming the system, the company staggered the rollout of the new software across different regions.
- **考频**: 1
## stalk

- **音标**: stɒ:k
- **释义**: n. 茎, 追踪, 高视阔步\nvi. 悄悄靠近, 蹑手蹑脚地走近, 蔓延, 高视阔步\nvt. 追踪, 搜索
- **英文释义**: n. the main stem of a plant; or the act of following someone secretly; vi./vt. to follow or approach stealthily, often with hostile or obsessive intent
- **词根词缀**: stalk→茎,跟踪
- **例句**:
- The botanist carefully examined the stalk of the sunflower to study nutrient transport.
- Security footage showed the suspect stalking the victim for over two hours before the assault.
- **考频**: 5
