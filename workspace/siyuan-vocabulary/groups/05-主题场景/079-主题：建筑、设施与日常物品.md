## mill

- **音标**: mil
- **释义**: n. 压榨机, 磨坊, 制造厂\nvt. 碾磨, 磨细, 搅拌, 使乱转\nvi. 乱转, 被碾磨
- **英文释义**: A building or machine for grinding grain into flour; a factory, especially one producing steel or textiles; to grind, crush, or process something mechanically or repeatedly.
- **词根词缀**: mill(磨)→磨坊
- **例句**:
- The old water-powered mill still stands beside the river, though it no longer grinds wheat.
- After hours of intense negotiation, the proposal was sent back to committee to be milled over once more.
- **考频**: 22
## motel

- **音标**: mәu'tel
- **释义**: n. 汽车旅馆
- **英文释义**: A hotel designed for motorists, typically with rooms accessible directly from a parking area.
- **词根词缀**: motor(汽车)+hotel(旅馆)→汽车旅馆
- **例句**:
- After driving for six hours, we checked into a roadside motel with free Wi-Fi and ample parking.
- The conference organizers arranged discounted rates at a nearby motel to accommodate out-of-town attendees.
- **考频**: 6
