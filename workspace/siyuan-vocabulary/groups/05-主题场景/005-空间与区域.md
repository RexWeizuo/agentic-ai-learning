# 空间与区域

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 表示空间、区域、角度的词汇，arena 指“竞技场/舞台”，acre 是面积单位“英亩”。

**6 词**

---

## angle

- **音标**: 'æŋgl
- **释义**: ==n. 角, 角度, 角落\nvi. 钓鱼, 谋取, 博取, 斜向移动, 转变角度\nvt. 使转动角度, 在...钓鱼, 获取==
- **英文释义**: A figure formed by two rays sharing a common endpoint; a viewpoint or approach; to fish with a hook and line; to attempt to gain something indirectly.
- **词根词缀**: ang(弯曲)+le→弯曲处→角
- **例句**:
- The architect calculated the angle of the roof to ensure proper water drainage.
- She angled for a promotion by volunteering for high-visibility projects.
- **考频**: 16
## area

- **音标**: 'єәriә
- **释义**: ==n. 区域, 面积, 范围, 空地\n[计] 区域==
- **英文释义**: A part of a surface or space; a geographical or functional region; the extent of a two-dimensional surface measured in square units.
- **词根词缀**: area(广阔)→地区
- **例句**:
- The research focuses on a specific area of cognitive neuroscience.
- Urban planners are concerned with the efficient use of limited land area in densely populated cities.
- **考频**: 542
## arena

- **音标**: ә'ri:nә
- **释义**: ==n. 角斗场, 舞台, 场地\n[医] 尿沉渣, 尿沙; 脑沙==
- **英文释义**: A place or setting where important events, conflicts, or competitions occur; also used metaphorically for a field of activity or debate.
- **词根词缀**: aren(sand)+a→sand place→竞技场
- **例句**:
- The international summit became the political arena for resolving the regional crisis.
- In medical diagnostics, calcified particles in urine sediment—known as 'arena'—may indicate chronic kidney disease.
- **考频**: 2
## apartment

- **音标**: ә'pɑ:tmәnt
- **释义**: ==n. 房间, 公寓==
- **英文释义**: A set of rooms for living in, usually on one floor of a building, especially in the US.
- **词根词缀**: apart(分开)+ment→分开的房间→公寓
- **例句**:
- She rents a one-bedroom apartment in downtown Chicago.
- After graduation, he moved into a shared apartment with two classmates to save money.
- **考频**: 74
## acre

- **音标**: 'eikә
- **词性**: n.
- **释义**: ==n. 英亩==
- **英文释义**: n. a unit of area (4840 square yards) used in English-speaking countries\nn. a territory of western Brazil bordering on Bolivia and Peru\nn. a town and port in northwestern Israel in the eastern Mediterranean
- **同义词**: hectare, square mile, square yard
- **词根词缀**: Old English 'æcer'(field, open land)+no productive modern affix→cognate with German 'Acker'(field)→retained specialized meaning as a unit of land area
- **搭配**: acres of land, hundred-acre estate, acre-foot (unit of volume)
- **例句**:
- The farm covers over two hundred acres of fertile land.
- A standard American football field is about 1.32 acres in area.
- **考频**: 15
## apart

- **音标**: ә'pɑ:t
- **释义**: ==adv. 成零碎, 成距离, 分别地, 分离着\na. 分离的==
- **英文释义**: s. remote and separate physically or socially\ns. having characteristics not shared by others\nr. separated or at a distance in place or position or time\nr. not taken into account or excluded from consideration
- **同义词**: separate, aside
- **反义词**: together
- **词根词缀**: a(分开)+part(部分)→分开
- **搭配**: apart from
- **例句**:
- The two houses are 100 meters apart.
- The old building fell apart.
- **考频**: 95
