# 经济/商业/工业

> **类型**: 主题场景

> **学习提示**: 经济与商业场景。infrastructure (基础设施)；inventory (存货清单/详细目录)。

**10 词**

## holder

- **音标**: 'hәuldә
- **释义**: n. 持有人, 所有人, 支持物\n[化] 夹持器; 夹具; 贮罐
- **英文释义**: a person who owns, holds, or is responsible for something; a device that supports, secures, or contains an object
- **词根词缀**: hold(持有)+er(人/物)→持有者
- **例句**:
- The patent holder has exclusive rights to license the invention.
- A test tube holder is essential for safely handling heated glassware in the chemistry lab.
- **考频**: 36
## industrial

- **音标**: in'dʌstriәl
- **释义**: a. 工业的, 供工业用的, 工业高度发展的, 产业的\nn. 工业工人, 工业股票
- **英文释义**: relating to industry, especially large-scale manufacturing or industrial development
- **词根词缀**: in(在…内)+dustr(行业)+ial(形容词)→行业的→工业的
- **例句**:
- The government introduced new policies to boost the industrial sector and attract foreign investment.
- As an industrial powerhouse, Germany relies heavily on advanced manufacturing and engineering exports.
- **考频**: 179
## industrialise

- **音标**: in'dʌstriәlaiz
- **释义**: vi.vt. (使)工业化
- **英文释义**: to develop industry in a country or region; to make something industrial in character or scale
- **词根词缀**: in(在…内)+dustr(行业)+ial(形容词)+ise(动词)→使工业化
- **例句**:
- China began to industrialise rapidly after the economic reforms of 1978.
- Many developing countries struggle to industrialise without compromising environmental sustainability.
- **考频**: 8
## inflation

- **音标**: in'fleiʃәn
- **释义**: n. 胀大, 夸张, 通货膨胀\n[化] 充气吹胀; 膨胀
- **英文释义**: A sustained increase in the general price level of goods and services in an economy over time.
- **词根词缀**: in(向内)+flat(吹)+ion(名词)→吹进去→通货膨胀
- **例句**:

- **考频**: 81
## infrastructure

- **音标**: 'infrәstrʌktʃә
- **释义**: n. 基础结构, 基础设施\n[经] 基础设施
- **英文释义**: The basic physical and organizational structures and facilities (e.g., buildings, roads, power supplies, telecommunications) needed for the operation of a society or enterprise.
- **词根词缀**: infra(在下)+struct(建造)+ure(名词)→下层建筑→基础设施
- **例句**:
- Investing in digital infrastructure is crucial for bridging the urban-rural divide in education.
- The government announced a $2 trillion plan to modernize aging transportation and energy infrastructure.
- **考频**: 59

## inventory

- **音标**: 'invәntәri
- **释义**: n. 详细目录, 存货清单\nvt. 列入详细目录, 清点存货\n[计] 存货清单
- **英文释义**: A complete list of items, especially goods held in stock by a business or assets owned by an organization.
- **词根词缀**: in(进入)+vent(来)+ory(名词)→进入清单→存货
- **例句**:
