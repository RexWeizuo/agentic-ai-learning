# 主题：政治与政府

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 政治场景核心词。parliament(议会), party(政党), policy(政策), premier(总理), poll(民意测验/投票)。

**6 词**

---

## parliament

- **音标**: 'pɑ:lәmәnt
- **释义**: n. 国会, 议会\n[法] 会议, 国会, 议院
- **英文释义**: the national legislative body of a country, especially in the UK and other Commonwealth nations
- **词根词缀**: parl(说话)+ia(名词)+ment(名词)→说话的地方→议会
- **例句**:
- The bill was passed by Parliament after months of debate.
- Members of Parliament are elected to represent the interests of their constituents.
- **考频**: 44
## party

- **音标**: 'pɑ:ti
- **释义**: n. 宴会, 党, 政党, 团体, 当事人, 聚会\nv. 举办聚会
- **英文释义**: A social gathering for celebration or entertainment; a political organization; a person or group involved in a legal or contractual matter.
- **词根词缀**: part(部分)+y(名词)→部分→聚会、党派
- **例句**:
- The diplomatic party included representatives from ten countries, reflecting broad international cooperation.
- In the contract dispute, both parties agreed to resolve the matter through arbitration rather than litigation.
- **考频**: 236
## policy

- **音标**: 'pɒlisi
- **释义**: n. 政策, 方针, 策略, 保险单\n[医] 凭单, 保险单
- **英文释义**: A set of principles or rules guiding decisions and actions, especially by a government, organization, or individual; also, an insurance contract.
- **词根词缀**: policy(词根:政策)→政策
- **例句**:
- The government announced a new environmental policy aimed at reducing carbon emissions by 2030.
- She carefully reviewed the terms of her health insurance policy before signing the agreement.
- **考频**: 358
## premier

- **音标**: 'pri:mjә
- **释义**: n. 总理, 首相\na. 首位的, 最初的
- **英文释义**: the head of government in some parliamentary systems; first or most important
- **词根词缀**: pre(前)+mier(第一)→第一的→首要的
- **例句**:
- The French premier announced new economic reforms during the televised address.
- As the premier institution for legal education in the country, the university attracts top applicants nationwide.
- **考频**: 13
## poll

- **音标**: pәul
- **释义**: n. 投票, 民意测验, 选举投票, 投票数, 一组人中的一个, 头颈和后脑部, 鹦鹉\nvt. 对...进行民意测验, 获得...票, 剪树枝, 轮询\nvi. 投票\na. 剪过毛的, 修过枝的\n[计] 轮询
- **英文释义**: a survey of people's opinions or preferences, typically conducted by asking a representative sample questions; also refers to the act or process of voting
- **词根词缀**: poll(词根:人头)→投票
- **例句**:
- According to the latest poll, over 60% of respondents support the new environmental policy.
- The candidate campaigned vigorously in swing states to sway undecided voters before the final poll.
- **考频**: 48
## patriotic

- **音标**: .peitri'ɒtik
- **释义**: a. 爱国的, 有爱国心的\n[法] 爱国的, 有爱国心的
- **英文释义**: showing love, support, and loyalty for one's country
- **词根词缀**: patri(父亲)+otic(形容词)→像父亲一样爱国的→爱国的
- **例句**:
- Her patriotic speech at the national ceremony inspired widespread pride and unity.
- The film portrays ordinary citizens performing heroic deeds out of deep patriotic conviction.
- **考频**: 2
