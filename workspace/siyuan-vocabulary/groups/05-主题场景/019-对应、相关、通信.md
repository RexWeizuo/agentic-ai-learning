# 对应、相关、通信

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 表示事物之间的匹配、关联或通信往来。

**5 词**

---

## correspond

- **音标**: .kɒri'spɒnd
- **释义**: vi. 符合, 通信, 相当\n[法] 符合, 一致, 相当
- **英文释义**: to have a close similarity or match; to communicate by exchanging letters or emails
- **词根词缀**: cor(共同)+respond(回应)→相互回应→通信
- **例句**:
- The experimental results correspond closely with the theoretical predictions.
- She has corresponded with her pen pal in France for over ten years.
- **考频**: 968
## correspondence

- **音标**: .kɒri'spɒndәns
- **释义**: n. 相符, 通信, 信件\n[医] 对应, 相对
- **英文释义**: 1. the similarity or equivalence between two things; 2. letters or emails exchanged between people
- **词根词缀**: cor(共同)+respond(回应)+ence(名词)→通信
- **例句**:
- There is a close correspondence between the symptoms described in the textbook and those observed in the patient.
- Her academic success was largely attributed to the regular correspondence she maintained with her research supervisor abroad.
- **考频**: 10
## correspondent

- **音标**: .kɒri'spɒndәnt
- **释义**: n. 通讯记者, 通信者\n[经] 客户, 代理商行, 代理银行
- **英文释义**: A person who reports news from a particular place or for a particular organization; or a person or institution with whom one exchanges correspondence, especially in business or banking.
- **词根词缀**: cor(共同)+respond(回应)+ent(名词)→通信者→记者
- **例句**:
- The BBC's Paris correspondent filed a detailed report on the summit negotiations.
- Our bank maintains correspondent relationships with several major financial institutions in Asia.
- **考频**: 5
## correlate

- **音标**: 'kɒrәleit
- **释义**: n. 有相互关系的东西, 相关物\nvt. 使有相互关系\nvi. 相关
- **英文释义**: to have or show a mutual relationship or connection; a thing that is related to another in a particular way
- **词根词缀**: cor(共同)+relate(关联)→相互关联
- **例句**:
- High levels of stress often correlate with poor sleep quality.
- Researchers found that academic performance does not always correlate with socioeconomic background.
- **考频**: 24
## counterpart

- **音标**: 'kauntәpɑ:t
- **释义**: n. 副本, 复本, 配对物, 相应物\n[经] 副本, 正副二份中之一
- **英文释义**: A person or thing that corresponds to or is equivalent to another in a different place, context, or system
- **词根词缀**: counter(对应)+part(部分)→对应的人或物
- **例句**:
- The Chinese delegation held talks with their American counterparts on climate policy.
- In the UK, the Secretary of State for Education serves as the counterpart to the U.S. Secretary of Education.
- **考频**: 60
