# 食物、厨房、烹饪

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 与饮食、厨房设施及食物口感相关。

**4 词**

## cook

- **音标**: kuk
- **释义**: n. 厨子, 厨师\nvt. 烹调, 煮饭, 加热\nvi. 在煮着
- **英文释义**: a person who prepares food; to prepare food by heating it
- **词根词缀**: cook(烹饪)→烹饪
- **例句**:
- The head cook devised an innovative menu for the banquet.
- Don't overheat the sauce—just let it cook gently for ten minutes.
- **考频**: 214
## cupboard

- **音标**: 'kʌpbɒ:d
- **释义**: n. 食橱, 碗柜, 餐具柜
- **英文释义**: A piece of furniture with doors and shelves for storing dishes, food, or other household items.
- **词根词缀**: cup(杯子)+board(板子)→放杯子的板子→橱柜
- **例句**:
- She opened the cupboard to retrieve a clean teacup.
- The recipe called for sugar, which he found in the kitchen cupboard.
- **考频**: 5
## dairy

- **音标**: 'dєәri
- **释义**: n. 牛奶公司, 乳品店, 乳牛场
- **英文释义**: A place where milk and milk products are produced, processed, or sold
- **词根词缀**: dair(牛奶)+y(场所)→牛奶场
- **例句**:
- The dairy supplied organic milk to several local schools.
- She grew up on a family-run dairy in Wisconsin, learning firsthand about sustainable livestock management.
- **考频**: 20
## crisp

- **音标**: krisp
- **释义**: a. 脆的, 新鲜的, 活泼的\nv. (使)烘脆, (使)拳曲, (使)起皱\nn. 松脆物
- **英文释义**: firm and brittle; fresh and invigorating; or a thin, crunchy snack
- **词根词缀**: crisp(卷曲)→卷曲的→脆的
- **例句**:
- The autumn air was crisp and refreshing, perfect for a brisk walk.
- She crumbled the crisp lettuce over the salad to add texture and freshness.
- **考频**: 5
