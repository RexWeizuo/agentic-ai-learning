# 货币、经济、金融

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 经济与金融流通相关，current 可指“流通的”或“当前的”。

**4 词**

---

## currency

- **音标**: 'kʌrәnsi
- **释义**: n. 货币, 通货, 流通, 通用\n[计] 货币, 货币型
- **英文释义**: A system of money in general use in a particular country or context; the state of being current or widely accepted.
- **词根词缀**: curr(跑)+ency(名词)→流通的东西→货币
- **例句**:
- The euro serves as the official currency of 19 European Union member states.
- Her innovative theory gained currency among economists after the publication of her landmark paper.
- **考频**: 35
## credit

- **音标**: 'kredit
- **释义**: n. 信用, 信任, 荣誉, 贷款, 学分\nvt. 归功于, 赞颂, 信任, 相信\n[计] 信用量
- **英文释义**: trust in someone's ability to pay back money or fulfill an obligation; academic unit awarded for completing a course; recognition given for an achievement
- **词根词缀**: cred(相信)+it(名词)→信任→信用
- **例句**:
- Her excellent credit rating enabled her to secure a low-interest mortgage.
- The breakthrough in renewable energy research must be credited to the interdisciplinary team's persistent efforts.
- **考频**: 207
## current

- **音标**: 'kʌrәnt
- **释义**: n. 涌流, 趋势, 流\na. 流通的, 现在的, 当前的, 流行的\n[计] 当前的
- **英文释义**: n. a flow of water, air, or electricity; a prevailing trend or movement; a. existing or happening now; in general use at the present time
- **词根词缀**: curr(跑)+ent(形容词)→正在跑的→当前的
- **例句**:
- The rapid current of technological advancement makes it essential for professionals to engage in lifelong learning.
- Although the current economic policy aims to curb inflation, its long-term impact on employment remains uncertain.
- **考频**: 284
## cumulative

- **音标**: 'kju:mjulәtiv
- **释义**: a. 累积的\n[医] 蓄积的, 累积的
- **英文释义**: Increasing or growing by successive additions; accumulating over time.
- **词根词缀**: cumul(堆积)+ative(形容词后缀)→累积的
- **例句**:
- The cumulative effect of years of poor diet and lack of exercise significantly raises the risk of cardiovascular disease.
- Research shows that sleep deprivation has a cumulative impact on cognitive performance, especially in tasks requiring sustained attention.
- **考频**: 13
