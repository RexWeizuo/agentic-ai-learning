# 主题：交通与运输工具

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 涵盖陆地与水上交通工具，vessel 除“船”外，在生物语境中也指“血管/脉管”。

**5 词**

---

## wagon

- **音标**: 'wægәn
- **释义**: n. 四轮马车, 货车\n[法] 囚车, 马车, 运货车
- **英文释义**: A four-wheeled vehicle, typically pulled by horses, used for transporting goods or people; in modern usage, often refers to a station wagon (a car with an extended rear cargo area).
- **词根词缀**: wagon(马车)→马车
- **例句**:
- The pioneers loaded their belongings onto a covered wagon before heading west across the Great Plains.
- She prefers a station wagon for its spacious interior and practicality on family road trips.
- **考频**: 4
## vehicle

- **音标**: 'vi:ikl
- **释义**: n. 交通工具, 车辆, 传播媒介\n[化] 载体; 运载体; 漆料
- **英文释义**: A means of transporting people or goods, or a medium for conveying ideas, information, or substances.
- **词根词缀**: veh(携带)+icle(小东西)→携带工具→车辆
- **例句**:
- The electric car is an environmentally friendly vehicle that reduces carbon emissions.
- In scientific research, a virus can serve as a vehicle for delivering genetic material into cells.
- **考频**: 130
## trolley

- **音标**: 'trɒli
- **释义**: n. 电车, 手推车, 台车\nvt. 用手推车运\nvi. 乘电车
- **英文释义**: A wheeled vehicle, especially a streetcar powered by overhead electricity or a small cart used for carrying goods or food.
- **词根词缀**: 词根不确定，可能源自方言
- **例句**:
- The commuters rushed to board the trolley before it pulled away from the stop.
- She trolleyed the heavy groceries from the car to the kitchen.
- **考频**: 4
## tram

- **音标**: træm
- **释义**: n. 电车轨道, 煤车, 纬纱, 纬丝\nvt. 用煤车运载\nvi. 乘电车
- **英文释义**: A rail vehicle that runs on tracks in urban areas, typically powered by electricity.
- **词根词缀**: tram→有轨电车
- **例句**:
- The students took the tram to campus to avoid traffic congestion.
- She lives near a tram line, making her daily commute both convenient and eco-friendly.
- **考频**: 4
## vessel

- **音标**: 'vesl
- **释义**: n. 船, 容器, 脉管\n[医] 管, 脉管, (容)器
- **英文释义**: A hollow container for holding something; a ship or large boat; or a tube in the body that carries blood or lymph.
- **词根词缀**: vess(容器)+el(小)→小容器→船只
- **例句**:
- The research team used a specialized vessel to transport the fragile biological samples across the ocean.
- During the surgery, the surgeon carefully ligated the damaged blood vessel to prevent excessive bleeding.
- **考频**: 21
