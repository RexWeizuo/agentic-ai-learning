## astronaut

- **音标**: 'æstrәnɒ:t
- **释义**: ==n. 太空旅行者, 宇航员\n[法] 美国太空人==
- **英文释义**: A person trained to travel and work in space.
- **词根词缀**: astro(星星)+naut(航海者)→星际航海者→宇航员
- **例句**:
- The astronaut conducted a series of experiments aboard the International Space Station.
- Becoming an astronaut requires not only exceptional physical fitness but also advanced expertise in engineering and science.
- **考频**: 15
## astronomy

- **音标**: ә'strɒnәmi
- **释义**: ==n. 天文学==
- **英文释义**: The scientific study of celestial objects, space, and the physical universe beyond Earth's atmosphere.
- **词根词缀**: astro(星星)+nomy(规律)→星星的规律→天文学
- **例句**:
- Astronomy has revealed that our solar system is just a tiny part of a vast, expanding universe.
- Students pursuing physics often take advanced courses in astronomy to deepen their understanding of cosmology and astrophysics.
- **考频**: 8
