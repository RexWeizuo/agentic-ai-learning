# 主题：职业/身份

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 具体职业词汇：carpenter(木匠), cashier(出纳员), chef(厨师), chemist(化学家/药剂师)。

**4 词**

---

## carpenter

- **音标**: 'kɑ:pintә
- **释义**: n. 木匠
- **英文释义**: a skilled worker who makes and repairs things made of wood, such as buildings, furniture, or ships
- **词根词缀**: carpent(车)+er(人)→造车的人→木匠
- **例句**:
- The carpenter installed custom wooden cabinets in the newly renovated kitchen.
- To ensure structural integrity, the architect consulted a licensed carpenter before finalizing the roof design.
- **考频**: 9
## cashier

- **音标**: kæ'ʃiә
- **释义**: n. 出纳员\n[经] 司库, 出纳员, 收支员
- **英文释义**: A person who handles cash transactions, such as receiving payments and giving change, typically in a store, bank, or restaurant.
- **词根词缀**: cash(现金)+ier(人)→管现金的人→出纳员
- **例句**:
- The cashier mistakenly gave me too much change, and I returned the excess immediately.
- At the bank, the cashier verified my identity before processing the large cash withdrawal.
- **考频**: 4
## chef

- **音标**: ʃef
- **释义**: n. 厨师
- **英文释义**: A professional cook, especially one in charge of a kitchen or restaurant.
- **词根词缀**: 来自法语，意为首领→厨师长
- **例句**:
- The chef meticulously plated the dish with artistic precision.
- Aspiring chefs often train for years under renowned culinary experts before opening their own restaurants.
- **考频**: 30
## chemist

- **音标**: 'kemist
- **释义**: n. 化学家, 药剂师\n[化] 化学家; 化学师; 化学工作者; 药剂师; 药房
- **英文释义**: A scientist who studies or works in chemistry; also, especially in British English, a person qualified to prepare and sell medicines.
- **词根词缀**: chem(化学)+ist(人)→化学家
- **例句**:
- The chemist synthesized a novel compound that showed promising anti-cancer activity.
- She consulted the local chemist for advice on over-the-counter allergy medication.
- **考频**: 12
