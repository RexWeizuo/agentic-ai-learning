## cube

- **音标**: kju:b
- **释义**: n. 立方体, 立方\n[机] 立方体, 立方
- **英文释义**: A three-dimensional solid object bounded by six equal square faces.
- **词根词缀**: cube(立方体)→立方体
- **例句**:
- The volume of a cube is calculated by raising the length of one side to the third power.
- In the geometry problem, the cube was inscribed in a sphere, requiring precise spatial reasoning.
- **考频**: 11
## cylinder

- **音标**: 'silindә
- **释义**: n. 圆筒, 圆筒状物, 汽缸, 柱面\n[计] 柱面
- **英文释义**: A three-dimensional geometric shape with two parallel circular bases connected by a curved surface, or a hollow tube-like object used in engines, hydraulics, or computing (e.g., disk storage).
- **词根词缀**: cylind+-er(…的人/…物/更…)
- **例句**:
- The engine's power output depends on the displacement volume of each cylinder.
- In hard disk drives, data is stored on concentric tracks across multiple platters, and a single cylinder consists of all tracks that can be accessed without moving the actuator arm.
## curve

- **音标**: kә:v
- **释义**: n. 曲线, 弯曲, 曲线球\nvt. 弯, 使弯曲\nvi. 成曲形
- **英文释义**: A line or shape that bends smoothly without angles; to bend or cause to bend.
- **词根词缀**: curve(弯曲)→曲线
- **例句**:
- The graph shows a steep upward curve in global temperatures over the past decade.
- The pitcher threw a sharp curve that fooled the batter into swinging late.
- **考频**: 19
## crystal

- **音标**: 'kristl
- **释义**: n. 水晶, 水晶装饰品, 结晶\na. 水晶的, 水晶一样的, 透明的
- **英文释义**: A clear, solid substance formed by the regular arrangement of atoms or molecules; also refers to a transparent, high-quality glass object or a decorative item made of such material.
- **词根词缀**: crystall(冰)→水晶
- **例句**:
- The mineralogist examined the crystal under polarized light to study its internal structure.
- Her argument was praised for its crystal clarity and logical rigor.
