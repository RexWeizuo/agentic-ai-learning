# 犯罪、刑罚、法律

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 法律与司法系统核心词汇，custody 可指“拘留”或“监护权”。

**6 词**

---

## crime

- **音标**: kraim
- **释义**: n. 犯罪, 罪行, 罪恶\n[法] 犯罪, 罪, 罪恶
- **英文释义**: An illegal act punishable by law; a serious offense against morality or justice.
- **词根词缀**: crim(罪行)+e→罪行
- **例句**:
- The government has launched a nationwide campaign to reduce violent crime.
- She testified in court as a key witness in the murder crime case.
- **考频**: 202
## criminal

- **音标**: 'kriminәl
- **释义**: n. 罪犯, 犯人, 刑事\na. 犯了罪的, 刑事的, 有罪的
- **英文释义**: a person who has committed a crime or been legally convicted of one
- **词根词缀**: crim(罪行)+in+al(形容词)→犯罪的
- **例句**:
- The police apprehended the criminal after a nationwide manhunt.
- Not all suspects are criminals; guilt must be proven beyond reasonable doubt in court.
- **考频**: 116
## convict

- **音标**: kәn'vikt
- **释义**: n. 囚犯, 罪犯\nvt. 宣告有罪, 使知罪
- **英文释义**: n. a person found guilty of a crime and sentenced to prison; vt. to declare someone guilty of a crime in a court of law
- **词根词缀**: con(加强)+vict(征服)→彻底征服→定罪
- **例句**:
- The court convicted him of fraud after reviewing overwhelming evidence.
- Many convicts participate in rehabilitation programs to prepare for reintegration into society.
- **考频**: 10
## conviction

- **音标**: kәn'vikʃәn
- **释义**: n. 定罪, 信服, 坚信\n[法] 定罪, 证明有罪, 判罪
- **英文释义**: 1. A formal declaration by a court that someone is guilty of a criminal offense; 2. A firmly held belief or opinion.
- **词根词缀**: con(加强)+vict(征服)+ion(名词)→彻底征服→定罪
- **例句**:
- Her conviction on charges of fraud was upheld by the appellate court.
- He spoke with such conviction that even skeptical listeners were persuaded.
- **考频**: 15
## culprit

- **音标**: 'kʌlprit
- **释义**: n. 犯人, 罪犯, 刑事被告\n[法] 犯罪者, 犯人, 罪犯
- **英文释义**: a person who has committed a crime or is accused of one
- **词根词缀**: culp(罪)+rit(名词)→有罪的人→罪犯
- **例句**:
- The police identified the culprit through CCTV footage and forensic evidence.
- Although no one was arrested, investigators believe the culprit is someone familiar with the building's security system.
- **考频**: 1
## custody

- **音标**: 'kʌstәdi
- **释义**: n. 监护, 拘留, 监禁\n[经] 保管, 照顾, 保护
- **英文释义**: legal responsibility for and control over a person or property, especially a child or assets
- **词根词缀**: custod(看守)+y(名词)→看守→监护
- **例句**:
- The court awarded sole custody of the children to the mother after the divorce.
- As the custodian of the company's financial records, she bore full custody and accountability for their accuracy.
- **考频**: 9
