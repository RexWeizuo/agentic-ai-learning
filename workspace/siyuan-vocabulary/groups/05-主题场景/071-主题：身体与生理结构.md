# 主题：身体与生理结构

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 描述人体器官、生理状态及身体特征的词汇。

**6 词**

---

## muscle

- **音标**: 'mʌsl
- **释义**: n. 肌肉, 臂力\n[医] 肌
- **英文释义**: A band of tissue that connects to bones and contracts to produce movement or maintain posture.
- **词根词缀**: mus(老鼠)+cle(小)→小老鼠→肌肉
- **例句**:
- Regular strength training helps build muscle mass and improve overall physical endurance.
- The surgeon carefully avoided damaging the facial muscles during the reconstructive procedure.
- **考频**: 69
## muscular

- **音标**: 'mʌskjulә
- **释义**: a. 强壮的, 肌肉发达的, 有力的\n[医] 肌的; 肌肉发达的
- **英文释义**: having well-developed muscles; strong and powerful
- **词根词缀**: muscul(肌肉)+ar(形容词)→肌肉的
- **例句**:
- His muscular frame made him a natural choice for the rugby team.
- The sculpture depicted a muscular deity wielding a thunderbolt, symbolizing divine strength.
- **考频**: 7
## nerve

- **音标**: nә:v
- **释义**: n. 精神, 勇气, 叶脉, 神经\nvt. 鼓起勇气
- **英文释义**: courage or mental strength needed to face danger, pain, or difficulty
- **词根词缀**: nerv(神经)+e→神经
- **例句**:
- It took a lot of nerve to challenge the professor's controversial theory in front of the entire seminar.
- She didn't have the nerve to submit her groundbreaking but unorthodox research for peer review.
- **考频**: 32
## nervous

- **音标**: 'nә:vәs
- **释义**: a. 神经紧张的, 不安的, 神经的\n[医] 神经的; 神经质的, 神经过敏的
- **英文释义**: feeling anxious, worried, or uneasy about something that is going to happen
- **词根词缀**: nerv(神经)+ous(形容词)→神经的→紧张的
- **例句**:
- She felt nervous before delivering her presentation at the international conference.
- Many students become nervous when facing open-ended essay questions in the postgraduate entrance exam.
- **考频**: 64
## obesity

- **音标**: әu'bi:siti
- **释义**: n. 肥胖\n[医] 肥胖, 多脂
- **英文释义**: Excessive accumulation of body fat that poses a risk to health
- **词根词缀**: ob(过度)+es(吃)+ity(名词)→肥胖
- **例句**:
- The rising prevalence of childhood obesity has become a major public health concern.
- Obesity is strongly associated with an increased risk of type 2 diabetes, cardiovascular disease, and certain cancers.
- **考频**: 117
## oral

- **音标**: 'ɒ:rәl
- **释义**: n. 口试\na. 口头的, 口述的, 口部的
- **英文释义**: spoken rather than written; relating to the mouth
- **词根词缀**: or(嘴)+al(形容词后缀)→嘴的→口头的
- **例句**:
- All candidates must complete both a written exam and an oral examination.
- She delivered her presentation with confidence during the oral defense of her thesis.
- **考频**: 10
