# 地理与空间方位

> **类型**: 主题

## latitude

- **音标**: 'lætitju:d
- **释义**: n. 纬度, 范围\n[化] 宽容度; 伟度
- **英文释义**: the angular distance of a place north or south of the earth's equator, measured in degrees; also, freedom from strict rules or limits
- **词根词缀**: lat(词根:宽)+itude(名词)→宽度→纬度
- **例句**:
- The city lies at a latitude of 40 degrees north, giving it four distinct seasons.
- Academic research often requires considerable latitude in methodology and interpretation.
- **考频**: 6
## longitude

- **音标**: 'lɒndʒitju:d
- **释义**: n. 经度, 经线\n[电] 经度
- **英文释义**: The angular distance of a place east or west of the prime meridian, measured in degrees.
- **词根词缀**: long(长)+itude(名词)→经度
- **例句**:
- The ship's position was determined by its latitude and longitude.
- Accurate navigation requires precise measurement of both latitude and longitude.
- **考频**: 1
## lunar

- **音标**: 'lu:nә
- **释义**: a. 阴历的, 月的, 微亮的(指光)\n[医] 月的, 银的
- **英文释义**: relating to the Moon or based on the Moon's orbit around Earth, especially in contrast to solar time
- **词根词缀**: lun(月亮)+ar(形容词)→月亮的
- **例句**:
- The lunar calendar is widely used in traditional Chinese festivals such as Spring Festival.
- Astronauts collected lunar rocks during the Apollo 11 mission to study the Moon's geological history.
- **考频**: 4
## landscape

- **音标**: 'lændskeip
- **释义**: n. 风景, 山水, 风景画\nvi. 从事景观美化\nvt. 美化...景观\n[计] 横向
- **英文释义**: natural scenery of an area, especially in terms of its visual appearance; also, the activity or practice of modifying outdoor spaces for aesthetic or functional improvement
- **词根词缀**: land(词根:土地)+scape(词根:景色)→土地景色→风景
- **例句**:
- The painting captures the serene landscape of the Scottish Highlands with remarkable detail.
- Urban planners collaborated with landscape architects to landscape the derelict industrial site into a sustainable public park.
- **考频**: 59
## lane

- **音标**: lein
- **释义**: n. 小路, 巷, 弄, 单行道
- **英文释义**: a narrow road or path, especially one bordered by trees or buildings; also a designated section of a road for traffic moving in one direction
- **词根词缀**: lane(词根:小路)→小路
- **例句**:
- The ancient stone lane wound through the quiet village, lined with centuries-old cottages.
- During rush hour, each lane on the motorway was congested with vehicles moving at crawling speed.
- **考频**: 44
