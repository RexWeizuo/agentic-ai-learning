# 航空与交通

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 交通与航空场景词汇，aisle 指“过道/走廊”，ambulance 为“救护车”。

**7 词**

---

## aerial

- **音标**: 'єәriәl
- **词性**: ['adj.', 'n.']
- **释义**: ==a. 空中的, 航空的, 空气的, 空想的\nn. 天线==
- **英文释义**: a. existing or living or growing or operating in the air
- **同义词**: aerobic, airborne, ethereal
- **反义词**: terrestrial, grounded
- **词根词缀**: aer(空气)+ial→空中的
- **搭配**: aerial photograph, aerial survey, aerial antenna
- **例句**:
- The drone captured stunning aerial views of the coastline.
- She installed a new aerial on the roof to improve TV reception.
- **考频**: 3
## aeroplane

- **音标**: 'єәrәplein
- **词性**: n.
- **释义**: ==n. 飞机\n[机] 飞机==
- **英文释义**: n an aircraft that has a fixed wing and is powered by propellers or jets
- **同义词**: airplane, plane, aircraft
- **词根词缀**: aero-(air, atmosphere)+plane-(flat surface, from Greek 'platus' meaning broad)→a vehicle with broad, wing-like surfaces designed to fly through the air→aircraft with fixed wings
- **搭配**: board an aeroplane, pilot an aeroplane, aeroplane engine
- **例句**:
- The aeroplane took off smoothly from Heathrow Airport.
- She has never flown in an aeroplane before.
- **考频**: 5
## aircraft

- **音标**: 'єәkræft
- **词性**: n.
- **释义**: ==n. 航空器, 飞机\n[机] 航空器==
- **英文释义**: n. a vehicle that can fly
- **同义词**: airplane, aeroplane, plane
- **词根词缀**: air(空气)+craft(技艺、工具，古英语cræft→指人造的、有特定功能的装置)→能于空气中飞行的人造装置→航空器
- **搭配**: military aircraft, civilian aircraft, aircraft carrier
- **例句**:
- The airport handles over 1,000 commercial aircraft every day.
- Unmanned aircraft are increasingly used for surveillance and delivery.
- **考频**: 48
## airline

- **音标**: 'єәlain
- **词性**: n.
- **释义**: ==n. 航线, 航线的设备, 航空公司==
- **英文释义**: n. a hose that carries air under pressure\nn. a commercial enterprise that provides scheduled flights for passengers
- **同义词**: air carrier, aviation company, carrier
- **词根词缀**: air(空气，天空)+line(线路，航线)→字面指‘空中线路’，引申为运营空中运输服务的公司→航空公司
- **搭配**: major airline, budget airline, airline ticket
- **例句**:
- She works for a major international airline.
- The airline announced new direct flights to Tokyo next summer.
- **考频**: 76
## airport

- **音标**: 'єәpɒ:t
- **词性**: n.
- **释义**: ==n. 飞机场\n[机] 航空站, 机场==
- **英文释义**: n. an airfield equipped with control tower and hangars as well as accommodations for passengers and cargo
- **同义词**: aerodrome, airfield, aviation hub
- **词根词缀**: air(空气，航空)+port(港口，停泊处)→航空器停泊/起降的场所→飞机场
- **搭配**: airport security, airport terminal, arrive at the airport
- **例句**:
- We arrived at the airport two hours before our international flight.
- The new high-speed rail connects the city center directly to the airport.
- **考频**: 117
## aisle

- **音标**: ail
- **词性**: n.
- **释义**: ==n. 走廊, 侧廊, 过道\n[机] 走道==
- **英文释义**: n. a long narrow passage (as in a cave or woods)\nn. passageway between seating areas as in an auditorium or passenger vehicle or between areas of shelves of goods as in stores\nn. part of a church divided laterally from the nave proper by rows of pillars or columns
- **同义词**: passage, corridor, walkway
- **词根词缀**: Old French 'ele' (wing of a church, lateral division)+influence of 'isle' (due to spelling folk etymology)→no true native English root/affix; modern form reflects orthographic assimilation rather than morphological derivation→n. a longitudinal passageway, especially between rows of seats or in a church
- **搭配**: down the aisle, aisle seat, center aisle
- **例句**:
- Please walk down the aisle to your seat.
- She walked down the aisle in a beautiful white gown.
- **考频**: 9
## ambulance

- **音标**: 'æmbjulәns
- **释义**: ==n. 救护车\n[医] 救护车==
- **英文释义**: A vehicle equipped for transporting sick or injured people to a hospital.
- **词根词缀**: ambul(行走)+ance→移动医院的车辆→救护车
- **例句**:
- An ambulance rushed the unconscious cyclist to the nearest emergency department.
- When her asthma attack worsened, she called 911 and an ambulance arrived within five minutes.
