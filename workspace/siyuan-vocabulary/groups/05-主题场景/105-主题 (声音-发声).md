# 主题 (声音/发声)

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 声音与发声动作。sound(声音/听起来), song(歌曲), sing(唱), shout(呼喊), sigh(叹息)。

**5 词**

---

## sound

- **音标**: saund
- **释义**: n. 声音, 语音, 吵闹, 声调, 听力范围, 探条, 海峡\na. 健全的, 可靠的, 合理的, 健康的, 彻底的, 资金充实的\nadv. 彻底地, 充分地\nvi. 发出声音, 回响, 测深, 试探, 听起来\nvt. 使发声, 宣告, 听诊, 测...深, 试探\n[计] 声音
- **英文释义**: n. vibrations transmitted through air or other media that can be heard; a. free from defect or error, reliable, healthy, or thorough; vi./vt. to emit, perceive, or measure sound, or to test opinions or conditions
- **词根词缀**: sound→声音
- **例句**:
- The sudden sound of breaking glass startled everyone in the room.
- Her argument is logically sound and supported by empirical evidence.
- **考频**: 329
## song

- **音标**: sɒŋ
- **释义**: n. 歌, 曲, 鸣声, 歌唱, 歌曲, 诗歌
- **英文释义**: A short musical composition with lyrics, typically performed by singing.
- **词根词缀**: song(词根)→歌曲
- **例句**:
- Her debut album features ten original songs that blend folk and jazz elements.
- The poet transformed the ancient legend into a lyrical song echoing themes of loss and resilience.
- **考频**: 45
## sing

- **音标**: siŋ
- **释义**: vi. 唱, 唱歌, 演唱, 鸣, 啼\nvt. 唱, 歌颂\nn. 嗖嗖声
- **英文释义**: to produce musical sounds with the voice, often in a structured or melodic way; to make a high-pitched, vibrating sound (e.g., of wind or metal)
- **词根词缀**: sing(唱)→唱
- **例句**:
- She can sing opera in five languages, a skill that impressed the panel at her graduate school interview.
- The wire fence began to sing in the strong wind, a phenomenon frequently cited in physics-based reading passages.
- **考频**: 26
## shout

- **音标**: ʃaut
- **释义**: n. 呼喊, 喊声\nvi. 呼喊, 喊叫, 嚷\nvt. 高喊
- **英文释义**: to speak or cry out loudly, often to express strong emotion, attract attention, or communicate over distance
- **词根词缀**: shout(喊叫)→喊叫
- **例句**:
- She shouted for help when she realized she was locked in the basement.
- The coach shouted instructions to the players during the tense final minutes of the match.
- **考频**: 21
## sigh

- **音标**: sai
- **释义**: n. 叹息\nvi. 叹息, 渴望\nvt. 叹息着说
- **英文释义**: a long, deep audible breath expressing sadness, relief, tiredness, or longing
- **词根词缀**: sigh(叹息)→叹息
- **例句**:
- She let out a weary sigh after finishing the grueling exam.
- He sighed wistfully as he gazed at the photograph of his childhood home.
- **考频**: 7
