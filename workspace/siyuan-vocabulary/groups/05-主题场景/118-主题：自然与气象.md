# 主题：自然与气象

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 描述自然现象与气候带，torrent 常比喻“倾盆大雨”或“信息的洪流”。

**5 词**

---

## torrent

- **音标**: 'tɒ:rәnt
- **释义**: n. 奔流, 倾盆大雨, 倾注, 迸发
- **英文释义**: a rapid, powerful, and often overwhelming flow of something (e.g., water, words, or information)
- **词根词缀**: torr(湍流)+ent(名词)→急流
- **例句**:
- The speaker unleashed a torrent of criticism against the proposed policy.
- Heavy rain turned the mountain trail into a dangerous torrent that swept away several hikers' equipment.
- **考频**: 4
## waterfall

- **音标**: 'wɒtәfɒ:l
- **释义**: n. 瀑布
- **英文释义**: a stream of water falling vertically from a height, often over rocks or a cliff
- **词根词缀**: water(水)+fall(落下)→瀑布
- **例句**:
- The hikers paused to admire the thundering waterfall cascading down the granite face.
- Environmental scientists warned that dam construction upstream could severely disrupt the ecosystem of the ancient waterfall.
- **考频**: 1
## typhoon

- **音标**: tai'fu:n
- **释义**: n. 台风
- **英文释义**: A powerful tropical cyclone occurring in the northwestern Pacific Ocean.
- **词根词缀**: typhoon
- **例句**:
- The typhoon forced the evacuation of over 100,000 residents along the southeastern coast.
- Climate scientists warn that rising sea temperatures may increase the frequency and intensity of typhoons.
## tropic

- **音标**: 'trɒpik
- **释义**: n. 回归线
- **英文释义**: Either of the two parallels of latitude (23.5°N or 23.5°S) where the sun appears directly overhead at noon on the solstices.
- **词根词缀**: trop(转)+ic(形容词)→太阳转向的地方→热带
- **例句**:
- The Amazon rainforest lies largely within the tropic of Capricorn.
- Climate change is causing shifts in weather patterns even in regions near the tropics.
- **考频**: 8
## tropical

- **音标**: 'trɒpikl
- **释义**: a. 热带的, 热情的\n[医] 热带的
- **英文释义**: relating to the tropics, the region between the Tropic of Cancer and the Tropic of Capricorn; characterized by high temperatures and often high humidity
- **词根词缀**: trop(转)+ical(形容词)→热带的
- **例句**:
- The Amazon rainforest is one of the most biodiverse tropical ecosystems on Earth.
- Her speech was delivered with such tropical enthusiasm that it instantly energized the entire audience.
- **考频**: 30
