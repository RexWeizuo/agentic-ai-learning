# 代表、反映与参考

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: represent 为代表，reflection 为反映/反思，reference 为参考/提及。

**4 词**

---

## represent

- **音标**: .repri'zent
- **释义**: vt. 表现, 表示, 描绘, 讲述, 代表, 象征, 回忆, 再赠送, 再上演\nvi. 提出异议
- **英文释义**: to serve as a symbol or sign of something; to act on behalf of someone or something; to depict or describe in a particular way
- **词根词缀**: re(再次)+present(呈现)→再次呈现→代表
- **例句**:
- The dove represents peace in many cultures.
- She was chosen to represent the university at the international academic conference.
- **考频**: 147
## representative

- **音标**: .repri'zentәtiv
- **释义**: n. 代表, 众议员, 典型\na. 描写的, 表现的, 代理的, 代表的, 代议制的, 典型的
- **英文释义**: a person chosen or appointed to act or speak for others; something that typifies a group or category
- **词根词缀**: re(再次)+present(呈现)+ative(形容词/名词)→代表的
- **例句**:
- The union elected a representative to negotiate with management on behalf of all employees.
- This case study is not representative of the broader population, so generalizing the findings would be unwarranted.
- **考频**: 48
## reflection

- **音标**: ri'flekʃәn
- **释义**: n. 反映, 沉思, 映像, 想法, 责难\n[计] 反射
- **英文释义**: the act of thinking seriously and carefully about something; an image seen in a mirror or on a shiny surface; a sign that something exists or is happening
- **词根词缀**: re(回)+flect(弯曲)+ion(名词)→弯回→反射
- **例句**:
- Her essay was a thoughtful reflection on the ethical implications of artificial intelligence.
- The calm lake provided a perfect reflection of the surrounding mountains, symbolizing clarity and self-awareness.
- **考频**: 36
## reference

- **音标**: 'refәrәns
- **释义**: n. 参考, 索引, 参照\nvt. 给...加上参考资料\nvt. 引用\nvi. 引用\n[计] 引用
- **英文释义**: a source of information used to support or verify something; the act of citing a source or directing attention to something
- **词根词缀**: re(再)+fer(带)+ence(名词)→再带来→参考
- **例句**:
- The author provides extensive references at the end of the paper to support her arguments.
- When writing an academic essay, always reference your sources to avoid plagiarism.
- **考频**: 68
