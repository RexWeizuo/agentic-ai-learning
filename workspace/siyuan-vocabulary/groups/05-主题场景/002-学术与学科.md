## academic

- **音标**: .ækә'demik
- **词性**: ['adj.', 'n.']
- **释义**: ==a. 学院的, 学术的, 不切实际的\nn. 大学生, 大学教师, 学者, 学会会员==
- **英文释义**: a. associated with academia or an academy\ns. hypothetical or theoretical and not expected to produce an immediate or practical result\ns. marked by a narrow focus on or display of learning especially its trivial aspects
- **同义词**: scholarly, educational
- **反义词**: practical
- **词根词缀**: academ(学院)+ic→学院的
- **搭配**: academic achievement, academic year, academic integrity
- **例句**:
- Her academic achievements earned her a full scholarship to Oxford.
- He has been an academic at Harvard for over twenty years, specializing in medieval history.

## academy

- **释义**: ==n. 学院, 院校, 学会==

- **同义词**: institute, college, school
- **词根词缀**: akadēm- (from Akadēmeia, the garden near Athens where Plato taught) + -y (noun-forming suffix denoting 'place or institution') → a place of learning or scholarly association → 'institution for specialized education or research'
- **搭配**: military academy, academy award, academy of sciences
- **例句**:
- She enrolled in a prestigious dance academy in New York.
