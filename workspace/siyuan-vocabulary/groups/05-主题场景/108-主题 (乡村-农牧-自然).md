# 主题 (乡村/农牧/自然)

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 乡村与农牧场景。rural(乡村的), scenery(风景), sheep(羊), shepherd(牧羊人), shed(车棚/小屋)。

**5 词**

---

## rural

- **音标**: 'ruәrәl
- **释义**: a. 乡下的, 田园的, 乡村风味的\n[法] 农村的, 乡村的, 有关农业的
- **英文释义**: relating to the countryside rather than towns or cities
- **词根词缀**: rur(乡村)+al(形容词后缀)→乡村的
- **例句**:
- The government has launched new policies to boost economic development in rural areas.
- Students from rural backgrounds often face greater challenges in accessing higher education.
- **考频**: 61
## scenery

- **音标**: 'si:nәri
- **释义**: n. 风景, 景色, 舞台布景
- **英文释义**: the natural features of a landscape, especially when picturesque; also, painted or constructed backgrounds used on stage
- **词根词缀**: scen(场景)+ery(名词)→风景
- **例句**:
- The breathtaking scenery of the Swiss Alps attracted countless photographers and hikers.
- The play's emotional impact was heightened by minimalist scenery that shifted subtly with each scene.
- **考频**: 15
## sheep

- **音标**: ʃi:p
- **释义**: n. 羊, 胆小者
- **英文释义**: A domesticated ruminant mammal raised for wool, meat, or milk; figuratively, a timid or easily led person.
- **词根词缀**: sheep(羊)→羊
- **例句**:
- The farmer herded the sheep across the pasture to new grazing land.
- In group discussions, he rarely voiced his own opinion and often just followed others like a sheep.
- **考频**: 12
## shepherd

- **音标**: 'ʃepәd
- **释义**: n. 牧羊人, 牧羊犬, 牧师, 保护者\nvt. 看守, 领导, 指导, 牧羊
- **英文释义**: a person who tends sheep; a leader or guide, especially of people or ideas
- **词根词缀**: shepherd(牧羊人)→牧羊人
- **例句**:
- The shepherd guided his flock across the rugged hills at dawn.
- As a seasoned policymaker, she has shepherded several major education reforms through Congress.
- **考频**: 2
## shed

- **音标**: ʃed
- **释义**: n. 车棚, 小屋, 脱落之物, 分水岭\nvt. 使流出, 放射, 脱落, 散发, 摆脱\nvi. 流出, 散布, 脱落
- **英文释义**: n. a small, simple building; vt. to get rid of something unwanted or unnecessary, or to emit or discharge something (e.g., light, tears, skin)
- **词根词缀**: shed(脱落)→脱落
- **例句**:
- The old shed behind the garage collapsed after the heavy rain.
- She struggled to shed her ingrained habits during the intensive training program.
- **考频**: 49
