# 主题：情绪、心理与性格

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: 描述内心情感、心理状态及性格特征的词汇。

**7 词**

## mood

- **音标**: mu:d
- **释义**: n. 心情, 气氛, 生气, 基调\n[医] 心境
- **英文释义**: a temporary state of mind or feeling
- **词根词缀**: mood(情绪)→情绪
- **例句**:
- Her cheerful mood lifted everyone's spirits during the meeting.
- The film's eerie mood was established through dim lighting and unsettling music.
- **考频**: 83
## miserable

- **音标**: 'mizәrәbl
- **释义**: a. 悲惨的；痛苦的；卑鄙的
- **英文释义**: wretched and unhappy; causing great discomfort or distress
- **词根词缀**: miser(可怜)+able(形容词)→悲惨的
- **例句**:
- The refugees lived in miserable conditions with little food or shelter.
- After failing the exam for the third time, she felt utterly miserable and questioned her academic abilities.
- **考频**: 20
## misery

- **音标**: 'mizәri
- **释义**: n. 痛苦, 悲惨, 不幸, 穷困
- **英文释义**: great suffering, either physical or mental; extreme distress or unhappiness
- **词根词缀**: miser(可怜)+y(名词)→痛苦
- **例句**:
- The refugees endured months of misery in the overcrowded camp.
- Poverty and lack of healthcare condemned many families to a life of chronic misery.
- **考频**: 21
## mourn

- **音标**: mɒ:n
- **释义**: v. 哀悼, 忧伤, 服丧
- **英文释义**: to feel or express deep sorrow, especially for someone who has died
- **词根词缀**: mourn(悲伤)→悲伤
- **例句**:
- The entire nation mourned the loss of the beloved leader.
- She still mourns her father's death years after his passing.
- **考频**: 2
## obsession

- **音标**: әb'seʃәn
- **释义**: n. 困扰, 困扰人的情绪\n[医] 强迫观念
- **英文释义**: an idea or desire that constantly occupies a person's mind, often causing anxiety or distress
- **词根词缀**: ob(加强)+sess(坐)+ion(名词)→坐立不安→痴迷
- **例句**:
- Her obsession with perfection led to chronic stress and burnout.
- The novel explores the protagonist's dangerous obsession with revenge, blurring the line between justice and pathology.
- **考频**: 11
## nostalgic

- **音标**: nɒs'tældʒik
- **释义**: a. 乡愁的, 怀旧的\n[医] 怀乡病的
- **英文释义**: feeling sentimental longing or wistful affection for the past
- **词根词缀**: nost(回家)+alg(痛)+ic(形容词)→思乡的
- **例句**:
- Listening to those old jazz records always makes her feel nostalgic.
- The film's sepia-toned cinematography evokes a deeply nostalgic atmosphere, resonating with audiences' collective memory of the 1950s.
- **考频**: 1
## naive

- **音标**: nɑ:'i:v
- **释义**: a. 天真的, 纯真的, 幼稚的
- **英文释义**: lacking experience, wisdom, or judgment; overly trusting or simple
- **词根词缀**: naiv(天然)+e→天真的
- **例句**:
- Her naive belief that everyone was honest led to a serious financial loss.
- The policy was criticized as naive, ignoring the complex geopolitical realities.
- **考频**: 9
