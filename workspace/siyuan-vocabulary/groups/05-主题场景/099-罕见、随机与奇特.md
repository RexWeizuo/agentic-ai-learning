# 罕见、随机与奇特

> **类型**: 主题

> **学习提示**: rare/rarely 表稀有，queer 表奇怪/反常，random 表随机/随意。

**4 词**

---

## rare

- **音标**: rєә
- **释义**: a. 稀罕的, 罕有的, 珍奇的, 稀薄的, 半熟的, 非常好的\nadv. 非常
- **英文释义**: not common or frequent; occurring infrequently
- **词根词缀**: rare(稀)→稀有的
- **例句**:
- Fossilized dinosaur eggs are extremely rare and highly valuable to paleontologists.
- It is rare for such a comprehensive policy reform to be implemented without significant public debate.
- **考频**: 84
## rarely

- **音标**: 'rєәli
- **释义**: adv. 很少地, 罕有地
- **英文释义**: not often; almost never
- **词根词缀**: rare(稀)+ly(副词)→很少
- **例句**:
- She rarely misses a deadline, which makes her one of the most reliable team members.
- In academic writing, passive voice is rarely used in the introduction section, as authors typically emphasize agency and clarity.
- **考频**: 94
## queer

- **音标**: kwiә
- **释义**: n. 怪人, 同性恋者\na. 奇怪的, 不舒服的, 可疑的\nvt. 搞糟
- **英文释义**: n. a person who identifies as LGBTQ+; a. strange or unusual, or causing discomfort or doubt
- **词根词缀**: qu+-eer(从事…的人)
- **例句**:
- Her queer theory seminar challenged traditional notions of gender and identity.
- The sudden change in the contract terms struck him as queer, prompting a thorough legal review.
## random

- **音标**: 'rændәm
- **释义**: n. 随意, 随机\na. 任意的, 随便的, 胡乱的, 随机的\nadv. 胡乱地
- **英文释义**: made, done, or occurring without method or conscious decision; lacking pattern or predictability
- **词根词缀**: random(随机)→随机的
- **例句**:
- The study used a random sampling method to ensure unbiased representation of the population.
- Don't just pick a random answer on the exam—review the evidence in the passage first.
- **考频**: 42
