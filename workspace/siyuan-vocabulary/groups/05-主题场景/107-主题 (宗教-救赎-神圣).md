## sacred

- **音标**: 'seikrid
- **释义**: a. 神圣的, 献给上帝的, 庄严的, 祭祀的\n[法] 神圣的, 不可侵犯的
- **英文释义**: dedicated to a religious purpose or a deity; holy and deserving reverence
- **词根词缀**: sacr(神圣)+ed(形容词后缀)→神圣的
- **例句**:
- The temple is considered a sacred site by millions of pilgrims.
- Freedom of speech is regarded as a sacred principle in democratic societies.
- **考频**: 9

- **词根词缀**: salv(救)+ation(名词后缀)→拯救
- **例句**:
