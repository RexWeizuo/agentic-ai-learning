# 词族/主题 (空间/范围)

> **类型**: 词族

> **学习提示**: 空间与范围。spacecraft(航天器), spacious(宽敞的), span(跨度), sphere(球体/领域)。

**5 词**

---

## space

- **音标**: speis
- **释义**: n. 位置, 空间, 距离, 太空, 空白, 间隔, (期刊等的)篇幅\nvt. 隔开, 分隔\nvi. 留间隔\n[计] 空白, 空格校验
- **英文释义**: a continuous area or expanse which is free, available, or unoccupied; the physical universe beyond Earth's atmosphere; or the distance or interval between objects
- **词根词缀**: space→空间
- **例句**:
- The article was cut to fit the limited space available in the journal.
- Scientists study cosmic radiation to better understand the conditions of outer space.
- **考频**: 523
## spacecraft

- **音标**: 'speiskræft
- **释义**: n. 航天飞船
- **英文释义**: A vehicle designed to travel in outer space, especially one carrying crew or instruments.
- **词根词缀**: space(空间)+craft(船)→太空船
- **例句**:
- The spacecraft successfully entered Mars' orbit after a seven-month journey.
- Scientists are developing a reusable spacecraft to reduce the cost of interplanetary missions.
- **考频**: 13
## spacious

- **音标**: 'speiʃәs
- **释义**: a. 广大的, 宽广的
- **英文释义**: Having ample space; roomy and uncluttered
- **词根词缀**: space(空间)+ious(形容词)→空间大的→宽敞的
- **例句**:
- The newly renovated library boasts a spacious reading hall with natural lighting and ergonomic furniture.
- Compared to the cramped dormitory rooms, the graduate student apartments are notably more spacious and well-equipped.
- **考频**: 15
## span

- **音标**: spæn
- **释义**: n. 指距, 全长, 跨距, 一段时间, 小范围\nvt. 以手指测量, 跨越, 架设, 持续
- **英文释义**: n. the distance or length between two points; a period of time; vt. to extend across or over something, to measure with the handspan, or to last throughout a period
- **词根词缀**: span→跨度
- **例句**:
- The bridge has a main span of over 1,200 meters.
- Her influence on educational policy spanned three consecutive administrations.
- **考频**: 45
## sphere

- **音标**: sfiә
- **释义**: n. 球, 球面, 球体, 天体, 地球仪, 范围\nvt. 包围, 使成球体, 放入球内
- **英文释义**: A perfectly round three-dimensional geometrical object; also used metaphorically to denote a particular area or domain of activity, influence, or expertise.
- **词根词缀**: sphere→球体
- **例句**:
- The Earth is often modeled as a sphere in introductory physics courses.
- Her research lies at the intersection of ethics and artificial intelligence, occupying a unique sphere within contemporary philosophy.
- **考频**: 11
