# 词族 as- 前缀派生词

> **类型**: 词族

> **学习提示**: 包含多组 as- 前缀（ad-变体）派生的词族，注意名词后缀 -ment, -ance, -tion 的规律。

## assume

- **音标**: /əˈsuːm/
- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: ==假设，假定；承担；呈现==
- **英文释义**: to suppose something to be the case without proof; to take on responsibility
- **同义词**: presume, suppose, take on, adopt
- **反义词**: doubt, question, reject
- **词根词缀**: as(加强)+sume(拿)→拿住→假设；承担
- **搭配**: assume responsibility, assume control, wrongly assume
- **例句**:
- I assume you have read the report.
- She assumed responsibility for the project.
- **考频**: 134
