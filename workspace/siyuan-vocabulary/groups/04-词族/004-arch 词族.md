# arch 词族

> **类型**: 词族

> **学习提示**: 共享词根 arch（拱/古老/建筑），注意 architect（建筑师）与 architecture（建筑学）的后缀区别。

**4 词**

---

## arch

- **音标**: ɑ:tʃ
- **释义**: ==n. 拱门, 拱形, 足弓\nvt. 使成弓形\nvi. 拱起, 成弓形\na. 主要的, 调皮的, 傲慢无礼的, 狡猾的==
- **英文释义**: A curved structure that supports weight above an opening; or to bend into a curved shape.
- **词根词缀**: arch(弓)→拱形
- **例句**:
- The ancient Roman aqueduct features a series of graceful stone arches.
- She arched her eyebrows skeptically when he claimed to have finished the thesis early.
- **考频**: 3
## architect

- **音标**: 'ɑ:kitekt
- **释义**: ==n. 建筑师, 设计者, 缔造者==
- **英文释义**: a person who designs buildings or other structures, or who plans and directs the development of a complex system or project
- **词根词缀**: archi(主要的)+tect(建造者)→建筑师
- **例句**:
- As the chief architect of the company's digital transformation, she restructured the entire IT infrastructure.
- The ancient Roman architect Vitruvius emphasized harmony, proportion, and durability in building design.
- **考频**: 31
## architecture

- **音标**: 'ɑ:kitektʃә
- **释义**: ==n. 建筑学, 建筑式样\n[计] 体系结构==
- **英文释义**: the art and science of designing and constructing buildings; also, the structural design of a system, especially in computing
- **词根词缀**: arch(chief)+tect(builder)+ure(noun)→chief builder's work→建筑学
- **例句**:
- The Gothic architecture of Notre-Dame Cathedral reflects medieval religious ideals and engineering ingenuity.
- Modern software development increasingly emphasizes modular architecture to enhance scalability and maintainability.
- **考频**: 38
## archeology

- **音标**: ɑ:ki'ɔlәdʒi
- **释义**: ==n. 考古学==
- **英文释义**: The scientific study of human history and prehistory through the excavation of sites and analysis of artifacts and other physical remains.
- **词根词缀**: arch(rule/chief/ancient)+e+-ology(…学/…学科)
- **例句**:
- Archaeology has provided crucial evidence about the daily life of ancient civilizations.
- Her dissertation in archaeology focused on Bronze Age burial practices in the Aegean region.
