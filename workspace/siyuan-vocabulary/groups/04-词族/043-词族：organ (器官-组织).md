# 词族：organ (器官/组织)

> **类型**: 词族

> **学习提示**: 由 organ 派生出的词族，涵盖器官、有机体、组织等含义。

**5 词**

---

## organ

- **音标**: 'ɒ:gәn
- **释义**: n. 风琴, 器官, 元件, 机构, 机关\n[电] 风琴
- **英文释义**: A musical instrument played with keys and pipes that produce sound by air; or a specialized structure in a living organism that performs a specific function; also used metaphorically for an official publication or a formal body.
- **词根词缀**: organ(工具/器官)→器官
- **例句**:
- The symphony concluded with a powerful rendition of Bach's Toccata and Fugue on the pipe organ.
- The liver is a vital organ responsible for detoxification, protein synthesis, and biochemical production.
- **考频**: 30
## organic

- **音标**: ɒ:'gænik
- **释义**: a. 器官的, 有机的, 组织的, 根本的\n[医] 器官的, 有生命的, 有机的, 器质的
- **英文释义**: relating to living organisms; derived from or characteristic of living things; not synthetic or artificial
- **词根词缀**: organ(器官)+ic(形容词后缀)→器官的→有机的
- **例句**:
- The study focused on organic compounds found in plant tissues.
- She advocates for organic farming methods to reduce environmental impact and improve soil health.
- **考频**: 80
## organisation

- **音标**: ,ɔ: ^әnaizeiʃən; - ni'z-
- **释义**: n. 组织, 团体, 体制, 编制
- **英文释义**: a group of people who work together for a shared purpose, or the way in which something is structured or arranged
- **词根词缀**: organ(工具)+is(使)+ation(名词)→组织
- **例句**:
- The United Nations is an international organisation dedicated to maintaining peace and security.
- Effective organisation of resources is crucial for the success of large-scale research projects.
- **考频**: 32
## organise

- **音标**: 'ɒ:gәnaiz
- **释义**: vt. 组织, 有机化, 给予生机
- **英文释义**: to arrange or plan something systematically; to form into a coherent and functional whole
- **词根词缀**: organ(工具)+ise(动词后缀)→组织
- **例句**:
- The student union will organise a series of seminars on academic integrity ahead of the final exam period.
- Effective time management requires students to organise their study schedule around key deadlines and revision priorities.
- **考频**: 13
## organism

- **音标**: 'ɒ:gәnizm
- **释义**: n. 生物, 有机体, 社会组织\n[医] 生物[体],[有]机体
- **英文释义**: A living thing, such as an animal, plant, or microorganism, capable of carrying out life processes.
- **词根词缀**: organ(器官)+ism(名词后缀)→有机体
- **例句**:
- The human body hosts trillions of microorganisms, many of which are essential for maintaining a healthy organism.
- Ecologists study how each organism interacts with its environment and other species within the ecosystem.
- **考频**: 37
