# 词族 mechan (机械)

> **类型**: 词族

> **学习提示**: 机械、机构及其派生词

**5 词**

## machine

- **音标**: mә'ʃi:n
- **释义**: n. 机器, 机械装置, 机构, 自动售货机, 机械般工作的人\nvt. 以机器制造
- **英文释义**: A device that uses power to perform a specific task, especially one with moving parts.
- **词根词缀**: machin(机器)+e→机器
- **例句**:
- The factory upgraded its production line by introducing advanced CNC machines.
- She worked so tirelessly during the deadline week that her colleagues jokingly called her a 'thinking machine.'
- **考频**: 317
## machinery

- **音标**: mә'ʃi:nәri
- **释义**: n. 机器, 机械装置, 机构\n[化] 机械
- **英文释义**: machines and mechanical equipment collectively, especially those used for a particular industrial or technical purpose
- **词根词缀**: machin(机器)+ery(集合名词)→机器(总称)
- **例句**:
- The factory upgraded its machinery to improve production efficiency.
- Bureaucratic machinery often slows down policy implementation.
- **考频**: 38
## mechanic

- **音标**: mi'kænik
- **释义**: n. 机械工, 技工\na. 手工的
- **英文释义**: a person who repairs and maintains machinery, especially engines and vehicles
- **词根词缀**: mechan(机械)+ic(人)→机械师
- **例句**:
- The mechanic diagnosed the engine problem within minutes and replaced the faulty fuel injector.
- Unlike modern computerized systems, older cars often required a highly skilled mechanic to adjust the carburetor manually.
- **考频**: 30
## mechanical

- **音标**: mi'kænikәl
- **释义**: a. 机械的, 机械性的, 力学的\n[医] 机械的, 力学的
- **英文释义**: relating to machines, machinery, or the principles of mechanics; involving routine physical action rather than thought
- **词根词缀**: mechan(机械)+ical(形容词)→机械的
- **例句**:
- The robot's movements were precise but entirely mechanical, lacking any sign of autonomous decision-making.
- In biomechanics, researchers study the mechanical properties of human tissues to improve prosthetic design.
- **考频**: 40
## mechanism

- **音标**: 'mekәnizm
- **释义**: n. 机械, 机构, 结构, 机理, 技巧\n[化] 机理; 历程; 机构
- **英文释义**: A system of parts working together in a machine or natural process; the way something works or is done.
- **词根词缀**: mechan(机械)+ism(名词)→机制
- **例句**:
- The mechanism by which enzymes catalyze biochemical reactions remains a central topic in molecular biology.
- Effective policy implementation requires not only clear objectives but also a well-designed administrative mechanism.
- **考频**: 48
