# 词族 uni- (一，统一)

> **类型**: 词族

> **学习提示**: uni- 表示“单一”，universal 引申为“普遍的、宇宙的”，unity 强调“团结、一致性”。

**4 词**

---

## unify

- **音标**: 'ju:nifai
- **释义**: v. 统一, 使成一体
- **英文释义**: to make different things or groups into a single, cohesive whole
- **词根词缀**: uni(一)+fy(使)→使统一
- **例句**:
- The new policy aims to unify the fragmented regulatory frameworks across EU member states.
- Scientists hope that the theory will unify quantum mechanics and general relativity into a single coherent model.
- **考频**: 14
## unit

- **音标**: 'ju:nit
- **释义**: n. 单位, 分队, 部队, 单元, 部件, 装置\na. 单位的, 单元的\n[计] 单元常数; 部件
- **英文释义**: A single thing or person regarded as a separate entity; a distinct part of a larger whole, such as a group, system, or device.
- **词根词缀**: uni(一)+t(名词)→单位
- **例句**:
- The hospital has a specialized unit for treating infectious diseases.
- In computer architecture, the arithmetic logic unit (ALU) performs mathematical and logical operations.
- **考频**: 66
## unity

- **音标**: 'ju:niti
- **释义**: n. 一致, 联合, 单一(性), 个体\n[经] 统一
- **英文释义**: the state of being joined as a whole or acting together harmoniously
- **词根词缀**: uni(一)+ty(名词)→统一
- **例句**:
- The success of the peace agreement depends on national unity and political compromise.
- Scientists emphasized the unity of natural laws across different physical phenomena.
- **考频**: 22
## universal

- **音标**: .ju:ni'vә:sl
- **释义**: a. 全世界的, 普遍的, 宇宙的, 通用的\nn. 一般概念
- **英文释义**: existing or happening everywhere or in all cases; applicable to all people, things, or situations
- **词根词缀**: uni(一)+vers(转)+al(形容词)→转向一的→普遍的
- **例句**:
- The principle of human rights is considered a universal value across cultures.
- A universal translator remains a theoretical device, though advances in AI are bringing it closer to reality.
- **考频**: 48
