# 词族：plex/plic (折叠，复杂，完成)

> **类型**: 词族

> **学习提示**: plex/plic 表示“折叠”，complex 是“复杂的/综合体”，complicate 是“使复杂化”。

**4 词**

---

## complete

- **音标**: kәm'pli:t
- **释义**: a. 完全的, 十足的, 完成的\nvt. 完成, 完工, 使圆满
- **英文释义**: having all necessary parts; finished; to finish something successfully
- **词根词缀**: com(加强)+ple(填充)+te(动词)→填充满→完成
- **例句**:
- The project was completed ahead of schedule, demonstrating the team's efficiency.
- She is a complete professional—competent, ethical, and deeply committed to her work.
- **考频**: 488
## complex

- **音标**: kәm'pleks
- **释义**: n. 综合体, 情结, 络合物\na. 复杂的, 组合的
- **英文释义**: Having many interconnected parts; not simple. Also: a group of related buildings or structures; a psychological pattern of emotions and ideas; a chemical compound formed by coordination bonds.
- **词根词缀**: com(一起)+plex(编织)→编织在一起→复杂的
- **例句**:
- The human brain is one of the most complex organs in the body.
- She has an inferiority complex that stems from childhood experiences.
- **考频**: 159
## complicate

- **音标**: 'kɒmplikeit
- **释义**: vt. 弄复杂, 使错综, 使恶化\nvi. 变复杂
- **英文释义**: to make something more complex, difficult, or confusing
- **词根词缀**: com(一起)+plic(折叠)+ate(动词)→折叠在一起→使复杂
- **例句**:
- The sudden resignation of the project manager complicated the already tight deadline.
- Introducing new regulations at this stage will only complicate the approval process.
- **考频**: 5
## complicated

- **音标**: 'kɒmplikeitid
- **释义**: a. 复杂的\n[医] 并发的
- **英文释义**: difficult to understand or deal with because of having many parts or aspects
- **词根词缀**: com(一起)+plic(折叠)+ated(形容词)→折叠在一起的→复杂的
- **例句**:
- The pathophysiology of this disease is highly complicated, involving multiple organ systems.
- She found the legal contract so complicated that she consulted a lawyer before signing it.
- **考频**: 88
