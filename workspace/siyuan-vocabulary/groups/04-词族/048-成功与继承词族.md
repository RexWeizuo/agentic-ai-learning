# 成功与继承词族

> **类型**: 词族

> **学习提示**: 围绕 success 派生的动词、形容词和名词，注意 succession (连续/继承) 与 successor (继承者) 的拼写。

**6 词**

---

## succeed

- **音标**: sәk'si:d
- **释义**: vi. 成功, 继承, 继续\nvt. 继承, 接替
- **英文释义**: to achieve a desired aim or result; to follow in order or take over a position or title
- **词根词缀**: suc(在…之下)+ceed(走)→成功
- **例句**:
- After years of persistent research, she finally succeeded in developing a novel vaccine.
- He will succeed his father as CEO of the company next year.
- **考频**: 137
## success

- **音标**: sәk'ses
- **释义**: n. 成功, 成就, 胜利
- **英文释义**: the accomplishment of an aim or purpose; the attainment of fame, wealth, or social status
- **词根词缀**: suc(在…之下)+cess(走)→成功
- **例句**:
- Her promotion to CEO was widely regarded as a testament to her professional success.
- Success in graduate entrance exams requires not only diligence but also strategic preparation.
- **考频**: 417
## successful

- **音标**: sәk'sesful
- **释义**: a. 成功的, 一帆风顺的, 顺利的\n[经] 成功的
- **英文释义**: having achieved a desired aim or result; accomplishing something effectively
- **词根词缀**: suc(在…之下)+cess(走)+ful(形容词)→成功的
- **例句**:
- Her successful negotiation secured the company a major contract.
- To become a successful researcher, one must combine creativity with rigorous methodology.
- **考频**: 221
## succession

- **音标**: sәk'seʃәn
- **释义**: n. 连续, 继承权, 继位, 演替, 地层次序\n[法] 继承, 继承权, 继位
- **英文释义**: the sequence in which one person or thing follows another, especially in office, rank, or inheritance
- **词根词缀**: suc(在…之下)+cess(走)+ion(名词)→连续
- **例句**:
- The peaceful succession of power after the president's resignation demonstrated the strength of the country's democratic institutions.
- In ecology, primary succession refers to the gradual establishment of plant and animal communities on bare rock or volcanic soil.
- **考频**: 12
## successive

- **音标**: sәk'sesiv
- **释义**: a. 继承的, 连续的\n[经] 接续承运人; 连续的
- **英文释义**: Following one after another in order without interruption
- **词根词缀**: suc(在…之下)+cess(走)+ive(形容词)→连续的
- **例句**:
- The company reported profits for five successive years.
- Successive governments have failed to address the housing crisis effectively.
- **考频**: 11
## successor

- **音标**: sәk'sesә
- **释义**: n. 继承者, 接任者\n[计] 后继
- **英文释义**: a person or thing that follows another in office, position, or function
- **词根词缀**: suc(在…之下)+cess(走)+or(人)→继承者
- **例句**:
- After the CEO's retirement, her deputy was named the official successor.
- The new software version is designed to be a more efficient successor to the legacy system.
- **考频**: 10
