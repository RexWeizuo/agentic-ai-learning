# extend 延伸词族

**4 词**

## extend

- **音标**: ik'stend
- **释义**: v. 扩充, 延伸, 伸展, 扩大\n[计] 扩展
- **英文释义**: to make something longer, larger, or more comprehensive
- **词根词缀**: ex(向外)+tend(伸展)→向外伸展→延伸
- **例句**:
- The government decided to extend the deadline for tax filing by two weeks.
- This software update will extend the device's battery life and add new security features.
- **考频**: 145
## extension

- **音标**: ik'stenʃәn
- **释义**: n. 延长, 扩充, 范围, 扩展名\na. 伸缩的\n[计] 扩展名, 扩充名
- **英文释义**: the act of making something longer, larger, or more comprehensive; in computing, a suffix added to a filename to indicate its format or type
- **词根词缀**: ex(向外)+tens(伸展)+ion(名词)→向外伸展→延伸
- **例句**:
- The university granted her a six-month extension to complete her thesis.
- Files with the '.pdf' extension can be opened using most document readers.
- **考频**: 42
## extensive

- **音标**: ik'stensiv
- **释义**: a. 广的, 广泛的, 多方面的\n[经] 广大的, 扩大的
- **英文释义**: covering a large area, number, or range; thorough and comprehensive
- **词根词缀**: ex(向外)+tens(伸展)+ive(形容词)→向外伸展的→广泛的
- **例句**:
- The research team conducted an extensive survey of economic indicators across 30 countries.
- She has an extensive knowledge of classical literature, which greatly enriched her thesis on Renaissance humanism.
- **考频**: 51
## extent

- **音标**: ik'stent
- **释义**: n. 范围, 程度, 区域\n[计] 范围
- **英文释义**: the degree or range to which something happens, exists, or is true
- **词根词缀**: ex(向外)+tent(伸展)→伸展出去的范围→程度
- **例句**:
- The extent of the environmental damage was not fully understood until years later.
- To what extent do social media platforms influence political opinions?
- **考频**: 88
