# apply 词族

> **类型**: 词族

> **学习提示**: apply 的派生词，注意 appliance 意为“家用电器/器械”，与 application（应用/申请）区分。

**4 词**

---

## apply

- **音标**: ә'plai
- **释义**: ==vt. 涂, 应用\nvi. 申请, 适用==
- **英文释义**: v. be pertinent or relevant or applicable\nv. ask (for something)\nv. refer (a word or name) to a person or thing\nv. apply oneself to
- **同义词**: use, utilize
- **词根词缀**: ap(加强)+ply(折叠)→一再折叠到→应用
- **搭配**: apply for, apply to
- **例句**:
- I applied for the position.
- The rule applies to everyone.
- **考频**: 265
## application

- **音标**: .æpli'keiʃәn
- **释义**: ==n. 应用, 申请, 志愿书, 应用程序\n[计] 应用, 应用程序==
- **英文释义**: The use of something for a particular purpose; a formal request for something, such as a job or admission; a computer program designed for a specific function.
- **词根词缀**: applic(应用)+ation→申请；应用
- **例句**:
- Her application to the graduate program was accepted after careful review.
- Modern smartphones support thousands of applications, from productivity tools to entertainment platforms.
- **考频**: 149
## applicable

- **音标**: /əˈplɪkəbl/
- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: ==可适用的，可应用的；合适的==
- **英文释义**: relevant or appropriate; able to be applied
- **同义词**: relevant, appropriate, suitable, pertinent
- **反义词**: inapplicable, irrelevant, unsuitable
- **词根词缀**: ap(加强)+plic(折叠)+able→可折叠到…上→可适用的
- **搭配**: directly applicable, applicable to, generally applicable
- **例句**:
- This rule is not applicable to foreigners.
- The principle is applicable in many situations.
- **考频**: 20
## appliance

- **音标**: ә'plaiәns
- **释义**: ==n. 器械, 用具, 应用\n[医] 矫正器; 器==
- **英文释义**: A device or piece of equipment designed for a specific practical purpose, especially in the home or in medical treatment.
- **词根词缀**: appli(应用)+ance→应用器具→器具
- **例句**:
- Modern kitchen appliances such as microwaves and dishwashers have greatly improved household efficiency.
- The orthodontist fitted her with a dental appliance to gradually correct her misaligned teeth.
- **考频**: 35
