# 词族 limit (限制与边缘)

> **类型**: 词族

> **学习提示**: 界限、限制及边缘概念的近义与派生词

**5 词**

---

## limit

- **音标**: 'limit
- **释义**: n. 界限, 边界, 限度, 极限, 限制\nvt. 限制, 限定
- **英文释义**: a point or level beyond which something does not or may not extend or pass
- **词根词缀**: limit(界限)→界限
- **例句**:
- The speed limit on this highway is 60 mph.
- The government imposed strict limits on foreign investment to protect domestic industries.
- **考频**: 252
## limitation

- **音标**: .limi'teiʃәn
- **释义**: n. 限制, 缺陷, 限额\n[医] 限界, 限制, 限度
- **英文释义**: a restriction on the size, amount, or scope of something; a weakness or deficiency
- **词根词缀**: limit(界限)+ation(名词)→界限→限制
- **例句**:
- The study's findings are subject to several limitations, including a small sample size and lack of long-term follow-up.
- While the new policy aims to promote innovation, its strict regulatory limitations may hinder entrepreneurial flexibility.
- **考频**: 41
## limited

- **音标**: 'limitid
- **释义**: a. 有限制的, 有限的, 有限责任的\nn. 特别快车
- **英文释义**: having a restricted or specified extent, capacity, or scope; not unlimited
- **词根词缀**: limit(界限)+ed(形容词)→有界限的→有限的
- **例句**:
- The company is a limited liability corporation, protecting shareholders from personal financial liability.
- Due to limited funding and time, the research team could only examine a small sample size.
- **考频**: 167
## margin

- **音标**: 'mɑ:dʒin
- **释义**: n. 页边的空白, 边缘, 界限, 余裕, 差数, 差额, 保证金\nvt. 加边于, 加旁注于\n[计] 页边距
- **英文释义**: The space between the text and the edge of a page; also, a limit, boundary, or small amount of difference or surplus.
- **词根词缀**: margin→边缘
- **例句**:
- The editor asked the author to reduce the font size to fit more content within the margin.
- The company reported a profit margin of 12%, slightly above the industry average.
- **考频**: 27
## marginal

- **音标**: 'mɑ:dʒinәl
- **释义**: a. 边缘的, 最低限度的, 有旁注的\n[医] 缘的
- **英文释义**: relating to or situated at a margin; minimal or barely sufficient; annotated with notes in the margin
- **词根词缀**: margin(边缘)+al(形容词)→边缘的
- **例句**:
- The company's profit margin was marginal this quarter, raising concerns about its long-term viability.
- The marginal notes in the textbook helped clarify the author's complex argument on epistemology.
- **考频**: 11
