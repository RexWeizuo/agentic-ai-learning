## engage

- **音标**: in'geidʒ
- **释义**: vi. 答应, 从事, 交战\nvt. 使忙碌, 雇佣, 预定, 使从事于, 使参加
- **英文释义**: to become involved in an activity; to hire or secure the services of someone; to attract and hold attention
- **词根词缀**: en(使)+gage(承诺)→使承诺→从事
- **例句**:
- The government has engaged several international experts to advise on climate policy.
- Students must actively engage with the material rather than passively memorize it to succeed in the exam.
- **考频**: 196
## engagement

- **音标**: in'geidʒdmәnt
- **释义**: n. 诺言, 约会, 婚约, 交战\n[医] 衔接
- **英文释义**: a formal agreement to do something or a commitment, especially to marry or to participate in an activity
- **词根词缀**: engage(从事)+ment(名词)→从事→订婚
- **例句**:
- Her engagement to the prominent human rights lawyer was announced last month.
- Sustained civic engagement is essential for the health of any democracy.
- **考频**: 24
## engine

- **音标**: 'endʒin
- **释义**: n. 引擎, 发动机, 机车\nvt. 安装发动机于
- **英文释义**: A machine that converts energy into mechanical motion, especially one that powers a vehicle or device.
- **词根词缀**: en(在…内)+gine(产生)→内在产生→引擎
- **例句**:
- The new electric car features a highly efficient engine that reduces carbon emissions significantly.
- Engineers are working to engine the prototype with a hybrid power system to improve fuel economy.
- **考频**: 93
## engineering

- **音标**: .endʒi'niәriŋ
- **释义**: n. 工程学, 工程, 操纵\n[化] 机器; 机器学
- **英文释义**: the branch of science and technology concerned with the design, building, and use of engines, machines, structures, and systems
- **词根词缀**: engine(引擎)+er(人)+ing(名词)→引擎相关→工程学
- **例句**:
- Civil engineering requires a strong foundation in mathematics and physics.
- The company invested heavily in software engineering to enhance its product development capabilities.
- **考频**: 106
