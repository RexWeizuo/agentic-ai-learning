# 合成词/词根 hand- (手)

> **类型**: 词族

> **学习提示**: hand- 构成的合成词。handicap 原指手里拿着帽子乞讨，后引申为“障碍、不利条件”。

**5 词**

---

## handbook

- **音标**: 'hænd,buk
- **释义**: n. 手册\n[计] 手册
- **英文释义**: A concise reference book providing practical information or instructions on a particular subject.
- **词根词缀**: hand(手)+book(书)→手册
- **例句**:
- The IT department distributed a new handbook on cybersecurity best practices to all employees.
- Before the lab experiment, students were required to read the safety handbook thoroughly.
- **考频**: 3
## handful

- **音标**: 'hændful
- **释义**: n. 少数, 一把, 棘手事
- **英文释义**: a small number of people or things; a small amount that can be held in one hand; a person or situation that is difficult to control
- **词根词缀**: hand(手)+ful(充满)→一把
- **例句**:
- Only a handful of students passed the rigorous entrance exam.
- Managing the project turned out to be a real handful due to constant scope changes.
- **考频**: 19
## handicap

- **音标**: 'hændikæp
- **释义**: n. 障碍, 困难, 不利条件\nvt. 加障碍于, 妨碍
- **英文释义**: A condition or circumstance that makes something difficult or puts someone at a disadvantage.
- **词根词缀**: hand(手)+i+cap(帽子)→障碍
- **例句**:
- Her lack of formal education was a significant handicap in the competitive job market.
- The new regulations handicapped small businesses more than large corporations.
- **考频**: 15
## handle

- **音标**: hændl
- **释义**: n. 柄, 把手, 把柄, 柄状物, 手感\nvt. 触摸, 运用, 买卖, 处理, 操作\nvi. 搬运, 易于操纵\nn. 句柄\n[计] 句柄
- **英文释义**: n. a part of an object designed to be held or grasped; vt. to manage, deal with, or operate something effectively
- **词根词缀**: hand(手)+le(小)→把手
- **例句**:
- The handle of the frying pan became too hot to touch.
- Experienced managers know how to handle complex negotiations under pressure.
- **考频**: 132
## handwriting

- **音标**: 'hændraitiŋ
- **释义**: n. 笔迹
- **英文释义**: the style and appearance of a person's written characters
- **词根词缀**: hand(手)+writing(书写)→笔迹
- **例句**:
- Her handwriting is so neat and legible that it is often used as a model in the classroom.
- The forensic expert confirmed the suspect's identity by analyzing the unique features of his handwriting on the forged document.
- **考频**: 10
