# expend/expense 花费词族

> **类型**: 词族

> **学习提示**: 花费、开支相关词汇

**4 词**

---

## expend

- **音标**: ik'spend
- **释义**: vt. 花费, 消耗, 用光
- **英文释义**: to spend or use up (money, time, energy, etc.)
- **词根词缀**: ex(出)+pend(称量)→称量出去→花费
- **例句**:
- The government plans to expend an additional $2 billion on renewable energy infrastructure.
- She expended considerable effort to complete the research before the conference deadline.
- **考频**: 6
## expenditure

- **音标**: ik'spenditʃә
- **释义**: n. 开支, 费用, 用光, 消费额\n[经] 支出, 费用, 消费
- **英文释义**: The act of spending money or the amount of money spent.
- **词根词缀**: ex(出)+pend(称量)+iture(名词)→称量出去→支出
- **例句**:
- The government announced a 10% increase in public expenditure to boost economic recovery.
- High household expenditure on housing has become a major concern for policymakers.
- **考频**: 17
## expense

- **音标**: ik'spens
- **释义**: n. 费用, 代价, 开支, 损失\n[经] 费用, 开支, 将支出转为费用
- **英文释义**: The cost incurred in carrying out an activity or achieving a goal; money spent on something.
- **词根词缀**: ex(出)+pens(称量)+e→称量出去→花费
- **例句**:
- The company decided to cut unnecessary expenses to improve its profit margin.
- Pursuing a graduate degree involves significant time and financial expense.
- **考频**: 154
## expensive

- **音标**: ik'spensiv
- **释义**: a. 贵的, 奢华的, 费用浩大的, 乱化钱的\n[经] 高价的, 昂贵的, 浪费的
- **英文释义**: costing a lot of money; high in price or cost
- **词根词缀**: ex(出)+pens(称量)+ive(形容词)→称量出去的→昂贵的
- **例句**:
- This luxury watch is too expensive for my budget.
- Investing in renewable energy infrastructure is expensive but essential for long-term sustainability.
- **考频**: 207
