# 词族/近义 (问候/打招呼)

> **类型**: 词族

> **学习提示**: greet 是动词问候，greeting 是名词。hail 除了冰雹，也有“欢呼、打招呼”之意。

**4 词**

---

## greet

- **音标**: gri:t
- **释义**: vt. 问候, 致敬, 欢迎, 映入眼帘
- **英文释义**: to say hello to someone, often formally or politely; to welcome or acknowledge someone or something
- **词根词缀**: greet→问候
- **例句**:
- The ambassador was greeted with full diplomatic honors upon arrival.
- As we rounded the hill, a breathtaking vista greeted us.
- **考频**: 31
## greeting

- **音标**: 'gri:tiŋ
- **释义**: n. 祝贺, 问候
- **英文释义**: a word or phrase used to say hello or express good wishes
- **词根词缀**: greet(问候)+ing(名词)→问候
- **例句**:
- She offered a warm greeting to each guest at the conference.
- The email began with a formal greeting and ended with a polite closing.
- **考频**: 3
## hail

- **音标**: heil
- **释义**: n. 冰雹, 致敬, 欢呼, 招呼\nvt. 向...欢呼, 致敬, 招呼, 使象下雹样落下\nvi. 招呼, 下雹\ninterj. 万岁, 欢迎
- **英文释义**: n. frozen raindrops falling from clouds; a greeting or expression of approval; v. to greet enthusiastically, to originate from; interj. an exclamation of welcome or acclaim
- **词根词缀**: hail(冰雹)→冰雹
- **例句**:
- The storm brought heavy hail that damaged the crops and shattered car windows.
- She was hailed as a visionary leader after her groundbreaking policy reform.
- **考频**: 11
## hello

- **音标**: hә'lәu
- **释义**: interj. 喂, 嘿
- **英文释义**: An interjection used to express greeting, attract attention, or begin a conversation.
- **词根词缀**: hello(喂)→问候语
- **例句**:
- Hello, Professor Smith—I’m here for my scheduled office hours.
- She hesitated before dialing, then said 'Hello?' softly into the phone.
- **考频**: 8
