# 词族 vari- (变化)

> **类型**: 词族

> **学习提示**: 源自拉丁语 varius (不同的)，掌握其在数学、统计及日常语境中的名词与形容词形态。

**5 词**

## variable

- **音标**: 'vєәriәbl
- **释义**: n. 易变的事物, 变数, 可变物, 变量\na. 可变的, 不定的, 易变的, 变量的\n[计] 变量
- **英文释义**: A quantity or element that can change or vary; in mathematics and computing, a symbol representing a value that may change during calculation or execution.
- **词根词缀**: vari(变化)+able(能…的)→能变化的→可变的
- **例句**:
- The study identified socioeconomic status as a key variable influencing academic performance.
- In programming, declaring a variable before use ensures type safety and improves code readability.
- **考频**: 34
## variance

- **音标**: 'vєәriәns
- **释义**: n. 不一致, 变化, 变异, 分歧, 不和\n[化] 方差; 变种
- **英文释义**: The degree to which a set of data points differs from their mean (in statistics); or general deviation, disagreement, or inconsistency.
- **词根词缀**: vari(变化)+ance(名词后缀)→变化→差异
- **例句**:
- There is a significant variance between the experimental results and the theoretical predictions.
- The committee rejected the proposal due to the wide variance in members' opinions on its feasibility.
- **考频**: 1
## variation

- **音标**: .vєәri'eiʃәn
- **释义**: n. 变更, 变化, 变种, 变奏\n[医] 变异, 变易; 变度
- **英文释义**: a change or slight difference in condition, amount, or level, typically within certain limits
- **词根词缀**: vari(变化)+ation(名词后缀)→变化→变异
- **例句**:
- The experiment yielded significant variation in results across different age groups.
- Musical variation involves altering a theme while retaining its essential character.
- **考频**: 36
## various

- **音标**: 'vєәriәs
- **释义**: a. 不同的, 各种的, 多方面的, 许多的, 个别的, 杂色的\n[法] 不同的, 种种的, 各式各样的
- **英文释义**: different from each other; of many kinds or types
- **词根词缀**: vari(变化)+ous(形容词后缀)→变化的→各种各样的
- **例句**:
- The conference attracted participants from various countries and academic backgrounds.
- Researchers have proposed various approaches to address the issue of climate change.
- **考频**: 229
## vary

- **音标**: 'vєәri
- **释义**: vt. 改变, 使多样化\nvi. 变化, 有不同, 违反
- **英文释义**: to change or differ in some way; to make something diverse or varied
- **词根词缀**: vari(变化)→变化
- **例句**:
- The difficulty of the questions varies significantly across different sections of the exam.
- Researchers found that dietary habits vary widely among populations in different geographic regions.
- **考频**: 98
