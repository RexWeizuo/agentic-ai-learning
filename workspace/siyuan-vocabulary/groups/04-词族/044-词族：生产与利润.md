# 词族：生产与利润

> **类型**: 词族

> **学习提示**: 经济与商业核心词族。product(产品) -> productive(多产的) -> productivity(生产力)；profit(利润) -> profitable(有利可图的)。

**5 词**

---

## product

- **音标**: 'prɒdʌkt
- **释义**: n. 产品, 结果, 乘积\n[化] 生产物
- **英文释义**: something that is made or grown to be sold, the result of a process, or the number obtained by multiplying two or more numbers
- **词根词缀**: pro(向前)+duct(引导)→向前引导→产品
- **例句**:
- The company launched a new software product targeting enterprise clients.
- Economic growth is not the sole product of technological innovation; institutional reform also plays a critical role.
- **考频**: 644
## productive

- **音标**: prә'dʌktiv
- **释义**: a. 能生产的, 有生产价值的, 多产的\n[医] 产出性的, 产生性的
- **英文释义**: producing good or useful results; efficient and effective in achieving a purpose
- **词根词缀**: pro(向前)+duct(引导)+ive(形容词)→能引导向前的→多产的
- **例句**:
- The team held a highly productive meeting that led to concrete action plans.
- She managed her time so well that she completed three research papers during the semester, demonstrating an exceptionally productive academic approach.
- **考频**: 86
## productivity

- **音标**: .prәudʌk'tiviti
- **释义**: n. 生产力\n[经] 生产率, 生产能力
- **英文释义**: The efficiency of producing goods or services, typically measured as output per unit of input (e.g., labor, time, or capital)
- **词根词缀**: pro(向前)+duct(引导)+ivity(名词)→多产性
- **例句**:
- Improving workplace ergonomics significantly boosted employee productivity.
- The country's long-term economic growth depends heavily on sustained gains in labor productivity.
- **考频**: 122
## profit

- **音标**: 'prɒfit
- **释义**: n. 利润, 赢利, 利益\nvi. 有益, 获利, 赚钱\nvt. 有益于
- **英文释义**: Financial gain obtained when revenue exceeds expenses; benefit or advantage derived from an action or situation
- **词根词缀**: pro(向前)+fit(做)→向前做→利润
- **例句**:
- The company reported a 12% increase in profit after implementing cost-cutting measures.
- Both parties profited from the strategic partnership, as it enhanced market share and operational efficiency.
- **考频**: 190
## profitable

- **音标**: 'prɒfitәbl
- **释义**: a. 有利润的, 有利益的, 赚钱的\n[经] 有利(可图)的, 合算的
- **英文释义**: producing a financial gain or advantage
- **词根词缀**: pro(向前)+fit(做)+able(能)→能获利的
- **例句**:
- Investing in renewable energy has become increasingly profitable for multinational corporations.
- The merger proved highly profitable, boosting the company's annual revenue by 23%.
- **考频**: 60
