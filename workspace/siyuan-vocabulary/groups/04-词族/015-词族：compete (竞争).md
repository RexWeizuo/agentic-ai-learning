# 词族：compete (竞争)

> **类型**: 词族

> **学习提示**: compete 动词，competition 名词，competitive 形容词，competent 指“胜任的、有能力的”。

**4 词**

---

## compete

- **音标**: kәm'pi:t
- **释义**: vi. 竞争, 对抗
- **英文释义**: to take part in a contest or rivalry to win or achieve something
- **词根词缀**: com(一起)+pet(追求)→一起追求→竞争
- **例句**:
- Athletes from over 200 countries compete in the Olympic Games for gold medals.
- In today's globalized economy, companies must constantly innovate to compete effectively in international markets.
- **考频**: 152
## competent

- **音标**: 'kɒmpitәnt
- **释义**: a. 能干的, 胜任的, 有效的, 足够的\n[经] 有权的, 授权的, 胜任的
- **英文释义**: having the necessary ability, knowledge, or skill to do something well
- **词根词缀**: com(一起)+pet(追求)+ent(形容词)→一起追求的→有能力的
- **例句**:
- She is a highly competent lawyer who handled the complex merger with remarkable efficiency.
- The committee must consist of competent professionals authorized to make binding decisions on regulatory matters.
- **考频**: 25
## competition

- **音标**: .kɒmpi'tiʃәn
- **释义**: n. 竞争, 竞赛\n[经] 竞争, 竞销, 比赛
- **英文释义**: A contest or rivalry between two or more parties for the same objective, especially for superiority, market share, or a prize.
- **词根词缀**: com(一起)+pet(追求)+ition(名词)→一起追求→竞争
- **例句**:
- Intense global competition has forced many domestic manufacturers to innovate rapidly.
- Her outstanding performance in the international mathematics competition earned her a full scholarship.
- **考频**: 287
## competitive

- **音标**: kәm'petitiv
- **释义**: a. 竞争的\n[经] 竞争的
- **英文释义**: involving or characterized by competition; striving to win or be better than others
- **词根词缀**: com(一起)+pet(追求)+itive(形容词)→竞争的
- **例句**:
- The global market has become increasingly competitive, forcing companies to innovate continuously.
- She possesses strong analytical skills and a highly competitive mindset, which helped her secure the top position in the national economics competition.
- **考频**: 114
