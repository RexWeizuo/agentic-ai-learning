# 允许与接纳

> **类型**: 词族

> **学习提示**: 动词与名词/形容词的词族对应，allowance 常指“津贴/限额”，admission 可指“入场费/承认”。

**8 词**

---

## accept

- **音标**: әk'sept
- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: ==vt. 接受, 承认, 同意, 相信, 赞成, 承担, 承兑, 采纳, 接纳, 容忍\nvi. 同意==
- **英文释义**: v. consider or hold as true\nv. receive willingly something given or offered\nv. give an affirmative reply to; respond favorably to\nv. react favorably to; consider right and proper
- **同义词**: receive, admit, approve
- **反义词**: reject, refuse
- **词根词缀**: ac-(to, towards)+cept(take, seize)→'take towards oneself'→voluntarily take or receive something offered, acknowledged, or imposed
- **搭配**: accept responsibility, accept an invitation, accept payment
- **例句**:
- She accepted the job offer without hesitation.
- The committee refused to accept the flawed data as evidence.
- **考频**: 299
## acceptance

- **音标**: әk'septәns
- **词性**: n.
- **释义**: ==n. 接受, 接纳, 承认, 同意, 赞同, 容忍, 相信\n[经] 承兑, 认付, (工程)验收==
- **英文释义**: n. the state of being acceptable and accepted\nn. (contract law) words signifying consent to the terms of an offer (thereby creating a contract)\nn. banking: a time draft drawn on and accepted by a bank\nn. the act of taking something that is offered
- **同义词**: adoption, approval, recognition
- **反义词**: rejection, refusal
- **词根词缀**: accept(接受)+-ance(名词后缀，表行为、状态)→表示‘接受’这一行为或所处的状态→接受, 接纳, 承认等抽象名词义
- **搭配**: acceptance rate, terms of acceptance, letter of acceptance
- **例句**:
- Her acceptance of the job offer was announced at the team meeting.
- The project’s success depended on the client’s formal acceptance of the final deliverables.
- **考频**: 32

## allow

- **音标**: ә'lau
- **释义**: ==vt. 允许, 同意给予, 承认\nvi. 容许, 猜想\n[计] 允许命令==
- **英文释义**: v. let have\nv. give or assign a resource to a particular person or cause\nv. allow or plan for a certain possibility; concede the truth or validity of something\nv. grant as a discount or in exchange
- **同义词**: permit, let
- **反义词**: forbid
- **词根词缀**: al(加强)+low(准许)→允许
- **搭配**: allow sb to do, allow for
- **例句**:

- The plan allows for unexpected delays.
- **考频**: 773
## allowance

- **音标**: ә'lauәns
- **释义**: ==n. 津贴, 零用钱, 限额, 折扣, 允许\nvt. 定量供应==
- **英文释义**: a sum of money given regularly for a specific purpose, or the amount allowed within a limit
- **词根词缀**: al(to)+low(praise)+ance(noun)→praise given→允许,津贴
- **例句**:

- **考频**: 25
