# 词族 lay (放置/层)

> **类型**: 词族

> **学习提示**: lay 的派生词，涵盖层次、外行、解雇与布局

**4 词**

---

## layer

- **音标**: 'leiә
- **释义**: n. 层, 产卵鸡, 放置者\nvt. 分层堆积, 压植\n[计] 层
- **英文释义**: A thin sheet or level of material, substance, or information placed or occurring one above another
- **词根词缀**: lay(词根:放)+er(名词)→放的东西→层
- **例句**:
- The Earth's crust consists of several distinct geological layers.
- In object-oriented programming, the application logic is often separated into a presentation layer, a business logic layer, and a data access layer.
- **考频**: 40
## layman

- **音标**: 'leimәn
- **释义**: n. 俗人, 门外汉, 凡人\n[医] 外行, 业外人
- **英文释义**: a person who is not a specialist or professional in a particular field; a non-expert
- **词根词缀**: lay(词根:外行的)+man(词根:人)→外行人
- **例句**:
- The article explains quantum physics in simple terms so that even a layman can understand it.
- Doctors often struggle to communicate complex diagnoses to laymen without using technical jargon.
- **考频**: 6
## layoff

- **音标**: 'leiɒf
- **释义**: n. 临时解雇, 操作停止, 活动停止期间
- **英文释义**: the temporary dismissal of employees, typically due to economic downturn or restructuring
- **词根词缀**: lay(词根:放)+off(词根:离开)→放离开→解雇
- **例句**:
- The company announced a major layoff amid declining profits and market contraction.
- Many tech workers faced layoffs after the startup failed to secure its second-round funding.
- **考频**: 10
## layout

- **音标**: 'leiaut
- **释义**: n. 布局, 陈列, 规划图, 页面布局, 版面编排\n[计] 页面布局, 版面
- **英文释义**: The arrangement of elements on a page, screen, or space, especially in design, publishing, or computing.
- **词根词缀**: lay(词根:放)+out(词根:出)→放出来→布局
- **例句**:
- The new website features a clean and responsive layout that enhances user experience.
- When preparing the conference proceedings, the editor carefully reviewed the layout to ensure consistent typography and proper figure placement.
- **考频**: 13
