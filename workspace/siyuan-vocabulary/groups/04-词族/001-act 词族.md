# act 词族

> **类型**: 词族

> **学习提示**: 共享词根 act（行动/做），注意 activate 和 activity 的拼写与词性变化。

**5 词**

---

## action

- **音标**: 'ækʃәn
- **词性**: n., vt.
- **释义**: ==n. 行动, 活动, 动作, 作用, 战斗, 行为, 诉讼\nvt. 对...起诉\n[计] 方式==
- **英文释义**: n. something done (usually as opposed to something said)\nn. the state of being active\nn. the series of events that form a plot\nn. the trait of being active and energetic and forceful
- **同义词**: activity, movement, procedure
- **反义词**: inaction, idleness
- **词根词缀**: act(做，驱动)+ -ion(名词后缀，表行为或结果)→表示‘所做之事’或‘行为过程’→行动，活动，作用
- **搭配**: take action, in action, call to action
- **例句**:
- The government promised swift action to address the crisis.
- She filed a lawsuit and took legal action against the company.
- **考频**: 291
## activate

- **音标**: 'æktiveit
- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: ==vt. 使活动, 使激活, 正式建成\nvi. 有活力==
- **英文释义**: v. make active or more active\nv. make more adsorptive\nv. aerate (sewage) so as to favor the growth of organisms that decompose organic matter\nv. make (substances) radioactive
- **同义词**: initiate, trigger, enable
- **反义词**: deactivate, disable
- **词根词缀**: act(做，行动)+ -ivate(使…化，动词后缀)→使进入行动状态→使活动；使激活
- **搭配**: activate a function, activate an account, activate a license
- **例句**:
- The system will automatically activate when motion is detected.
- You must activate your account by clicking the link in the confirmation email.
- **考频**: 29
## active

- **音标**: 'æktiv
- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: ==a. 活跃的, 起作用的, 积极的, 有效的, 主动的, 活性的, 现行的, 现役的\nn. 主动语态, 积极分子==
- **英文释义**: n. a person who is a participating member of an organization\na. tending to become more severe or wider in scope\ns. engaged in or ready for military or naval operations\na. disposed to take action or effectuate change
- **同义词**: energetic, dynamic, engaged
- **反义词**: inactive, passive
- **词根词缀**: act(做，行动)+ -ive(形容词后缀，表‘具有…性质的’)→具有行动力的、正在起作用的→活跃的，起作用的，积极的
- **搭配**: active participation, active lifestyle, active ingredient
- **例句**:
- She has been an active member of the environmental committee for over ten years.
- The volcano is currently active, emitting steam and ash regularly.
- **考频**: 108
## activity

- **音标**: æk'tiviti
- **词性**: n.
- **释义**: ==n. 活动, 行动, 活跃, 活力\n[计] 活动==
- **英文释义**: n. any specific behavior\nn. (chemistry) the capacity of a substance to take part in a chemical reaction
- **同义词**: action, operation, exercise
- **反义词**: inactivity, idleness
- **词根词缀**: act(做，行动)+ -ivity(名词后缀，表状态或性质)→表示‘行动的状态’或‘具有行动特征的现象’→活动，行动，活跃度
- **搭配**: physical activity, classroom activity, economic activity
- **例句**:
- The museum organizes educational activities for children every weekend.
- There has been a sudden increase in seismic activity near the fault line.
- **考频**: 485
## actor

- **音标**: 'æktә
- **词性**: n.
- **释义**: ==n. 男演员, 行动者\n[医] 作用物, 反应物==
- **英文释义**: n. a theatrical performer\nn. a person who acts and gets things done
- **同义词**: performer, thespian, player
- **反义词**: audience, spectator
- **词根词缀**: act(行动)+-or(表示‘执行者’的名词后缀)→执行行动的人→指在戏剧、电影中扮演角色的人，或广义上采取行动的人；在古医学术语中引申为参与反应的物质
- **搭配**: lead actor, supporting actor, film actor
- **例句**:
- Leonardo DiCaprio is a highly acclaimed actor known for his versatile roles.
- In chemistry, an actor refers to a substance that participates in a reaction—though this usage is rare and largely archaic.
- **考频**: 88
