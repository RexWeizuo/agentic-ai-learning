# 词族 (特殊/专门/种类)

> **类型**: 词族

> **学习提示**: 源自 speci(种类/特殊)。specialise(专门研究), species(物种), specific(具体的/特效的), specify(详细说明)。

**7 词**

---

## specialise

- **音标**: 'speʃә,laiz
- **释义**: vt. 特加指明, 列举, 使专门化, 限定...的范围\nvt.vi. (使)特化, (使)专化\nvi. 成为专家, 专务, 专攻, 专门研究, 逐条详述
- **英文释义**: to focus on a particular area of study, work, or activity and become an expert in it
- **词根词缀**: spec(看)+ial(形容词)+ise(动词)→专门看→专门化
- **例句**:
- Many medical graduates choose to specialise in cardiology after completing their residency.
- The law firm specialises in international intellectual property disputes, making it a preferred choice for multinational clients.
- **考频**: 6
## specialist

- **音标**: 'speiʃәlist
- **释义**: n. 专门医师, 专家\na. 专业的, 专家的
- **英文释义**: a person who has specialized knowledge or training in a particular field
- **词根词缀**: spec(看)+ial(形容词)+ist(人)→专门看的人→专家
- **例句**:
- She is a cardiovascular specialist with over fifteen years of clinical experience.
- When dealing with rare neurological disorders, patients are often referred to a specialist rather than a general practitioner.
- **考频**: 79
## speciality

- **音标**: .speʃi'æliti
- **释义**: n. 专长, 擅长, 特种技能, 详细说明, 特性, 特产, 专业\n[化] 特制品
- **英文释义**: A subject or skill in which someone has particular expertise or interest; a distinctive feature or product of a place or field.
- **词根词缀**: spec(看)+ial(形容词)+ity(名词)→专门看的事物→特产
- **例句**:
- Her speciality is computational linguistics, which played a key role in her award-winning thesis.
- This region's culinary speciality—smoked duck—is renowned nationwide for its unique flavor and traditional preparation.
- **考频**: 3
## species

- **音标**: 'spi:ʃiz
- **释义**: n. 种, 类, 外形\n[医] 茶剂; 种
- **英文释义**: A group of living organisms that can interbreed and produce fertile offspring under natural conditions.
- **词根词缀**: spec(look/kind)+ies→看
- **例句**:
- The Amazon rainforest is home to thousands of plant and animal species.
- Conservation efforts aim to protect endangered species from extinction.
## specific

- **音标**: spi'sifik
- **释义**: n. 特效药, 特性\na. 特殊的, 明确的, 具有特效的, 特定地, 具体地
- **英文释义**: clearly defined or identified; relating to a particular person, thing, or situation
- **词根词缀**: spec(看)+ific(使)→使能看清→具体的
- **例句**:
- The doctor prescribed a specific antibiotic for the patient's rare bacterial infection.
- The research focuses on a specific demographic group—urban women aged 25 to 34—to ensure more accurate and generalizable results.
- **考频**: 178
