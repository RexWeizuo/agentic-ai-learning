# 词族 base (基础)

> **类型**: 词族

> **学习提示**: 同源词族。base(基础/基地), basic(基本的), basis(基础/根据), basement(地下室)。

**4 词**

---

## base

- **音标**: beis
- **释义**: n. 底部, 垒, 基础, 基地\nvt. 以...作基础\na. 卑鄙的, 低劣的\n[计] 基准
- **英文释义**: the bottom or supporting part of something; a fundamental principle or starting point; in baseball, one of the four corners of the diamond that runners must touch; a place from which operations are conducted
- **词根词缀**: bas(低)+e→基础
- **例句**:
- The theory is based on solid empirical evidence.
- The military established a forward operating base near the border.
- **考频**: 2332
## basic

- **音标**: 'beisik
- **释义**: n. 基本原理, 要素, 基本规律\na. 基本的, 碱性的\n(计算机)BASIC语言
- **英文释义**: fundamental; essential; relating to alkalis or having a pH greater than 7; also refers to the BASIC programming language
- **词根词缀**: bas(基础)+ic(形容词)→基本的
- **例句**:
- A solid grasp of basic economic principles is essential for understanding macroeconomic policy.
- The solution exhibited basic properties, turning red litmus paper blue.
- **考频**: 228
## basis

- **音标**: 'beisis
- **释义**: n. 基础, 主要成分\n[化] 基底
- **英文释义**: the fundamental principle, fact, or element that something is based on or built upon
- **词根词缀**: bas(基础)+is(名词)→基础
- **例句**:
- The theory is based on the assumption that economic growth is the basis for social stability.
- In chemistry, a base is defined as a substance that can accept protons, forming the basis of acid-base reaction theory.
- **考频**: 123
## basement

- **音标**: 'beismәnt
- **释义**: n. 地下室, 墙脚\n[化] 地下室
- **英文释义**: A floor of a building that is partly or entirely below ground level, often used for storage, utilities, or living space.
- **词根词缀**: base(基础)+ment(名词)→地下室
- **例句**:
- The furnace and water heater are located in the basement.
- After the flood, mold began to grow on the walls of the damp basement.
- **考频**: 8
