# 建议与提倡

> **类型**: 词族

> **学习提示**: advise 是动词，advice 是名词，advisable 是形容词（明智的），advocate 提倡/主张。

**4 词**

---

## advise

- **音标**: әd'vaiz
- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: ==vt. 劝告, 给...出主意, 通知, 建议\nvi. 提意见, 商量==
- **英文释义**: v. inform (somebody) of something
- **同义词**: recommend, suggest, counsel
- **反义词**: dissuade, discourage
- **词根词缀**: ad-(to, toward)+vise (from Latin 'videre', meaning 'to see')→literally 'to look toward' → to consider carefully and offer guidance → to give advice or recommend a course of action
- **搭配**: advise sb. to do sth., advise against (doing) sth., advise on sth.
- **例句**:
- The doctor advised her to get more rest.
- We advise against traveling during the storm.
- **考频**: 98
## advice

- **音标**: әd'vais
- **词性**: n.
- **释义**: ==n. 忠告, 劝告, 意见, 报道, 通知\n[经] 通知书, 通知, 建议==
- **英文释义**: n. a proposal for an appropriate course of action
- **同义词**: recommendation, counsel, guidance
- **反义词**: misinformation, deception
- **词根词缀**: ad(朝向)+vice(看法)→朝向别人的看法→建议
- **搭配**: give advice, take one's advice, a piece of advice
- **例句**:
- She gave me some good advice.
- I'd like some advice on career planning.
- **考频**: 184
## advisable

- **音标**: әd'vaizәbl
- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: ==a. 适当的, 明智的==
- **英文释义**: a. worthy of being recommended or suggested; prudent or wise
- **同义词**: wise, prudent
- **反义词**: inadvisable
- **词根词缀**: advise(建议)+-able(能…的，值得…的)→能被建议的/值得被建议的→明智的、适当的
- **搭配**: advisable to do something, highly advisable, not advisable
- **例句**:
- It is advisable to back up your files regularly.
- Given the weather forecast, it is advisable not to travel today.
- **考频**: 18
## advocate

- **音标**: /ˈædvəkeɪt/
- **词性**: v./n.
- **释义**: ==提倡，主张；倡导者==
- **英文释义**: to publicly support or recommend a particular cause or policy; a supporter
- **同义词**: support, promote, champion, proponent
- **反义词**: oppose, criticize, opponent
- **词根词缀**: ad(加强)+voc(声音)+ate→发出声音支持→提倡
- **搭配**: advocate reform, advocate change, an advocate of
- **例句**:
- He advocates reducing the military budget.
- She is a strong advocate of free education.
- **考频**: 130
