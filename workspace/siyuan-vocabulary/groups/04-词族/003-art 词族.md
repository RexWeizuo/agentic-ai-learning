# art 词族

> **类型**: 词族

> **学习提示**: 共享词根 art（艺术/技艺），artificial 意为“人造的”，artefact 意为“人工制品”。

**4 词**

---

## artist

- **音标**: 'ɑ:tist
- **释义**: ==n. 艺术家, 画家==
- **英文释义**: a person who creates art, especially paintings or drawings
- **词根词缀**: art(艺术)+ist→艺术家
- **例句**:
- The museum featured works by both emerging and established artists.
- As a meticulous artist, she spent months perfecting the composition and color palette of her latest painting.
- **考频**: 129
## artistic

- **音标**: ɑ:'tistik
- **释义**: ==a. 艺术的, 艺术家的, 富有艺术性的==
- **英文释义**: having a natural ability or strong appreciation for art; characterized by artistic skill, sensitivity, or expression
- **词根词缀**: art(art)+ist(person)+ic(adjective)→pertaining to artist→艺术的
- **例句**:
- Her artistic vision transformed the ordinary gallery space into an immersive sensory experience.
- The film's artistic direction, including its lighting and composition, earned widespread critical acclaim.
- **考频**: 52
## artificial

- **音标**: .ɑ:ti'fiʃәl
- **释义**: ==a. 人造的, 假的, 非原地产的\n[医] 人工的, 人造的, 伟牟==
- **英文释义**: made or produced by human beings rather than occurring naturally; not genuine or spontaneous
- **词根词缀**: artific(技巧)+ial→人造的
- **例句**:
- The museum displayed an artificial heart used in early medical experiments.
- Her smile seemed artificial, lacking the warmth and sincerity expected in such a heartfelt moment.
- **考频**: 106
## artefact

- **音标**: 'ɑ:tifækt
- **释义**: ==n. 人工制品, 制造物, 人为现象, 膺象, 矫作物, 古代文物\n[医] 人为现象, 人工产物==
- **英文释义**: An object made by humans, typically one of historical or cultural interest; in medicine, a false or misleading result caused by external factors.
- **词根词缀**: art(skill/joint)+efact
- **例句**:
- The museum's collection includes Bronze Age artefacts such as pottery, tools, and ceremonial weapons.
- The blurred region on the MRI scan was later identified as an imaging artefact rather than a pathological lesion.
