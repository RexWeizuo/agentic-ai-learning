# 词族 live (生活/生命)

> **类型**: 词族

> **学习提示**: live 的派生词，涵盖活泼、肝脏、生活与客厅

**4 词**

---

## lively

- **音标**: 'laivli
- **释义**: a. 活泼的, 鲜明的, 生动的
- **英文释义**: full of energy, enthusiasm, or vitality; vivid and striking
- **词根词缀**: live(生命)+ly(形容词)→活泼的
- **例句**:
- The candidate delivered a lively and persuasive speech that captivated the entire audience.
- Her lively description of the ancient city made the historical account far more engaging for readers.
- **考频**: 15
## liver

- **音标**: 'livә
- **释义**: n. 肝脏, 生活者, 居民\n[医] 肝
- **英文释义**: A large glandular organ in the abdomen that detoxifies blood, produces bile, and metabolizes nutrients.
- **词根词缀**: live(生命)+er(名词)→肝脏
- **例句**:
- The liver plays a crucial role in metabolizing drugs and filtering toxins from the bloodstream.
- Chronic alcohol abuse can lead to irreversible damage to the liver, such as cirrhosis.
- **考频**: 9
## living

- **音标**: 'liviŋ
- **释义**: n. 生活, 生计, 生存\na. 活的, 逼真的, 现存的
- **英文释义**: n. the means of supporting oneself; a. alive, vivid, or currently existing
- **词根词缀**: liv+-ing(正在…/…的动作/…的)
- **例句**:
- Her teaching job provides her with a modest but stable living.
- The museum's lifelike wax figures create a strikingly living impression of historical figures.
## living room

- **释义**: 客厅, 起居室
- **英文释义**: A room in a home used for relaxing, entertaining guests, or general family activities.
- **词根词缀**: living room
- **例句**:
- We hosted the post-conference gathering in the living room, where participants engaged in informal academic discussions.
- Her meticulous analysis of spatial organization included how furniture arrangement in the living room influences social interaction patterns.
