# ex- 期待/专家

> **类型**: 词族

> **学习提示**: 期待与专家相关词汇

**4 词**

---

## expect

- **音标**: iks'pekt
- **释义**: vt. 预期, 盼望, 期待
- **英文释义**: to think or believe that something will happen or that someone will do something
- **词根词缀**: ex(出)+spect(看)→向外看→期待
- **例句**:
- The committee expects the report to be completed by next Friday.
- Students are expected to submit their thesis proposals before the end of the semester.
- **考频**: 463
## expectation

- **音标**: .ekspek'teiʃәn
- **释义**: n. 期待, 指望, 展望\n[化] 期望值
- **英文释义**: a strong belief that something will happen or that someone will do something
- **词根词缀**: ex(出)+spect(看)+ation(名词)→向外看→期待
- **例句**:
- The company's stock price surged as investors' expectations of quarterly profits rose.
- Her academic performance exceeded all expectations, earning her a full scholarship to Oxford.
- **考频**: 119
## expert

- **音标**: 'ekspә:t
- **释义**: n. 专家, 行家\na. 老练的, 内行的, 专门的\n[计] 高级
- **英文释义**: a person who has a high level of skill or knowledge in a particular field
- **词根词缀**: ex(出)+pert(尝试)→尝试出来→专家
- **例句**:
- As an expert in computational linguistics, she was invited to keynote the international conference.
- The court appointed an independent expert to evaluate the technical evidence.
- **考频**: 304
## expertise

- **音标**: .ekspә:'ti:z
- **释义**: n. 专家意见, 专门技术\n[法] 专门知识, 专家意见
- **英文释义**: specialized knowledge, skill, or experience in a particular field
- **词根词缀**: ex(出)+pert(尝试)+ise(名词)→尝试出来的知识→专长
- **例句**:
- Her expertise in computational linguistics was crucial to the success of the NLP project.
- The court appointed an independent expert to provide technical expertise on the cybersecurity breach.
- **考频**: 37
