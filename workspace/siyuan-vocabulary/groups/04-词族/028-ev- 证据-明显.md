# ev- 证据/明显

> **类型**: 词族

> **学习提示**: 证据、事件与最终结果相关词汇

**4 词**

---

## evidence

- **音标**: 'evidәns
- **释义**: n. 根据, 证据, 迹象\n[经] 证据, 凭证
- **英文释义**: facts or signs that make something seem true or help to prove a claim
- **词根词缀**: e(出)+vid(看)+ence(名词)→看得见的东西→证据
- **例句**:
- There is growing evidence that climate change is accelerating.
- The prosecution presented new evidence linking the suspect to the crime scene.
- **考频**: 283
## evident

- **音标**: 'evidәnt
- **释义**: a. 显然的, 明显的
- **英文释义**: clearly seen or understood; obvious
- **词根词缀**: e(出)+vid(看)+ent(形容词)→看得见的→明显的
- **例句**:
- It was evident from her expression that she disagreed with the proposal.
- The benefits of regular exercise are evident in improved cardiovascular health and mental well-being.
- **考频**: 40
## event

- **音标**: i'vent
- **释义**: n. 事件, 结果, 事情的进程, 竞赛项目\n[计] 事件
- **英文释义**: A thing that happens, especially something important or noteworthy.
- **词根词缀**: e(出)+vent(来)→出来的事→事件
- **例句**:
- The outbreak of war was a pivotal event in modern history.
- Her winning the national championship was the defining event of her athletic career.
- **考频**: 270
## eventually

- **音标**: i'ventʃuәli
- **释义**: adv. 最后, 终于
- **英文释义**: in the end, especially after a long time or a series of events
- **词根词缀**: e(出)+vent(来)+ual(形容词)+ly(副词)→最终地
- **例句**:
- After years of persistent research, she eventually discovered a novel therapeutic approach for the disease.
- Although the project faced numerous setbacks, the team eventually delivered it ahead of schedule.
- **考频**: 131
