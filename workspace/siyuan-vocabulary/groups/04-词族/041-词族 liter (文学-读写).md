# 词族 liter (文学/读写)

> **类型**: 词族

> **学习提示**: 源自 littera (字母)，涵盖读写能力与文学

**4 词**

---

## literacy

- **音标**: 'litәrәsi
- **释义**: n. 读写能力, 识字, 精通文学
- **英文释义**: the ability to read and write
- **词根词缀**: liter(文字)+acy(名词)→文字能力→读写能力
- **例句**:
- Digital literacy is now as essential as traditional literacy in higher education.
- Improving functional literacy among adults remains a key goal of national education policy.
- **考频**: 61
## literally

- **音标**: 'litәrәli
- **释义**: adv. 逐字地, 按照字面上地, 不夸张地
- **英文释义**: in a literal sense; exactly as stated, without exaggeration or metaphor
- **词根词缀**: liter(文字)+al(形容词)+ly(副词)→按照字面意思地
- **例句**:
- The report states literally every detail of the experiment, leaving no room for ambiguity.
- She was so exhausted she could literally not lift her head off the desk—a fact confirmed by the lab supervisor's notes.
- **考频**: 37
## literary

- **音标**: 'litәrәri
- **释义**: a. 文学的, 文艺的, 精通文学的\n[法] 文学的, 从事文学的, 从事写作的
- **英文释义**: relating to literature or written works, especially those valued for artistic merit
- **词根词缀**: liter(文字)+ary(形容词)→文学的
- **例句**:
- Her literary criticism offers profound insights into 19th-century British novels.
- The conference attracted scholars from diverse fields, but the literary panel focused exclusively on narrative structure and intertextuality.
- **考频**: 68
## literature

- **音标**: 'litәrәtʃә
- **释义**: n. 文学, 文艺, 著作\n[经] 广告, 商品介绍等文学
- **英文释义**: written works, especially those considered to have artistic or intellectual value; also refers to published material on a particular subject
- **词根词缀**: liter(文字)+ature(名词)→文学
- **例句**:
- The course covers both classical and modern English literature.
- A comprehensive review of the scientific literature reveals conflicting evidence on this issue.
- **考频**: 191
