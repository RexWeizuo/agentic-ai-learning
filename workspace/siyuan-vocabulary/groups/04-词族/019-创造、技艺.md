# 创造、技艺

> **类型**: 词族

> **学习提示**: 与创造、制作及生物相关，craft 侧重手工技艺或精巧的设计。

**4 词**

---

## create

- **音标**: kri:'eit
- **释义**: vt. 创造, 建造, 引起, 任命
- **英文释义**: to bring something new into existence, especially through imagination or effort
- **词根词缀**: cre(创造)+ate(动词)→创造
- **例句**:
- The government's new policy is designed to create more job opportunities for young graduates.
- Her innovative approach to renewable energy helped create a sustainable model for urban development.
- **考频**: 581
## creative

- **音标**: kri:'eitiv
- **释义**: a. 有创造力的, 创作的, 产生的
- **英文释义**: Having the ability to produce original and imaginative ideas or works
- **词根词缀**: cre(创造)+ative(形容词)→有创造力的
- **例句**:
- Her creative approach to problem-solving impressed the entire research team.
- The exam requires not only analytical rigor but also creative thinking to formulate compelling arguments.
- **考频**: 165
## creature

- **音标**: 'kri:tʃә
- **释义**: n. 人, 动物, 创造物, 生物
- **英文释义**: a living being, especially an animal or human; sometimes used to refer to a person in a sympathetic, affectionate, or pitying way
- **词根词缀**: cre(创造)+ature(名词)→被创造物→生物
- **例句**:
- The forest was teeming with strange and beautiful creatures.
- She treated every creature—human or animal—with equal compassion, a quality highly valued in ethical philosophy.
- **考频**: 35
## craft

- **音标**: kræft
- **释义**: n. 技艺, 手艺, 诡计\nvt. 精心制作
- **英文释义**: skill in making things by hand; artistry or expertise in a particular field; also, a deceitful plan or trick
- **词根词缀**: craft(技能)→技能
- **例句**:
- The artisan's craft was evident in the intricate woodcarving of the cabinet.
- Politicians sometimes resort to political craft to manipulate public opinion.
- **考频**: 65
