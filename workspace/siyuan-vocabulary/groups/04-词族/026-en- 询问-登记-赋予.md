# en- 询问/登记/赋予

> **类型**: 词族

> **学习提示**: 询问、登记与赋予权利相关词汇

**4 词**

---

## enquire

- **音标**: in'kwaiә
- **释义**: v. 询问
- **英文释义**: to ask for information
- **词根词缀**: en(使)+quire(问)→询问
- **例句**:
- She enquired about the application deadline for the graduate program.
- Before accepting the offer, he enquired whether remote work would be permitted during the first year.
- **考频**: 1
## enquiry

- **音标**: in'kwaiәri
- **释义**: n. 询问\n[经] 询价, 询盘
- **英文释义**: The act of asking for information or clarification; in business contexts, a request for pricing or product details.
- **词根词缀**: en(使)+quir(问)+y(名词)→询问
- **例句**:
- The customer sent an enquiry about the delivery time and available payment methods.
- Before placing a bulk order, we received several formal enquires from overseas importers regarding minimum order quantity and lead time.
- **考频**: 2
## enrol

- **音标**: in'rәul
- **释义**: vt. 登记, 使加入\nvi. 参军, 注册
- **英文释义**: to officially register as a member, student, or participant
- **词根词缀**: en(使)+rol(卷)→使卷入→登记
- **例句**:
- All new students must enrol online before the start of the semester.
- She decided to enrol in a postgraduate course in artificial intelligence to advance her career.
- **考频**: 26
## entitle

- **音标**: in'taitl
- **释义**: vt. 给...权利, 取名为, 给予名称, 叫做\n[法] 给...权利, 使有资格, 称呼
- **英文释义**: to give someone the right or qualification to do or have something; to give a title or name to something
- **词根词缀**: en(使)+title(标题)→使有标题→给权利
- **例句**:
- A university degree does not automatically entitle you to a high-paying job.
- The author entitled her latest novel 'Shadows of Memory' to reflect its thematic focus on loss and remembrance.
- **考频**: 69
