## accord

- **音标**: ә'kɒ:d
- **词性**: ['n.', 'vt.', 'vi.']
- **释义**: ==n. 一致, 调和, 协定\nvt. 给与, 使一致\nvi. 相符合==
- **英文释义**: n. concurrence of opinion\nn. sympathetic compatibility\nv. allow to have
- **同义词**: agreement, harmony
- **反义词**: disagreement
- **词根词缀**: ac(共同)+cord(心)→心心相印→一致
- **搭配**: in accord with, of one's own accord
- **例句**:
- The two parties reached an accord after weeks of negotiation.
- Her actions do not accord with her stated principles.
- **考频**: 1184
## accordance

- **音标**: ә'kɒ:dәns
- **词性**: n.
- **释义**: ==n. 一致, 和谐==
- **英文释义**: n. the act of granting rights
- **同义词**: agreement, conformity, harmony
- **反义词**: disagreement, inconsistency
- **词根词缀**: ac-(to)+cord-(heart, agreement)+-ance(noun suffix)→'to heart' → mutual alignment or agreement → consistency/harmony
- **搭配**: in accordance with
- **例句**:
- The policy was implemented in accordance with national regulations.
- Her actions were not in accordance with the company's ethical guidelines.
- **考频**: 12
## according to

- **音标**: /əˈkɔːr.dɪŋ tə/
- **词性**: phrase
- **释义**: ==根据, 按照；取决于；据…所说==
- **英文释义**: as stated by or based on the information provided by someone or something
- **同义词**: in accordance with, based on, as stated by
- **词根词缀**: ac-(to, toward)+cord-(heart, agreement)+ing(gerund suffix)+to(preposition)→literally 'in heartward alignment' → expressing conformity or dependence on a source or standard→'according to'
- **搭配**: according to plan, according to schedule, according to research
- **例句**:
- According to the latest research, regular physical activity significantly reduces the risk of cardiovascular disease.
- The essay's conclusion must be revised according to the professor's feedback.
## accordingly

- **音标**: ә'kɒ:diŋli
- **词性**: adv.
- **释义**: ==adv. 相应地, 因此, 于是==
- **英文释义**: r. in accordance with
- **同义词**: therefore, thus, consequently
- **反义词**: contrarily, inconsistently
- **词根词缀**: ac-(to)+cord(heart, agreement)+-ing(gerund/adverbial suffix)+-ly(adverb-forming suffix)→'in agreement (with what precedes)'→'in a way that matches or follows logically from what has been stated'→'accordingly'
- **搭配**: accordingly to plan, accordingly to evidence, act accordingly
- **例句**:
- The weather was extremely cold; accordingly, the school canceled outdoor activities.
- She had no formal training in coding, and accordingly, she started with beginner-level courses.
- **考频**: 16
