# 批评、评论

> **类型**: 词族

> **学习提示**: critic 是批评家，critical 可指“批评的”或“关键的/危急的”。

**4 词**

---

## critic

- **音标**: 'kritik
- **释义**: n. 批评家, 鉴定家
- **英文释义**: a person who judges and evaluates works of art, literature, music, etc., often professionally
- **词根词缀**: crit(判断)+ic(名词)→判断者→批评家
- **例句**:
- The film critic praised the director's bold narrative choices in her review.
- As a literary critic, he challenged the traditional interpretation of the novel's ending.
- **考频**: 106
## critical

- **音标**: 'kritikәl
- **释义**: a. 批评的, 决定性的, 危险的, 临界的\n[医] 危象的; 临界的, 极期的
- **英文释义**: expressing adverse or disapproving comments; essential or decisive for a result; at a dangerous or unstable point, especially in illness
- **词根词缀**: crit(判断)+ical(形容词)→判断的→批评的
- **例句**:
- Her critical analysis of the policy revealed serious flaws that had been overlooked.
- The patient's condition is critical, and immediate intervention is required.
- **考频**: 225
## criticise

- **音标**: 'kritisaiz
- **释义**: v. 批评, 吹毛求疵, 非难
- **英文释义**: to express disapproval of someone or something, especially by pointing out faults
- **词根词缀**: crit(判断)+ic+ise(动词)→判断→批评
- **例句**:
- The policy has been widely criticised by economists for its lack of long-term vision.
- She was accused of criticising her colleague's work unfairly during the team meeting.
- **考频**: 8
## criticism

- **音标**: 'kritisizm
- **释义**: n. 批评, 评论, 非难\n[法] 批判, 批评, 评论
- **英文释义**: the act of judging or evaluating something, especially in a negative or disapproving way; also, the analysis and interpretation of literary or artistic works
- **词根词缀**: crit(判断)+ic+ism(名词)→判断行为→批评
- **例句**:
- Her latest novel has received widespread criticism for its stereotypical characters.
- The professor's criticism of the methodology was both incisive and constructive.
- **考频**: 111
