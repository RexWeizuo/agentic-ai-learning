# 词族：class (阶级，分类，经典)

> **类型**: 词族

> **学习提示**: classic 指“经典的、杰作”，classical 指“古典的（如古典音乐）”，注意区分。

**5 词**

---

## classic

- **音标**: 'klæsik
- **释义**: n. 古典作品, 杰作, 大艺术家\na. 第一流的, 最优秀的, 古典的
- **英文释义**: A work of enduring excellence or high artistic merit; also, something typical or quintessential of its kind.
- **词根词缀**: class(等级)+ic(形容词)→第一等的→经典的
- **例句**:
- Shakespeare's 'Hamlet' is widely regarded as a classic of English literature.
- The 1972 Munich Olympics is often cited as a classic example of geopolitical tension intersecting with international sport.
- **考频**: 39
## classical

- **音标**: 'klæsikl
- **释义**: a. 古典的, 正统派的, 经典的\n[医] 古典的; 标准的, 典型的
- **英文释义**: relating to the ancient Greek and Roman world, or conforming to established principles or traditional standards
- **词根词缀**: class(等级)+ical(形容词)→古典的
- **例句**:
- Mozart's symphonies are considered masterpieces of classical music.
- The classical theory of economics assumes rational behavior and perfect market information.
- **考频**: 35
## classification

- **音标**: .klæsifi'keiʃәn
- **释义**: n. 分类, 分级\n[化] 分级; 分粒; 分类
- **英文释义**: the act or process of grouping things or people according to shared characteristics
- **词根词缀**: class(等级)+i+fic(做)+ation(名词)→分成等级→分类
- **例句**:
- The classification of living organisms into kingdoms, phyla, and species is fundamental to biology.
- In machine learning, accurate classification of data points is essential for building reliable predictive models.
- **考频**: 11
## classify

- **音标**: 'klæsifai
- **释义**: vt. 分类, 归类, 分等\n[建] 分类, 分级, 分粒
- **英文释义**: to arrange things or people into groups according to shared characteristics
- **词根词缀**: class(等级)+i+fy(使)→使成等级→分类
- **例句**:
- Scientists classify animals based on their evolutionary relationships and anatomical features.
- The new data must be carefully classified before it can be used in the machine learning model.
- **考频**: 28
## classroom

- **音标**: 'klɑ:sru:m
- **释义**: n. 教室
- **英文释义**: A room in a school or institution where lessons are taught.
- **词根词缀**: class(班级)+room(房间)→教室
- **例句**:
- The teacher encouraged active participation to foster critical thinking in the classroom.
- Research shows that flexible seating arrangements can enhance student engagement and collaboration within the classroom.
- **考频**: 141
