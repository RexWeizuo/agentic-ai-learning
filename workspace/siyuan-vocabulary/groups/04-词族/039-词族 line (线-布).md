# 词族 line (线/布)

> **类型**: 词族

> **学习提示**: line 的派生词及形近词（linen 词源不同但形近）

**4 词**

---

## line

- **音标**: lain
- **释义**: n. 列, 线, 绳, 电线, 线路, 路线, 航线, 作业线, 界线, 战线, 外形, 排, 家系\nvt. 排成一行, 顺...排列, 划线于, 加衬里, 使有线条, 使起皱纹\nvi. 排队\n[计] 线路
- **英文释义**: A long narrow mark, band, or strip; a row of people or things; a route, system, or channel for communication, transport, or operation.
- **词根词缀**: line(线)→线
- **例句**:
- The company has established a direct line of communication with its overseas partners.
- Students formed a line outside the registration office to submit their graduate school applications.
- **考频**: 1511
## linear

- **音标**: 'liniә
- **释义**: a. 线的, 直线的, 线状的\n[计] 线性
- **英文释义**: Of, relating to, or resembling a line; having a one-dimensional, sequential, or proportional relationship.
- **词根词缀**: line(线)+ar(形容词)→线的→线性的
- **例句**:
- The algorithm's time complexity is linear, meaning it scales directly with the input size.
- Unlike nonlinear systems, linear equations can be solved using superposition and exhibit predictable, proportional behavior.
- **考频**: 4
## linen

- **音标**: 'linin
- **释义**: n. 亚麻布, 亚麻线, 亚麻制品\na. 亚麻布制的, 亚麻的
- **英文释义**: fabric made from flax fibers, or household items (e.g., sheets, tablecloths) made from this fabric
- **词根词缀**: lin+-en(使…/…的/小)
- **例句**:
- The antique dresser was draped with crisp, ivory linen.
- Her minimalist bedroom featured natural wood furniture and high-thread-count linen bedding.
## liner

- **音标**: 'lainә
- **释义**: n. 班机, 划线者, 定期船, 衬里\n[化] 衬垫; 垫带; 套管
- **英文释义**: A scheduled aircraft or ship service; a protective or functional inner layer in a container, pipe, or structure.
- **词根词缀**: line(线)+er(名词)→沿着线的东西→班轮
- **例句**:
- The liner from Shanghai to New York departs every Tuesday and Friday.
- The chemical storage tank is equipped with a corrosion-resistant polymer liner to prevent leakage.
- **考频**: 3
