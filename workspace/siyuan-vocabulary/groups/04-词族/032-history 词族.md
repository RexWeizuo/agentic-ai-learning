# history 词族

> **类型**: 词族

> **学习提示**: 注意 historic (历史上著名的/有历史意义的) 与 historical (历史学的/关于历史的) 的区别。

**4 词**

---

## historian

- **音标**: hi'stɒ:riәn
- **释义**: n. 历史学家, 记事者
- **英文释义**: a person who studies or writes about history
- **词根词缀**: histor(历史)+ian(人)→历史学家
- **例句**:
- As a renowned historian, she has published several authoritative works on medieval Europe.
- The historian critically examined primary sources to challenge the traditional narrative of the revolution.
- **考频**: 52
## historic

- **音标**: hi'stɒrik
- **释义**: a. 历史上著名的, 有历史性的
- **英文释义**: important or famous in history; having great significance for the past or future
- **词根词缀**: histor(历史)+ic(形容词)→有历史意义的
- **例句**:
- The signing of the treaty was a historic moment that reshaped international relations.
- Her election as the first female prime minister marked a historic breakthrough for gender equality in the country.
- **考频**: 26
## historical

- **音标**: hi'stɒrikәl
- **释义**: a. 历史的, 史实的, 历史上的\n[法] 历史性的
- **英文释义**: relating to history or past events; based on or supported by historical evidence
- **词根词缀**: histor(历史)+ical(形容词)→历史的
- **例句**:
- The treaty marked a historical turning point in international relations.
- Scholars rely on historical documents to reconstruct the social conditions of the 18th century.
- **考频**: 116
## history

- **音标**: 'histәri
- **释义**: n. 历史, 过去, 经历, 发展过程, 历史学, 过去的事, 历史记录\n[计] 历史记录
- **英文释义**: the study of past events, especially those concerning human societies; a record or account of such events
- **词根词缀**: hist(知道)+ory(名词)→知道的事情→历史
- **例句**:
- The rise and fall of the Roman Empire is a pivotal chapter in world history.
- In her thesis, she critically examined the colonial history of Southeast Asia through indigenous archival sources.
- **考频**: 429
