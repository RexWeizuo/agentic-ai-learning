# identify 词族

> **类型**: 词族

> **学习提示**: identical 强调完全相同；identity 指身份或同一性。

**4 词**

## identical

- **音标**: ai'dentikәl
- **释义**: a. 同一的, 恒等的, 完全相同的\n[医] 同一的, 同等的
- **英文释义**: exactly the same in every detail; indistinguishable
- **词根词缀**: ident(相同)+ical→相同的
- **例句**:
- The DNA profiles of the two brothers were found to be identical, confirming their monozygotic twin status.
- Although the two studies used different methodologies, their conclusions were virtually identical, reinforcing the robustness of the findings.
- **考频**: 36
## identification

- **音标**: ai.dentifi'keiʃәn
- **释义**: n. 识别, 身份证明, 认同\n[化] 鉴定; 鉴别; 鉴别法; 认证
- **英文释义**: the process of recognizing or establishing the identity of someone or something
- **词根词缀**: ident(相同)+ific(使)+ation(名词)→使相同→识别
- **例句**:
- Biometric identification, such as fingerprint scanning, is increasingly used for secure access to digital systems.
- The laboratory's accurate identification of the chemical compound was critical to the success of the experiment.
- **考频**: 15
## identify

- **音标**: ai'dentifai
- **释义**: vt. 识别, 认为...等同于, 确定, 使参与\nvi. 一致, 认同
- **英文释义**: to recognize or establish the identity of someone or something; to associate oneself with a group, idea, or cause
- **词根词缀**: ident(相同)+ify(使)→使相同→识别
- **例句**:
- The researchers used facial recognition software to identify suspects from surveillance footage.
- Many young people identify strongly with environmental activism and see it as central to their personal values.
- **考频**: 410
## identity

- **音标**: ai'dentiti
- **释义**: n. 身份, 相同, 一致, 特性, 恒等式\n[计] (打)标记, 标识
- **英文释义**: the characteristics determining who or what a person or thing is; individuality or sameness
- **词根词缀**: ident(相同)+ity(名词)→相同性→身份
- **例句**:
- Her cultural background plays a crucial role in shaping her personal identity.
- In mathematics, the equation x + 0 = x is an example of an identity.
- **考频**: 121
