# intellect 词族

> **类型**: 词族

> **学习提示**: intelligible 意为可理解的、易懂的，不要与 intelligent (聪明的) 混淆。

**4 词**

---

## intellectual

- **音标**: .intә'lektʃuәl
- **释义**: n. 有知识者, 知识分子, 凭理智做事者\na. 智力的, 用脑力的, 聪明的
- **英文释义**: a person who uses reason and intellect, especially one engaged in academic or cultural pursuits; relating to the intellect or rational thought
- **词根词缀**: intel(在…之间)+lect(选择)+ual(形容词)→在之间选择→智力的
- **例句**:
- Many intellectuals criticized the policy for its lack of empirical evidence.
- The essay demands intellectual rigor and sustained analytical engagement.
- **考频**: 173
## intelligence

- **音标**: in'telidʒәns
- **释义**: n. 智力, 情报, 信息\n[医] 智力
- **英文释义**: the ability to learn, understand, and apply knowledge; also, secret or classified information gathered for national security or military purposes
- **词根词缀**: intel(在…之间)+lig(选择)+ence(名词)→选择能力→智力
- **例句**:
- Her exceptional intelligence enabled her to solve complex logical problems in minutes.
- The agency intercepted enemy communications and used the intelligence to prevent a major terrorist attack.
- **考频**: 271
## intelligent

- **音标**: in'telidʒәnt
- **释义**: a. 聪明的, 智能的, 了解的
- **英文释义**: having the ability to learn, understand, and think in a logical way about things
- **词根词缀**: intel(在…之间)+lig(选择)+ent(形容词)→有选择能力的→聪明的
- **例句**:
- Her intelligent analysis of the economic data impressed the panel of experts.
- Modern AI systems are designed to mimic human intelligent behavior in specific tasks.
- **考频**: 112
## intelligible

- **音标**: in'telidʒәbl
- **释义**: a. 可理解的, 易理解的, 纯概念性的
- **英文释义**: able to be understood; clear and comprehensible
- **词根词缀**: intel(在…之间)+lig(选择)+ible(可…的)→可理解的
- **例句**:
- The lecturer spoke slowly and used simple terminology, making her explanation fully intelligible to first-year students.
- Although the legal document was dense, the revised version was carefully edited to be more intelligible to non-experts.
- **考频**: 3
