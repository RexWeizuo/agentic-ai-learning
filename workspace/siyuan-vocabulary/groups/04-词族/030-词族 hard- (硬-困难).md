# 词族 hard- (硬/困难)

> **类型**: 词族

> **学习提示**: hard 的派生词。注意 hardly 意思是“几乎不”，而 hardship 是“艰难、困苦”，hardware 是“硬件”。

**4 词**

---

## harden

- **音标**: hɑ:dn
- **释义**: vt. 使变硬, 使坚强, 使冷酷\nvi. 变硬, 变冷酷
- **英文释义**: to make or become firm, strong, or emotionally unfeeling
- **词根词缀**: hard(硬)+en(使)→使变硬
- **例句**:
- The concrete will harden over the next 48 hours.
- Years of adversity hardened her resolve and made her less susceptible to emotional manipulation.
- **考频**: 2
## hardly

- **音标**: 'hɑ:dli
- **释义**: adv. 刚刚, 几乎不, 勉强是
- **英文释义**: almost not; only just; with great difficulty
- **词根词缀**: hard(困难)+ly(副词)→几乎不
- **例句**:
- She could hardly believe her eyes when she saw the results of the experiment.
- Hardly had he finished the presentation when the audience burst into applause.
- **考频**: 162
## hardship

- **音标**: 'hɑ:dʃip
- **释义**: n. 艰难, 辛苦, 苦难\n[法] 受苦, 吃苦, 苦难
- **英文释义**: a difficult or painful situation, especially involving suffering, poverty, or deprivation
- **词根词缀**: hard(困难)+ship(状态)→苦难
- **例句**:
- Despite the financial hardship caused by the recession, she continued her graduate studies with a scholarship.
- The refugees endured extreme hardship during their long journey to safety.
- **考频**: 26
## hardware

- **音标**: 'hɑ:dwєә
- **释义**: n. 硬件, 五金器具, 零件\n[计] 硬件
- **英文释义**: The physical components of a computer or electronic device; also, metal tools, fixtures, and fittings used in construction or manufacturing.
- **词根词缀**: hard(硬)+ware(物品)→硬件
- **例句**:
- The new software requires upgraded hardware to run efficiently.
- When renovating the old building, they replaced all the outdated door handles and hinges with high-quality hardware.
- **考频**: 16
