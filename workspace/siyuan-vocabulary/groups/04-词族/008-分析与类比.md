# 分析与类比

> **类型**: 词族

> **学习提示**: analyse/analysis/analytic 为分析词族；analog/analogy 为类比词族，注意 analogy 的发音和拼写。

**5 词**

---

## analyse

- **音标**: 'ænәlaiz
- **释义**: ==vt. 分析, 细察, 分解\n[经] 分析==
- **英文释义**: to examine something in detail, typically to understand its nature or to determine its essential features
- **词根词缀**: ana(through)+lys(loosen)+e→loosen through→分析
- **例句**:
- The researchers analysed the survey data to identify key trends in consumer behavior.
- In her essay, she carefully analyses how Shakespeare uses irony to develop character motivation.
- **考频**: 11
## analysis

- **音标**: ә'nælәsis
- **释义**: ==n. 分析\n[计] 分析机; 分析员; 分析; 分析程序==
- **英文释义**: the process of examining something carefully in order to understand it or discover its meaning
- **同义词**: examination, study
- **词根词缀**: ana(分开)+lysis(松开)→分开研究→分析
- **搭配**: analysis of, carry out analysis
- **例句**:
- The research paper includes a detailed statistical analysis of the survey data.
- Critical analysis of primary sources is essential for writing a strong thesis in history.
- **考频**: 128
## analytic

- **音标**: .ænә'litik
- **释义**: ==a. 分析的, 善于分析的, 解析的\n[医] 分析的==
- **英文释义**: relating to or using analysis, especially logical or mathematical analysis
- **词根词缀**: ana(through)+lyt(loosen)+ic(adjective)→pertaining to loosening→分析的
- **例句**:
- Her analytic approach enabled her to identify subtle patterns in the clinical data.
- The exam tests not only factual knowledge but also analytic reasoning and critical thinking skills.
- **考频**: 5
## analog

- **音标**: 'ænәlɔ^
- **释义**: ==[计] 模拟==
- **英文释义**: Relating to a system that represents data using continuous physical quantities, such as voltage or sound waves, rather than discrete digital values.
- **词根词缀**: an(无/加强)+log(speech/word/study)→说/学
- **例句**:
- Analog signals are more susceptible to noise and distortion than digital ones.
- The engineer compared the precision of analog oscilloscopes with modern digital alternatives.
## analogy

- **音标**: /əˈnælədʒi/
- **词性**: n.
- **释义**: ==类比，类推；相似==
- **英文释义**: a comparison between two things to explain an idea
- **同义词**: comparison, parallel, similarity
- **反义词**: difference, contrast
- **词根词缀**: ana(再)+log(说话)+y→再说一遍→类比
- **搭配**: draw an analogy, by analogy, close analogy
- **例句**:
- He drew an analogy between the brain and a computer.
- The analogy between heart and pump is useful.
- **考频**: 12
