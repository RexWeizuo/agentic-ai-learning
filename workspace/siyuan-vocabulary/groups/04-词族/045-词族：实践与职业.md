# 词族：实践与职业

> **类型**: 词族

> **学习提示**: practise(实践/练习) -> practitioner(从业者)；profession(职业) -> professional(专业的/专业人士)。

**4 词**

---

## practise

- **音标**: 'præktis
- **释义**: v. 实践, 实行, 练习, 实习, 从事(职业)
- **英文释义**: to do something regularly to improve a skill or to apply a theory in real situations
- **词根词缀**: pract(实践)+ise(动词)→实践
- **例句**:
- Medical students must practise clinical skills under supervision before qualifying.
- To master academic writing, you should practise summarising complex arguments concisely.
- **考频**: 16
## practitioner

- **音标**: præk'tiʃәnә
- **释义**: n. 从业者, 开业者\n[医] 行医者, 医师
- **英文释义**: a person who is professionally qualified to practice a particular profession, especially medicine or law
- **词根词缀**: pract(实践)+itioner(人)→实践者
- **例句**:
- As a seasoned medical practitioner, she emphasized evidence-based treatment in her clinical decisions.
- The conference brought together legal practitioners and academic researchers to discuss recent reforms in corporate law.
- **考频**: 20
## profession

- **音标**: prә'feʃәn
- **释义**: n. 职业, 表白, 声明\n[化] 工种; 职业
- **英文释义**: a paid occupation requiring prolonged training and formal qualification
- **词根词缀**: pro(向前)+fess(说)+ion(名词)→公开说→职业
- **例句**:
- Choosing a profession demands careful consideration of one's aptitudes and societal needs.
- Her dedication to the medical profession was evident in her decades of service and research.
- **考频**: 79
## professional

- **音标**: prә'feʃәnl
- **释义**: n. 专业人才\na. 专业的, 职业的
- **英文释义**: a person who is engaged in a specified occupation, especially one that requires specialized education and training; characterized by or conforming to the standards of a profession
- **词根词缀**: pro(向前)+fess(说)+ional(形容词)→职业的
- **例句**:
- She is a highly respected professional in the field of environmental law.
- The conference emphasized the importance of maintaining professional conduct during client interactions.
- **考频**: 338
