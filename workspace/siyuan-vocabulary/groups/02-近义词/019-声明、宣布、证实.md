# 声明、宣布、证实

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 官方或正式的宣告与确认，decree 特指法令或判决。

**4 词**

---

## declare

- **音标**: di'klєә
- **释义**: v. 宣布, 声明, 申报, 断言
- **英文释义**: to state something formally or publicly
- **词根词缀**: de(加强)+clar(清楚)+e→使清楚→宣布
- **例句**:
- The government declared a state of emergency following the natural disaster.
- In her thesis, the researcher declared that climate change is the primary driver of biodiversity loss.
- **考频**: 68
## declaration

- **音标**: .deklә'reiʃәn
- **释义**: n. 宣告, 说明, 宣布\n[计] 说明
- **英文释义**: A formal statement announcing or explaining something, often made publicly or officially.
- **词根词缀**: de(加强)+clar(清楚)+ation(名词)→使清楚→宣言
- **例句**:
- The government issued a declaration of emergency to mobilize resources immediately.
- In programming, a variable declaration specifies its name and data type before use.
- **考频**: 11
## decree

- **音标**: di'kri:
- **释义**: n. 法令, 判决, 天意\nvt. 颁布, 判决\nvi. 发布命令
- **英文释义**: An official order issued by a legal authority or ruler; a formal judicial decision.
- **词根词缀**: de(分开)+cre(判断)+e→分开判断→法令
- **例句**:
- The court issued a decree dissolving the marriage.
- The government decreed that all public gatherings would require prior approval.
- **考频**: 3
## confirm

- **音标**: kәn'fә:m
- **释义**: vt. 证实, 确定, 批准, 使巩固\n[计] 确认
- **英文释义**: to state or show that something is definitely true or correct; to approve or finalize a decision, arrangement, or appointment
- **词根词缀**: con(加强)+firm(坚固)→使坚固→确认
- **例句**:
- The laboratory results confirmed the doctor's initial diagnosis.
- Before boarding, passengers must confirm their flight details online.
- **考频**: 66
