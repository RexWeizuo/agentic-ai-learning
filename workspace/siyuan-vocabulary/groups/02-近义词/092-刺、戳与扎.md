# 刺、戳与扎

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 与尖锐物体或刺痛感相关的词。stab (刺/戳), sting (叮/刺痛), stitch (缝合/剧痛), thrust (猛推/刺), thorn (荆棘)。

**5 词**

---

## stab

- **音标**: stæb
- **释义**: n. 刺, 戳, 剧痛, 尝试, 努力\nvt. 刺, 戳, 刺入, 刺痛, 直入, 使伤心\nvi. 刺, 刺伤
- **英文释义**: A sudden, sharp blow or piercing with a pointed object; a brief attempt at doing something; a sudden, intense pain.
- **词根词缀**: stab→刺,戳
- **例句**:
- He made a stab at solving the complex calculus problem but quickly realized he needed more background knowledge.
- The victim suffered a fatal stab wound to the chest during the altercation.
- **考频**: 2
## sting

- **音标**: stiŋ
- **释义**: n. 叮, 刺痛, 刺激, 讽刺\nvt. 叮, 刺痛, 刺激, 使苦恼\nvi. 叮, 刺痛
- **英文释义**: A sharp, sudden pain caused by an insect bite or a pointed object; to cause such pain or a sudden emotional discomfort.
- **词根词缀**: st(stand)
- **例句**:
- The bee's sting left a red, swollen mark on her arm.
- His harsh criticism stung more than he intended, revealing how sensitive she was to public judgment.
## stitch

- **音标**: stitʃ
- **释义**: n. 一针, 疼痛, 针法, 碎布条, 针脚\nv. 缝, 缝合, 装订
- **英文释义**: A single loop of thread made by a needle in sewing, surgery, or knitting; also, the act of making such loops to join materials.
- **词根词缀**: stitch→缝
- **例句**:
- The surgeon used fine sutures to close the incision with precise stitches.
- She spent hours stitching the quilt together, each stitch reflecting careful craftsmanship.
- **考频**: 4
## thrust

- **音标**: θrʌst
- **释义**: n. 插, 戳, 刺, 猛推, 口头攻击, 推力\nvt. 插入, 猛推, 刺, 戳, 强加, 延伸\nvi. 插, 刺, 戳, 延伸, (用力)推
- **英文释义**: A sudden, forceful push or stab; the act of imposing something forcefully; forward-directed force in physics.
- **词根词缀**: thrust(推)→推
- **例句**:
- The speaker's sharp thrust at the opposing policy drew immediate applause and criticism.
- In rocket science, thrust must exceed gravitational force for liftoff to occur.
- **考频**: 4
## thorn

- **音标**: θɒ:n
- **释义**: n. 刺, 棘, 荆棘
- **英文释义**: A sharp, stiff point growing on a plant stem or branch, often as a defense mechanism.
- **词根词缀**: thorn
- **例句**:
- The rose bush was covered in thorns, making it difficult to prune without gloves.
- His proposal, though well-intentioned, contained several thorns—practical objections that needed careful addressing.
