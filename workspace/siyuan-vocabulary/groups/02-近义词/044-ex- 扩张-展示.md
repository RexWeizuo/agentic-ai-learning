## expand

- **音标**: ik'spænd
- **释义**: vt. 使膨胀, 详述, 扩张\nvi. 张开, 发展\nvt. 展开\nvi. 展开\n[计] 展开; DOS外部命令:将原始DOS磁盘上的压缩文件解压缩并拷贝到硬盘上
- **英文释义**: to become or make something larger in size, number, or scope; to elaborate on an idea or detail
- **词根词缀**: ex(出)+pand(伸展)→伸展出去→扩展
- **例句**:
- The company plans to expand its operations into three new Asian markets next year.
- In academic writing, it is essential to expand on key arguments with relevant evidence and analysis.
- **考频**: 174

## exhaust

- **音标**: ig'zɒ:st
- **释义**: n. 排气, 排气装置, 废气\nvt. 抽完, 用尽, 耗尽, 使精疲力尽\nvi. 排气
- **英文释义**: n. the waste gases expelled from an engine; vt. to use up completely or tire out physically or mentally
- **词根词缀**: ex(出)+haust(抽)→抽出→耗尽
- **例句**:
- The car's exhaust system was leaking, causing a sharp odor in the cabin.
- After hours of intense concentration on the reading comprehension section, she felt completely exhausted.
- **考频**: 36
## exhibit
