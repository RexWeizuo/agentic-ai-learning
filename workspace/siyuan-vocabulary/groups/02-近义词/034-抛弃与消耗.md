# 抛弃与消耗

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 表示物理上的丢弃、资源的耗尽以及抽象意义上的拒绝与否认。

**6 词**

---

## discard

- **音标**: dis'kɑ:d
- **释义**: vt. 丢弃, 抛弃\nvi. 垫牌\nn. 垫牌, 抛弃
- **英文释义**: to get rid of something useless, unwanted, or unnecessary
- **词根词缀**: dis(去掉)+card(纸牌)→扔掉纸牌→丢弃
- **例句**:
- The committee decided to discard the outdated proposal in favor of a more innovative solution.
- In bridge, players must carefully consider which card to discard when unable to follow suit.
- **考频**: 28
## dump

- **音标**: dʌmp
- **释义**: n. 垃圾场\nvt. 倾倒, 倾销\nvi. 倒垃圾, 倾销商品\n[计] 转出; 转储; 倾卸; 切断电源
- **英文释义**: n. an area where waste is disposed of; vt./vi. to get rid of something quickly or carelessly, often in large quantities; (computing) to transfer data from memory to storage or output
- **词根词缀**: dump→倾倒
- **例句**:
- The city plans to close the illegal dump on the outskirts to reduce environmental pollution.
- To counter unfair trade practices, the government imposed anti-dumping duties on imported steel.
- **考频**: 24
## drain

- **音标**: drein
- **释义**: n. 排水沟, 消耗, 排水\nvt. 排出, 喝光, 耗尽\nvi. 排水, 流干
- **英文释义**: n. a channel or pipe for carrying off water or waste; the gradual loss of resources, energy, or strength; vt. to remove liquid from something; to consume completely (e.g., a drink); to exhaust gradually; vi. to flow away or out
- **词根词缀**: drain→排水
- **例句**:
- The prolonged negotiations proved a significant drain on the company's financial resources.
- Before serving, drain the pasta thoroughly to prevent the sauce from becoming watery.
## deplete

- **音标**: di'pli:t
- **释义**: vt. 耗尽, 使衰竭\n[医] 排除, 减少
- **英文释义**: to reduce something substantially, especially a resource or supply, to the point of exhaustion
- **词根词缀**: de(去掉)+plete(充满)→倒空→耗尽
- **例句**:
- Overfishing has depleted fish stocks in many coastal regions.
- Chronic stress can deplete the body's immune reserves, increasing susceptibility to illness.
- **考频**: 8
## deny

- **音标**: di'nai
- **释义**: v. 否认, 拒绝
- **英文释义**: to state that something is not true or that one does not accept it as true; to refuse to give or allow something
- **词根词缀**: de(加强)+ny(否定)→否认
- **例句**:
- The suspect continued to deny any involvement in the crime, despite overwhelming evidence.
- The university denied her application for the scholarship due to insufficient academic credentials.
- **考频**: 81
## denial

- **音标**: di'naiәl
- **释义**: n. 否认\n[法] 否定, 否认, 拒绝接受
- **英文释义**: the act of stating that something is not true or refusing to accept a fact or reality
- **词根词缀**: de(加强)+ni(否定)+al(名词)→否定→否认
- **例句**:
- His denial of involvement in the scandal was met with widespread skepticism.
- Psychologists often regard persistent denial of addiction as a key obstacle to recovery.
- **考频**: 12
