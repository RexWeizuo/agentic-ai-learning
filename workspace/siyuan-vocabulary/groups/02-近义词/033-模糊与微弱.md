# 模糊与微弱

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 形容光线暗淡、感觉迟钝、声音沉闷或态度不确定的形容词。

**4 词**

---

## dim

- **音标**: dim
- **释义**: a. 暗淡的, 模糊的\nvt. 使暗淡\n[计] 数据解释模块
- **英文释义**: lacking brightness or clarity; to reduce the brightness or intensity of something
- **词根词缀**: de(加强)+im(模仿)→加强模仿→暗淡的
- **例句**:
- The room was dim, and she had to squint to read the fine print.
- Prolonged exposure to blue light can dim the contrast sensitivity of the human eye.
- **考频**: 19
## dull

- **音标**: dʌl
- **释义**: a. 钝的, 无趣的, 呆滞的, 阴暗的\nvt. 使迟钝, 使阴暗, 缓和\nvi. 变迟钝, 减少
- **英文释义**: lacking sharpness, interest, brightness, or sensitivity; to make or become less sharp, vivid, intense, or responsive
- **词根词缀**: dull→迟钝的
- **例句**:
- The knife had become so dull that it could hardly cut through a tomato.
- Prolonged isolation can dull one's emotional responsiveness and critical thinking skills.
- **考频**: 29
## dumb

- **音标**: dʌm
- **释义**: a. 哑的, 不说话的, 无声音的\n[医] 哑的
- **英文释义**: unable to speak, especially due to a physical or neurological condition
- **词根词缀**: dumb→哑的
- **例句**:
- The patient remained dumb following the severe stroke affecting Broca's area.
- Though born deaf and dumb, she communicated fluently using sign language and lip-reading.
- **考频**: 3
## dubious

- **音标**: 'dju:biәs
- **释义**: a. 可疑的, 不确定的
- **英文释义**: causing doubt; not certain or reliable
- **词根词缀**: dub(怀疑)+ious(形容词)→可疑的
- **例句**:
- The authenticity of the ancient manuscript remains dubious, prompting further scholarly investigation.
- Her sudden change of heart seemed dubious to the committee, raising concerns about her true motives.
- **考频**: 13
