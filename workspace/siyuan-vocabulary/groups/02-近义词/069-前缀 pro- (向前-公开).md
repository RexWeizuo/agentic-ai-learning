# 前缀 pro- (向前/公开)

> **类型**: 词缀

> **学习提示**: pro- 表示“向前、公开、赞成”。如 proclaim(宣布), project(向前抛出/计划), prolong(延长)。

**6 词**

---

## proclaim

- **音标**: prә'kleim
- **释义**: vt. 宣布, 公告, 宣言, 表明, 赞扬\n[法] 宣布, 宣告, 公布
- **英文释义**: to announce something officially or publicly
- **词根词缀**: pro(向前)+claim(喊叫)→向前喊→宣告
- **例句**:
- The government proclaimed a state of emergency following the natural disaster.
- Her groundbreaking research proclaims a new direction for sustainable energy development.
- **考频**: 13
## project

- **音标**: 'prɒdʒekt
- **释义**: n. 计划, 设计, 事业\nvt. 计划, 设计, 投掷, 发射, 使凸出, 放映\nvi. 凸出
- **英文释义**: A planned piece of work with a specific goal, or to plan, present, or cause something to extend outward or be displayed.
- **词根词缀**: pro(向前)+ject(投掷)→向前投掷→项目
- **例句**:
- The government launched a major infrastructure project to boost regional development.
- Her confidence and enthusiasm projected strongly during the presentation, leaving a lasting impression on the panel.
- **考频**: 334
