# 近义词 (严肃/理智/清醒)

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 形容态度或心智状态。solemn(庄严的), sober(清醒的/稳重的), sane(神智健全的), severe(严格的/严重的)。

**4 词**

---

## solemn

- **音标**: 'sɒlәm
- **释义**: a. 严肃的, 郑重的, 庄严的
- **英文释义**: formal and serious in manner or tone, especially to show the importance or gravity of an occasion
- **词根词缀**: solemn(词根)→庄严的
- **例句**:
- The judge delivered her verdict in a solemn voice that silenced the entire courtroom.
- The ceremony began with a solemn pledge to uphold academic integrity and ethical research standards.
- **考频**: 10
## sober

- **音标**: 'sәubә
- **释义**: a. 未醉的, 稳重的, 节制的, 严肃的, 不夸大的, 合理的, 朴素的\nvt. 使醒酒, 使严肃\nvi. 从醉里醒来, 变严肃
- **英文释义**: not intoxicated by alcohol; serious, calm, and sensible in behavior or tone
- **词根词缀**: sober(词根)→清醒的
- **例句**:
- After the party, he drank coffee to sober up before driving home.
- The committee issued a sober assessment of the economic risks, avoiding sensationalism.
- **考频**: 8
## sane

- **音标**: sein
- **释义**: a. 神智健全的, 稳健的, 没病的, 合理的\n[医] 精神健全的
- **英文释义**: having a healthy mind; able to think and behave in a reasonable and rational way
- **词根词缀**: san(健康)+e→神志健全的
- **例句**:
- After months of therapy, she regained her ability to make sane decisions about her future.
- It's not sane to invest all your savings in a single volatile cryptocurrency without proper research.
- **考频**: 1
## severe

- **音标**: si'viә
- **释义**: a. 严格的, 尖锐的, 严肃的, 严重的, 严厉的, 朴素的\n[法] 严厉的, 苛刻的, 严重的
- **英文释义**: very serious, harsh, or intense
- **词根词缀**: sever(分开)+e→分开的→严厉的
- **例句**:
- The patient suffered from a severe allergic reaction requiring immediate medical intervention.
- The government imposed severe restrictions on exports to curb the economic crisis.
- **考频**: 81
