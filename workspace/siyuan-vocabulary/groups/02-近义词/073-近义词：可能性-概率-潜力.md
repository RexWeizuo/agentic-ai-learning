# 近义词：可能性/概率/潜力

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: possibility 指一般的可能性；probability 指概率、或然率；potential 指潜在的、未发挥的能力。

**4 词**

---

## possibility

- **音标**: .pɒsә'biliti
- **释义**: n. 可能性, 可能的事\n[法] 可能性, 可能发生的事, 不确定权
- **英文释义**: the fact that something may happen or be true
- **词根词缀**: poss(能够)+ibility(名词)→可能性
- **例句**:
- There is a real possibility that the project will be delayed due to funding shortages.
- The court ruled that the defendant's alibi did not eliminate the possibility of his involvement in the crime.
- **考频**: 149
## probability

- **音标**: .prɒbә'biliti
- **释义**: n. 可能性, 或然率, 几率, 概率\n[化] 概率; 几率
- **英文释义**: The extent to which something is likely to happen or be the case.
- **词根词缀**: prob(证明)+ability(能力)→可证明性→概率
- **例句**:
- There is a high probability that the project will be completed ahead of schedule.
- According to statistical analysis, the probability of inheriting this genetic disorder is 25%.
- **考频**: 15
## percentage

- **音标**: pә'sentidʒ
- **释义**: n. 百分比, 比率, 部分, 可能性\n[计] 百分比
- **英文释义**: A rate or proportion expressed as a fraction of 100.
- **词根词缀**: per(每)+cent(百)+age(名词)→每百→百分比
- **例句**:
- Over 75% of the respondents supported the policy, indicating a strong majority percentage.
- The report highlights a significant increase in the percentage of renewable energy used in the national grid over the past decade.
- **考频**: 78
## potential

- **音标**: pә'tenʃәl
- **释义**: n. 潜在性, 可能性, 潜力, 潜能, 势, 位\na. 有潜力的, 可能的, 潜在的
- **英文释义**: the possibility of something happening or of someone developing in a particular way; latent ability or capacity
- **词根词缀**: potent(力量)+ial(形容词)→有力量的→潜在的
- **例句**:
- Her academic performance and leadership experience indicate strong potential for success in graduate studies.
- The new technology has the potential to revolutionize renewable energy systems.
- **考频**: 299
