# 近义词 (部分/区域/范围)

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 均含“部分”或“范围”之意。section(章节/部门), sector(扇形/领域), segment(片段), scope(范围), scale(规模/比例)。

**5 词**

---

## section

- **音标**: 'sekʃәn
- **释义**: n. 区段, 部分, 区域, 节, 截面, 处, 科, 区, 扇区\nvt. 把...分段, 把...切片\nvi. 被切成片\n[计] 扇区
- **英文释义**: A distinct part or portion of something, such as a text, object, area, or organization; in computing, a subdivision of a storage device.
- **词根词缀**: sect(切割)+ion(名词)→部分
- **例句**:
- The report is divided into five main sections, each addressing a different aspect of the policy.
- Before the surgery, the radiologist examined the cross-sectional images to identify the exact section of the tumor.
- **考频**: 2670
## sector

- **音标**: 'sektә
- **释义**: n. 扇形, 部门, 部分, 函数尺, 象限仪, 段, 区段\nvt. 把...分成扇形\n[计] 扇面; 扇区; 段; 区段
- **英文释义**: A distinct part or division of a field, industry, economy, or physical space, often bounded by specific criteria or function.
- **词根词缀**: sect(切割)+or(名词)→部门
- **例句**:
- The technology sector has seen rapid growth over the past decade.
- Each sector of the circular chart represents a different demographic group.
- **考频**: 113
## segment

- **音标**: 'segmәnt
- **释义**: n. 片段, 部分, 分节, 段\nv. 分割, 分裂\n[计] 段
- **英文释义**: A distinct part or portion of something, especially one that is separated by function, structure, or purpose; to divide something into such parts.
- **词根词缀**: seg(切割)+ment(名词)→部分
- **例句**:
- The market was segmented into three distinct groups based on income and lifestyle.
- In computer memory management, the program is divided into segments such as code, data, and stack.
- **考频**: 59
## scope

- **音标**: skәup
- **释义**: n. 范围, 机会, 广度, 眼界, 观察仪器, 导弹射程\n[计] 作用域
- **英文释义**: the extent of the area or subject that something deals with or to which it is relevant
- **词根词缀**: scop(看)+e→范围
- **例句**:
- The scope of this research project is limited to urban populations in developed countries.
- Object-oriented programming provides greater scope for code reusability and modularity.
- **考频**: 37
## scale

- **音标**: skeil
- **释义**: n. 刻度, 衡量, 比例, 比例尺, 数值范围, 等级, 规模, 天平, 秤, 鳞, 积垢\nvt. 依比例决定, 攀登, 测量, 绘制, 刮鳞, 使生垢, 过秤\nvi. 剥落, 生水垢, 重量为, 攀登, 衡量\n[计] 刻度
- **英文释义**: A graduated range of values, a ratio representing relative size or magnitude, or the act of climbing or removing layers such as scale from a surface.
- **词根词缀**: scal(爬)+e→刻度
- **例句**:
- The data was plotted on a logarithmic scale to better visualize exponential growth.
- To address climate change effectively, policies must be scaled up globally, not just implemented at the local level.
- **考频**: 149
