# 堆积与储存

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 与物品存放或大量聚集相关。stack (堆叠), store (贮藏), stuff (原料/东西), staple (主要产物/常用品)。

**4 词**

---

## stack

- **音标**: stæk
- **释义**: n. 堆叠, 堆, 大量, 书库, 枪架, 烟囱, 堆积, 存贮栈\nvt. 堆积, 堆满, 暗中对...做手脚\nvi. 堆起\n[计] 堆积, 堆栈
- **英文释义**: A pile of objects placed one on top of another; in computing, a data structure that stores items in last-in, first-out (LIFO) order.
- **词根词缀**: stack→堆,垛
- **例句**:
- The librarian organized the returned books into a neat stack on the front desk.
- When a function is called, its local variables are pushed onto the call stack.
- **考频**: 17
## store

- **音标**: stɒ:
- **释义**: n. 商店, 贮藏, 仓库, 备用品, 存储器\nvt. 储存, 贮藏, 供给\nvi. 贮藏\na. 贮藏的, 现成的\n[计] 存储器操作; 存储
- **英文释义**: A place where goods are sold; to keep something for future use.
- **词根词缀**: store→商店
- **例句**:
- She bought a new laptop at the electronics store.
- The system stores user data securely in encrypted format.
- **考频**: 389
## stuff

- **音标**: stʌf
- **释义**: n. 原料, 要素, 东西, 材料, 素质, 织品, 废物, 废话\nvt. 装填\nvi. 狼吞虎咽
- **英文释义**: Informal term for material, items, or content; also means to fill tightly or eat greedily.
- **词根词缀**: stuff(填充物)→填充物
- **例句**:
- The report lacks substantive analysis and is full of irrelevant stuff.
- She stuffed the envelope with confidential documents before sealing it.
- **考频**: 58
## staple

- **音标**: 'steipl
- **释义**: n. 主要产物, 常用品, 主要要素, 原料, 订书钉, 钩环\na. 主要的, 重要的\nvt. 分级, 钉住
- **英文释义**: A basic or essential item, product, or element; commonly used as a noun, adjective, or verb (to fasten with staples or to classify).
- **词根词缀**: staple→订书钉,主要产品
- **例句**:
- Rice is a staple food in many Asian countries.
- The university's curriculum includes several staple courses that all majors must complete.
- **考频**: 10
