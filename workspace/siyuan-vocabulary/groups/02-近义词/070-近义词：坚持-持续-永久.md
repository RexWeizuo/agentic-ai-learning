# 近义词：坚持/持续/永久

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: persevere 和 persist 强调行为上的“坚持、固执”；permanent 和 perpetual 强调状态上的“永久、持续”。

**4 词**

---

## persevere

- **音标**: .pә:si'viә
- **释义**: vi. 坚持, 孜孜不倦, 不屈不挠, (辩论中)固执己见
- **英文释义**: to continue steadily in a course of action despite difficulties or delays
- **词根词缀**: per(完全)+sever(严格)+e→严格坚持→坚持不懈
- **例句**:
- Despite repeated setbacks, she persevered in her research and eventually published a groundbreaking paper.
- Graduate students must persevere through rigorous coursework and lengthy thesis writing to earn their degrees.
- **考频**: 6
## persist

- **音标**: pә'sist
- **释义**: vi. 坚持, 固执, 持续, 坚称\n[经] 持续
- **英文释义**: to continue to exist or occur over a long period of time, especially despite difficulties or opposition
- **词根词缀**: per(贯穿)+sist(站立)→一直站立→坚持
- **例句**:
- Despite repeated setbacks, she persisted in her efforts to reform the healthcare system.
- The economic downturn persisted for over two years, challenging policymakers' assumptions.
- **考频**: 32
## permanent

- **音标**: 'pә:mәnәnt
- **释义**: a. 永久的, 不变的, 固定的, 持久的\nn. 烫发\n[计] 永久的
- **英文释义**: lasting or intended to last indefinitely; not temporary
- **词根词缀**: per(贯穿)+man(停留)+ent(形容词)→一直停留→永久的
- **例句**:
- The company offered her a permanent position after the six-month probationary period.
- Climate change poses a permanent threat to coastal ecosystems if no decisive action is taken.
- **考频**: 59
## perpetual

- **音标**: pә'petʃuәl
- **释义**: a. 永久的, 不断的, 无期限的, 没完没了的, 四季开花的\n[经] 永远的, 终身的, 持续的
- **英文释义**: lasting forever or for an indefinitely long time; continuous without interruption
- **词根词缀**: per(贯穿)+pet(追求)+ual(形容词)→一直追求→永久的
- **例句**:
- The machine requires perpetual maintenance, which significantly increases operational costs.
- She was frustrated by the perpetual delays in the project schedule, undermining team morale.
- **考频**: 11
