# f- 虚假/错误

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 虚假、寓言与错误相关词汇

**4 词**

---

## fake

- **音标**: feik
- **释义**: n. 假货, 欺骗, 诡计\na. 假的\nvt. 假造, 仿造\nvi. 伪装
- **英文释义**: not genuine; made to imitate something real, often to deceive
- **词根词缀**: fake(伪造)→伪造
- **例句**:
- The museum discovered that the ancient vase on display was a fake.
- She faked an injury to avoid taking the exam.
- **考频**: 38
## false

- **音标**: fɒ:ls
- **释义**: a. 错误的, 虚伪的, 假的, 不老实的\nadv. 不准确地, 欺诈地
- **英文释义**: not true, correct, or genuine; deliberately deceptive
- **词根词缀**: fals(欺骗)+e→欺骗的→假的
- **例句**:
- The witness gave false testimony, leading to a miscarriage of justice.
- Many students fall for false correlations in data interpretation, mistaking coincidence for causation.
- **考频**: 60
## fable

- **音标**: 'feibl
- **释义**: n. 寓言, 神话, 谎言\nvi. 虚构, 作寓言
- **英文释义**: A short story, typically with animals as characters, conveying a moral lesson; or a false or invented story.
- **词根词缀**: abl(able)+e
- **例句**:
- Aesop's Fables teach timeless moral lessons through simple narratives involving animals.
- The politician's account of the incident was later exposed as a complete fable, unsupported by any evidence.
## fall

- **音标**: fɒ:l
- **释义**: n. 落下, 瀑布, 采伐量, 下降, 落差, 降低, 堕落, 秋天\nvi. 倒下, 落下, 来临, 失守, 阵亡, 下跌, 减弱, 倾斜, 垮台, 轮到, 变成, 降低\na. 秋天的
- **英文释义**: to move downward under gravity; to decline in value, quantity, or strength; the season between summer and winter (autumn)
- **词根词缀**: fall(落下)→落下
- **例句**:
- The sudden fall in oil prices triggered a global market adjustment.
- As autumn sets in, the vibrant fall foliage attracts thousands of tourists to New England.
- **考频**: 492
