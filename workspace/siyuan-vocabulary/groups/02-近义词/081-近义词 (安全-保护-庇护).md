# 近义词 (安全/保护/庇护)

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 安全与保护主题。safeguard/shield/shelter 侧重保护动作或庇护所，secure/security/safety 侧重安全状态。

**6 词**

---

## safeguard

- **音标**: 'seifgɑ:d
- **释义**: n. 保卫, 保护措施, 防护设施\nvt. 保卫, 保护
- **英文释义**: a measure or action taken to protect someone or something from harm, loss, or unauthorized access
- **词根词缀**: safe(安全)+guard(保卫)→保卫
- **例句**:
- The new data privacy law introduces strict safeguards to prevent misuse of personal information.
- To safeguard national security, the government has enhanced border surveillance and intelligence sharing.
- **考频**: 11
## safety

- **音标**: 'seifti
- **释义**: n. 安全, 保险, 平安, 保安设备\nvt. 保护, 防护
- **英文释义**: the condition of being protected from danger, risk, or injury
- **词根词缀**: safe(安全)+ty(名词后缀)→安全
- **例句**:
- Wearing a helmet is essential for the cyclist's safety.
- The company has implemented new protocols to ensure employee safety in the workplace.
- **考频**: 173
## secure

- **音标**: si'kjuә
- **释义**: a. 无虑的, 安心的, 安全的, 可靠的, 保险的\nvt. 固定, 获得, 保证, 使安全, 掩护, 招致\nvi. 停止操作, 船抛锚
- **英文释义**: adj. safe from danger or harm; confident and free from anxiety; reliable or certain; vt. to make safe, obtain firmly, or fasten securely
- **词根词缀**: se(分开)+cur(关心)+e→无忧的→安全的
- **例句**:
- The government has taken stringent measures to secure the country's borders against terrorist threats.
- She worked diligently to secure a scholarship for her postgraduate studies at a top-tier university.
- **考频**: 90
## security

- **音标**: si'kjuriti
- **释义**: n. 安全, 安全性, 防护物, 保安, 可靠性, 担保人, 抵押品, 保证金\n[计] 安全性, 保密性, 安全检查程序
- **英文释义**: the state of being protected from danger, loss, or unauthorized access; measures taken to ensure safety or confidentiality
- **词根词缀**: se(分开)+cur(关心)+ity(名词)→安全
- **例句**:
- The company implemented advanced encryption to enhance data security.
- National security concerns led the government to revise its foreign policy.
- **考频**: 276
## shield

- **音标**: 'ʃi:ld
- **释义**: n. 盾, 防卫物, 保护者, 屏蔽\nvt. 保护, 遮蔽, 屏蔽, 庇护, 挡开, 避开\nvi. 起保护作用
- **英文释义**: A device or structure used for protection against attack, harm, or exposure; to protect or defend from danger, criticism, or unwanted influence.
- **词根词缀**: shield(盾牌)→盾牌
- **例句**:
- The government introduced new regulations to shield small businesses from sudden market fluctuations.
- Her diplomatic skills helped shield the team from public scrutiny during the investigation.
- **考频**: 13
## shelter

- **音标**: 'ʃeltә
- **释义**: n. 庇护所, 避难所, 庇护, 隐蔽处, 掩蔽\nvt. 庇护, 保护, 隐匿\nvi. 躲避
- **英文释义**: a place that provides protection from danger, bad weather, or attack; to protect or hide someone or something
- **词根词缀**: shelter(庇护)→庇护
- **例句**:
- The refugees sought shelter in a makeshift camp after the earthquake.
- Governments have a moral obligation to shelter vulnerable populations from systemic discrimination.
- **考频**: 41
