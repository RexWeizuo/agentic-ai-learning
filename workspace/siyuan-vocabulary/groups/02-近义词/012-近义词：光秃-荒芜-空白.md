# 近义词：光秃/荒芜/空白

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 均含有“空、光秃、荒凉”之意。bare(赤裸的), bald(秃头的), barren(贫瘠的), blank(空白的), bleak(荒凉的)。

**5 词**

---

## bare

- **音标**: bєә
- **释义**: a. 赤裸的, 缺少的, 无遮蔽的, 坦率的\nvt. 使赤裸, 露出
- **英文释义**: adj. without covering, protection, or additions; minimal or basic; vt. to uncover or expose
- **词根词缀**: bare(赤裸)→赤裸的
- **例句**:
- The bare floorboards creaked under her feet, emphasizing the room's stark emptiness.
- The report laid bare the company's financial mismanagement, prompting immediate regulatory scrutiny.
- **考频**: 15
## bald

- **音标**: bɒ:ld
- **释义**: a. 秃头的, 光秃的, 单调的, 赤裸的\nvi. 变秃
- **英文释义**: having no hair on the head; lacking covering, ornament, or detail; plain and direct
- **词根词缀**: bald(光秃)→秃头
- **例句**:
- His bald head glistened under the conference room lights.
- The report presented a bald statement of facts without any supporting evidence.
- **考频**: 13
## barren

- **音标**: /ˈbærən/
- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 贫瘠的，不毛的；不结果实的
- **英文释义**: (of land) too poor to produce much or any vegetation; not producing offspring
- **同义词**: infertile, unproductive, sterile, bare
- **反义词**: fertile, productive, fruitful
- **词根词缀**: barr(障碍)+en→不能生长的→贫瘠的
- **搭配**: barren land, barren desert, barren discussion
- **例句**:
- The land was too barren to grow crops.
- A barren landscape stretched before them.
- **考频**: 4
## blank

- **音标**: blæŋk
- **释义**: n. 空格, 空白\na. 空白的, 空虚的, 完全的, 无色的\nvi. 消失, 成为空白\nvt. 使无效, 取消, 封锁\n[计] 空白
- **英文释义**: A space left empty, especially for writing or printing; having no content, expression, or effect.
- **词根词缀**: blank(白)→空白
- **例句**:
- The application form has several blank lines for your personal details.
- Her face went blank when asked about the incident, suggesting she couldn't recall anything.
- **考频**: 866
## bleak

- **音标**: bli:k
- **释义**: a. 萧瑟的, 荒凉的, 阴冷的
- **英文释义**: cold, empty, and without hope or comfort
- **词根词缀**: bleak(荒凉)→荒凉的
- **例句**:
- The economic outlook for the region remains bleak after three consecutive quarters of recession.
- She stared out at the bleak landscape—gray skies, bare trees, and frozen ground—wondering how long winter would last.
- **考频**: 9
