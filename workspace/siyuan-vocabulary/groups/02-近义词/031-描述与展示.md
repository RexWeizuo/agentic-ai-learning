# 描述与展示

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 表示通过语言、动作或符号来展示、指明或证明某事的动词。

**5 词**

---

## depict

- **音标**: di'pikt
- **释义**: vt. 描述, 描写
- **英文释义**: to represent or describe something in words, pictures, or film
- **词根词缀**: de(加强)+pict(描绘)→描绘
- **例句**:
- The novel vividly depicts the social inequalities of Victorian England.
- The documentary depicts the challenges faced by refugees resettling in urban areas.
- **考频**: 22
## display

- **音标**: dis'plei
- **释义**: n. 显示, 陈列, 炫耀, 显示器\nvt. 陈列, 显示, 表现, 夸示\n[计] 显示器; 显示
- **英文释义**: to show or present something clearly, especially for others to see; a visible arrangement of items or information
- **词根词缀**: dis(展开)+play(折叠)→展开折叠→展示
- **例句**:
- The museum display vividly illustrates the evolution of ancient civilizations.
- Her calm demeanor during the crisis was a remarkable display of leadership and composure.
- **考频**: 128
## denote

- **音标**: di'nәut
- **释义**: vt. 指示, 表示
- **英文释义**: to serve as a sign or name for; to indicate or mean
- **词根词缀**: de(向下)+note(标记)→向下标记→表示
- **例句**:
- The red light denotes danger and requires immediate attention.
- In mathematics, the symbol '∈' denotes membership in a set.
- **考频**: 7
## designate

- **音标**: 'dezigneit
- **释义**: vt. 指定, 指明, 称呼\na. 已选出而未上任的
- **英文释义**: to officially choose or appoint someone for a particular role or purpose; to indicate or specify something clearly
- **词根词缀**: de(加强)+sign(标记)+ate(动词)→标记→指定
- **例句**:
- The committee designated her as the project leader for the upcoming infrastructure initiative.
- The newly designated president-elect will assume office in January after completing the transition process.
- **考频**: 10
## demonstrate

- **音标**: 'demәnstreit
- **释义**: vt. 示范, 证明\nvi. 示威
- **英文释义**: to show clearly that something exists or is true; to perform an action to show how it is done
- **词根词缀**: de(加强)+monstr(展示)+ate(动词)→展示→证明
- **例句**:
- The study demonstrates a strong correlation between sleep deprivation and impaired cognitive function.
- Thousands gathered in the capital to demonstrate against the proposed environmental policy.
- **考频**: 97
