# 疲劳与艰辛

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 表示令人疲倦或需要付出巨大努力的词。tedious 侧重“冗长乏味”，strenuous 侧重“费劲的/艰苦的”。

**4 词**

---

## tedious

- **音标**: 'ti:diәs
- **释义**: a. 沉闷的, 冗长乏味的
- **英文释义**: boring because very long, slow, or repetitive
- **词根词缀**: ted(厌倦)+ious(形容词)→令人厌倦的
- **例句**:
- The legal document was so tedious that it took me hours to read and understand.
- Preparing the annual financial report is a tedious but necessary task for compliance.
- **考频**: 21
## tiresome

- **音标**: 'taiәsәm
- **释义**: a. 累人的, 烦人的
- **英文释义**: causing fatigue or boredom; annoyingly tedious
- **词根词缀**: tire(疲劳)+some(形容词后缀)→令人疲劳的
- **例句**:
- The endless committee meetings were so tiresome that several members fell asleep.
- She found the lecturer's monotonous tone and repetitive examples deeply tiresome, making it hard to concentrate.
- **考频**: 1
## strenuous

- **音标**: 'strenjuәs
- **释义**: a. 奋发的, 费劲的, 艰苦的
- **英文释义**: requiring or using great effort; demanding and exhausting
- **词根词缀**: st(stand)+renu+-ous(有…性质的/多…的)
- **例句**:
- The climbers made a strenuous ascent of Mount Everest in harsh weather conditions.
- Preparing for the GRE involves a strenuous regimen of vocabulary study and timed practice tests.
## toil

- **音标**: tɒil
- **释义**: n. 辛苦, 苦工, 网, 罗网, 圈套\nvi. 苦干, 跋涉
- **英文释义**: hard, exhausting work; to work hard and persistently
- **词根词缀**: toil→辛苦工作
- **例句**:
- Farmers toil from dawn to dusk under the scorching sun.
- Despite years of intellectual toil, she finally published a groundbreaking thesis on cognitive linguistics.
- **考频**: 9
