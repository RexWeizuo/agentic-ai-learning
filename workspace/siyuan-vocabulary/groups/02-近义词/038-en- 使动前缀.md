## enable

- **音标**: i'neibl
- **释义**: vt. 使能够\n[计] 允许, 使能, 打开
- **英文释义**: to make it possible for someone to do something or for something to happen
- **词根词缀**: en(使)+able(能够)→使能够→启用
- **例句**:
- The new software update enables users to encrypt their messages end-to-end.
- Access to high-speed internet enables remote workers to collaborate in real time.
- **考频**: 188
