# 近义词 (旋转/螺旋)

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 旋转与螺旋运动。rotate(旋转), spiral(螺旋形), spin(自旋/纺), screw(螺钉/螺旋桨)。

**4 词**

---

## rotate

- **音标**: 'rәuteit
- **释义**: a. 辐状的\nvt. 使旋转, 使转动, 使轮流\nvi. 旋转, 循环\n[计] 旋转
- **英文释义**: to turn around a central point or axis; to take turns in a sequence
- **词根词缀**: rot(轮)+ate(动词)→使轮转→旋转
- **例句**:
- The Earth rotates on its axis once every 24 hours.
- To ensure fairness and prevent burnout, team members rotate responsibilities each quarter.
- **考频**: 18
## spiral

- **音标**: 'spairәl
- **释义**: n. 螺旋形之物, 螺线\na. 螺旋形的, 盘旋的\nvi. 螺旋形下降, 螺旋形上升, 螺旋形行进\nvt. 使螺旋形行进
- **英文释义**: A curve that winds around a central point while moving progressively farther away or closer to it; also used as a verb to describe rapid, often uncontrolled increase or decrease.
- **词根词缀**: spir(螺旋)+al(形容词)→螺旋的
- **例句**:
- Inflation has begun to spiral upward, prompting urgent policy responses from the central bank.
- The aircraft entered a dangerous spiral descent after losing engine power at high altitude.
- **考频**: 2
## spin

- **音标**: spin
- **释义**: n. 旋转, 自旋, 疾驰, 情绪低落\nvt. 纺织, 纺, 使旋转, 编造\nvi. 纺纱, 吐丝, 作茧, 结网, 旋转, 自旋, 疾驰
- **英文释义**: to rotate rapidly around a central point; to produce yarn by twisting fibers; to fabricate a story or explanation, often misleadingly
- **词根词缀**: spin→旋转
- **例句**:
- The electron's intrinsic angular momentum is called its spin, a key concept in quantum physics.
- The politician tried to spin the scandal as a minor administrative oversight, but the media exposed the full extent of the deception.
- **考频**: 16
## screw

- **音标**: skru:
- **释义**: n. 螺旋, 螺杆, 螺钉, 螺旋桨, 吝啬鬼\nvt. 调节, 扭紧, 旋, 拧, 加强, 压榨, 勒索\nvi. 转动, 旋, 拧
- **英文释义**: A threaded cylindrical fastener used to join objects, or the act of rotating something tightly; also used informally to mean to exploit or deceive someone.
- **词根词缀**: screw(螺丝)→螺丝
- **例句**:
- The engineer used a torque wrench to tighten the screw without stripping the threads.
- Critics accused the corporation of screwing small suppliers by unilaterally cutting payment terms.
- **考频**: 5
