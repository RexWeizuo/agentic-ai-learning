# 增加/推动/提高

> **类型**: 近义词

> **学习提示**: impetus 和 incentive 都含有动力、动机的意思；hike 可指价格或数量的大幅上升。

**9 词**

---

## hoist

- **音标**: hɒist
- **释义**: n. 升高, 起重机, 推动\nv. 升起, 升高, 举起
- **英文释义**: to lift or raise something, especially using a device such as a pulley or crane
- **词根词缀**: hoist(举起)→举起
- **例句**:
- The workers used a crane to hoist the steel beams into place.
- She was hoisted onto the stage by her fellow performers amid thunderous applause.
- **考频**: 3
## hike

- **音标**: haik
- **释义**: n. 徒步旅行, 远足, 涨价, 提高\nvi. 步行, 徒步旅行, 上升\nvt. 使高涨, 拉起
- **英文释义**: a long walk, typically in the countryside; or a sudden increase in price, rate, or level
- **词根词缀**: hike(徒步)→徒步旅行
- **例句**:
- The students organized a weekend hike to the Blue Ridge Mountains to practice field ecology.
- Inflation has triggered a sharp hike in interest rates, raising concerns among graduate students planning to take out loans.
- **考频**: 6
## increase

- **音标**: in'kri:s
- **释义**: n. 增加, 增进, 利益\nvt. 增加, 加大\nvi. 增加, 繁殖
- **英文释义**: to become or make something larger in amount, number, degree, or intensity
- **词根词缀**: in(加强)+creas(生长)+e→增长→增加
- **例句**:
- The government has introduced new policies to increase employment opportunities for graduates.
- Global carbon emissions continue to increase despite international climate agreements.

## impetus

- **音标**: 'impitәs
- **释义**: n. 动力, 推动力, 动量\n[医] 冲动, 动能, 起始(病)
- **英文释义**: a force or energy that makes something happen or progress
- **词根词缀**: im(加强)+pet(追求)+us(名词)→追求的动力→推动力
