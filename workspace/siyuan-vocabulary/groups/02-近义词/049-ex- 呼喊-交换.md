# ex- 呼喊/交换

> **类型**: 形近

> **学习提示**: 呼喊、交换、借口与摘录相关词汇

**4 词**

## exclaim

- **音标**: iks'kleim
- **释义**: v. 大叫, 呼喊, 大声叫
- **英文释义**: to cry out suddenly and loudly, often due to surprise, pain, or strong emotion
- **词根词缀**: ex(出)+claim(喊)→喊出→呼喊
- **例句**:
- She exclaimed in disbelief when she heard the unexpected news.
- He exclaimed with delight upon seeing the long-awaited results of his research.
- **考频**: 7
## exchange

- **音标**: iks'tʃeindʒ
- **释义**: n. 交换, (电话)交换局, 交换机, 汇兑, 交易所\nv. 交换, 交易, 兑换\n[计] 交换; 电话局
- **英文释义**: to give something and receive something else in return; a place or system where goods, services, information, or money are exchanged
- **词根词缀**: ex(出)+change(换)→换出→交换
- **例句**:
- The two countries agreed to an exchange of prisoners as part of the peace deal.
- Stock prices fluctuated sharply after the announcement from the New York Stock Exchange.
- **考频**: 111
## excuse

- **音标**: ik'skju:z
- **释义**: vt. 原谅, 申辩, 做为...的托辞\nn. 致歉, 理由, 饶恕, 借口
- **英文释义**: to forgive someone for a mistake or offense; to offer a reason (often weak or false) for one's behavior
- **词根词缀**: ex(出)+cuse(理由)→给出理由→原谅
- **例句**:
- Please excuse my late submission—I was hospitalized unexpectedly.
- He used his busy schedule as an excuse to avoid helping with the project.
- **考频**: 56
## excerpt

- **音标**: 'eksә:pt
- **释义**: n. 摘录\nv. 引用, 摘录
- **英文释义**: A short passage taken from a longer text, speech, or piece of music.
- **词根词缀**: ex(出)+cerpt(摘)→摘出→摘录
- **例句**:
- The professor assigned an excerpt from Shakespeare's 'Hamlet' for close reading and analysis.
- In her thesis, she excerpted key paragraphs from three seminal studies to support her argument.
- **考频**: 72
