# 近义词 (观察/搜索/细看)

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 观察与搜索动作。scan(扫描/细看), scrutiny(仔细检查), skim(略读/掠过), scout(侦察/搜索)。

**4 词**

---

## scan

- **音标**: skæn
- **释义**: n. 审视, 浏览, 扫描, 细查\nvt. 细看, 浏览, 扫描, 详细调查, 标出格律\nvi. 押韵, 扫描\n[计] 网络软件目录, 编码与分析系统
- **英文释义**: to examine something quickly and carefully, especially to find specific information or to check for problems
- **词根词缀**: scan(爬)→扫描
- **例句**:
- Before submitting the essay, she took a minute to scan it for grammatical errors.
- The doctor ordered a brain scan to rule out any structural abnormalities.
- **考频**: 126
## scrutiny

- **音标**: 'skru:tini
- **释义**: n. 细看, 仔细检查, 监视, 选票检查\n[经] 复查, 评核, 仔细检查
- **英文释义**: close, careful examination or observation
- **词根词缀**: scrut(检查)+iny(名词)→仔细检查
- **例句**:
- The company's financial statements are subject to rigorous scrutiny by independent auditors.
- Her research methodology came under intense scrutiny during the thesis defense.
- **考频**: 17
## skim

- **音标**: skim
- **释义**: vt. 撇取浮沫, 略读, 掠过\nvi. 擦过, 浏览\nn. 脱脂乳, 擦过, 表层物\na. 撇去浮沫的
- **英文释义**: to read something quickly without paying attention to details; to remove a thin layer (e.g., foam or fat) from the surface of a liquid
- **词根词缀**: skim(撇去)→浏览
- **例句**:
- Before the exam, she skimmed through the textbook to identify key concepts.
- The chef carefully skimmed the excess fat off the stock to make it lighter.
- **考频**: 75
## scout

- **音标**: skaut
- **释义**: n. 守候, 侦察, 搜索, 侦察员, 侦察机\nvi. 守候, 侦察, 巡视, 嘲笑\nvt. 侦察, 跟踪, 发现, 监视, 嘲弄
- **英文释义**: a person or aircraft engaged in reconnaissance; to explore or examine an area carefully to gather information
- **词根词缀**: scout(侦察)→侦察兵
- **例句**:
- The military sent a scout ahead to assess enemy positions before the main force advanced.
- She spent weeks scouting potential venues for the international conference, evaluating accessibility and facilities.
- **考频**: 2
