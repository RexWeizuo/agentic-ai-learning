# 伤害/损害

> **类型**: 近义词

> **学习提示**: impair 侧重削弱或损害功能；inflict 强调把痛苦、伤害强加于人 (inflict sth on sb)。

**4 词**

---

## hurt

- **音标**: hә:t
- **释义**: n. 伤害, 创伤, 损害\nv. 伤害, (使)伤心, 危害, 刺痛
- **英文释义**: to cause physical pain, emotional distress, or damage
- **词根词缀**: hurt(伤害)→伤害
- **例句**:
- The athlete withdrew from the competition after hurting his knee in training.
- Her harsh criticism deeply hurt his confidence, affecting his performance in subsequent interviews.
- **考频**: 102
## impair

- **音标**: im'pєә
- **释义**: vt. 损害, 减少, 削弱\n[法] 损害, 伤害, 减少
- **英文释义**: to weaken or damage something so that it is less effective, healthy, or valuable
- **词根词缀**: im(使)+pair(更坏)→使更坏→损害
- **例句**:
- Chronic sleep deprivation can significantly impair cognitive function and memory consolidation.
- Environmental pollution may impair respiratory health, especially in children and the elderly.
- **考频**: 9
## injure

- **音标**: 'indʒә
- **释义**: vt. 伤害, 损害, 使受冤屈\n[医] 损伤
- **英文释义**: to cause physical harm, damage, or injustice to someone or something
- **词根词缀**: in(不)+jur(法律)+e→不合法→伤害
- **例句**:
- The athlete was forced to withdraw from the competition after he injured his knee during training.
- False accusations can seriously injure a person's reputation and career.
- **考频**: 58
## inflict

- **音标**: in'flikt
- **释义**: vt. 施以, 加害, 使承受\n[法] 处, 加, 予以
- **英文释义**: to cause something unpleasant, such as pain, suffering, or damage, to someone or something
- **词根词缀**: in(不/无/进入)+flict(strike)
- **例句**:
- The court inflicted a heavy fine on the corporation for environmental violations.
- Years of civil conflict have inflicted lasting psychological trauma on the civilian population.
