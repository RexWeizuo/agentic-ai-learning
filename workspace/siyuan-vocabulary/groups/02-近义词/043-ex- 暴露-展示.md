# ex- 暴露/展示

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 暴露、明确与流放相关词汇

**4 词**

---

## expose

- **音标**: ik'spәuz
- **释义**: vt. 使暴露, 使曝光, 揭穿, 陈列\n[医] 暴露, 露置
- **英文释义**: to make something visible or known, especially something hidden, harmful, or illegal
- **词根词缀**: ex(向外)+pos(放置)+e→向外放置→暴露
- **例句**:
- The investigative report exposed widespread corruption in the government.
- Surgeons must carefully expose the affected tissue during the operation.
- **考频**: 125
## exposure

- **音标**: ik'spәuʒә
- **释义**: n. 暴露, 揭发, 揭露\n[化] 曝光量; 照射; 照射量
- **英文释义**: the state of being unprotected from harmful or dangerous influences; the act of revealing something hidden or secret; in photography/medicine, the amount of radiation or light received
- **词根词缀**: ex(向外)+pos(放置)+ure(名词)→向外放置的状态→暴露
- **例句**:
- Prolonged exposure to ultraviolet radiation increases the risk of skin cancer.
- The journalist's investigation led to the exposure of widespread corruption in the agency.
- **考频**: 76
## explicit

- **音标**: ik'splisit
- **释义**: a. 详述的, 清楚的, 直言的
- **英文释义**: stated clearly and in detail, leaving no room for confusion or doubt
- **词根词缀**: ex(出)+plic(折叠)+it(形容词)→折叠出来的→明确的
- **例句**:
- The instructions were explicit, specifying each step required to assemble the device.
- The contract contained explicit terms regarding confidentiality and intellectual property rights.
- **考频**: 11
## exile

- **音标**: 'eksail
- **释义**: n. 放逐, 流放, 被放逐者\nvt. 放逐, 流放, 使背井离乡
- **英文释义**: the punishment of forcing someone to live away from their home country, or a person who is forced to live in exile
- **词根词缀**: ex(出)+ile(坐)→赶出去坐→流放
- **例句**:
- After the coup, the deposed president fled into exile in Canada.
- The poet spent two decades in exile, writing powerful works that criticized authoritarian rule.
- **考频**: 6
