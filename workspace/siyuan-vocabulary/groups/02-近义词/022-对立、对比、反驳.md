# 对立、对比、反驳

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 表示矛盾、相反或冲突，contradict 侧重言语上的反驳。

**4 词**

---

## contradict

- **音标**: .kɒntrә'dikt
- **释义**: vt. 反驳, 与...抵触, 与...矛盾\nvi. 反驳
- **英文释义**: to assert the opposite of a statement or to be inconsistent with something
- **词根词缀**: contra(相反)+dict(说)→说相反的话→矛盾
- **例句**:
- His testimony directly contradicts the evidence presented by the prosecution.
- The new data contradict the long-held assumption that economic growth always reduces unemployment.
- **考频**: 23
## contrary

- **音标**: 'kɒntrәri
- **释义**: a. 相反的, 矛盾的, 对立的\nn. 相反, 对立面\nadv. 相反地
- **英文释义**: opposite in nature, direction, or meaning; contradictory
- **词根词缀**: contra(相反)+ry(形容词)→相反的
- **例句**:
- His actions were contrary to his stated principles.
- Contrary to popular belief, the study found no link between the two variables.
- **考频**: 64
## contrast

- **音标**: kәn'træst
- **释义**: n. 差别, 对比, 对照物\nvt. 使对比\nvi. 成对照\n[计] 反差; 对比度
- **英文释义**: A noticeable difference between two or more things, especially when compared; to compare in order to show differences.
- **词根词缀**: contra(相反)+st(站立)→站在对面→对比
- **例句**:
- The stark contrast between urban prosperity and rural poverty highlights deep socioeconomic divides.
- Her calm demeanor sharply contrasts with her brother's impulsive behavior, making their interactions particularly revealing.
- **考频**: 130
## conflict

- **音标**: 'kɒnflikt
- **释义**: n. 战斗, 冲突, 矛盾, 争执\nvi. 争执, 战斗, 冲突, 抵触\n[计] 冲突
- **英文释义**: A serious disagreement or argument, typically a protracted one; incompatibility or inconsistency between two or more elements.
- **词根词缀**: con(共同)+flict(打击)→互相打击→冲突
- **例句**:
- The conflict between economic development and environmental protection remains unresolved.
- Her ethical principles conflicted with the company's profit-driven policies.
- **考频**: 232
