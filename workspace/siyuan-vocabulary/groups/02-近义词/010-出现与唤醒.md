# 出现与唤醒

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 表示“出现、显露、唤醒”的词汇，arise 多指问题/情况出现，arouse 指唤醒/引起（情感）。

**5 词**

---

## appear

- **音标**: ә'piә
- **释义**: ==vi. 出现, 显得, 来到\n[法] 出庭, 到案, 出现==
- **英文释义**: to come into sight or become visible; to seem to be a certain way; to be present officially (e.g., in court)
- **同义词**: emerge, seem
- **反义词**: disappear
- **词根词缀**: ap(朝向)+pear(出现)→出现
- **例句**:
- A figure appeared suddenly at the end of the corridor.
- The evidence appears to contradict the defendant's statement.
- **考频**: 323
## appearance

- **音标**: ә'piәrәns
- **释义**: ==n. 出现, 露面, 外观, 外表, 出版\n[计] 外观, 版面==
- **英文释义**: the act of coming into sight or becoming visible; the way something or someone looks
- **词根词缀**: ap(to)+pear(come in sight)+ance(noun)→coming to sight→出现
- **例句**:
- Her sudden appearance at the conference startled many attendees.
- The new edition features a completely revised appearance, with updated typography and layout.
- **考频**: 106
## apparent

- **音标**: /əˈpærənt/
- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: ==明显的，显而易见的；表面上的==
- **英文释义**: clearly visible or understood; seeming to be true but not necessarily so
- **同义词**: obvious, evident, visible, seeming
- **反义词**: hidden, obscure, unclear
- **词根词缀**: ap(加强)+par(出现)+ent→明显出现→明显的
- **搭配**: apparent reason, become apparent, for no apparent reason
- **例句**:
- It was apparent that he was lying.
- The benefits are apparent to everyone.
- **考频**: 35
## arise

- **音标**: ә'raiz
- **释义**: ==vi. 站立, 出现, 起来==
- **英文释义**: v. originate or come into being\nv. rise to one's feet\nv. result or issue
- **同义词**: emerge, occur
- **词根词缀**: a(向上)+rise(升起)→升起→出现
- **搭配**: arise from
- **例句**:
- Problems may arise during the project.
- A new opportunity has arisen.
- **考频**: 92
## arouse

- **音标**: ә'rauz
- **释义**: ==vt. 唤醒, 鼓励, 引起\nvi. 醒来==
- **英文释义**: to cause a feeling, reaction, or interest to begin; to wake someone up
- **词根词缀**: a(向上)+rouse(唤醒)→激起
- **例句**:
- The documentary aroused widespread public concern about climate change.
- His passionate speech aroused enthusiasm among the graduate students.
- **考频**: 50
