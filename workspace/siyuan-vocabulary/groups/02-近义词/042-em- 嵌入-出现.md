## embed

- **音标**: im'bed
- **释义**: vt. 使插入, 使嵌入, 使深留脑中\n[计] 嵌入
- **英文释义**: to fix something firmly into a surrounding mass or context
- **词根词缀**: em(进入)+bed(床)→放入床中→嵌入
- **例句**:
- The journalist was embedded with the military unit to report directly from the front line.
- Cultural assumptions are often deeply embedded in language and difficult to recognize.
- **考频**: 10
