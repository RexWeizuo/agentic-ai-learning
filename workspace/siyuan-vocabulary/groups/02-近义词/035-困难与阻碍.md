# 困难与阻碍

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 描述客观存在的难题、进退两难的困境以及主观上的阻挠与耽搁。

**6 词**

---

## difficult

- **音标**: 'difikәlt
- **释义**: a. 困难的
- **英文释义**: not easy to do, understand, or deal with
- **词根词缀**: dis(不)+fac(做)+ult(形容词)→不容易做→困难的
- **例句**:
- The final question on the exam was particularly difficult, requiring advanced analytical skills.
- Negotiating a mutually acceptable agreement proved difficult due to conflicting interests.
- **考频**: 473
## difficulty

- **音标**: 'difikәlti
- **释义**: n. 困难, 难点
- **英文释义**: the state of being hard to do, understand, or deal with
- **词根词缀**: dis(不)+fac(做)+ult(形容词)+y(名词)→不容易做→困难
- **例句**:
- She encountered considerable difficulty in interpreting the complex legal text.
- The candidate struggled with the abstract reasoning section, citing difficulty in identifying underlying assumptions.
- **考频**: 215
## dilemma

- **音标**: di'lemә
- **释义**: n. 困境, (进退两难的)窘境
- **英文释义**: A situation in which a difficult choice has to be made between two or more alternatives, often equally undesirable.
- **词根词缀**: di(两个)+lemma(假设)→两个假设→困境
- **例句**:
- The government faces a dilemma: raising taxes could curb inflation but may trigger public backlash.
- She was caught in a moral dilemma when asked to conceal the truth to protect her colleague.
- **考频**: 24
## discourage

- **音标**: dis'kʌridʒ
- **释义**: vt. 使气馁, 阻碍
- **英文释义**: to make someone less confident, enthusiastic, or determined; to try to prevent something from happening
- **词根词缀**: dis(否定)+courage(勇气)→使失去勇气→劝阻
- **例句**:
- Her repeated failures did not discourage her from pursuing a career in scientific research.
- Strict regulations and high entry barriers tend to discourage foreign investment in the sector.
- **考频**: 73
## deter

- **音标**: di'tә:
- **释义**: vt. 制止, 吓住, 威慑
- **英文释义**: to prevent someone from doing something by making them fear the consequences
- **词根词缀**: de(向下)+ter(吓唬)→向下吓唬→阻止
- **例句**:
- Strict penalties are designed to deter people from committing financial fraud.
- The presence of security cameras may deter potential thieves from targeting the building.
- **考频**: 5
## detain

- **音标**: di'tein
- **释义**: vt. 扣留, 扣押, 耽搁\n[法] 拘留, 扣押, 留住
- **英文释义**: to keep someone in a place by force or legal authority, or to delay something
- **词根词缀**: de(向下)+tain(拿住)→向下拿住→扣留
- **例句**:
- The police detained the suspect for questioning without charging him.
- Her visa application was detained at customs due to incomplete documentation.
- **考频**: 4
