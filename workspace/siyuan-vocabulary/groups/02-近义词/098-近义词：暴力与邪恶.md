# 近义词：暴力与邪恶

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: violate (违反/侵犯)，violent (暴力的)，vicious (邪恶的/恶毒的)，wicked (缺德的/坏的)。

**4 词**

---

## violate

- **音标**: 'vaiәleit
- **释义**: vt. 违犯, 亵渎, 违反, 侵犯, 妨碍\n[经] 违犯, 违反
- **英文释义**: to break or fail to comply with a rule, law, agreement, or principle
- **词根词缀**: viol(力量)+ate(动词)→使用力量→违反
- **例句**:
- The company was fined for violating environmental regulations.
- Entering the sacred site without permission is considered a violation that violates centuries-old religious customs.
- **考频**: 20
## violent

- **音标**: 'vaiәlәnt
- **释义**: a. 暴力的, 猛烈的, 激烈的, 极端的, 凶暴的
- **英文释义**: involving or caused by physical force intended to hurt, damage, or kill; extremely intense or sudden
- **词根词缀**: viol(力量)+ent(形容词)→有力量的→暴力的
- **例句**:
- The government deployed troops to suppress the violent uprising.
- She experienced a violent allergic reaction after taking the medication.
- **考频**: 43
## vicious

- **音标**: 'viʃәs
- **释义**: a. 邪恶的, 堕落的, 品性不端的, 恶毒的, 恶性的, 有错误的\n[医] 缺点的, 恶的
- **英文释义**: cruel, aggressive, or morally repulsive; characterized by harmful or destructive behavior or effects
- **词根词缀**: vici(恶)+ous(形容词)→恶的→恶毒的
- **例句**:
- The politician launched a vicious personal attack on his opponent during the debate.
- The disease entered a vicious cycle of inflammation and tissue damage, making treatment increasingly difficult.
- **考频**: 9
## wicked

- **音标**: 'wikid
- **释义**: a. 坏的, 邪恶的, 缺德的, 刻毒的, 恶劣的, 淘气的
- **英文释义**: morally wrong, evil, or deliberately harmful; also used informally to mean excellent or impressive
- **词根词缀**: wick+-ed(…的/已…的（过去式）)
- **例句**:
- The novel portrays the villain as a wicked manipulator who exploits others' vulnerabilities.
- Her wicked sense of humor made even the most tedious seminar entertaining.
