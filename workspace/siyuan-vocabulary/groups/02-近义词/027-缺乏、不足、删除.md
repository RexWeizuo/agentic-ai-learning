# 缺乏、不足、删除

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 表示数量上的不足、缺失或动作的推迟/消除。

**4 词**

---

## deficiency

- **音标**: di'fiʃәnsi
- **释义**: n. 缺乏, 不足\n[医] 缺乏, 不足
- **英文释义**: the state of not having enough of something necessary, especially a nutrient or essential element
- **词根词缀**: de(向下)+fic(做)+iency(名词)→做得不够→缺乏
- **例句**:
- Vitamin D deficiency is common among people with limited sun exposure.
- The study linked iron deficiency to impaired cognitive development in children.
- **考频**: 26
## deficit

- **音标**: 'defisit
- **释义**: n. 赤字, 不足额\n[医] 短缺
- **英文释义**: A shortfall in funds or resources; the amount by which expenditures exceed income or requirements exceed supply.
- **词根词缀**: de(向下)+fic(做)+it(名词)→做得不够→赤字
- **例句**:
- The government announced measures to reduce the budget deficit by cutting non-essential spending.
- Cognitive deficits following traumatic brain injury often require long-term rehabilitation.
- **考频**: 41
## delete

- **音标**: di'li:t
- **释义**: vt. 删除\n[计] 删除
- **英文释义**: To remove data, a file, or an item from a computer system or document.
- **词根词缀**: de(离开)+let(让)+e→让离开→删除
- **例句**:
- Before submitting your thesis, make sure to delete all redundant footnotes and annotations.
- The administrator deleted the corrupted file to prevent system instability.
- **考频**: 69
## delay

- **音标**: di'lei
- **释义**: n. 耽搁, 迟滞\nv. 耽搁, 延迟\n[计] 延迟, 延时
- **英文释义**: to postpone or slow down the occurrence or progress of something
- **词根词缀**: de(离开)+lay(放)→放一边→延迟
- **例句**:
- The flight was delayed by two hours due to severe weather.
- She delayed making a decision until she had reviewed all the evidence.
- **考频**: 101
