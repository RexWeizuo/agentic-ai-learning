# 破坏、撞击、破裂

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 表示物理上的破坏、撞击或压碎。

**4 词**

---

## crack

- **音标**: kræk
- **释义**: n. 裂缝, 爆裂声\nv. (使)爆裂, (使)裂开, (使)发出爆裂声\na. 第一流的\nadv. 啪地一声
- **英文释义**: a narrow break or split in a surface; a sudden sharp sound like a breaking noise; to break or split suddenly; (adj.) excellent, top-quality
- **词根词缀**: crack(拟声词根)→爆裂声→破裂
- **例句**:
- The earthquake caused a deep crack to appear in the wall.
- She cracked the tough negotiation with a brilliant compromise, proving she's a crack negotiator.
- **考频**: 37
## crash

- **音标**: kræʃ
- **释义**: n. 哗啦声, 猛撞, 崩溃, 粗布\nv. 撞碎, 破碎, (使)...坠毁\na. 速成的\n[计] 崩溃
- **英文释义**: A sudden loud noise, violent collision, or abrupt failure; to collide violently or break down suddenly.
- **词根词缀**: crash(拟声词根)→碰撞声→碰撞
- **例句**:
- The stock market crash of 1929 triggered a global economic depression.
- The pilot lost control, and the plane crashed into the mountainside.
- **考频**: 87
## crush

- **释义**: n. 压碎, 粉碎, 群众, 迷恋\nvt. 压破, 征服, 塞, 弄皱, 榨出\nvi. 被压碎, 起皱, 挤
- **英文释义**: to press or squeeze with great force so as to break, deform, or destroy; also used figuratively for intense infatuation or overwhelming pressure
- **词根词缀**: crush(压碎)→压碎
- **例句**:

- She has had a secret crush on her professor since the first lecture, but she knows it's unprofessional.
- **考频**: 7
## damage
