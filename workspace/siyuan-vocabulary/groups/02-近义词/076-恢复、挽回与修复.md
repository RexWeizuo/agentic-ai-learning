# 恢复、挽回与修复

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 均含有“恢复原状、挽回损失或取回”之意，注意 reclaim(开垦/回收) 与 retrieve(检索/挽回) 的语境差异。

**5 词**

---

## reclaim

- **音标**: ri'kleim
- **释义**: vt. 开垦, 改造, 感化, 纠正, 回收\nvi. 喊叫, 抗议
- **英文释义**: to recover or restore something useful, valuable, or usable; to bring land into cultivation or productive use; to reform someone morally or behaviorally
- **词根词缀**: re(回)+claim(喊)→喊回→收回
- **例句**:
- The government launched a project to reclaim degraded farmland and improve food security.
- Educators believe that even delinquent youth can be reclaimed through compassionate guidance and structured support.
- **考频**: 6
## recovery

- **音标**: ri'kʌvәri
- **释义**: n. 恢复, 复原, 痊愈, 重获\n[计] 恢复
- **英文释义**: the process of returning to a normal state after illness, injury, financial loss, or system failure
- **词根词缀**: re(回)+cover(盖)+y(名词)→盖回→恢复
- **例句**:
- After the surgery, her recovery was remarkably fast, allowing her to resume academic work within three weeks.
- The IT department implemented a robust backup protocol to ensure rapid system recovery in case of data corruption.
- **考频**: 82
## redeem

- **音标**: ri'di:m
- **释义**: vt. 赎回, 挽回, 恢复, 偿还, 补偿\n[经] 履行(契约), 买回, 赎回
- **英文释义**: to fulfill a promise or obligation; to buy back something previously sold or pledged; to make up for a fault or failure
- **词根词缀**: re(回)+deem(买)→买回→赎回
- **例句**:
- The company sought to redeem its reputation after the scandal by launching a major corporate social responsibility initiative.
- She worked tirelessly to redeem the mistakes of her early career and eventually earned promotion to senior management.
- **考频**: 1
## restore

- **音标**: ri'stɒ:
- **释义**: vt. 回复, 恢复, 归还, 修补, 修复\nvt. 还原\nvi. 还原\n[计] 还原; DOS 外部命令:从备份盘中取回文件
- **英文释义**: to bring something back to its original or former condition, position, or use
- **词根词缀**: re(回)+store(储存)→恢复
- **例句**:
- The government has launched a project to restore the historic temple to its 18th-century appearance.
- After the system crash, administrators used backup files to restore all critical data within two hours.
- **考频**: 60
## retrieve

- **音标**: ri'tri:v
- **释义**: vt. 取回, 挽回, 弥补, 恢复, 补偿, 回忆, 检索\nvi. 找回猎物\nn. 取回, 恢复, 救险球\n[计] 检索
- **英文释义**: to get or bring something back, especially after it has been lost, removed, or stored
- **词根词缀**: re(回)+trieve(找到)→找回→取回
- **例句**:
- The database system can retrieve relevant records in less than a millisecond.
- She struggled to retrieve the precise details of the conversation from her memory.
- **考频**: 23
