# 开始/创新/引入

> **类型**: 近义词

> **学习提示**: inaugurate 指举行就职典礼或开创；initiative 指主动权或首创精神。

**9 词**

## inaugurate

- **音标**: i'nɒ:gjureit
- **释义**: vt. 开始, 举行...就职典礼
- **英文释义**: to formally begin something, especially a ceremony marking the start of a new position or activity
- **词根词缀**: in(进入)+augur(预兆)+ate(动词)→预兆进入→开始
- **例句**:
- The new president will inaugurate her term with a speech at the Capitol.
- The university inaugurated its first AI research center last month.
- **考频**: 1
## initiate

- **音标**: i'niʃieit
- **释义**: n. 入会, 开始\na. 新加入的\nvt. 开始, 传授基本知识给
- **英文释义**: to begin a process or activity; to admit someone into a group or organization and teach them its basic principles
- **词根词缀**: in(进入)+it(走)+iate(动词)→使开始
- **例句**:
- The government has initiated a nationwide campaign to promote renewable energy.
- Senior researchers initiated the new graduate students into the lab's protocols and safety procedures.
- **考频**: 20
