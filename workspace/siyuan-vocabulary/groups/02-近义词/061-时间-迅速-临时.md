# 时间/迅速/临时

> **类型**: 近义词

> **学习提示**: instantaneous 强调瞬间发生的、猝发的；interim 指暂时的、过渡期的 (in the interim)。

**4 词**

---

## instant

- **音标**: 'instәnt
- **释义**: n. 立即, 瞬间\na. 紧急的, 立即的, 即时的
- **英文释义**: a moment in time; happening or done immediately
- **词根词缀**: in(在)+st(站立)+ant(形容词)→站立的→立即的
- **例句**:
- The rescue team arrived on the scene in an instant.
- She made an instant decision to withdraw from the competition due to injury.
- **考频**: 54
## instantaneous

- **音标**: .instәn'teinjәs
- **释义**: a. 即时的, 瞬间的, 猝发的\n[机] 瞬时的
- **英文释义**: Happening or done in an instant; without any delay
- **词根词缀**: in(在)+st(站立)+aneous(形容词)→立即的
- **例句**:
- The system provides instantaneous feedback to user inputs, which is critical for real-time control applications.
- In physics, instantaneous velocity refers to the velocity of an object at a precise moment in time, calculated as the limit of average velocity as the time interval approaches zero.
- **考频**: 7
## interim

- **音标**: 'intәrim
- **释义**: a. 暂时的, 临时的, 间歇的\nn. 过渡时期
- **英文释义**: serving temporarily or for a limited time; occurring between two events or periods
- **词根词缀**: inter(在…之间)+im(时间)→在中间的时间→过渡期间
- **例句**:
- The company appointed an interim CEO while searching for a permanent replacement.
- Students must complete the interim assessment before proceeding to the final project phase.
- **考频**: 1
## hurry

- **音标**: 'hʌri
- **释义**: n. 匆忙, 急忙, 急促\nvt. 急派, 催促\nvi. 匆忙, 赶快
- **英文释义**: n. a state of urgency or speed; v. to move or act quickly, or to urge someone to do so
- **词根词缀**: hurry(匆忙)→匆忙
- **例句**:
- There's no need to be in such a hurry—the meeting doesn't start for another thirty minutes.
- The manager hurried the team to finalize the report before the client's deadline.
- **考频**: 37
