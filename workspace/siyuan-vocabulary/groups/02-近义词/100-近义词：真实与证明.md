# 近义词：真实与证明

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: truth (事实)，valid (有法律效力的/正当的)，verify (查证/证实)，witness (证人/目击)。

**4 词**

---

## truth

- **音标**: tru:θ
- **释义**: n. 事实, 实情\n[法] 真实, 真相, 事实
- **英文释义**: the quality or state of being in accordance with fact or reality
- **词根词缀**: tru(真实)+th(名词)→真实
- **例句**:
- The journalist was committed to uncovering the truth behind the scandal.
- While his testimony sounded plausible, further investigation revealed that it did not reflect the truth.
- **考频**: 125
## valid

- **音标**: 'vælid
- **释义**: a. 有确实根据的, 有法律效力的, 正当的, 正确的\n[经] 有效的
- **英文释义**: having a sound basis in logic, fact, or law; legally or officially acceptable
- **词根词缀**: val(强)+id(形容词)→强有力的→有效的
- **例句**:
- The court ruled that the contract was no longer valid due to a breach of its fundamental terms.
- Her argument is logically valid, but it rests on an assumption that lacks empirical support.
- **考频**: 56
## verify

- **音标**: 'verifai
- **释义**: vt. 证明, 查证, 证实, 检验, 校验\n[计] 校验, 检验; DOS内部命令:设定写入磁盘时是否验证写入的正确性
- **英文释义**: to check or prove that something is true, correct, or accurate
- **词根词缀**: ver(真实)+ify(使)→使真实→证实
- **例句**:
- Before publishing the report, the researchers verified all data with independent sources.
- The bank requires customers to verify their identity through a government-issued ID and a one-time password.
- **考频**: 20
## witness

- **音标**: 'witnis
- **释义**: n. 证人, 目击者, 证据, 证词\nvt. 目击, 作证, 证明, 表明\nvi. 作证人, 作为证据
- **英文释义**: a person who sees an event happen, especially a crime or accident; or evidence that supports a fact
- **词根词缀**: wit(知道)+ness(名词)→证人
- **例句**:
- The court relied on the testimony of three independent witnesses to convict the suspect.
- Her rapid promotion witnessed her exceptional competence and dedication.
- **考频**: 68
