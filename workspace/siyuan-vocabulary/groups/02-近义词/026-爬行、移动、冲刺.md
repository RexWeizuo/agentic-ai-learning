# 爬行、移动、冲刺

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 表示不同速度和方式的移动，creep 侧重蹑手蹑脚或缓慢蔓延。

**4 词**

---

## crawl

- **音标**: krɒ:l
- **释义**: n. 爬行, 匍匐而行, 养鱼池\nv. 爬行
- **英文释义**: to move slowly on hands and knees or with the body close to the ground; also used metaphorically for very slow progress
- **词根词缀**: crawl(爬行)→爬行
- **例句**:
- The infant began to crawl across the carpet at six months old.
- Negotiations have been crawling for months, with little substantive progress made.
- **考频**: 18
## creep

- **音标**: kri:p
- **释义**: n. 爬, 徐行, 蠕动\nvi. 爬, 蔓延, 潜行
- **英文释义**: to move slowly and quietly, often in a way that is stealthy, unpleasant, or unsettling
- **词根词缀**: creep(爬行)→爬行
- **例句**:
- The fog began to creep across the valley as dusk fell.
- A sense of dread crept over her as she read the ominous letter.
- **考频**: 19
## cruise

- **音标**: kru:z
- **释义**: n. 巡航, 巡弋, 漫游\nv. 巡航, 巡弋, 漫游
- **英文释义**: to travel steadily and smoothly, especially on water or in a vehicle, often for pleasure or patrol
- **词根词缀**: cruis(十字)+e→十字航行→巡航
- **例句**:
- The ship will cruise the Mediterranean for two weeks.
- He cruised through the exam, finishing well ahead of time.
- **考频**: 16
## dash

- **音标**: dæʃ
- **释义**: n. 冲撞, 破折号, 短跑\nvi. 猛冲, 冲撞\nvt. 泼溅, 使猛撞, 搀和, 使破灭, 使沮丧
- **英文释义**: A sudden, rapid movement or action; a short burst of speed; a punctuation mark (—); to move quickly and forcefully; to spoil or destroy something abruptly.
- **词根词缀**: dash(冲)→冲
- **例句**:
- The athlete made a final dash for the finish line, securing first place.
- His hopes of promotion were dashed when the company announced a hiring freeze.
- **考频**: 16
