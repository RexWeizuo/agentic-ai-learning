# ext- 熄灭/提取

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 灭绝、熄灭与提取、奢侈相关词汇

**4 词**

---

## extinct

- **音标**: ik'stiŋkt
- **释义**: a. 熄灭的, 灭绝的, 耗尽的\n[法] 消灭了的, 过时的, 无效的
- **英文释义**: no longer existing or living; having died out or ceased to be used
- **词根词缀**: ex(出)+stinct(刺)→刺出→熄灭→灭绝的
- **例句**:
- The dodo bird has been extinct for over three centuries.
- Many scholars argue that traditional print journalism is becoming increasingly extinct in the digital age.
- **考频**: 13
## extinguish

- **音标**: ik'stiŋgwiʃ
- **释义**: vt. 熄灭, 使消亡, 压制, 使减色\n[医] 消灭
- **英文释义**: to put out a fire or light; to bring to an end or destroy completely
- **词根词缀**: ex(出)+stingu(刺)+ish(动词)→刺出→熄灭
- **例句**:
- The firefighters worked tirelessly to extinguish the blaze before it spread to neighboring buildings.
- His harsh criticism seemed designed to extinguish any remaining confidence the young researcher had in her work.
- **考频**: 10
## extract

- **音标**: ik'strækt
- **释义**: n. 榨出物, 精汁, 摘录, 选段\nvt. (费力地)取出, 采掘, 榨取, 摘录, 吸取\n[计] 提取
- **英文释义**: n. a concentrated substance obtained by extraction; a passage selected from a longer text. vt. to remove or obtain something with effort, especially from a source.
- **词根词缀**: ex(向外)+tract(拉)→向外拉→提取
- **例句**:
- The scientist isolated the active ingredient from the plant extract.
- The essay question required students to extract key arguments from a dense academic passage.
- **考频**: 27
## extravagant

- **音标**: ik'strævgәnt
- **释义**: a. 奢侈的, 挥霍无度的, 浪费的
- **英文释义**: spending or using more money, resources, or time than is necessary or reasonable
- **词根词缀**: extra(超出)+vag(漫游)+ant(形容词)→漫游超出→奢侈的
- **例句**:
- Her extravagant lifestyle—featuring designer clothes, luxury vacations, and daily gourmet meals—quickly depleted her inheritance.
- The government was criticized for approving an extravagant budget for the new stadium while public schools faced severe funding shortages.
- **考频**: 14
