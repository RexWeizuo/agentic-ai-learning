# 赞美与欣赏

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 表示赞赏、喝彩或娱乐的词汇，applaud 是动词，applause 是名词。

**6 词**

---

## acclaim

- **音标**: ә'kleim
- **词性**: ['n.', 'v.']
- **释义**: ==n. 喝彩, 欢呼, 赞同\nv. 欢呼, 喝彩, 称赞==
- **英文释义**: n. enthusiastic approval\nv. praise vociferously
- **同义词**: applause, praise, acclamation
- **反义词**: criticism, condemnation
- **词根词缀**: ad-(to, toward)+clamare(to cry out)→'cry out toward'→ publicly express enthusiastic approval→ 'acclaim'
- **搭配**: receive acclaim, acclaim as, acclaim for
- **例句**:
- The film received widespread acclaim from critics around the world.
- She was acclaimed as a visionary leader at the international summit.
- **考频**: 8
## admire

- **音标**: әd'maiә
- **释义**: ==vt. 赞美, 钦佩, 爱慕\nvi. 称赞, 惊奇==
- **英文释义**: v. feel admiration for\nv. look at with admiration
- **同义词**: respect, appreciate
- **反义词**: despise
- **词根词缀**: ad(加强)+mir(惊奇)+e→令人惊奇→钦佩
- **搭配**: admire sb for
- **例句**:
- I admire her courage.
- We stopped to admire the beautiful view.
- **考频**: 52
## applaud

- **音标**: ә'plɒ:d
- **释义**: ==v. 拍手喝彩, 称赞, 赞同==
- **英文释义**: to show approval or praise, typically by clapping hands or expressing agreement
- **同义词**: clap, praise
- **反义词**: boo
- **词根词缀**: ap(加强)+plaud(拍手)→拍手→鼓掌
- **例句**:
- The audience rose to applaud the speaker's impassioned defense of academic freedom.
- Many economists applaud the government's decision to prioritize long-term fiscal sustainability over short-term stimulus.
- **考频**: 7
## applause

- **音标**: ә'plɒ:z
- **释义**: ==n. 鼓掌, 喝彩, 赞许==
- **英文释义**: expressions of approval or admiration, typically in the form of clapping
- **词根词缀**: ap(to)+plaud(clap)+se→clap to→鼓掌
- **例句**:
- The speaker was greeted with thunderous applause after delivering the keynote address.
- Her innovative research earned widespread applause from the academic community.
- **考频**: 5
## appreciate

- **音标**: /əˈpriːʃieɪt/
- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: ==欣赏；感激；理解；增值==
- **英文释义**: to be grateful for; to understand a situation; to increase in value
- **同义词**: value, recognize, understand, be grateful
- **反义词**: disregard, depreciate, misunderstand
- **词根词缀**: ap(加强)+preci(价值)+ate→一再增加价值→欣赏；增值
- **搭配**: appreciate the importance, greatly appreciate, appreciate the value
- **例句**:
- I really appreciate your help.
- The property has appreciated in value.
- **考频**: 74
## amuse

- **音标**: ә'mju:z
- **释义**: ==vt. 消遣, 娱乐, 使发笑==
- **英文释义**: to entertain or make someone laugh
- **同义词**: entertain, delight
- **反义词**: bore
- **词根词缀**: a(加强)+muse(想)→使想着好玩→逗乐
- **例句**:
- The comedian's witty remarks never failed to amuse the sophisticated audience.
- Reading historical satires can both inform and amuse readers preparing for the graduate entrance exam.
- **考频**: 22
