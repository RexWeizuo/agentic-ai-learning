# 停止与停滞

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 表示不流动或暂停的状态。suspend (暂停/悬挂), stagnant (停滞的/不景气的), stagnate (使停滞)。

**4 词**

---

## stay

- **音标**: stei
- **释义**: n. 停留, 逗留, 制止, 延缓, 停止, 支柱, 支撑物, 支索\nvt. 制止, 延缓, 坚持, 支持, 支撑, 用支索固定\nvi. 停留, 逗留, 暂停, 坚持, 中止
- **英文释义**: to remain in a place for a period of time; to halt or prevent something from happening or progressing
- **词根词缀**: stay→停留
- **例句**:
- Her stay in London lasted six months while she completed her research fellowship.
- The court issued an emergency stay to prevent the enforcement of the controversial regulation.
- **考频**: 404
## suspend

- **音标**: sә'spend
- **释义**: vt. 悬, 吊, 使悬浮, 暂停, 中止, 推迟\nvi. 暂停, 中止, 悬浮, 停止偿付债务\n[计] 暂停
- **英文释义**: to temporarily stop something from continuing or operating
- **词根词缀**: sus(向下)+pend(悬挂)→向下挂→悬挂
- **例句**:
- The government suspended diplomatic relations with the country following the border clash.
- The university suspended the student for academic dishonesty pending a full investigation.
- **考频**: 33
## stagnant

- **音标**: 'stægnәnt
- **释义**: a. 不流动的, 停滞的, 不景气的, 萧条的, 污浊的, 迟钝的\n[经] 萧条的
- **英文释义**: not flowing or moving; lacking development, activity, or progress
- **词根词缀**: stagn(停滞)+ant(形容词)→停滞的
- **例句**:
- The economy has remained stagnant for three consecutive quarters, with no growth in GDP.
- Stagnant water in the pond bred mosquitoes and posed a public health risk.
- **考频**: 4
## stagnate

- **音标**: 'stægneit
- **释义**: v. (使)淤塞, (使)停滞, (使)变萧条
- **英文释义**: to stop developing, growing, or progressing; to become inactive or motionless
- **词根词缀**: stagn(停滞)+ate(动词)→停滞
- **例句**:
- The economy stagnated after the sudden withdrawal of foreign investment.
- Without regular practice, language skills tend to stagnate.
- **考频**: 3
