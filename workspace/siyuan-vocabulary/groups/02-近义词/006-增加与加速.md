# 增加与加速

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 表示增加、放大或前进的词汇，amplify 指“放大/详述”，advent 指“来临/出现”。

**6 词**

---

## addition

- **音标**: ә'diʃәn
- **释义**: ==n. 加法, 增加的人(或物)\n[计] 加法==
- **英文释义**: n. a component that is added to something to improve it\nn. the act of adding one thing to another\nn. a quantity that is added\nn. a suburban area laid out in streets and lots for a future residential area
- **同义词**: increase, sum
- **反义词**: subtraction
- **词根词缀**: add(增加)+ition→增加
- **搭配**: in addition, in addition to
- **例句**:
- In addition to English, she studies French.
- The new wing is a recent addition.
- **考频**: 97
## additional

- **音标**: ә'diʃәnәl
- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: ==a. 附加的, 另外的, 额外的\n[机] 加添的, 附加的==
- **英文释义**: s further or added
- **同义词**: extra, further
- **反义词**: essential, primary
- **词根词缀**: addit-(from Latin 'addere', meaning 'to add')+ -ional (adjectival suffix indicating 'pertaining to') → pertaining to adding → having the quality of being added → extra, supplementary
- **搭配**: additional cost, additional information, additional support
- **例句**:
- We need to collect additional information before making a decision.
- The software update includes additional features and improved security.
- **考频**: 83
## amplify

- **音标**: 'æmplifai
- **释义**: ==vt. 增强, 放大, 扩大, 详述\nvi. 引伸==
- **英文释义**: to increase the strength, volume, or effect of something
- **词根词缀**: ampl(大)+ify(使)→使变大→放大
- **例句**:
- The new evidence served to amplify the prosecution's argument.
- Digital signal processors can amplify weak audio signals without introducing significant noise.
- **考频**: 10
## advance

- **音标**: әd'vɑ:ns
- **释义**: ==n. 前进, 进展, 行过的路程\nvi. 前进, 进展, 提高, 上涨\nvt. 使前进, 促进, 提出, 提高, 使提前, 预付\na. 前面的, 预先的==
- **英文释义**: n. an amount paid before it is earned\nn. increase in price or value\nv. move forward, also in the metaphorical sense\nv. bring forward for consideration or acceptance
- **同义词**: progress, proceed
- **反义词**: retreat
- **词根词缀**: ad(向前)+vance(走)→向前走→前进
- **搭配**: in advance, in advance of
- **例句**:
- Technology has advanced rapidly.
- We made significant advances.
- **考频**: 169
## accelerate

- **音标**: әk'selәreit
- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: ==v. 加速, 促进==
- **英文释义**: v. move faster\nv. cause to move faster
- **同义词**: speed up, hasten
- **反义词**: decelerate, slow
- **词根词缀**: ac(加强)+celer(快)+ate→加速
- **搭配**: accelerate growth, accelerate development, accelerate progress
- **例句**:
- The car accelerated rapidly as it merged onto the highway.
- Investment in renewable energy is expected to accelerate the transition to a low-carbon economy.
- **考频**: 60
## advent

- **音标**: 'ædvәnt
- **词性**: n.
- **释义**: ==n. 来到, 来临==
- **英文释义**: n. arrival that has been awaited (especially of something momentous)\nn. the season including the four Sundays preceding Christmas
- **同义词**: arrival, coming, onset
- **反义词**: departure, exit
- **词根词缀**: ad(朝向)+vent(来)→到来
- **搭配**: advent of technology, advent of spring, advent calendar
- **例句**:
- The advent of the internet revolutionized global communication.
- Her sudden advent at the meeting surprised everyone.
- **考频**: 6
