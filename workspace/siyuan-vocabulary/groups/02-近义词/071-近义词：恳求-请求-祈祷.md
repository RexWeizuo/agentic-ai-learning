# 近义词：恳求/请求/祈祷

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: plea/plead 多指法庭上的抗辩或强烈的恳求；petition 指正式的请愿书；prayer 指祈祷。

**4 词**

---

## plea

- **音标**: pli:
- **释义**: n. 恳求, 辩解, 抗辩, 诉讼, 请愿, 托词\n[法] 抗辩, 申诉案件, 答辩
- **英文释义**: A formal request, appeal, or defense, especially in a legal context.
- **词根词缀**: plea(词根:恳求)→恳求
- **例句**:
- The defendant entered a guilty plea to avoid a lengthy trial.
- Her impassioned plea for climate action moved many audience members to tears.
- **考频**: 5
## plead

- **音标**: pli:d
- **释义**: vi. 辩护, 恳求\nvt. 为...辩护, 提出...借口, 托称, 恳求
- **英文释义**: to make an emotional appeal or request; to formally state one's defense or position in court
- **词根词缀**: plead(词根:恳求)→恳求
- **例句**:
- The defendant chose to plead guilty to avoid a lengthy trial.
- She pleaded with the committee to reconsider the funding cut for graduate research.
- **考频**: 10
## petition

- **音标**: pi'tiʃәn
- **释义**: n. 请愿, 诉状, 陈情书, 申请, 祈求, 祷文\nv. 正式请求, 恳求, 请愿
- **英文释义**: A formal written request, typically signed by many people, appealing to authority for a specific action or change; or a legal document initiating a court proceeding.
- **词根词缀**: pet(寻求)+ition(名词)→寻求→请愿
- **例句**:
- Thousands of citizens signed a petition demanding stricter environmental regulations.
- The defendant filed a petition for habeas corpus to challenge the legality of his detention.
- **考频**: 3
## prayer

- **音标**: prєә. 'preiә
- **释义**: n. 祈祷, 恳求, 祷辞, 祈祷者
- **英文释义**: a solemn request to God or a god for help, guidance, or thanks; also refers to the words spoken during such an act
- **词根词缀**: pray(祈祷)+er(人)→祈祷者
- **例句**:
- Her daily prayer for strength and wisdom reflected her deep faith and resilience.
- The judge emphasized that justice must be served not by prayer but by evidence and due process.
- **考频**: 15
