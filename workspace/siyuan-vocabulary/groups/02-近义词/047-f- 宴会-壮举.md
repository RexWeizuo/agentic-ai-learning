## feasible

- **音标**: 'fi:zәbl
- **释义**: a. 能实行的, 可行的, 适宜的\n[法] 可行的, 可执行的, 可完成的
- **英文释义**: capable of being done or carried out; practical and realistic
- **词根词缀**: feas(做)+ible(形容词)→可做的→可行的
- **例句**:
- Given the current budget constraints, building a new research center is not feasible in the short term.
- The committee concluded that the proposed policy change was both legally sound and politically feasible.
- **考频**: 23
## feast

- **音标**: fi:st
- **释义**: n. 宴会, 酒席, 享受, 节日\nvt. 款待, 享乐, 请客\nvi. 参加宴会, 尽情地吃, 享受
- **英文释义**: a large, elaborate meal or celebration; to eat or enjoy something with great pleasure
- **词根词缀**: feast(节日)→盛宴
- **例句**:
- The royal wedding was followed by a lavish feast attended by dignitaries from over fifty countries.
- After months of rigorous preparation, she finally feasted on the fruits of her labor—acceptance to Oxford's graduate program.
- **考频**: 3
## feat

- **音标**: fi:t
- **释义**: n. 壮举, 功绩, 技艺表演\na. 灵巧的, 合适的, 整洁的
- **英文释义**: an achievement that requires great skill, effort, or courage
- **词根词缀**: feat(做)→功绩
- **例句**:
- Climbing Mount Everest without supplemental oxygen was a remarkable feat of endurance and willpower.
- Designing an AI system that passes the Turing test remains one of the greatest intellectual feats in computer science.
- **考频**: 7
## feature

- **音标**: 'fi:tʃә
- **释义**: n. 面孔的一部分(如眼、口等), 特征, 容貌, 特色, 特写\nvt. 是...的特色, 特写, 放映\nvi. 起重要作用\n[计] 特性
- **英文释义**: A distinctive attribute, part of the face, or a prominent element in a system, story, or event.
- **词根词缀**: feat(做)+ure(名词)→做出来的东西→特征
- **例句**:
- The film's most striking feature is its innovative use of non-linear narrative.
- Her high cheekbones and sharp jawline are defining features of her facial structure.
- **考频**: 223
## fellowship

- **音标**: 'felәuʃip
- **释义**: n. 伙伴关系, 共同参与, 团体, 奖学金, 友谊\n[经] 合伙关系, 研究员薪金, 研究员基金
- **英文释义**: A group of people with a shared interest or purpose; a grant or scholarship supporting advanced study or research; companionship based on mutual respect and common goals.
- **词根词缀**: fellow(伙伴)+ship(身份)→伙伴关系
- **例句**:
- The university awarded her a prestigious fellowship to pursue postdoctoral research in renewable energy.
- Building genuine fellowship across disciplines is essential for solving complex global challenges.
