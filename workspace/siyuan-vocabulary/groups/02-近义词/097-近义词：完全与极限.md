# 近义词：完全与极限

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: wholly (完全地)，utter (绝对的/全然的)，utmost (极限/极度)，ultimate (终极的/根本的)。

**4 词**

---

## wholly

- **音标**: 'hәuli
- **释义**: adv. 完全地, 整个, 统统, 全部\n[法] 完全地, 统统地
- **英文释义**: completely; entirely
- **词根词缀**: whole(完整)+ly(副词)→完全地
- **例句**:
- The project failed because it was wholly dependent on external funding.
- She was wholly unprepared for the sudden change in exam format.
- **考频**: 14
## utter

- **音标**: 'ʌtә
- **释义**: a. 全然的, 绝对的, 完全的\nvt. 发出声音, 做声, 发表, 发射, 流通
- **英文释义**: adj. complete and absolute; vt. to speak or express (a sound, word, or statement)
- **词根词缀**: ut(外)+er(动词)→说出
- **例句**:
- His failure was an utter disaster that undermined years of careful planning.
- She refused to utter a single word during the entire interrogation.
- **考频**: 19
## utmost

- **音标**: 'ʌtmәust
- **释义**: n. 极限, 最大限度, 极度\na. 极度的, 最远的
- **英文释义**: the greatest possible degree or amount; extreme
- **词根词缀**: ut(外)+most(最)→最外面的→极度的
- **例句**:
- She devoted her utmost attention to the complex data analysis required for the thesis.
- The research team made the utmost effort to ensure the validity and reliability of their experimental results.
- **考频**: 15
## ultimate

- **音标**: 'ʌltimit
- **释义**: n. 终极, 根本, 顶点, 基本原则\na. 终极的, 根本的, 极限的, 最远的, 最后的, 最大的
- **英文释义**: final, fundamental, or most extreme in degree or importance
- **词根词缀**: ultim(最后)+ate(形容词)→最后的
- **例句**:
- The ultimate goal of the research is to develop a cure for Alzheimer's disease.
- She pushed her endurance to the ultimate limit during the marathon.
- **考频**: 39
