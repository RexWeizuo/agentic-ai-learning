# 近义词 (巨大/宏大)

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 都表示“大”。giant/gigantic 侧重体型或规模巨大，grand 侧重宏伟、庄重，great 侧重伟大、重要。

**4 词**

---

## giant

- **音标**: 'dʒaiәnt
- **释义**: n. 巨人, 大力士, 巨大怪物\na. 庞大的, 巨大的
- **英文释义**: a very large person, creature, or thing; or a person of exceptional ability or influence
- **词根词缀**: giant(巨大)→巨人
- **例句**:
- The company has become a global giant in the field of renewable energy.
- In Greek mythology, the Titans were primordial giants who ruled before the Olympian gods.
- **考频**: 89
## gigantic

- **音标**: dʒai'gæntik
- **释义**: a. 巨人般的, 巨大的
- **英文释义**: extremely large in size, scale, or degree
- **词根词缀**: giant(巨大)+ic(形容词)→巨大的
- **例句**:
- The project required a gigantic effort from the entire research team.
- She faced a gigantic challenge when she decided to rebuild her startup from scratch after bankruptcy.
- **考频**: 15
## grand

- **音标**: grænd
- **释义**: a. 庄重的, 壮观的, 显赫的, 重大的, 最高的, 雄伟的, 宏大的, 豪华的, 傲慢的\n[法] 重大的, 主要的, 伟大的
- **英文释义**: impressive in size, appearance, or importance; magnificent or dignified
- **词根词缀**: grand→宏伟的
- **例句**:
- The palace features grand staircases and ornate ceilings.
- She delivered a grand speech that emphasized the historical significance of the reform.
- **考频**: 49
## great

- **音标**: greit
- **释义**: a. 大的, 非常的, 主要的, 重大的, 崇高的, 伟大的\nadv. 顺利地, 得意地\nn. 全部, 大人物, 大师
- **英文释义**: very large in size, degree, or importance; outstanding or remarkable
- **词根词缀**: great→伟大的
- **例句**:
- Her contribution to the field was of great significance in the development of modern linguistics.
- The conference was a great success, attracting scholars from over 30 countries.
- **考频**: 1166
