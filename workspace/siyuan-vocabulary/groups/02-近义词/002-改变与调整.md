# 改变与调整

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 表示“改变、适应、调整”的动词，注意 adapt（适应）与 adopt（采用）的拼写区别。

**6 词**

---

## alter

- **音标**: 'ɒ:ltә
- **释义**: ==v. 改变==
- **英文释义**: to make a change to something, typically in a way that affects its structure, character, or function
- **同义词**: change, modify
- **反义词**: maintain
- **词根词缀**: alter(其他)→变成其他→改变
- **搭配**: alter one's mind
- **例句**:
- The government plans to alter the tax policy to reduce income inequality.
- She altered her research methodology after reviewing new experimental data.
- **考频**: 107
## amend

- **音标**: ә'mend
- **释义**: ==vt. 修改, 改善, 改良\nvi. 改过自新==
- **英文释义**: to make minor changes to a text, law, or plan to improve it or correct errors
- **同义词**: modify, revise
- **词根词缀**: e(去掉)+mend(缺点)→去掉缺点→修正
- **例句**:
- The committee proposed to amend the draft regulation to strengthen data protection.
- The senator urged her colleagues to amend the bill before the final vote to address public concerns.
- **考频**: 4
## accommodate

- **音标**: ә'kɒmәdeit
- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: ==vt. 使适应, 调和, 通融, 容纳, 向...提供\nvi. 适应==
- **英文释义**: v. provide with something desired or needed\nv. have room for; hold without crowding\nv. make (one thing) compatible with (another)
- **同义词**: house, lodge
- **反义词**: exclude, reject
- **词根词缀**: ac(加强)+com(共同)+mod(模式)+ate→使适应模式→容纳
- **搭配**: accommodate someone's needs, accommodate a request, accommodate changes
- **例句**:
- The hotel can accommodate up to 300 guests.
- She had to accommodate her schedule to her child's school hours.
- **考频**: 29
## adjust

- **音标**: ә'dʒʌst
- **释义**: ==vt. 调整, 使适应于, 校准\nvi. 适应于, 被调节, 相互熟悉而适应==
- **英文释义**: v. alter or regulate so as to achieve accuracy or conform to a standard\nv. adapt or conform oneself to new or different conditions\nv. make correspondent or conformable\nv. decide how much is to be paid on an insurance claim
- **同义词**: modify, alter
- **词根词缀**: ad(加强)+just(正确的)→使正确→调整
- **搭配**: adjust to, adjust oneself to
- **例句**:
- You need to adjust the volume.
- She quickly adjusted to college life.
- **考频**: 76
## adapt

- **音标**: /əˈdæpt/
- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: ==适应；改编，改写==
- **英文释义**: to change your behavior to suit a new situation; to modify something for a new use
- **同义词**: adjust, modify, accommodate
- **反义词**: maintain, preserve
- **词根词缀**: ad(加强)+apt(适应)→适应
- **搭配**: adapt to, adapt for, adapt oneself to
- **例句**:
- We must adapt to the changing environment.
- The novel was adapted into a film.
- **考频**: 151
## adopt

- **音标**: ә'dɒpt
- **释义**: ==vt. 采用, 正式通过, 收养, 接受\n[医] 采取==
- **英文释义**: v. choose and follow; as of theories, ideas, policies, strategies or plans\nv. take up and practice as one's own\nv. take into one's family
- **同义词**: embrace, accept
- **反义词**: reject
- **词根词缀**: opt(选择)→选择采纳→采纳
- **搭配**: adopt a child, adopt an approach
- **例句**:
- They decided to adopt a child.
- We should adopt a different approach.
- **考频**: 147
