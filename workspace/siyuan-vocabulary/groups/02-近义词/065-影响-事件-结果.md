# 影响/事件/结果

> **类型**: 近义词

> **学习提示**: incidence 指发生率；incident 指事件或事变；incidentally 意为顺便提一句。

**6 词**

---

## impact

- **音标**: 'impækt
- **释义**: n. 冲击, 冲突, 影响, 效果\nvt. 挤入, 撞击, 压紧, 对...发生影响
- **英文释义**: a strong effect or influence on someone or something
- **词根词缀**: im(进入)+pact(压紧)→压入→冲击
- **例句**:
- The rapid development of artificial intelligence has a profound impact on the future of work.
- The policy was designed to mitigate the negative impact of inflation on low-income households.
- **考频**: 322
## influence

- **音标**: 'influәns
- **释义**: n. 影响力, 权力, 势力\nvt. 影响, 改变
- **英文释义**: the power to affect someone's behavior, decisions, or development without using direct force
- **词根词缀**: in(向内)+flu(流)+ence(名词)→流入→影响
- **例句**:
- Her research has had a profound influence on modern economic policy.
- Social media increasingly influences how young people perceive body image and self-worth.
- **考频**: 362
## influential

- **音标**: .infu'enʃәl
- **释义**: a. 有影响的, 有势力的
- **英文释义**: having great influence or authority, especially in a particular field or on public opinion
- **词根词缀**: in(向内)+flu(流)+ential(形容词)→有影响的
- **例句**:
- Her influential research on climate change has shaped national environmental policy.
- As an influential member of the committee, he played a key role in drafting the new education reform bill.
- **考频**: 56
## incidence

- **音标**: 'insidәns
- **释义**: n. 发生, 发生率, 影响, 负担者, 入射, 倾角\n[医] 入射, 发生率, 发生数
- **英文释义**: the rate or frequency at which something occurs, especially a disease or phenomenon in a population
- **词根词缀**: in(加强)+cid(落下)+ence(名词)→落下→发生率
- **例句**:
- The incidence of diabetes has risen sharply among young adults in recent decades.
- Public health officials monitor the incidence of infectious diseases to guide vaccination policies.
- **考频**: 22
## incident

- **音标**: 'insidәnt
- **释义**: n. 事件, 事变, 小事\na. 附带的, 易于发生的, 外来的, 入射的
- **英文释义**: a happening, especially one of limited scope and significance; often used for an unexpected or unpleasant event
- **词根词缀**: in(加强)+cid(落下)+ent(名词)→落下的事件→事件
- **例句**:
- The security team investigated the incident involving unauthorized access to the server.
- Although the border dispute was minor, it escalated into a diplomatic incident that drew international attention.
- **考频**: 53
## incidentally

- **音标**: .insi'dentәli
- **释义**: adv. 伴随地, 偶然, 顺便
- **英文释义**: by the way; as a secondary or unplanned point
- **词根词缀**: in(加强)+cid(落下)+ent(名词)+ally(副词)→偶然地
- **例句**:
- Incidentally, the data we discussed yesterday has just been updated.
- She mentioned, incidentally, that she’d already submitted the proposal before the deadline.
- **考频**: 13
