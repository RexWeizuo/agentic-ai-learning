# 近义词：精确/准确

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: precise/precision 强调“精确、严谨”；perfect 强调“完美、完成”；plausible 指“貌似合理的”。

**4 词**

---

## precise

- **音标**: pri'sais
- **释义**: a. 精确的, 严谨的, 明确的\n[机] 精密的, 正确的
- **英文释义**: exactly accurate and clear in detail
- **词根词缀**: pre(前)+cise(切)→预先切好→精确的
- **例句**:
- The experiment requires precise measurements to ensure valid results.
- She gave a precise definition of the term, leaving no room for ambiguity.
- **考频**: 35
## precision

- **音标**: pri'siʒәn
- **释义**: n. 精密, 精确, 精确度, 精度\na. 精密的, 精确的\n[计] 精度
- **英文释义**: the quality of being exact and accurate
- **词根词缀**: pre(前)+cise(切)+ion(名词)→精确
- **例句**:
- The experiment requires extreme precision in measuring temperature changes.
- Modern GPS systems operate with remarkable precision, enabling location accuracy within a few meters.
- **考频**: 22
## perfect

- **音标**: 'pә:fikt
- **释义**: n. 完成时\na. 完美的, 完好的, 理想的, 熟练的, 精确的, 完成式的\nvt. 使完美, 修改, 使精通, 改善, 使熟练
- **英文释义**: adj. having no flaws; complete and correct in every way; grammatically denoting a completed action; n. the perfect tense in grammar
- **词根词缀**: per(完全)+fect(做)→完全做好→完美的
- **例句**:
- Her command of classical Chinese poetry is perfect, reflecting years of rigorous scholarly training.
- The verb 'have' functions as an auxiliary in the present perfect tense, as in 'She has already finished the thesis draft.'
- **考频**: 115
## plausible

- **音标**: 'plɒ:zibl
- **释义**: a. 貌似真实的, 貌似合理的, 说得煞有其事的\n[法] 花言巧语的, 似乎有理的
- **英文释义**: seemingly reasonable or probable, though possibly false
- **词根词缀**: plaus(词根:鼓掌)+ible(形容词后缀)→看似合理的
- **例句**:
- His alibi sounded plausible, but further investigation revealed inconsistencies.
- The theory is scientifically plausible, yet it lacks empirical support.
- **考频**: 18
