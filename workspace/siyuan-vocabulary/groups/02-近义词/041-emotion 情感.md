# emotion 情感

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 情感、共鸣与感觉相关词汇

**4 词**

---

## emotion

- **音标**: i'mәuʃәn
- **释义**: n. 情绪, 激动, 强烈的情感\n[医] 情绪, 情感
- **英文释义**: a strong feeling such as love, fear, anger, or joy
- **词根词缀**: e(出)+mot(移动)+ion(名词)→移动出来→情感
- **例句**:
- Her voice trembled with emotion as she delivered the eulogy.
- Cognitive behavioral therapy helps patients recognize and regulate intense emotions.
- **考频**: 148
## empathy

- **音标**: 'empæθik
- **释义**: n. 移情作用, 共鸣\n[医] 移情, 神入
- **英文释义**: The ability to understand and share the feelings of another person.
- **词根词缀**: em(进入)+pathy(感情)→进入感情→共情
- **例句**:
- Her empathy toward patients made her an exceptional physician.
- Effective leadership requires not only authority but also genuine empathy for team members' challenges.
- **考频**: 19
## feeling

- **音标**: 'fi:liŋ
- **释义**: n. 摸, 触觉, 知觉, 感觉, 情绪, 同情\na. 有同情心的, 有感觉的, 仁慈的, 动人的
- **英文释义**: a physical sensation or an emotional state; the ability to perceive through touch or emotion
- **词根词缀**: feel(感觉)+ing(名词)→感觉
- **例句**:
- She had a strange feeling that something was wrong, which later proved true.
- His speech was filled with genuine feeling, moving the entire audience to tears.
- **考频**: 177
## excitement

- **音标**: ik'saitmәnt
- **释义**: n. 刺激, 兴奋\n[医] 兴奋, 激动
- **英文释义**: a feeling of great enthusiasm and eagerness
- **词根词缀**: ex(出)+cit(唤起)+e+ment(名词)→唤起出来→兴奋
- **例句**:
- The announcement of the breakthrough discovery triggered widespread excitement among researchers.
- Her voice trembled with excitement as she opened the envelope containing her graduate school admission letter.
- **考频**: 27
