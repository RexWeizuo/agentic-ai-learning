# 近义词：描绘/肖像/轮廓

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: portrait/portray 侧重人物肖像和描绘；profile 侧重侧面轮廓或人物简介；picture 为通用词。

**4 词**

---

## portrait

- **音标**: 'pɒ:treit
- **释义**: n. 肖像, 人像, 半身像, 描写, 竖排格式\n[计] 纵向
- **英文释义**: A painting, photograph, or other artistic representation of a person, typically showing the face and upper body; also, a vivid written description or a page orientation with height greater than width.
- **词根词缀**: port(描绘)+rait→描绘→肖像
- **例句**:
- The museum acquired a rare 17th-century portrait of Queen Elizabeth I.
- Her novel offers a nuanced portrait of urban alienation in contemporary society.
- **考频**: 10
## portray

- **音标**: pɒ:'trei
- **释义**: vt. 描绘, 描写, 描绘...的肖像
- **英文释义**: to represent someone or something in a work of art, literature, or film, especially in a particular way
- **词根词缀**: port(描绘)+ray→描绘→刻画
- **例句**:
- The novel portrays the protagonist as both vulnerable and fiercely resilient.
- Contemporary media often portray complex social issues with oversimplified narratives.
- **考频**: 27
## profile

- **音标**: 'prәufail
- **释义**: n. 侧面, 轮廓, 传略\nvt. 描绘...轮廓, 写...的传略\n[计] 提问档; 剖面图法; 剖面法
- **英文释义**: A side view of a person's face or head; a brief descriptive account of a person, organization, or topic; or a quantitative analysis of characteristics (e.g., in computing or science).
- **词根词缀**: pro(向前)+fil(线条)→前面的线条→轮廓
- **例句**:
- The journalist wrote a detailed profile of the Nobel laureate, highlighting both her scientific achievements and personal struggles.
- Network administrators use profiling tools to monitor system performance and identify potential security threats.
- **考频**: 30
## picture

- **音标**: 'piktʃә
- **释义**: n. 图画, 照片, 景色, 美丽如画的人(或物), 化身, 生动的描述, 想像, 形象思维\nvt. 画, 拍摄, 用图说明, 描写, 想像\n[计] 图象; 形象; 字形
- **英文释义**: a visual representation of something, such as a painting, photograph, or mental image
- **词根词缀**: pict(画)+ure(名词)→画→图片
- **例句**:
- The museum displayed a rare Renaissance picture depicting a biblical scene.
- It is difficult to picture life in the 18th century without consulting primary historical sources.
- **考频**: 271
