# 近义词：刺/探/钻

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: penetrate(穿透/渗透), poke(戳/刺), probe(探索/探针), plunge(猛冲/跳入)。

**4 词**

---

## penetrate

- **音标**: 'penitreit
- **释义**: vt. 穿透, 刺穿, 渗透, 看穿, 洞察, 了解, 使充溢\nvi. 刺入, 看穿, 渗透, 打进
- **英文释义**: to pass into or through something, especially with force or gradually; to understand or perceive something deeply or clearly
- **词根词缀**: penetr(进入)+ate(动词)→进入→穿透
- **例句**:
- The laser beam can penetrate even the toughest metal alloys.
- Her sharp analysis penetrated the superficial arguments and revealed the underlying bias.
- **考频**: 7
## poke

- **音标**: pәuk
- **释义**: n. 刺, 戳, 袋子\nvt. 戳, 捅, 拨弄, 刺\nvi. 戳, 刺, 捅, 搜索, 伸出, 行动散慢\n[计] 存数
- **英文释义**: to push or prod something lightly with a finger, stick, or pointed object; also refers to a light jab or a small bag/pouch
- **词根词缀**: poke(词根:刺)→戳
- **例句**:
- She gave the sleeping cat a gentle poke to wake it up.
- The programmer used a 'poke' instruction to write a value directly into a specific memory address.
- **考频**: 7
## probe

- **音标**: prәub
- **释义**: n. 探索, 调查, 探针, 探测器\nv. 用探针探测, 调查, 探索
- **英文释义**: to examine or investigate something carefully, especially to discover facts or hidden information; a device used for examining or measuring something
- **词根词缀**: prob(证明)+e→证明的工具→探针
- **例句**:
- The journalist decided to probe deeper into the government's financial records.
- Scientists used a robotic probe to explore the surface of Mars.
- **考频**: 17
## plunge

- **音标**: plʌndʒ
- **释义**: n. 钻进, 跳进, 跳水, 跳水池, 猛跌, 落下, 投入, 开始从事, 盲目投资\nvi. 投入, 投身于, 跳进, 陷入, 下降\nvt. 使投入, 使插入, 使陷入, 使遭受
- **英文释义**: to move or cause to move suddenly and forcefully downward or into something
- **词根词缀**: plunge(词根:跳入)→跳入
- **例句**:
- The stock market took a sharp plunge after the unexpected policy announcement.
- She decided to plunge into the demanding research project without prior experience.
- **考频**: 31
