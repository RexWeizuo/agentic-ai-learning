# 主题/近义 (发光与视觉)

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 与光和视觉相关。flare 是闪耀/爆发，glare 是刺眼的光，glitter 是闪烁，glow 是红光/发热，glimpse 是一瞥。

**5 词**

---

## flare

- **音标**: 'flєә
- **释义**: n. 闪光, 闪耀\nvi. 闪光, 闪耀\nvt. 用发光信号发出, 使闪耀, 使张开
- **英文释义**: a sudden burst of light, flame, or color; or to shine or burn suddenly and brightly
- **词根词缀**: flare(闪耀)→闪耀
- **例句**:
- The flare from the explosion temporarily blinded everyone nearby.
- Tensions between the two countries flared up again after the diplomatic incident.
- **考频**: 3
## glare

- **音标**: glєә
- **释义**: n. 闪耀光, 刺眼\nvi. 发眩光, 瞪视\nvt. 瞪眼表示
- **英文释义**: a bright, harsh light that is difficult to look at; to stare angrily or intensely
- **词根词缀**: glare(闪耀)→怒视
- **例句**:
- The glare of the midday sun made it hard to see the road ahead.
- She glared at him in silence, her disapproval unmistakable.
- **考频**: 7
## glitter

- **音标**: 'glitә
- **释义**: n. 灿烂, 闪烁, 辉耀\nvi. 灿烂, 闪烁, 辉耀
- **英文释义**: bright, sparkling light or appearance
- **词根词缀**: glitter(闪烁)→闪烁
- **例句**:
- The mountain peaks glittered in the morning sun.
- Her argument lacked substance, relying instead on superficial glitter and rhetorical flourish.
- **考频**: 8
## glow

- **音标**: glәu
- **释义**: n. 赤热, 光辉, 热情, 热烈, 红光\nvi. 发红光, 红光焕发, 发热, 洋溢, 激动
- **英文释义**: to emit a steady, soft light or heat; to show visible warmth, excitement, or vitality
- **词根词缀**: glow(发光)→发光
- **例句**:
- Her face began to glow with pride after receiving the scholarship.
- The embers in the fireplace continued to glow long after the flames had died down.
- **考频**: 12
## glimpse

- **音标**: glimps
- **释义**: n. 一瞥, 一闪\nvi. 投以一瞥, 闪烁不定\nvt. 瞥见
- **英文释义**: a brief or partial view of something
- **词根词缀**: glimpse(瞥见)→一瞥
- **例句**:
- She caught a glimpse of the CEO entering the conference room just before the door closed.
- The report offers only a fleeting glimpse into the complexities of global supply chain dynamics.
- **考频**: 17
