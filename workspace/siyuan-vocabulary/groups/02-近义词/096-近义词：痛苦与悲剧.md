# 近义词：痛苦与悲剧

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: torment 和 torture 侧重身心折磨，tragedy/tragic 指悲剧，turmoil 指社会或内心的骚动混乱。

**5 词**

---

## torment

- **音标**: 'tɒ:ment
- **释义**: n. 使人痛苦的东西, 折磨者, 苦痛\nvt. 使苦恼, 纠缠, 歪曲, 拷问
- **英文释义**: severe physical or mental suffering; to cause intense pain or distress
- **词根词缀**: tor(扭曲)+ment(名词)→折磨
- **例句**:
- The survivor still suffers from the torment of wartime memories.
- She was tormented by self-doubt throughout her preparation for the graduate entrance exam.
- **考频**: 4
## torture

- **音标**: 'tɒ:tʃә
- **释义**: n. 拷问, 苦闷\nvt. 拷问, 曲解, 折磨, 使弯曲
- **英文释义**: the act of causing severe physical or mental pain to extract information, punish, or intimidate; or the state of extreme suffering
- **词根词缀**: tort(扭曲)+ure(名词)→折磨
- **例句**:
- The prisoner was subjected to torture during illegal interrogation.
- She felt torture watching her presentation slide malfunction repeatedly in front of the entire faculty.
- **考频**: 6
## tragedy

- **音标**: 'trædʒidi
- **释义**: n. 悲剧, 惨案, 悲剧作品\n[法] 惨事, 灾难, 不辛
- **英文释义**: A very sad event or situation, especially one involving death or suffering; a serious drama with an unhappy ending.
- **词根词缀**: trag(山羊)+edy(诗)→悲剧
- **例句**:
- The plane crash was a profound tragedy that claimed over 200 lives.
- Shakespeare's 'Hamlet' is widely regarded as one of the greatest tragedies in English literature.
- **考频**: 42
## tragic

- **音标**: 'trædʒik
- **释义**: a. 悲惨的, 悲剧的
- **英文释义**: causing or characterized by great sadness or suffering; relating to tragedy
- **词根词缀**: trag(山羊)+ic(形容词)→悲剧的
- **例句**:
- The plane crash was a tragic event that claimed over 200 lives.
- Her early death at age 32 was a tragic loss to the scientific community.
- **考频**: 14
## turmoil

- **音标**: 'tә:mɒil
- **释义**: n. 骚动, 混乱
- **英文释义**: a state of great confusion, uncertainty, or disorder
- **词根词缀**: turb(搅动)+moil(劳动)→搅动劳动→混乱
- **例句**:
- The country descended into political turmoil after the sudden resignation of the prime minister.
- Her personal life was in turmoil following the breakup, making it difficult to focus on her graduate studies.
- **考频**: 5
