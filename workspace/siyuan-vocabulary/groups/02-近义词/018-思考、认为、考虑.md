# 思考、认为、考虑

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 表示深思熟虑或认为的动词，注意 contemplate 侧重沉思，deliberate 侧重仔细权衡。

**4 词**

---

## consider

- **音标**: kәn'sidŋ
- **释义**: v. 考虑, 思考, 认为
- **英文释义**: to think carefully about something before making a decision or forming an opinion
- **词根词缀**: con(加强)+sider(星)→观察星象→考虑
- **例句**:
- The committee is still considering the proposal for environmental reform.
- Many scholars consider this theory to be the most compelling explanation of economic inequality.
- **考频**: 560
## contemplate

- **音标**: 'kɒntempleit
- **释义**: vt. 注视, 沉思, 盘算\nvi. 冥思苦想
- **英文释义**: to think deeply and carefully about something, often before making a decision
- **词根词缀**: con(加强)+templ(观察)+ate(动词)→仔细观察→沉思
- **例句**:
- She spent the evening contemplating the ethical implications of the new policy.
- The committee is still contemplating whether to approve the controversial funding proposal.
- **考频**: 13
## deliberate

- **音标**: di'libәrәt
- **释义**: a. 深思熟虑的, 故意的, 从容的\nv. 仔细考虑
- **英文释义**: done consciously and intentionally after careful thought; or to think carefully about something before making a decision
- **词根词缀**: de(加强)+liber(平衡)+ate(动词)→平衡→仔细考虑
- **例句**:
- The committee made a deliberate decision to postpone the policy implementation until further evidence was gathered.
- Before submitting her thesis, she deliberately revised each paragraph to ensure logical coherence and academic rigor.
- **考频**: 33
## deem

- **音标**: di:m
- **释义**: v. 认为, 相信
- **英文释义**: to consider or judge something in a particular way
- **词根词缀**: deem(认为)→认为
- **例句**:
- The court deemed the evidence insufficient to support the conviction.
- Many experts deem this policy shift as a critical step toward sustainable development.
- **考频**: 25
