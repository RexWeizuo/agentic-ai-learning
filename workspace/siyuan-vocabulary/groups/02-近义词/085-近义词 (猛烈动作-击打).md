# 近义词 (猛烈动作/击打)

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 猛烈或突然的动作。shove(推/挤), slam(砰然关上), slap(掴/拍击), snap(猛咬/折断)。

**4 词**

---

## shove

- **音标**: ʃʌv
- **释义**: n. 推, 挤\nvt. 推挤, 猛推, 强使\nvi. 推
- **英文释义**: to push someone or something roughly or forcefully
- **词根词缀**: shove(推)→推
- **例句**:
- He gave the heavy door a hard shove to get it open.
- She shoved her way through the crowded subway platform to catch the last train.
- **考频**: 4
## slam

- **音标**: slæm
- **释义**: n. 砰然声, 猛然, 猛烈的抨击\nvt. 猛然关上, 砰地关上, 猛烈抨击\nvi. 砰地关上, 猛攻
- **英文释义**: A loud, forceful sound or action, especially of something being closed violently; to criticize harshly or attack vigorously.
- **词根词缀**: slam(砰地关上)→猛击
- **例句**:
- The door slammed shut as she stormed out of the room.
- The editorial slammed the government's new policy as reckless and ill-informed.
- **考频**: 3
## slap

- **音标**: slæp
- **释义**: n. 掴, 掌击, 侮辱, 拍击声\nv. 拍击, 惩罚\nadv. 正面地, 直接地, 突然地
- **英文释义**: to hit someone or something with the open hand, often producing a sharp sound; also used figuratively to mean a sudden, harsh rebuke or setback
- **词根词缀**: slap(拍打)→拍打
- **例句**:
- The judge's ruling was seen as a slap in the face to the entire industry.
- She slapped the table in frustration, making everyone jump.
- **考频**: 6
## snap

- **音标**: snæp
- **释义**: vt. 使突然中断, 猛咬, 争夺, 拉断, 使有啪啪声, 厉声说, 突然射击, 用快照拍摄\nvi. 咬, 扑, 抓, 折断, 劈啪地响, 厉声说, 砰然关上\nn. 猛咬, 猛扑, 劈啪声, 申斥, 快照, 活力, 一般时期的寒冷天气, 揿钮\na. 突然的, 装搭扣的\nadv. 猛地\n[计] 子网访问协议
- **英文释义**: To break or cause to break suddenly with a sharp cracking sound; to speak or act abruptly and sharply; to take a quick photograph.
- **词根词缀**: snap(拟声词根)→折断
- **例句**:
- The tension between them snapped after years of unresolved conflict.
- She snapped a photo of the protesters before the police dispersed the crowd.
- **考频**: 21
