# 混合/整合/同质

> **类型**: 近义词

> **学习提示**: homogeneous (同种的/同质的) 反义为 heterogeneous；integrity 除了正直，还有完整性的意思。

**6 词**

---

## homogeneous

- **音标**: .hәumә'dʒi:niәs
- **释义**: a. 同种的, 同质的, 齐性的\n[医] 同种的, 纯一的, 同质的, 均匀的
- **英文释义**: Of the same or similar kind, nature, or structure; uniform in composition or character.
- **词根词缀**: homo(相同)+gen(产生)+eous(形容词)→产生相同的→同质的
- **例句**:
- The research team ensured that the sample was homogeneous before conducting the statistical analysis.
- A homogeneous population simplifies epidemiological studies but may limit the generalizability of the findings.
- **考频**: 7
## hybrid

- **音标**: 'haibrid
- **释义**: n. 混血儿, 杂种, 混合物\na. 混合的, 杂种的, 混合语的\n[计] NetWare的主机实用程序, 双重用户建立程序
- **英文释义**: a thing made by combining two different elements, especially in biology, technology, or culture
- **词根词缀**: hybrid(混合)→杂交种
- **例句**:
- The new electric car is a hybrid of traditional combustion engine and battery-powered systems.
- Her novel is a hybrid of memoir and fiction, blending personal experience with imaginative storytelling.
- **考频**: 14
## incorporate

- **音标**: in'kɒ:pәreit
- **释义**: a. 合并的, 组成公司的, 一体化的\nvt. 吸收, 合并, 使组成公司, 体现\nvi. 合并, 混合, 组成公司
- **英文释义**: to include something as a part of a whole; to form a company with legal status
- **词根词缀**: in(进入)+corpor(身体)+ate(动词)→使成为一体→合并
- **例句**:
- The new environmental regulations will be incorporated into the company's compliance policy.
- Many startups choose to incorporate in Delaware due to its business-friendly laws.
- **考频**: 26
## integrate

- **音标**: 'intigreit
- **释义**: vt. 综合, 使完整, 使成整体\nvi. 成一体\na. 完整的, 完全的
- **英文释义**: to combine parts into a whole; to make something part of a larger unit or system
- **词根词缀**: in(不)+teg(接触)+rate(动词)→使完整→整合
- **例句**:
- The new curriculum aims to integrate science and humanities to foster interdisciplinary thinking.
- After the merger, the two companies struggled to integrate their IT systems and corporate cultures.
- **考频**: 46
## integral

- **音标**: 'intigrәl
- **释义**: n. 整体, 积分, 整数\na. 固有的, 整体的, 整数的, 积分的\n[计] 积分
- **英文释义**: Essential and necessary for completeness; in mathematics, a function representing the area under a curve or a whole number.
- **词根词缀**: in(不)+teg(接触)+ral(形容词)→未接触→完整的
- **例句**:
- Teamwork is an integral part of successful project management.
- To solve the differential equation, we must compute the definite integral of the given function over the interval [0, 1].
- **考频**: 13
## integrity

- **音标**: in'tegriti
- **释义**: n. 正直, 廉正, 完整\n[计] 完整性
- **英文释义**: the quality of being honest and having strong moral principles; the state of being whole and undivided
- **词根词缀**: in(不)+teg(接触)+rity(名词)→完整→正直
- **例句**:
- Her integrity was never in doubt, even when she faced intense pressure to compromise her values.
- Data integrity is essential in database management to ensure information remains accurate and consistent over its entire lifecycle.
- **考频**: 35
