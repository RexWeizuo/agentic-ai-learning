# 控制、限制、容纳

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 表示对事物或行为的约束与控制，curb 常指抑制不良情绪或行为。

**4 词**

---

## contain

- **音标**: kәn'tein
- **释义**: vt. 包含, 容纳, 控制\nvi. 自制
- **英文释义**: to hold or include something within limits; to keep under control
- **词根词缀**: con(一起)+tain(保持)→保持在一起→包含
- **例句**:
- The report contains detailed statistical analysis of regional economic trends.
- Despite the provocation, she managed to contain her anger and responded calmly.
- **考频**: 331
## control

- **音标**: kәn'trәul
- **释义**: n. 控制, 管理, 克制, 控制器, 操纵装置\nvt. 控制, 操纵, 抑制\n[计] 控制; 控制器
- **英文释义**: the power to influence or direct people's behavior or the course of events; to regulate or restrain an action, feeling, or process
- **词根词缀**: contra(相反)+rol(轮子)→相反轮子→控制
- **例句**:
- Effective time management is essential for students to maintain control over their academic workload.
- The software allows administrators to remotely control multiple devices on the network.
- **考频**: 524
## constrain

- **音标**: kәn'strein
- **释义**: vt. 强迫, 限制, 关押
- **英文释义**: to force or restrict someone or something within certain limits
- **词根词缀**: con(加强)+strain(拉紧)→拉紧→约束
- **例句**:
- Tight budgetary constraints constrain the government's ability to invest in infrastructure.
- Scientists must constrain their hypotheses with empirical evidence to ensure academic rigor.
- **考频**: 17
## curb

- **音标**: kә:b
- **释义**: n. 抑制, 勒马绳, 边石\nvt. 抑制, 束缚, 勒住
- **英文释义**: to control or limit something, especially something negative or harmful
- **词根词缀**: curb(弯曲)→使弯曲→抑制
- **例句**:
- The government introduced new regulations to curb inflation.
- She struggled to curb her impulsive spending habits.
- **考频**: 38
