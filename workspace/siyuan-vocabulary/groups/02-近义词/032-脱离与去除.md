# 脱离与去除

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 表示从原有状态、位置或关系中分离、脱离或剥夺的动词。

**5 词**

---

## deviate

- **音标**: 'di:vieit
- **释义**: vi. 脱离, 偏离\nvt. 使偏离\na. 脱离常规的\nn. 脱离常规的人
- **英文释义**: to depart from an established course, standard, or norm
- **词根词缀**: de(偏离)+vi(路)+ate(动词)→偏离道路→偏离
- **例句**:
- The experiment's results deviated significantly from the theoretical predictions.
- While the essay was well-structured, it occasionally deviated from the assigned topic, weakening its coherence.
- **考频**: 4
## detach

- **音标**: di'tætʃ
- **释义**: vt. 使分离, 分遣\n[机] 摘下, 分离, 卸下
- **英文释义**: to separate or disconnect something from something else
- **词根词缀**: de(分开)+tach(钉)→分开→拆卸
- **例句**:
- The surgeon carefully detached the tumor from the surrounding tissue.
- She managed to detach herself emotionally from the outcome of the experiment.
- **考频**: 17
## divorce

- **音标**: di'vɒ:s
- **释义**: n. 离婚\nvt. 与...离婚
- **英文释义**: the legal ending of a marriage
- **词根词缀**: di(分开)+vorc(转)+e→转开→离婚
- **例句**:
- After ten years of marital discord, they filed for divorce last spring.
- The court ruled that the couple must divorce and divide their assets equitably.
- **考频**: 66
## disappear

- **音标**: .disә'piә
- **释义**: vi. 消失, 不见
- **英文释义**: to cease to be visible or to no longer exist
- **词根词缀**: dis(否定)+appear(出现)→不出现→消失
- **例句**:
- The sun disappeared behind the clouds just before the storm broke.
- Many ancient species have disappeared due to climate change and habitat loss.
- **考频**: 89
## deprive

- **音标**: di'praiv
- **释义**: vt. 剥夺, 使丧失\n[法] 剥夺, 剥夺, 夺去
- **英文释义**: to take something away from someone, especially something that is necessary or important
- **词根词缀**: de(去掉)+prive(私人)→剥夺
- **例句**:
- Prolonged sleep deprivation can deprive individuals of cognitive clarity and emotional stability.
- The court ruled that the government could not deprive citizens of their fundamental rights without due process.
- **考频**: 62
