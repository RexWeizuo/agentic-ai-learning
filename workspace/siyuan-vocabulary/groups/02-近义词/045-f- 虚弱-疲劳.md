# f- 虚弱/疲劳

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 褪色、昏厥与疲劳、虚弱相关词汇

**4 词**

---

## fade

- **音标**: feid
- **释义**: vi. 褪色, 消失, 凋谢\nvt. 使褪色\nn. 淡入, 淡出\na. 平淡的
- **英文释义**: to gradually disappear, lose color or strength, or become less noticeable
- **词根词缀**: fad(消失)+e→逐渐消失
- **例句**:
- The ink on the old document began to fade after years of exposure to sunlight.
- As the debate dragged on, public interest in the issue started to fade.
- **考频**: 38
## faint

- **音标**: feint
- **释义**: n. 昏厥, 昏倒\na. 模糊的, 微弱的, 无力的\nvi. 昏倒, 变得微弱
- **英文释义**: to lose consciousness temporarily due to insufficient blood flow to the brain; also: lacking strength, clarity, or intensity
- **词根词缀**: faint(虚弱)→虚弱的
- **例句**:
- She felt dizzy and had to sit down before she fainted in the crowded lecture hall.
- The faint sound of distant thunder barely penetrated the thick library walls.
- **考频**: 16
## fatigue

- **音标**: fә'ti:g
- **释义**: n. 疲乏, 疲劳\nvt. 使疲劳, 使心智衰弱\nvi. 疲劳\na. 疲劳的
- **英文释义**: physical or mental exhaustion caused by prolonged effort, stress, or illness
- **词根词缀**: fatig(疲劳)+ue→疲劳
- **例句**:
- Prolonged screen time can lead to visual fatigue and reduced concentration.
- The surgeon refused to perform the operation, citing severe fatigue after a 16-hour shift.
- **考频**: 42
## feeble

- **音标**: fi:bl
- **释义**: a. 微弱的, 衰弱的, 无效的\n[医] 无力的, 衰弱的
- **英文释义**: lacking physical strength, energy, or power; weak and ineffective
- **词根词缀**: feebl(哭泣)+e→虚弱的
- **例句**:
- His feeble attempt to refute the argument was dismissed by the panel.
- The patient's feeble pulse and shallow breathing indicated severe cardiovascular compromise.
- **考频**: 8
