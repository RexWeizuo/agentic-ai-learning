# 接近与邻近

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 表示“接近、邻近、入口”的词汇，adjacent 指“毗连的”，approximate 指“大约的/近似的”。

**4 词**

## approach

- **音标**: ә'prәutʃ
- **释义**: ==n. 接近, 入门\nvt. 接近, 近似, 找...商量\nvi. 靠近==
- **英文释义**: n. ideas or actions intended to deal with a problem or situation\nn. the act of drawing spatially closer to something\nn. the event of one object coming closer to another\nn. the temporal property of becoming nearer in time
- **同义词**: near, method
- **反义词**: retreat
- **词根词缀**: ap(朝向)+proach(接近)→接近
- **搭配**: approach to, approach the problem
- **例句**:
- The train approached the station.
- We need a new approach.
- **考频**: 273
## approximate

- **音标**: ә'prɒksimәt
- **释义**: ==a. 大约的, 接近的, 近似的\nvt. 接近, 使接近, 粗略估计\nvi. 接近于==
- **英文释义**: not exact but close enough to be useful; to come near to in value, amount, or quality
- **同义词**: rough, estimated
- **反义词**: exact
- **词根词缀**: ap(朝向)+proxim(最近)+ate→接近的→大约的
- **例句**:
- The project will cost an approximate total of $2 million.
- As the deadline approaches, the model's predictions begin to approximate the observed data more closely.
- **考频**: 2
