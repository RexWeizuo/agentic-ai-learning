# 区分与辨别

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 表示识别差异、区分不同事物的动词及名词，注意 discrepancy 侧重于数据或事实的出入。

**5 词**

---

## distinguish

- **音标**: dis'tiŋgwiʃ
- **释义**: v. 区别, 辨别
- **英文释义**: to recognize or show the difference between two or more things
- **词根词缀**: dis(分开)+sting(刺)+uish(动词)→分开刺→区分
- **例句**:
- It is crucial for medical students to distinguish between symptoms of similar diseases.
- The essay fails to distinguish fact from opinion, undermining its credibility.
- **考频**: 90
## distinct

- **音标**: dis'tiŋkt
- **释义**: a. 清楚的, 显著的, 不同的
- **英文释义**: easily perceived or understood; clearly different or separate
- **词根词缀**: dis(分开)+stinct(刺)→刺开→明显的
- **例句**:
- The two theories offer distinct explanations for the same phenomenon.
- She has a distinct advantage over her competitors due to her bilingual proficiency.
- **考频**: 36
## distinction

- **音标**: dis'tiŋkʃәn
- **释义**: n. 区别
- **英文释义**: a difference or contrast between similar things
- **词根词缀**: dis(分开)+stinct(刺)+ion(名词)→刺开→区别
- **例句**:
- The distinction between 'affect' and 'effect' is often confused by English learners.
- Her thesis makes a clear distinction between correlation and causation, which impressed the examiners.
- **考频**: 65
## discriminate

- **音标**: dis'krimineit
- **释义**: v. 区别, 差别待遇
- **英文释义**: to recognize or treat different things or people in different ways, especially unfairly
- **词根词缀**: dis(分开)+crimin(区分)+ate(动词)→区分开→歧视
- **例句**:
- The law prohibits employers from discriminating against job applicants on the basis of race or gender.
- Children as young as five can discriminate between right and wrong, but may struggle to apply those judgments consistently.
- **考频**: 13
## discrepancy

- **音标**: dis'krepәnsi
- **释义**: n. 不符合, 差异, 不相符之处\n[化] 不符值; 偏差值
- **英文释义**: A difference or inconsistency between two or more facts, figures, or statements.
- **词根词缀**: dis(分开)+crep(嘎吱声)+ancy(名词)→不一致的声音→差异
- **例句**:
- There is a significant discrepancy between the reported sales figures and the actual audit results.
- The researcher noted a minor discrepancy in the experimental data that required further investigation.
- **考频**: 8
