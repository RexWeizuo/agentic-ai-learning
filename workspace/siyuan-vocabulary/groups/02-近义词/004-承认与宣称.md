# 承认与宣称

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 表示“承认、断言、宣称”的动词，allege 常指“无证据的宣称”，ascribe 意为“归因于”。

**6 词**

---

## acknowledge

- **音标**: /əkˈnɒlɪdʒ/
- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: ==承认；认可；感谢==
- **英文释义**: to accept or admit that something is true; to thank somebody publicly
- **同义词**: admit, recognize, confess, accept
- **反义词**: deny, reject, ignore
- **词根词缀**: ac(加强)+knowledge(知道)→承认
- **搭配**: acknowledge responsibility, acknowledge the fact, widely acknowledged
- **例句**:
- He acknowledged his mistake publicly.
- She is widely acknowledged as an expert.
- **考频**: 76
## affirm

- **音标**: ә'fә:m
- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: ==v. 断言, 确认==
- **英文释义**: v. to declare or affirm solemnly and formally as true\nv. say yes to
- **同义词**: declare, assert
- **反义词**: deny
- **词根词缀**: af(加强)+firm(坚定)→坚定地声明→断言
- **搭配**: affirm a belief, affirm a decision, affirm one's identity
- **例句**:
- The court affirmed the lower court's decision.
- She affirmed her commitment to environmental sustainability in the interview.
- **考频**: 6
## allege

- **音标**: ә'ledʒ
- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: ==vt. 宣称, 主张, 提出, 断言\n[法] 断言, 指称, 指证==
- **英文释义**: v. report or maintain
- **同义词**: claim, assert
- **反义词**: deny, refute
- **词根词缀**: al(加强)+leg(法律)+e→在法律上宣称→宣称
- **搭配**: allege fraud, allege misconduct, allege wrongdoing
- **例句**:
- The plaintiff alleged that the company had violated environmental regulations.
- She alleged discrimination in the workplace but provided no concrete evidence.
- **考频**: 9
## announce

- **音标**: ә'nauns
- **释义**: ==vt. 宣布, 声称, 显示, 预告\nvi. 当报幕员, 宣布参加竞选==
- **英文释义**: to make a formal or public statement about something
- **词根词缀**: an(加强)+nounc(报告)+e→报告→宣布
- **例句**:
- The company announced its quarterly earnings yesterday, which exceeded market expectations.
- She announced her candidacy for mayor at a press conference held in the city hall.
- **考频**: 76
## ascertain

- **音标**: .æsә'tein
- **释义**: ==vt. 确定, 查明\n[法] 查明, 弄清, 确定==
- **英文释义**: to find out or establish something with certainty, especially through investigation or examination
- **词根词缀**: as(加强)+certain(确定)→确定
- **例句**:
- The committee was tasked with ascertaining the cause of the financial irregularities.
- Before submitting the research proposal, she spent two weeks ascertaining whether similar studies had already been published.
- **考频**: 7
## ascribe

- **音标**: /əˈskraɪb/
- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: ==把…归因于；把…归于==
- **英文释义**: to say or think that something is caused by something else
- **同义词**: attribute, assign, credit, impute
- **反义词**: disassociate, detach
- **词根词缀**: a(加强)+scribe(写)→归到…名下→归因于
- **搭配**: ascribe to, ascribe importance to, ascribe a cause
- **例句**:
- He ascribed his success to hard work.
- The painting is ascribed to da Vinci.
- **考频**: 15
