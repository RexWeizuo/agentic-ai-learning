# 咨询、协商、授予

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 寻求建议或给予指导，counsel 可作名词“忠告”或“法律顾问”。

**4 词**

---

## consult

- **音标**: kәn'sʌlt
- **释义**: vi. 商讨, 商量, 协商, 会诊\nvt. 向...请教, 查阅, 考虑
- **英文释义**: to seek advice or information from someone or something, or to discuss something with others to reach a decision
- **词根词缀**: con(共同)+sult(坐)→坐在一起→咨询
- **例句**:
- Before finalizing the research design, the team consulted several experts in epidemiology.
- She consulted the Oxford English Dictionary to verify the precise usage of the archaic term.
- **考频**: 51
## consultant

- **音标**: kәn'sʌltәnt
- **释义**: n. 顾问, 征询意见者\n[医] 顾问医师
- **英文释义**: A professional who provides expert advice in a specific field, often on a contractual or temporary basis.
- **词根词缀**: con(一起)+sult(坐)+ant(人)→坐在一起的人→顾问
- **例句**:
- The company hired an IT consultant to redesign its cybersecurity infrastructure.
- As a senior consultant at the hospital, she oversees complex surgical cases and mentors junior physicians.
- **考频**: 31
## counsel

- **音标**: 'kaunsәl
- **释义**: n. 商议, 忠告, 法律顾问\nv. 商议, 劝告
- **英文释义**: advice given to help someone make a decision; or a lawyer who gives legal advice or represents a client in court
- **词根词缀**: counsel(建议)→建议
- **例句**:
- The judge urged both parties to seek independent counsel before signing the settlement agreement.
- Her mentor's counsel proved invaluable when she faced a critical career decision.
- **考频**: 8
## confer

- **音标**: kәn'fә:
- **释义**: vt. 授予, 带来\nvi. 协商
- **英文释义**: to grant something formal, such as a degree or honor; to discuss something seriously with others
- **词根词缀**: con(一起)+fer(带来)→带来一起→商议
- **例句**:
- The university will confer an honorary doctorate on the renowned scientist at graduation.
- Before finalizing the policy, the committee members conferred for two hours to weigh its potential impacts.
- **考频**: 15
