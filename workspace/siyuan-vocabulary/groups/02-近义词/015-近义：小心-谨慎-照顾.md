# 近义：小心/谨慎/照顾

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: care/careful 侧重“关心、照料、小心”，caution/cautious 侧重“谨慎、警惕、避免危险”。

**4 词**

---

## care

- **音标**: kєә
- **释义**: n. 小心, 照料, 忧虑\nvi. 关心, 介意\nvt. 在意, 喜欢
- **英文释义**: attention and effort to avoid harm or error; responsibility for someone's health or well-being; concern or worry; to feel that something is important or matters
- **词根词缀**: care(关心)→关心
- **例句**:
- She handled the fragile documents with great care to prevent any damage.
- Many young adults hesitate to discuss mental health issues, even though they deeply care about their own well-being.
- **考频**: 607
## careful

- **音标**: 'kєәful
- **释义**: a. 小心的, 谨慎的
- **英文释义**: showing caution to avoid danger, mistakes, or harm
- **词根词缀**: care(关心)+ful(充满)→充满关心的→小心的
- **例句**:
- She was careful not to reveal any confidential information during the interview.
- The researchers were careful to control for confounding variables in their experimental design.
- **考频**: 90
## caution

- **音标**: 'kɒ:ʃәn
- **释义**: n. 小心, 慎重, 警示\nvt. 警告\n[计] 警告
- **英文释义**: careful attention to avoid danger or mistakes; a warning to be careful
- **词根词缀**: caut(小心)+ion(名词)→小心→谨慎
- **例句**:
- The judge urged caution in interpreting the new regulation, emphasizing its ambiguous wording.
- Before investing, financial advisors always caution clients against putting all their capital into a single volatile asset.
- **考频**: 40
## cautious

- **音标**: /ˈkɔːʃəs/
- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 小心的，谨慎的
- **英文释义**: careful to avoid potential problems or dangers
- **同义词**: careful, prudent, wary, watchful
- **反义词**: careless, reckless, rash
- **词根词缀**: caut(小心)+ious→小心的
- **搭配**: highly cautious, cautious approach, cautious optimism
- **例句**:
- Be cautious when investing money.
- She gave a cautious response.
- **考频**: 49
