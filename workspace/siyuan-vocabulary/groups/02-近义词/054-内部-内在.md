# 内部/内在

> **类型**: 近义词

> **学习提示**: inner/internal/interior 多指物理或抽象的内部；inherent/intrinsic 强调事物固有的、本质的内在属性。

**6 词**

---

## inner

- **音标**: 'inә
- **释义**: a. 内部的, 内心的\nn. 内部
- **英文释义**: located or existing inside; relating to the mind or emotions
- **词根词缀**: in(在…内)+ner(比较级)→更内的→内部的
- **例句**:
- The inner workings of the human brain remain largely mysterious to scientists.
- She struggled to confront her inner fears during the mindfulness retreat.
- **考频**: 52
## interior

- **音标**: in'tiәriә
- **释义**: n. 内部, 内政\na. 内部的, 心灵的, 内地的, 内政的
- **英文释义**: the inside part of something; relating to the inner part, domestic affairs, or the mind
- **词根词缀**: inter(在…之间)+ior(比较级)→内部的
- **例句**:
- The interior of the ancient cathedral was adorned with frescoes and stained-glass windows.
- The minister emphasized that economic reform was an internal matter, stressing the principle of non-interference in a country's interior affairs.
- **考频**: 29
## internal

- **音标**: in'tә:nәl
- **释义**: a. 内在的, 国内的\n[医] 内的, 内部的
- **英文释义**: located or existing inside something; not external
- **词根词缀**: inter(在…之间)+n+al(形容词)→内部的
- **例句**:
- The company conducted an internal audit to assess its financial controls.
- Chronic stress can have serious internal effects on the body, including hypertension and gastrointestinal disorders.
- **考频**: 63
## inherent

- **音标**: in'hiәrәnt
- **释义**: a. 固有的, 与生俱来的\n[医] 固有的, 生来的
- **英文释义**: existing as a natural or permanent quality in someone or something
- **词根词缀**: in(在内)+her(粘)+ent(形容词)→粘在里面的→固有的
- **例句**:
- The risks associated with nuclear power are inherent and cannot be completely eliminated.
- Creativity is not an inherent trait limited to artists; it can be developed through practice and exposure.
- **考频**: 27
## intrinsic

- **音标**: in'trinsik
- **释义**: a. 本质的, 原有的, 真正的\n[医] 内部的, 内在的
- **英文释义**: belonging naturally to something; essential and inseparable from it
- **词根词缀**: intr(在内)+insic(内在的)→内在的
- **例句**:
- The intrinsic value of gold lies in its rarity and durability.
- Effective learning depends not only on external rewards but also on the intrinsic motivation to understand.
- **考频**: 14
## inland

- **音标**: 'inlәnd
- **释义**: a. 内陆的, 国内的\nn. 内地
- **英文释义**: located or situated away from the coast or border; internal to a country or region
- **词根词缀**: in(在…内)+land(陆地)→内陆
- **例句**:
- The inland areas of China experience more extreme temperature variations than coastal cities.
- Goods transported by inland waterways are often cheaper than those moved by road or rail.
- **考频**: 6
