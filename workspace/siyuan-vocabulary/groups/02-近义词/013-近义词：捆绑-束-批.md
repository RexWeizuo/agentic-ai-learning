# 近义词：捆绑/束/批

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 均表示“捆绑、束、批”。bind(绑), bond(结合/债券), bundle(捆), bunch(串/束), batch(一批), band(带子)。

**6 词**

---

## bind

- **音标**: baind
- **释义**: vt. 绑, 约束, 装订, 包扎, 使结合\nvi. 凝固, 有约束力\n[计] 赋值, 绑定
- **英文释义**: to tie, fasten, or secure something physically; to impose a legal or moral obligation; to join or connect things closely; in computing, to associate a value or resource with a variable or operation
- **词根词缀**: bind(绑)→绑
- **例句**:
- The treaty binds all signatory nations to reduce carbon emissions by 2030.
- In object-oriented programming, method binding determines which implementation of a function is called at runtime.
- **考频**: 72
## bond

- **音标**: bɒnd
- **释义**: n. 捆绑物, 结合, 债券, 契约, 粘合剂, 保证人, 键, 关栈保留\nvt. 存入关栈, 使黏合\nvi. 结合
- **英文释义**: A connection or relationship between people or things; a written promise to pay money back with interest; a chemical link between atoms.
- **词根词缀**: bond(绑定)→绑定
- **例句**:
- The strong emotional bond between the siblings helped them overcome adversity.
- Government bonds are considered low-risk investments for long-term financial planning.
- **考频**: 56
## bundle

- **音标**: 'bʌndl
- **释义**: n. 捆, 束\nvt. 包扎, 胡乱塞进\nvi. 匆忙离开
- **英文释义**: A group of items bound or wrapped together; to wrap up or pack something hastily; to leave quickly.
- **词根词缀**: bund(绑)+le(小)→小捆→捆
- **例句**:
- She grabbed her coat and bundled the children into the car before the storm hit.
- The startup raised a $50 million funding bundle in its Series B round.
- **考频**: 9
## bunch

- **音标**: bʌntʃ
- **释义**: n. 串, 束\n[医] 骨肿块(马)
- **英文释义**: A group of things or people gathered or growing together; a cluster or collection.
- **词根词缀**: bunch→一束
- **例句**:
- She carried a bunch of grapes and a bunch of flowers to the picnic.
- The researchers identified a bunch of abnormal cells near the tumor site, suggesting early-stage metastasis.
- **考频**: 12
## batch

- **音标**: bætʃ
- **释义**: n. 一次所烘的面包, 一次所制之量, 一组, 批, 成批, 分批\nv. 成批, 分批处理\n[计] 一批
- **英文释义**: A group of things or people dealt with or produced at the same time
- **词根词缀**: bake(烤)+tch→一批烤好的面包→一批
- **例句**:
- The latest batch of graduates performed exceptionally well in the national entrance exam.
- To improve efficiency, the software processes data in batches rather than in real time.
- **考频**: 1
## band

- **音标**: bænd
- **释义**: n. 带子, 队, 乐队\nv. 联合, 结合\n[计] 频带; 波段; 区
- **英文释义**: A thin, flat strip of material; a group of musicians who play together; a group of people united for a common purpose; to join or bind together.
- **词根词缀**: band(带子)→带子
- **例句**:
- The surgeon used a rubber band to restrict blood flow during the procedure.
- Several tech startups have banded together to lobby for clearer AI regulations.
- **考频**: 67
