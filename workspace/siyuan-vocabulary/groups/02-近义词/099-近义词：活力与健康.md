# 近义词：活力与健康

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: vigorous (精力充沛的)，vital (至关重要的/生命的)，wholesome (有益健康的)，vivid (生动的/鲜明的)。

**4 词**

---

## vigorous

- **音标**: 'vigәrәs
- **释义**: a. 精力充沛的, 元气旺盛的, 茁壮的, 有力的
- **英文释义**: full of physical strength, energy, or intensity
- **词根词缀**: vig(活力)+or(名词)+ous(形容词)→充满活力的→精力充沛的
- **例句**:
- The government launched a vigorous campaign to reduce carbon emissions.
- She maintained a vigorous exercise routine despite her busy schedule.
- **考频**: 24
## vital

- **音标**: 'vaitl
- **释义**: a. 生命的, 重要的, 充满活力的, 生死攸关的, 致命的\n[医] 生命的, 生活的, 生活上必需的, 紧要的
- **英文释义**: absolutely necessary; essential for life or for success
- **词根词缀**: vit(生命)+al(形容词)→生命的→至关重要的
- **例句**:
- Access to clean water is vital for public health.
- A strong foundation in grammar is vital for mastering academic writing.
- **考频**: 80
## wholesome

- **音标**: 'hәulsәm
- **释义**: a. 有益健康的, 合乎卫生的, 健全的, 有益的, 有生气的, 审慎的
- **英文释义**: promoting good health, moral well-being, or sound development; beneficial and nourishing
- **词根词缀**: whole(完整)+some(有…性质的)→有益健康的
- **例句**:
- A balanced diet rich in fruits and vegetables is essential for a wholesome lifestyle.
- The film offers a wholesome alternative to the increasingly cynical and sensationalist media landscape.
- **考频**: 3
## vivid

- **音标**: 'vivid
- **释义**: a. 生动的, 鲜明的, 鲜艳的, 活泼的, 逼真的, 清晰的
- **英文释义**: producing powerful feelings or strong, clear images in the mind; bright and striking in color or detail
- **词根词缀**: viv(生命)+id(形容词)→有生命的→生动的
- **例句**:
- Her vivid description of the war made the audience feel as if they were there.
- The novel's vivid imagery and rich characterization earned it critical acclaim.
- **考频**: 23
