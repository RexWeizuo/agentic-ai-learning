# 近义词 (能力/精明/熟练)

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 形容人的能力或事物的复杂性。shrewd(精明的), skilful/skilled(熟练的), sophisticated(老练的/复杂的)。

**4 词**

---

## shrewd

- **音标**: ʃru:d
- **释义**: a. 精明的, 锐利的, 剧烈的, 机灵的, 厉害的
- **英文释义**: having or showing sharp powers of judgment and understanding, especially in practical matters
- **词根词缀**: shrewd(精明)→精明的
- **例句**:
- Her shrewd investment strategy helped the company weather the financial crisis.
- The negotiator’s shrewd questioning exposed inconsistencies in the opponent’s argument.
- **考频**: 6
## skilful

- **音标**: 'skilful
- **释义**: a. 灵巧的, 熟练的, 制作精巧的
- **英文释义**: Having or showing skill, especially in performing a task with precision and expertise
- **词根词缀**: skill(技能)+ful(充满)→有技能的→熟练的
- **例句**:
- The skilful surgeon completed the complex operation in under two hours.
- Her skilful use of rhetorical devices greatly enhanced the persuasiveness of the argument in her IELTS writing task.
- **考频**: 3
## skilled

- **音标**: 'skild
- **释义**: a. 熟练的\n[经] 熟练的, 有技能的
- **英文释义**: having practical knowledge and training in a particular activity
- **词根词缀**: skill(技能)+ed(形容词)→有技能的
- **例句**:
- She is a highly skilled surgeon with over fifteen years of experience.
- The project requires skilled professionals who can handle complex data analysis.
- **考频**: 74
## sophisticated

- **音标**: sә'fistikeitid
- **释义**: a. 复杂的, 久经世故的\n[法] 尖端的, 高级的, 非常有经验的
- **英文释义**: highly developed, complex, or refined; having worldly experience and subtle judgment
- **词根词缀**: soph(智慧)+istic(形容词后缀)+ated(复合后缀)→复杂的
- **例句**:
- The new missile system employs a sophisticated guidance mechanism that integrates AI and real-time satellite data.
- She displayed a sophisticated understanding of international trade law, impressing even the most seasoned professors.
- **考频**: 50
