# 近义词 (群/帮/集合)

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 表示“群”。flock 多指羊群/鸟群，herd 指牛/象等兽群，gang 指一帮人，galaxy 指星系或一群显赫人物。

**4 词**

---

## flock

- **音标**: flɒk
- **释义**: n. 兽群, 鸟群, 一群人, 大量, 棉束\nvi. 成群而行, 聚集\nvt. 用棉束填满
- **英文释义**: a large group of birds, sheep, or other animals; or a crowd of people
- **词根词缀**: flock(一群)→一群
- **例句**:
- Tourists flocked to the historic site after it was featured in the documentary.
- The government's new policy triggered a flock of applications from young entrepreneurs.
- **考频**: 14
## herd

- **音标**: hә:d
- **释义**: n. 兽群, 人群, 牧人\nvt. 群集, 使成群, 放牧\nvi. 聚在一起
- **英文释义**: a large group of animals or people; to gather or move in a group, or to tend livestock
- **词根词缀**: herd(兽群)→兽群
- **例句**:
- The documentary showed a herd of elephants crossing the savanna.
- Urban professionals often herd into coffee shops during lunch breaks, seeking both caffeine and social connection.
- **考频**: 24
## gang

- **音标**: gæŋ
- **释义**: n. 队, 群, 帮\nvi. 成群结队, 结成一伙\nvt. 使成群结队
- **英文释义**: a group of people who associate closely, often with a shared purpose or identity; sometimes implies informality, solidarity, or (in negative contexts) criminal association
- **词根词缀**: gang(帮派)→帮派
- **例句**:
- The construction workers moved in a gang to lift the heavy beam.
- She refused to join the clique, preferring not to be part of any social gang.
- **考频**: 1
## galaxy

- **音标**: 'gælәksi
- **释义**: n. 银河, 星系, 一群(显赫的人物)\n[电] 银河系
- **英文释义**: a large system of stars, gas, and dust held together by gravity; the Milky Way is one such system
- **词根词缀**: galax(银河)+y→银河系
- **例句**:
- The Hubble Space Telescope has captured stunning images of distant galaxies.
- She was hailed as a galaxy of talent in the field of theoretical physics.
- **考频**: 8
