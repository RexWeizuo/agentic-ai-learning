# 改变与偏离

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 表示事物形态、方向或本质发生改变、产生差异或追溯来源的动词。

**5 词**

---

## deviate

- **音标**: 'di:vieit
- **释义**: vi. 脱离, 偏离\nvt. 使偏离\na. 脱离常规的\nn. 脱离常规的人
- **英文释义**: to depart from an established course, standard, or norm
- **词根词缀**: de(偏离)+vi(路)+ate(动词)→偏离道路→偏离
- **例句**:
- The experiment's results deviated significantly from the theoretical predictions.
- While the essay was well-structured, it occasionally deviated from the assigned topic, weakening its coherence.
- **考频**: 4
## distort

- **音标**: dis'tɒ:t
- **释义**: vt. 扭曲, 歪曲\n[法] 歪区, 曲解, 纂改
- **英文释义**: to twist or change something so that it is no longer true, accurate, or natural
- **词根词缀**: dis(离开)+tort(扭曲)→扭曲离开→歪曲
- **例句**:
- The media often distort facts to attract more readers.
- His memory of the event was distorted by fear and time.
- **考频**: 18
## differ

- **音标**: 'difә
- **释义**: vi. 不一致, 不同\n[机] 差异, 不同
- **英文释义**: to be unlike or distinct from something else in nature, form, or quality
- **词根词缀**: dis(分开)+fer(带来)→分开带来→不同
- **例句**:
- The two theories differ significantly in their assumptions about human behavior.
- While both candidates support economic reform, they differ sharply on how to implement it.
- **考频**: 110
## differentiate

- **音标**: .difә'renʃieit
- **释义**: v. 区别, 区分
- **英文释义**: to recognize or show the difference between two or more things
- **词根词缀**: dis(分开)+fer(带来)+ent(形容词)+iate(动词)→使分开→区分
- **例句**:
- It is crucial for medical students to differentiate between symptoms of similar diseases.
- The essay must clearly differentiate the author's argument from prevailing scholarly views.
- **考频**: 7
## derive

- **音标**: di'raiv
- **释义**: vt. 得自\nvi. 起源
- **英文释义**: to obtain or get something from a source; to have its origin in something
- **词根词缀**: de(从)+rive(河流)→从河流引出→起源
- **例句**:
- The word 'biology' is derived from Greek roots meaning 'life' and 'study'.
- Many scientific theories derive from careful observation and experimentation.
- **考频**: 162
