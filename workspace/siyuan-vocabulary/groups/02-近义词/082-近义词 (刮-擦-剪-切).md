# 近义词 (刮/擦/剪/切)

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 表面损伤或切割动作。scrape(刮), scratch(抓/挠), shear(修剪/剪), slice(切片), scissors(剪刀)。

**5 词**

---

## scrape

- **音标**: skreip
- **释义**: n. 刮, 擦, 挖, 擦痕, 刮擦声, 困境\nvi. 刮掉, 擦掉, 刮出刺耳声\nvt. 刮, 擦, 擦伤, 挖成, 积蓄
- **英文释义**: to rub or scratch a surface roughly, often causing damage or noise; also used figuratively to mean a difficult or awkward situation
- **词根词缀**: scrap(刮)+e→刮擦
- **例句**:
- He narrowly scraped through the final exam with a score just above the passing threshold.
- The chair legs scraped loudly against the floor as she dragged it across the room.
- **考频**: 11
## scratch

- **音标**: skrætʃ
- **释义**: n. 抓痕, 搔, 抓, 擦伤, 刮擦声, 乱写, 零分, 起跑线\nvt. 搔, 抓, 挖出, 擦, 刮, 乱涂, 勾抹掉\nvi. 搔, 抓, 发刮擦声, 勉强糊口\na. 碰巧的, 凑合的, 打草稿用的\n[计] 擦除
- **英文释义**: To scrape or rub lightly with something sharp or rough; a superficial mark or injury caused by such action; also used figuratively for an initial or makeshift effort.
- **词根词缀**: scratch(抓)→抓痕
- **例句**:
- The cat scratched the wooden door, leaving deep scratch marks.
- She started from scratch when rewriting her thesis after the advisor rejected the first draft.
- **考频**: 24
## shear

- **音标**: ʃiә
- **释义**: n. 修剪, 剪下的东西, 大剪刀, 切变, 剪切机, 切\nvt. 修剪, 割, 剥夺, 横越, 削剪\nvi. 剪, 修剪, 剪羊毛, 切断
- **英文释义**: to cut or trim with shears or scissors; also refers to the mechanical force causing parallel internal surfaces of a material to slide past one another
- **词根词缀**: shear(剪)→剪
- **例句**:
- Farmers shear sheep in spring to collect wool for textile production.
- The structural failure was attributed to excessive shear stress at the beam-column joint.
- **考频**: 3
## slice

- **音标**: slais
- **释义**: n. 薄的切片, 一部分, 菜刀\nvt. 切成薄片, 切下\nvi. 切\n[计] 片
- **英文释义**: a thin, flat piece cut from something larger; to cut something into thin, flat pieces
- **词根词缀**: slice(切片)→切片
- **例句**:
- The report was divided into logical slices, each addressing a distinct aspect of the policy.
- She carefully sliced the apple into uniform pieces to ensure even cooking.
- **考频**: 27
## scissors

- **音标**: 'sizәz
- **释义**: pl. 剪刀\n[医] 剪
- **英文释义**: A hand-held tool with two pivoted blades used for cutting paper, cloth, or other thin materials.
- **词根词缀**: cis(cut)+sors→切
- **例句**:
- She carefully trimmed the edges of the fabric with a pair of sharp scissors.
- In surgical procedures, sterile scissors are essential for precise tissue dissection.
