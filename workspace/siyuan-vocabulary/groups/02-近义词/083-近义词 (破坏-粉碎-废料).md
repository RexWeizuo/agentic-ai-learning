# 近义词 (破坏/粉碎/废料)

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 破坏或废弃。shatter(粉碎/破灭), smash(打碎/猛击), scrap(废料/碎片), spoil(破坏/溺爱/变质)。

**4 词**

---

## shatter

- **音标**: 'ʃætә
- **释义**: n. 碎片, 粉碎, 落叶, 喷洒\nvt. 打碎, 使散开, 粉碎, 破坏\nvi. 粉碎, 损坏, 脱落
- **英文释义**: to break suddenly into many small pieces; to destroy or severely damage something, especially an idea, belief, or hope
- **词根词缀**: shatter(粉碎)→粉碎
- **例句**:
- The sudden news of his dismissal shattered her confidence in the company's leadership.
- Years of meticulous research were shattered when a critical experiment failed irreversibly.
- **考频**: 8
## smash

- **音标**: smæʃ
- **释义**: n. 打碎, 粉碎, 打碎时哗啦声, 猛击, 扣球, 杀球, 经营失败, 破产, 硬币, 假硬币\na. 非常轰动的, 了不起的\nvt. 打碎, 粉碎, 击溃, 使破产, 使裂变, 使用假硬币\nvi. 碎裂, 猛撞, 破产, 扣球, 杀球\nadv. 轰隆一声, 哗啦一声
- **英文释义**: To break something violently into pieces; also used figuratively for defeating decisively, failing spectacularly, or achieving great success.
- **词根词缀**: smash(拟声词根)→打碎
- **例句**:
- The vase smashed to the floor when she dropped it.
- Her debut album was a smash hit, topping the charts for eight consecutive weeks.
- **考频**: 10
## scrap

- **音标**: skræp
- **释义**: n. 碎片, 残余物, 些微, 片断, 铁屑, 吵架\nvt. 扔弃, 敲碎, 拆毁\nvi. 互相殴打\na. 零碎拼凑成的, 废弃的
- **英文释义**: A small piece or fragment, especially of something discarded or leftover; also, to discard or cancel something formally.
- **词根词缀**: scrap(碎片)→碎片
- **例句**:
- The engineers decided to scrap the outdated design and start from scratch.
- She saved every scrap of paper to reuse in her notes.
- **考频**: 8
## spoil

- **音标**: spɒil
- **释义**: n. 战利品, 赃物, 奖品, 变质, 次品\nvt. 损坏, 破坏, 溺爱\nvi. 腐坏, 掠夺
- **英文释义**: to damage or ruin something, especially by making it unusable or less valuable; also refers to goods obtained by force (e.g., in war or theft) or food that has gone bad
- **词根词缀**: spoil→破坏
- **例句**:
- The heavy rain spoiled our plans for a picnic.
- Sanctions were imposed to prevent the rebels from selling spoils of war.
- **考频**: 34
