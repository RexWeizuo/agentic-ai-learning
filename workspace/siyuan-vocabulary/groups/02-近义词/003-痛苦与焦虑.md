# 痛苦与焦虑

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 表示极度痛苦、焦虑或恐惧的词汇，agony 和 anguish 程度极深，appal 意为“使惊骇”。

**6 词**

---

## agony

- **音标**: 'ægәni
- **词性**: n.
- **释义**: ==n. 极大的痛苦, 挣扎\n[医] 濒死苦闷; 剧痛==
- **英文释义**: n. intense feelings of suffering; acute mental or physical pain\nn. a state of acute pain
- **同义词**: anguish, torment
- **反义词**: joy
- **词根词缀**: ag(痛苦)+ony→极大痛苦
- **搭配**: in agony, agony column, agony aunt
- **例句**:
- She cried out in agony as the doctor administered the injection.
- The athlete collapsed in agony after tearing his ACL during the final match.
- **考频**: 10
## anguish

- **音标**: 'æŋgwiʃ
- **释义**: ==n. 剧痛, 痛苦\nvt. 使极痛苦\nvi. 感到痛苦==
- **英文释义**: intense physical or mental suffering
- **同义词**: agony, torment
- **反义词**: joy
- **词根词缀**: angu(痛苦)+ish→极度痛苦
- **例句**:
- The mother's face was contorted with anguish as she heard the news of her son's accident.
- He sat in silence, unable to speak, overwhelmed by the anguish of losing his life's work.
- **考频**: 1
## anxiety

- **音标**: æŋ'zaiәti
- **释义**: ==n. 焦虑, 忧虑, 令人焦虑的事\n[医] 焦虑==
- **英文释义**: n. (psychiatry) a relatively permanent state of worry and nervousness occurring in a variety of mental disorders, usually accompanied by compulsive behavior or attacks of panic\nn. a vague unpleasant emotion that is experienced in anticipation of some (usually ill-defined) misfortune
- **同义词**: worry, concern
- **反义词**: calm
- **词根词缀**: anxi(不安)+ety→焦虑
- **搭配**: anxiety about, anxiety over
- **例句**:
- She suffers from severe anxiety.
- He felt anxiety about the exam.
- **考频**: 103
## anxious

- **音标**: 'æŋʃәs
- **释义**: ==a. 忧虑的, 发愁的, 渴望的==
- **英文释义**: worried or nervous about something that might happen; having a strong desire for something
- **同义词**: worried, concerned
- **反义词**: calm, confident
- **词根词缀**: anxi(不安)+ous→焦虑的
- **例句**:
- She felt increasingly anxious about her upcoming thesis defense.
- He was anxious to start his research internship at the prestigious institute.
- **考频**: 52
## appal

- **音标**: ә'pɒ:l
- **释义**: ==vt. 使惊骇, 使吓坏==
- **英文释义**: to shock or horrify someone deeply
- **词根词缀**: ap(to)+pal(pale)→make pale→使惊骇
- **例句**:
- The scale of environmental degradation in the region appalled scientists and policymakers alike.
- Her graphic account of the war atrocities appalled the entire audience.
- **考频**: 1
## afraid

- **音标**: ә'freid
- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: ==a. 害怕的, 恐怕的, 遗憾的==
- **英文释义**: a. filled with fear or apprehension\ns. filled with regret or concern; used often to soften an unpleasant statement\ns. feeling worry or concern or insecurity\ns. having feelings of aversion or unwillingness
- **同义词**: fearful, scared, apprehensive
- **反义词**: fearless, brave
- **词根词缀**: a(在)+fraid(惊吓)→处于惊吓→害怕的
- **搭配**: be afraid of (something), be afraid to do something, I'm afraid (that)
- **例句**:
- She was afraid to walk home alone at night.
- I'm afraid I can't attend the meeting tomorrow.
- **考频**: 96
