# 巨大/极多

> **类型**: 近义词

> **学习提示**: immense 强调体积或程度极大；innumerable 强调数量多得数不清。

**4 词**

---

## huge

- **音标**: hju:dʒ
- **释义**: a. 极大的, 巨大的, 无限的
- **英文释义**: very large in size, amount, or degree
- **词根词缀**: huge(巨大)→巨大的
- **例句**:
- The project requires a huge investment of both time and capital.
- She faced a huge challenge when she was appointed to lead the reform initiative.
- **考频**: 193
## immense

- **音标**: i'mens
- **释义**: a. 极广大的, 无边的, 非常好的
- **英文释义**: extremely large in size, scale, or degree
- **词根词缀**: im(不)+mens(测量)+e→无法测量→巨大的
- **例句**:
- The project requires an immense amount of time and resources.
- She has an immense talent for linguistic analysis, which gives her a significant edge in comparative literature studies.
- **考频**: 18
## infinite

- **音标**: 'infinit
- **释义**: n. 无限, 神\na. 无穷的, 无限的, 极大的
- **英文释义**: having no limits in time, space, extent, or size
- **词根词缀**: in(不)+fin(结束)+ite(形容词)→没有结束的→无限的
- **例句**:
- The universe may be infinite in both space and time.
- Her patience seemed infinite, even after hours of technical troubleshooting.
- **考频**: 14
## innumerable

- **音标**: i'nju:mәrәbl
- **释义**: a. 无数的, 数不清的
- **英文释义**: too many to be counted
- **词根词缀**: in(不)+numer(数)+able(可…的)→不可数的
- **例句**:
- The library contains innumerable volumes on philosophy and science.
- Her contributions to the field have inspired innumerable researchers worldwide.
- **考频**: 5
