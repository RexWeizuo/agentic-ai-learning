# 近义：抓/握/紧贴

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: catch(捕捉), clasp(紧握/扣住), clutch(抓紧/离合器), cling(紧贴/坚持)。

**4 词**

---

## catch

- **音标**: kætʃ
- **释义**: n. 捕捉, 陷阱, 捕捉之物, 抓, 拉手\nvt. 捕捉, 赶上, 感染, 听清楚\nvi. 抓住, 燃着
- **英文释义**: to seize or capture something/someone; to become affected by an illness; to hear or understand something clearly; to reach and board a transport just before it departs
- **词根词缀**: catch(抓)→抓住
- **例句**:
- The detective managed to catch the suspect after a high-speed chase.
- She caught the flu during the winter exam period and missed the final test.
- **考频**: 219
## clasp

- **音标**: klɑ:sp
- **释义**: n. 扣子, 钩, 紧握\nv. 扣紧, 紧握
- **英文释义**: A device that fastens two things together, or the act of holding something tightly with the hands.
- **词根词缀**: clasp(抓)→扣子；紧握
- **例句**:
- She unfastened the delicate clasp on her necklace before the surgery.
- He clasped his hands together nervously while waiting for the interview results.
- **考频**: 3
## clutch

- **音标**: klʌtʃ
- **释义**: n. 抓紧, 掌握, 离合器, 一窝小鸡\nvt. 抓住, 踩汽车离合器踏板\nvi. 抓\n[计] 联轴器; 离合器
- **英文释义**: to grasp or hold something tightly; a mechanical device that connects and disconnects rotating shafts, especially in vehicles
- **词根词缀**: clutch(抓)→抓住→离合器
- **例句**:
- She clutched her passport tightly as she entered the immigration checkpoint.
- To shift gears smoothly, the driver must depress the clutch pedal fully before engaging the next gear.
- **考频**: 2
## cling

- **音标**: kliŋ
- **释义**: vi. 粘紧, 附着, 紧贴, 坚持
- **英文释义**: to hold on tightly or stick closely to something, often physically or emotionally
- **词根词缀**: cling(粘着)→紧贴
- **例句**:
- The damp shirt clung to his back after the heavy rain.
- Many traditional beliefs still cling to rural communities despite rapid modernization.
- **考频**: 20
