# 调查/解释/洞察

> **类型**: 近义词

> **学习提示**: inspect 侧重检查、视察；investigate 侧重深入调查；insight 指深刻的洞察力。

**4 词**

---

## inspect

- **音标**: in'spekt
- **释义**: vt. 检查, 检阅, 检验\nvi. 检查
- **英文释义**: to look at something carefully in order to check its condition, quality, or correctness
- **词根词缀**: in(向内)+spect(看)→向内看→检查
- **例句**:
- The safety inspector will inspect the factory for compliance with occupational health regulations.
- Before signing the contract, the buyer insisted on inspecting the machinery thoroughly.
- **考频**: 5
## investigate

- **音标**: in'vestigeit
- **释义**: v. 调查, 审查
- **英文释义**: to examine or study something carefully in order to discover the truth or gather information
- **词根词缀**: in(进入)+vestig(脚印)+ate(动词)→追踪脚印→调查
- **例句**:
- The police are investigating the cause of the fatal car accident.
- Researchers investigated the long-term effects of sleep deprivation on cognitive performance.
- **考频**: 54
## insight

- **音标**: 'insait
- **释义**: n. 察看, 洞察力, 见识\n[医] 自知力, 洞察, 顿悟
- **英文释义**: the ability to understand the true nature of something, often through intuition or deep reflection
- **词根词缀**: in(进入)+sight(看)→看进去→洞察力
- **例句**:
- Her psychological insight enabled her to identify the underlying causes of the patient's anxiety.
- The report offers valuable insight into how socioeconomic factors influence educational outcomes.
- **考频**: 51
## interpret

- **音标**: in'tә:prit
- **释义**: vt. 解释, 演出, 翻译, 理解\nvi. 翻译, 解释
- **英文释义**: to explain the meaning of something, especially something that is difficult or ambiguous; to translate spoken language orally; to perform or present artistically
- **词根词缀**: inter(在…之间)+pret(价值)→在价值之间→解释
- **例句**:
- The historian interpreted the ancient manuscript as evidence of early diplomatic relations.
- Simultaneous interpreters must process and convey meaning in real time without losing nuance.
- **考频**: 104
