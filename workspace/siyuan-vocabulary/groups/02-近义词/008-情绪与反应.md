# 情绪与反应

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 表示引起某种情绪或反应的动词，affect 意为“影响/感动”，alarm 意为“使惊恐”。

**6 词**

---

## amaze

- **音标**: ә'meiz
- **释义**: ==vt. 使吃惊==
- **英文释义**: to fill with great surprise or wonder
- **同义词**: astonish, surprise
- **词根词缀**: a(加强)+maze(迷惑)→使迷惑→使惊奇
- **例句**:
- Her profound knowledge of quantum physics never fails to amaze her professors.
- The speed with which the startup scaled its operations continued to amaze industry analysts.
- **考频**: 2
## annoy

- **音标**: ә'nɒi
- **释义**: ==vt. 使恼怒, 骚扰==
- **英文释义**: to make someone slightly angry or impatient
- **词根词缀**: an(加强)+noy(伤害)→一再伤害→使恼怒
- **例句**:
- The constant buzzing of the fluorescent light began to annoy her during the long exam.
- He was annoyed by the repetitive questions from the interviewer, which seemed irrelevant to his research.
- **考频**: 30
## anger

- **音标**: 'æŋgә
- **释义**: ==n. 忿怒\nvt. 激怒, 使发怒\nvi. 发怒==
- **英文释义**: n. a strong emotion; a feeling that is oriented toward some real or supposed grievance\nn. the state of being angry\nv. make angry\nv. become angry
- **同义词**: rage, wrath
- **反义词**: calm
- **词根词缀**: ang(愤怒)+er→愤怒
- **搭配**: in anger, anger at
- **例句**:
- He was filled with anger at the injustice.
- Don't anger the boss today.
- **考频**: 43
## affect

- **音标**: /əˈfekt/
- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: ==影响；感动；假装==
- **英文释义**: to have an influence on somebody/something or to cause a change
- **同义词**: influence, impact, alter, impress
- **反义词**: ignore, neglect
- **词根词缀**: af(加强)+fect(做)→一再做→影响
- **搭配**: affect behavior, affect performance, adversely affect
- **例句**:
- The disease affects millions of people worldwide.
- Climate change affects every aspect of our lives.
- **考频**: 328
## alarm

- **音标**: ә'lɑ:m
- **词性**: ['n.', 'vt.']
- **释义**: ==n. 惊恐, 警报, 警钟\nvt. 使惊恐, 警告\n[计] 报警信号==
- **英文释义**: n. fear resulting from the awareness of danger\nn. a device that signals the occurrence of some undesirable event\nn. an automatic signal (usually a sound) warning of danger\nv. warn or arouse to a sense of danger or call to a state of preparedness
- **同义词**: warning, alert
- **反义词**: calm
- **词根词缀**: a(加强)+larm(警戒)→警戒→警报
- **搭配**: raise an alarm, set off an alarm, alarm clock
- **例句**:
- The fire alarm went off at midnight, waking everyone in the building.
- She was alarmed by the sudden drop in her computer's performance and ran a virus scan.
- **考频**: 64
## alert

- **音标**: ә'lә:t
- **词性**: ['adj.', 'n.', 'vt.']
- **释义**: ==a. 警觉的, 灵敏的, 留心的\nn. 警报\nvt. 使警觉, 通知, 使意识到==
- **英文释义**: n. condition of heightened watchfulness or preparation for action\nn. a warning serves to make you more alert to danger\na. engaged in or accustomed to close observation\ns. quick and energetic
- **同义词**: vigilant, watchful
- **反义词**: careless
- **词根词缀**: al(完全)+ert(警戒)→完全警戒→警觉的
- **搭配**: on alert, alert system, alert someone to something
- **例句**:
- The security guard remained alert throughout the night shift.
- She was alerted to the error by her colleague's email.
- **考频**: 53
