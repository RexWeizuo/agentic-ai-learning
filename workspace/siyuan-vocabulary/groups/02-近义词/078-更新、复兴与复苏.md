# 更新、复兴与复苏

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 均含“使重新焕发生机”之意，renaissance 特指文艺复兴或大复兴，renovate 多指建筑翻新。

**4 词**

---

## renaissance

- **音标**: ri'neisәns
- **释义**: n. 复活, 复兴, 文艺复兴\na. 文艺复兴的
- **英文释义**: A period of new growth or renewed interest, especially in art, culture, and learning; historically, the 14th–17th century European cultural movement marked by a revival of classical antiquity.
- **词根词缀**: re(再次)+naiss(出生)+ance(名词)→再次出生→文艺复兴
- **例句**:
- The digital revolution has sparked a renaissance in online education.
- Her groundbreaking research heralded a renaissance in renewable energy policy.
- **考频**: 14
## renew

- **音标**: ri'nju:
- **释义**: vt. 使更新, 使恢复, 复兴, 修补, 补充, 继续, 重订, 重申, 续借\nvi. 更新, 重新开始
- **英文释义**: to make something new, valid, or active again
- **词根词缀**: re(再次)+new(新)→再次变新→更新
- **例句**:
- The university library allows students to renew their borrowed books online before the due date.
- After years of diplomatic tension, both countries agreed to renew their commitment to peaceful cooperation.
- **考频**: 40
## renovate

- **音标**: 'renәuveit
- **释义**: vt. 更新, 革新, 刷新, 修复, 使恢复活力
- **英文释义**: to repair, improve, or modernize something, especially a building or system, to restore its condition or functionality
- **词根词缀**: re(再次)+nov(新)+ate(动词)→再次变新→翻新
- **例句**:
- The university plans to renovate the century-old library to accommodate digital resources and collaborative learning spaces.
- After decades of neglect, the historic theater was completely renovated to preserve its architectural integrity while upgrading acoustics and accessibility.
- **考频**: 15
## revive

- **音标**: ri'vaiv
- **释义**: vt. 使苏醒, 使复兴, 使振奋, 回想起, 重播\nvi. 苏醒, 复活, 复兴, 恢复精神
- **英文释义**: to bring back to life, consciousness, activity, or use; to recover strength or vitality
- **词根词缀**: re(再)+viv(活)+e→再活→复活
- **例句**:
- The doctors managed to revive the patient after cardiac arrest.
- Many traditional crafts are being revived as part of the cultural heritage preservation initiative.
- **考频**: 26
