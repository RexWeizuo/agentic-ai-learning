# 信任、信心、说服

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 与信任、确信相关，confidential 指“机密的”（基于信任）。

**4 词**

---

## confidence

- **音标**: 'kɒnfidәns
- **释义**: n. 信心\n[电] 可靠
- **英文释义**: the feeling or belief that one can rely on someone or something; firm trust or self-assurance
- **词根词缀**: con(完全)+fid(信任)+ence(名词)→完全信任→信心
- **例句**:
- Her confidence in her research methodology enabled her to defend her thesis with clarity and conviction.
- The new encryption protocol has gained widespread confidence among cybersecurity professionals due to its proven reliability.
- **考频**: 139
## confident

- **音标**: 'kɒnfidәnt
- **释义**: a. 有信心的, 有把握的
- **英文释义**: feeling certain about one's abilities, decisions, or the outcome of a situation
- **词根词缀**: con(完全)+fid(信任)+ent(形容词)→完全信任的→自信的
- **例句**:
- She delivered the presentation with remarkable confidence, impressing the panel of examiners.
- Having thoroughly reviewed the key concepts, he felt confident about acing the graduate entrance exam.
- **考频**: 76
## confidential

- **音标**: .kɒnfi'denʃәl
- **释义**: a. 机密的, 获他人信赖的, 易于信任他人的\n[经] 秘密件
- **英文释义**: intended to be kept secret or private; entrusted with sensitive information
- **词根词缀**: con(完全)+fid(信任)+ential(形容词)→完全信任的→机密的
- **例句**:
- All patient records are strictly confidential and must not be disclosed without consent.
- The report contains confidential financial data that is accessible only to senior management.
- **考频**: 15
## convince

- **音标**: kәn'vins
- **释义**: vt. 说服, 使相信\n[法] 使确信, 使信服, 使人认识错误
- **英文释义**: to make someone believe or accept something is true or right, especially through reasoning or evidence
- **词根词缀**: con(加强)+vinc(征服)+e→彻底征服→说服
- **例句**:
- The lawyer presented compelling evidence to convince the jury of her client's innocence.
- She struggled to convince her professor that the experimental methodology was sound.
- **考频**: 101
