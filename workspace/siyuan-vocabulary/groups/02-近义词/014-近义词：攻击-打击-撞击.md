# 近义词：攻击/打击/撞击

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 表示“攻击、打击、撞击”。attack(攻击), assault(袭击), beat(打/心跳), bang(重击), blow(打击), bump(撞击)。

**6 词**

---

## attack

- **音标**: ә'tæk
- **释义**: ==n. 攻击, 抨击\nvt. 攻击, 抨击, 动手干\nvi. 攻击==
- **英文释义**: to act aggressively against someone or something, either physically, verbally, or in a strategic context
- **同义词**: assault, strike
- **反义词**: defend
- **词根词缀**: at(朝向)+tack(钉子)→钉向→攻击
- **例句**:
- The opposition launched a fierce verbal attack on the government's new economic policy.
- She decided to attack the complex problem systematically rather than tackling it haphazardly.
- **考频**: 168
## assault

- **音标**: ә'sɒ:t
- **释义**: ==n. 攻击, 袭击\nvt. 袭击, 攻击\nvi. 发动攻击==
- **英文释义**: a violent physical or verbal attack; to attack someone physically or verbally
- **词根词缀**: as(朝向)+sault(跳)→跳向攻击→袭击
- **例句**:
- The defendant was charged with aggravated assault after seriously injuring the victim.
- Critics have launched a sustained intellectual assault on the theory's underlying assumptions.
- **考频**: 8
## beat

- **音标**: bi:t
- **释义**: n. 心跳(声), 打, 敲打声, 拍子\nv. 打, 拍打, 打败\na. 疲乏的, 颓废的\nbeat的过去式\n[计] 拍; 节拍
- **英文释义**: to defeat an opponent; a regular pulse or rhythm; the sound of a single stroke or heartbeat; exhausted
- **词根词缀**: beat(打)→打→击败
- **例句**:
- The underdog team managed to beat the defending champions in a stunning upset.
- Her heart skipped a beat when she heard the unexpected news.
- **考频**: 69
## bang

- **音标**: bæŋ
- **释义**: n. 重击, 突然巨响, 刘海\nv. 发巨响, 重击\nadv. 砰然地, 突然巨响地, 直接地\n[计] 撞击符
- **英文释义**: A sudden loud noise; a forceful blow; or a fringe of hair cut straight across the forehead.
- **词根词缀**: bang(拟声)→砰
- **例句**:
- The door slammed shut with a loud bang, startling everyone in the room.
- She swept her bangs to one side as she reviewed the algorithm's time complexity.
- **考频**: 15
## blow

- **音标**: blәu
- **释义**: n. 吹, 打击, 殴打, 花开\nv. 吹, 风吹, 吹响, 开花
- **英文释义**: v. to move air forcefully; to produce sound by breathing out; to bloom (of flowers); n. a sudden physical or emotional shock or setback
- **词根词缀**: blow(吹)→吹
- **例句**:
- The economic crisis dealt a severe blow to the country's export industry.
- As spring arrives, cherry trees begin to blow, filling the streets with delicate pink blossoms.
- **考频**: 35
## bump

- **音标**: bʌmp
- **释义**: n. 撞击, 肿块\nvt. 碰撞\nvi. 撞, 颠簸而行
- **英文释义**: a sudden, forceful impact or collision; a raised area on a surface or body
- **词根词缀**: bump(碰撞)→碰撞
- **例句**:
- The car hit a pothole and gave a loud bump as it jolted forward.
- She accidentally bumped into her professor in the hallway and quickly apologized.
- **考频**: 9
