# 伴随与附加

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 表示“伴随、附加、依附”的词汇，accessory 指“附件/从犯”，appendix 指“附录/阑尾”。

**5 词**

---

## accompany

- **音标**: ә'kʌmpәni
- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: ==vt. 陪伴, 伴随, 补充, 为...伴奏\nvi. 伴奏, 伴唱==
- **英文释义**: v. go or travel along with
- **同义词**: escort, attend
- **反义词**: separate from, leave behind
- **词根词缀**: ac(一起)+company(陪伴)→陪伴
- **搭配**: accompany someone to, accompany with music, accompany a meal
- **例句**:
- She will accompany her friend to the hospital tomorrow.
- The violinist accompanied the singer on the piano.
- **考频**: 46
## accessory

- **音标**: әk'sesәri
- **词性**: n./adj.
- **释义**: ==n. 附件, 零件, 附加物, 从犯, 同谋\na. 附属的, 补充的, 同谋的, 帮凶的, 非主要的, 副的\n[计] 附件==
- **英文释义**: n. clothing that is worn or carried, but not part of your main clothing\nn. a supplementary component that improves capability\nn. someone who helps another person commit a crime\ns. furnishing added support
- **同义词**: attachment, addition, accompaniment
- **反义词**: essential, necessity
- **词根词缀**: access(接近，进入)+-ory(表示‘与…有关的’形容词后缀)→与‘进入/获取’相关的事物→引申为‘辅助性物品’或‘协助犯罪的人’→词义：附件；从犯
- **搭配**: fashion accessory, car accessory, accessory to crime
- **例句**:
- She wore a silk scarf as a fashionable accessory.
- He was charged as an accessory to the robbery for driving the getaway car.
- **考频**: 11
## appendix

- **音标**: ә'pendiks
- **释义**: ==n. 附录, 附加物, 阑尾\n[化] 增补==
- **英文释义**: A supplementary section at the end of a book or document, or a small pouch-like structure branching from the cecum in the human digestive system.
- **词根词缀**: ap(to)+pend(hang)+ix→something hanging→附录
- **例句**:
- The author provides detailed statistical data in the appendix to support the main argument.
- Appendicitis, an inflammation of the appendix, is a common cause of acute abdominal pain requiring prompt surgical intervention.
- **考频**: 1
## affiliate

- **音标**: ә'filieit
- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: ==vt. 使紧密联系, 使附属, 接纳, 收养\nvi. 发生联系, 参加==
- **英文释义**: n. a subordinate or subsidiary associate; a person who is affiliated with another or with an organization\nn. a subsidiary or subordinate organization that is affiliated with another organization\nv. join in an affiliation
- **同义词**: associate, connect, link
- **反义词**: detach, sever
- **词根词缀**: af(加强)+fili(儿子)+ate→成为儿子→隶属
- **搭配**: affiliate with, affiliate to, affiliate oneself with
- **例句**:
- The university affiliated with several research institutes to enhance its academic capacity.
- She decided to affiliate herself with a local environmental NGO to support sustainability initiatives.
- **考频**: 8
## adhere

- **音标**: әd'hiә
- **词性**: vi./vt.
- **释义**: ==vi. 依附, 粘着, 坚持, 追随, 拥护\nvt. 使粘附==
- **英文释义**: v. be compatible or in accordance with\nv. follow through or carry out a plan without deviation\nv. be a devoted follower or supporter\nv. stick to firmly
- **同义词**: stick, cling
- **反义词**: detach
- **词根词缀**: ad(朝向)+here(粘)→粘向→粘附
- **搭配**: adhere to a principle, adhere to regulations, adhere to a schedule
- **例句**:
- The label adheres firmly to the bottle surface.
- All members must adhere to the organization's code of conduct.
- **考频**: 12
