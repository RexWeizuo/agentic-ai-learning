# 丰富与充足

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 表示“大量、充足、丰富”的近义词，accumulate 和 aggregate 侧重“积累/总计”。

**7 词**

---

## abound

- **音标**: ә'baund
- **词性**: vi.
- **释义**: ==vi. 大量存在, 富于, 充满==
- **英文释义**: v. be abundant or plentiful; exist in large quantities\nv. be in a state of movement or action
- **同义词**: overflow, teem
- **反义词**: lack, dwindle
- **词根词缀**: a(在)+bound(边界)→在边界内→大量存在
- **搭配**: abound in, abound with, abound everywhere
- **例句**:
- Wildlife abounds in this national park.
- The library abounds with rare manuscripts.
- **考频**: 4
## abundant

- **音标**: ә'bʌndәnt
- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: ==a. 丰富的, 充分的, 大量的==
- **英文释义**: a. present in great quantity
- **同义词**: plentiful, copious, ample
- **反义词**: scarce, insufficient
- **词根词缀**: ab-(away, from)+und(=wave, flowing)+-ant(adj. suffix)→'flowing away' or 'overflowing'→ suggesting overflowing quantity → abundant
- **搭配**: abundant evidence, abundant opportunity, abundant rainfall
- **例句**:
- The region is abundant in natural resources such as oil and timber.
- She has an abundant supply of patience when teaching beginners.
- **考频**: 35
## affluent

- **音标**: 'æfluәnt
- **词性**: ['adj.', 'n.']
- **释义**: ==a. 丰富的, 富裕的\nn. 支流, 富人==
- **英文释义**: n. an affluent person; a person who is financially well off\ns. having an abundant supply of money or possessions of value
- **同义词**: wealthy, prosperous, well-off
- **反义词**: impoverished, destitute
- **词根词缀**: ad-(to)+flu-(flow)+-ent(形容词后缀，表‘具有…性质的’)→字面义‘（财富/资源）持续流入的’→引申为‘富裕的；丰富的’；作名词时特指‘富人’（由形容词转类而来）
- **搭配**: affluent society, affluent lifestyle, affluent suburb
- **例句**:
- The affluent neighborhood features gated communities and private schools.
- Many affluent families donate generously to educational charities.
- **考频**: 17
## adequate

- **音标**: /ˈædɪkwət/
- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: ==足够的；适当的，恰当的==
- **英文释义**: enough in quantity or of a good enough quality for a particular purpose
- **同义词**: sufficient, enough, appropriate
- **反义词**: inadequate, insufficient, deficient
- **词根词缀**: ad(到)+equ(平等)+ate→达到需要的标准→足够的
- **搭配**: adequate supply, adequate preparation, more than adequate
- **例句**:
- The hotel room was adequate for our needs.
- Adequate funding is essential for the project.
- **考频**: 61
## ample

- **音标**: 'æmpl
- **释义**: ==a. 大量的, 充足的, 丰富的==
- **英文释义**: sufficient or more than enough in size, quantity, or extent
- **同义词**: abundant, plentiful
- **反义词**: scant
- **词根词缀**: amp(大)+le→大的→充足的
- **例句**:
- The report provides ample evidence to support the conclusion.
- She has ample experience in international project management, making her a strong candidate for the role.
- **考频**: 9
## accumulate

- **音标**: ә'kju:mjuleit
- **词性**: v.
- **释义**: ==v. 积聚, 堆积==
- **英文释义**: v. collect or gather
- **同义词**: gather, collect
- **反义词**: disperse, scatter
- **词根词缀**: ac(加强)+cumul(堆积)+ate→堆积→积累
- **搭配**: accumulate evidence, accumulate wealth, accumulate experience
- **例句**:
- Dust tends to accumulate on unused furniture over time.
- She has accumulated extensive knowledge in linguistics through years of research.
- **考频**: 47
## aggregate

- **音标**: /ˈæɡrɪɡət/
- **词性**: n./adj./v.
- **释义**: ==总计，合计；集合，聚集==
- **英文释义**: a whole formed by combining several elements; the total amount
- **同义词**: total, sum, collect, combine
- **反义词**: separate, divide, individual
- **词根词缀**: ag(朝向)+greg(群体)+ate→聚集到一起→总计
- **搭配**: in the aggregate, aggregate demand, aggregate data
- **例句**:
- The aggregate cost exceeded our budget.
- Data aggregated from multiple sources.
- **考频**: 3
