# 叛乱、暴动与反抗

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 表示反抗或暴乱，rebel/rebellion 侧重反叛政权，revolt 侧重起义或反感，riot 侧重群体暴乱。

**4 词**

---

## rebel

- **音标**: 'rebl
- **释义**: n. 叛徒, 反叛者\nvi. 造反, 反抗, 抵抗, 反感\na. 造反的, 反抗的
- **英文释义**: a person who resists authority, control, or tradition; to oppose or defy established rules, government, or norms
- **词根词缀**: re(反)+bel(战争)→反战→反叛
- **例句**:
- The young poet was seen as a rebel against literary convention.
- Many students began to rebel against the rigid curriculum and demanded more creative freedom.
- **考频**: 11
## rebellion

- **音标**: ri'beljәn
- **释义**: n. 谋反, 叛乱, 反抗\n[法] 造反, 反叛, 叛乱
- **英文释义**: open resistance against an established government or authority
- **词根词缀**: re(反)+bell(战争)+ion(名词)→反战→反叛
- **例句**:
- The government swiftly suppressed the armed rebellion in the southern region.
- Her refusal to conform to rigid academic norms was seen not as mere disobedience, but as a quiet intellectual rebellion.
- **考频**: 7
## revolt

- **音标**: ri'vәult
- **释义**: n. 叛乱, 反抗, 反感\nvi. 叛乱, 反抗, 起义, 厌恶, 反感\nvt. 使反感, 使恶心
- **英文释义**: a sudden, violent attempt to overthrow a government or authority; strong feeling of disgust or aversion
- **词根词缀**: re(反)+volt(转)→反转→反抗
- **例句**:
- The people staged a revolt against the corrupt regime after decades of oppression.
- The graphic descriptions of the experiment revolted many members of the ethics committee.
- **考频**: 4
## riot

- **音标**: 'raiәt
- **释义**: n. 暴动, 喧闹, 放纵\nvi. 发动, 暴动, 纵情, 放荡\nvt. 浪费, 挥霍
- **英文释义**: A violent public disturbance involving a crowd, or unrestrained behavior or indulgence.
- **词根词缀**: riot(暴乱)→暴乱
- **例句**:
- The city imposed a curfew after riots broke out in response to the controversial court ruling.
- She rioted in laughter at his absurd imitation, completely losing her composure.
- **考频**: 13
