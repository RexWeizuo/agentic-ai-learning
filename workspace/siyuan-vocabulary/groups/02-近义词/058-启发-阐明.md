# 启发/阐明

> **类型**: 近义词

> **学习提示**: illuminate 侧重照明或阐明深奥的道理；illustrate 侧重用例子或图表来说明。

**5 词**

---

## illuminate

- **音标**: i'lju:mineit
- **释义**: vt. 照明, 用灯装饰, 阐明, 说明, 使灿烂\nvi. 照亮, 用灯装饰
- **英文释义**: to provide light for; to clarify or explain something difficult
- **词根词缀**: il(进入)+lumin(光)+ate→进入光→照亮
- **例句**:
- The professor's insightful commentary helped illuminate the complex philosophical arguments in the text.
- Modern LED lights can illuminate vast public spaces efficiently while reducing energy consumption.
- **考频**: 16
## illustrate

- **音标**: 'ilәstreit
- **释义**: vt. 举例说明, 作图解, 阐明\nvi. 举例说明
- **英文释义**: to explain or clarify something using examples, diagrams, or evidence
- **词根词缀**: il(进入)+lustr(光)+ate→进入光→说明
- **例句**:
- The author uses case studies to illustrate the impact of climate change on coastal communities.
- A well-designed chart can effectively illustrate the correlation between income level and educational attainment.
- **考频**: 108
## illustration

- **音标**: .ilә'streiʃәn
- **释义**: n. 例证, 插图\n[化] 插图
- **英文释义**: a visual image or diagram that explains or decorates a text; an example used to clarify or support a point
- **词根词缀**: il(进入)+lustr(光)+ation(名词)→说明
- **例句**:
- The textbook includes numerous illustrations to help students visualize complex biological processes.
- Her speech was enriched with vivid illustrations from historical events, making the abstract concept of justice more tangible.
- **考频**: 11
## inspiration

- **音标**: .inspә'reiʃәn
- **释义**: n. 灵感, 鼓舞人心的人或物, 妙计, 吸气\n[医] 吸[气]
- **英文释义**: the process of being mentally stimulated to do something creative; a person, thing, or experience that stimulates such creativity or motivation
- **词根词缀**: in(进入)+spir(呼吸)+ation(名词)→吸入灵感→灵感
- **例句**:
- Her dedication to social justice was a profound inspiration to the entire community.
- The architect's design for the museum was widely praised as a stroke of inspiration.
- **考频**: 21
## inspire

- **音标**: in'spaiә
- **释义**: vt. 使感动, 激发, 启示, 吸入, 鼓舞, 产生, 使生灵感\nvi. 吸入, 赋予灵感
- **英文释义**: to fill someone with the urge or ability to do something, especially something creative or positive
- **词根词缀**: in(进入)+spir(呼吸)→吸入→激励
- **例句**:
