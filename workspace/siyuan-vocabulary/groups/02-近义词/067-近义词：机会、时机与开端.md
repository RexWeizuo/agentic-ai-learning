# 近义词：机会、时机与开端

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 表示机会、可能性或事物开端的近义词。

**4 词**

---

## occasion

- **音标**: ә'keiʒәn
- **释义**: n. 场合, 时机, 机会, 诱因, 理由\nvt. 惹起, 引起
- **英文释义**: a particular time when something happens; a special or formal event; a reason or cause for something
- **词根词缀**: oc(加强)+cas(落下)+ion(名词)→落下的时机→场合
- **例句**:
- The wedding was a joyous occasion that brought together family members from across the country.
- His careless remarks on social media occasioned widespread criticism and calls for accountability.
- **考频**: 73
## opportunity

- **音标**: .ɒpә'tju:niti
- **释义**: n. 机会, 时机
- **英文释义**: a favorable set of circumstances that makes it possible to do something
- **词根词缀**: op(向)+port(港口)+unity(名词)→朝向港口→机会
- **例句**:
- Securing an internship at a top-tier firm was a golden opportunity to gain practical experience in finance.
- The rapid development of AI presents both unprecedented challenges and tremendous opportunities for innovation in education.
- **考频**: 387
## odds

- **音标**: ɒdz
- **释义**: n. 可能性, 几率, 机会, 胜算, 不平等
- **英文释义**: the probability or likelihood that something will happen; often used to express comparative chances or imbalance
- **词根词缀**: od(road/song)+ds
- **例句**:
- The odds of being accepted into the program are extremely low, given the high number of qualified applicants.
- Despite the overwhelming odds against them, the underdog team managed to win the championship.
## opening

- **音标**: 'әupәniŋ
- **释义**: n. 开始, 口子, 穴, 揭幕\na. 开始的
- **英文释义**: the start of an event or period; a hole or gap in something; the first part of something
- **词根词缀**: open(打开)+ing(名词)→开口
- **例句**:
- The opening ceremony of the Olympic Games attracted global attention.
- She found an opening in the fence and slipped through quietly.
- **考频**: 10
