# 动作：翻转与振动

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: whirl (回旋/旋转)，tumble (跌倒/翻滚)，turnover (翻转/营业额)，vibrate (振动/颤动)。

**4 词**

---

## whirl

- **音标**: hwә:l
- **释义**: n. 回旋, 旋转, 急走, 眩晕\nvt. 使旋转, 使回旋, 卷走\nvi. 回旋, 旋转, 急走, 发晕
- **英文释义**: to move or cause to move rapidly in a circle or spiral; a rapid spinning or rushing motion
- **词根词缀**: whirl(旋转)→旋转
- **例句**:
- The leaves whirled in the autumn wind, creating a miniature vortex on the pavement.
- She felt a sudden whirl of dizziness as she stood up too quickly after the long lecture.
- **考频**: 1
## tumble

- **音标**: tʌmbl
- **释义**: n. 跌倒, 摔跤, 翻斤斗\nvi. 翻倒, 摔倒, 倒塌, 滚动, 翻斤斗, 仓促地行动\nvt. 使摔倒, 使滚翻, 弄乱
- **英文释义**: to fall suddenly and uncontrollably, often head over heels; or to decrease rapidly (e.g., prices, numbers)
- **词根词缀**: tumb(翻倒)+le→翻倒→跌倒
- **例句**:
- The stock market continued to tumble amid growing economic uncertainty.
- She lost her balance on the icy pavement and tumbled down the stairs.
- **考频**: 13
## turnover

- **音标**: 'tә:n.әuvә
- **释义**: n. 翻倒, 翻转, 半圆酥饼, 营业额, 流通, 周转\na. 可翻转的
- **英文释义**: the total amount of money a business earns from sales in a given period; also refers to the rate at which employees or inventory are replaced
- **词根词缀**: turn(转)+over(超过)→翻转→营业额
- **例句**:
- The company reported a 12% increase in annual turnover, reflecting strong market demand.
- High staff turnover is a major concern for HR departments, as it undermines organizational stability and increases training costs.
- **考频**: 16
## vibrate

- **音标**: 'vaibreit
- **释义**: vi. 振动, 颤动, 激动, 摇摆, 踌躇\nvt. 使颤动, 使振动, 使摆动
- **英文释义**: to move rapidly back and forth or up and down; to shake with small, quick movements
- **词根词缀**: vibr(振动)+ate(动词)→振动
- **例句**:
- The guitar string began to vibrate softly after being plucked.
- She could feel the floor vibrate as the subway train rushed beneath the building.
- **考频**: 1
