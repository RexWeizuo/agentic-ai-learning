# 时间与周期

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 与时间、周期、历史相关的词汇，aftermath 指“后果/余波”，annual 指“每年的”。

**6 词**

---

## ancient

- **音标**: 'einʃәnt
- **释义**: ==a. 古代的, 古老的, 年老的, 旧的==
- **英文释义**: belonging to or dating from the very distant past, especially before the end of the Roman Empire
- **同义词**: old, antique
- **反义词**: modern
- **词根词缀**: ant(前)+ient→以前的→古代的
- **例句**:
- The archaeologists uncovered ancient pottery fragments dating back to the 5th century BCE.
- Ancient philosophical texts continue to influence modern ethical debates in graduate seminars.
- **考频**: 145
## antique

- **音标**: æn'ti:k
- **释义**: ==n. 古董, 古物\na. 古老的, 古风的, 旧式的, 过时的==
- **英文释义**: A collectible object of considerable age, often valued for its beauty, rarity, or historical significance; also used as an adjective meaning very old or belonging to an earlier period.
- **词根词缀**: anti(前)+que→以前的→古董
- **例句**:
- The museum's collection includes several 18th-century antiques, each meticulously cataloged and preserved.
- Though she admired the antique furniture's craftsmanship, she found its style too ornate for her modern apartment.
- **考频**: 8
## anniversary

- **音标**: æni'vә:sәri
- **释义**: ==n. 周年纪念==
- **英文释义**: the date on which an event occurred in a previous year, especially a celebration of such a date
- **词根词缀**: ann(年)+ivers(转)+ary→每年转回来→周年
- **例句**:
- The couple celebrated their 25th wedding anniversary with a trip to Paris.
- This year marks the 100th anniversary of the founding of the university, commemorated by academic conferences and exhibitions.
- **考频**: 12
## annual

- **音标**: 'ænjuәl
- **释义**: ==n. 年刊, 年报\na. 每年的, 一年一次的, 全年的, 一年生的==
- **英文释义**: occurring once every year; lasting for one year; a publication issued yearly
- **同义词**: yearly
- **词根词缀**: ann(年)+ual→每年的
- **例句**:
- The company publishes an annual report detailing its financial performance and strategic goals.
- Many plants, such as marigolds and petunias, are annuals, completing their life cycle within a single growing season.
- **考频**: 93
## aftermath

- **音标**: 'ɑ:ftәmæθ
- **词性**: n.
- **释义**: ==n. 结果, 后果\n[法] 后果, 结果==
- **英文释义**: n. the consequences of an event (especially a catastrophic event)
- **同义词**: consequence, outcome, repercussion
- **词根词缀**: after(在…之后)+math(古英语 mǣþ，意为‘割下的草；刈割’，引申为‘继之而来的结果’)→字面义‘收割之后留下的茬’→比喻义‘事件发生后随之而来的状况或后果’→词义：结果，后果
- **搭配**: in the aftermath of, aftermath of war, aftermath of a disaster
- **例句**:
- The city faced severe economic hardship in the aftermath of the earthquake.
- She struggled with anxiety and depression in the aftermath of her divorce.
- **考频**: 7
## afterward

- **音标**: 'ɑ:ftәwәd
- **词性**: adv.
- **释义**: ==adv. 然后, 后来==
- **英文释义**: r happening at a time subsequent to a reference time
- **同义词**: later, afterwards, subsequently
- **反义词**: beforehand, previously
- **词根词缀**: after(在…之后)+ward(朝向某一方向/时间)→表示‘朝向之后的时间’→然后，后来
- **搭配**: shortly afterward, soon afterward, immediately afterward
- **例句**:
- We finished dinner, and afterward we watched a movie.
- She didn’t understand the instructions at first, but afterward she grasped them completely.
- **考频**: 6
