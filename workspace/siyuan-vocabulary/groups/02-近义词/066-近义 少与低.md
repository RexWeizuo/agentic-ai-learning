# 近义 少与低

> **类型**: 近义

> **学习提示**: 表示数量少、程度低或位置低的比较级与基础词

**4 词**

---

## least

- **音标**: li:st
- **释义**: n. 最少, 最小, 最小限度\na. 最少的, 最小的\nadv. 最小, 最少
- **英文释义**: the smallest or lowest in amount, degree, or importance
- **词根词缀**: least(词根:最少)→最少
- **例句**:
- She contributed the least among all team members, yet her insight proved invaluable.
- Of all the proposed solutions, this one requires the least financial investment and carries the least risk.
- **考频**: 570
## less

- **音标**: les
- **释义**: n. 较少, 较小\na. 少的, 小的\nadv. 较少, 较小, 较差\n[计] 最低成本估算与调度法
- **英文释义**: a smaller amount or degree; not as much or as many
- **词根词缀**: less(更少)→更少
- **例句**:
- The new policy aims to reduce energy consumption by 20%, resulting in less environmental impact.
- Compared to traditional methods, this algorithm achieves higher accuracy with less computational overhead.
- **考频**: 1190
## little

- **音标**: 'litl
- **释义**: n. 一点点, 少许, 一会儿, 短时间\na. 小的, 很少的, 幼小的, 琐碎的, 短暂的, 矮小的\nadv. 很少, 稍微, 完全不
- **英文释义**: small in size, amount, or degree; not much or not many
- **词根词缀**: little(小)→小的

- **考频**: 688
## lower

- **释义**: a. 低的, 下级的, 下层的\nvi. 降低, 跌落, 减弱\nvt. 放下, 降下, 减弱, 贬低
