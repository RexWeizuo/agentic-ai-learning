# 概念、观念、关心

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: con- 开头的抽象名词与形容词，concept 和 conception 近义，concise 指“简洁的”。

**4 词**

---

## concept

- **音标**: 'kɒnsept
- **释义**: n. 观念, 概念\n[医] 概念
- **英文释义**: an abstract idea or a general understanding of something
- **词根词缀**: con(一起)+cept(拿)→拿到一起→概念
- **例句**:
- The theory introduces a revolutionary concept of time and space.
- She struggled to grasp the philosophical concept of existentialism in her graduate seminar.
- **考频**: 202
## conception

- **音标**: kɒn'sepʃәn
- **释义**: n. 观念, 概念\n[医] 妊娠, 受孕; 概念
- **英文释义**: 1. An idea or understanding of something; 2. The beginning of pregnancy (medical usage).
- **词根词缀**: con(一起)+cept(拿)+ion(名词)→拿到一起→概念
- **例句**:
- Her conception of justice is deeply influenced by her philosophical training.
- The study investigates the relationship between maternal age and time to conception.
- **考频**: 18
## concern

- **音标**: kәn'sә:n
- **释义**: n. 关心, 忧虑\nvt. 与...有关, 使担心, 使挂念
- **英文释义**: n. worry or anxiety about something; vt. to relate to or affect someone/something, or to cause worry
- **词根词缀**: con(一起)+cern(分开)→分开后相关→关心
- **例句**:
- The government expressed deep concern over the rising unemployment rate.
- This policy concerns all employees, not just senior management.
- **考频**: 373
## concise

- **音标**: kәn'sais
- **释义**: a. 简洁的, 简明的
- **英文释义**: expressing much in few words; brief but clear
- **词根词缀**: con(完全)+cise(切)→切掉多余的→简洁的
- **例句**:
- Her concise report impressed the committee with its clarity and precision.
- To succeed in academic writing, aim for concise sentences that eliminate redundancy without sacrificing meaning.
- **考频**: 9
