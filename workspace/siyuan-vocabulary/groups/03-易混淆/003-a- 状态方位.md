# a- 状态方位

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: a- 开头的状态或方位副词/形容词，aboard（在船上/飞机上）与 abroad（在国外）极易混淆。

**7 词**

## aboard

- **音标**: ә'bɒ:d
- **词性**: ['adv.', 'prep.']
- **释义**: ==adv. 在船上, 在火车上, 在飞机上\nprep. 在...之上==
- **英文释义**: r. on a ship, train, plane or other vehicle\nr. on first or second or third base\nr. side by side\nr. part of a group
- **同义词**: on board, on the plane/train/ship, aboard the vessel
- **反义词**: ashore, off board
- **词根词缀**: a-(on, in)+board(n. 甲板，船面)→字面义‘在甲板上’→引申为‘在交通工具之上’→词义：在船上/火车上/飞机上；在…之上
- **搭配**: aboard the ship, aboard a flight, aboard the International Space Station
- **例句**:
- All passengers must be aboard the aircraft 30 minutes before departure.
- She jumped aboard the moving train just as the doors were closing.
- **考频**: 12

- **同义词**: overseas, abroadly, internationally
- **反义词**: domestically, locally

- **搭配**: study abroad, live abroad, work abroad
- **例句**:
- She studied abroad in Japan for a year.
- Many young people dream of working abroad after graduation.
- **考频**: 103
