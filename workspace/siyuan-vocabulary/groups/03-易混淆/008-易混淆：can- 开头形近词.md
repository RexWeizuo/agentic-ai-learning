# 易混淆：can- 开头形近词

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: can- 开头形近词：canal(运河), cancel(取消), cannon(大炮), canoe(独木舟), canteen(食堂), canvas(帆布)。

**6 词**

---

## canal

- **音标**: kә'næl
- **释义**: n. 运河, 水道, 管, 沟渠\nvt. 开运河
- **英文释义**: an artificial waterway constructed for navigation, irrigation, or water supply
- **词根词缀**: canal→运河
- **例句**:
- The Suez Canal significantly reduces sailing time between Europe and Asia.
- Engineers proposed to canal the river to prevent future flooding in the low-lying region.
- **考频**: 18
## cancel

- **音标**: 'kænsәl
- **释义**: n. 取消, 撤消, 盖销(邮票)\nvt. 取消, 删去, 抵销, 盖销\nvi. 相互抵销\n[计] 作废
- **英文释义**: to stop or end something before it happens or is completed; to remove or nullify something officially or mathematically
- **词根词缀**: cancell(格子)→划格子→取消
- **例句**:
- The conference was canceled due to the sudden outbreak of the epidemic.
- The positive and negative charges cancel each other out, resulting in a net charge of zero.
- **考频**: 80
## cannon

- **音标**: 'kænәn
- **释义**: n. 大炮, 加农炮\nv. 炮轰
- **英文释义**: a large, heavy piece of artillery that fires heavy projectiles over long distances
- **词根词缀**: cannon→大炮
- **例句**:
- The fortress was reduced to rubble after being cannoned for three consecutive hours.
- In the 18th century, naval warfare relied heavily on cannons mounted along the sides of warships.
- **考频**: 2
## canoe

- **音标**: kә'nu:
- **释义**: n. 独木舟, 轻舟\nvi. 乘独木舟\nvt. 用独木舟运
- **英文释义**: A narrow, lightweight boat propelled by paddling, typically used on calm water.
- **词根词缀**: canoe→独木舟
- **例句**:
- They spent the weekend canoeing down the serene river, navigating gentle rapids and spotting wildlife.
- The expedition team had to canoe across the lake to reach the remote research station.
- **考频**: 5
## canteen

- **音标**: kæn'ti:n
- **释义**: n. 水壶, 军中俱乐部, 临时流动餐馆, 小卖部
- **英文释义**: A portable container for carrying drinking water, especially for military use; also refers to a cafeteria, snack bar, or recreational facility in institutions or workplaces.
- **词根词缀**: cant(唱歌)+een(小)→小歌厅→食堂
- **例句**:
- Soldiers were instructed to fill their canteens before the desert patrol began.
- The university canteen offers affordable meals and serves as a social hub for students.
- **考频**: 7
## canvas

- **音标**: 'kænvәs
- **释义**: n. 帆布, 画布, 油画\n[化] 帆布
- **英文释义**: a strong, coarse cloth made of cotton or linen, used for sails, tents, artist's painting surfaces, or as a base for digital learning platforms
- **词根词缀**: canvas→帆布
- **例句**:
- The artist stretched the oil paint across the taut canvas with confident, deliberate strokes.
- Many universities now use Canvas as their primary learning management system to deliver course materials and track student progress.
- **考频**: 4
