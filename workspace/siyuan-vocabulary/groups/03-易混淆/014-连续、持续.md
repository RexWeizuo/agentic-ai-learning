# 连续、持续

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: continual 指频繁重复（有间断），continuous 指不间断，consecutive 指按顺序连续。

**4 词**

---

## continual

- **音标**: kәn'tinjuәl
- **释义**: a. 持续不断的, 继续的\n[建] 连续的
- **英文释义**: happening repeatedly over a period of time, with possible short breaks in between
- **词根词缀**: con(一起)+tin(保持)+ual(形容词)→保持在一起的→连续的
- **例句**:
- The project was delayed by continual technical problems.
- She faced continual criticism from her supervisor despite her strong performance.
- **考频**: 11
## continue

- **音标**: kәn'tinju:
- **释义**: vi. 继续, 延续, 延长\nvt. 使继续, 使延长
- **英文释义**: to keep doing or happening without stopping
- **词根词缀**: con(一起)+tin(保持)+ue→保持在一起→继续
- **例句**:
- The government has decided to continue its economic reforms despite public opposition.
- Having completed the first phase, the research team will continue the experiment next month.
- **考频**: 396
## continuous

- **音标**: kәn'tinjuәs
- **释义**: a. 连续的, 继续的, 连续不断的\n[计] 连续的
- **英文释义**: happening without interruption or pause
- **词根词缀**: con(一起)+tin(保持)+uous(形容词)→保持在一起的→连续的
- **例句**:
- The continuous rise in global temperatures poses a serious threat to biodiversity.
- She maintained a continuous focus on her research throughout the three-year project.
- **考频**: 34
## consecutive

- **音标**: kәn'sekjutiv
- **释义**: a. 连续的, 联贯的\n[计] 连续的; 连接的
- **英文释义**: Following one after another in unbroken sequence
- **词根词缀**: con(共同)+secut(跟随)+ive(形容词)→一个跟一个→连续的
- **例句**:
- The team won three consecutive championships from 2020 to 2022.
- She missed five consecutive days of class due to illness, which affected her midterm performance.
- **考频**: 7
