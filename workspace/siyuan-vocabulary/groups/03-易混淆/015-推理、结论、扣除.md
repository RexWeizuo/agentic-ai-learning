# 推理、结论、扣除

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: deduce 是逻辑推论，deduct 是数学或财务上的扣除。

**4 词**

---

## conclude

- **音标**: kәn'klu:d
- **释义**: vt. 结束, 作结论, 推断\nvi. 结束, 推断
- **英文释义**: to bring something to an end; to reach a decision or judgment based on evidence or reasoning
- **词根词缀**: con(一起)+clude(关闭)→一起关闭→结束
- **例句**:
- The conference concluded with a keynote speech by the Nobel laureate.
- Based on the experimental data, the researchers concluded that the hypothesis was unsupported.
- **考频**: 143
## conclusion

- **音标**: kәn'klu:ʒәn
- **释义**: n. 结论, 结尾, 推论\n[法] 缔结, 结论, 推论
- **英文释义**: A judgment or decision reached after considering evidence or facts; the end or final part of something.
- **词根词缀**: con(一起)+clus(关闭)+ion(名词)→一起关闭→结论
- **例句**:
- The study's conclusion was that regular exercise significantly improves cognitive function in older adults.
- After reviewing all the evidence, the court reached the conclusion that the defendant was not guilty.
- **考频**: 143
## deduce

- **音标**: di'dju:s
- **释义**: vt. 推论, 演绎出\n[法] 推断, 推出, 演绎
- **英文释义**: to reach a conclusion by reasoning from given information or evidence
- **词根词缀**: de(向下)+duc(引导)+e→向下引导→推论
- **例句**:
- From the defendant's inconsistent statements, the jury was able to deduce that he was lying.
- Scientists deduced the existence of the planet from subtle gravitational perturbations in the orbit of nearby stars.
- **考频**: 14
## deduct

- **音标**: di'dʌkt
- **释义**: vt. 扣除, 减去\n[经] 扣除, 减去, 折扣
- **英文释义**: to subtract an amount from a total, especially for tax or payment purposes
- **词根词缀**: de(向下)+duct(引导)→向下引导→扣除
- **例句**:
- The company will deduct $200 from your salary to cover the training fee.
- You can deduct charitable donations from your taxable income when filing your tax return.
- **考频**: 1
