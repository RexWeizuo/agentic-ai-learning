# 易混淆 (gu- 开头的词)

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: guidance 指导，guideline 方针，guild 协会，guitar 吉他，gulf 海湾/隔阂，gymnasium 体育馆。

**6 词**

---

## guidance

- **音标**: 'gaidns
- **释义**: n. 指导, 领导\n[医] 导
- **英文释义**: advice or direction intended to help someone make decisions or behave in a particular way
- **词根词缀**: guid(引导)+ance(名词后缀)→引导
- **例句**:
- The committee provided expert guidance on ethical research practices.
- Students often rely on academic advisors for guidance in selecting graduate programs.
- **考频**: 35
## guideline

- **音标**: 'gaidlain
- **释义**: n. 指导路线, 方针, 指标\n[经] 指导路线, 方针, 准则
- **英文释义**: an official recommendation or instruction that tells people how something should be done
- **词根词缀**: guide(引导)+line(线)→指导方针
- **例句**:
- The World Health Organization issued new guidelines for the management of infectious diseases.
- Before submitting your thesis, please consult the department's formatting guidelines to ensure compliance.
- **考频**: 62
## guild

- **音标**: gild
- **释义**: n. 团体, 协会, 行业协会\n[经] 同业公会
- **英文释义**: an association of people with common interests or goals, especially one formed to protect the interests of its members in a particular profession or trade
- **词根词缀**: guild(行会)→行会
- **例句**:
- The medieval guild controlled the quality and pricing of goods produced by its members.
- Modern professional associations, such as the American Bar Association, evolved from the traditional guild system.
- **考频**: 6

## gulf

- **音标**: gʌlf
- **释义**: n. 海湾, 深渊, 漩涡, 隔阂\nvt. 吞没
- **英文释义**: a large area of sea partially enclosed by land; a deep chasm or whirlpool; a wide gap or division, especially between people or groups
- **词根词缀**: gulf(海湾)→海湾
- **例句**:
- The Persian Gulf is a strategically vital body of water in the Middle East.
- A growing gulf between the rich and the poor threatens social cohesion in many developed countries.
- **考频**: 13
## gymnasium

- **音标**: dʒim'neiziәm
- **释义**: n. 健身房, 体育馆\n[医] 体育馆, 健身室, 运动场
- **英文释义**: A large room or building equipped for sports, physical education, or exercise.
- **词根词缀**: gymnast(体操)+ium(场所)→体育馆
- **例句**:
- The university's new gymnasium features state-of-the-art fitness equipment and a regulation basketball court.
- Students are required to attend physical education classes in the school gymnasium twice a week.
- **考频**: 2
