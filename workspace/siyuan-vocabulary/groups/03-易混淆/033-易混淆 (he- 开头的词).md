# 易混淆 (he- 开头的词)

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: he- 开头词汇。headquarters 总部，heave 举起/起伏，hedge 树篱/套期保值，heir 继承人，hemisphere 半球。

**10 词**

---

## headquarters

- **音标**: hed'kwɒ:tәz
- **释义**: n. 总部, 司令部, 总部人员\n[经] 本部, 总部, 总署
- **英文释义**: The main office or central location from which an organization is managed or controlled.
- **词根词缀**: headquarters
- **例句**:
- Apple's global headquarters is located in Cupertino, California.
- The military launched the operation from its forward headquarters near the border.
## heal

- **音标**: hi:l
- **释义**: vi. 痊愈\nvt. 使复原, 使和解, 治愈
- **英文释义**: to become healthy again after an illness or injury; to restore to soundness or wholeness
- **词根词缀**: heal(治愈)→治愈
- **例句**:
- The wound took two weeks to heal completely.
- Time and empathy are essential to heal the rift between the two communities.
- **考频**: 12
## healthy

- **音标**: 'helθi
- **释义**: a. 健康的, 有益健康的, 卫生的\n[医] 健康的
- **英文释义**: in good physical or mental condition; promoting good health
- **词根词缀**: heal(治愈)+th(状态)→健康状态→健康的
- **例句**:
- A balanced diet and regular exercise are essential for maintaining a healthy lifestyle.
- The government has introduced new policies to promote healthy eating habits among schoolchildren.
- **考频**: 216
## hearing

- **音标**: 'hiәriŋ
- **释义**: n. 听, 听觉, 听讯\n[医] 听, 听觉
- **英文释义**: the ability to perceive sound; a formal legal proceeding where evidence and arguments are presented before a judge or tribunal
- **词根词缀**: hear(听)+ing(动名词)→听力
- **例句**:
- Her hearing has gradually declined since the age of sixty.
- The defendant will appear at the hearing next Monday to contest the charges.
- **考频**: 5
## heat

- **音标**: hi:t
- **释义**: n. 热, 热度, 体温, 高潮\nvi. 加热, 激昂, 加剧\nvt. 把...加热, 使激动
- **英文释义**: the quality of being hot; thermal energy; a period of intense emotion or activity
- **词根词缀**: heat(热)→热
- **例句**:
- The heat from the sun made the asphalt shimmer.
- Political tensions are rising, and the heat of the debate is intensifying ahead of the election.
- **考频**: 121
## heave

- **音标**: hi:v
- **释义**: n. 举, 抛, 起伏, 鼓起\nvt. 用力举起, 使举起, 使鼓起\nvi. 抛出, 起伏, 喘息, 凸起
- **英文释义**: to lift or pull something heavy with great effort; to rise and fall rhythmically (e.g., chest, sea); to vomit (informal)
- **词根词缀**: heave(举起)→举起
- **例句**:
- The sailors heaved the anchor aboard with strained groans.
- Her chest heaved as she struggled to catch her breath after the grueling marathon.
- **考频**: 6
## hedge

- **音标**: hedʒ
- **释义**: n. 树篱, 障碍, 套头交易\nvt. 用树篱围, 套期保值, 妨碍, 两面下注以防...的损失\nvi. 筑树篱, 躲闪, 两面下注以防损失\na. 树篱的, 偷偷摸摸的
- **英文释义**: A barrier of closely planted shrubs or bushes; to reduce risk in finance by offsetting potential losses; to avoid commitment or give an evasive answer.
- **词根词缀**: hedge(树篱)→树篱
- **例句**:
- The company used futures contracts to hedge against rising raw material costs.
- She hedged her statement with qualifiers like 'possibly' and 'to some extent', making it difficult to hold her accountable.
- **考频**: 3
## heighten

- **音标**: 'haitn
- **释义**: vt. 增高, 提高, 加强\nvi. 升高, 变大
- **英文释义**: to increase the intensity, degree, or strength of something
- **词根词缀**: height(高度)+en(使)→使增高
- **例句**:
- The government's new policy is expected to heighten public concern about environmental protection.
- Stress can heighten sensitivity to pain and impair cognitive performance.
- **考频**: 19
## heir

- **音标**: єә
- **释义**: n. 继承人, 嗣子, 后嗣\n[法] 继承人, 后嗣
- **英文释义**: a person legally entitled to inherit property, title, or office after another's death
- **词根词缀**: heir(继承)→继承人
- **例句**:
- As the eldest son, he is the heir to the family estate and its centuries-old business empire.
- The monarch announced her youngest daughter as the heir apparent, signaling a historic shift in the line of succession.
- **考频**: 5
## hemisphere

- **音标**: 'hemisfiә
- **释义**: n. 半球, 地球的半面, 大脑半球\n[医] 半球
- **英文释义**: One of the two equal parts into which a sphere, especially the Earth or the brain, is divided by a plane passing through its center.
- **词根词缀**: hemi(半)+sphere(球)→半球
- **例句**:
- The equator divides the Earth into the Northern and Southern Hemispheres.
- Damage to the left cerebral hemisphere often impairs language abilities in right-handed individuals.
- **考频**: 32
