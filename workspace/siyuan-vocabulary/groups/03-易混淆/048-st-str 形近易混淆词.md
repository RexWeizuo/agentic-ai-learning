# st/str 形近易混淆词

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: 拼写相近但意思不同：stain (污点), strain (拉紧/压力), strand (股/海滨), strap (皮带), strip (长条/剥夺), stripe (条纹)。

**6 词**

---

## stain

- **音标**: stein
- **释义**: n. 污染, 污点, 着色剂\nvt. 沾染, 染污, 着色\nvi. 变脏
- **英文释义**: a mark or discoloration caused by a foreign substance; to cause such a mark or discoloration
- **词根词缀**: stain→污点,染色
- **例句**:
- The coffee left a stubborn stain on his white shirt, undermining his professional appearance.
- Her reputation was permanently stained by the scandal, despite her repeated denials.
- **考频**: 16
## strain

- **音标**: strein
- **释义**: n. 紧张, 拉紧, 张力, 过劳, 扭伤, 血缘, 种, 族, 气质, 曲调, 旋律, 口吻\nvt. 使劳累, 拉紧, 过分使用, 扭伤, 滥用, 曲解, 滤\nvi. 尽力, 努力, 紧拉, 弯曲, 被滤出
- **英文释义**: n. physical or mental tension; excessive demand on resources; a distinct inherited type within a species; a musical phrase or tone quality; vt./vi. to exert excessively, stretch tightly, filter, or distort
- **词根词缀**: strain→拉紧
- **例句**:
- Prolonged screen time can cause eye strain and mental fatigue.
- The researcher had to strain every resource to complete the experiment before the deadline.
- **考频**: 56
## strand

- **音标**: strænd
- **释义**: n. (绳索的)股, 绳, 串, 海滨, 河岸\nvi. 搁浅\nvt. 使搁浅, 使落后, 使陷于困境, 弄断, 搓
- **英文释义**: A single thread or fiber forming part of a rope; a stretch of shore; to leave (a ship or person) stranded, especially on land or in difficulty.
- **词根词缀**: strand→股
- **例句**:
- The rescue team worked tirelessly to free the stranded hikers trapped by the sudden landslide.
- Her argument lacked coherence, with several key ideas left as disconnected strands rather than a unified line of reasoning.
- **考频**: 8
## strap

- **音标**: stræp
- **释义**: n. 带, 皮带\nvt. (用皮带)捆扎
- **英文释义**: A narrow strip of material, typically leather or fabric, used for fastening, supporting, or carrying something.
- **词根词缀**: strap→带子
- **例句**:
- She tightened the strap of her backpack before hiking up the steep trail.
- The surgeon used a sterile strap to immobilize the patient's wrist during the procedure.
- **考频**: 6
## strip

- **音标**: strip
- **释义**: n. 长条, 条状, 带, 脱衣舞\nvt. 脱衣, 被剥去, 剥夺, 拆卸\nvi. 脱衣服
- **英文释义**: n. a long, narrow piece of material; a strip of land or fabric; also, a type of erotic dance. vt. to remove clothing, coverings, or parts from something; to deprive someone of power, rights, or possessions.
- **词根词缀**: strip→条
- **例句**:
- The engineers had to strip the engine down to its core components for inspection.
- The government was accused of stripping civil liberties from citizens under the new security law.
- **考频**: 24
## stripe

- **音标**: straip
- **释义**: n. 斑纹, 条纹\n[医] 纹, 条纹
- **英文释义**: a long, narrow band of a different color or texture from its surroundings
- **词根词缀**: stripe→条纹
- **例句**:
- The zebra's black and white stripes provide camouflage in the savanna.
- The surgeon noticed an unusual vertical stripe of discoloration on the patient's thigh, prompting further dermatological evaluation.
- **考频**: 10
