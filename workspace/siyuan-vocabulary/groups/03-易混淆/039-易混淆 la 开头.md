## lake

- **音标**: leik
- **释义**: n. 湖, 池, 色淀\nv. (使)血球溶解
- **英文释义**: a large body of water surrounded by land; also, to undergo or cause hemolysis (rare, technical usage)
- **词根词缀**: lake(词根:湖)→湖
- **例句**:
- The ancient lake has existed for over ten thousand years, preserving unique geological records.
- In the laboratory, certain toxins can lake red blood cells, leading to hemolytic anemia.
- **考频**: 51
## lawn

- **音标**: lɒ:n
- **释义**: n. 草地, 草坪, 上等细麻布\n[机] 细筛
- **英文释义**: an area of short, regularly mowed grass, typically in a garden or park
- **词根词缀**: lawn(词根:草地)→草地
- **例句**:
- The children were playing soccer on the lush green lawn.
- She sat on the porch overlooking the neatly trimmed lawn and old oak tree.
- **考频**: 17
## lamb

- **音标**: læm
- **释义**: n. 小羊, 羔羊\nv. 产羊羔
- **英文释义**: a young sheep, typically under one year old; to give birth to a lamb
- **词根词缀**: lamb(词根:羔羊)→羔羊
- **例句**:
- The farmer checked the ewes regularly to ensure they would lamb safely in spring.
- Lamb is often preferred over other red meats in gourmet cooking due to its tenderness and mild flavor.
- **考频**: 11
## lame

- **音标**: leim
- **释义**: a. 跛足的, 僵痛的, 不完全的, 金属薄板, 不知内情的人\nvi. 变跛\nvt. 使成残废, 使无用
- **英文释义**: having a physical disability that impairs walking; also used informally to mean weak, unconvincing, or inadequate
- **词根词缀**: lame(词根:跛的)→跛的
- **例句**:
- His argument was so lame that no one in the seminar took it seriously.
- After the accident, he developed a lame leg and required long-term rehabilitation.
