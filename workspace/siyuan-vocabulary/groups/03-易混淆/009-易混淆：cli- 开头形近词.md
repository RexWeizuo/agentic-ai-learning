# 易混淆：cli- 开头形近词

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: cli-/clo- 形近词：cliff(悬崖), climate(气候), climax(高潮), climb(攀登), clip(修剪/夹子), cloak(斗篷/掩护)。

**6 词**

---

## cliff

- **音标**: klif
- **释义**: n. 悬崖, 绝壁
- **英文释义**: a steep rock face, especially at the edge of the sea or a high place
- **词根词缀**: cliff(悬崖)→悬崖
- **例句**:
- The hikers paused at the edge of the cliff to admire the panoramic ocean view.
- Erosion has weakened the cliff face, prompting authorities to close the coastal path for safety.
- **考频**: 19
## climate

- **音标**: 'klaimit
- **释义**: n. 气候, 社会趋势, 气候区\n[医] 气候
- **英文释义**: the typical weather conditions in a particular area over a long period; also used metaphorically to describe prevailing social, political, or cultural conditions
- **词根词缀**: clim(倾斜)+ate(名词)→倾斜的地区→气候
- **例句**:
- Global warming is significantly altering the Earth's climate and increasing the frequency of extreme weather events.
- The current political climate makes it difficult for bipartisan legislation to pass through Congress.
- **考频**: 243
## climax

- **音标**: 'klaimæks
- **释义**: n. 高潮, 极点, 层进法\nv. (使)达到顶点, (使)达到高潮
- **英文释义**: The highest or most intense point in a sequence of events, development, or narrative; also, a rhetorical device involving gradual increase in force or importance.
- **词根词缀**: cli(倾斜)+max(最大)→最大倾斜→高潮
- **例句**:
- The assassination of the president was the dramatic climax of the political thriller.
- Her groundbreaking discovery climaxed a decade of relentless research in molecular biology.
- **考频**: 3
## climb

- **音标**: klaim
- **释义**: v. 攀登, 上升, 爬\nn. 攀登, 爬升
- **英文释义**: to go up a slope, stairs, ladder, or mountain; to increase gradually
- **词根词缀**: climb(爬)→攀登
- **例句**:
- The hikers began to climb the steep trail at dawn.
- Stock prices continued to climb despite rising interest rates.
- **考频**: 92
## clip

- **音标**: klip
- **释义**: n. 修剪, 夹子, 回形针, 剪下来的东西\nvt. 修剪, 痛打, 夹牢, 剪报\nvi. 剪报
- **英文释义**: n. a small device for holding things together; a short excerpt from audio/video; or a piece cut from something. vt. to cut, trim, or fasten with a clip.
- **词根词缀**: clip(剪)→夹子；剪
- **例句**:
- She used a metal clip to hold the stack of documents together.
- The journalist clipped several articles about climate policy for her research.
- **考频**: 9
## cloak

- **音标**: klәuk
- **释义**: n. 斗蓬, 大氅, 掩护\nvt. 遮掩, 隐匿, 使披斗蓬
- **英文释义**: A loose outer garment, typically worn for warmth or style; also, to conceal or disguise something.
- **词根词缀**: cloak(斗篷)→斗篷
- **例句**:
- The spy used his diplomatic status as a cloak for espionage activities.
- Her calm demeanor served as a cloak for the anxiety she felt before the presentation.
- **考频**: 2
