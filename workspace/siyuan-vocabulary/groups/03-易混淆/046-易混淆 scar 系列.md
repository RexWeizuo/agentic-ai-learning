# 易混淆 scar 系列

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: 拼写相近：scar(疤痕), scare(惊吓), scarce(缺乏的), scarcely(几乎不), scarf(围巾)。

**5 词**

---

## scar

- **音标**: skɑ:
- **释义**: n. 疤痕, 伤痕, 悬崖\nvi. 结疤, 愈合, 痊愈\nvt. 使有伤痕
- **英文释义**: A mark left on the skin or surface after a wound, burn, or injury has healed; also used metaphorically for lasting emotional or psychological damage.
- **词根词缀**: scar(疤痕)→疤痕
- **例句**:
- The surgeon minimized the scar with precise sutures, ensuring minimal cosmetic impact.
- Years after the economic crisis, many industries still bear the deep scars of lost investment and eroded trust.
- **考频**: 3
## scare

- **音标**: skєә
- **释义**: n. 惊吓, 恐慌\nvt. 惊吓, 使恐慌\nvi. 受惊
- **英文释义**: to frighten someone suddenly or severely; a sudden feeling of fear
- **词根词缀**: scare(惊吓)→惊吓
- **例句**:
- The loud thunderclap gave the children a real scare.
- Investors panicked and sold off stocks, triggering a market-wide scare.
- **考频**: 8
## scarce

- **音标**: skɑ:s
- **释义**: a. 难得的, 稀罕的, 缺乏的
- **英文释义**: insufficient in quantity or number; not easy to find or obtain
- **词根词缀**: scarc(缺乏)+e→缺乏的
- **例句**:
- Fresh drinking water is scarce in many arid regions.
- Skilled engineers remain scarce despite growing demand in the tech industry.
- **考频**: 11
## scarcely

- **音标**: 'skɑ:sli
- **释义**: adv. 简直不, 一定不, 仅仅
- **英文释义**: almost not; only just or barely
- **词根词缀**: scarce(缺乏)+ly(副词后缀)→几乎不
- **例句**:
- She had scarcely entered the room when the phone rang.
- So few people attended the lecture that the speaker could scarcely be heard.
- **考频**: 14
## scarf

- **音标**: skɑ:f
- **释义**: n. 围巾, 头巾, 领带, 领巾, 嵌接\nvt. 用围巾围, 嵌接
- **英文释义**: A piece of fabric worn around the neck, head, or shoulders for warmth, fashion, or function; also, a joint made by overlapping and fastening two pieces of material.
- **词根词缀**: scarf(围巾)→围巾
- **例句**:
- She wrapped a silk scarf around her neck to complement the winter coat.
- The carpenter used a scarf joint to strengthen the wooden beam.
- **考频**: 5
