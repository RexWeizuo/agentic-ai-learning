# angel/angle 形近

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: 拼写极度相似的形近词：angel（天使），angle（角度），ankle（踝），anchor（锚）。

**4 词**

---

## angel

- **音标**: 'eindʒәl
- **释义**: ==n. 天使, 守护神, 善人==
- **英文释义**: A spiritual being believed to act as a messenger or protector, often depicted as benevolent and divine.
- **词根词缀**: angel(使者)→神的使者→天使
- **例句**:
- She has been a true angel to me during this difficult time, offering unwavering support.
- In literature, the fallen angel symbolizes moral ambiguity and the complexity of human nature.
- **考频**: 6
## angle

- **音标**: 'æŋgl
- **释义**: ==n. 角, 角度, 角落\nvi. 钓鱼, 谋取, 博取, 斜向移动, 转变角度\nvt. 使转动角度, 在...钓鱼, 获取==
- **英文释义**: A figure formed by two rays sharing a common endpoint; a viewpoint or approach; to fish with a hook and line; to attempt to gain something indirectly.
- **词根词缀**: ang(弯曲)+le→弯曲处→角
- **例句**:
- The architect calculated the angle of the roof to ensure proper water drainage.
- She angled for a promotion by volunteering for high-visibility projects.
- **考频**: 16
## ankle

- **音标**: 'æŋkl
- **释义**: ==n. 踝\n[医] 踝, 踝关节==
- **英文释义**: The joint connecting the foot to the leg, where the tibia and fibula meet the talus bone.
- **词根词缀**: ank(bend)+le(small)→small bend→脚踝
- **例句**:
- She sprained her ankle while running on uneven terrain, causing swelling and limited mobility.
- During the physical examination, the doctor assessed the range of motion in both ankles to rule out ligament damage.
- **考频**: 4
## anchor

- **音标**: 'æŋkә
- **释义**: ==n. 锚\nvt. 抛锚停泊, 使固定\n[计] 锚==
- **英文释义**: A heavy metal object dropped from a ship to hold it in place; to secure or fix something firmly.
- **词根词缀**: anch(锚)+or→锚
- **例句**:
- The ship dropped anchor in the sheltered bay before nightfall.
- Her strong leadership anchored the team during the crisis, preventing panic and maintaining focus.
- **考频**: 8
