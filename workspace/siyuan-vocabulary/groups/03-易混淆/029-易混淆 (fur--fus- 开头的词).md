# 易混淆 (fur-/fus- 开头的词)

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: furnace 是火炉，furnish 是提供/布置，furious 是狂怒的，fuse 是保险丝/熔化。

**4 词**

---

## furnace

- **音标**: 'fә:nis
- **释义**: n. 火炉, 熔炉, 考验\nvt. 在炉中烧
- **英文释义**: A device or structure for producing intense heat, used for heating, smelting, or industrial processes; figuratively, a place or situation of intense trial.
- **词根词缀**: furn(炉)+ace(名词)→熔炉
- **例句**:
- The steel industry relies on blast furnaces to melt iron ore and extract pure iron.
- Facing relentless public scrutiny during the scandal, she described the experience as a moral furnace that tested her integrity.
- **考频**: 7
## furnish

- **音标**: 'fә:niʃ
- **释义**: vt. 供给, 提供, 装设\n[化] 供应; 供给; 配料
- **英文释义**: to provide something needed or useful, especially information, equipment, or furniture
- **词根词缀**: furn(提供)+ish(动词)→提供
- **例句**:
- The government furnished evidence to support its claim in the international tribunal.
- The contract requires the landlord to furnish the apartment with essential appliances before the tenant moves in.
- **考频**: 22
## furious

- **音标**: 'fjuәriәs
- **释义**: a. 狂怒的, 激烈的, 吵闹的
- **英文释义**: extremely angry or intense
- **词根词缀**: fur(愤怒)+ious(形容词)→狂怒的
- **例句**:
- She was furious when she discovered her colleague had taken credit for her research.
- The debate became furious as both sides refused to compromise on the core policy issue.
- **考频**: 3
## fuse

- **音标**: fju:z
- **释义**: n. 保险丝, 导火线\nv. 熔化, 融合
- **英文释义**: n. a safety device that melts and breaks an electrical circuit when current is too high; v. to melt or blend together
- **词根词缀**: fuse(熔合)→熔合
- **例句**:
- The circuit tripped when the fuse blew due to an overload.
- Cultural traditions from different regions have begun to fuse into a new, hybrid identity.
- **考频**: 4
