# 易混淆 (her- 开头的词)

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: herald 是先驱/使者，herb 是药草，herd 是兽群，heritage 是遗产。注意词义完全不同。

**4 词**

---

## herald

- **音标**: 'herәld
- **释义**: n. 使者, 传令官, 先驱\nvt. 预报, 宣布, 传达, 欢呼
- **英文释义**: A person or thing that announces or signals the approach of something important; to announce or signal the beginning or arrival of something.
- **词根词缀**: herald(传令官)→传令官
- **例句**:
- The cherry blossoms herald the arrival of spring in Japan.
- Her groundbreaking research heralded a new era in renewable energy technology.
- **考频**: 3
## herb

- **音标**: hә:b
- **释义**: n. 药草, 香草\n[医] 草, 草本, 草药
- **英文释义**: A plant or part of a plant valued for its medicinal, savory, or aromatic qualities.
- **词根词缀**: herb(草)→草本植物
- **例句**:
- Many traditional Chinese medicines are derived from herbal remedies using dried herb roots and leaves.
- The chef enhanced the dish with fresh herbs, including basil, thyme, and parsley.
- **考频**: 3
## herd

- **音标**: hә:d
- **释义**: n. 兽群, 人群, 牧人\nvt. 群集, 使成群, 放牧\nvi. 聚在一起
- **英文释义**: a large group of animals or people; to gather or move in a group, or to tend livestock
- **词根词缀**: herd(兽群)→兽群
- **例句**:
- The documentary showed a herd of elephants crossing the savanna.
- Urban professionals often herd into coffee shops during lunch breaks, seeking both caffeine and social connection.
- **考频**: 24
## heritage

- **音标**: 'heritidʒ
- **释义**: n. 遗产, 祖先遗留物, 继承物\n[医] 遗传性
- **英文释义**: something inherited from the past, such as traditions, culture, or biological traits
- **词根词缀**: herit(继承)+age(状态)→遗产
- **例句**:
- The Great Wall is a symbol of China's rich cultural heritage.
- Her susceptibility to hypertension may be linked to her genetic heritage.
- **考频**: 34
