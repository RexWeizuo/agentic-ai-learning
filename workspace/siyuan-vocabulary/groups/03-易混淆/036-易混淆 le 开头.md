# 易混淆 le 开头

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: le 开头易混淆动词，注意 lapse (流逝/失效) 与 leap (跳跃)

**4 词**

---

## lapse

- **音标**: læps
- **释义**: n. 过失, 流逝, 失效\nvi. 犯错, 堕落, 减退, 消失, 流逝\nvt. 使失效
- **英文释义**: a temporary failure or decline; the passing of time; the termination of a right or agreement due to inaction or expiration
- **词根词缀**: lapse(词根:滑落)→滑落
- **例句**:
- His security clearance was revoked after a lapse in background verification.
- The insurance policy lapsed when the premium payment was missed for three consecutive months.
- **考频**: 8
## lean

- **音标**: li:n
- **释义**: n. 瘦肉, 倾斜, 倾斜度\na. 瘦的, 贫乏的, 歉收的\nvi. 倚靠, 倾斜, 依赖\nvt. 使倾斜
- **英文释义**: v. to incline or rest against something for support; a. having little fat or excess; n. lean meat or a tendency toward scarcity
- **词根词缀**: lean(词根:倾斜)→倾斜
- **例句**:
- The economy is still recovering from a period of lean growth, with GDP rising by less than 1% annually.
- She leaned forward attentively during the professor's lecture on macroeconomic policy.
- **考频**: 52
## leap

- **音标**: li:p
- **释义**: n. 跳跃, 剧增, 急变, 被越过之物\nvi. 跳跃, 突然经过\nvt. 跃过, 使跃过
- **英文释义**: to jump suddenly and energetically, or to increase or change dramatically in a short time
- **词根词缀**: leap(词根:跳跃)→跳跃
- **例句**:
- The company's profits made a significant leap after the successful product launch.
- She leapt over the fence to escape the pursuing dog.
- **考频**: 28
## learn

- **音标**: lә:n
- **释义**: vt. 学习；认识到；得知
- **英文释义**: to gain knowledge or skill through study, experience, or being taught
- **词根词缀**: learn(词根:学习)→学习
- **例句**:
- She learned classical piano for ten years before entering the conservatory.
- The committee learned that the project had been delayed due to unforeseen regulatory hurdles.
- **考频**: 1597
