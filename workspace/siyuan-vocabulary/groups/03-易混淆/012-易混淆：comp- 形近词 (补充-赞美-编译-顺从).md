# 易混淆：comp- 形近词 (补充/赞美/编译/顺从)

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: complement(补充)与 compliment(赞美)是经典易混词；compile(编译/收集)，comply(顺从/遵守)。

**4 词**

---

## complement

- **音标**: 'kɒmplimәnt
- **释义**: n. 补足物, 补语, 补数\nvt. 补充, 补足\n[计] 补码; 反相器; 补数
- **英文释义**: Something that completes or enhances something else; to complete or enhance by providing what is missing
- **词根词缀**: com(一起)+ple(填充)+ment(名词)→填充在一起→补充
- **例句**:
- A strong background in statistics complements her expertise in machine learning.
- In digital logic, the two's complement is used to represent negative numbers efficiently.
- **考频**: 15
## compliment

- **音标**: 'kɒmplimәnt
- **释义**: n. 称赞, 恭维, 敬意\nvt. 称赞, 褒扬, 恭维
- **英文释义**: A polite expression of praise or admiration.
- **词根词缀**: com(一起)+pli(填满)+ment(名词)→一起填满→赞美
- **例句**:
- Her thoughtful presentation earned sincere compliments from the faculty.
- He complimented her on her insightful analysis during the seminar discussion.
- **考频**: 16
## compile

- **音标**: kәm'pail
- **释义**: vt. 编译, 编辑, 编纂, 收集\n[计] 编译
- **英文释义**: to gather and put together information, code, or material in a systematic way
- **词根词缀**: com(一起)+pile(堆)→堆在一起→汇编
- **例句**:
- The software developer must compile the source code before running the program.
- Researchers compiled data from over 50 clinical trials to write their meta-analysis.
- **考频**: 9
## comply

- **音标**: kәm'plai
- **释义**: vi. 顺从, 依从\n[法] 遵守, 承诺, 照做
- **英文释义**: to act in accordance with a request, rule, or law
- **词根词缀**: com(一起)+ply(折叠)→一起折叠→顺从
- **例句**:
- All companies must comply with the new data protection regulations.
- The defendant refused to comply with the court's order, leading to further legal consequences.
- **考频**: 22
