# 易混淆 ir 开头

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: ir 开头词汇，注意 irrigate (灌溉) 与 irritate (激怒)

**4 词**

---

## irrigate

- **音标**: 'irigeit
- **释义**: vt. 灌溉, 冲洗伤口, 使清新\nvi. 灌溉
- **英文释义**: to supply water to land or plants, especially to help crops grow; or to wash out a wound or cavity with liquid
- **词根词缀**: ir(进入)+rig(水)+ate(动词)→引入水→灌溉
- **例句**:
- Farmers in arid regions must irrigate their fields regularly to ensure crop survival.
- The surgeon used saline solution to irrigate the wound before suturing.
- **考频**: 5
## irritate

- **音标**: 'iriteit
- **释义**: vt. 激怒, 使发怒, 使兴奋, 使发炎\nvi. 引起不快
- **英文释义**: to make someone feel annoyed, impatient, or angry
- **词根词缀**: ir(进入)+rit(摩擦)+ate(动词)→摩擦进入→刺激
- **例句**:
- The constant noise from the construction site began to irritate the residents.
- Her dismissive tone irritated him so much that he interrupted her mid-sentence.
- **考频**: 12
## irony

- **音标**: 'aiәrәni
- **释义**: n. 反语, 讽刺, 具有讽刺意味的事
- **英文释义**: A situation or statement where the intended meaning is opposite to the literal or apparent meaning, often highlighting contradiction or absurdity.
- **词根词缀**: iron(铁)+y(名词)→铁一般的讽刺→讽刺
- **例句**:
- It's a bitter irony that the climate summit was held in a city suffering its worst drought in decades.
- The author uses verbal irony when the narrator praises the 'efficient' bureaucracy—while describing hours of pointless waiting.
- **考频**: 8
## irrespective

- **音标**: .iri'spektiv
- **释义**: a. 不顾的, 无关的, 没关系的
- **英文释义**: without regard to; regardless of
- **词根词缀**: ir(不)+re(再)+spect(看)+ive(形容词)→不看→不顾的
- **例句**:
- The policy applies to all employees, irrespective of their seniority.
- Admission to the program is based solely on merit, irrespective of the applicant's background.
- **考频**: 5
