# 易混淆：colon- / col- 形近词

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: colonel(上校，发音特殊), colonial(殖民的), colony(殖民地), column(圆柱/专栏)，注意拼写和发音。

**4 词**

---

## colonel

- **音标**: 'kә:nәl
- **释义**: n. 陆军上校, 长官
- **英文释义**: a senior military officer rank, typically above lieutenant colonel and below brigadier general
- **词根词缀**: co(共同)+lonel
- **例句**:
- The colonel briefed the joint task force on the new counterinsurgency strategy.
- She was promoted to colonel after two decades of distinguished service in the U.S. Army.
## colonial

- **音标**: kә'lәunjәl
- **释义**: a. 殖民的, 殖民地的\n[法] 殖民地居民
- **英文释义**: relating to colonies or colonialism, especially the political and cultural influence of a foreign power over a dependent territory
- **词根词缀**: colon(耕种)+ial(形容词)→殖民的
- **例句**:
- The colonial architecture in Goa reflects centuries of Portuguese rule.
- Postcolonial studies critically examine the lasting social and economic impacts of colonial administration.
- **考频**: 9
## colony

- **音标**: 'kɒlәni
- **释义**: n. 殖民地, 移民队\n[医] 菌(集)落, 菌丛; 移民区
- **英文释义**: A territory under the political control of a distant country; a group of organisms (e.g., bacteria) growing together as a distinct mass; or a settlement of people living abroad who maintain ties with their native land.
- **词根词缀**: colon(耕种)+y(名词)→殖民地
- **例句**:
- The American colonies declared independence from Britain in 1776.
- Under the microscope, the bacterial colony appeared as a dense, circular patch on the agar plate.
- **考频**: 37
## column

- **音标**: 'kɒlәm
- **释义**: n. 专栏, 圆柱, 纵队, 列, 柱形物\n[计] 列, 柱形图
- **英文释义**: A vertical arrangement of items, such as text in a newspaper, data in a table, or a solid cylindrical structure.
- **词根词缀**: column(柱子)→柱子；专栏
- **例句**:
- The editor invited her to write a monthly opinion column for the national newspaper.
- In the dataset, the 'revenue' column contains figures for each fiscal quarter.
- **考频**: 38
