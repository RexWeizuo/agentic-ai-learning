# stat/sta 系列易混淆词

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: 重点区分：stationary (静止的) vs stationery (文具)；statue (雕像) vs status (地位) vs statute (法令)。

**8 词**

---

## station

- **音标**: 'steiʃәn
- **释义**: n. 车站, 站, 局, 驻地, 位置, 身分, 地位\nvt. 安置, 配置, 驻扎\n[计] 站
- **英文释义**: A place where vehicles stop for passengers to get on or off; a fixed location for operation, service, or duty.
- **词根词缀**: stat(站立)+ion(名词)→车站,位置
- **例句**:
- The train will arrive at Beijing South Railway Station in ten minutes.
- Each department has been assigned a specific station in the emergency response plan.
- **考频**: 144
## stationary

- **音标**: 'steiʃ(ә)nәri
- **释义**: a. 不动的, 静止的, 不变的, 固定的, 停留的\n[计] 定常, 定态, 固定的
- **英文释义**: not moving, not changing, or fixed in position or condition
- **词根词缀**: station(静止)+ary(形容词)→静止的
- **例句**:
- The car remained stationary at the red light for over a minute.
- In a stationary economic model, key variables such as output and employment are assumed to remain constant over time.
- **考频**: 9
## stationery

- **音标**: 'steiʃәnәri
- **释义**: n. 文具, 信纸\n[法] 文具
- **英文释义**: Writing materials and office supplies, such as pens, paper, envelopes, and notebooks.
- **词根词缀**: station(商店)+ery(名词)→文具
- **例句**:
- She ordered high-quality stationery for her business correspondence.
- The conference provided branded stationery to all participants, including notepads and ballpoint pens.
- **考频**: 2
## statue

- **音标**: 'stætju
- **释义**: vt. 以雕像装饰\nn. 雕像
- **英文释义**: a three-dimensional work of art, typically representing a person or animal, made from materials such as stone, metal, or wood
- **词根词缀**: stat(站立)+ue(名词)→雕像
- **例句**:
- The ancient Greek statue of Athena, carved from marble, exemplifies classical ideals of harmony and proportion.
- The city council voted to erect a bronze statue in honor of the Nobel laureate, symbolizing her enduring contribution to science.
- **考频**: 8
## status

- **音标**: 'steitәs
- **释义**: n. 状态, 情形, 地位, 要人身份\n[计] 状态
- **英文释义**: a person's position or rank in relation to others; the current condition or state of something
- **词根词缀**: stat(站立)+us(名词)→地位,状态
- **例句**:
- Her status as a leading expert in artificial intelligence was confirmed by her keynote address at the international conference.
- The software displays the real-time status of each server, including uptime, CPU usage, and connection health.
- **考频**: 229
## statute

- **音标**: 'stætju:t
- **释义**: n. 法令, 成文法律, 法规, 章程, 条例\n[法] 法令, 法规, 成文法
- **英文释义**: A written law formally passed by a legislative body.
- **词根词缀**: stat(站立)+ute(名词)→法令,法规
- **例句**:
- The new environmental statute imposes strict penalties on industrial pollution.
- Courts must interpret the statute in light of its legislative history and purpose.
- **考频**: 4
## statistics

- **音标**: stә'tistiks
- **释义**: n. 统计学, 统计资料\n[计] 统计信息
- **英文释义**: The science of collecting, analyzing, interpreting, and presenting numerical data.
- **词根词缀**: stat(stand/state)+ist+-ics(…学/…学科)→站/状态
- **例句**:
- According to recent statistics, the unemployment rate has dropped to 4.2%.
- A solid understanding of statistics is essential for conducting rigorous empirical research in social sciences.
## statesman

- **音标**: 'steitsmәn
- **释义**: n. 政治家
- **英文释义**: a skilled and experienced politician who is respected for wisdom and integrity
- **词根词缀**: state(国家)+s(复数)+man(人)→政治家
- **例句**:
- As a seasoned statesman, she played a pivotal role in brokering the peace agreement.
- His statesmanlike conduct during the crisis earned widespread bipartisan admiration.
- **考频**: 9
