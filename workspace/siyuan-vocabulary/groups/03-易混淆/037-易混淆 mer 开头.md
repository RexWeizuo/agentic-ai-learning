# 易混淆 mer 开头

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: mer 开头形近词，涵盖水银、仁慈、仅仅与导师

**4 词**

---

## mercury

- **音标**: 'mә:kjuri
- **释义**: n. 水银, 汞, 使者\n[化] 汞Hg
- **英文释义**: A toxic silvery-white metallic chemical element (Hg) liquid at room temperature; also refers to the Roman messenger god or a swift messenger.
- **词根词缀**: mercur(水银)+y→水银
- **例句**:
- The laboratory banned mercury thermometers due to environmental and health risks.
- Like Mercury in Roman mythology, she delivered the urgent message across continents in under an hour.
- **考频**: 5
## mercy

- **音标**: 'mә:si
- **释义**: n. 仁慈, 宽恕, 慈悲, 怜悯, 幸运\n[法] 权宜处置权, 决定权, 宽恕
- **英文释义**: compassion or forgiveness shown toward someone whom it is within one's power to punish or harm
- **词根词缀**: merc(怜悯)+y→怜悯
- **例句**:
- The judge showed mercy and reduced the sentence despite the severity of the crime.
- She pleaded for mercy, knowing that her fate rested entirely in the committee's hands.
- **考频**: 9
## mere

- **音标**: miә
- **释义**: n. 小湖, 池塘\na. 仅仅的, 只不过的
- **英文释义**: n. a small lake or pond, especially in Britain; a. nothing more than; only
- **词根词缀**: mere(单纯的)→仅仅
- **例句**:
- The ancient village lies beside a tranquil mere surrounded by reeds.
- His apology was a mere formality, lacking any genuine remorse.
- **考频**: 79
## mentor

- **音标**: 'mentɔ:
- **释义**: n. 指导者, 良师益友
- **英文释义**: A wise and trusted counselor or teacher who guides a less experienced person
- **词根词缀**: ment(思考)+or(人)→导师
- **例句**:
- Her academic mentor helped her refine her research methodology for the thesis.
- Many graduate students seek a mentor to provide career advice and professional networking opportunities.
- **考频**: 9
