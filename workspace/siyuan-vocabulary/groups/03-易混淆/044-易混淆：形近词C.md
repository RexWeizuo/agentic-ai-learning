## phase

- **音标**: feiz
- **释义**: n. 时期, 局面, 方面, 位相, 相, 阶段\nvt. 使调整相位, 使定相, 使一致, 逐步执行, 实行\n[计] 阶段
- **英文释义**: A distinct period or stage in a process, development, or cycle; also, a particular aspect or state of something.
- **词根词缀**: phase(阶段)→阶段
- **例句**:
- The project is currently in its final phase before launch.
- Economic policy must be carefully phased in to avoid market disruption.
- **考频**: 46

- **英文释义**: n. a living organism that typically grows in soil, makes its own food via photosynthesis; also refers to a factory or industrial facility. vt. to put (seeds, seedlings, or ideas) into the ground or into a particular context for growth or development.

- **例句**:
- The government has introduced strict regulations to reduce emissions from every major industrial plant.
