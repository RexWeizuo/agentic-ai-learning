# 易混淆 lu 开头

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: lu 开头词汇，注意 lumber (木材) 与 lump (块/肿块)

**4 词**

---

## lumber

- **音标**: 'lʌmbә
- **释义**: n. 木材, 破旧家俱, 废物, 隆隆声\nvt. 砍伐木材, 乱堆\nvi. 笨拙地移动, 喧闹地向前走, 隆隆地移动
- **英文释义**: n. sawn wood for construction; v. to move in a heavy, clumsy way or to cut down trees for timber
- **词根词缀**: lumber→木材
- **例句**:
- The old warehouse was filled with lumber and discarded furniture.
- He lumbered across the stage, tripping slightly on the loose cable.
- **考频**: 2
## litter

- **音标**: 'litә
- **释义**: n. 垃圾, 杂乱, 轿, 担架\nvt. 乱丢, 铺草, 弄乱\nvi. 产仔, 乱丢垃圾
- **英文释义**: n. scattered waste or debris; a messy accumulation; vt. to discard trash carelessly; vi. (of animals) to give birth to a group of young
- **词根词缀**: litter(床铺)→杂乱的东西
- **例句**:
- The park was closed temporarily after visitors littered the grounds with plastic bottles and food wrappers.
- Environmental campaigns urge people not to litter, emphasizing that even one discarded cigarette butt contributes to ecosystem damage.
- **考频**: 20
## lump

- **音标**: lʌmp
- **释义**: n. 块, 瘤, 很多, 肿块, 笨人\nvt. 使成块状, 混在一起\nvi. 结块
- **英文释义**: A small, solid mass of something; a swelling or growth on the body; to combine things together without distinction
- **词根词缀**: lump→块
- **例句**:
- She felt a hard lump in her throat and struggled to speak during the presentation.
- The report lumps all developing countries together, ignoring their significant economic differences.
- **考频**: 16
## luxury

- **音标**: 'lʌkʃәri
- **释义**: n. 奢侈, 豪华\na. 奢侈的, 豪华的
- **英文释义**: something expensive and enjoyable but not necessary for basic living
- **词根词缀**: luxur(奢侈)+y(名词)→奢侈
- **例句**:
- Owning a private jet is a luxury few can afford.
- The hotel offers luxury accommodation with panoramic sea views.
- **考频**: 59
