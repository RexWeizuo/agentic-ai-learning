# 易混淆 some- 系列

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: 拼写相近但词性和用法不同：sometime(某时-adj/adv), sometimes(有时-adv), somewhat(稍微-adv), somewhere(某处-adv)。

**4 词**

---

## sometime

- **音标**: 'sʌmtaim
- **释义**: a. 以前的, 某一时间的\nadv. 改天, 来日
- **英文释义**: at an unspecified time in the past or future
- **词根词缀**: some(某个)+time(时间)→某个时候
- **例句**:
- She worked as a journalist sometime in the 1990s.
- Let’s meet for coffee sometime next week.
- **考频**: 10
## sometimes

- **音标**: 'sʌmtaimz
- **释义**: adv. 有时, 时常, 往往
- **英文释义**: on some occasions but not regularly or always
- **词根词缀**: some(某个)+time(时间)+s(副词后缀)→有时
- **例句**:
- Sometimes, despite careful preparation, unexpected challenges arise in academic research.
- She sometimes questions the validity of widely accepted theories in her graduate seminars.
- **考频**: 252
## somewhat

- **音标**: 'sʌmhwɒt
- **释义**: n. 某物, 几分\nadv. 多少, 几分
- **英文释义**: to a moderate or limited extent; slightly
- **词根词缀**: some(某个)+what(什么)→稍微
- **例句**:
- The results were somewhat disappointing, though not entirely unexpected.
- She was somewhat hesitant to accept the offer without reviewing the contract carefully.
- **考频**: 74
## somewhere

- **音标**: 'sʌmhwєә
- **释义**: adv. 到某处, 在某处
- **英文释义**: in or to an unspecified or unknown place
- **词根词缀**: some(某个)+where(地方)→某处
- **例句**:
- She must have left her keys somewhere in the office.
- If we keep walking, we'll eventually find somewhere to rest.
- **考频**: 40
