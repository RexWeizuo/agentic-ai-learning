# 易混淆 (gr- 开头的动词/名词)

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: 拼写相近的 gr- 词汇。grief/grieve 是悲伤，grim 是冷酷，grin 是露齿笑，grind 是磨，grip 是紧握，groan 是呻吟，grope 是摸索。

**8 词**

---

## grief

- **音标**: gri:f
- **释义**: n. 伤心, 忧愁, 悲痛, 不幸, 灾难
- **英文释义**: intense sorrow, especially caused by someone's death or a deep personal loss
- **词根词缀**: grief→悲伤
- **例句**:
- The sudden death of her father filled her with overwhelming grief.
- He struggled to cope with the grief following his wife's illness and eventual passing.
- **考频**: 18
## grieve

- **音标**: gri:v
- **释义**: vt. 使悲伤\nvi. 悲痛, 伤心
- **英文释义**: to feel intense sorrow, especially because someone has died
- **词根词缀**: grieve→悲伤
- **例句**:
- She continues to grieve the loss of her father two years after his death.
- The entire nation grieved when the beloved leader passed away.
- **考频**: 6
## grim

- **音标**: grim
- **释义**: a. 冷酷的, 坚强的, 残忍的, 可怕的, 讨厌的
- **英文释义**: severe, forbidding, or unrelenting in manner or appearance; harsh and unpleasant
- **词根词缀**: grim→冷酷的
- **例句**:
- The government released a grim forecast of economic contraction for the next two years.
- She faced the grim reality of her diagnosis with quiet resolve.
- **考频**: 4
## grin

- **音标**: grin
- **释义**: n. 露齿笑\nv. 露齿而笑
- **英文释义**: a broad smile that shows the teeth
- **词根词缀**: grin→露齿笑
- **例句**:
- She couldn't suppress a grin when she heard the good news.
- He grinned nervously as he stepped onto the stage to deliver his thesis defense.
- **考频**: 7
## grind

- **音标**: graind
- **释义**: n. 磨, 碾, 苦差, 摩擦声, 用功的学生\nvt. 磨擦, 磨碎, 磨光, 折磨, 压榨\nvi. 磨, 磨碎, 苦干
- **英文释义**: to crush, rub, or sharpen something by pressing it against a rough surface; also used figuratively to describe tedious, exhausting work or persistent effort
- **词根词缀**: grind→磨碎
- **例句**:
- The graduate student spent months grinding through dense academic papers to complete her literature review.
- The constant grind of preparing for the GRE left him mentally exhausted but academically sharper.
- **考频**: 3
## grip

- **音标**: grip
- **释义**: n. 紧握, 柄, 握力, 握手方式, 手提包, 掌握, 支配, 控制\nvt. 抓紧, 抱住, 吸住, 掌握\nvi. 握牢, 有吸引力
- **英文释义**: the act of holding something firmly; a firm hold or control; the ability to grasp or understand something
- **词根词缀**: grip→紧握
- **例句**:
- The speaker's compelling narrative had a strong grip on the audience's attention.
- To maintain balance on the icy slope, she tightened her grip on the trekking pole.
- **考频**: 19
## groan

- **音标**: grәun
- **释义**: n. 呻吟, 叹息\nvi. 呻吟, 抱怨, 受压迫\nvt. 呻吟地说
- **英文释义**: a low, deep sound expressing pain, discomfort, annoyance, or effort
- **词根词缀**: groan→呻吟
- **例句**:
- She let out a groan when she saw the pile of essays waiting to be graded.
- The old floorboards groaned under the weight of the heavy furniture.
- **考频**: 8
## grope

- **音标**: grәup
- **释义**: v. 摸索\nn. 摸索
- **英文释义**: to feel around with hands in order to find or locate something, especially in darkness or uncertainty
- **词根词缀**: grope→摸索
- **例句**:
- He groped for the light switch in the pitch-black room.
- The committee has been groping for a solution to the complex economic crisis.
- **考频**: 4
