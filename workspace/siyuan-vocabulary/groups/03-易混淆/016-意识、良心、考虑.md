# 意识、良心、考虑

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: 形近词辨析：conscious(有意识的)，conscience(良心)，considerate(体贴的)，considerable(相当大的)。

**5 词**

---

## conscious

- **音标**: 'kɒnʃәs
- **释义**: a. 有意识的, 知觉的, 觉察的\n[医] 有意识的, 清醒的
- **英文释义**: aware of and responding to one's surroundings; having mental faculties fully active
- **词根词缀**: con(共同)+sci(知道)+ous(形容词)→知道的→有意识的
- **例句**:
- She remained conscious throughout the minor surgical procedure.
- He became increasingly conscious of the ethical implications of his research.
- **考频**: 69
## conscience

- **音标**: 'kɒnʃәns
- **释义**: n. 良心\n[法] 良心, 道德感, 正义感
- **英文释义**: A person's moral sense of right and wrong, guiding their behavior and decisions.
- **词根词缀**: con(共同)+sci(知道)+ence(名词)→共同知道→良心
- **例句**:
- Her conscience compelled her to confess the mistake, even though it might cost her the promotion.
- The journalist refused to publish the unverified story, citing his professional conscience.
- **考频**: 11
## conscientious

- **音标**: .kɒnʃi'enʃәs
- **释义**: a. 有责任心的, 负责的, 本着良心的\n[法] 凭良心做的, 有道德感的
- **英文释义**: careful, thorough, and diligent in doing one's work or fulfilling responsibilities
- **词根词缀**: con(共同)+sci(知道)+ent(形容词)+ious(形容词)→知道良心的→尽责的
- **例句**:
- She is a conscientious researcher who double-checks all her data before publication.
- The conscientious student submitted the assignment two days ahead of the deadline to ensure accuracy and completeness.
- **考频**: 5
## considerate

- **音标**: kәn'sidәrit
- **释义**: a. 体贴的, 体谅的, 考虑周到的
- **英文释义**: Showing careful thought for the needs and feelings of others
- **词根词缀**: con(加强)+sider(星)+ate(形容词)→考虑周到的→体贴的
- **例句**:
- Her considerate gesture of bringing soup to her sick colleague greatly boosted team morale.
- A considerate leader always listens attentively before making decisions that affect the entire department.
- **考频**: 19
## considerable

- **音标**: kәn'sidәrәbl
- **释义**: a. 相当的, 可观的, 重要的
- **英文释义**: large in amount, degree, or importance
- **词根词缀**: con(加强)+sider(星)+able(能够)→能考虑的→相当大的
- **例句**:
- The project requires a considerable amount of time and resources.
- Her contribution to the field has been considerable, earning her international recognition.
- **考频**: 75
