# 易混淆 in 开头

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: in 开头动词与形容词，注意 invoke (援引/祈求) 与 involve (包括/陷入)

**4 词**

---

## inward

- **音标**: 'inwәd
- **释义**: a. 内心的, 向内的, 本来的\n[医] 向内的
- **英文释义**: directed or moving toward the inside; internal or mental rather than external or physical
- **词根词缀**: in(向内)+ward(方向)→向内的
- **例句**:
- Her inward struggle with self-doubt was invisible to others, yet it profoundly affected her performance.
- The surgeon made an inward incision along the medial aspect of the thigh to access the femoral artery.
- **考频**: 5
## invisible

- **音标**: in'vizәbl
- **释义**: a. 看不见的, 无形的\n[经] 无形的, 表面上看不见的, 未列在公开帐目的
- **英文释义**: not able to be seen; not visible to the naked eye or undetectable by ordinary means
- **词根词缀**: in(不)+vis(看)+ible(能)→看不见的
- **例句**:
- The virus is invisible to the naked eye, requiring electron microscopy for observation.
- Many economic activities in the informal sector remain invisible to official statistics and tax authorities.
- **考频**: 29
## invoke

- **音标**: in'vәuk
- **释义**: vt. 祈求, 恳求, 实行, 援引, 引起\n[计] 调用; 请求
- **英文释义**: to call upon a law, rule, or authority for support or justification; to activate or use a function or feature (especially in computing)
- **词根词缀**: in(不/无/进入)+voke
- **例句**:
- The defense attorney invoked the Fifth Amendment to avoid self-incrimination.
- When debugging the program, developers often invoke the debugger to inspect variable states.
## involve

- **音标**: in'vɒlv
- **释义**: vt. 包括, 使陷于, 潜心于, 包围\n[医] 累及, 牵涉, 包含
- **英文释义**: to include something as a necessary part or result; to cause someone to participate or be affected
- **词根词缀**: in(进入)+volv(卷)+e→卷入→涉及
- **例句**:
- The project involves close collaboration with international researchers.
- Diabetes can involve complications affecting the eyes, kidneys, and cardiovascular system.
- **考频**: 410
