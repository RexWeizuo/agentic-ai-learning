# 易混淆 (hel- 开头的词)

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: 拼写相近。helicopter 直升机，hell 地狱，helmet 头盔，helpful 有帮助的。

**4 词**

---

## helicopter

- **音标**: 'helikɒptә
- **释义**: n. 直升机\nvt. 由直升机运送\nvi. 乘直升机
- **英文释义**: A powered aircraft that can take off and land vertically, hover, and fly in all directions using rotating overhead blades.
- **词根词缀**: helico(螺旋)+pter(翼)→螺旋翼→直升机
- **例句**:
- The rescue team was airlifted to the earthquake zone by helicopter.
- Troops were helicoperted into the remote mountain area to secure the border.
- **考频**: 53
## hell

- **音标**: hel
- **释义**: n. 地狱, 邪恶势力, 苦境, 阴间, 毁坏, 训斥\nvi. 狂饮, 飞驰
- **英文释义**: a place of eternal punishment in Christian belief; often used informally to refer to a state of extreme suffering, chaos, or difficulty
- **词根词缀**: hell(地狱)→地狱
- **例句**:
- The refugees endured hell in the war-torn region, with no food, shelter, or medical care.
- After the merger, the company's internal restructuring turned the finance department into absolute hell for months.
- **考频**: 10
## helmet

- **音标**: 'helmit
- **释义**: n. 钢盔, 盔, 防护帽\nvt. 给...戴头盔
- **英文释义**: A protective head covering, typically made of hard material, worn to prevent injury.
- **词根词缀**: helm(头盔)+et(小)→小头盔→头盔
- **例句**:
- All construction workers are required to wear a helmet on site.
- The cyclist was unharmed in the crash thanks to her properly fitted helmet.
- **考频**: 16
## helpful

- **音标**: 'helpful
- **释义**: a. 有帮助的, 有益的, 有用的
- **英文释义**: providing useful assistance or support
- **词根词缀**: help(帮助)+ful(充满)→有帮助的
- **例句**:
- The professor's detailed feedback was extremely helpful for revising my thesis.
- A strong foundation in statistics is helpful when interpreting empirical research in social sciences.
- **考频**: 70
