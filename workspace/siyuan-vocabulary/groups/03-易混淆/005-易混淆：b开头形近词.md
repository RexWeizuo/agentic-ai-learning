# 易混淆：b开头形近词

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: 拼写极相近但意思完全不同的词对：bail(保释)/bait(饵), brake(刹车)/break(打破), bride(新娘)/bribe(贿赂), brow(眉毛)/browse(浏览)。

**8 词**

---

## bail

- **音标**: beil
- **释义**: n. 保释, 拎环, 杓, 栅栏\nvt. 保释, 舀水
- **英文释义**: n. the temporary release of an accused person awaiting trial, often on condition of a sum of money being paid; vt. to release (someone) on bail or to remove water from a boat using a container
- **词根词缀**: bail(保证)→保释
- **例句**:
- The judge denied bail for the suspect due to the severity of the charges.
- After the storm, the crew had to bail water continuously to keep the small vessel afloat.
- **考频**: 5
## bait

- **音标**: beit
- **释义**: n. 饵\nvt. 以饵引诱, 欺负\nvi. 中途休息
- **英文释义**: n. something used to attract or lure, especially to catch animals or people; vt. to provoke or harass deliberately; to place bait in order to catch fish
- **词根词缀**: bait(引诱)→诱饵
- **例句**:
- The police used false information as bait to trap the fraudster.
- He was accused of baiting his opponent with sarcastic remarks during the debate.
- **考频**: 2
## brake

- **音标**: breik
- **释义**: n. 刹车, 阻碍, 丛林\nv. 刹车
- **英文释义**: A device for slowing or stopping a vehicle, or the action of applying it; also, something that slows progress or development.
- **词根词缀**: brak(断裂)+e→使断裂→刹车
- **例句**:
- The driver slammed on the brake to avoid hitting the pedestrian.
- Strict regulations on data usage can act as a brake on technological innovation.
- **考频**: 20
## break

- **音标**: breik
- **释义**: n. 休息, 中断, 破裂处, 绝交, 破晓, 突变\nvt. 打破, 弄破, 弄坏, 破坏, 违反, 打断, 削弱, 放弃\nvi. 破碎, 决裂, 破晓, 突变, 变弱, 暂停\nn. 分隔符\n[计] 分隔符; 中断; DOS内部命令:设定扫描中断按键的时机
- **英文释义**: a pause or interruption in an activity; the act or result of separating into pieces; a violation of a rule or agreement; the first appearance of light in the sky before sunrise
- **词根词缀**: break(打破)→打破
- **例句**:
- After two hours of intense study, she took a ten-minute break to refresh her mind.
- The sudden news of the merger caused a break in diplomatic relations between the two countries.
- **考频**: 384
## bride

- **音标**: braid
- **释义**: n. 新娘
- **英文释义**: A woman who is about to be married or has just been married.
- **词根词缀**: bride(新娘)→新娘
- **例句**:
- The bride wore a stunning ivory gown and a delicate lace veil.
- At the reception, the bride and groom shared their first dance to a song they had chosen together.
- **考频**: 7
## bribe

- **音标**: braib
- **释义**: n. 贿赂\nvt. 贿赂, 收买\nvi. 行贿
- **英文释义**: Something of value given or offered to influence someone's behavior, especially to act dishonestly or illegally.
- **词根词缀**: bribe(面包屑)→小恩小惠→贿赂
- **例句**:
- The official was dismissed after being caught accepting a bribe from a construction company.
- Corporations that bribe foreign officials risk severe legal penalties under the Foreign Corrupt Practices Act.
- **考频**: 1
## brow

- **音标**: brau
- **释义**: n. 眉毛, 额\n[医] 额, 眉, 睫毛
- **英文释义**: The part of the face above the eye; the forehead or eyebrow.
- **词根词缀**: brow(眉毛)→眉毛
- **例句**:
- She raised her brow in mild surprise at the unexpected question.
- The patient exhibited increased tension in the brow, suggesting underlying anxiety.
- **考频**: 3
## browse

- **音标**: brauz
- **释义**: v. 浏览, 吃草\nn. 浏览, 吃草\n[计] 浏览
- **英文释义**: to look through information, items, or a website casually and without a specific goal; or to eat grass or leaves, especially as a herbivore
- **词根词缀**: brows(嫩芽)+e→吃嫩芽→浏览
- **例句**:
- She spent the afternoon browsing academic journals for relevant literature.
- Before submitting his research proposal, he browsed several databases to ensure originality.
- **考频**: 9
