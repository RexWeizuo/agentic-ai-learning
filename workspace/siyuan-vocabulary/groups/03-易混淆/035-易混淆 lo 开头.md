# 易混淆 lo 开头

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: 拼写相近的 lo 开头词汇，注意词义区分

**4 词**

---

## loyal

- **音标**: 'lɒiәl
- **释义**: a. 忠诚的, 忠实的, 忠贞的
- **英文释义**: having or showing strong support for and faithfulness to a person, group, or cause
- **词根词缀**: loyal(忠诚的)→忠诚的
- **例句**:
- She remained loyal to her principles despite intense political pressure.
- A truly loyal employee not only follows instructions but also advocates for the company's long-term interests.
- **考频**: 18
## loyalty

- **音标**: 'lɒiәlti
- **释义**: n. 忠贞, 忠诚, 忠实\n[法] 忠诚, 忠心
- **英文释义**: the quality of being loyal, especially to a person, cause, or organization
- **词根词缀**: loyal(忠诚的)+ty(名词)→忠诚
- **例句**:
- Her unwavering loyalty to the company earned her a promotion to senior management.
- The essay critically examines the tension between national loyalty and universal human rights.
- **考频**: 37
## lord

- **音标**: lɒ:d
- **释义**: n. 统治者, 阁下, 上帝\nvi. 称王, 作威作福\nvt. 使成贵族
- **英文释义**: a person who has power and authority over others; a title of respect for a British peer or judge; used as a formal or religious reference to God or Christ
- **词根词缀**: lord(主人)→贵族
- **例句**:
- The lord of the manor exercised considerable influence over local agricultural policies.
- In medieval England, feudal lords granted land to vassals in exchange for military service.
- **考频**: 21
## lofty

- **音标**: 'lɒfti
- **释义**: a. 高的, 傲慢的, 崇高的, 高级的, 玄虚的
- **英文释义**: high in position, rank, or ambition; noble and exalted in character or purpose; or arrogantly superior
- **词根词缀**: loft(高空)+y(形容词)→高耸的
- **例句**:
- The summit offered a lofty view of the entire mountain range.
- Her lofty ideals about social justice inspired many students to pursue public service.
- **考频**: 7
