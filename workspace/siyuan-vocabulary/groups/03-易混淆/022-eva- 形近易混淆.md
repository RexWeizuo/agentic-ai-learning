# eva- 形近易混淆

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: eva- 开头，考研常考形近动词

**4 词**

## evacuate

- **音标**: i'vækjueit
- **释义**: v. 疏散, 撤出, 排泄
- **英文释义**: to remove people from a dangerous place to a safer one; to discharge waste from the body
- **词根词缀**: e(出)+vac(空)+uate(动词)→使空出→疏散
- **例句**:
- Emergency services ordered residents to evacuate the flood-prone area before the dam overflowed.
- The hospital had to evacuate patients to nearby facilities after the earthquake damaged its structural integrity.
- **考频**: 5
## evade

- **音标**: i'veid
- **释义**: v. (巧妙地)逃脱, 规避, 逃避
- **英文释义**: to avoid or escape from something, especially in a clever or deceitful way
- **词根词缀**: e(出)+vad(走)+e→走出去→逃避
- **例句**:
- The suspect managed to evade arrest by slipping through a back alley.
- Many multinational corporations use offshore accounts to evade paying higher taxes in their home countries.
- **考频**: 11
## evaluate

- **音标**: i'væljueit
- **释义**: vt. 评估, 评价, 赋值
- **英文释义**: to judge or calculate the quality, importance, amount, or value of something
- **词根词缀**: e(出)+valu(价值)+ate(动词)→给出价值→评价
- **例句**:
- Researchers must carefully evaluate the validity of each dataset before drawing conclusions.
- The committee will evaluate candidates' academic records, research experience, and letters of recommendation.
- **考频**: 58
## evaporate

- **音标**: i'væpәreit
- **释义**: vi. 蒸发, 失去水分, 消失\nvt. 使蒸发, 使脱水, 使消失
- **英文释义**: to change from a liquid into a vapor, or to disappear gradually
- **词根词缀**: e(出)+vapor(蒸汽)+ate(动词)→蒸发
- **例句**:
- The puddle evaporated quickly under the hot sun.
- Many small businesses evaporated during the economic crisis.
- **考频**: 11
