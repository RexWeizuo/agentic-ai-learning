# 易混淆：dra- 形近词

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: 以 dra- 开头的形近词，注意 draft(草稿/汇票) 与 drawback(缺点) 的熟词僻义。

**5 词**

---

## draft

- **音标**: dræft. drɑ:ft
- **释义**: n. 气流, 草稿, 汇票, 草案\nvt. 起草, 征兵\n[计] 草稿
- **英文释义**: a preliminary version of a written document; a current of air; a formal financial order for payment; the process of selecting people for military service
- **词根词缀**: draft(拉)→拉→草稿
- **例句**:
- The editor asked the author to revise the first draft before submission.
- A sudden draft from the open window made the candle flicker during the meeting.
- **考频**: 41
## drag

- **音标**: dræg
- **释义**: n. 拖, 拖累\nv. 拖累, 拖拉, 沉重缓慢地走, 拖动\n[计] 拖动
- **英文释义**: to pull something or someone along with effort; also, a slowing or hindering influence
- **词根词缀**: drag(拉)→拉→拖
- **例句**:
- The constant delays dragged the project out for months.
- Her lack of experience was a drag on the team's overall performance.
- **考频**: 17
## draw

- **音标**: drɒ:
- **释义**: vi. 拉, 拖, 拔剑\nvt. 拖拉, 挨近, 领取, 打成平局, 引导, 抽签决定, 画, 描写, 制订, 草拟, 吸引\nn. 拉, 拖, 拔出, 抽签, 平局\n[计] 翻牌, 绘图
- **英文释义**: To pull or drag something; to make a picture with a pencil, pen, or brush; to attract attention; to end in a tie (in games); to withdraw money or resources; to formulate or draft (e.g., a plan or document).
- **词根词缀**: draw→拉
- **例句**:
- The two teams drew 2–2 in the final match, resulting in a tense penalty shootout.
- She drew up a detailed contract before launching the joint venture.
- **考频**: 310
## drawing

- **音标**: 'drɒ:iŋ
- **释义**: n. 图画, 制图, 拉\n[计] 绘图
- **英文释义**: a picture or diagram made with pencil, pen, or other instrument; the act or art of making such pictures
- **词根词缀**: draw(拉)+ing(名词)→图画
- **例句**:
- The architect presented detailed technical drawings to the construction team.
- Her expressive charcoal drawing of the old cathedral was selected for the graduate exhibition.
- **考频**: 16
## drawback

- **音标**: 'drɒ:bæk
- **释义**: n. 缺点, 退税\n[法] 退款, 退税
- **英文释义**: a disadvantage or problem that makes something less attractive, effective, or desirable
- **词根词缀**: draw(拉)+back(回)→拉回→缺点
- **例句**:
- One major drawback of remote work is the lack of face-to-face interaction with colleagues.
- Although the policy aims to boost exports, its main drawback is the potential loss of domestic jobs.
- **考频**: 12
