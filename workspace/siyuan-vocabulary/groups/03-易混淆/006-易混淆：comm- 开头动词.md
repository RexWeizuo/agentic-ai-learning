# 易混淆：comm- 开头动词

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: 拼写相近的 comm- 动词：command(命令), commemorate(纪念), commence(开始), commend(推荐/嘉奖), comment(评论)。

**5 词**

---

## command

- **音标**: kә'mɑ:nd
- **释义**: n. 命令, 指挥, 控制, 部队, 司令部\nv. 命令, 指挥, 控制\n[计] 命令; 指令; DOS外部命令:启动新的命令处理器
- **英文释义**: to order someone to do something; the power or authority to control or direct others; a formal instruction or directive
- **词根词缀**: com(加强)+mand(命令)→命令
- **例句**:
- The general issued a command to advance at dawn.
- She has full command of both English and French, which gives her a significant advantage in international negotiations.
- **考频**: 45
## commemorate

- **音标**: kә'memәreit
- **释义**: vt. 纪念
- **英文释义**: to honor or remember an important event or person, typically with a ceremony or special action
- **词根词缀**: com(一起)+memor(记忆)+ate(动词)→一起记忆→纪念
- **例句**:
- The university held a solemn ceremony to commemorate the 100th anniversary of its founding.
- A national memorial was erected to commemorate the soldiers who lost their lives in the war.
- **考频**: 3
## commence

- **音标**: kә'mens
- **释义**: v. (使)开始, 得学位
- **英文释义**: to begin or start something, especially something formal or official
- **词根词缀**: com(加强)+mence(开始)→开始
- **例句**:
- The trial will commence at 9 a.m. sharp.
- She commenced her doctoral research after completing her master's degree.
- **考频**: 3
## commend

- **音标**: kә'mend
- **释义**: vt. 嘉奖, 委托给, 推荐
- **英文释义**: to praise formally or officially; to entrust or recommend with confidence
- **词根词缀**: com(加强)+mend(委托)→委托→称赞
- **例句**:
- The committee commended her for her outstanding contribution to public health.
- The report commends the new policy to the government for urgent implementation.
- **考频**: 6
## comment

- **音标**: 'kɒment
- **释义**: n. 注解, 批评, 评论, 备注\nvi. 评论, 注解\n[计] 备注
- **英文释义**: a statement expressing an opinion, explanation, or criticism about something
- **词根词缀**: com(一起)+ment(思考)→一起思考→评论
- **例句**:
- The professor asked students to add brief comments to their essays to clarify key arguments.
- She refused to comment on the controversial policy, citing a need for further review.
- **考频**: 184
