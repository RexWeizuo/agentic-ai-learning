# 易混淆：co- 开头短词

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: co- 开头短词：coarse(粗糙的), coat(外套), code(代码), coke(焦炭/可乐), coil(线圈), comb(梳子)。

**6 词**

---

## coarse

- **音标**: kɒ:s
- **释义**: a. 粗糙的, 下等的, 粗俗的\n[计] 粗略的
- **英文释义**: rough in texture, low in quality, or lacking in refinement and sensitivity
- **词根词缀**: coarse(粗糙的)→粗糙的
- **例句**:
- The coarse sandpaper quickly removed the old paint but left deep scratches.
- His coarse humor offended several colleagues during the formal meeting.
- **考频**: 1
## coat

- **音标**: kәut
- **释义**: n. 外套\nvt. 外面覆盖, 给...穿外套
- **英文释义**: A piece of outer clothing worn on the upper body for warmth or protection; to cover a surface with a layer of something.
- **词根词缀**: coat(外套)→外套
- **例句**:
- She wore a tailored wool coat to the academic conference.
- The researchers coated the nanoparticles with a polymer layer to enhance biocompatibility.
- **考频**: 34
## code

- **音标**: kәud
- **释义**: n. 代码, 密码, 法规, 法典\nvt. 把...编码\n[计] 代码
- **英文释义**: A system of symbols, letters, or numbers used to represent information, especially for secrecy or computer processing; also, a set of rules or laws.
- **词根词缀**: code(法典)→代码
- **例句**:
- The software developer wrote clean, well-documented code to ensure maintainability.
- The judge ruled that the new environmental regulation was part of the state's administrative code.
- **考频**: 128
## coke

- **音标**: kәuk
- **释义**: n. 可口可乐, 焦炭\nv. (使)成焦炭
- **英文释义**: 1. n. a carbonaceous residue obtained by heating coal in the absence of air; 2. n. a trademarked cola-flavored soft drink; 3. v. to convert (coal or other organic material) into coke by destructive distillation
- **词根词缀**: coke(焦炭)→焦炭；可口可乐简称
- **例句**:
- The steel industry relies heavily on coke as a fuel and reducing agent in blast furnaces.
- He ordered a coke with ice, but the waiter mistakenly brought him a glass of water instead.
- **考频**: 9
## coil

- **音标**: kɒil
- **释义**: n. 线圈, 盘绕, 骚动, 卷\nvt. 盘绕, 卷
- **英文释义**: A length of wire or rope wound into a series of loops, or the act of winding something into such a shape.
- **词根词缀**: coil(卷)→卷曲→线圈
- **例句**:
- The electrician carefully connected the two ends of the coil to complete the circuit.
- She watched as the snake began to coil itself around the branch, ready to strike.
- **考频**: 2
## comb

- **音标**: kәum
- **释义**: n. 头梳, 鸡冠\nvt. 梳头发, 梳毛\nvi. (浪)涌起
- **英文释义**: A toothed tool for arranging or cleaning hair or fur; also, the fleshy red crest on a rooster's head; to pass a comb through (hair or wool) to clean or arrange it; (of waves) to rise and break in a steady succession.
- **词根词缀**: comb(梳子)→梳子
- **例句**:
- She used a fine-toothed comb to remove the tangles from her wet hair.
- The coastal research team observed how swells would comb steadily toward the shore under consistent offshore winds.
- **考频**: 2
