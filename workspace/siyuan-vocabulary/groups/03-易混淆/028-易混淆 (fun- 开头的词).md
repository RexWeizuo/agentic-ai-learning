# 易混淆 (fun- 开头的词)

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: function 是功能，fund 是资金，fundamental 是基础的，funeral 是葬礼，funny 是好笑的。

**5 词**

---

## function

- **音标**: 'fʌŋkʃәn
- **释义**: n. 官能, 职务, 功能, 函数\nvi. 活动, 运行, 行使职责\n[计] 功能, 函数
- **英文释义**: A natural or normal activity of a part of the body, an organization, or a system; or a relationship between two sets in mathematics where each element of the first set corresponds to exactly one element of the second set.
- **词根词缀**: funct(执行)+ion(名词)→功能
- **例句**:
- The liver performs several vital functions, including detoxification and protein synthesis.
- In programming, a function is a block of code designed to perform a specific task when called.
- **考频**: 259
## fund

- **音标**: fʌnd
- **释义**: n. 基金, 资金, 存款, 财源, 贮藏\nvt. 提供资金, 积累
- **英文释义**: A sum of money saved or collected for a specific purpose; to provide money for something.
- **词根词缀**: fund(基金)→基金
- **例句**:
- The government established a special fund to support renewable energy projects.
- Many universities rely on private donations to fund their research initiatives.
- **考频**: 173
## fundamental

- **音标**: .fʌndә'mentәl
- **释义**: n. 基本原理, 原则, 基波\na. 基本的, 重要的, 原音的
- **英文释义**: serving as a basis or foundation; essential and primary
- **词根词缀**: fundament(基础)+al(形容词)→基本的
- **例句**:
- The theory of relativity is a fundamental concept in modern physics.
- Respect for human rights is a fundamental principle of international law.
- **考频**: 97
## funeral

- **音标**: 'fju:nәrәl
- **释义**: n. 葬礼, 出殡
- **英文释义**: A ceremony held to honor and remember a deceased person, typically before burial or cremation.
- **词根词缀**: funer(葬礼)+al(名词)→葬礼
- **例句**:
- The entire town attended the funeral of the beloved teacher, reflecting her profound impact on generations of students.
- In his will, he requested a simple funeral with no religious rites, emphasizing dignity and brevity.
- **考频**: 12
## funny

- **音标**: 'fʌni
- **释义**: a. 好笑的, 有趣的, 滑稽的\nn. 滑稽人物
- **英文释义**: causing laughter or amusement; humorous or comical
- **词根词缀**: fun(乐趣)+ny(形容词)→有趣的
- **例句**:
- Her witty remarks and exaggerated gestures made her a funny presence at the seminar.
- The passage describes the protagonist’s funny misunderstanding of academic jargon, highlighting cultural differences in communication.
- **考频**: 23
