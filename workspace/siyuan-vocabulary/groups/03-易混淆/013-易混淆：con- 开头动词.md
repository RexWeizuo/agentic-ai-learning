# 易混淆：con- 开头动词

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: con- 开头动词：conceal(隐藏), concede(承认/退让), conceive(构思/怀孕), concentrate(集中/浓缩)。

**4 词**

---

## conceal

- **音标**: kәn'si:l
- **释义**: vt. 隐藏, 掩盖, 隐瞒\n[法] 隐瞒, 隐匿, 保守秘密
- **英文释义**: to hide something from view or knowledge, often deliberately
- **词根词缀**: con(完全)+ceal(隐藏)→完全隐藏→隐藏
- **例句**:
- The government was accused of concealing vital information about the environmental disaster.
- She tried to conceal her anxiety by smiling brightly during the interview.
- **考频**: 18
## concede

- **音标**: kәn'si:d
- **释义**: vt. 承认, 退让\nvi. 让步
- **英文释义**: to admit something is true, especially unwillingly; to yield a point or advantage in an argument or competition
- **词根词缀**: con(完全)+cede(走)→完全走开→让步
- **例句**:
- Although reluctant, the candidate conceded defeat after the final vote count was announced.
- The government conceded that its economic policy had failed to curb inflation.
- **考频**: 8
## conceive

- **音标**: kәn'si:v
- **释义**: vt. 构思, 认为\nvi. 怀孕
- **英文释义**: to form an idea or plan in the mind; to become pregnant
- **词根词缀**: con(一起)+ceive(拿)→拿到一起→构想
- **例句**:
- The architect conceived the building as a fusion of traditional and modern elements.
- It is difficult to conceive how such a complex system could evolve without natural selection.
- **考频**: 28
## concentrate

- **音标**: 'kɒnsәntreit
- **释义**: n. 浓缩, 精选\nv. 集中, 专心
- **英文释义**: v. to focus all one's attention or effort on something; n. a substance made by removing water or other diluting agents from a solution
- **词根词缀**: con(一起)+centr(中心)+ate(动词)→使到中心→集中
- **例句**:
- Students must concentrate on their research to produce high-quality theses.
- The company developed a fruit concentrate to extend shelf life and reduce transportation costs.
- **考频**: 86
