## plastic

- **音标**: 'plæstik
- **释义**: n. 塑料, 可塑体, 可塑性物质\na. 塑料的, 塑造的, 有可塑性的, 造型的, 易受影响的, 有创造力的
- **英文释义**: a synthetic polymer material that can be molded when heated and set into a rigid or slightly elastic form; also refers to the quality of being easily shaped or influenced
- **词根词缀**: plastic(词根:可塑的)→塑料
- **例句**:
- The widespread use of single-use plastic has led to severe environmental pollution.
- Her plastic personality allowed her to adapt quickly to diverse academic environments, making her an outstanding candidate for interdisciplinary research.
- **考频**: 83

- **考频**: 3
