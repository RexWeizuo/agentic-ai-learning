# ad- 动词易混

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: ad- 前缀动词，拼写相近但意思不同：address（地址/演说），addict（使上瘾），adhere（粘着/坚持）。

**4 词**

---

## address

- **音标**: ә'dres
- **词性**: ['n.', 'v.']
- **释义**: ==n. 住址, 演说, 举止, 灵巧, 求爱\nvt. 发表(演说或讲话), 对付, 写地址\n[计] 地址, 寻址==
- **英文释义**: n. (computer science) the code that identifies where a piece of information is stored\nn. the place where a person or organization can be found or communicated with\nn. the act of delivering a formal spoken communication to an audience\nn. the manner of speaking to another individual
- **同义词**: deal with, tackle
- **反义词**: departure, absence
- **词根词缀**: ad(朝向)+dress(整理)→处理
- **搭配**: address the issue, address the problem
- **例句**:
- The speaker delivered a powerful address on climate change.
- Please address the envelope carefully before mailing it.
- **考频**: 202
## addict

- **音标**: ә'dikt
- **词性**: ['n.', 'vt.']
- **释义**: ==vt. 使沉溺, 使上瘾\nn. 入迷的人, 上瘾者==
- **英文释义**: n. someone who is so ardently devoted to something that it resembles an addiction\nn. someone who is physiologically dependent on a substance; abrupt deprivation of the substance produces withdrawal symptoms\nv. to cause (someone or oneself) to become dependent (on something, especially a narcotic drug)
- **同义词**: fanatic, enthusiast, junkie
- **反义词**: teetotaler, abstainer
- **词根词缀**: ad(加强)+dict(说)→一再吩咐→使沉迷
- **搭配**: drug addict, internet addict, addict to gambling
- **例句**:
- He’s a coffee addict who drinks five cups before noon.
- Social media companies are accused of designing apps to addict users deliberately.
- **考频**: 38
## adhere

- **音标**: әd'hiә
- **词性**: vi./vt.
- **释义**: ==vi. 依附, 粘着, 坚持, 追随, 拥护\nvt. 使粘附==
- **英文释义**: v. be compatible or in accordance with\nv. follow through or carry out a plan without deviation\nv. be a devoted follower or supporter\nv. stick to firmly
- **同义词**: stick, cling
- **反义词**: detach
- **词根词缀**: ad(朝向)+here(粘)→粘向→粘附
- **搭配**: adhere to a principle, adhere to regulations, adhere to a schedule
- **例句**:
- The label adheres firmly to the bottle surface.
- All members must adhere to the organization's code of conduct.
- **考频**: 12

- **反义词**: despise

- **例句**:
