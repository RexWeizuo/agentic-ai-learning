# am- 形容词易混

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: am- 开头的形容词/名词，ambiguous（模棱两可），ambitious（有雄心的），amiable（和蔼的）。

**4 词**

## ambiguous

- **音标**: /æmˈbɪɡjuəs/
- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: ==模棱两可的，含糊不清的，有歧义的==
- **英文释义**: having more than one possible meaning; not clear
- **同义词**: vague, unclear, equivocal
- **反义词**: clear, definite, explicit
- **词根词缀**: ambi(两边)+gu(驱动)+ous→两边推→模棱两可
- **搭配**: ambiguous statement, ambiguous answer, highly ambiguous
- **例句**:
- The statement was deliberately ambiguous.
- An ambiguous answer is not helpful.
- **考频**: 33
## ambitious

- **音标**: /æmˈbɪʃəs/
- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: ==有抱负的，雄心勃勃的；有野心的==
- **英文释义**: having a strong desire to be successful or powerful
- **同义词**: aspiring, determined, driven, motivated
- **反义词**: lazy, unambitious, indifferent
- **词根词缀**: ambit(周围)+ious→到处走→有野心的
- **搭配**: ambitious plan, highly ambitious, ambitious project
- **例句**:
- She is an ambitious young lawyer.
- The company has ambitious expansion plans.
- **考频**: 47
## amiable

- **音标**: 'eimjәbl
- **释义**: ==a. 亲切的, 和蔼可亲的\n[法] 亲和的, 和蔼可亲的==
- **英文释义**: having a friendly and pleasant manner
- **词根词缀**: am(love)+iable(able)→lovable→和蔼可亲的
- **例句**:
- Her amiable disposition made her popular among both colleagues and students.
- Despite the tense negotiations, the ambassador maintained an amiable tone throughout the meeting.
- **考频**: 1
## amateur

- **音标**: 'æmәtә
- **释义**: ==n. 业余爱好者, 外行, 爱好者\n[电] 业余家==
- **英文释义**: a person who engages in an activity for pleasure rather than as a profession; a non-expert
- **同义词**: beginner, novice
- **反义词**: professional
- **词根词缀**: amat(爱)+eur→因爱好而做→业余的
- **例句**:
- As an amateur photographer, she lacked formal training but had a remarkable eye for composition.
- The debate was dominated by professionals, leaving little room for meaningful contributions from amateurs.
- **考频**: 53
