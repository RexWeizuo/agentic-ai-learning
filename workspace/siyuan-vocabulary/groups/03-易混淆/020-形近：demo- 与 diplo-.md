# 形近：demo- 与 diplo-

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: 前缀形近。demo- 意为人民(populace)，diplo- 意为双重/折叠(double)，注意词义区分。

**5 词**

---

## diploma

- **音标**: di'plәumә
- **释义**: n. 文凭, 毕业证书\n[医] 文凭
- **英文释义**: An official document awarded to a student after completing a course of study or passing an examination.
- **词根词缀**: di(两个)+ploma(折叠)→折叠的纸→文凭
- **例句**:
- She proudly displayed her university diploma on the wall.
- The job requires a diploma in engineering or a related field.
- **考频**: 14
## diplomatic

- **音标**: .diplә'mætik
- **释义**: a. 外交的, 老练的\n[法] 外交的, 外交上的, 文献上的
- **英文释义**: relating to diplomacy or skilled in handling sensitive situations tactfully
- **词根词缀**: di(两个)+ploma(折叠)+tic(形容词)→折叠的→外交的
- **例句**:
- The ambassador adopted a diplomatic approach to resolve the border dispute without escalating tensions.
- Her diplomatic response to the controversial question earned praise from both sides of the debate.
- **考频**: 12
## democracy

- **音标**: di'mɒkrәsi
- **释义**: n. 民主政治, 民主主义\n[法] 民主, 民主政治, 民主政体
- **英文释义**: A system of government in which power is vested in the people, who rule either directly or through elected representatives.
- **词根词缀**: demo(人民)+cracy(统治)→人民统治→民主
- **例句**:
- The United States is often cited as a representative democracy with a constitutional framework.
- Sustaining democracy requires not only free elections but also an independent judiciary and a vigilant civil society.
- **考频**: 36
## democratic

- **音标**: .demә'krætik
- **释义**: a. 民主的\n[法] 民主的, 民主政体的, 平民的
- **英文释义**: relating to or supporting democracy, a system of government in which power is held by the people, typically through elected representatives
- **词根词缀**: demo(人民)+crat(统治者)+ic(形容词)→民主的
- **例句**:
- The country has made significant progress toward establishing a democratic political system.
- A democratic society values freedom of speech, equal rights, and fair elections.
- **考频**: 52
## demographic

- **音标**: .demә'græfik
- **释义**: a. 人口统计的\n[法] 人口统计的, 人口学的
- **英文释义**: relating to the statistical characteristics of human populations, such as age, race, income, and education
- **词根词缀**: demo(人民)+graph(写)+ic(形容词)→写人民的→人口统计的
- **例句**:
- The marketing team analyzed demographic data to tailor their campaign to urban millennials.
- Rising life expectancy has significantly altered the country's demographic profile, increasing the proportion of elderly citizens.
- **考频**: 22
