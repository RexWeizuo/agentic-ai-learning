# 易混淆：com- 委托/委员会/妥协

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: commit(承诺/犯罪), committee(委员会), commission(佣金/委任), compromise(妥协)，注意拼写和词义区分。

**4 词**

---

## commit

- **音标**: kә'mit
- **释义**: vt. 委托(托付), 犯罪, 指派...作战, 使承担义务\n[法] 犯, 做, 把...交托给
- **英文释义**: to do something illegal or harmful; to pledge oneself to a course of action or responsibility
- **词根词缀**: com(加强)+mit(送)→送交→委托
- **例句**:
- The government has committed significant resources to renewable energy development.
- He was arrested for committing fraud against the financial institution.
- **考频**: 110
## committee

- **音标**: kә'miti
- **释义**: n. 委员会\n[经] 委员会
- **英文释义**: a group of people appointed or elected to perform a specific function or make decisions on behalf of a larger organization
- **词根词缀**: com(加强)+mit(送)+ee(人)→被委托的人→委员会
- **例句**:
- The committee reviewed the proposal and unanimously approved the budget allocation.
- She was appointed to the ethics committee to help develop new guidelines for clinical research.
- **考频**: 67
## commission

- **音标**: kә'miʃәn
- **释义**: n. 委任状, 任官令, 所委职责, 佣金, 犯, 委托, 所托之事\nvt. 委任, 委托制作, 使服役
- **英文释义**: A formal authorization to perform a specific task or duty; a fee paid for services rendered, especially in sales; or the act of officially appointing someone or ordering the creation of something.
- **词根词缀**: com(加强)+miss(送)+ion(名词)→送交→委托
- **例句**:
- The government issued a commission to the expert panel to investigate the environmental impact.
- She earned a 10% commission on each software license sold, which significantly boosted her annual income.
- **考频**: 84
## compromise

- **音标**: 'kɒmprәmaiz
- **释义**: n. 妥协, 折中, 折中方案, 和解\nvi. 妥协处理\nvt. 危害
- **英文释义**: A settlement of differences in which each side makes concessions; to settle a dispute by mutual concessions; to endanger the security or integrity of something.
- **词根词缀**: com(一起)+promise(承诺)→共同承诺→妥协
- **例句**:
- Finding a compromise between economic growth and environmental protection remains a key challenge for policymakers.
- The government refused to compromise on national security, rejecting any proposal that might weaken border controls.
- **考频**: 45
