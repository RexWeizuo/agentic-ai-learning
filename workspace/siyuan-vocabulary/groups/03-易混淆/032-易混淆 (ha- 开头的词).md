# 易混淆 (ha- 开头的词)

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: ha- 开头的高频词。habitat 栖息地，hamper 阻碍，harassment 骚扰，harness 马具/利用，hazard 危险。

**10 词**

---

## habitat

- **音标**: 'hæbitæt
- **释义**: n. 栖息地, 居留地, 自生地, 聚集处\n[医] 习生地, 产地, 生境, 栖所
- **英文释义**: The natural environment where a particular species lives and grows.
- **词根词缀**: habit(居住)+at(场所)→栖息地
- **例句**:
- Deforestation threatens the habitat of countless endangered species.
- Conservation efforts aim to restore the native habitat for migratory birds.
- **考频**: 34
## hail

- **音标**: heil
- **释义**: n. 冰雹, 致敬, 欢呼, 招呼\nvt. 向...欢呼, 致敬, 招呼, 使象下雹样落下\nvi. 招呼, 下雹\ninterj. 万岁, 欢迎
- **英文释义**: n. frozen raindrops falling from clouds; a greeting or expression of approval; v. to greet enthusiastically, to originate from; interj. an exclamation of welcome or acclaim
- **词根词缀**: hail(冰雹)→冰雹
- **例句**:
- The storm brought heavy hail that damaged the crops and shattered car windows.
- She was hailed as a visionary leader after her groundbreaking policy reform.
- **考频**: 11

- **释义**: n. 停止, 立定, 休息\nvt. 使停止, 使立定\nvi. 立定, 停止, 蹒跚, 踌躇, 有缺点\n[计] 停止
- **英文释义**: to stop moving or proceeding; a temporary stop or pause
- **词根词缀**: halt(停止)→停止
- **例句**:
- The train came to a sudden halt just before the station.
- The government imposed new regulations to halt the rapid spread of misinformation online.
- **考频**: 26
## hamper

## hang

## harassment

- **音标**: 'hærәsmәnt
- **释义**: n. 困扰, 烦扰, 烦恼\n[法] 折磨, 骚扰, 侵扰
- **英文释义**: unwanted and repeated behavior that causes distress or anxiety, especially in a workplace or educational setting
- **词根词缀**: harass(骚扰)+ment(名词)→骚扰

## harmony

- **音标**: 'hɑ:mәni
- **释义**: n. 协调, 和睦, 调和\n[电] 和声学
- **英文释义**: the state of being in agreement or peaceful relations; also, the combination of musical notes that sound pleasant together
- **词根词缀**: harmon(和谐)+y(名词)→和谐
- **例句**:

## hazard

- **音标**: 'hæzәd
- **释义**: n. 冒险, 危险, 机会\nvt. 冒...的危险, 赌运气, 使冒危险\n[计] 相关危险; 冒险
