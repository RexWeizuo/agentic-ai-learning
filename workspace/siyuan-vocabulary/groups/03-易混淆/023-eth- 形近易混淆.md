# eth- 形近易混淆

> **类型**: 易混淆

> **学习提示**: 拼写相近，注意审美、伦理、种族与永恒的区别

**4 词**

---

## esthetic

- **音标**: es'θetik
- **释义**: a. 审美的\n[医] 感觉的
- **英文释义**: relating to aesthetics or the appreciation of beauty; in medicine, relating to sensation or sensory perception
- **词根词缀**: aisth(感觉)+etic(形容词)→感觉的→美学的
- **例句**:
- The architect's design emphasized esthetic harmony rather than mere functional efficiency.
- In neurology, an esthetic deficit may manifest as impaired perception of touch, temperature, or pain.
- **考频**: 1
## ethic

- **音标**: 'eθik
- **释义**: n. 道德规范, 伦理
- **英文释义**: a set of moral principles or values that guide behavior, especially in a particular context or profession
- **词根词缀**: eth(道德)+ic(名词)→道德
- **例句**:
- The medical profession is bound by a strict ethic of patient confidentiality.
- Her decision to refuse the bribe reflected a strong personal ethic of integrity.
- **考频**: 48
## ethnic

- **音标**: 'eθnik
- **释义**: a. 人种的, 种族的\n[医] 人种的
- **英文释义**: relating to a group of people who share a common cultural, national, or racial background
- **词根词缀**: ethn(种族)+ic(形容词)→种族的
- **例句**:
- The museum features exhibitions on ethnic traditions from across Southeast Asia.
- Public health policies must account for ethnic disparities in disease prevalence and treatment access.
- **考频**: 53
## eternal

- **音标**: i'tә:nl
- **释义**: a. 永恒的, 永远的, 不灭的
- **英文释义**: lasting forever; never ending or changing
- **词根词缀**: aetern(永恒)+al(形容词)→永恒的
- **例句**:
- The concept of eternal truth is central to many philosophical traditions.
- She sought eternal peace through meditation and self-reflection.
- **考频**: 9
