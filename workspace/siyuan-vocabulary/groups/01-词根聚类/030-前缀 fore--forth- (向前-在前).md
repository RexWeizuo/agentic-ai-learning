# 前缀 fore-/forth- (向前/在前)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: fore- 表示“前面、预先”，forth 表示“向前、向外”，注意 foresee 和 forecast 的近义关系。

**8 词**

---

## fore

- **音标**: fɒ:
- **释义**: a. 在前的, 以前的\nadv. 在前面, 在船头\nprep. 在...前
- **英文释义**: located or occurring at the front; before in time or position
- **词根词缀**: fore(前)→前部
- **例句**:
- The fore part of the ship was damaged in the storm.
- She had a foreknowledge of the economic downturn, which helped her avoid major losses.
- **考频**: 7
## forecast

- **音标**: 'fɒ:kɑ:st
- **释义**: n. 预想, 预测, 预报\nvt. 预想, 预测, 预报\n[计] 趋势预测
- **英文释义**: A prediction or estimate of future events, especially weather or economic trends.
- **词根词缀**: fore(前)+cast(投掷)→预先投掷→预测
- **例句**:
- The meteorologist issued a detailed weather forecast for the coming week.
- Economists revised their GDP forecast downward in light of rising inflation and slowing consumer demand.
- **考频**: 38
## forehead

- **音标**: 'fɒ:rid
- **释义**: n. 额, 前额, 前部\n[医] 额
- **英文释义**: The part of the face above the eyes and below the hairline.
- **词根词缀**: fore(前)+head(头)→前额
- **例句**:
- She wiped the sweat from her forehead after the intense debate.
- The patient presented with a contusion on the forehead following the fall.
- **考频**: 1
## foremost

- **音标**: 'fәumәust
- **释义**: a. 最初的, 最重要的\nadv. 在最前
- **英文释义**: most important or prominent; at the front in position or rank
- **词根词缀**: fore(前)+most(最)→最前面的
- **例句**:
- She is one of the foremost scholars in the field of cognitive linguistics.
- Foremost among the challenges facing policymakers is climate change mitigation.
- **考频**: 9
## foresee

- **音标**: fɒ:'si:
- **释义**: vt. 预见, 预知
- **英文释义**: to see or know something before it happens
- **词根词缀**: fore(前)+see(看)→预见
- **例句**:
- Economists failed to foresee the severity of the financial crisis.
- It is impossible to foresee all the consequences of such a radical policy change.
- **考频**: 26
## forth

- **音标**: fɒ:θ
- **释义**: adv. 往前, 以后, 向外
- **英文释义**: forward in space or time; out from a place or source
- **词根词缀**: forth(向前)→向前
- **例句**:
- The discussion moved forth steadily, addressing each point with logical rigor.
- She stepped forth confidently to present her research at the international conference.
- **考频**: 41
## forthcoming

- **音标**: 'fɒ:θ'kʌmɑŋ
- **释义**: a. 即将来临的\nn. 来临
- **英文释义**: coming soon; about to happen or appear
- **词根词缀**: forth(向前)+coming(来)→即将到来的
- **例句**:
- The forthcoming conference on climate change will gather experts from over 50 countries.
- Her forthcoming book on cognitive linguistics is highly anticipated by scholars in the field.
- **考频**: 6
## forward

- **音标**: 'fɒ:wәd
- **释义**: a. 向前的, 早的, 迅速的, 在前的, 进步的\nvt. 促进...的生长, 转寄, 运送\nadv. 向前地\n[计] 前推, 转信
- **英文释义**: moving or directed toward a point ahead; promoting progress; sent on to another destination
- **词根词缀**: for(前)+ward(方向)→向前
- **例句**:
- The company has made forward strides in artificial intelligence research.
- Please forward the email to all department heads by noon.
- **考频**: 132
