# 词根 loc (地方)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: 源自拉丁语 locus (地方、位置)

**4 词**

---

## local

- **音标**: 'lәukәl
- **释义**: a. 地方性的, 当地的, 局部的, 乡土的, 本地的\nn. 当地居民, 本地新闻, 局部\n[计] 本地的; 局部
- **英文释义**: relating to a particular area or community, not national or international
- **词根词缀**: loc(地方)+al(形容词)→当地的
- **例句**:
- The local government has introduced new regulations to improve public transportation.
- She volunteered with a local charity that supports underprivileged children in the neighborhood.
- **考频**: 419
## locality

- **音标**: lәu'kæliti
- **释义**: n. 地方, 所在, 位置\n[法] 位置, 场所, 地区
- **英文释义**: a particular place or area, especially one that is small or specific
- **词根词缀**: loc(地方)+ality(名词)→地点
- **例句**:
- The study focused on the health outcomes of residents in a rural locality.
- Environmental regulations may vary depending on the locality in which a business operates.
- **考频**: 6
## locate

- **音标**: 'lәukeit
- **释义**: vt. 找出, 设于, 位于\nvi. 定居
- **英文释义**: to find the exact position or place of something; to establish or be situated in a particular location
- **词根词缀**: loc(地方)+ate(动词)→定位
- **例句**:
- The researchers managed to locate the source of the signal after days of analysis.
- Many tech companies choose to locate their headquarters in cities with strong talent pools and infrastructure.
- **考频**: 76
## location

- **音标**: lәu'keiʃәn
- **释义**: n. 位置, 场所, 特定区域\n[计] 位置
- **英文释义**: the place where someone or something is situated
- **词根词缀**: loc(地方)+ation(名词)→位置
- **例句**:
- The exact location of the ancient ruins remains unknown to archaeologists.
- Choosing an appropriate location for the data center is critical for network latency and security.
- **考频**: 73
