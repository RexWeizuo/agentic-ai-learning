# 词根/近义：数量与多数

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: 包含 multi-/numer- 词根及表示“多、大部分”的近义词。

## multiple

- **音标**: 'mʌltipl
- **释义**: n. 倍数, 并联\na. 多样的, 许多的, 多功能的
- **英文释义**: n. a number that can be divided by another number without a remainder; a system or device with several parallel components. adj. consisting of, involving, or having many parts, elements, or instances.
- **词根词缀**: multi(多)+ple(折叠)→多折叠→多样的
- **例句**:
- The least common multiple (LCM) of 4 and 6 is 12.
- Modern smartphones are designed with multiple functions, integrating communication, navigation, and payment capabilities in a single device.
- **考频**: 174
## multiply

- **音标**: 'mʌltiplai
- **释义**: v. 繁殖, 乘, 增加\n[计] 乘
- **英文释义**: to increase in number or amount; to perform the mathematical operation of multiplication
- **词根词缀**: multi(多)+ply(折叠)→多折叠→乘
- **例句**:
- The bacterial population can multiply rapidly under favorable conditions.
- To find the area of a rectangle, multiply its length by its width.
- **考频**: 19
## multitude

- **音标**: 'mʌltitju:d
- **释义**: n. 多数, 群众
- **英文释义**: a large number of people or things
- **词根词缀**: multi(多)+tude(名词)→多→大量
- **例句**:
- The multitude of applicants made it difficult to select the most qualified candidates.
- Despite the multitude of opinions expressed in the debate, a consensus gradually emerged.
