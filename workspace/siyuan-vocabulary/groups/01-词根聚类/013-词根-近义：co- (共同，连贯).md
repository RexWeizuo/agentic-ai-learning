# 词根/近义：co- (共同，连贯)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: co- 前缀表示“共同”：coherent(连贯的), cohesive(有凝聚力的), coincide(同时发生/一致), collaborate(合作)。

**4 词**

---

## coherent

- **音标**: /kəʊˈhɪərənt/
- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 连贯的，一致的；条理清楚的
- **英文释义**: (of an argument or theory) logical and consistent; able to speak clearly and logically
- **同义词**: logical, consistent, rational, clear
- **反义词**: incoherent, illogical, confused
- **词根词缀**: co(共同)+her(粘)+ent→粘在一起的→连贯的
- **搭配**: coherent argument, coherent policy, coherent strategy
- **例句**:
- She gave a coherent explanation.
- The essay lacks a coherent structure.
- **考频**: 10
## cohesive

- **音标**: kәu'hi:siv
- **释义**: a. 黏合在一起的\n[医] 内聚的
- **英文释义**: forming a united whole; sticking together firmly
- **词根词缀**: co(共同)+hes(粘)+ive(形容词)→粘在一起的→有凝聚力的
- **例句**:
- The team demonstrated a cohesive strategy that led to their decisive victory in the debate competition.
- A cohesive essay presents clear logical connections between paragraphs and maintains consistent tone and focus throughout.
- **考频**: 2
## coincide

- **音标**: .kәuin'said
- **释义**: vi. 一致, 符合\n[化] 重合
- **英文释义**: to occur at the same time or in the same place; to agree exactly
- **词根词缀**: co(共同)+in(进入)+cid(落下)+e→共同落下→巧合
- **例句**:
- The conference coincides with the annual science fair, making it difficult for many researchers to attend both.
- Her political views closely coincide with those of the new policy initiative.
- **考频**: 17
## collaborate

- **音标**: kә'læbәreit
- **释义**: vi. 合作, 勾结\n[法] 合作, 合著, 通敌
- **英文释义**: to work together with others, especially on a project or activity
- **词根词缀**: col(共同)+labor(劳动)+ate(动词)→共同劳动→合作
- **例句**:
- Researchers from five universities collaborated to develop the new AI model.
- The two companies collaborated on a joint venture to expand into emerging markets.
- **考频**: 19
