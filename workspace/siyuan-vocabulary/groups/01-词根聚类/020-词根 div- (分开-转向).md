# 词根 div- (分开/转向)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: 源自拉丁语 dividere (分开) 或 divertere (转向)，divine 为同源引申(神圣的分离)。

**6 词**

---

## divide

- **音标**: di'vaid
- **释义**: vi. 分开, 分配, 分裂\nvt. 分, 分开, 分裂, 除\nn. 分配, 分水岭\n[计] 除
- **英文释义**: to separate into parts or groups; to perform the mathematical operation of division
- **词根词缀**: di(分开)+vid(分)+e→分开→划分
- **例句**:
- The river divides the city into two distinct cultural zones.
- When dividing a polynomial by a binomial, always check for remainders.
- **考频**: 136
## division

- **音标**: di'viʒәn
- **释义**: n. 分, 分开, 除法, 部门(如部、处、系等), 师\n[计] 部分
- **英文释义**: the act or process of separating something into parts, a mathematical operation of sharing or grouping, or an organizational unit within a larger structure
- **词根词缀**: di(分开)+vis(分)+ion(名词)→分开→划分
- **例句**:
- The division of labor among team members significantly improved project efficiency.
- In mathematics, division is the inverse operation of multiplication.
- **考频**: 40
## diverse

- **音标**: dai'vә:s
- **释义**: a. 不同的, 变化多的
- **英文释义**: showing a great deal of variety; very different from each other
- **词根词缀**: di(分开)+verse(转)→转开→不同的
- **例句**:
- The university prides itself on its diverse student body, representing over 80 countries.
- A diverse range of perspectives is essential for innovative problem-solving in interdisciplinary research.
- **考频**: 50
## divert

- **音标**: dai'vә:t
- **释义**: vt. 转移, 使欢娱\nvi. 转移
- **英文释义**: to cause something to change direction or use; to entertain or amuse
- **词根词缀**: di(分开)+vert(转)→转开→转移
- **例句**:
- The government diverted funds from education to military spending.
- She tried to divert the child's attention from the pain by telling a funny story.
- **考频**: 20
## diversion

- **音标**: dai'vә:ʒәn
- **释义**: n. 转移\n[医] 转向
- **英文释义**: A temporary distraction or deviation from a main activity, topic, or path; in medicine, the redirection of blood flow or physiological function.
- **词根词缀**: di(分开)+vers(转)+ion(名词)→转开→转移
- **例句**:
- The magician's sleight of hand served as a clever diversion while his assistant concealed the prop.
- In patients with portosystemic shunting, hepatic blood flow undergoes pathological diversion, leading to complications such as hepatic encephalopathy.
- **考频**: 6
## divine

- **音标**: di'vain
- **释义**: a. 神的, 神圣的, 非凡的\nn. 神学家
- **英文释义**: of or relating to a god or deity; sacred; exceptionally good or beautiful
- **词根词缀**: div(神)+ine(形容词)→神的→神圣的
- **例句**:
- The cathedral's stained-glass windows cast a divine light over the altar.
- Her performance was so flawless and moving that critics called it nothing short of divine.
- **考频**: 9
