# 词根 medi (中间/媒介)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: medi 相关词，注意 medieval (中世纪) 和 meditation (冥想) 的词源差异

**4 词**

---

## mediate

- **音标**: 'mi:dieit
- **释义**: a. 居间的, 间接的\nvt. 斡旋, 调停\nvi. 处于中间, 调解
- **英文释义**: to intervene between conflicting parties to help them reach an agreement; or to be situated between two things
- **词根词缀**: medi(中间)+ate(动词)→调解
- **例句**:
- The United Nations sent envoys to mediate the ceasefire negotiations between the warring factions.
- In cognitive psychology, attention is often described as a mediate process that filters sensory input before conscious perception.
- **考频**: 13
## medieval

- **音标**: .medi'i:vl
- **释义**: a. 中古的, 中世纪的
- **英文释义**: relating to the Middle Ages (roughly 5th to late 15th century) in European history
- **词根词缀**: medi(中间)+ev(时代)+al(形容词)→中世纪的
- **例句**:
- The cathedral's architecture is a stunning example of medieval Gothic style.
- Medieval manuscripts often feature elaborate illuminations and Latin text.
- **考频**: 12
## meditation

- **音标**: .medi'teiʃәn
- **释义**: n. 沉思, 冥想
- **英文释义**: the practice of thinking deeply in silence, especially for spiritual purposes or to achieve calm and relaxation
- **词根词缀**: medit(思考)+ation(名词)→冥想
- **例句**:
- Regular meditation has been shown to reduce stress and improve focus among graduate students.
- Her daily meditation routine helps her maintain mental clarity during intensive exam preparation.
- **考频**: 10
## medium

- **音标**: 'mi:diәm
- **释义**: n. 媒体, 方法, 媒介\na. 半生熟的, 中间的\n[计] 媒体, 中
- **英文释义**: A channel or system for transmitting information, ideas, or artistic expression; also, something that serves as an intermediary or means of achieving an effect.
- **词根词缀**: medi(中间)+um(名词)→媒介
- **例句**:
- Social media has become a dominant medium for political campaigning.
- Oil paint is a traditional medium for fine art, valued for its richness and versatility.
- **考频**: 401
