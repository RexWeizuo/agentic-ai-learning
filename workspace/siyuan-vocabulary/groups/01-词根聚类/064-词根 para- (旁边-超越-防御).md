# 词根 para- (旁边/超越/防御)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: para- 表示“旁边、超越、防御”。parachute(降落伞/防御), paradox(悖论/超越常理), parallel(平行/旁边)。

**8 词**

---

## parachute

- **音标**: 'pærәʃu:t
- **释义**: n. 降落伞\nvi. 跳伞
- **英文释义**: A device consisting of a large fabric canopy designed to slow the descent of a person or object through the air.
- **词根词缀**: para(保护)+chute(降落)→保护降落→降落伞
- **例句**:
- The pilot deployed the parachute seconds before the plane crashed.
- She decided to parachute into the remote region to conduct field research.
- **考频**: 3
## parade

- **音标**: pә'reid
- **释义**: n. 游行, 炫耀, 阅兵\nv. 游行, 炫耀, (使)列队行进
- **英文释义**: A public procession, often ceremonial or celebratory; or to display something ostentatiously.
- **词根词缀**: par(准备)+ade(行为)→准备展示→游行
- **例句**:
- The city held a grand parade to commemorate its 200th anniversary.
- She paraded her new promotion in front of colleagues, drawing both admiration and subtle resentment.
- **考频**: 13
## paradigm

- **音标**: 'pærәdaim
- **释义**: n. 范例, 式样, 词形变化表, 纵聚合关系语言项
- **英文释义**: A typical example or pattern of something, especially one that serves as a model or framework for understanding or analysis.
- **词根词缀**: para(旁边)+digm(显示)→在旁边显示→范例
- **例句**:
- The rise of artificial intelligence represents a paradigm shift in how we approach problem-solving.
- Her research challenges the dominant paradigm in cognitive psychology by emphasizing embodied cognition.
- **考频**: 3
## paradise

- **音标**: 'pærәdais
- **释义**: n. 天堂, 乐园, 伊甸园
- **英文释义**: A place or state of exceptional happiness, beauty, or peace; often used to describe an ideal or perfect environment.
- **词根词缀**: para(围绕)+dise(墙)→围墙内的花园→天堂
- **例句**:
- The secluded island resort was a paradise for those seeking tranquility and natural beauty.
- For many environmental scientists, the untouched rainforest represents one of the last remaining paradises on Earth.
- **考频**: 18
## paradox

- **音标**: 'pærәdɒks
- **释义**: n. 似非而是的论点, 自相矛盾的话, 悖论, 怪人怪事\n[化] 佯谬
- **英文释义**: A statement or situation that seems absurd or self-contradictory but may express a deeper truth.
- **词根词缀**: para(相反)+dox(观点)→相反的观点→悖论
- **例句**:
- The paradox of modern life is that greater connectivity often leads to deeper isolation.
- Einstein's theory of relativity resolved the apparent paradox between the constancy of the speed of light and the principles of classical mechanics.
- **考频**: 19
## parallel

- **音标**: 'pærәlel
- **释义**: n. 平行, 对比, 相匹敌之物\na. 平行的, 相似的\nvt. 与...平行, 与...相似, 相比, 使平行\n[计] 并联; 并行
- **英文释义**: Extending in the same direction, equidistant at all points, and never meeting; also used to denote similarity, equivalence, or comparison between two things.
- **词根词缀**: para(旁边)+allel(彼此)→彼此旁边→平行的
- **例句**:
- The rise of digital literacy runs parallel to the expansion of internet access worldwide.
- Her career trajectory parallels that of her mentor—both began as researchers before transitioning into policy leadership.
- **考频**: 34
## parameter

- **音标**: pә'ræmitә
- **释义**: n. 参变数, 参变量, 参数, 参量\n[计] 参量; 参数
- **英文释义**: A measurable factor that defines a system or process and determines its behavior or output.
- **词根词缀**: para(旁边)+meter(测量)→在旁边测量→参数
- **例句**:
- The study controlled for several key parameters, including age, gender, and socioeconomic status.
- In programming, a function's behavior often depends on the values passed to it as parameters.
- **考频**: 4
## panorama

- **音标**: .pænә'rɑ:mә
- **释义**: n. 全景, 全貌, 概论, 范围
- **英文释义**: A wide and comprehensive view of a subject, situation, or area; often used to describe an overall perspective or survey.
- **词根词缀**: pan(全部)+orama(景色)→全景→全景画
- **例句**:
- The book offers a panoramic overview of European history from the Middle Ages to the present.
- Standing on the hilltop, she was struck by the breathtaking panorama of snow-capped mountains and winding rivers.
