# 词根：cent (百，中心)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: cent 词根表示“百”或“中心”，centigrade 是百分度（摄氏），century 是百年（世纪）。

**5 词**

---

## centigrade

- **音标**: 'sentigreid
- **释义**: a. 百分度的, 摄氏的\n[医] 百分度
- **英文释义**: Relating to or measured on the Celsius temperature scale, where water freezes at 0° and boils at 100° under standard atmospheric pressure.
- **词根词缀**: centi(百)+grade(等级)→百分度→摄氏的
- **例句**:
- The experiment required precise temperature control in centigrade units.
- Normal human body temperature is approximately 37 degrees centigrade.
- **考频**: 2
## centimetre

- **音标**: 'senti,mi:tә(r)
- **释义**: n. 厘米, 公分
- **英文释义**: A unit of length equal to one hundredth of a metre.
- **词根词缀**: centi(百)+metre(米)→百分之一米→厘米
- **例句**:
- The pencil is exactly fifteen centimetres long.
- Scientists measured the growth of the plant to the nearest centimetre over a three-week period.
- **考频**: 3
## central

- **音标**: 'sentrәl
- **释义**: a. 中央的, 重要的\n[医] 中央的, 中心的, 中枢的
- **英文释义**: located at or relating to the center; of great importance or significance
- **词根词缀**: centr(中心)+al(形容词)→中心的
- **例句**:
- The central nervous system includes the brain and spinal cord.
- Economic policy reform remains central to the government's agenda.
- **考频**: 133
## centre

- **音标**: 'sentә
- **释义**: n. 中心, 中心点, 中锋\na. 中央的, 位在正中的\nvt. 集中, 定中心\nvi. 居中
- **英文释义**: the middle point or part of something; a place or institution that serves as a focus for a particular activity
- **词根词缀**: centr(中心)→中心
- **例句**:
- The conference was held at the cultural centre of the city.
- Her research findings centre on the relationship between language acquisition and cognitive development.
- **考频**: 782
## century

- **音标**: 'sentʃuri
- **释义**: n. 世纪, 百年
- **英文释义**: A period of one hundred years.
- **词根词缀**: cent(百)+ury(名词)→一百年→世纪
- **例句**:
- The Industrial Revolution began in the late 18th century.
- China has undergone tremendous social changes over the past century.
- **考频**: 577
