# 词根 sens/sent (感觉/心智)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: 感觉与情感。sensation(轰动/感觉), sensible(明智的), sensitive(敏感的), sentiment(情绪/观点)。

**4 词**

---

## sensation

- **音标**: sen'seiʃәn
- **释义**: n. 感觉, 轰动\n[医] 感觉
- **英文释义**: a physical feeling or mental perception caused by external or internal stimuli; also, a person or thing that causes widespread excitement or interest
- **词根词缀**: sens(感觉)+ation(名词)→感觉
- **例句**:
- The sudden drop in temperature gave him a chilling sensation.
- Her debut novel became a literary sensation, topping bestseller lists for twelve weeks.
- **考频**: 7
## sensible

- **音标**: 'sensәbl
- **释义**: a. 有感觉的, 敏感的, 明智的\n[医] 可感觉的
- **英文释义**: having or showing good sense and sound judgment; capable of perceiving stimuli
- **词根词缀**: sens(感觉)+ible(能)→明智的
- **例句**:
- It would be sensible to review all the data before making a final decision.
- The patient reported that the affected area remained sensible to light touch after the surgery.
- **考频**: 52
## sensitive

- **音标**: 'sensitiv
- **释义**: a. 敏感的, 易感的, 灵敏的, 感光的\n[机] 敏感的, 灵敏的, 感度高的
- **英文释义**: easily affected or influenced by something; responsive to slight changes, stimuli, or emotions
- **词根词缀**: sens(感觉)+itive(形容词)→敏感的
- **例句**:
- Children are particularly sensitive to criticism from their parents.
- This high-resolution camera sensor is highly sensitive to low-light conditions.
- **考频**: 107
## sentiment

- **音标**: 'sentimәnt
- **释义**: n. 感情, 感伤, 情操, 情绪, 感想, 意见\n[医] 情感, 情操
- **英文释义**: A feeling, opinion, or attitude, especially one based on emotion rather than reason
- **词根词缀**: sent(感觉)+i+ment(名词)→感觉→情感
- **例句**:
- Public sentiment strongly favored the new environmental policy.
- Her speech was criticized for appealing to sentiment rather than presenting factual evidence.
- **考频**: 18
