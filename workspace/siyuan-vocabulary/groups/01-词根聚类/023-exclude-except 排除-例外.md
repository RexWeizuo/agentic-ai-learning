# exclude/except 排除/例外

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: 词根 clud/cept 表示关闭、排除、例外

**5 词**

---

## except

- **音标**: ik'sept
- **释义**: vt. 除, 除外\nvi. 反对\nprep. 除了...之外, 若不是, 除非\nconj. 只是
- **英文释义**: prep. not including; conj. if it were not for; vt. to exclude
- **词根词缀**: ex(出)+cept(拿)→拿出→除外
- **例句**:
- Everyone passed the exam except John, who missed the final due to illness.
- The policy applies to all departments except those already operating under special regulatory frameworks.
- **考频**: 239
## exception

- **音标**: ik'sepʃәn
- **释义**: n. 例外, 除外, 异议\n[计] 例外; 异常
- **英文释义**: a case or instance that does not follow a general rule or pattern
- **词根词缀**: ex(出)+cept(拿)+ion(名词)→拿出→例外
- **例句**:
- There are no exceptions to this safety regulation—everyone must wear protective gear.
- Her admission to the program was an exception to the usual requirement of three years of work experience.
- **考频**: 53
## exceptional

- **音标**: ik'sepʃәnәl
- **释义**: a. 例外的, 异常的, 特别的
- **英文释义**: unusually good or different from what is normal
- **词根词缀**: ex(出)+cept(拿)+ion(名词)+al(形容词)→超出一般拿出的→例外的
- **例句**:
- Her performance in the research project was exceptional, earning her a full scholarship to Oxford.
- The committee noted that the candidate's analytical skills were exceptional, far surpassing those of her peers.
- **考频**: 63
## exclude

- **音标**: iks'klu:d
- **释义**: vt. 除外, 排除, 排斥\n[医] 除外(诊断)
- **英文释义**: to deliberately leave someone or something out, especially from a group, activity, or process
- **词根词缀**: ex(出)+clud(关闭)+e→关在外面→排除
- **例句**:
- The committee decided to exclude applicants without relevant work experience.
- In differential diagnosis, clinicians must systematically exclude life-threatening conditions before confirming a benign diagnosis.
- **考频**: 46
## exclusive

- **音标**: ik'sklu:siv
- **释义**: a. 排外的, 独占的, 唯一的\n[计] 独占的
- **英文释义**: excluding others; limited to a particular person, group, or purpose
- **词根词缀**: ex(出)+clus(关闭)+ive(形容词)→关在外面的→排他的
- **例句**:
- The club has an exclusive membership policy that admits only senior executives.
- This software license grants the company exclusive rights to distribute the application in Asia.
- **考频**: 31
