# 词根 man/manu (手)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: 源自拉丁语 manus (手)，引申为操作、制造、命令

**6 词**

---

## manifest

- **音标**: 'mænifest
- **释义**: n. 载货单, 运货单, 旅客名单\na. 显然的, 明白的\nvi. 显示, 出现\nvt. 表明, 表现, 证明
- **英文释义**: adj. clearly apparent or easy to understand; n. a detailed list of cargo or passengers on a ship or aircraft; v. to show or demonstrate something clearly
- **词根词缀**: man(手)+fest(打击)→明显的
- **例句**:
- The company's financial difficulties began to manifest in declining stock prices and employee layoffs.
- The customs officer requested the ship's manifest to verify the cargo before allowing it to dock.
- **考频**: 23
## manipulate

- **音标**: mә'nipjuleit
- **释义**: vt. 操纵, 利用, 操作, 巧妙地处理, 假造
- **英文释义**: to control or influence someone or something, often in a clever or unfair way
- **词根词缀**: man(手)+ipul(操作)+ate(动词)→操纵
- **例句**:
- The media can manipulate public opinion through selective reporting.
- She was accused of manipulating financial data to inflate the company's profits.
- **考频**: 29
## manoeuvre

- **音标**: mә'nu:vә
- **释义**: n. 调遣, 演习, 策略\nvi. 调动, 演习, 用策略\nvt. 调动, 操纵
- **英文释义**: A planned movement or action, especially to gain an advantage or achieve a specific goal; often used in military, political, or strategic contexts.
- **词根词缀**: man(手)+oeuvre(工作)→操纵
- **例句**:
- The general ordered a complex tactical manoeuvre to outflank the enemy forces.
- She employed a series of diplomatic manoeuvres to secure support for the controversial policy.
- **考频**: 1
## manual

- **音标**: 'mænjuәl
- **释义**: n. 手册, 指南\na. 手的, 手动的, 手工的, 体力的\n[计] 人工的, 手动的
- **英文释义**: A book or document containing instructions or information about how to use or operate something; done or operated by hand rather than automatically or electronically.
- **词根词缀**: man(手)+ual(形容词)→手工的
- **例句**:
- Before using the new software, please read the user manual carefully.
- The machine can be switched to manual mode when automatic control fails.
- **考频**: 25
## manufacture

- **音标**: .mænju'fæktʃә
- **释义**: n. 产品, 制造\nvt. 制造, 假造\nvi. 制造
- **英文释义**: to make goods, especially in large quantities using machines; or to invent something false deliberately
- **词根词缀**: manu(手)+fact(做)+ure(名词)→制造
- **例句**:
- The company manufactures high-precision components for aerospace applications.
- The prosecution argued that the key witness had manufactured evidence to support the accusation.
- **考频**: 131
## mandate

- **音标**: 'mændeit
- **释义**: n. 命令, 指令, 要求\nvt. 委任统治
- **英文释义**: an official order or command; a formal authorization to act, often given by voters or a higher authority
- **词根词缀**: mand(命令)+ate(动词)→命令
- **例句**:
- The new environmental policy was implemented under a clear mandate from the electorate.
- The UN Security Council mandated the peacekeeping force to protect civilians in the conflict zone.
- **考频**: 9
