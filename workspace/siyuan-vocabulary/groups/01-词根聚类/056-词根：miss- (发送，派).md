# 词根：miss- (发送，派)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: 共享词根 miss-/mit-，表示“发送、派、释放”。

**4 词**

---

## missile

- **音标**: 'misail
- **释义**: n. 发射物, 导弹, 飞弹, 火箭\na. 可发射的
- **英文释义**: A guided weapon or projectile designed to be launched and travel through the air to hit a target.
- **词根词缀**: miss(发送)+ile(名词)→导弹
- **例句**:
- The defense system successfully intercepted the incoming ballistic missile.
- Scientists are developing hypersonic missiles capable of evading traditional radar detection.
- **考频**: 2
## mission

- **音标**: 'miʃәn
- **释义**: n. 任务, 代表团, 使命, 传教团\nvt. 派遣, 向...传教
- **英文释义**: an important task or duty, especially one assigned by an organization or government; a group of people sent to carry out such a task
- **词根词缀**: miss(发送)+ion(名词)→任务
- **例句**:
- The UN peacekeeping mission was deployed to stabilize the conflict zone.
- Her mission is to improve literacy rates in rural communities.
- **考频**: 77
## missionary

- **音标**: 'miʃәnәri
- **释义**: n. 传教士, 负有任务者, 工作人员\na. 传教的, 传教士的
- **英文释义**: A person sent by a religious organization to spread its faith, often in a foreign country; also used more broadly for someone entrusted with a special purpose or mission.
- **词根词缀**: mission(任务)+ary(名词)→传教士
- **例句**:
- The missionary spent ten years working in rural communities in sub-Saharan Africa, providing both spiritual guidance and medical care.
- Her dedication to environmental education earned her the reputation of a missionary for sustainability in academic circles.
## messenger

- **音标**: 'mesindʒә
- **释义**: n. 报信者, 使者\n[经] 使者, 送信者, 通信员
- **英文释义**: a person who carries messages or information from one person or place to another
- **词根词缀**: mess(消息)+enger(人)→送信人
- **例句**:
- In medieval times, royal messengers rode swiftly across the kingdom to deliver urgent decrees.
- The company appointed a dedicated messenger to ensure confidential documents were transported securely between offices.
- **考频**: 10
