> **类型**: 词根

## entity

- **音标**: 'entiti
- **释义**: n. 实体, 实存物, 存在\n[计] 实体
- **英文释义**: Something that exists as a distinct and independent unit, whether physical, conceptual, or digital.
- **词根词缀**: ent(存在)+ity(名词)→存在物→实体
- **例句**:
- The company is treated as a legal entity separate from its owners.
- In database design, each 'customer' is modeled as an entity with attributes like name and address.
- **考频**: 25
## exist

- **音标**: ig'zist
- **释义**: vi. 存在, 生存, 发生
- **英文释义**: to have reality or being; to be present in a particular place or situation
- **词根词缀**: ex(出)+ist(站)→站出来→存在
- **例句**:
- Life as we know it cannot exist without water.
- The threat of inflation still exists, despite recent policy adjustments.
- **考频**: 263
## existence

- **音标**: ig'zistәns
- **释义**: n. 存在, 生存\n[法] 存在, 存在状态, 实体
- **英文释义**: the fact or state of living or having objective reality
- **词根词缀**: ex(出)+ist(站)+ence(名词)→站出来→存在
- **例句**:
- The philosopher questioned the very existence of free will.
- Scientific evidence confirms the existence of dark matter in the universe.
- **考频**: 92
## exit

- **音标**: 'eksit
- **释义**: n. 出口, 退场, 离去, 去世\nvi. 退出, 脱离, 去世\n[计] 退出; DOS内部命令:本命令用于退出当前的命令处理器(COMMAND.COM)\n恢复前一个命令处理器
- **英文释义**: A way out of a place; the act of leaving or departing; in computing, a command to terminate a program or session.
- **词根词缀**: ex(出)+it(走)→走出去→出口
- **例句**:
- Please use the nearest exit in case of fire.
- The CEO announced her exit from the company after ten years of leadership.
- **考频**: 18
