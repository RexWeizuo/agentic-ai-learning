# 词根 graph-/gram- (写/画/重量)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: gram/graph 表示“写、画、记录”。graph 是图表，graphic 是生动的/图形的，grammar 是语法规则。

**4 词**

---

## gram

- **音标**: græm
- **释义**: n. 克, 绿豆, 鹰嘴豆\n[医] 克
- **英文释义**: A metric unit of mass equal to one thousandth of a kilogram; also used to refer to certain legumes, especially chickpeas or mung beans.
- **词根词缀**: gram→克
- **例句**:
- The prescription calls for 500 mg of the drug, which is equivalent to 0.5 gram.
- In Indian cuisine, gram flour, made from ground chickpeas, is commonly used in savory pancakes and fritters.
- **考频**: 16
## grammar

- **音标**: 'græmә
- **释义**: n. 语法学, 入门书\n[计] 语法检查
- **英文释义**: The system of rules governing the structure and usage of a language, especially concerning syntax, morphology, and word formation.
- **词根词缀**: gram(写)+ar(名词)→语法
- **例句**:
- A solid grasp of English grammar is essential for writing coherent academic essays.
- The programming language's compiler performs rigorous grammar checking before execution.
- **考频**: 100
## graph

- **音标**: græf
- **释义**: n. 曲线图, 图表, 图形\n[计] 图形
- **英文释义**: A diagram showing the relation between variable quantities, typically of two variables, represented as a line or curve on a set of axes.
- **词根词缀**: graph→图表
- **例句**:
- The graph illustrates a steady decline in unemployment over the past five years.
- According to the bar graph, consumer spending peaked in December before dropping sharply in January.
- **考频**: 12
## graphic

- **音标**: 'græfik
- **释义**: a. 生动的, 轮廓分明的, 绘画似的, 图解的\n[计] 图形的
- **英文释义**: vividly detailed and clear, especially in a way that may be disturbing; relating to visual representation or diagrams
- **词根词缀**: graph(写)+ic(形容词)→图表的
- **例句**:
- The report contained graphic descriptions of the accident scene, making it difficult for some readers to continue.
- Modern software allows users to manipulate graphic elements such as vectors and bitmaps with precision.
- **考频**: 10
