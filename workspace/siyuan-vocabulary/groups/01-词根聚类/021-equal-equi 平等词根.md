# equal/equi 平等词根

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: 词根 equ- 表示平等、相等

**5 词**

---

## equal

- **音标**: 'i:kwәl
- **释义**: n. 对手, 匹敌, 同辈\na. 相等的, 平等的, 胜任的, 合适的, 平静的, 不相上下的\nvt. 等于, 比得上\n[计] 等长度编码
- **英文释义**: having the same quantity, size, value, or status; matching in ability or quality
- **词根词缀**: equ(相等)+al(形容词)→相等的
- **例句**:
- She fought for equal rights for all citizens, regardless of gender or race.
- His performance in the final exam was equal to that of the top student in the class.
- **考频**: 138
## equality

- **音标**: i:'kwɒliti
- **释义**: n. 平等, 均匀, 相等\n[法] 同等, 平等, 相等
- **英文释义**: the state of being equal in status, rights, opportunities, or value
- **词根词缀**: equ(相等)+ality(名词)→相等
- **例句**:
- Gender equality is a fundamental principle enshrined in the United Nations Charter.
- Achieving racial equality requires both legal reform and sustained social commitment.
- **考频**: 69
## equation

- **音标**: i'kweiʃәn
- **释义**: n. 相等, 等式, 平衡\n[化] 方程式; 等式; 反应式; 公式
- **英文释义**: A mathematical statement that two expressions are equal, typically containing variables and constants.
- **词根词缀**: equ(相等)+ation(名词)→等式
- **例句**:
- Solving this quadratic equation requires applying the quadratic formula.
- In economics, supply and demand must be in equilibrium—an equation where quantity supplied equals quantity demanded.
- **考频**: 19
## equity

- **音标**: 'ekwiti
- **释义**: n. 公平, 公正\n[经] 权益, 产权
- **英文释义**: Fairness and impartiality in treatment or representation; also, the value of an asset after subtracting liabilities, especially in finance.
- **词根词缀**: equ(相等)+ity(名词)→公平
- **例句**:
- Promoting gender equity in leadership positions remains a key goal for corporate governance.
- The company's balance sheet showed a sharp decline in shareholder equity following the market downturn.
- **考频**: 6
## equivalent

- **音标**: i'kwivәlәnt
- **释义**: n. 同等物, 等价物, 相等物\na. 相等的, 相当的, 同意义的\n[计] 等价的
- **英文释义**: having the same value, function, meaning, or effect as something else
- **词根词缀**: equ(相等)+val(价值)+ent(形容词)→等价的
- **例句**:
- The metric system uses grams as the equivalent of the imperial ounce in measuring mass.
- In formal logic, 'if P then Q' is not equivalent to 'if Q then P', a common reasoning error tested in GRE and考研 English reading.
- **考频**: 39
