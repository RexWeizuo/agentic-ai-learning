# terr 词根 (恐惧/土地)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: terr- 有两个来源：恐惧 (terrify, terror) 和 土地 (territory)。terrific 原意为“可怕的”，现多指“极好的”。

**4 词**

---

## terrify

- **音标**: 'terifai
- **释义**: vt. 使恐惧, 恐吓
- **英文释义**: to fill someone with extreme fear
- **词根词缀**: terr(害怕)+ify(使)→使害怕→恐吓
- **例句**:
- The sudden explosion terrified the children, leaving them trembling and speechless.
- Many students are terrified of failing the graduate entrance exam, which drives them to study relentlessly.
- **考频**: 17
## terror

- **音标**: 'terә
- **释义**: n. 恐怖, 可怕的人\n[医] 惊吓, 惊悸
- **英文释义**: extreme fear, often caused by threat of violence or danger
- **词根词缀**: terr(害怕)+or(名词)→害怕→恐怖
- **例句**:
- The terrorist attack spread terror throughout the city.
- She froze in terror when she heard the gunshot.
- **考频**: 10
## terrific

- **音标**: tә'rifik
- **释义**: a. 非常好的, 极端的, 可怕的
- **英文释义**: extremely good or impressive; also used ironically or in informal contexts to mean 'terrible' or 'frightening'
- **词根词缀**: terr(害怕)+ific(产生…的)→产生害怕的→可怕的
- **例句**:
- The candidate delivered a terrific speech that impressed the entire panel.
- The storm caused terrific damage to coastal infrastructure, prompting an emergency response.
- **考频**: 8
## territory

- **音标**: 'teritәri
- **释义**: n. 领土, 领地, 版图, 地区, 活动范围\n[经] (推销员等的)推销区域
- **英文释义**: an area of land under the jurisdiction of a ruler, government, or organization; a region where someone or something operates or has influence
- **词根词缀**: terr(土地)+it(走)+ory(场所)→走遍的土地→领土
- **例句**:
- The dispute centers on the sovereignty of the disputed territory in the South China Sea.
- Each sales representative is assigned a specific territory to cover, ensuring no overlap in client outreach.
- **考频**: 40
