# 词根 vers/vert (转)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: vers/vert 表示“转”，versatile (多才多艺的/万用的) 指能灵活转变，versus 表示“对抗/转向对面”。

**5 词**

---

## versatile

- **音标**: 'vә:sәtail
- **释义**: a. 多才多艺的, 万用的, 万向的
- **英文释义**: able to adapt or be used in many different ways; having many skills or functions
- **词根词缀**: vers(转)+atile(能…的)→能转动的→多才多艺的
- **例句**:
- Her versatile talent enabled her to excel in both classical music and modern jazz.
- This versatile software supports data analysis, visualization, and real-time collaboration—making it ideal for graduate research.
- **考频**: 6
## verse

- **音标**: vә:s
- **释义**: n. 诗, 韵文, 诗句\nvt. 用诗表达\nvi. 作诗
- **英文释义**: A single line of poetry or a stanza; poetic composition in general.
- **词根词缀**: vers(转)+e→转行→诗歌
- **例句**:
- Shakespeare's sonnets are celebrated for their mastery of iambic pentameter and rich verse.
- The poet spent years refining her verse to achieve both emotional depth and formal precision.
- **考频**: 10
## version

- **音标**: 'vә:ʒәn
- **释义**: n. 一种描述, 版本, 译文\n[计] 版本
- **英文释义**: a particular form or adaptation of something, such as a text, product, or software
- **词根词缀**: vers(转)+ion(名词)→转述→版本
- **例句**:
- The latest version of the software includes several security enhancements.
- Her version of the events differed significantly from the eyewitness accounts.
- **考频**: 78
## versus

- **音标**: 'vә:sәs
- **释义**: prep. 对...\n[法] 诉, 对, 相形
- **英文释义**: used to indicate a contest, comparison, or contrast between two people, teams, ideas, or things
- **词根词缀**: vers(转)+us(拉丁后缀)→转向→对抗
- **例句**:
- The debate centered on democracy versus authoritarianism.
- In the landmark case, Brown v. Board of Education, the Supreme Court ruled that segregation in public schools was unconstitutional.
- **考频**: 20
## vertical

- **音标**: 'vә:tikl
- **释义**: a. 垂直的, 直立的\n[医] 垂直的, 顶的, 头顶的
- **英文释义**: perpendicular to the horizon or to a base line; standing upright
- **词根词缀**: vert(转)+ical(形容词)→转动的→垂直的(来自顶点)
- **例句**:
- The cliff face rises in a nearly vertical wall over 300 meters high.
- In anatomical position, the spine maintains a vertical alignment from the skull to the pelvis.
- **考频**: 11
