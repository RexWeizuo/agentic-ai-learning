# 建立、构造

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: 共享词根 -stitu-/-struct- (建立/建造)，constitution 可指宪法或体质。

**4 词**

---

## constituent

- **音标**: kәn'stitjuәnt
- **释义**: n. 成分, 选民, 构成物\na. 构成的, 组织的, 选举的
- **英文释义**: A part or element that makes up a whole; a voter in a particular electoral district
- **词根词缀**: con(共同)+stitut(建立)+ent(名词/形容词)→共同建立→成分
- **例句**:
- Water is a vital constituent of all living cells.
- Each constituent has the right to contact their representative regarding proposed legislation.
- **考频**: 10
## constitute

- **音标**: kәn'stitjut
- **释义**: vt. 构成, 组成, 任命\n[建] 构造, 组成
- **英文释义**: to form or make up a whole; to appoint officially
- **词根词缀**: con(共同)+stitut(建立)+e→共同建立→构成
- **例句**:
- These five elements constitute the core framework of the theory.
- The committee was constituted by experts from eight different countries.
- **考频**: 56
## constitution

- **音标**: .kɒnsti'tju:ʃәn
- **释义**: n. 构成, 宪法, 体格\n[医] 体质; 结构, 组织
- **英文释义**: The fundamental principles and established precedents that govern a nation or organization; also, the physical and mental makeup of a person.
- **词根词缀**: con(共同)+stitut(建立)+ion(名词)→共同建立→宪法
- **例句**:
- The U.S. Constitution establishes the framework for federal government and guarantees basic rights.
- Her robust constitution enabled her to recover quickly from the illness, despite the demanding treatment regimen.
- **考频**: 30
## construct

- **音标**: kәn'strʌkt
- **释义**: vt. 构造, 建造, 对...进行构思, 作图\nn. 构成物
- **英文释义**: to build or form something, especially in a careful or deliberate way; to create an idea, theory, or interpretation
- **词根词缀**: con(共同)+struct(建造)→共同建造→建造
- **例句**:
- Researchers must be cautious not to construct biased conclusions from incomplete data.
- The essay requires students to construct a coherent argument supported by empirical evidence.
- **考频**: 54
