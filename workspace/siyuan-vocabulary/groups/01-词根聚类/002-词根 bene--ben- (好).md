# 词根 bene-/ben- (好)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: 词根 bene-/ben- 表示“好”。benefit(利益), beneficial(有益的), benevolent(慈善的), benign(良性的), bonus(奖金/额外的好事)。

**5 词**

---

## benefit

- **音标**: 'benifit
- **释义**: n. 利益\nvt. 有益于\nvi. 受益
- **英文释义**: an advantage or profit gained from something; to help or improve someone or something; to receive help or improvement
- **词根词缀**: bene(好)+fit(做)→做好事→利益
- **例句**:
- Regular exercise can benefit both physical and mental health.
- Students who participate in internships often benefit significantly from real-world experience.
- **考频**: 604
## beneficial

- **音标**: .beni'fiʃәl
- **释义**: a. 有益的, 受益的\n[经] 有使用权的, 可享利益的
- **英文释义**: producing good or helpful results; advantageous
- **词根词缀**: bene(好)+fic(做)+ial(形容词)→做好的→有益的
- **例句**:
- Regular physical exercise is highly beneficial to both physical and mental health.
- The new trade agreement proved beneficial for small businesses, granting them preferential market access.
- **考频**: 46
## benevolent

- **音标**: bi'nevәlәnt
- **释义**: a. 善意的, 慈善的
- **英文释义**: well-meaning and kindly; showing goodwill or charity
- **词根词缀**: bene(好)+vol(意愿)+ent(形容词)→好意的→仁慈的
- **例句**:
- The benevolent donor established a scholarship fund for underprivileged students.
- Her benevolent attitude toward refugees earned her widespread respect in the international community.
- **考频**: 3
## benign

- **音标**: bi'nain
- **释义**: a. 善良的, 良性的\n[医] 良性的
- **英文释义**: not harmful or dangerous; kindly and gentle in nature
- **词根词缀**: beni(好)+gn(产生)→产生好的→良性的
- **例句**:
- The tumor was confirmed to be benign after the biopsy.
- Her benign smile and calm demeanor helped ease the tension in the room.
- **考频**: 5
## bonus

- **音标**: 'bәunәs
- **释义**: n. 奖金, 红利\n[经] 奖金, 红利, 额外补贴
- **英文释义**: An extra payment or reward given to an employee, often based on performance or company profits.
- **词根词缀**: bon(好)+us(名词)→好处→奖金
- **例句**:
- She received a substantial year-end bonus for exceeding her sales targets.
- The company announced a profit-sharing bonus for all full-time employees after record quarterly earnings.
- **考频**: 19
