# 词根 ball- (球/舞会)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: 词根 ball- (球/舞会)。ball(球/舞会), balloon(气球), ballet(芭蕾舞), ballot(投票/小球), baseball(棒球), basketball(篮球)。

**6 词**

---

## ball

- **音标**: bɒ:l
- **释义**: n. 球, 舞会, 球状物\nv. 捏成球形
- **英文释义**: A round object used in games or sports; a formal dance event; or to form something into a spherical shape.
- **词根词缀**: ball(球)→球
- **例句**:
- The soccer player kicked the ball with great precision, scoring a decisive goal.
- She wore an elegant gown to the annual charity ball held at the historic mansion.
- **考频**: 31
## balloon

- **音标**: bә'lu:n
- **释义**: n. 气球\nvt. 使成气球状\nvi. 膨胀如气球, 激增
- **英文释义**: A flexible bag filled with air or gas, typically used for decoration, play, or flight; to swell or increase rapidly.
- **词根词缀**: ball(球)+oon(大)→大球→气球
- **例句**:
- The company's debt ballooned to over $2 billion within two years.
- As the hot-air balloon rose, the landscape below seemed to shrink into a miniature map.
- **考频**: 7
## ballet

- **音标**: 'bælei
- **释义**: n. 芭蕾舞
- **英文释义**: a classical form of theatrical dance characterized by formalized movements, precise gestures, and often accompanied by orchestral music
- **词根词缀**: ball(舞)+et(小)→小舞蹈→芭蕾舞
- **例句**:
- She trained rigorously for ten years before joining a professional ballet company.
- The choreographer fused contemporary dance techniques with traditional ballet to create an innovative performance.
- **考频**: 10
## ballot

- **音标**: 'bælәt
- **释义**: n. 投票, 投票用纸, 抽签\nvi. 投票, 抽签\nvt. 投票选出, 拉选票
- **英文释义**: a method of voting, especially in secret, or the paper used for voting
- **词根词缀**: ball(球)+ot(小)→小球→选票
- **例句**:
- The committee will hold a secret ballot to elect its new chairperson.
- All eligible citizens are encouraged to cast their ballot in the upcoming general election.
- **考频**: 8
## baseball

- **音标**: 'beisbɒ:l

- **英文释义**: A team sport played with a bat and ball between two teams of nine players each, involving hitting, running bases, and fielding.
- **词根词缀**: base(基础)+ball(球)→垒球→棒球
- **例句**:

- **考频**: 7
## basketball

- **音标**: 'bɑ:skitbɒ:l
- **释义**: n. 篮球
- **英文释义**: A team sport played between two teams of five players each, using a spherical ball and aiming to score points by throwing the ball through the opponent's hoop.
- **词根词缀**: basket(篮子)+ball(球)→篮子球→篮球
- **例句**:
- Basketball requires not only physical agility but also strategic thinking and teamwork.
- Many universities in the United States offer scholarships to outstanding basketball players.
