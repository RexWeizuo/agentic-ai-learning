# 前缀 un- (否定)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: un- 是最常见的否定前缀，unanimous (意见一致的) 词根为 anim (生命/心智)，uncover 意为“揭露”。

**8 词**

---

## unanimous

- **音标**: ju:'nænimәs
- **释义**: a. 意见一致的, 无异议的
- **英文释义**: Agreeing completely; having no disagreement among all members of a group
- **词根词缀**: un(one)+anim(mind)+ous(形容词)→意见一致的
- **例句**:
- The committee reached a unanimous decision to approve the funding proposal.
- The Supreme Court's ruling was unanimous, reflecting broad consensus on the constitutional issue.
- **考频**: 2
## uncover

- **音标**: .ʌn'kʌvә
- **释义**: vt. 揭露, 揭开, 暴露, 脱帽致敬\nvi. 脱帽致敬, 揭去盖子
- **英文释义**: to remove a cover or concealment; to reveal something previously hidden or unknown
- **词根词缀**: un(打开)+cover(覆盖)→揭开覆盖物→揭露
- **例句**:
- The investigative journalist uncovered evidence of widespread corruption in the agency.
- New archaeological findings have uncovered ancient trade routes that were previously undocumented.
- **考频**: 13
## undoubtedly

- **音标**: ʌn'dajtidli
- **释义**: adv. 无疑, 必定, 确实地
- **英文释义**: without any doubt; certainly
- **词根词缀**: un(不)+doubt(怀疑)+ed(形容词)+ly(副词)→毫无疑问地
- **例句**:
- The discovery of penicillin was undoubtedly one of the most significant medical breakthroughs of the 20th century.
- She is undoubtedly the strongest candidate for the position, given her extensive research experience and publication record.
- **考频**: 21
## uneasy

- **音标**: .ʌn'i:zi
- **释义**: a. 心神不安的, 不自在的, 不稳定的, 不舒服的
- **英文释义**: feeling anxious, worried, or uncomfortable; not stable or secure
- **词根词缀**: un(不)+easy(容易的)→不安的
- **例句**:
- She felt uneasy about the sudden change in the project timeline.
- The government's latest policy has left financial markets uneasy.
- **考频**: 13
## unexpected

- **音标**: .ʌnik'spektid
- **释义**: a. 料想不到的, 突然的, 意外的\n[法] 不能预料的, 意外的
- **英文释义**: happening or appearing in a way that is not expected
- **词根词缀**: un(不)+expect(期望)+ed(形容词)→意外的
- **例句**:
- The project was delayed by an unexpected technical failure.
- Her sudden resignation came as an unexpected blow to the team.
- **考频**: 53
## unfortunately

- **音标**: ʌn'fɔ:tjjnәtli
- **释义**: adv. 恐怕, 不幸的是
- **英文释义**: used to introduce a statement expressing regret or disappointment about something
- **词根词缀**: un(不)+fortunate(幸运的)+ly(副词)→不幸地
- **例句**:
- Unfortunately, the experiment failed due to a critical design flaw.
- Unfortunately, only a small proportion of participants met the inclusion criteria, limiting the study's generalizability.
- **考频**: 57
## unlikely

- **音标**: .ʌn'laikli
- **释义**: a. 不太可能的
- **英文释义**: not likely to happen or be true
- **词根词缀**: un(不)+like(像)+ly(副词)→不像的→不太可能的
- **例句**:
- It is unlikely that the government will reverse its decision before the election.
- Given the tight deadline and limited resources, it is highly unlikely that the project will be completed on time.
- **考频**: 73
## unload

- **音标**: .ʌn'lәud
- **释义**: vi. 卸货\nvt. 从...卸下, 摆脱...之负担, 倾销, 卸(货)\n[计] 卸载
- **英文释义**: to remove goods from a vehicle or container; to get rid of a burden, responsibility, or unwanted emotion; to remove software or data from a system
- **词根词缀**: un(取消)+load(负载)→卸货
- **例句**:
- The cargo ship will unload at Shanghai Port tomorrow morning.
- She needed to unload her anxieties before the important presentation.
- **考频**: 3
