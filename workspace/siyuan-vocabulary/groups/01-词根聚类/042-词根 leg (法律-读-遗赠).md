# 词根 leg (法律/读/遗赠)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: leg/lex 词根，涵盖法律、立法及遗赠

**4 词**

---

## legacy

- **音标**: 'legәsi
- **释义**: n. 祖先传下来之物, 遗赠物\n[经] 遗产, 遗赠物
- **英文释义**: Something handed down from an ancestor or predecessor, such as property, a tradition, or a system of beliefs; a gift of money or property left to someone in a will.
- **词根词缀**: leg(词根:送)+acy(名词)→送的东西→遗产
- **例句**:
- The company's outdated IT infrastructure is a legacy system that hinders digital transformation.
- Her commitment to social justice remains a powerful legacy for future generations of activists.
- **考频**: 13
## legal

- **音标**: 'li:gәl
- **释义**: a. 法律的, 法定的, 合法的\n[经] 法定权利; 法律(上)的, 合法的
- **英文释义**: allowed or required by law; relating to the law
- **词根词缀**: leg(词根:法律)+al(形容词)→法律的
- **例句**:
- The company ensured all its operations complied with legal regulations.
- She challenged the decision on legal grounds, arguing it violated constitutional rights.
- **考频**: 172
## legislation

- **音标**: .ledʒis'leiʃәn
- **释义**: n. 立法, 法律\n[医] 立法, 法规
- **英文释义**: the process of making and enacting laws by a legislative body
- **词根词缀**: leg(法律)+isl(做)+ation(名词)→制定法律→立法
- **例句**:
- The new environmental legislation aims to reduce carbon emissions by 40% before 2030.
- Judges must interpret legislation in light of constitutional principles and prior case law.
- **考频**: 51
## legitimate

- **音标**: li'dʒitimәt
- **释义**: a. 合法的, 正当的, 婚生的\nvt. 认为正当, 立为嫡嗣, 使合法
- **英文释义**: lawful, valid, or justified according to law, logic, or custom
- **词根词缀**: leg(法律)+itim(最)+ate(形容词)→最符合法律的→合法的
- **例句**:
- The government's authority must be based on legitimate institutions and democratic processes.
- She questioned whether the company's data collection practices were legitimate under current privacy laws.
- **考频**: 20
