# 连接、接触

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: 共享词根 -nect-/-tact- (连接/接触)，conjunction 常指语法连词或结合。

**4 词**

---

## connect

- **音标**: kә'nekt
- **释义**: v. 连接, 联合, 联系
- **英文释义**: to join or link two or more things together, physically or conceptually
- **词根词缀**: con(共同)+nect(连接)→连接在一起→连接
- **例句**:
- The new subway line will connect the downtown area with the eastern suburbs.
- Researchers aim to connect cognitive decline with specific genetic markers in their latest study.
- **考频**: 132
## connection

- **音标**: kә'nekʃәn
- **释义**: n. 连接, 关系, 前后关系\n[计] 连接
- **英文释义**: a relationship or link between people, things, or ideas; also refers to the act of connecting devices or systems
- **词根词缀**: con(共同)+nect(连接)+ion(名词)→连接在一起→连接
- **例句**:
- There is a strong connection between regular exercise and improved mental health.
- The server failed to establish a secure connection to the database, causing the application to crash.
- **考频**: 132
## conjunction

- **音标**: kәn'dʒʌŋkʃәn
- **释义**: n. 连接词, 联合, 结合\n[计] 合取
- **英文释义**: A word used to connect clauses, sentences, or words (e.g., 'and', 'but', 'because'); also refers to the act of joining or occurring together.
- **词根词缀**: con(共同)+junct(连接)+ion(名词)→连接在一起→结合
- **例句**:
- Coordinating conjunctions like 'for', 'and', 'nor', 'but', 'or', 'yet', and 'so' link grammatically equal elements.
- The astronomical conjunction of Jupiter and Saturn occurs approximately every 20 years, a phenomenon often cited in academic writing on celestial mechanics.
- **考频**: 8
## contact

- **音标**: 'kɒntækt
- **释义**: n. 联系, 交际, 熟人, 接触\nvi. 接触, 联系\nvt. 使接触
- **英文释义**: to communicate with someone, typically for a specific purpose; or the state of being in touch or connection with someone or something
- **词根词缀**: con(一起)+tact(接触)→一起接触→接触
- **例句**:
- Please contact the admissions office if you have any questions about the application process.
- Maintaining professional contact with former colleagues can be valuable for career development.
- **考频**: 186
