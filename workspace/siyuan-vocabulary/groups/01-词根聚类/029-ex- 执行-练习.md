## execute

- **音标**: 'eksikju:t
- **释义**: vt. 执行, 实行, 完成, 处死, 制成\n[计] 执行
- **英文释义**: to carry out or complete a plan, action, or task; to put into effect; in computing, to run a program or instruction; historically, to kill as a lawful punishment
- **词根词缀**: ex(出)+ecut(跟随)+e→跟随出来→执行
- **例句**:
- The company failed to execute its digital transformation strategy effectively.
- Before the program can be executed, it must be compiled and linked.
- **考频**: 7
## executive

- **释义**: n. 执行部门, 执行委员会, 执行者, 经理主管人员\na. 执行的, 善于执行的, 善于经营的\n[计] 执行程序
- **英文释义**: A person or group responsible for managing and implementing policies or operations, especially in a business or government context; also, an adjective meaning having the power or function of carrying out decisions.
- **词根词缀**: ex(出)+ecut(跟随)+ive(形容词)→执行的→行政的
