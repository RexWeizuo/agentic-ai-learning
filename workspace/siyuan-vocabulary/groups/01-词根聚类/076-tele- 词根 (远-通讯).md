# tele- 词根 (远/通讯)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: tele- 表示“远距离”。这组词是现代通讯与光学仪器的基础词汇。

**4 词**

---

## telegraph

- **音标**: 'teligrɑ:f
- **释义**: n. 电报, 电报机\nvi. 打电报\nvt. 打电报给, 电汇, 流露, 显示出要...的样子
- **英文释义**: A device or system for sending messages over long distances using electrical signals; to send a message by telegraph; to reveal something unintentionally or in advance.
- **词根词缀**: tele(远)+graph(写)→远距离书写→电报
- **例句**:
- The journalist telegraphed the main argument of her article in the opening paragraph, making the conclusion predictable.
- Though he tried to remain neutral, his clenched jaw and furrowed brow telegraphed his deep disapproval.
- **考频**: 15
## telephone

- **音标**: 'telifәun
- **释义**: n. 电话, 电话机\nv. 打电话
- **英文释义**: a device that transmits sound, especially the human voice, over long distances via electrical signals; to communicate with someone using such a device
- **词根词缀**: tele(远)+phone(声音)→远距离声音→电话
- **例句**:
- She telephoned her supervisor immediately after discovering the error.
- The committee decided to hold the meeting by telephone rather than in person due to the pandemic.
- **考频**: 93
## telescope

- **音标**: 'teliskәup
- **释义**: n. 望远镜\nvi. 叠缩, 嵌进, 缩短\nvt. 使叠缩, 使缩短
- **英文释义**: An optical instrument for making distant objects appear nearer; also, to collapse or compress into a smaller space, often by sliding sections together.
- **词根词缀**: tele(远)+scope(看)→远距离看→望远镜
- **例句**:
- Astronomers used a powerful space-based telescope to observe distant galaxies.
- The project timeline was telescoped from twelve months to eight without compromising key milestones.
- **考频**: 19
## television

- **音标**: 'teli.viʒәn
- **释义**: n. 电视\n[电] 电视
- **英文释义**: A system for transmitting visual images and sound over distance using electromagnetic waves or cable, or the device used to receive such signals.
- **词根词缀**: tele(远)+vision(视觉)→远距离视觉→电视
- **例句**:
- The rise of television has profoundly influenced public opinion and cultural norms.
- Many scholars argue that excessive television consumption may impair children's cognitive development.
- **考频**: 319
