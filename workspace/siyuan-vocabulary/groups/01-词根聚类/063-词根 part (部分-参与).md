# 词根 part (部分/参与)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: part 表示“部分、分开”。participant(参与者), partial(部分的/偏袒的), particle(微粒), portion(一份)。

**7 词**

---

## participant

- **音标**: pɑ:'tisipәnt
- **释义**: n. 参加者, 参与者\na. 有份的, 参加的, 参与的
- **英文释义**: a person who takes part in an activity, event, or process
- **词根词缀**: part(部分)+cip(拿)+ant(人)→拿一部分的人→参与者
- **例句**:
- All participants in the international conference were required to submit their abstracts two weeks in advance.
- The study involved 120 participants randomly assigned to either the control or experimental group.
- **考频**: 149
## participate

- **音标**: pɑ:'tisipeit
- **释义**: vi. 参加, 分享, 参与, 带有\nvt. 分享, 分担
- **英文释义**: to take part in an activity or event
- **词根词缀**: part(部分)+cip(拿)+ate(动词)→拿一部分→参与
- **例句**:
- All students are encouraged to participate actively in class discussions.
- She declined to participate in the controversial policy-making process.
- **考频**: 98
## partial

- **音标**: 'pɑ:ʃәl
- **释义**: a. 部分的, 偏袒的, 偏爱的\nn. 分音
- **英文释义**: existing in part only; showing unfair favoritism; a component tone of a complex sound
- **词根词缀**: part(部分)+ial(形容词)→部分的
- **例句**:
- The report provides only a partial account of the incident, omitting key evidence.
- Judges must remain impartial and avoid any partial treatment of the parties involved.
- **考频**: 17
## particle

- **音标**: 'pɑ:tikl
- **释义**: n. 颗粒, 粒子, 质点, 极小量\n[化] 粒子; 质点
- **英文释义**: A very small piece of matter; a minute unit of a substance, often studied in physics or chemistry.
- **词根词缀**: part(部分)+icle(小)→小部分→微粒
- **例句**:
- Even the smallest particle of dust can interfere with the precision of this optical instrument.
- According to quantum physics, subatomic particles do not always follow classical laws of motion.
- **考频**: 16
## portion

- **音标**: 'pɒ:ʃәn
- **释义**: n. 部分, 一份, 命运, 嫁妆\nvt. 分配, 给...嫁妆
- **英文释义**: A part or share of something; a specific amount or quantity assigned or taken.
- **词根词缀**: port(部分)+ion(名词)→部分
- **例句**:
- The government allocated a substantial portion of the budget to healthcare reform.
- Her success was not due to luck alone, but to a large portion of disciplined effort and strategic planning.
- **考频**: 49
## patch

- **音标**: pætʃ
- **释义**: n. 片, 补缀, 碎片, 斑, 傻瓜\nvt. 补缀, 掩饰, 拼凑, 平息\n[计] 修补; 拼凑
- **英文释义**: A small piece of material used to repair or cover a hole or weak spot; also, a small area that differs in color, texture, or composition from its surroundings.
- **词根词缀**: patch(词根：补丁)→补丁
- **例句**:
- The software developer released a security patch to fix the vulnerability.
- She wore a colorful patch on her denim jacket, symbolizing her environmental activism.
- **考频**: 22
## piece

- **音标**: pi:s
- **释义**: n. 块, 片, 篇, 碎片, 部分, 部件, 标准量\nvt. 修补, 修理, 拼合, 接线头\nvi. 吃零食
- **英文释义**: A single part or portion of something, often separated from a larger whole; also, a distinct item (e.g., of art, writing, or machinery).
- **词根词缀**: piece(片)→片
- **例句**:
- She cut a small piece of cake and offered it to him.
- The historian analyzed each piece of evidence carefully before drawing conclusions.
