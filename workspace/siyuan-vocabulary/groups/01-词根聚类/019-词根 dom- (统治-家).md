# 词根 dom- (统治/家)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: 源自拉丁语 dominus (主人) 或 domus (家)，引申为统治、领域或家庭。

**5 词**

---

## dominant

- **音标**: 'dɒminәnt
- **释义**: a. 占优势的, 支配的\n[医] 优性的, 显性的
- **英文释义**: having the greatest influence, importance, or power; genetically expressed over a recessive allele
- **词根词缀**: dom(统治)+in+ant(形容词)→统治的→支配的
- **例句**:
- The dominant culture in the region has shaped its educational system for centuries.
- In Mendelian genetics, the brown-eye allele is dominant over the blue-eye allele.
- **考频**: 41
## dominate

- **音标**: 'dɒmineit
- **释义**: v. 支配, 占优势
- **英文释义**: to have control or influence over something or someone; to be the most important or powerful element
- **词根词缀**: dom(统治)+in+ate(动词)→统治→支配
- **例句**:
- The ruling party has dominated national politics for over two decades.
- In this industry, technological innovation dominates strategic decision-making.
- **考频**: 98
## domain

- **音标**: dәu'mein
- **释义**: n. 领域, 领土, 产业, 范围\n[计] 域, 区域, 支配命令
- **英文释义**: An area of knowledge, activity, or control; in computing, a group of networked computers under common administration.
- **词根词缀**: dom(统治)+ain(名词)→统治的区域→领域
- **例句**:
- Artificial intelligence is now a rapidly expanding domain in computer science.
- The government asserted its sovereignty over the disputed domain.
- **考频**: 14
## domestic

- **音标**: dәu'mestik
- **释义**: a. 家庭的, 国内的, 驯养的\n[医] 家庭的, 家用的
- **英文释义**: relating to the home, family, or internal affairs of a country; tamed and kept by humans
- **词根词缀**: dom(家)+estic(形容词)→家的→国内的
- **例句**:
- The government introduced new policies to boost domestic consumption and reduce reliance on exports.
- Unlike wild wolves, domestic dogs have evolved to live closely with humans for thousands of years.
- **考频**: 117
## dome

- **音标**: dәum
- **释义**: n. 圆顶\nvt. 加圆顶\nvi. 成圆顶状
- **英文释义**: A rounded vault forming the roof of a building or structure; to cover with or form into a dome.
- **词根词缀**: dom(家)+e→家→圆顶
- **例句**:
- The Capitol Building in Washington, D.C. features an iconic white marble dome.
- As the molten lava cooled, the surface of the volcano began to dome upward.
- **考频**: 10
