# fabric 制造/结构

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: 词根 fab 表示制造、结构，引申为极好的

**4 词**

---

## fabric

- **音标**: 'fæbrik
- **释义**: n. 织物, 布, 结构, 构造, 建筑物\n[经] 组织, 建筑物, 工厂
- **英文释义**: A woven or knitted material, typically used for clothing or upholstery; also, the underlying structure or essential nature of something.
- **词根词缀**: fabr(制作)+ic(名词)→制作出的东西→织物
- **例句**:
- The designer chose a lightweight cotton fabric for the summer collection.
- Social cohesion is vital to the fabric of any democratic society.
- **考频**: 15
## fabricate

- **音标**: 'fæbrikeit
- **释义**: vt. 制造, 建造, 装配, 伪造
- **英文释义**: to make something, often in a factory or workshop; to invent false information deliberately
- **词根词缀**: fabr(制作)+ic+ate(动词)→制作→捏造
- **例句**:
- The company fabricated high-precision components for aerospace applications.
- He was accused of fabricating evidence to mislead the investigation.
- **考频**: 2
## fabulous

- **音标**: 'fæbjulәs
- **释义**: a. 传说的, 寓言般的, 难以置信的
- **英文释义**: extremely good, impressive, or astonishing; sometimes used to describe something mythical or hard to believe
- **词根词缀**: fab(说)+ul+ous(形容词)→像寓言般的→极好的
- **例句**:
- Her performance in the final exam was absolutely fabulous, earning her the highest score in the department.
- The ancient manuscript described a fabulous city of gold, long believed to be mere legend.
- **考频**: 5
## facet

- **音标**: 'fæsit
- **释义**: n. (多面体的)面, 方面, 琢面\n[医] 小平面, 小面
- **英文释义**: A distinct aspect or feature of something, especially one of many that together make up a complex issue or situation; also, a flat surface on a cut gemstone or geometric solid.
- **词根词缀**: fac(面)+et(小)→小面→方面
- **例句**:
- The economic facet of climate change policy is often overlooked in public debates.
- Each facet of the diamond reflected light with exceptional brilliance, demonstrating the precision of the cutter's work.
- **考频**: 9
