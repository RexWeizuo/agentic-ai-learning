# ext- 外部/极端

> **类型**: 词根

**4 词**

## exterior

- **音标**: ik'stiәriә
- **释义**: n. 外部, 表面, 外貌\na. 外部的, 外在的, 表面的
- **英文释义**: the outer surface or outside part of something; outward appearance
- **词根词缀**: ex(向外)+ter(比较级)+ior(名词/形容词)→更外面的→外部
- **例句**:
- The building's exterior is clad in glass and steel, reflecting modern architectural principles.
- Despite his calm exterior, he was deeply anxious about the upcoming exam.
- **考频**: 2
## external

- **音标**: ik'stә:nl
- **释义**: n. 外部, 外面\na. 外部的, 客观的, 表面的
- **英文释义**: located or happening outside something; not part of the internal structure or nature
- **词根词缀**: ex(向外)+tern(外部)+al(形容词)→外部的
- **例句**:
- The company's financial difficulties were exacerbated by external economic pressures.
- She attributed her failure to external factors rather than personal shortcomings.
- **考频**: 48
## extra

- **音标**: 'ekstrә
- **释义**: n. 额外的事物, 另外的收费\na. 额外的, 特别的\nadv. 额外地, 特别地, 非常地
- **英文释义**: beyond what is usual, necessary, or expected; additional or supplementary
- **词根词缀**: ex(超出)+tra→超出→额外的
- **例句**:
- The hotel charges an extra $20 per night for a sea-view room.
- She worked extra hours to meet the deadline for her thesis submission.
- **考频**: 161
## extreme

- **音标**: ik'stri:m
- **释义**: n. 极端, 末端\na. 极端的, 尽头的, 极度的, 偏激的
- **英文释义**: very great in degree; situated at the far end of a scale or range
- **词根词缀**: ex(超出)+trem(极限)+e→超出极限→极端的
- **例句**:
- The climbers faced extreme cold and thin air above 8,000 meters.
- His political views are so extreme that they alienate even moderate supporters.
- **考频**: 81
