# 词根 fract-/frag- (破碎)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: fract 和 frag 同源，表示“打破、破碎”。fraction 是分数/小部分，fragile 是易碎的。

**4 词**

---

## fraction

- **音标**: 'frækʃәn
- **释义**: n. 小部分, 破片, 分数\n[医] 部分, 成分, 分散
- **英文释义**: A small part or portion of something; in mathematics, a numerical quantity that is not a whole number, expressed as one integer divided by another.
- **词根词缀**: fract(打破)+ion(名词)→碎片
- **例句**:
- Only a fraction of the applicants were shortlisted for the final interview.
- The drug was administered in minute fractions to minimize side effects.
- **考频**: 20
## fracture

- **音标**: 'fræktʃә
- **释义**: n. 破碎, 骨折\nv. (使)破碎, (使)破裂
- **英文释义**: A break or crack in a bone or hard material; to break or cause to break into fragments.
- **词根词缀**: fract(打破)+ure(名词)→骨折
- **例句**:
- The radiologist confirmed a hairline fracture in her left tibia.
- Prolonged stress on the metal component led to a catastrophic fracture under load.
- **考频**: 9
## fragile

- **音标**: 'frædʒail
- **释义**: a. 易碎的, 脆的\n[医] 脆弱的, 脆的
- **英文释义**: easily broken, damaged, or destroyed; not strong or stable
- **词根词缀**: frag(打破)+ile(易)→易碎的
- **例句**:
- The antique vase is extremely fragile and must be handled with great care.
- Her mental health remained fragile after the traumatic experience, requiring ongoing professional support.
- **考频**: 15
## fragment

- **音标**: 'frægmәnt
- **释义**: n. 碎片, 破片, 片段\n[计] 段落; 片段; 分段
- **英文释义**: A small part broken off or separated from something larger; a piece or section of something incomplete.
- **词根词缀**: frag(打破)+ment(名词)→碎片
- **例句**:
- The archaeologists carefully reconstructed the ancient vase from dozens of ceramic fragments.
- Modernist literature often employs narrative fragmentation, presenting events as disjointed fragments rather than a linear story.
- **考频**: 15
