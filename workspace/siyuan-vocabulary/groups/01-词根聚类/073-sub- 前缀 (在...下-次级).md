# sub- 前缀 (在...下/次级)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: sub- 表示“在...下面、次级、接近”。subordinate (下级的) 和 subsequent (随后的) 是考研高频词。

**9 词**

---

## submarine

- **音标**: 'sʌbmәri:n
- **释义**: n. 潜水艇, 海底生物\na. 海中的, 生长在海中的\nvt. 用潜水艇攻击\nvi. 在下疾行, 在下滑动
- **英文释义**: A naval vessel capable of operating underwater; also used as an adjective meaning 'underwater' or 'submerged'.
- **词根词缀**: sub(在…之下)+mar(海)+ine(名词)→潜艇
- **例句**:
- The navy deployed a nuclear-powered submarine to monitor maritime activity in the South China Sea.
- During the marine biology expedition, researchers observed several rare submarine species thriving near hydrothermal vents.
- **考频**: 4

- **音标**: sәb'mә:dʒ
- **释义**: vt. 使浸水, 使陷入, 淹没\nvi. 潜入水中
- **英文释义**: to put or go under water; to overwhelm or absorb completely
- **词根词缀**: sub(在…之下)+merg(浸)+e→浸没
- **例句**:

- **考频**: 6
## subordinate

- **音标**: sә'bɒ:dnit
- **释义**: n. 属下, 附属物\na. 下级的, 次要的, 附属的\nvt. 使居下位, 使服从
- **英文释义**: A person under the authority or control of another; less important or secondary in rank, status, or function; to place in a lower or inferior position.
- **词根词缀**: sub(在…之下)+ordin(顺序)+ate(形容词)→从属的
- **例句**:
- As a team leader, she ensured that every subordinate had clear responsibilities and adequate support.
- The clause was deliberately subordinate to the main provision, reflecting its secondary legal weight.
- **考频**: 17
