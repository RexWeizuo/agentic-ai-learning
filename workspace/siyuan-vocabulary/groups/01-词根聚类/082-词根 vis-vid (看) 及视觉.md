# 词根 vis/vid (看) 及视觉

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: vis/vid 表示“看”，visit 本意为“去看望”，viewpoint 由 view (看) 派生，表示“观点”。

**6 词**

---

## visible

- **音标**: 'vizәbl
- **释义**: a. 看得见的, 明显的, 显然的\nn. 可见物
- **英文释义**: able to be seen; obvious or easily perceived
- **词根词缀**: vis(看)+ible(能)→能看见的→可见的
- **例句**:
- The mountain peak was barely visible through the morning mist.
- There has been no visible improvement in his condition despite the intensive treatment.
- **考频**: 34
## vision

- **音标**: 'viʒәn
- **释义**: n. 视觉, 眼光, 视力, 幻想\nvt. 梦见, 想象, 显示
- **英文释义**: the ability to see; the ability to think about or plan the future with imagination and wisdom; a mental image or illusion
- **词根词缀**: vis(看)+ion(名词)→看的行为→视力
- **例句**:
- Her long-term vision for sustainable development guided the company's strategic decisions.
- The architect presented a bold vision of the new campus, complete with 3D renderings and detailed feasibility analysis.
- **考频**: 101
## visual

- **音标**: 'viʒuәl
- **释义**: a. 视觉的\n[医] 视觉的, 视力的, 视觉性记忆优势者
- **英文释义**: relating to sight or the sense of vision
- **词根词缀**: vis(看)+ual(形容词)→看的→视觉的
- **例句**:
- The lecture included numerous visual aids such as charts and diagrams to enhance understanding.
- Some learners are visual learners, meaning they retain information more effectively when it is presented in images or spatially organized formats.
- **考频**: 59
## visit

- **音标**: 'vizit
- **释义**: n. 拜访, 访问, 游览, 视察\nvt. 拜访, 访问, 参观, 视察, 降临\nvi. 访问, 参观, 闲谈
- **英文释义**: to go to see a person, place, or institution for a specific purpose such as social contact, inspection, tourism, or official duty
- **词根词缀**: vis(看)+it(去)→去看→参观
- **例句**:
- The CEO made an unannounced visit to the Shanghai branch to assess operational efficiency.
- Tourists are advised to visit the ancient temple before sunrise to avoid the crowds and experience its serene atmosphere.
- **考频**: 279
## visitor

- **音标**: 'vizitә
- **释义**: n. 参观者, 游客, 访客\n[法] 视察人, 检视人, 检查员
- **英文释义**: a person who visits a place, especially for a short time
- **词根词缀**: vis(看)+it(去)+or(人)→去看的人→参观者
- **例句**:
- The museum provides audio guides for each visitor to enhance their understanding of the exhibits.
- As a foreign visitor, she was required to register with the local police within 24 hours of arrival.
- **考频**: 114
## viewpoint

- **音标**: 'vju:pɒint
- **释义**: n. 观点
- **英文释义**: a particular way of considering or thinking about something
- **词根词缀**: view(看)+point(点)→观看的点→观点
- **例句**:
- From a feminist viewpoint, the policy reinforces gender stereotypes.
- The essay presents a balanced analysis by incorporating multiple cultural viewpoints.
- **考频**: 10
