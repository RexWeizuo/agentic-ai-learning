# 词根：mod- (模式，度量，适度)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: 共享词根 mod-，表示“模式、度量、适度、方式”。

**5 词**

---

## mode

- **音标**: mәud
- **释义**: n. 模态, 调式, 样式, 文体, 状态, 方式, 风尚\n[计] 方式; DOS外部命令:设定各种设备命令
- **英文释义**: A particular way or manner in which something exists, functions, or is experienced; in computing, a setting that determines how a device or program operates.
- **词根词缀**: mod(方式)+e→方式
- **例句**:
- The software can operate in either batch mode or interactive mode.
- In statistics, the mode is the value that appears most frequently in a data set.
- **考频**: 40
## moderate

- **音标**: 'mɒdәrәt
- **释义**: a. 适度的, 稳健的, 中等的, 节制的\nvt. 节制, 减轻, 使缓和\nvi. 变缓和, 主持会议
- **英文释义**: not excessive or extreme; to preside over a discussion or reduce the intensity of something
- **词根词缀**: mod(方式)+er(人)+ate(形容词)→有方式的→适度的
- **例句**:
- The government adopted a moderate policy to balance economic growth and environmental protection.
- She moderated the panel discussion, ensuring all speakers adhered to time limits and maintained respectful dialogue.
- **考频**: 21
## modernisation

- **音标**: ,mɔdәnai'zeiʃәn;-ni'z-
- **释义**: n. <主英>=modernization
- **英文释义**: the process of making something modern, especially by introducing new technology or methods
- **词根词缀**: mod(mode/manner/measure)+ernis+-ation(行为/状态/结果)→方式
- **例句**:
- The government's infrastructure modernisation programme aims to upgrade railways, energy grids, and digital networks.
- Rapid industrialisation often accompanies economic modernisation, transforming traditional agrarian societies.
## modify

- **音标**: 'mɒdifai
- **释义**: vt. 修正, 变更, 修饰, 缓和, 减轻\nvi. 被修改\n[计] 修改
- **英文释义**: to make partial or minor changes to something in order to improve it or make it more suitable
- **词根词缀**: mod(方式)+ify(使)→使有方式→修改
- **例句**:
- The committee proposed several amendments to modify the original policy to better address environmental concerns.
- In object-oriented programming, a subclass may override a method to modify its behavior while inheriting from the parent class.
- **考频**: 61
## module

- **音标**: 'mɒdju:l
- **释义**: n. 组件, 模数\n[计] 模块
- **英文释义**: A self-contained unit of software or hardware that performs a specific function and can be combined with other modules to form a larger system.
- **词根词缀**: mod(方式)+ule(小)→小方式→模块
- **例句**:
- The software architecture consists of several independent modules, each handling a distinct task such as user authentication or data encryption.
- In modern programming courses, students learn to design reusable code modules to improve maintainability and scalability.
- **考频**: 2
