# 前缀：ob-/op- (反对，阻碍，面对)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: 共享前缀 ob-/op-，表示“反对、阻碍、面对、在...之前”。

**8 词**

---

## obstacle

- **音标**: 'ɒbstәkl
- **释义**: n. 障碍, 妨害物, 阻碍
- **英文释义**: Something that blocks or hinders progress or achievement
- **词根词缀**: ob(在对面)+sta(站立)+cle(小)→站在对面的东西→障碍
- **例句**:
- Lack of funding remains a major obstacle to the project's completion.
- She overcame numerous personal and academic obstacles to earn her PhD.
- **考频**: 57
## obstruct

- **音标**: әb'strʌkt
- **释义**: vt. 阻隔, 妨碍, 阻塞, 遮没\nvi. 设障碍
- **英文释义**: to block or hinder something from happening, moving, or being seen
- **词根词缀**: ob(在对面)+struct(建造)→在对面建造→阻碍
- **例句**:
- The fallen tree obstructed the road and delayed emergency vehicles.
- His refusal to cooperate seriously obstructed the investigation into the financial fraud.
- **考频**: 2
## obstruction

- **音标**: әb'strʌʃәn
- **释义**: n. 障碍, 妨碍, 闭塞物\n[医] 梗阻, 不通
- **英文释义**: Something that blocks or hinders movement, progress, or function; a physical or abstract barrier.
- **词根词缀**: ob(在对面)+struct(建造)+ion(名词)→阻碍
- **例句**:
- The fallen tree caused a serious obstruction on the highway, leading to a two-hour traffic delay.
- In medical imaging, intestinal obstruction is often identified by the presence of dilated bowel loops and air-fluid levels.
- **考频**: 2
## object

- **音标**: 'ɒbdʒekt
- **释义**: n. 物体, 目标, 目的, 对象, 宾语, 客体\nvi. 反对, 抱反感\nvt. 提出...来反对\n[计] 对象
- **英文释义**: A thing that can be seen or touched; a goal or purpose; the noun phrase receiving the action of a verb; a person or thing regarded as external to the self; in computing, a self-contained unit of data and functions.
- **词根词缀**: ob(反对)+ject(投掷)→反对
- **例句**:
- The primary object of the research is to understand cognitive development in early childhood.
- She objected strenuously to the proposed policy change, arguing that it violated fundamental principles of fairness.
- **考频**: 195

## opponent

- **音标**: ә'pәunәnt
- **释义**: n. 对手, 敌手, 反对者\na. 敌对的, 反对的, 对面的
- **英文释义**: a person who competes against or opposes someone in a contest, debate, or argument
- **词根词缀**: op(相反)+pon(放置)+ent(人)→站在对面的人→对手
- **例句**:
- In the final round of the debate, she skillfully refuted every argument raised by her opponent.
- The incumbent candidate faced a formidable opponent who gained widespread support among young voters.
- **考频**: 53
## oppress

- **释义**: vt. 压迫, 压抑, 使烦恼
- **英文释义**: to subject someone to cruel or unjust treatment, especially by those in power
- **词根词缀**: op(加强)+press(压)→压迫
- **例句**:

- The authoritarian regime oppressed political dissent through censorship and imprisonment.
- **考频**: 10
