# 词根 aud- (听)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: 词根 aud- 表示“听”。audience(听众), audio(音频), audit(审计/听账), auditorium(礼堂), aural(听觉的)。

**5 词**

---

## audience

- **音标**: 'ɒ:diәns
- **释义**: ==n. 听众, 观众, 读者\n[法] 听讼, 观众, 听众==
- **英文释义**: the group of people who watch a performance, read a book, or listen to a speech
- **同义词**: spectators, viewers
- **词根词缀**: audi(听)+ence→听的人→听众
- **例句**:
- The speaker adjusted her tone to better engage the academic audience.
- This novel has gained a wide international audience due to its universal themes.
- **考频**: 144
## audio

- **音标**: 'ɒ:diou
- **释义**: ==a. 音频的, 声音的\n[电] 声频==
- **英文释义**: relating to sound or the sense of hearing, especially in electronic transmission or recording
- **词根词缀**: audi(听)+o→音频的
- **例句**:
- The lecture was recorded in high-definition audio to ensure clarity for online learners.
- This software supports both video and audio playback, but the audio track must be synchronized with the visual content.
- **考频**: 8
## audit

- **音标**: 'ɒ:dit
- **释义**: ==n. 审计, 查帐\nvt. 查(帐), 旁听\nvi. 查账\n[计] 查帐; 审查; 检查==
- **英文释义**: A formal examination of financial records or systems to ensure accuracy and compliance.
- **词根词缀**: audi(听)+t→听报告→审计
- **例句**:
- The company underwent a rigorous financial audit before the merger.
- Graduate students may audit the advanced linguistics course without receiving credit.
- **考频**: 6
## auditorium

- **音标**: .ɒ:di'tɒ:riәm
- **释义**: ==n. 听众席, 礼堂\n[医] 大讲堂, 大会堂==
- **英文释义**: a large room or hall where people gather to watch performances, lectures, or meetings
- **词根词缀**: audit(hear)+orium(place)→place for hearing→礼堂
- **例句**:
- The speaker's voice echoed clearly throughout the vast auditorium.
- Students filled the university auditorium for the annual medical ethics lecture.
- **考频**: 2
## aural

- **音标**: 'ɒ:rәl
- **释义**: ==a. 耳的, 听觉的, 气氛的, 气味的, 光环的\n[医] 耳的; 先兆的==
- **英文释义**: relating to the ear or the sense of hearing
- **词根词缀**: aur+-al(…的/行为)
- **例句**:
- Students with strong aural skills can easily distinguish subtle pronunciation differences in English.
- The exam includes an aural comprehension section that tests listening accuracy and speed.
