# 前缀 under- (在...之下，不足)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: under- 表示“在...下面”或“不足、暗中”，注意 undermine (暗中破坏) 和 underlying (潜在的/基础的) 的熟词生义。

**11 词**

---

## underestimate

- **音标**: .ʌndәr'estimeit
- **释义**: n. 低估\nvt. 低估, 看轻
- **英文释义**: to judge something or someone as being less important, effective, or capable than they actually are
- **词根词缀**: under(低于)+estimate(估计)→低估
- **例句**:
- Many investors underestimated the long-term impact of regulatory changes on the tech sector.
- The committee's failure to underestimate the logistical challenges led to significant delays in the project rollout.
- **考频**: 28
## undergo

- **音标**: .ʌndә'gәu
- **释义**: vt. 遭受, 经历, 忍受\n[法] 经受, 经历, 忍受
- **英文释义**: to experience or be subjected to something, especially something difficult, unpleasant, or transformative
- **词根词缀**: under(在...下)+go(走)→在下面走→经历
- **例句**:
- All candidates must undergo a rigorous background check before employment.
- The patient will undergo surgery tomorrow to remove the tumor.
- **考频**: 32
## undergraduate

- **音标**: .ʌndә'grædjuit
- **释义**: n. 大学本科生, 大学肄业生\na. 大学生的
- **英文释义**: A student studying for their first degree at a college or university.
- **词根词缀**: under(低于)+graduate(毕业生)→尚未毕业的学生→本科生
- **例句**:
- Many undergraduates participate in research projects alongside faculty members.
- She published her first academic paper while still an undergraduate at Stanford.
- **考频**: 77

- **音标**: .ʌndә'lai
- **释义**: vt. 位于...之下, 成为...的基础
- **英文释义**: to serve as a basis or foundation for something
- **词根词缀**: under(在...下)+lie(躺)→躺在下面→构成基础
- **例句**:
- Economic inequality underlies many social problems in modern societies.
- A deep-seated cultural belief underlies the country's resistance to policy reform.
- **考频**: 26
## underline

- **音标**: 'ʌndәlain
- **释义**: vt. 在...下面划线, 作...的衬里, 强调\nn. 下划线, 图下说明文字\n[计] 加下划线; 下划线
- **英文释义**: to draw a line beneath (a word or phrase) to emphasize it or indicate its importance
- **词根词缀**: under(在...下)+line(线)→在下面划线→强调
- **例句**:
- The author underlines the urgency of climate action in the concluding paragraph.

- **考频**: 171
## underlying

- **音标**: .ʌndә'laiiŋ
- **释义**: a. 在下面的\n[经] 优先的, 基础的, 放在下面的
- **英文释义**: existing or operating beneath the surface; fundamental or basic, often not immediately apparent
- **词根词缀**: under(在下/不足)+ly+-ing(正在…/…的动作/…的)
- **例句**:
- The underlying cause of the economic crisis was a lack of regulatory oversight.
- Her anxiety disorder had an underlying biological component that required medical intervention.

- **释义**: vt. 在...下面挖, 渐渐破坏, 暗地里破坏\n[法] 暗中破坏, 以阴谋中伤伤害
