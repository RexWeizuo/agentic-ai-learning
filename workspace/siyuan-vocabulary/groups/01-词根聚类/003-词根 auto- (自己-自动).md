# 词根 auto- (自己/自动)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: 词根 auto- (自己)。automatic(自动的), automation(自动化), automobile(汽车), autonomy(自治), authentic(真实的), author(作者)。

**6 词**

---

## automatic

- **音标**: .ɒ:tә'mætik
- **释义**: ==n. 自动手枪, 自动机械\na. 自动的, 机械的, 必然的, 无意识的==
- **英文释义**: operating independently of human control or conscious thought
- **同义词**: mechanical, self-operating
- **反义词**: manual
- **词根词缀**: auto(自己)+mat(动)+ic→自己动的→自动的
- **例句**:
- The factory has adopted automatic assembly lines to improve efficiency.
- She reacted with automatic suspicion when asked about her past experience.
- **考频**: 40
## automation

- **音标**: .ɒ:tә'meiʃәn
- **释义**: ==n. 自动化, 自动操作\n[计] 自动学; 自动化==
- **英文释义**: The use of machines, computers, or robots to do work that was previously done by people.
- **词根词缀**: auto(self)+mat(act)+ion(noun)→self-acting→自动化
- **例句**:
- The rapid advancement of automation has transformed manufacturing industries worldwide.
- Many economists warn that widespread automation may displace low-skilled workers and widen income inequality.
- **考频**: 65
## automobile

- **音标**: 'ɒ:tәmәbi:l
- **释义**: ==n. 汽车\n[机] 汽车==
- **英文释义**: a road vehicle with four wheels, powered by an internal combustion engine or electric motor, used for transporting people
- **词根词缀**: auto(自己)+mobile(移动的)→自动移动的车→汽车
- **例句**:
- The rapid development of the automobile industry has significantly transformed urban planning and commuting patterns.
- In many developed countries, the widespread ownership of automobiles has contributed to both economic growth and environmental challenges.
- **考频**: 44
## autonomy

- **音标**: ɒ:'tɒnәmi
- **释义**: ==n. 自治, 自治权\n[医] 自主性==
- **英文释义**: the right or ability to govern oneself or make independent decisions
- **词根词缀**: auto(自己)+nomy(法律)→自己立法→自治
- **例句**:
- The university has been granted greater autonomy in setting its own curriculum and hiring policies.
- Respecting patient autonomy is a fundamental principle in medical ethics, requiring informed consent before treatment.
- **考频**: 18
## authentic

- **音标**: /ɔːˈθentɪk/
- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: ==真实的，真正的；可靠的==
- **英文释义**: true to its nature or character; genuine
- **同义词**: genuine, real, legitimate, original
- **反义词**: fake, false, counterfeit
- **词根词缀**: authent(作者)+ic→原作者的→真实的
- **搭配**: authentic experience, authentic source, fully authentic
- **例句**:
- The document is authentic.
- She has an authentic talent for music.
- **考频**: 23
## author

- **音标**: 'ɒ:θә
- **释义**: ==n. 作家, 作家的著作, 创始人\n[法] 作者, 著作人, 本人==
- **英文释义**: a person who writes a book, article, or other text; the creator or originator of an idea, plan, or action
- **词根词缀**: auth(创造)+or→创造者→作者
- **例句**:
- The author's meticulous research is evident throughout the monograph.
- She is widely regarded as the author of the modern theory of cognitive development.
- **考频**: 1492
