# 词根：mun (公共，服务)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: mun 表示“公共、服务”，communicate 是“交流”，community 是“社区”，commute 是“通勤/交换”。

**4 词**

---

## communicate

- **音标**: kә'mju:nikeit
- **释义**: vt. 显露, 传达, 感染\nvi. 通讯
- **英文释义**: to share or exchange information, ideas, or feelings through speaking, writing, or using other mediums
- **词根词缀**: com(共同)+mun(服务)+ic(形容词)+ate(动词)→共同服务→交流
- **例句**:
- Effective leaders must communicate their vision clearly to inspire confidence among team members.
- Scientists use precise terminology to communicate complex findings accurately in academic journals.
- **考频**: 122
## communication

- **音标**: kә.mju:ni'keiʃәn
- **释义**: n. 交流, 交通, 通讯\n[计] 通信
- **英文释义**: The process of exchanging information, ideas, or feelings between people through speaking, writing, or using other mediums.
- **词根词缀**: com(共同)+mun(服务)+ic(形容词)+ation(名词)→共同服务→交流
- **例句**:
- Effective communication is essential for successful teamwork in academic research.
- The rapid development of digital technology has revolutionized global communication.
- **考频**: 314
## community

- **音标**: kә'mju:niti
- **释义**: n. 社区, 公众, 共有, 共同体\n[经] 公众, 共有, 社会
- **英文释义**: A group of people living in the same area or sharing common interests, values, or goals; a unified body with shared identity or purpose.
- **词根词缀**: com(共同)+mun(服务)+ity(名词)→共同服务→社区
- **例句**:
- Local governments are increasingly involving the community in urban planning decisions.
- The scientific community widely accepted the findings after peer review.
- **考频**: 392
## commute

- **音标**: kә'mju:t
- **释义**: vt. 交换, 折偿, 减轻\nvi. 代偿, 经常乘车来往
- **英文释义**: to exchange a punishment for a less severe one; to travel regularly between home and work
- **词根词缀**: com(一起)+mut(改变)→一起改变→通勤
- **例句**:
- The judge commuted his death sentence to life imprisonment.
- She commutes over an hour each way to her job in the city.
- **考频**: 21
