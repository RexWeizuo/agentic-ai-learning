# 词根 form-/fram- (形状/结构)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: 源自拉丁语 forma（形状），frame 和 framework 侧重于物理或抽象的“框架、结构”。

**7 词**

---

## formal

- **音标**: 'fɒ:mәl
- **释义**: a. 正式的, 形式的, 礼仪的, 拘于礼节的, 拘谨的\nn. 正式的社交活动
- **英文释义**: following established conventions or rules, especially in behavior, dress, or language; official and serious rather than casual
- **词根词缀**: form(形式)+al(形容词)→正式的
- **例句**:
- The conference required formal attire and adherence to a strict agenda.
- She delivered a formal apology on behalf of the company during the press briefing.
- **考频**: 116
## format

- **音标**: 'fɒ:mæt
- **释义**: n. 开本, 版式, 形式, 格式\nvt. 格式化\n[计] 格式; DOS外部命令:对磁盘进行格式化
- **英文释义**: the arrangement or structure of information, especially in a document, file, or storage device; also, to prepare a disk or drive for use by organizing it into sectors and tracks
- **词根词缀**: form(形式)+at(动词)→格式化
- **例句**:
- The report must follow the university's prescribed format for academic submissions.
- Before installing the operating system, technicians usually format the hard drive to erase all existing data and set up a new file system.
- **考频**: 34
## formation

- **音标**: fɒ:'meiʃәn
- **释义**: n. 形成, 构造, 编队\n[医] 形成, 结构
- **英文释义**: the process of forming or being formed; a particular arrangement or structure
- **词根词缀**: form(形式)+ation(名词)→形成
- **例句**:
- The formation of blood cells takes place in the bone marrow.
- Strategic military formations require precise coordination and discipline.
- **考频**: 41
## formula

- **音标**: 'fɒ:mjulә
- **释义**: n. 客套语, 公式, 准则\n[计] 公式
- **英文释义**: A fixed or standard way of expressing something; a mathematical or scientific expression showing the relationship between quantities; a set of instructions or principles for achieving a result.
- **词根词缀**: form(形式)+ula(小)→小形式→公式
- **例句**:
- The politician's speech was filled with empty formulas rather than substantive policy proposals.
- Einstein's famous formula E = mc² revolutionized our understanding of energy and mass.
- **考频**: 18
## formulate

- **音标**: 'fɒ:mjuleit
- **释义**: vt. 用公式表示, 明确叙述, 制订\n[机] 公式化, 公式表示
- **英文释义**: to express something clearly and precisely, especially in a systematic or formal way, such as in a theory, plan, or rule
- **词根词缀**: formul(公式)+ate(动词)→公式化→制定
- **例句**:
- The committee formulated a comprehensive strategy to address climate change.
- Scientists spent years observing the phenomenon before they could formulate a testable hypothesis.
- **考频**: 21
## frame

- **音标**: freim
- **释义**: n. 框, 结构, 体格\nvt. 构成, 设计, 制定, 使适合, 陷害\n[计] 框架, 图文框, 帧
- **英文释义**: A rigid structure that surrounds or supports something; the basic structure or underlying organization of a concept, system, or argument; in computing, a single complete image in a sequence of images or a unit of data transmission.
- **词根词缀**: fram(框架)+e→框架
- **例句**:
- The government's new policy was framed as a measure to promote social equity, but critics argued it would disproportionately affect small businesses.
- In digital video, each frame is displayed in rapid succession to create the illusion of motion.
- **考频**: 30
## framework

- **音标**: 'freimwә:k
- **释义**: n. 结构, 骨架, 参照标准, 准则, 观点\n[医] 构架组织
- **英文释义**: A basic structure that supports or organizes a system, concept, or activity
- **词根词缀**: frame(框架)+work(工作)→框架
- **例句**:
- The new policy provides a flexible framework for regional economic cooperation.
- Researchers developed a theoretical framework to analyze the impact of digital literacy on academic performance.
