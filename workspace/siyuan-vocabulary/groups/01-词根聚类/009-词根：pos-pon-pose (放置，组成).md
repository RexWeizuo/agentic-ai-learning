# 词根：pos/pon/pose (放置，组成)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: pose/pon 词根表示“放置”，引申为“组成、构成”，component 是“组件”，compound 是“化合物/复合词”。

**5 词**

---

## component

- **音标**: kәm'pәunәnt
- **释义**: n. 元件, 组件, 成分\na. 组成的, 构成的\n[计] 组件
- **英文释义**: A part or element that makes up a larger whole, especially one that is essential or functional.
- **词根词缀**: com(一起)+pon(放置)+ent(名词)→放在一起的东西→成分
- **例句**:
- Each component of the software system must undergo rigorous testing before integration.
- The success of the project depends on the seamless interaction of its various components.
- **考频**: 46
## compose

- **音标**: kәm'pәuz
- **释义**: vt. 组成, 写作, 作曲, 使平静\nvi. 创作, 排字\n[计] 编写
- **英文释义**: to create and write (e.g., music, literature, or software); to constitute or make up a whole; to calm oneself
- **词根词缀**: com(一起)+pos(放置)+e→放在一起→组成
- **例句**:
- The symphony was composed by a young prodigy who had never studied formally with a master.
- A diverse team of engineers composed the core development group responsible for the new AI framework.
- **考频**: 43
## composite

- **音标**: kәm'pɒzit
- **释义**: a. 合成的, 混合成的, 菊科的\nn. 合成物, 复合材料, 菊科
- **英文释义**: made up of two or more different parts or elements; compound
- **词根词缀**: com(一起)+posit(放置)+e→放在一起的→合成的
- **例句**:
- Carbon fiber reinforced polymer is a high-strength composite widely used in aerospace engineering.
- The composite nature of modern society requires interdisciplinary approaches to solve complex problems.
- **考频**: 1
## composition

- **音标**: kɒmpә'ziʃәn
- **释义**: n. 作文, 创作, 组成\n[化] 成分; 组成
- **英文释义**: the act of writing or creating something, especially a piece of music or literature; the way in which something is structured or made up
- **词根词缀**: com(一起)+posit(放置)+ion(名词)→放在一起→组成
- **例句**:
- Her composition on climate change demonstrated exceptional analytical skills and linguistic precision.
- The chemical composition of the alloy determines its strength and resistance to corrosion.
- **考频**: 226
## compound

- **音标**: kәm'paund
- **释义**: n. 混合物, 复合词, 化合物, 院子\na. 复合的, 混合的, 化合的\nvi. 化合, 和解, 妥协\nvt. 使复合, 使化合
- **英文释义**: A substance formed by chemical combination of two or more elements in fixed proportions; also, a word formed by combining two or more words, or a compound structure (e.g., enclosed yard). As an adjective: composed of two or more parts; as a verb: to combine, settle (a dispute), or intensify (a problem).
- **词根词缀**: com(一起)+pound(放置)→放在一起→化合物
- **例句**:
- Water is a chemical compound consisting of hydrogen and oxygen.
- The linguist analyzed the compound 'notebook' as a noun-noun combination rather than a phrase.
- **考频**: 37
