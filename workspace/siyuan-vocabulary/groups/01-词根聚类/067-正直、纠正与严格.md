# 正直、纠正与严格

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: rect- 意为直/正确(rectify 纠正)，rig- 意为僵硬/严格(rigid 刚性的，rigorous 严密的)。

**4 词**

---

## rectangle

- **音标**: 'rektæŋgl
- **释义**: n. 长方形, 矩形\n[化] 矩形
- **英文释义**: A four-sided flat shape with four right angles and opposite sides of equal length.
- **词根词缀**: re(再/回/相反)+tang(touch)+le→接触
- **例句**:
- The architect specified that the window should be a perfect rectangle to maximize natural light.
- In coordinate geometry, the area of a rectangle is calculated by multiplying its length by its width.
## rectify

- **音标**: 'rektifai
- **释义**: vt. 订正, 矫正, 改正, 调正, 校正, 精馏, 整流\n[化] 精馏
- **英文释义**: to correct something that is wrong or inaccurate
- **词根词缀**: rect(直)+ify(使)→使直→纠正
- **例句**:
- The company issued a statement to rectify the misinformation published in yesterday's report.
- Before submitting the final thesis, she spent two days carefully reviewing and rectifying all grammatical and factual errors.
- **考频**: 9
## rigid

- **音标**: 'ridʒid
- **释义**: a. 坚硬的, 刚性的, 严格的, 精密的, 刻板的
- **英文释义**: not able to bend or be bent; strictly fixed or unchanging
- **词根词缀**: rig(硬)+id(形容词)→硬的→僵硬的
- **例句**:
- The spacecraft's heat shield required a rigid, high-temperature-resistant material.
- Her rigid adherence to the experimental protocol ensured data reliability but limited creative problem-solving.
- **考频**: 20
## rigorous

- **音标**: 'rigәrәs
- **释义**: a. 严厉的, 严酷的, 严格的, 苛刻的, 严密的, 精确的
- **英文释义**: extremely thorough, careful, and demanding in attitude or standards
- **词根词缀**: rig(硬)+or(名词)+ous(形容词)→硬的→严格的
- **例句**:
- The study was conducted under rigorous experimental conditions to ensure validity.
- Graduate programs in physics often impose rigorous coursework and research requirements.
- **考频**: 27
