# 词根 geo- 及 ge- 易混淆

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: 前三个是 geo-（地球）词根：地理、地质、几何。后三个是 ge- 开头：细菌、手势、鬼魂。

**6 词**

---

## geography

- **音标**: dʒi'ɒgrәfi
- **释义**: n. 地理学, 地理\n[医] 地理
- **英文释义**: The study of the Earth's physical features, climate, populations, and resources, and how they interact.
- **词根词缀**: geo(地球)+graph(写)+y(名词)→关于地球的描述→地理学
- **例句**:
- Her research in human geography explores the impact of urbanization on community identity.
- Understanding regional geography is essential for analyzing global trade patterns and geopolitical risks.
- **考频**: 27
## geology

- **音标**: dʒi'ɒlәdʒi
- **释义**: n. 地质学, 地质情况
- **英文释义**: The scientific study of the Earth's structure, composition, and history, including rocks, fossils, and geological processes.
- **词根词缀**: geo(地球)+logy(学科)→地质学
- **例句**:
- Her research in geology focuses on plate tectonics and its implications for earthquake prediction.
- A solid foundation in geology is essential for understanding natural resource distribution and environmental change over geological time.
- **考频**: 12
## geometry

- **音标**: dʒi'ɒmәtri
- **释义**: n. 几何学\n[机] 几何学
- **英文释义**: A branch of mathematics concerned with the properties and relations of points, lines, surfaces, and solids.
- **词根词缀**: geo(地球)+metry(测量)→测量地球→几何学
- **例句**:
- The proof relies on fundamental principles of Euclidean geometry.
- Modern physics often requires non-Euclidean geometry to describe spacetime curvature.
- **考频**: 4
## germ

- **音标**: dʒә:m
- **释义**: n. 细菌, 种子, 生殖细胞, 根源\n[医] 芽胞, 胚, 胚芽, 病菌
- **英文释义**: a microorganism, especially one that causes disease; also used figuratively to mean the earliest stage or origin of something
- **词根词缀**: germ(芽)→微生物
- **例句**:
- The lab test confirmed the presence of harmful germs in the water sample.
- Her innovative idea was the germ of a much larger project that later won national recognition.
- **考频**: 5
## gesture

- **音标**: 'dʒestʃә
- **释义**: n. 手势, 姿态\nvi. 作手势, 作姿态
- **英文释义**: a movement of part of the body, especially the hands or head, to express an idea or feeling
- **词根词缀**: gest(携带)+ure(名词)→携带的动作→手势
- **例句**:
- She made a dismissive gesture with her hand when he suggested the plan again.
- The diplomat's conciliatory gesture—offering a public apology—helped ease tensions between the two countries.
- **考频**: 41
## ghost

- **音标**: gәust
- **释义**: n. 鬼, 灵魂, 幻影, 一丝, 一点\nv. 鬼似地游荡
- **英文释义**: a spirit of a dead person, especially one believed to appear to the living; or something that resembles a ghost in being faint, elusive, or insubstantial
- **词根词缀**: ghost(鬼)→鬼魂
- **例句**:
- The old mansion was said to be haunted by the ghost of its former owner.
- She felt a ghost of her former confidence return as she stepped onto the stage.
- **考频**: 11
