# 词根：circ/circum (圆，环，周围)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: circ/circum 表示“圆、环、周围”，circumstance 原指“周围的情况”，引申为“环境、状况”。

**6 词**

---

## circle

- **音标**: 'sә:kl
- **释义**: n. 圆周, 社交圈, 循环, 范围\nvt. 围着, 环绕\nvi. 盘旋, 循环
- **英文释义**: A round plane figure whose boundary consists of points equidistant from a fixed point; also used to denote a group of people with shared interests or relationships, or a recurring process.
- **词根词缀**: circ(圆)+le(名词后缀)→圆
- **例句**:
- The debate circled back to the original question after hours of discussion.
- She moved in elite academic circles, regularly attending international conferences.
- **考频**: 105
## circuit

- **音标**: 'sә:kit
- **释义**: n. 电路, 环(行)道, 巡回\n[计] 线路; 电路
- **英文释义**: A closed path through which an electric current can flow; also, a regular route or series of places visited in sequence.
- **词根词缀**: circ(圆)+uit(走)→绕圈走→电路
- **例句**:
- The short circuit caused the entire power system to shut down immediately.
- As a federal judge, she serves on the Ninth Circuit Court of Appeals, covering several western states.
- **考频**: 25
## circular

- **音标**: /ˈsɜːrkjələr/
- **词性**: adj.
- **释义**: 圆形的；循环的；绕圈子的
- **英文释义**: shaped like a circle; moving in a circle; (of reasoning) using the conclusion as a premise
- **同义词**: round, cyclic, rotary
- **反义词**: linear, rectangular
- **词根词缀**: circ(圆)+ular→圆形的
- **搭配**: circular reasoning, circular motion, circular economy
- **例句**:
- They sat around a circular table.
- The argument is circular and proves nothing.
- **考频**: 5
## circulate

- **音标**: 'sә:kjuleit
- **释义**: v. (使)流通, (使)循环, (使)传播
- **英文释义**: to move continuously through a system, area, or group of people
- **词根词缀**: circ(圆)+ul(小)+ate(动词)→使成小圆→循环
- **例句**:
- The government took measures to circulate more money into the economy.
- Rumors about the merger began to circulate among employees before the official announcement.
- **考频**: 14
## circumstance

- **音标**: 'sә:kәmstәns
- **释义**: n. 环境, 状况, 事件
- **英文释义**: a fact or condition connected with an event or situation
- **词根词缀**: circum(周围)+st(站)+ance(名词)→站在周围的东西→环境
- **例句**:
- Under no circumstances should you reveal confidential information to unauthorized personnel.
- Her success was remarkable, given the difficult circumstances in which she grew up.
- **考频**: 108
## circus

- **音标**: 'sә:kәs
- **释义**: n. 马戏团, 马戏, 竞技场
- **英文释义**: A traveling company of performers, including acrobats, clowns, and trained animals, who give shows in a large tent or arena.
- **词根词缀**: circ(圆)+us(名词)→圆形场地→马戏团
- **例句**:
- The children were thrilled by the dazzling acrobatics and comic routines at the circus.
- Critics argue that using wild animals in the circus raises serious ethical concerns.
- **考频**: 3
