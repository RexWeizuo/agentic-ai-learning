# 定义、明确、限制

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: 共享词根 -fin- (边界/结束)，confine 指限制在特定边界内。

**4 词**

---

## define

- **音标**: di'fain
- **释义**: vt. 定义, 规定, 使明确\n[计] 定义
- **英文释义**: to state the exact meaning of a word, concept, or term; to specify clearly
- **词根词缀**: de(加强)+fin(界限)+e→加强界限→定义
- **例句**:
- The textbook defines 'algorithm' as a finite sequence of well-defined instructions.
- In the contract, the responsibilities of each party are carefully defined to avoid ambiguity.
- **考频**: 188
## definite

- **音标**: 'definit
- **释义**: a. 明确的, 一定的\n[法] 明确的, 确切的, 一定的
- **英文释义**: clearly defined or certain; not vague or doubtful
- **词根词缀**: de(加强)+fin(界限)+ite(形容词)→有界限的→明确的
- **例句**:
- There is no definite evidence linking the two events.
- She gave a definite answer, leaving no room for ambiguity.
- **考频**: 30
## definition

- **音标**: .defi'niʃәn
- **释义**: n. 定义, 精确度, 清晰度\n[计] 清晰度; 清晰度
- **英文释义**: A statement of the exact meaning of a word, phrase, or concept.
- **词根词缀**: de(加强)+fin(界限)+ition(名词)→界限→定义
- **例句**:

## confine

- **音标**: kәn'fain
- **释义**: vt. 限制, 使不外出, 禁闭\nvi. 邻接, 交界\nn. 边缘, 范围, 区域
- **英文释义**: to restrict within limits; to keep within a certain area or condition
- **词根词缀**: con(共同)+fin(界限)+e→共同界限→限制
- **例句**:
- The government confined the outbreak to a single province through strict quarantine measures.
- Her research is confined to 19th-century British literature, excluding American works.
- **考频**: 37
