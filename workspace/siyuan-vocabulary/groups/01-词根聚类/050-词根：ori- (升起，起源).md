# 词根：ori- (升起，起源)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: 共享词根 ori-，表示“升起、起源、东方”。

**6 词**

---

## orient

- **音标**: 'ɒ:riәnt
- **释义**: n. 东方, 东方诸国\na. 东方的\nvi. 向东\nvt. 使朝东, 使适应, 确定方向
- **英文释义**: to adjust or align oneself or something to a particular situation, environment, or direction; to determine one's position relative to surroundings
- **词根词缀**: ori(升起)+ent(名词/动词后缀)→太阳升起的地方→东方
- **例句**:
- Students must orient themselves to the rigorous demands of graduate-level research.
- The new policy aims to orient the company's strategy toward sustainable development.
- **考频**: 46
## oriental

- **音标**: .ɒ:ri'entl
- **释义**: n. 东方人, 亚洲人\na. 东方诸国的, 亚洲的, 东方的
- **英文释义**: relating to the East, especially East Asia; historically used to refer to people or things from Asia, though now often considered outdated or potentially offensive when referring to people
- **词根词缀**: orient(东方)+al(形容词后缀)→东方的
- **例句**:
- The museum's collection includes oriental ceramics dating back to the Ming Dynasty.
- Scholars caution against using 'oriental' as a noun to describe people, recommending 'Asian' or specific national identifiers instead.
- **考频**: 8
## orientation

- **音标**: .ɒ:rien'teiʃәn
- **释义**: n. 定方位, 适应, 向东方\nn. 方向\n[计] 方向
- **英文释义**: The process of familiarizing someone with a new situation, environment, or role; also, the direction or position of something relative to its surroundings.
- **词根词缀**: orient(东方)+ation(名词)→定向
- **例句**:
- The university holds an orientation week to help freshmen adjust to campus life.
- Before deploying the satellite, engineers carefully calibrated its spatial orientation to ensure accurate data collection.
- **考频**: 15
## origin

- **音标**: 'ɒridʒin
- **释义**: n. 起源, 起因, 出身, 开端\n[计] 原点; 起始地址; 信件来源的相关数据
- **英文释义**: the point or place where something begins, arises, or is derived
- **词根词缀**: ori(升起)+gin(产生)→起源
- **例句**:
- The origin of the universe remains one of the greatest questions in modern physics.
- Her academic success can be traced back to her humble origin and relentless dedication.
- **考频**: 97
## original

- **音标**: ә'ridʒәnl
- **释义**: a. 最初的, 原始的, 有创意的\nn. 原物, 原作
- **英文释义**: existing from the beginning; not copied or imitated; showing fresh, inventive thinking
- **词根词缀**: origin(起源)+al(形容词后缀)→原始的
- **例句**:
- The museum displayed the original manuscript of the novel, not a later reproduction.
- Her original approach to data analysis impressed the thesis committee and led to a publication.
- **考频**: 120
## originate

- **音标**: ә'ridʒineit
- **释义**: vt. 创始, 发明, 发起\nvi. 发源, 发生, 起航\n[计] 发自
- **英文释义**: to begin or start something; to have its source or origin in a particular place, person, or thing
- **词根词缀**: origin(起源)+ate(动词后缀)→起源
- **例句**:
- The Industrial Revolution originated in Britain in the late 18th century.
- This innovative teaching methodology originated from a collaborative research project at Tsinghua University.
- **考频**: 48
