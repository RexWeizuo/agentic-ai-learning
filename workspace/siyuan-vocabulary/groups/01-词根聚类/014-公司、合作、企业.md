# 公司、合作、企业

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: 共享词根 -corpor- (身体/团体)，引申为公司与合作。

**4 词**

---

## corporate

- **音标**: 'kɒ:pәrit
- **释义**: a. 社团的, 合伙的, 公司的\n[经] 团体的, 法人的, 社团的
- **英文释义**: relating to a large company or corporation, especially in terms of its structure, governance, or activities
- **词根词缀**: corpor(身体)+ate(形容词)→团体的→公司的
- **例句**:
- The corporate headquarters is located in downtown New York.
- She resigned from her position due to ethical concerns about corporate governance.
- **考频**: 133
## corporation

- **音标**: .kɒ:pә'reiʃәn
- **释义**: n. 公司, 合作, 法人团体\n[法] 法人团体, 社团, 法人
- **英文释义**: A large company or group of companies authorized to act as a single entity and legally recognized as such.
- **词根词缀**: corpor(身体)+ation(名词)→团体→公司
- **例句**:
- The multinational corporation announced plans to expand its operations into Southeast Asia.
- Under U.S. law, a corporation is considered a legal person with rights and responsibilities distinct from its shareholders.
- **考频**: 95
## cooperate

- **音标**: kәu'ɒpәreit
- **释义**: vi. 合作, 协力, 配合\n[化] 合作
- **英文释义**: to work together with others toward a common goal
- **词根词缀**: co(共同)+oper(工作)+ate(动词)→共同工作→合作
- **例句**:
- Effective teamwork requires all members to cooperate fully and communicate openly.
- The two countries agreed to cooperate on climate change mitigation and share clean energy technologies.
- **考频**: 25
## cooperative

- **音标**: kәu'ɔpәrәtiv
- **释义**: a. 合作的；合作社的
- **英文释义**: willing to work together with others; characterized by cooperation
- **词根词缀**: co(共同)+oper(工作)+ative(形容词)→合作的
- **例句**:
- The project succeeded due to the team's highly cooperative attitude and effective communication.
- China has promoted the development of rural cooperative economies to enhance farmers' collective bargaining power.
- **考频**: 17
