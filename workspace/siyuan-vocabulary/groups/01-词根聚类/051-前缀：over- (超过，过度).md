# 前缀：over- (超过，过度)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: 共享前缀 over-，表示“在...之上、超过、过度”。

**15 词**

---

## overall

- **音标**: 'әuvәrɒ:l
- **释义**: a. 全部的, 全体的, 从头至尾的, 一切在内的\nadv. 从头到尾, 总的说来\nn. 罩衫, 工作服
- **英文释义**: considering all aspects; in total; a protective garment worn over regular clothes
- **词根词缀**: over(全部)+all(所有)→全部的
- **例句**:
- Overall, the study's findings challenge the traditional assumptions about cognitive development.
- The factory workers wore blue overalls to protect their clothing from oil and dirt.
- **考频**: 137

- **考频**: 47
