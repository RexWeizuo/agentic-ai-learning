# sym/syn- 前缀 (共同/相同)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: sym-/syn- 表示“共同、相同”。symposium (研讨会) 和 synthesis (综合) 常出现在学术阅读中。

**6 词**

---

## symmetry

- **音标**: 'simitri
- **释义**: n. 对称, 匀称\n[化] 对称; 对称性; 对称现象
- **英文释义**: balanced proportions or arrangement of parts on either side of a dividing line or plane, or around a center
- **词根词缀**: sym(相同)+metry(测量)→相同测量→对称
- **例句**:
- The symmetry of the building's façade reflects classical architectural principles.
- In molecular chemistry, symmetry determines whether a compound is optically active.
- **考频**: 1
## sympathetic

- **音标**: .simpә'θetik
- **释义**: a. 有同情心的, 合意的, 赞成的\nn. 交感神经, 容易感受的人
- **英文释义**: feeling or showing compassion and understanding for others' suffering; supportive or in agreement with a cause or person
- **词根词缀**: sym(相同)+path(感情)+etic(形容词)→相同感情的→同情的
- **例句**:
- Her sympathetic response to the refugee crisis prompted her to volunteer at a local shelter.
- The judge was sympathetic to the defendant's difficult background but upheld the law.
- **考频**: 37
## sympathise

- **音标**: 'simpәθaiz
- **释义**: vi. 同情, 表示同情, 同感, 一致, 共鸣, 同意, 赞同, 赞成, 吊唁, 吊慰
- **英文释义**: to feel or express sorrow or compassion for someone else's suffering
- **词根词缀**: sym(共同/相同)+path(feel/suffer/disease)→感受/病
- **例句**:
- She deeply sympathised with the refugees' plight after reading the harrowing reports.
- While he disagreed with their policy, he sympathised with their underlying concerns about economic inequality.
## symphony

- **音标**: 'simfәni
- **释义**: n. 交响乐, 交响曲
- **英文释义**: A long musical composition for full orchestra, typically in multiple movements.
- **词根词缀**: sym(相同)+phon(声音)+y(名词)→相同声音→交响乐
- **例句**:
- Beethoven's Seventh Symphony is renowned for its rhythmic vitality and emotional depth.
- The conductor led a powerful interpretation of Mahler's Second Symphony, drawing critical acclaim at the international music festival.
- **考频**: 5
## symposium

- **音标**: sim'pәuziәm
- **释义**: n. 讨论会, 座谈会, 专题报告会, 专题论文集
- **英文释义**: A formal meeting where experts deliver talks and discuss a specific academic or professional topic.
- **词根词缀**: sym(相同)+pos(放)+ium(场所)→放在一起的场所→研讨会
- **例句**:
- The international symposium on climate change brought together researchers from over 30 countries.
- Her paper was selected for publication in the symposium proceedings on artificial intelligence ethics.
- **考频**: 4
## synthesis

- **音标**: 'sinθisis
- **释义**: n. 综合, 组织, 综合体\n[化] 合成
- **英文释义**: The combination of ideas, elements, or substances to form a coherent whole; in chemistry, the production of a compound by chemical reaction.
- **词根词缀**: syn(一起)+thesis(放置)→放在一起→合成
- **例句**:
- Her thesis presents a sophisticated synthesis of economic theory and empirical data.
- The development of new pharmaceuticals often relies on the precise synthesis of complex organic molecules.
- **考频**: 3
