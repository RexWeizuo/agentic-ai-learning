# 词根：prehend/prise (抓，理解，包含)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: prehend/prise 原意为“抓”，引申为“大脑抓住（理解）”或“整体抓住（包含）”。

**4 词**

---

## comprehend

- **音标**: .kɒmpri'hend
- **释义**: vt. 理解, 包括
- **英文释义**: to understand something fully, especially something complex or abstract
- **词根词缀**: com(一起)+prehend(抓住)→一起抓住→理解
- **例句**:
- It is difficult to comprehend the vast scale of the universe.
- The legal implications of the new regulation are not immediately comprehended by most practitioners.
- **考频**: 14
## comprehension

- **音标**: .kɒmpri'henʃәn
- **释义**: n. 理解, 包含
- **英文释义**: the ability to understand something, especially a language or a complex idea
- **词根词缀**: com(一起)+prehens(抓住)+ion(名词)→一起抓住→理解
- **例句**:
- Her reading comprehension improved significantly after intensive practice with academic texts.
- The TOEFL test includes a section that assesses listening and reading comprehension of university-level materials.
- **考频**: 673
## comprehensive

- **音标**: .kɒmpri'hensiv
- **释义**: a. 广泛的, 有理解力的, 综合的\n[经] 广泛的, 综合的, 全面的
- **英文释义**: covering or including everything; thorough and complete
- **词根词缀**: com(一起)+prehens(抓住)+ive(形容词)→能抓住的→全面的
- **例句**:
- The report provides a comprehensive analysis of the economic impact of climate change.
- To succeed in the exam, students need a comprehensive understanding of both grammar and vocabulary.
- **考频**: 41
## comprise

- **音标**: kәm'praiz
- **释义**: vt. 包含, 构成
- **英文释义**: to consist of or be made up of (parts, elements, or members)
- **词根词缀**: com(一起)+prise(拿)→拿在一起→包含
- **例句**:
- The committee comprises five experts from different academic disciplines.
- A typical smartphone comprises a processor, memory, display, and battery.
- **考频**: 11
