# 前缀：out- (向外，超过)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: 共享前缀 out-，表示“向外、超过、外部”。

**12 词**

---

## outbreak

- **音标**: 'autbreik
- **释义**: n. 爆发, 暴动\n[医] 暴发
- **英文释义**: a sudden occurrence of something unwelcome, such as a disease, violence, or conflict
- **词根词缀**: out(向外)+break(打破)→爆发
- **例句**:
- The government imposed strict quarantine measures following the outbreak of avian flu in the region.
- Historians attribute the outbreak of the civil war to long-standing political and economic tensions.
- **考频**: 10
## outcome

- **音标**: 'autkʌm
- **释义**: n. 结果, 出口
- **英文释义**: the result or consequence of an action, process, or event
- **词根词缀**: out(向外)+come(来)→结果
- **例句**:
- The outcome of the clinical trial was highly encouraging, showing significant improvement in patient recovery rates.
- Policy makers emphasized that long-term economic stability depends not only on fiscal measures but also on the social outcomes they generate.
- **考频**: 95

- **音标**: 'autfit

- **词根词缀**: out(外)+fit(适合)→装备
- **例句**:

- The startup was quickly outfitted with state-of-the-art lab equipment and software.
- **考频**: 7
