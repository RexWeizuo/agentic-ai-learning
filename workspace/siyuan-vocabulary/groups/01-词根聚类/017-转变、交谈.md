# 转变、交谈

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: 共享词根 -vers- (转)，conversation 原指“来回交谈”，conversely 指“相反地”。

**4 词**

---

## conversation

- **音标**: .kɒnvә'seiʃәn
- **释义**: n. 会话, 说话, 交谈\n[法] 交谈, 社交, 性交
- **英文释义**: an informal exchange of ideas or thoughts between two or more people
- **词根词缀**: con(一起)+vers(转)+ation(名词)→一起转动→对话
- **例句**:
- The conversation quickly shifted from politics to personal experiences.
- She impressed the interviewers with her fluency and ability to sustain a thoughtful conversation on global economics.
- **考频**: 1254
## conversely

- **音标**: 'kɔnv\\\\:sli
- **释义**: adv. 相反, 逆, 颠倒, 反题, 反
- **英文释义**: In a contrary or opposite way; used to introduce a statement that reverses or contrasts with the previous one.
- **词根词缀**: con(加强)+vers(转)+ely(副词)→转向→相反地
- **例句**:
- The government aims to reduce inflation; conversely, its stimulus policies may fuel it.
- While high academic achievement often correlates with strong analytical skills, conversely, some highly creative individuals excel without formal credentials.
- **考频**: 10
## conversion

- **音标**: kәn'vә:ʃәn
- **释义**: n. 转变, 转换, 改变宗教信仰, 换位法\n[计] 转换
- **英文释义**: The process of changing from one form, state, or belief to another
- **词根词缀**: con(加强)+vers(转)+ion(名词)→转变→转换
- **例句**:
- The conversion of coal to electricity involves several energy-loss stages.
- Her conversion to Buddhism marked a profound shift in her philosophical outlook.
- **考频**: 6
## convert

- **音标**: kәn'vә:t
- **释义**: n. 皈依者, 改变宗教信仰者\nvt. 使改变信仰, 转换, 兑换, 倒置\nvi. 皈依\n[计] 转换
- **英文释义**: to change from one belief, form, or function to another; to change someone's religious or political beliefs
- **词根词缀**: con(加强)+vert(转)→转变→转换
- **例句**:
- The scientist converted her research data into a readable graph.
- After years of philosophical inquiry, he converted to Buddhism.
- **考频**: 49
