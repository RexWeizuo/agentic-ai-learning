# 词根：opt- (选择)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: 共享词根 opt-，表示“选择、最佳”。注意与 optical(眼睛) 区分。

**4 词**

---

## opt

- **音标**: ɒpt
- **释义**: vi. 选择
- **英文释义**: to make a choice, especially from a set of options
- **词根词缀**: opt(选择)→选择
- **例句**:
- Many students opt for online courses to balance work and study.
- When faced with multiple career paths, she opted to pursue research in renewable energy.
- **考频**: 24
## option

- **音标**: 'ɒpʃәn
- **释义**: n. 选择权, 挑选, 选项\n[计] 选项
- **英文释义**: A right or ability to choose from available alternatives
- **词根词缀**: opt(选择)+ion(名词)→选择
- **例句**:
- Students have the option to take either the online or in-person exam.
- The software provides several configuration options, allowing users to customize their experience.
- **考频**: 159
## optional

- **音标**: 'ɒpʃәnl
- **释义**: a. 可选择的, 随意的\n[化] 选购的
- **英文释义**: not required; left to one's choice
- **词根词缀**: opt(选择)+ion(名词)+al(形容词)→可选的
- **例句**:
- Attendance at the seminar is optional, but highly recommended for graduate students.
- The advanced statistics module is an optional course for economics majors.
- **考频**: 15
## optimum

- **音标**: 'ɒptimәm
- **释义**: n. 最适条件, 最佳效果\na. 最优的, 最适宜的
- **英文释义**: the most favorable condition or degree for a process or system to function effectively
- **词根词缀**: optim(最好)+um→最佳状态
- **例句**:
- The enzyme exhibits maximum activity at its optimum temperature and pH.
- Achieving the optimum balance between cost efficiency and product quality remains a key challenge in manufacturing.
- **考频**: 6
