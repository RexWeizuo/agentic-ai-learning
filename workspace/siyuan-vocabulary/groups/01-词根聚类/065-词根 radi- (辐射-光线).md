# 词根 radi- (辐射/光线)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: radi- 表示光线、辐射，注意 radioactive 和 radius 的拼写与衍生义。

**4 词**

---

## radiant

- **音标**: 'reidiәnt
- **释义**: a. 发光的, 明亮的, 辐射的, 容光焕发的\n[医] 放射的, 辐射的, 辐射物[质]
- **英文释义**: emitting light or energy; shining brightly; full of health and happiness
- **词根词缀**: radi(光线)+ant(形容词)→发光的
- **例句**:
- Her radiant smile lit up the entire room during the conference presentation.
- The sun's radiant energy is harnessed by solar panels to generate electricity.
- **考频**: 1
## radiate

- **音标**: 'reidieit
- **释义**: vt. 放射, 散发, 辐射, 传播, 广播\nvi. 发光, 辐射, 流露\na. 有射线的, 辐射状的
- **英文释义**: to emit energy, light, heat, or emotion outward from a source; to spread or convey widely
- **词根词缀**: radi(光线)+ate(动词)→辐射
- **例句**:
- The sun radiates heat and light across the solar system.
- Her confidence and warmth radiated throughout the room during the presentation.
- **考频**: 7
## radioactive

- **音标**: .reidiәu'æktiv
- **释义**: a. 放射性的, 有放射能的\n[医] 放射性的, 放射的
- **英文释义**: emitting radiation, especially alpha, beta, or gamma particles, as a result of nuclear decay
- **词根词缀**: radi(光线)+o+act(行动)+ive(形容词)→放射性的
- **例句**:
- The disposal of radioactive waste remains a major environmental challenge for nuclear power plants.
- Doctors used a radioactive tracer to monitor the patient's thyroid function in real time.
- **考频**: 1
## radius

- **音标**: 'reidiәs
- **释义**: n. 半径, 范围, 辐射光线\n[电] 半径
- **英文释义**: A straight line from the center of a circle or sphere to its circumference or surface; also used figuratively to denote the scope or reach of influence, activity, or effect.
- **词根词缀**: radi(光线)+us(名词)→半径
- **例句**:
- The radius of the circle is half its diameter, a fundamental concept in geometry.
- The new policy expanded the company's operational radius to include emerging markets in Southeast Asia.
- **考频**: 4
