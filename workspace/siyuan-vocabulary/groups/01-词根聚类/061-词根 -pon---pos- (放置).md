# 词根 -pon-/-pos- (放置)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: 表示“放置、位置”。postpone(向后放/延迟), posture(姿势), possess(占有/放置在自己手中)。

**5 词**

---

## pose

- **音标**: pәuz
- **释义**: n. 姿势, 姿态, 装模作样, 伪装\nvi. 摆姿势, 装模作样, 假装\nvt. 使摆好姿势, 提出, 造成
- **英文释义**: to stand or sit in a particular position, especially for a photograph or painting; to present or raise (a question, problem, or challenge); to pretend or behave artificially
- **词根词缀**: pos(放置)+e→放置→姿势
- **例句**:
- The candidate posed a difficult question about climate policy during the debate.
- She posed confidently before the camera, but her stiff posture betrayed her nervousness.
- **考频**: 80
## postpone

- **音标**: pәust'pәun
- **释义**: vt. 延迟, 使延期, 缓办, 搁延\nvi. 延缓, 延缓发作
- **英文释义**: to delay or put off an action, event, or decision to a later time
- **词根词缀**: post(后)+pone(放)→放到后面→推迟
- **例句**:
- The board decided to postpone the annual meeting until next month due to the CEO's sudden illness.
- Many students postpone writing their thesis until the final weeks, which often leads to unnecessary stress and lower quality work.
- **考频**: 19
## posture

- **音标**: 'pɒstʃә
- **释义**: n. 姿势, 态度, 情形, 形势\nvt. 作...的姿势\nvi. 作姿势
- **英文释义**: the position of the body when standing or sitting; also used figuratively to refer to a person's attitude or stance on an issue
- **词根词缀**: post(放置)+ure(名词)→放置的姿势→姿态
- **例句**:
- Her confident posture during the debate impressed the panel.
- The government adopted a more conciliatory posture toward climate policy after the international summit.
- **考频**: 7
## positive

- **音标**: 'pɒzitiv
- **释义**: a. 肯定的, 积极的, 有把握的\n[医] 正的, 阳性的
- **英文释义**: expressing agreement or affirmation; showing hope, confidence, or optimism; indicating the presence of a condition or substance (in medical contexts)
- **词根词缀**: posit(放置)+ive(形容词)→放置好的→积极的
- **例句**:
- The research yielded several positive outcomes that support the hypothesis.
- Despite the challenging circumstances, she maintained a positive attitude throughout the interview.
- **考频**: 237
## possess

- **音标**: pә'zes
- **释义**: vt. 持有, 占有, 拥有, 克制, 支配, 迷住\n[法] 持有, 占有, 具有
- **英文释义**: to have or own something, especially as a legal right; to control or dominate someone or something
- **词根词缀**: pos(能够)+sess(坐)→能够坐拥→拥有
- **例句**:
- The defendant failed to prove he possessed the property legally.
- Her calm demeanor and sharp intellect possessed an authority that silenced even the most skeptical critics.
- **考频**: 79
