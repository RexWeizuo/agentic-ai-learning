# 词根 mem (记忆/成员)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: mem 词根，涵盖记忆、备忘录及成员

**4 词**

---

## memory

- **音标**: 'memәri
- **释义**: n. 记忆, 记忆力, 回忆, 纪念, 存储\nn. 内存\n[计] 存储器, 内存, 查看内存实用程序
- **英文释义**: the ability to store, retain, and recall information or experiences
- **词根词缀**: memor(记忆)+y(名词)→记忆
- **例句**:
- Her exceptional memory enabled her to recite the entire poem after hearing it just once.
- Modern computers rely on high-speed memory to process large datasets efficiently.
- **考频**: 310
## memorial

- **音标**: mi'mɒ:riәl
- **释义**: n. 纪念物, 请愿书\na. 记念的, 记忆的
- **英文释义**: A structure, object, or ceremony intended to honor and remember a person or event; or a formal written statement of facts and requests submitted to an authority.
- **词根词缀**: memor(记忆)+ial(形容词)→纪念的
- **例句**:
- The Vietnam Veterans Memorial in Washington, D.C., is a powerful tribute to those who served and died in the war.
- The human rights organization submitted a detailed memorial to the United Nations outlining systemic abuses and calling for urgent intervention.
- **考频**: 5
## memorandum

- **音标**: .memә'rændәm
- **释义**: n. 备忘录, 外交备忘录, 交易备忘录\n[经] 备忘录, 便笺, 便函
- **英文释义**: An informal written record or communication, often used in business, law, or diplomacy to document decisions, agreements, or instructions.
- **词根词缀**: memor(记忆)+andum(东西)→备忘录
- **例句**:
- The legal team circulated a memorandum outlining the key terms of the merger agreement.
- Diplomats exchanged memoranda to clarify their respective positions before the summit.
- **考频**: 7
## member

- **音标**: 'membә
- **释义**: n. 成员, 会员\n[医] │肢, 肢体
- **英文释义**: a person who belongs to a group, organization, or institution
- **词根词缀**: mem(记忆)+ber(人)→成员
- **例句**:
- All members of the research team contributed to the final report.
- As a long-standing member of the British Medical Association, she was invited to speak at the annual conference.
- **考频**: 356
