# 词根 flu-/fluct- (流动)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: flu- 表示“流动”，fluent 指语言流利，fluid 指流体，fluctuate 指像波浪一样起伏波动。

**4 词**

---

## fluent

- **音标**: 'flu:әnt
- **释义**: a. 流利的, 流畅的
- **英文释义**: able to speak or write a language easily and accurately
- **词根词缀**: flu(流)+ent(形容词)→流动的→流利的
- **例句**:
- She is fluent in both English and Mandarin, which gave her a significant advantage in the international negotiation.
- To pass the CET-6 speaking test, candidates must demonstrate fluent and coherent expression on academic topics.
- **考频**: 4
## fluid

- **音标**: 'flu:id
- **释义**: n. 液体, 分泌液, 流体\na. 流动的, 可改变的
- **英文释义**: A substance that flows freely and has no fixed shape, such as a liquid or gas; also refers to bodily secretions like blood or lymph.
- **词根词缀**: flu(流)+id(形容词)→流动的→流体
- **例句**:
- The experiment requires precise measurement of the fluid flow rate through the narrow tube.
- Political alliances in the region remain highly fluid, with shifting coalitions reflecting changing strategic interests.
- **考频**: 13
## flush

- **音标**: flʌʃ
- **释义**: n. 流溢, 面红, 旺盛, 发烧, 惊飞的鸟, 同花牌\na. 丰足的, 齐平的, 满盈的, 泛滥的\nadv. 齐平地, 直接地\nvi. 脸发红, 奔涌, 旺盛, 惊飞\nvt. 淹没, 冲洗, 使脸红, 使激动, 使齐平, 使惊飞
- **英文释义**: To suddenly become red in the face, especially from embarrassment or excitement; or to flow or rush out forcefully; also used as a noun for a sudden surge, reddening, or a state of abundance.
- **词根词缀**: flush(冲洗)→冲洗,脸红
- **例句**:
- She flushed with embarrassment when she realized she had mispronounced the word in front of the entire class.
- The government flushed out corrupt officials by launching a nationwide anti-graft campaign.
- **考频**: 8
## fluctuate

- **音标**: 'flʌktʃueit
- **释义**: vi. 变动, 起伏, 动摇\nvt. 使波动, 使动摇
- **英文释义**: to rise and fall irregularly in number, amount, or intensity
- **词根词缀**: fluct(流动)+uate(动词)→流动→波动
- **例句**:
- Stock prices tend to fluctuate sharply in response to geopolitical events.
- Her confidence fluctuated throughout the presentation, especially when answering difficult questions.
- **考频**: 12
