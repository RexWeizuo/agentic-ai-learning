# 词根 liber/libr (自由/书)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: liber (自由) 与 libr (书) 词根，license 为形近词

**4 词**

---

## liberal

- **音标**: 'libәrәl
- **释义**: n. 自由主义者\na. 慷慨的, 不拘泥的, 宽大的, 自由主义的
- **英文释义**: 1. (n.) a person who supports individual rights, democratic reform, and progressive social policies; 2. (adj.) generous in giving or sharing; not strict or literal in interpretation; open-minded and tolerant; advocating liberal political principles
- **词根词缀**: liber(自由)+al(形容词)→自由的→自由主义的
- **例句**:
- Her liberal donation to the scholarship fund enabled five students to attend college.
- The court adopted a liberal interpretation of the constitutional clause to protect minority rights.
- **考频**: 85
## liberty

- **音标**: 'libәli
- **释义**: n. 自由, 特权, 许可, 冒失\n[法] 自由, 自由权, 自由区域
- **英文释义**: The right and power to act, speak, or think as one wants without hindrance or restraint
- **词根词缀**: liber(自由)+ty(名词)→自由
- **例句**:
- The First Amendment guarantees freedom of speech as a fundamental liberty.
- While liberty is essential to democracy, it must sometimes be balanced against public safety.
- **考频**: 12
## library

- **音标**: 'laibrәri
- **释义**: n. 图书馆, 藏书, 库\n[计] 库
- **英文释义**: A building or room containing collections of books, periodicals, and sometimes electronic resources for use or borrowing.
- **词根词缀**: libr(书)+ary(场所)→书的场所→图书馆
- **例句**:
- The university library houses over two million volumes, including rare manuscripts and digital archives.
- In modern software development, a well-documented library can significantly reduce redundant coding efforts.
- **考频**: 159
## license

- **音标**: 'laisns
- **释义**: n. 执照, 许可证, 特许\nvt. 许可, 特许
- **英文释义**: An official document giving permission to do something, especially one issued by a government or authorized body.
- **词根词缀**: lic(允许)+ense(名词)→允许→许可证
- **例句**:
- All drivers must hold a valid license to operate a motor vehicle in this country.
- The pharmaceutical company was licensed to manufacture the new vaccine after passing rigorous regulatory reviews.
- **考频**: 62
