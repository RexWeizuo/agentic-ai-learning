# er- 消除/错误/侵蚀

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: er- 开头，多与消除、错误或爆发相关

**7 词**

## eradicate

- **音标**: i'rædikeit
- **释义**: vt. 根除, 扑灭, 根绝, 消灭
- **英文释义**: to completely destroy or get rid of something, especially something harmful or unwanted
- **词根词缀**: e(出)+radic(根)+ate(动词)→连根拔出→根除
- **例句**:
- The government launched a nationwide campaign to eradicate poverty in rural areas.
- Scientists are working to eradicate the invasive species before it disrupts the local ecosystem.
- **考频**: 5
## erase

- **音标**: i'reis
- **释义**: vt. 抹去, 擦掉\n[计] 清洗; 擦除; 抹除; DOS内部命令:从磁盘上删除一个或多个文件
- **英文释义**: to remove something written, drawn, or stored, especially by rubbing, deleting, or overwriting
- **词根词缀**: e(出)+ras(刮)+e→刮掉→擦除
- **例句**:
- The teacher asked the student to erase the incorrect answer from the blackboard.
- Before handing in the essay, she carefully erased all traces of informal language to meet academic standards.
- **考频**: 12
## erode

- **音标**: i'rәud
- **释义**: vt. 腐蚀, 侵蚀\nvi. 受腐蚀
- **英文释义**: to gradually destroy or wear away something, especially by natural forces such as water, wind, or acid
- **词根词缀**: e(出)+rod(咬)+e→咬掉→侵蚀
- **例句**:
- Coastal erosion has caused several villages to be relocated as sea levels rise.
- Prolonged exposure to stress can erode one's mental resilience over time.
- **考频**: 9

## erroneous

- **音标**: i'rәuniәs
- **释义**: a. 错误的, 不正确的\n[法] 错误的, 不正确的
- **英文释义**: containing or characterized by error; incorrect
- **词根词缀**: err(错误)+oneous(形容词)→错误的
- **例句**:

- **考频**: 9
## error

- **音标**: 'erә
- **释义**: n. 错误, 过失, 失误, 误差\n[计] 错误
- **英文释义**: a mistake or something that is wrong
- **词根词缀**: err(错误)+or(名词)→错误
- **例句**:
- A single calculation error led to the failure of the entire experiment.
- The software crashed due to a runtime error, highlighting the importance of rigorous debugging.
- **考频**: 207
## erupt

- **音标**: i'rʌpt
- **释义**: vi. 爆发\nvt. 喷出
- **英文释义**: to break out suddenly and violently, especially with force or intensity (intransitive); to expel or discharge something abruptly (transitive)
- **词根词缀**: e(出)+rupt(破)→破裂而出→爆发
- **例句**:
- Tensions between the two countries erupted into open conflict after the border incident.
- The volcano erupted, spewing ash and lava over the surrounding region.
- **考频**: 12
