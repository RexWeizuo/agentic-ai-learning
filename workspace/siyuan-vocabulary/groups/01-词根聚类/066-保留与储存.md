# 保留与储存

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: 均含“保留、储存”之意，reservoir 为水库/储备，reservation 为预定/保留地。

**4 词**

---

## retain

- **音标**: ri'tein
- **释义**: vt. 保持, 保有, 留住, 记得, 付定金聘请\n[经] 保留, 留存
- **英文释义**: to continue to have or hold something, such as a right, quality, memory, or employee; or to hire someone (e.g., a lawyer) by paying an initial fee
- **词根词缀**: re(回)+tain(保持)→保留
- **例句**:
- The company struggled to retain talented employees amid rising competition in the tech industry.
- Although the passage was dense, she managed to retain the key arguments for her essay.
- **考频**: 48
## retention

- **音标**: ri'tenʃәn
- **释义**: n. 保持, 留置, 保留, 保持力, 保留物, 记忆, 停滞, 固位\n[计] 保贸, 保持
- **英文释义**: The act of keeping or holding something, especially information, a substance, or a person, in a particular place or state.
- **词根词缀**: re(回)+tent(保持)+ion(名词)→保留
- **例句**:
- Long-term memory retention is critical for success in graduate-level examinations.
- The company implemented new training methods to improve employee knowledge retention and reduce turnover.
- **考频**: 4
## reservation

- **音标**: .rezә'veiʃәn
- **释义**: n. 保留, 预定, 保留品, 保留地\n[经] 预定, 预约, 权益保留
- **英文释义**: 1. An arrangement to hold a room, table, seat, etc. for future use; 2. A doubt or hesitation about something; 3. A tract of land reserved for a Native American tribe.
- **词根词缀**: re(回)+serv(保持)+ation(名词)→保留
- **例句**:
- She made a reservation for two at the restaurant for 7 p.m.
- He expressed a reservation about the feasibility of the proposed policy.
- **考频**: 28
## reservoir

- **音标**: 'rezәvwɑ:
- **释义**: n. 贮水池, 贮藏处, 贮备, 水库\nvt. 储藏
- **英文释义**: an artificial lake where water is collected and stored for use, or a natural or artificial place where something is held in reserve
- **词根词缀**: re(回)+serv(保持)+oir(场所)→储存场所→水库
- **例句**:
- The city relies on the mountain reservoir for its drinking water supply.
- Vaccines are kept in cold-chain reservoirs to maintain efficacy during distribution.
- **考频**: 5
