## facilitate

- **音标**: fә'siliteit
- **释义**: vt. 使容易, 促进, 帮助\n[法] 使容易, 使便利, 推进
- **英文释义**: to make an action or process easier or more likely to happen
- **词根词缀**: facil(容易)+it+ate(动词)→使容易→促进
- **例句**:
- The new digital platform will facilitate collaboration among researchers across different institutions.
- Effective communication skills facilitate leadership and team cohesion in high-stakes professional environments.
- **考频**: 50
## facility

- **音标**: fә'siliti
- **释义**: n. 容易, 灵巧, 设备\n[计] 设施; 设备; 装备
- **英文释义**: A building, piece of equipment, or service designed for a specific purpose; also, the ability to do something easily and well.
- **词根词缀**: facil(容易)+ity(名词)→容易→设施
- **例句**:
- The university has state-of-the-art research facilities, including advanced laboratories and high-performance computing systems.
- Her facility with languages enabled her to interpret fluently between English, French, and Mandarin during the international summit.
- **考频**: 119
## factor

- **音标**: 'fæktә
- **释义**: n. 因素, 因数, 系数, 基因, 代理人\n[计] 因式
- **英文释义**: A circumstance, fact, or influence that contributes to a result; in mathematics, a number or expression that divides another evenly.
- **词根词缀**: fact(做)+or(名词)→做事的人/物→因素
- **例句**:
- Economic instability is a key factor contributing to the country's declining investment rate.
- In the equation x² − 5x + 6 = 0, (x − 2) and (x − 3) are factors of the quadratic expression.
- **考频**: 311

## faculty

- **音标**: 'fækәlti
- **释义**: n. 才能, 能力, 全体教员, (大学的)系\n[医] 能力, [大学]院系
- **英文释义**: 1. an inherent mental or physical ability; 2. the teaching staff of a school or university; 3. a division within a university organized around a subject area

- **例句**:

- The Faculty of Medicine at Oxford University is renowned for its research excellence.
