# 词根 -cede/-ceed/-cess (走)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: 表示“走、前进、过程”，注意 precede(在...之前) 与 proceed(继续进行) 的拼写和用法区别。

**7 词**

---

## precede

- **音标**: .pri:'si:d
- **释义**: vt. 在...之前, 优于, 较...优先\nvi. 在前面
- **英文释义**: to come before something in time, order, or importance
- **词根词缀**: pre(前)+cede(走)→走在前面→领先
- **例句**:
- The safety briefing must precede any laboratory experiment.
- In formal logic, a necessary condition always precedes the occurrence of its sufficient condition.
- **考频**: 26
## precedent

- **音标**: 'presidәnt
- **释义**: n. 先例, 前例\na. 在先的, 在前的
- **英文释义**: A previous case or action that serves as an example or guide for future similar situations, especially in law.
- **词根词缀**: pre(前)+cede(走)+ent(名词)→先例
- **例句**:
- The Supreme Court's ruling set a binding precedent for lower courts.
- There is no clear precedent for handling such a complex international trade dispute.
- **考频**: 13
## preceding

- **音标**: .pri:'si:diŋ
- **释义**: a. 在前的, 前面的\n[法] 在前的, 在先的, 前面的
- **英文释义**: coming before something in time, order, or position
- **词根词缀**: pre(前/预)+ced(go/yield)→走
- **例句**:
- The data from the preceding quarter showed a significant decline in revenue.
- As stated in the preceding paragraph, the policy applies only to full-time employees.
## proceed

- **音标**: prәu'si:d
- **释义**: vi. 继续进行, 进行, 开始, 发出, 起诉\n[法] 所得, 收入, 收益
- **英文释义**: to continue doing something after a pause or interruption; to begin or carry on with a course of action
- **词根词缀**: pro(向前)+ceed(走)→向前走→进行
- **例句**:
- After the brief recess, the judge instructed the jury to proceed with deliberations.
- The research team will proceed with data collection once ethical approval is granted.
- **考频**: 40
## proceedings

- **释义**: n. 诉讼, 事项, 行径, 事件, 活动, 项目, 会议录, 记录汇编, 活动记录
- **英文释义**: Official records of a meeting or legal case; formal activities or events, especially those conducted in an organized or official manner.
- **词根词缀**: pro(向前/支持/代替)+ceedings
- **例句**:
- The proceedings of the international climate summit were published in a 200-page volume.
- The court dismissed the appeal on procedural grounds, citing irregularities in the trial proceedings.
## process

- **音标**: 'prɒses
- **释义**: n. 程序, 进行, 过程\nvt. 加工, 使...接受处理, 对...处置, 对...起诉\na. 经加工的, 有特殊光效的\n[计] 进程
- **英文释义**: A series of actions or steps taken to achieve a particular end; to handle or treat something, especially systematically or using a machine or computer.
- **词根词缀**: pro(向前)+cess(走)→向前走→过程
- **例句**:
- The application process for graduate school can take several months.
- Before the data is analyzed, it must be processed to remove inconsistencies.
- **考频**: 554
## procession

- **音标**: prә'seʃәn
- **释义**: n. 队伍, 连续, 列队行进, 涌出
- **英文释义**: A formal, organized group of people or vehicles moving forward together, often in a ceremonial or public context.
- **词根词缀**: pro(向前)+cess(走)+ion(名词)→向前走→行列
- **例句**:
- The royal procession moved slowly down the avenue amid cheering crowds.
- A solemn procession of mourners followed the coffin to the cemetery.
- **考频**: 10
