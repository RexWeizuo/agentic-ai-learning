# 词根 simil/simul (相似/同时/简单)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: 源自拉丁语 similis(相似) 或 simul(同时)。simulate(模拟) 与 simultaneous(同时的) 是考研高频词。

**5 词**

---

## simplicity

- **音标**: sim'plisiti
- **释义**: n. 单纯, 简朴, 朴素, 坦率, 天真\n[计] 简明性
- **英文释义**: The quality or condition of being simple, uncluttered, or free from unnecessary complexity.
- **词根词缀**: simpl(简单)+ic+ity(名词)→简单
- **例句**:
- The elegance of the design lies in its simplicity and functionality.
- In academic writing, clarity and simplicity are valued over ornate language.
- **考频**: 12
## simplify

- **音标**: 'simplifai
- **释义**: vt. 单一化, 简单化
- **英文释义**: to make something easier to understand, do, or use by reducing its complexity
- **词根词缀**: simpl(简单)+ify(使)→使简单→简化
- **例句**:
- The new software update simplifies data analysis for researchers.
- To improve clarity, the committee decided to simplify the application process by removing redundant steps.
- **考频**: 25
## simply

- **音标**: 'simpli
- **释义**: adv. 简单地, 只是, 简直, 简朴地, 坦白地
- **英文释义**: in a simple, straightforward, or unadorned way; merely; just
- **词根词缀**: simpl(简单)+y(副词)→简单地
- **例句**:
- The solution to the problem is simply to revise the algorithm.
- She was simply overwhelmed by the complexity of the passage.
- **考频**: 274
## simulate

- **音标**: 'simjuleit
- **释义**: vt. 模拟, 假装, 模仿\n[法] 伪装的, 模拟的; 假装的, 伪装的, 冒充
- **英文释义**: to imitate a situation, process, or system, especially for training, testing, or analysis
- **词根词缀**: simul(相似)+ate(动词)→使相似→模拟
- **例句**:
- The flight simulator can accurately simulate extreme weather conditions to train pilots.
- Researchers used computer models to simulate the spread of the virus under different public health interventions.
- **考频**: 11
## simultaneous

- **音标**: .simәl'teinjәs
- **释义**: a. 同时的, 同时发生的\n[计] 同时的
- **英文释义**: Happening or done at the same time.
- **词根词缀**: simul(相同)+taneous(形容词)→同时的
- **例句**:
- The conference featured simultaneous interpretation in six languages.
- Modern computers can perform multiple tasks simultaneously, greatly enhancing processing efficiency.
- **考频**: 13
