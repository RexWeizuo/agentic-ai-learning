# 词根 bio-/biblio- (生命/书)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: 词根 bio- (生命) 和 biblio- (书)。biology(生物学), biography(传记), bibliography(参考书目), bible(圣经), botany(植物学)。

**5 词**

---

## biology

- **音标**: bai'ɒlәdʒi
- **释义**: n. 生物学\n[化] 生物; 生物学
- **英文释义**: The scientific study of living organisms and life processes.
- **词根词缀**: bio(生命)+logy(学科)→研究生命的学科→生物学
- **例句**:
- She majored in biology at Harvard and later pursued research in molecular genetics.
- Understanding the principles of biology is essential for addressing global challenges such as climate change and pandemic preparedness.
- **考频**: 44
## biography

- **音标**: bai'ɒgrәfi
- **释义**: n. 传记
- **英文释义**: A detailed account of a person's life, written by someone else.
- **词根词缀**: bio(生命)+graph(写)+y(名词)→写生命→传记
- **例句**:
- Her biography reveals how early adversity shaped her groundbreaking scientific career.
- The Pulitzer Prize-winning biography meticulously reconstructs the political and personal struggles of the 20th-century statesman.
- **考频**: 38
## bibliography

- **音标**: .bibli'ɒgrәfi
- **释义**: n. 参考书目\n[计] 书目; 文献目录
- **英文释义**: A list of books, articles, and other sources used or referred to in a scholarly work.
- **词根词缀**: biblio(书)+graph(写)+y(名词)→写书→书目
- **例句**:
- The author's meticulous bibliography includes over 120 primary and secondary sources.
- Before submitting her thesis, she spent two days verifying every entry in the bibliography for consistency with APA style.
- **考频**: 2
## bible

- **音标**: 'baibl
- **释义**: n. 圣经
- **英文释义**: The sacred scripture of Christianity, consisting of the Old and New Testaments.
- **词根词缀**: bi(两/双)+ble
- **例句**:
- For many Christians, the Bible is the ultimate authority on moral and spiritual matters.
- Scholars often analyze the historical context in which different books of the Bible were written.
## botany

- **音标**: 'bɒtәni
- **释义**: n. 植物学\n[医] 植物学
- **英文释义**: The scientific study of plants, including their structure, function, growth, evolution, and classification.
- **词根词缀**: botany
- **例句**:
- Her research in botany has led to the discovery of several new species of alpine flora.
- A strong foundation in botany is essential for students pursuing careers in ecology or agricultural science.
