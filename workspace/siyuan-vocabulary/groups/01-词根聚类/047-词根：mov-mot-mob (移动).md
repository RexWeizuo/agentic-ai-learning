# 词根：mov/mot/mob (移动)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: 同源词根 mov/mot/mob，表示“移动、运动、动机”。

**7 词**

---

## mobile

- **音标**: 'mәubil
- **释义**: a. 移动的, 易变的, 机动的\nn. 活动物体
- **英文释义**: capable of moving or being moved freely; changeable in form, opinion, or condition
- **词根词缀**: mob(移动)+ile(形容词)→可移动的
- **例句**:
- The company developed a mobile application to enhance user engagement on the go.
- Her mobile personality made it difficult to predict her reaction to the policy change.
- **考频**: 83
## mobilise

- **音标**: 'mәjbilɑiz
- **释义**: vi.vt. 动员, 松动, 使活动, 调动, 发动
- **英文释义**: to organize and prepare people or resources for a particular purpose, especially for military service or political action
- **词根词缀**: mobil+-ise(使…化/…化)
- **例句**:
- The government mobilised thousands of volunteers to assist in the disaster relief efforts.
- Environmental activists are working to mobilise public opinion against the new industrial project.
## motion

- **音标**: 'mәuʃәn
- **释义**: n. 移动, 手势, 动作, 意向, 请求, 提议\nv. 打手势
- **英文释义**: the action or process of moving or being moved; a formal proposal made in a meeting or court
- **词根词缀**: mot(移动)+ion(名词)→移动→运动
- **例句**:
- The judge denied the defendant's motion to dismiss the case.
- She caught his attention with a subtle motion of her hand.
- **考频**: 45
## motivate

- **音标**: 'mәutiveit
- **释义**: vt. 给与动机, 刺激, 提高...的学习欲望, 促动\n[经] 促动, 激发, 激励
- **英文释义**: to provide a reason or incentive for someone to act in a particular way
- **词根词缀**: mot(移动)+iv(形容词)+ate(使)→使移动→激励
- **例句**:
- Her passion for environmental conservation motivated her to pursue a career in sustainable development.
- Effective feedback from teachers can strongly motivate students to improve their academic performance.
- **考频**: 44
## motive

- **音标**: 'mәutiv
- **释义**: n. 动机, 目的, 主题, 基调\na. 运动的, 成为动机的
- **英文释义**: a reason for doing something, especially one that is hidden or not obvious
- **词根词缀**: mot(移动)+ive(形容词)→移动的→动机
- **例句**:
- The detective struggled to identify the suspect's motive for the crime.
- Economic gain was the primary motive behind the company's controversial merger.
- **考频**: 36
## movement

- **音标**: 'mu:vmәnt
- **释义**: n. 运动, 动作, 运转, 移动, 倾向, 变化, 活动, 乐章\n[医] 运动
- **英文释义**: the act or process of moving, or a particular way of moving; also refers to a group of people working together for a shared social, political, or artistic goal
- **词根词缀**: move(移动)+ment(名词)→移动→运动
- **例句**:
- The symphony's third movement is marked by dramatic contrasts in tempo and dynamics.
- The civil rights movement fundamentally transformed American society and legislation.
- **考频**: 198
## movie

- **音标**: 'mu:vi
- **释义**: n. 电影
- **英文释义**: a story filmed for television or cinema, typically involving actors and dialogue
- **词根词缀**: mov(移动)+ie(小)→移动的小东西→电影
- **例句**:
- She analyzed the movie's narrative structure in her film studies thesis.
- The director's latest movie has been shortlisted for several prestigious international awards.
- **考频**: 147
