# 前缀 trans- (跨越，转移)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: trans- 表示“横穿、超越、转移”，是考研极高频前缀，注意各词在具体语境中的引申义。

## transaction

- **音标**: træn'sækʃәn
- **释义**: n. 交易, 办理, 学报, 和解协议\n[计] 事务处理
- **英文释义**: An exchange of goods, services, or money between parties; in computing, a sequence of operations treated as a single unit.
- **词根词缀**: trans(横穿)+act(行动)+ion(名词)→交易
- **例句**:
- The bank charges a fee for each financial transaction.
- In database systems, a transaction must satisfy the ACID properties to ensure data integrity.
- **考频**: 55
