# 词根：leg/lect (收集，选择)

> **类型**: 词根

> **学习提示**: lect/leg 表示“收集、选择”，colleague 是“被选在一起共事的人（同事）”，college 是“学院”。

**5 词**

---

## collect

- **音标**: kә'lekt
- **释义**: v. 收集, 聚集, 集中, 搜集\na. 由收到者付款的\nadv. 由收到者付款地
- **英文释义**: to gather or bring together items, information, or people; to receive payment from the recipient (in telephony/post).
- **词根词缀**: col(共同)+lect(收集)→收集到一起→收集
- **例句**:
- Researchers collected data from over 10,000 participants across five countries.
- The phone call was made collect, meaning the person receiving the call would be charged.
- **考频**: 134
## collection

- **音标**: kә'lekʃәn
- **释义**: n. 收集, 采集, (一批)收藏品, 募捐\n[医] 收集; 收集品, 标本
- **英文释义**: the act of gathering things together or the group of things gathered
- **词根词缀**: col(共同)+lect(收集)+ion(名词)→收集物→收藏
- **例句**:
- The museum's collection of ancient manuscripts is one of the most comprehensive in Europe.
- Researchers emphasized the importance of systematic sample collection to ensure data validity in clinical trials.
- **考频**: 90
## collective

- **音标**: kә'leiktiv
- **释义**: a. 集体的, 聚集的, 共同的\n[医] 集体的, 集合的
- **英文释义**: Shared by, done by, or involving a group of people rather than individuals
- **词根词缀**: col(共同)+lect(收集)+ive(形容词)→集体的
- **例句**:
- The success of the project depended on collective effort and mutual trust.
- Climate change is a global challenge that demands collective action from all nations.
- **考频**: 34
## colleague

- **音标**: 'kɒli:g
- **释义**: n. 同事, 同僚
- **英文释义**: a person with whom one works in the same profession or organization
- **词根词缀**: col(共同)+league(联盟)→共同联盟→同事
- **例句**:
- She collaborated closely with her colleagues on the interdisciplinary research project.
- He consulted several senior colleagues before finalizing the policy proposal.
- **考频**: 157
## college

- **音标**: 'kɒlidʒ
- **释义**: n. 学院, 大学, 学会
- **英文释义**: An institution of higher education that offers undergraduate degrees, often part of a larger university or an independent institution.
- **词根词缀**: col(共同)+leg(选择)+e→共同选择学习→学院
- **例句**:
- She was accepted to Harvard College, where she majored in economics.
- After graduating from college, he pursued a master's degree in computer science at a top-tier university.
- **考频**: 885
