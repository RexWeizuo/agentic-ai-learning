#### 参考答案

**1. 解析**

$$
2\alpha + 3\beta = (2, 4, 0, -6)^{\mathrm{T}} + (-3, 3, -12, 6)^{\mathrm{T}}
$$

$$

因为 $3\alpha_1 + x + 2(\alpha_2 - x) = 5(\alpha_3 + x)$,  
所以

$$
x = \frac{1}{6}(3\alpha_1 + 2\alpha_2 - 5\alpha_3)
$$

$$
= \frac{1}{6} \left[ (6, 15, 3, 9)^{\mathrm{T}} + (20, 2, 10, 20)^{\mathrm{T}} - (20, 5, -5, 5)^{\mathrm{T}} \right]
$$

$$
= (1, 2, 3, 4)^{\mathrm{T}}.
$$

**3. 解析**

$$
\alpha^{\mathrm{T}}\alpha = (1, 2, 1) \begin{pmatrix} 1 \\ 2 \\ 1 \end{pmatrix} = 6,

$$

**4. 解析**

$$
\alpha\alpha^{\mathrm{T}} = \begin{pmatrix} 1 \\ 2 \\ 3 \end{pmatrix} (1, 2, 3) = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 2 & 3 \\ 2 & 4 & 6 \\ 3 & 6 & 9 \end{pmatrix},
