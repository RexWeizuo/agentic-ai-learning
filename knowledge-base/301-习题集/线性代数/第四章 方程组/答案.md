(A, \beta) = \left( \begin{array}{ccc:c} 1 & 1 & a & 0 \\ 1 & 2 & 1 & 0 \\ 1 & -1 & a & 0 \\ 1 & -2 & 3 & 1 \end{array} \right) \xrightarrow{r} \left( \begin{array}{ccc:c} 1 & 2 & 1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 & 0 & 0 \\ 0 & 0 & 2 & 1 \\ 0 & 0 & 0 & a-1 \end{array} \right).
$$

当 $a=1$ 时，$r(A) = r(A, \beta) = 3$，方程组有解，  
故 $a=1$.
