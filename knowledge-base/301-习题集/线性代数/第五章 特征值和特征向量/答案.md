    当 $\lambda = 3$ 时，解 $(A - 3E)x = 0$ 得特征向量 $\alpha_1 = (-1, 0, 1)^T$，  
    当 $\lambda = -3$ 时，解 $(A + 3E)x = 0$ 得特征向量 $\alpha_2 = (1, -2, 1)^T$，  
    当 $\lambda = 0$ 时，解 $Ax = 0$ 得特征向量 $\alpha_3 = (1, 1, 1)^T$。  
    对 $\alpha_1, \alpha_2, \alpha_3$ 进行单位化，  
    $\gamma_1 = \frac{\alpha_1}{||\alpha_1||} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}(-1, 0, 1)^T, \quad \gamma_2 = \frac{\alpha_2}{||\alpha_2||} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{6}}(1, -2, 1)^T,$  
    $\gamma_3 = \frac{\alpha_3}{||\alpha_3||} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{3}}(1, 1, 1)^T,$  
    故 $Q = (\gamma_1, \gamma_2, \gamma_3), Q^T A Q = \Lambda = \begin{pmatrix} 3 & & \\ & -3 & \\ & & 0 \end{pmatrix}$。

## 第 270 页

24. 证明  
    由题意得

$$
\lambda_2 \alpha_1^T \alpha_2 = \alpha_1^T (\lambda_2 \alpha_2) = \alpha_1^T A \alpha_2 = \alpha_1^T A^T \alpha_2
$$

$$
= (A \alpha_1)^T \alpha_2 = (\lambda_1 \alpha_1)^T \alpha_2 = \lambda_1 \alpha_1^T \alpha_2,
$$

则 $(\lambda_2 - \lambda_1) \alpha_1^T \alpha_2 = 0$，又 $\lambda_1 \neq \lambda_2$，  
故 $\alpha_1^T \alpha_2 = 0$。
