由于 $\int_1^{+\infty} f(x) dx$ 收敛，因此以上两个积分都收敛.  
(1) $\int_1^e \frac{1}{(x-1)^{\alpha-1}} dx \stackrel{x-1=t}{=} \int_0^{e-1} \frac{1}{t^{\alpha-1}} dt,$  
因其收敛，所以 $\alpha-1 < 1$，即 $\alpha < 2$.  
(2) $\int_e^{+\infty} \frac{1}{x \ln^{\alpha+1} x} dx = \int_e^{+\infty} \frac{1}{\ln^{\alpha+1} x} d(\ln x) \stackrel{\ln x=t}{=} \int_1^{+\infty} \frac{1}{t^{\alpha+1}} dt,$  
因其收敛，所以 $\alpha+1 > 1$，即 $\alpha > 0$.  
综上，$0 < \alpha < 2$，本题选(D).

## 第 79 页

21、解析

利用分部积分法，得

$$
I_n = \int_0^1 x^a (\ln x)^n dx = \frac{1}{a+1} \int_0^1 (\ln x)^n d(x^{a+1})
$$

$$
= \frac{1}{a+1} x^{a+1} \cdot (\ln x)^n \Big|_0^1 - \frac{1}{a+1} \int_0^1 x^{a+1} d[(\ln x)^n]
$$

$$
= 0 - \lim_{x \to 0^+} \left[ \frac{1}{a+1} x^{a+1} \cdot (\ln x)^n \right] - \frac{n}{a+1} \int_0^1 x^a (\ln x)^{n-1} dx
$$

$$
= -\frac{n}{a+1} \int_0^1 x^a (\ln x)^{n-1} dx = -\frac{n}{a+1} I_{n-1}, \quad n=1, 2, 3, \cdots
$$

反复使用该递推公式，得

$$
I_n = -\frac{n}{a+1} I_{n-1} = (-1)^2 \frac{n(n-1)}{(a+1)^2} I_{n-2} = \cdots = (-1)^n \frac{n!}{(a+1)^n} I_0
$$

又

$$
I_0 = \int_0^1 x^a dx = \frac{1}{a+1}
$$

故

$$
I_n = (-1)^n \frac{n!}{(a+1)^{n+1}}
$$
