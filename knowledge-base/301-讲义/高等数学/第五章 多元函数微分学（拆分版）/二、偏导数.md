# 二、偏导数

 **(四) 偏导数 ( Partial Derivative )**   
**1、定义**  
**回顾导数**

$$
f'(x_0) = \lim_{\Delta x \to 0} \frac{\Delta y}{\Delta x} = \lim_{\Delta x \to 0} \frac{f(x_0 + \Delta x) - f(x_0)}{\Delta x}
$$

**偏导数**  
偏增量 : $\Delta z_x = f(x_0 + \Delta x, y_0) - f(x_0, y_0)$

$$
f_x(x_0, y_0) = \lim_{\Delta x \to 0} \frac{\Delta z_x}{\Delta x} = \lim_{\Delta x \to 0} \frac{f(x_0 + \Delta x, y_0) - f(x_0, y_0)}{\Delta x}
$$

同理有 $f_y(x_0, y_0)$.

**3、高阶偏导数**

$$
f_{xx} = \frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial x^2} = \frac{\partial}{\partial x} \left( \frac{\partial f}{\partial x} \right) \qquad f_{yy} = \frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial y^2} = \frac{\partial}{\partial y} \left( \frac{\partial f}{\partial y} \right)
$$

$$
f_{xy} = \frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial x \partial y} = \frac{\partial}{\partial y} \left( \frac{\partial f}{\partial x} \right) \qquad f_{yx} = \frac{\partial^2 f}{\partial y \partial x} = \frac{\partial}{\partial x} \left( \frac{\partial f}{\partial y} \right)
$$

注: 若 $f_{xy}(x, y)$ 与 $f_{yx}(x, y)$ 在 $(x_0, y_0)$ 处连续,  
则 $f_{xy}(x_0, y_0) = f_{yx}(x_0, y_0)$.  
例. 设 $f(x, y) = \ln x + \sin(x e^y)$, 求 $f_{xx}, f_{yy}, f_{xy}, f_{yx}$.
