# 四、位置关系

三、点、直线和平面的位置关系

(一) 点到平面的距离

[图片：点到平面距离的几何示意图，包含点P、平面π、法向量n、垂足H等]

$$
\begin{aligned}
& P(x_0, y_0, z_0) \\
& \vec{n} = (A, B, C) \\
& \pi: Ax + By + Cz + D = 0 \\
& \overrightarrow{PM} = (x - x_0, y - y_0, z - z_0) \\
& d(P, \pi) = |PH|, \text{为 } \overrightarrow{PM} \text{ 在 } \vec{n} \text{ 上的投影长度.} \\
& \text{故 } |PH| = |\overrightarrow{PM} \cdot \vec{n^0}| \\
& = \left| (x - x_0, y - y_0, z - z_0) \cdot \frac{1}{\sqrt{A^2 + B^2 + C^2}} (A, B, C) \right| \\
& = \frac{|A(x - x_0) + B(y - y_0) + C(z - z_0)|}{\sqrt{A^2 + B^2 + C^2}} \\
& = \frac{|Ax_0 + By_0 + Cz_0 + D|}{\sqrt{A^2 + B^2 + C^2}}
\end{aligned}
$$

例、设 $\pi_1: 4x - 3y + 12z - 11 = 0$, $\pi_2: 4x - 3y + 12z - 17 = 0$, 求到 $\pi_1$, $\pi_2$ 距离之比为 $1 : 2$ 的平面.

(二) 点到直线的距离

[图片：点到直线距离的几何示意图，包含点P、直线L、方向向量s、垂足H等]

$$
\begin{aligned}
& P(x_0, y_0, z_0) \\
& L: \frac{x - x_1}{m} = \frac{y - y_1}{n} = \frac{z - z_1}{p} \\
& \overrightarrow{PM} = (x_1 - x_0, y_1 - y_0, z_1 - z_0) \\
& \vec{s} = (m, n, p) \\
& d(P, L) = |\overrightarrow{PM}| \sin\theta = \frac{|\overrightarrow{PM} \times \vec{s}|}{|\vec{s}|} \\
& = \frac{\left| \begin{vmatrix} \vec{i} & \vec{j} & \vec{k} \\ x_1 - x_0 & y_1 - y_0 & z_1 - z_0 \\ m & n & p \end{vmatrix} \right|}{\sqrt{m^2 + n^2 + p^2}}
\end{aligned}
$$

(三) 两平面关系

$\pi_1: A_1x + B_1y + C_1z + D_1 = 0$, $\pi_2: A_2x + B_2y + C_2z + D_2 = 0$

$\vec{n}_1 = (A_1, B_1, C_1)$, $\vec{n}_2 = (A_2, B_2, C_2)$

1、平行 $\Rightarrow \vec{n}_1 // \vec{n}_2$

[图片：两个平行平面及其法向量的示意图]

$$
\frac{A_1}{A_2} = \frac{B_1}{B_2} = \frac{C_1}{C_2} \neq \frac{D_1}{D_2}
$$

---第206页---

2、垂直 $\Rightarrow \vec{n}_1 \perp \vec{n}_2$

$\Rightarrow \vec{n}_1 \cdot \vec{n}_2$

[图片：两个垂直平面及其法向量的示意图]

$$
= A_1A_2 + B_1B_2 + C_1C_2 = 0
$$

3、夹角为 $\theta$ ($\theta < \frac{\pi}{2}$)

平面夹角 $\theta$ 与法向量夹角 $\theta_0$ 相等或互补

若 $\theta = \theta_0$, 则 $\cos\theta = \cos\theta_0 > 0$

若 $\theta + \theta_0 = \pi$, 则 $\cos\theta = \cos(\pi - \theta_0) = -\cos\theta_0 > 0$

则 $\cos\theta = |\cos\theta_0| = \frac{|\vec{n}_1 \cdot \vec{n}_2|}{|\vec{n}_1| \cdot |\vec{n}_2|}$

[图片：两个相交平面及其夹角和法向量夹角的示意图]

$$
= \frac{|A_1A_2 + B_1B_2 + C_1C_2|}{\sqrt{A_1^2 + B_1^2 + C_1^2} \sqrt{A_2^2 + B_2^2 + C_2^2}}
$$

例、求过直线 $L: \begin{cases} x + 2z + 1 = 0 \\ x - y - z + 1 = 0 \end{cases}$, 且与平面 $\pi: x + y + 2z - 4 = 0$ 成 $\frac{\pi}{3}$ 夹角的平面方程.

（四）两直线关系

$L_1: \frac{x - x_1}{m_1} = \frac{y - y_1}{n_1} = \frac{z - z_1}{p_1}$, $\vec{s}_1 = (m_1, n_1, p_1)$

$L_2: \frac{x - x_2}{m_2} = \frac{y - y_2}{n_2} = \frac{z - z_2}{p_2}$, $\vec{s}_2 = (m_2, n_2, p_2)$

1、 $L_1 // L_2 \implies \vec{s}_1 // \vec{s}_2 \iff \frac{m_1}{m_2} = \frac{n_1}{n_2} = \frac{p_1}{p_2}$

2、 $L_1 \perp L_2 \iff \vec{s}_1 \perp \vec{s}_2 \iff m_1 m_2 + n_1 n_2 + p_1 p_2 = 0$

3、夹角为 $\theta$ $(\theta < \frac{\pi}{2})$

$$
\cos \theta = \frac{|\vec{s}_1 \cdot \vec{s}_2|}{|\vec{s}_1| \cdot |\vec{s}_2|} = \frac{|m_1 m_2 + n_1 n_2 + p_1 p_2|}{\sqrt{m_1^2 + n_1^2 + p_1^2} \cdot \sqrt{m_2^2 + n_2^2 + p_2^2}}
$$

4、 $L_1, L_2$ 共面 $\iff [\vec{s}_1, \vec{s}_2, \overrightarrow{M_1 M_2}] = 0$

（五）直线与平面的关系

$L: \frac{x - x_0}{m} = \frac{y - y_0}{n} = \frac{z - z_0}{p}$, $\vec{s} = (m, n, p)$

$\Pi: Ax + By + Cz + D = 0$, $\vec{n} = (A, B, C)$

1、 $L // \Pi \iff \vec{s} \perp \vec{n}$

$\iff Am + Bn + Cp = 0$,

且 $Ax_0 + By_0 + Cz_0 + D \neq 0$.

[图片：直线平行于平面示意图]

2、 $L \perp \Pi \iff \vec{s} // \vec{n}$

$\iff \frac{A}{m} = \frac{B}{n} = \frac{C}{p}$

[图片：直线垂直于平面示意图]

3、夹角为 $\theta$ $(\theta < \frac{\pi}{2})$

记 $\theta_0 = (\vec{s}, \vec{n})$

则 $\theta_0 < \frac{\pi}{2}$ 时， $\theta + \theta_0 = \frac{\pi}{2}$

$\sin \theta = \sin(\frac{\pi}{2} - \theta_0) = \cos \theta_0 > 0$

$\theta_0 > \frac{\pi}{2}$ 时， $\theta_0 - \theta = \frac{\pi}{2}$

$\sin \theta = \sin(\theta_0 - \frac{\pi}{2}) = -\cos \theta_0 > 0$

故 $\sin \theta = |\cos \theta_0| = \frac{|\vec{s} \cdot \vec{n}|}{|\vec{s}| \cdot |\vec{n}|}$

$= \frac{|Am + Bn + Cp|}{\sqrt{A^2 + B^2 + C^2} \cdot \sqrt{m^2 + n^2 + p^2}}$

[图片：直线与平面夹角示意图]
